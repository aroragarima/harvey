Commissioner of Income Tax v Venkateswara Hatcheries Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.P. Bharucha, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1195; (1999) 3 SCC 632; AIR 1999 SC 1225; 1999 (4) ALD(SC) 30; 1999 (153) CTR 105; [1999] 237 ITR 174; JT 1999 (2) SC 338; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 177; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 503; 1999 (2) TLR 407

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(ka) - Interpretation of Statutes - Word not defined in statute - Repeal of a provision and simultaneous re-enactment of similar provision - Effect - Held, re-enacted provision regarded to have force continuously - Adopting dictionary meaning - Not safe when it gives more than one meaning - Word to be construed in context of provisions of statute - Hatchery business - Neither an industrial undertaking - Nor a business involving production of articles or things.
