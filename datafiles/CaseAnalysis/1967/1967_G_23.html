Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City I v Greaves Cotton and Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, J.C. Shah, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 308; [1968] 68 ITR 200

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - Whether amount paid for the termination of their managing agency agreement was an admissible deduction? - ITAT has not taken into account all the relevant material adduced by the parties and its order is unsatisfactory - Question of law referred to High Court cannot be answered in view of the defective finding by the ITAT - Held, HC order set aside and ITAT will rehear the case - Appeal partly allowed.
