Kashi Math Samsthan and Another v Srimad Sudhindra Thirtha Swamy and Another

Supreme Court of India

02 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, H.L. Dattu

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1600; (2010) 1 SCC 689; AIR 2010 SC 296; 2010 (1) AWC 856; 2010 (1) JhrCR(SC) 118; JT 2009 (15) SC 33; 2010 (3) MLJ(SC) 351; 2010 (1) RCR(Civil) 581; 2010 (109) RD 558; 2010 (1) RLW 578; 2009(14) SCALE 274; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 1309; 2010 (1) UC 647

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Respondent No. 1 entrusted his authorities, powers and privileges as 20th Pontiff and head of Math to and in favour of Appellant no 2 on and w.e.f. 12-12-1994 - Appellant no. 2 instituted a suit to declare him as Mathadhipathi and 21st Pontiff of Math and also prayed for an injunction, restraining Respondent No. 1 from exercising powers, duties and privileges as Mathadhipathi of Math - Trial Court dismissed suit of appellants and allowed counter claim of respondent no. 1 by granting a decree for permanent/mandatory injunction thereby directing appellant no.2 to hand over articles in his possession to respondent no. 1 - On appeal, HC rejected interim applications filed by appellants seeking status quo and stay of execution of decree passed by Trial Court - Hence, present appeal - Whether HC's order could be upheld? - Held, succession to position of Mathadhipathi can only be done after death of existing Mathadhipathi and not before it - Moreover, perusal of proclamation dt. 12-12-1994 would not conclusively suggest that Respondent No. 1 had abdicated all his powers as Mathadhipathi of Math in favour of Appellant No. 2 - HC's order upheld - Appeals dismissed.
