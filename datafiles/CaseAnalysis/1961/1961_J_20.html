Jute and Gunny Brokers Limited And Another v Union of India and Others.(and connected appeals)

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 57; AIR 1961 SC 1214; [1962] 32 Comp Cas 845; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 820

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Civil Procedure; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Companies Act, 1913

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Defence of India Rules, 1939, rr.75 A(2), 75 A(3)and 119 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 29, r. 2 - Companies Act, 1913, s. 148 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, s. 18 - Order of requisition - Ownership in goods passed by pacca delivery orders - Stating opinion of Central Govt. - Expedient for maintaining supplies and services essential to life of community - (A) Validity - (B) Proper mode of service of requisition u/r. 2, O. 29 of Code - (C) Notice of acquisition of goods in possession of company who had issued pucca delivery orders - Whether service of notice on Co. as owner through its managing agent is valid - Held, opinion is final despite of non compliance of procedural conditions - Service on Managing agents who is principal officer is effective and proper - Service of notice held valid and effective u/r. 75 A(2) - Appeals Nos. 314 to 316 of 1957 dismissed ; Appeal No. 778 of 1957 allowed.
