Harjinder Singh v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 17; (1985) SCC (Cr) 93; (1985) 1 SCC 422; AIR 1985 SC 404; 1985 BBCJ 38; 1986 CRLJ 831; 1985 CRLR 66; 1984(2) SCALE 996

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Double Jeopardy

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 223 - Whether two sessions trials be consolidated and clubbed together and evidence recorded in one case be read as evidence in other - Held, it is not permissible for Court under s. 223 of Code to club and consolidated two different case which are materially different, contradictory and mutually exclusive - Appeal allowed.
