U. P. Pollution Control Board v Mohan Meakins Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 219; (2000) 3 SCC 745; AIR 2000 SC 1456; 2000 ALJ 872; 2000 (2) AWC 1462; 2000 (1) CCR 348; [2000] 101 Comp Cas 278; 2000 (3) CompLJ 408; 2000 (2) Crimes 35; 2000 CRLJ 1799; 2000 (2) JCC 627; JT 2000 (3) SC 466; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 421; 2000 (2) RCR(Rent) 421; 2000(2) SCALE 532; 2000 (3) SCJ 64; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 566; 2000 (3) SLT 162; 2000 (2) Supreme 520

Case Digest

Subject: Water

Keywords: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act.

Summary: Water - Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Water Pollution - Water (Prevention And Control Of Pollution) Act, 1974, ss. 43, 44 and 47 - Respondent discharged effluents thereby polluting Gomti river beyond permissible levels - State Pollution Control Board initiated proceedings against respondents but which remains pending for last 17 years - Process issued against respondent was quashed by sessions and board moved High Court against said order and HC took 15 years to dismiss revision petition by board - Held, at ouset, Sessions Judge wrongly quashed process issuing order as no such requirement in s. 204, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - Delay of 17 years not because of board and hence respondents cannot take benefit of it - HC order set aside - Direct Trial Court to proceed with case in accordance with law - Appeal allowed.
