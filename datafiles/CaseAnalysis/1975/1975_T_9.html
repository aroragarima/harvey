Gulam Mustafa and Others v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 249; (1976) 1 SCC 800; AIR 1977 SC 448; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 875; 1975 UJ 934

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Hyderabad District Municipalities Act, 1956

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Hyderabad District Municipalities Act, 1956, s. 2(26) - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Land acquisition by Local Govt. - Failure to utilize the land for the purpose the same acquired - Validity - Whether land acquisition by Municipality was colorable exercise of power? - Held, no - Once original acquisition is valid and title has vested in Municipality, how it uses excess land is no concern of original owner and cannot be basis for invalidating acquisition - Appeal dismissed.
