Regional Provident Fund Commissioner v S. D. College, Hoshiarpur and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 345; (1997) 1 SCC 241; (1997) SCC (L&S) 449; AIR 1997 SC 3645; 1998 (1) CLR 274; JT 1997 (10) SC 638; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 71; 1997 LabIC 910; 1997 (2) LLJ 55; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 27

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s. 14B - Delayed payment - Levy of penalty - Educational institution - Direction of court obliging institution to deposit amount to Provident Fund Commr. - Deposit of amount thereafter in University account by institution - (A) Whether proper? - Mere fact that University gave permission to re-deposit amount with Provident Fund Commr. - (B) Whether enables institution to plead that there was no non deposit? - (C) Whether penalty under Act can be waived on that count? - Held, (A) Improper - (B) Institution cannot plead no non deposit (C) Penalty cannot be waived on that count - Appeal allowed.
