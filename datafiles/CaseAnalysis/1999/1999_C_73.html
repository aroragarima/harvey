Collector Of Central Excise, Jaipur v J.K. Synthetics

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1982; (2000) 10 SCC 393; 2000 (120) E.L.T. 54

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excise Rules, 1944 - Whether Tribunal had erred in upholding respondent's claim that it was entitled to exemption given to MEG under Notification D/- 4-05-1987? - There had been substantial compliance with procedure given in said notification by respondents - Held, there is no additional duty that respondent MEG can be made liable to - Appeal dismissed.
