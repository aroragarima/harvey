J. D. Jain v The Management Of State Bank Of India & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Baharul Islam, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 326; (1982) SCC (L&S) 68; (1982) SCC (Cr) 122; (1982) 1 SCC 143; AIR 1982 SC 673; 1982 (44) FLR 65; 1982 LabIC 356; 1982 (1) LLJ 54; 1982 (1) LLN 33; 1981(3) SCALE 1884; [1982] 2 S.C.R. 227; 1982 (2) SLJ 96; 1981 (3) SLR 175; 1982 UJ 73

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 227 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11A, sch. 2, item 3 and 6 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 60 - Applicability of Rules of evidence to a domestic enquiry into discharge of bank employee - What amounts to hearsay evidence - Interference by High Court with award of Tribunal - Jurisdiction of the HC is limited - Appeal Dismissed.
