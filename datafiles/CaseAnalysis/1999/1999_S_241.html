P. Murugesan v State and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1665; (1999) 5 SCC 642; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1022; AIR 1999 SCW 4751; 1999 (3) CalLT(SC) 71; 2000 (1) CCR 258; 2000 (1) Crimes 21; 1999 CRLJ 5010; JT 1999 (9) SC 76; 2000 (2) SLT 311; 1999 (9) Supreme 467

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Detention - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Bootleggers, Drug Offenders, Forest Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Slum Grabbers Act, 1982 - Validity of - Refusal of request made by detenu to have assistance of friend who was present at same place - Held, denial of an opportunity to a detenu, if he made request and when such a friend was present, would violate constitutional provision and would vitiate detention - Discussed Anil Vats v. Union of India, 1990 Indlaw SC 549 - Appeal allowed.
