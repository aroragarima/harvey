Sher Singh & Ors v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 152; (1984) 1 SCC 107; 1984 (1) ACC 93; AIR 1984 SC 200; 1983 (3) CompLJ 306; 1983(2) SCALE 531; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 464; 1984 UJ 118

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules 1940

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - T.N. Motor Vehicles Rules, 1940 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 19(1) - Motor Vehicles - Application for renewal of permit u/r. 155A(3)(d) of 1940 Rules - Refusal to renew permit of private operators in comparison to the Undertaking - Conflict between ss. 47 (1H) and 58(2) of 1939 Act - Questions arised - (A) Does s. 47 (1H) violates the provisions of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) of Constitution - (B) Whether refusal is justified - Held, not violative - Refusal justified - Petition dismissed.
