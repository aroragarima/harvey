Koluttumottil Razak v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 172; (2000) 4 SCC 465; (2000) SCC (Cr) 829; 2001 (1) UC 111

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 21, 42 and 50 - Appellant convicted for being in possession of brown sugar and he was also convicted previously - Held, no independent witness except police witnesses - And in view of non - compliance with provisions of s. 42(1) and (2) besides s. 50, it is difficult to sustain conviction - Appeal allowed.
