Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961,ss.119,245B,245C,245D(4),(6),234A,234B,234C, 245H - Constitution of Commission - Settlement of case - Jurisdiction - Power of waiver or reduction of interest - Constitution of Commission and its powers were traceable to provisions of Chapter XIX-A of Act - S.245-B of 1961 Act provided for constitution of Commission by Central Govt. for settlement of cases - S.245-C provided for filing of application by an assessee at any stage of case giving full and true disclosure of his income not disclosed before assessing officer - Commission had authority to call for report from Commissioner of Income Tax - S.245D(4) confers wide power on Commission in process of settling a case - Commission had all powers of Board included Boards power u/s.119 of Act to relax rigors of ss.234A,234B,234C of Act - Commission observed that it had power of waiver or reduction of statutory interest to settle case without any restriction by applying rule of construction, - Hence instant appeal - Whether Commission constituted u/s.245B of Act had jurisdiction to reduce or waive interest chargeable u/ss.234A,234B,234C of Act - Held, s.158BF mandate that no interest under provisions of ss.234A,234B,234C of Act should levied or imposed upon assessee in respect of undisclosed income determined in block assessment - Interest contemplated was mandatory in nature and power of waiver or reduction have not expressly conferred on Commission - Statute had permitted it to pass such orders as it deems fit in regard to payment of interest - Commission would have to pass orders as it thinks fit on matters covered by application - S.245D(6) of Act was only procedural in nature not to empower Commission either to waive or reduce interest - Assessee liable for various penal actions for non disclosure - Commission had power to grant immunity to assessee from prosecution and penalty u/s.245H - Commission in exercise of its power u/ss.245(4),(6) of Act did not have power to reduce or waive interest statutorily payable u/ss.234A,234B,234C of Act except to extent of granting relief under Circulars issued by Board u/s.119 of Act - Matters placed before Division Bench for disposal in accordance with law - Order accordingly.
