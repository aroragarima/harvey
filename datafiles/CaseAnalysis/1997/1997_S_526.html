Supreme Court of India

28 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Judicial Service (Classification, Recruitment And Conditions Of Service)

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.309 and 311; M.P. Judicial Service (Classification, Recruitment and Condition of Services) Rules, 1955, r.24 - Probationer - Confirmation - Held, can be deemed when maximum period of probation is fixed by rules - Rule fixing maximum probation not whittled by any other provision in rules - Probationer Judicial Officer would be deemed to be confirmed on expiry of maximum period - His termination without enquiry violates art.311.
