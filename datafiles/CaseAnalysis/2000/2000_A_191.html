Election Commission Of India Through Secretary v Ashok Kumar And Others

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2833; (2000) 8 SCC 216; AIR 2000 SC 2979; JT 2000 (9) SC 529; 2000 (3) KLT 402; 2000(6) SCALE 182; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 34

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election

Keywords: Election Dispute, Delimitation Of Constituencies

Summary: Election - Constitution, arts. 324 and 329 - Conduct of Election Rules, 1961, r. 59A - Election Commission notification D/-28-09-99 for free and fair election, safety and security of electors, to prevent intimidation and victimisation of elect - Electoral matters are barred from interference of Courts - Counting of votes by mixing of votes of all booths - Notification challenged before HC - Interim order - Suspension amidst progress of election proceedings - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
