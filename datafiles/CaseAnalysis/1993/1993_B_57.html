Chetana Dilip Motghare v Bhide Girls’ Education Society Nagpur

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1639; (1995) Supp1 SCC 157; (1995) SCC (L&S) 312; AIR 1994 SC 1917; 1995 (29) ATC 107; 1994 LabIC 1608

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16(4) - Reservation in isolated post - Instant review petition against decision of SC which held that if there was only one post in cadre, there could be no reservation u/art. 16(4) of Constitution - Whether review petition is maintainable.

Held, decision of SC in Dr.Chakradhar Paswan v. State of Bihar, 1988 INDLAW SC 883 still holds the field and the decision by three Judge Bench dt.17-1-1992 does not lay down any law and is not an authority for holding that the principle of reservation has to be applied in case of even one isolated post also. Further, State Govt. had also issued circular after the decision in Dr.Chakradhar Paswan v. State of Bihar to effect that the principle of reservation would not apply in the case of an isolated post. Review dismissed.
