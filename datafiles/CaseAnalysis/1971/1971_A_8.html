Â M. Gurumoorthy v Accountant General Assam & Nagaland & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter, K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 170; (1971) 2 SCC 137; AIR 1971 SC 1850; 1971 (22) FLR 327; 1971 (2) LLJ 109; [1971] S.C.R. 420; 1971 (2) SLR 434; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 420

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 229 - Officers and servants of High Courts - Chief Justice of a HC or his nominee is the supreme authority in the matter of appointments of officers and servants of HC - Interference by Govt. - Permissibility - Held, interference by Govt. , except to the limited extent as provided in the Article, is not permissible - Appeal Allowed.
