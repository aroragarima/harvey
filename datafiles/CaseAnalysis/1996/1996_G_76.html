P. Ratnakar Rao And Ors. v Government Of Andhra Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3007; (1996) 5 SCC 359; 1996 (2) ACC 512; AIR 1996 SC 2523; JT 1996 (6) SC 624; 1996(5) SCALE 386; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 866

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Compounding Fee

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 194 and 200 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Compounding of offence u/s. 194 is not mandatory as it depends upon willingness of accused - (A) Whether power delegated u/s. 200 of 1988 Act to to state Govt. to prescribed compounding fees is unguided - (B) Validity of GOMS 54, D/- 31-03-1995 of Andhra Pradesh Govt. to be determined - Held, power not unguided - Valid - Petition dismissed.
