Union of India v Ajaib Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3392; (1996) 9 SCC 638; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 249; AIR 1996 SC 3148; 1996 (1) CCC 328; JT 1996 (6) SC 73; 1996 (1) RentLR 750; 1996(2) SCALE 594; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 460

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property Act, 1952, s. 8(1) - Acquisition of property - Compensation - Arbitrator awarded compensation with solatium and interest - Further enhancement of solatium and interest by High Court applying provisions of Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, without enhancing compensation - (A) If without jurisdiction - (B) Whether Land Acquisition Amendment Act 68 of 1984 would apply? - Held, (A) Is without jurisdiction - (B) Land Acquisition Act (1894) does not apply to acquisition of land under Act of 1952 and so Amendment Act 68 of 1984 equally would not apply - Appeal allowed.
