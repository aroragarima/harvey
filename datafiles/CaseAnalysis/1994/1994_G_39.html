V. Sreenivasa Reddy and Others v Government of A. P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2038; (1995) Supp1 SCC 572; (1995) SCC (L&S) 579; AIR 1995 SC 586; AIR 1994 SCW 4868; 1995 (29) ATC 495; 1995 (70) FLR 126; JT 1994 (6) SC 461; 1995 LabIC 319; 1994(4) SCALE 469; 1995 (1) SLJ 99; 1994 (5) SLR 715; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 233

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Service - Special Rules for Andhra Pradesh Panchayati Raj Engineering Service, r. 5 - Andhra Pradesh State, Subordinate Service rules, 1966, rr. 10(a)(i)(1), 23 and 33 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Panchayat Raj Engineering Service - Asst. Executive Engineers - Seniority among direct recruits and temporary appointees - Held, direct recruits appointed on 24-05-1984 held senior to temporary appointees whose services were regularised on 11-06-1984 - Appeal dismissed.
