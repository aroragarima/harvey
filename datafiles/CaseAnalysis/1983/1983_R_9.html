Gangadhar and Another v Raj Kumar

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 341; (1984) 1 SCC 121; AIR 1983 SC 1202; 1983 (9) ALR 672; 1983(2) SCALE 446

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 22 rr. 4 and 10A - Substitution of LRs on death of sole respondent - Knowledge of death - High court refusal to set aside abatement without considering new r. 10-A - Validity - Held, HC was in error in refusing to set aside abatement - Matter Remitted To High Court.
