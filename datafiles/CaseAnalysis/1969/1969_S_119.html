Hyderabad Asbestos Cement Products Limited v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 62; 1969 (24) STC 487

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957 - Appellant, a public limited co. carrying on business of manufacturing and selling asbestos cement sheets and other products -Uniform catalogue rate all over country charged by appellant - Customer paying amount of invoice less freight for releasing railway receipt and taking delivery of goods on payment of railway freight - Whether appellant is liable to pay sales tax on railway freight? - Company may be liable to refund amount of excess sales tax to its purchasers - But that is a matter between co. and purchasers and State cannot seek to levy tax on railway freight, if it is nor made a part of price - Held, under terms of contract there is no obligation on appellant to pay freight, and under terms of contract price received by appellant for sale of goods is invoice amount less freight - State cannot seek to levy tax on railway freight - Appeals allowed.
