Scientific Advisor To Raksha Mantri and Another v V. M. Joseph

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1374; (1998) 5 SCC 305; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1362; AIR 1998 SC 2318; JT 1998 (4) SC 20; 1999 (2) LLJ 508; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 177; 1998 (3) SLJ 245; 1998 (2) UPLBEC 1276

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Eligibility for promotion - Consideration of service rendered by employee at earlier place before transfer on compassionate ground on his own request, if allowed - Held, earlier services can not excluded, as eligibility and seniority are two distinct and different factors - Appeal dismissed.
