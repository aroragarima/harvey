Gowrishankar & Anr. v Joshi Amba Shankar Family Trust & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.K. Mukherjee, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 589; (1996) 3 SCC 310; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 440; AIR 1996 SC 2202; 1996 (2) CLT 172; JT 1996 (2) SC 560; 1996(2) SCALE 454; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 949

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts and Associations - Trusts Act, 1882, s.15 - Permission sought for sale of trust property by trustees without disclosin of higher offer by tenants - False statement as inability to secure higher offer -Held, sale liable to be set aside as permission obtained by fraud on court - And question whether the purchasers purchased the property bona fide subsequent to the permission so granted without notice of the appellants offer would be immaterial - Appeal allowed.
