Commissioner Of Central Excise v West Coast Industrial Gases Ltd

Supreme Court of India

23 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1103; 2003 (155) E.L.T. 11; 2003 (57) RLT 2

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 57F - Whether or not Modvat credit in respect of metal containers are required to be reversed, if metal container or, packing material in which inputs was received is not used in or in relation to manufacture of final product? - These drums/barrels could not be treated to be a waste arising out of processing of inputs for which credit has been taken - Held, while issuing circular D/- 19-07-1999, concerned authority has not applied its mind to ratio laid down by CEGAT in IOL's case 1992 Indlaw CEGAT 612 wherein it has been pointed out that there is no specific provision under Rules considering such barrels/drums as a waste arising out of manufacturing process - There is no specific rule levying duty on such drums/barrels/containers - Appeal dismissed.
