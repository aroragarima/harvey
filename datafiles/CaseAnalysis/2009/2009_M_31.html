M. Gopalakrishnan v State By Additional S. P. Cbi, B.S. and F.C

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 115; (2009) 2 SCC (Cr) 177; (2009) 3 SCC 681; AIR 2009 SC 2015; 2009 (2) JhrCR(SC) 93; JT 2009 (3) SC 497; 2009 (3) MLJ(Crl) 199; 2009 (2) RCR(Criminal) 534; 2009(2) SCALE 496; [2009] 3 S.C.R. 151

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Service

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 482 - Application contending that there was no sanction for prosecution dismissed - Appeal against - Held, in view of the specific provisions available for the removable of the Chairman under the Banking Regulation Act it is prima facie clear that the appellant will not come within the scope of Section 197 of Code - High Court's view does not suffer from infirmity to warrant interference - Appeal dismissed.
