Bachan & Another v Kankar & Others

Supreme Court of India

26 July 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 162; (1972) 2 SCC 555; AIR 1972 SC 2157; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 727; 1973 UJ 272

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Zamindar Abolition and Land reforms Act 1950 (Uttar Pradesh 1 of 1951), Land Revenue Act 1901

Summary: U.P.Z.A.&L.R. Act, 1951, s. 20(b)(1) - Revenue Records - An entry incorrectly introduced by the Patwari into the record of rights in favour of the respondents by reason of ill-will or hostility of Patwari against the appellants - Validity of entry - Entry held fictitious and respondents are not entitled to claim adhivasi rights u/s. 20(b) - Appeal Allowed.
