Patna Improvement Trust v Smt. Lakshmi Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, J.R. Madholkar, Syed Jaffer Imam, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 573; AIR 1963 SC 1077; 1965 (1) SCJ 1; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 812; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 812

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 50 - Bihar Town Planning and Improvement Trust Act, 1951, s. 71 - (A) Whether Bihar Act completely replaces Act, 1894 for acquisition of land for purpose of Improvement Trust? - Provisions of s. 71 of Bihar Act which modifies Act, 1894 states that for purpose of acquisition of land for Trust under Act, 1894, that Act shall be subject to specified modification - Held, even for purpose of acquiring land for Trust, machinery of Act, 1894, is contemplated by Bihar Act and it does not exclude application of Act, 1894 - (B) Whether notifications issued by State Govt. u/s. 4 of the Act, 1894 valid? - Further held, yes, as intention of legislature was not to exclude functions of Act, 1894 such as ss. 4, 6 and 50 etc. in matter of acquisition of land for purpose of a Trust - Notification issued by State Govt. is not, therefore, invalid - Case remitted to High Court - Appeals allowed.
