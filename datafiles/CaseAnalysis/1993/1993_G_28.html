U.P. Financial Corporation v Gem Cap (India) Private Limited And Others

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 349; (1993) 2 SCC 299; AIR 1993 SC 1435; AIR 1993 SCW 1189; 1993 ALJ 515; 1993 (2) AWC 1125; 1993 (1) CCC 581; [1993] 78 Comp Cas 408; JT 1993 (2) SC 226; 1993 (2) MLJ 23; 1993 (2) MLJ(SC) 23; 1993(1) SCALE 747; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 149

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Banking & Finance

Keywords: Administrative Action

Summary: Administrative - Banking and Finance - State Financial Corporations Act, 1951, s. 29 - Financial Corporation has to act fairly in recovery of loan - Whether HC can sit over administrative decision of Corporation to initiate recovery proceedings against its debtor - Held, cannot sit over as such - Appeal allowed.
