Income Tax Officer, Alleppey v M. C. Ponnoose and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 309; (1969) 2 SCC 351; AIR 1970 SC 385; [1970] 75 ITR 174; 1969 KLT 641; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 678

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Tax Recovery Officer, Recovery Officer, Finance Act, 1963

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 2(44)(ii) - Constitution, art. 245 - Notification u/s. 2(44)(ii) - Thereby State Govt. investing Tahsildar with powers of Tax Recovery Officer - Authorisation - Retrospectivity - Authorisation held, cannot be made retrospectively - Appeal Dismissed.
