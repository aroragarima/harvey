Koksingh v Smt. Deokabai

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 439; (1976) 1 SCC 383; AIR 1976 SC 634; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 963; 1976 UJ 149

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 33 - Prohibition Act - Power of appellate Court to grant relief in favour of respondent - Held, High Court under O. 41 r. 33, is competent to pass a decree for the enforcement of a charge in favour of the respondent notwithstanding the fact that the respondent did not file any appeal from the decree - Appeal Dismissed.
