Amin Chand Payarelal v Inspecting Assistant Commissioner, Income Tax and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 467; (2006) 7 SCC 483; 2006 (204) CTR 585; [2006] 285 ITR 546; JT 2006 (8) SC 373; 2006(9) SCALE 10; [2006] Supp5 S.C.R. 730; [2006] 155 TAXMAN 633

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: [A.] Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 139(4), 271(1)(a), 156 - Petition to withdraw orders of assessment and penalty proceeding - Whether penalty u/s. 271(1)(a) could be levied on a return filed u/s. 139(4)? - Held, s.139(4) provides for a situation where returns are not filed by an assessee within time allowed or within extended period for filing such returns - Returns in question had not been filed either within time allowed under Act or within extended period - Penalty could be levied in present case u/s. 271(1)(a) - Appeal dismissed.
