K. L. Shinde v State of Mysore

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 133; (1976) 3 SCC 76; (1976) SCC (L&S) 386; AIR 1976 SC 1080; 1976 LabIC 699; 1976 (1) LLN 465; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 913; 1976 SLJ 468; 1976 (2) SLR 102; 1976 UJ 429

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Police Manual, 1950

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 311(2) - Bombay Police Manual, 1950 - Police - Departmental enquiry - Question of fact - Evidence to justify dismissal order - (A) Reasonable opportunity to defend - (B) Reassessment to evidence in appeal to Supreme Court - Opportunity held not denied to delinquent officer - Supreme Court does not re-assess evidence in appeal and Rules of evidence in Evidence Act not applicable - Appeal dismissed.
