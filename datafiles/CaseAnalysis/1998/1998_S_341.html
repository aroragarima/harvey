Mariyappa and Others v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1485; (1998) 3 SCC 276; AIR 1998 SC 1334; JT 1998 (1) SC 734; 1998 (3) MLJ(SC) 34; 1998(1) SCALE 672; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 988; 1998 (3) SLT 297; 1998 (2) Supreme 503

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Calcutta Improvement Act, 1911, Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, City of Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, Madhya Pradesh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956, Karnataka Acquisition of Land For Grant of House Sites Act, 1972, Madhya Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1961

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 1, Pre. 11A - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Extent of applicability of Land Acquisition Act, 1894 to Karnataka Acquisition of Land for grant of House Sites Act, 1973 - Considering words 'Mutatis mutandis' - For subsequent changes, insertion of s. 11A and other amendments in Central Act - Held, subsequent changes made in Central Act after 1972 cannot get into Karnataka Act and s. 11A has to be read into Karnataka Act - Question as to applicability of other amendments made in 1984 in Central Act left open - Appeal allowed.
