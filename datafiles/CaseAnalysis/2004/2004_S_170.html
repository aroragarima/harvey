State of Himachal Pradesh v Shree Kant Shekari

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 730; (2004) 8 SCC 153; AIR 2004 SC 4404; 2004 CRLJ 4232; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 108; JT 2004 (8) SC 235; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 345; 2004(7) SCALE 647; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 380; 2005 (1) UC 68

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss 376, 506 - Rape - Consent - Trial court convicted but HC acquitted - Held, question of consent is really a matter of defence by accused and it was for him to place materials to show that there was consent - Conclusions of HC are not to be sustained - Order of trial Court restored - Appeal allowed.
