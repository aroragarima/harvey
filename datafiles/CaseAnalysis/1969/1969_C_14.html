Chanan Singh & Another v Jai Kaur

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 159; (1969) 2 SCC 429; AIR 1970 SC 349; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 803

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Punjab Pre-emption Act, 1913 - (A) First para of s. 15(2)(b) of the Act - Sale of property which was received by widow from her husband - Step-daughter's right of pre-emption - (B) Scope of s. 31 - Power of Appellate Court - To give retrospective effect - Held, can exercise right of pre-emption and Amendment Act of 1964 should be given retrospective effect - Can give retrospective effect to substantive provisions of Amending Act 1960 only - Appeal Dismissed.
