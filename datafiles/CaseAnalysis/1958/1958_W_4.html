Workmen of Dimakuchi Tea Estate v Management of Dimakuchi Tea Estate

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, Sudhi Ranjan Das, A.K. Sarkar

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 192; AIR 1958 SC 353; 1958 (1) LLJ 500; 1958 SCJ 637; [1958] 1 S.C.R. 1156

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Labour Appellate Tribunal

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law -Practice & Procedure - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(k) - (A) Whether expression 'any person' u/s. 2(k) includes person who is not a workman? - Held, a dispute concerning a person who is not a workman may be an industrial dispute - Workmen raising dispute must have a direct or substantial interest in employment, non employment, terms of employment or conditions of labour of person in whose reference industrial dispute has been raised - Appeal dismissed.
