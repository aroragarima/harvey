State Of Haryana v Basti Ram

Supreme Court of India

02 April 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Madan B. Lokur, A.K. Patnaik

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 193; (2013) 4 SCC 200; AIR 2013 SC 1307; 2013 ALL MR (Cri) 1904; 2013 (2) JCC 981; 2013 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1286; 2013 (2) MLJ(Crl) 866; 2013(5) SCALE 234B; [2013] 4 S.C.R. 850

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 342, 363, 366, 376, 376(2)(g), 506 - Code of Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 164, 313 - Kidnap and rape - Grant of bail - Legality - Prosecutrix was kidnapped and repeatedly raped by respondents/accused - Respondents were charged for offences punishable u/ss. 366, 342, 376(2)(g) and 506 IPC - Trial Court convicted accused for offence punishable u/ss. 366, 342, 376(2)(g) and 506 IPC and sentenced them to undergo 10 years RI - On appeal before HC, Single Judge allowed appeal and set aside conviction of accused - Hence, instant appeal - Whether HC erred in not taking into account statement and testimony of prosecutrix that respondent had raped her on several occasions and thereby acquitting him - Held, HC had not discussed statement of prosecutrix u/s. 164 CrPC before Magistrate nor her testimony before Trial Court - It was rather detailed and least that was expected of HC was to consider that statement - But, to completely ignore what prosecutrix had said, merely on basis of a handful of letters which she had written (even though she had explained circumstances in which she had written those letters) was rather unsatisfactory way of dealing with entire case - Thus, in absence of any discussion or analysis of evidence by HC in appeal, a right of appeal available to accused would be taken away without opinion of HC - Further, it was necessary for SC to have views of HC on record, since HC had reversed a finding of conviction given by Trial Court - Impugned judgment and order passed by HC aws set aside - Thus, matter was remanded back to HC for reconsideration on merits after taking into account entire evidence on record, including statement and testimony of prosecutrix - Appeal disposed of.
