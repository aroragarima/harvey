B.V. Radha Krishna v Sponge Iron India Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3066; (1997) 4 SCC 693; AIR 1997 SC 1324; 1997 (1) ARBLR 412; 1997 (3) AWC 1750; 1997 (2) CLT 108; JT 1997 (3) SC 327; 1997(2) SCALE 469; 1997 (1) SCJ 507; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 707; 1997 (3) Supreme 1; 1997 (1) UJ 661

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Interest Act, 1978, s. 3(1)(b) - (A) Scope of interference by High Court with arbitral award in dispute as to transport contract - (B) Demand notice calling company to settle Bills and claims - Determination of date from which interest is to be awarded by civil Court in light of notice - Held, HC cannot substitute its own view in place of Arbitrators view, it cannot examine matters as a regular Appellate Court - Interest has to be awarded from date mentioned in notice and not from date of notice - Appeal allowed.
