Council of Homeopathic System of Medicine, Punjab and Others v Suchintan and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, M.N. Venkatachaliah, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 807; (1993) Supp3 SCC 99; AIR 1994 SC 1761; JT 1993 (3) SC 727; 1993(2) SCALE 632; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 306; 1993 (2) SLR 567; 1993 (3) UPLBEC 1805

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Health & Drug; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Central Council, Homoeopathy Central Council Act, 1973, Homeopathy (Diploma Course) Dhms Regulations, 1983

Summary: Education - Homoeopathy Central Council Act, 1973, ss. 20(1), 33(i), 33(j) and 33(k) - Homoeopathy (Diploma Course) DHMS Regulations, 1983, Regns. 8, 9, 10 and 11 - Regulation - Interpretation - Examination - Condition, one year gap between passing of first year exam and appearing in second year exam - Held, candidate passing supplementary examination - Cannot take second year exam at next academic year.
