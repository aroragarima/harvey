Â Maharao Sahib Shri Bhim Singhji and Others v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 383; (1981) 1 SCC 166; AIR 1981 SC 234

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution, 1950, arts. 14, 31, 31A, 31B and 39 - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, ss. 2(f), 11(6), 23 and 27(1) - Prohibition Act - Constitutional validity of Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act - Except s. 27 (1), entire Act held valid - Petition Dismissed.
