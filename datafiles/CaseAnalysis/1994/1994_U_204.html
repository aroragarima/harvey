S. C. Girotra v United Commercial Bank (Uco Bank) and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1352; (1995) Supp3 SCC 212; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1140; 1995 (30) ATC 627; 1996 (1) LLJ 10; 1996 (1) LLN 26; 1995 (7) SLR 152

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether dismissal of appellant on basis of investigation report, justified? - Held, no opportunity given to appellant to cross examine either makers of that report or officers who led to order of dismissal passed by disciplinary authority - Refusal of permission to cross-examine witnesses was denial of reasonable opportunity of defence to appellant - Appeal allowed.
