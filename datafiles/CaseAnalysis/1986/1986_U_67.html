Shri Kashinath Dikshita v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 June 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 101; (1986) 3 SCC 229; (1986) SCC (L&S) 502; AIR 1986 SC 2118; 1986 ATC 176; 1986 (53) FLR 184; 1986 LabIC 1939; 1986 (2) LLJ 468; 1986 (2) LLN 375; 1986(1) SCALE 909; 1986 (2) SLJ 278; 1986 (2) SLR 620

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311(2) - Dismissal of services after disciplinary enquiry - But copies of statements of witnesses and of documents relied upon, not supplied - Failed to prove that no prejudice occasioned to employee - Constitutionality of Order - Held, Order was violative of art. 311(2) - Appeal allowed.
