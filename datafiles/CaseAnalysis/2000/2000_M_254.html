Modern Insulators Limited v Oriental Insurance Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 164; (2000) 2 SCC 734; AIR 2000 SC 1014; 2000 (38) ALR 814; 2000 (2) AWC 1282; 2000 (1) CLC 834; [2000] 100 Comp Cas 97; 2000 (1) CPJ 1B; 2000 (1) CPJ(Sc) 1; 2000 (1) CPR 93; JT 2000 (2) SC 289; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 17; 2000(2) SCALE 25; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1076

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Corporate; Trade

Summary: Insurance Act, 1938, Pre-Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 22 - 'All - risk insurance policy' - Disclosure of facts - Not to insured - Insurer cannot claim benefit of exclusion clause - Appeal before National Commission - New plea - Held, acceptance of said new ground and allowing appeal - Not sustainable in law - Disclosure of fact is duty of both parties.
