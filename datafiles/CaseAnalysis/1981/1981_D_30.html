Dr. Mrs. Veena Kapoor v Varinder Kumar Kapoor

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, V. Balakrishna Eradi, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 69; (1981) 3 SCC 92; (1981) SCC (Cr) 650; AIR 1982 SC 792; 1982 CRLJ 580; 1982 CRLR 11B

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950 - Habeas Corpus - Father and mother of one and half years old child not living together - Child in custody of respondent - Mother filed habeas corpus in High Court, asking for custody of child alleging father in illegal custody of child - High Court dismissed petition on ground that father's custody was not illegal - Whether High Court was justified in holding that custody of child with respondent cannot be said to be illegal? - In matters concerning custody of minor children, paramount consideration is welfare of minor and not legal right of this or that particular party - Held, High Court is not justified in dismissing petition on narrow ground that custody of child with respondent cannot be said to be illegal - Order accordingly.
