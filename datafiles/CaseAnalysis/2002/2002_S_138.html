Mahendra Pal v Shri Ram Dass Malanger and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 125; (2002) 3 SCC 457; AIR 2002 SC 1291; AIR 2002 SCW 1100; JT 2002 (2) SC 396; 2002(2) SCALE 389; 2002 (2) SCJ 250; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 169; 2002 (2) SLT 300B; 2002 (2) Supreme 164

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: District Election Officer

Summary: The High Court rightly refused to recounting of votes, as the appellant has not led any evidence or laid foundation stating that there was improper reception of vote in favour of the respondent or improper rejection of any vote which were in his favour, and since he has not raised any objection at the time of counting of votes on the basis of so-called excess of 8 ballot papers.
