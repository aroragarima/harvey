Employers In Relation To Digwadih Colliery v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 120; AIR 1966 SC 75; 1965 (11) FLR 99; 1965 (2) LLJ 118; [1965] 3 S.C.R. 448

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1964, Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946, Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1953

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss.2(00) and 25F - Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1953, ss. 2(eee), 25 B, 25 C and 25 F - Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1964, ss. 25 B and 25 F - Termination of workman - Continuous service of 240 days needed to be considered permanent - Whether definition of 'continuous service' in s. 25 F should govern meaning there of when what is meant by expression 'one year of continuous service' in s.25 F is specifically explained in s. 25 B? - Held, s. 25B says that for purpose of s. 25F a workman who, in a period of twelve calendar months has actually worked for not less than 240 days shall be deemed to have completed one year of continuous service - Service for 240 days in a period of twelve calendar months is equal not only to service for a year but is to be deemed continuous service even if interrupted - S. 25F speaks of continuous service for not less than one year under employer, both conditions are fulfilled if workman has actually worked for 240 days during a period of twelve calendar months - It is not necessary to read definition of continuous service into s.25B because fiction converts service of 240 days in a period of twelve calendar months into continuous service for one complete year - Amended s. 25B only consolidates previous ss. 25B and 2(eee) in one place, adding some other matters which are not relevant - but purport of new provisions is not different - Amendment of s. 25 F of principal Act by substitution in cl. (b) of words 'for every completed year of continuous service' for words 'for every completed year of service' removes a discordance between unamended s. 25 B and unamended cl.(b) of s. 25 B - Neither before these several changes nor after, is uninterrupted service necessary if total service is 240 days in a period of twelve calendar months - Appeal dismissed.
