Ram Pujan and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 431; (1973) 2 SCC 456; (1973) SCC (Cr) 870; AIR 1973 SC 2418; 1973 CRLJ 1612; 1973 CRLR 552

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 326 - Quantum of sentence - Consideration of fact of compromise between accused and injured - Offence is non-compoundable - Held, fact can be taken into account in determining quantum of sentence even if offence is non-compoundable - Order accordingly.
