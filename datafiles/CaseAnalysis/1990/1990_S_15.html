Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32, 226, 136 and 227 - Natural justice - Decision by Administrative authority - Held, reasons must be recorded - Administrative action must be supported by reasons as per Administrative law - Merit in this appeal and same is accordingly dismissed.
