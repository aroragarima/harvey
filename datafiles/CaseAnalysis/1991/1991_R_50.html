Assistant Collector Of Central Excise, Guntur v Ramdev Tobacco Company

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 480; (1991) 2 SCC 119; AIR 1991 SC 506; 1991 CRLR 223; 1991 CrLR(SC) 223; 1991 (33) ECR 1; 1991 (51) E.L.T. 631; JT 1991 (1) SC 199; 1991(1) SCALE 70; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 126; 1991 (1) UJ 323

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Constitution - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 40(2) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Appeal - New plea - Plea based on principle of 'ejusdem generis' not raised before High Court - Whether to be entertained by Supreme Court in appeal? - Expression 'other legal proceeding' - Preceding words 'suit' and 'prosecution' - Whether penalty and adjudication proceedings are covered by expression 'other legal proceeding'? - Held, entertained by Supreme Court, being a pure question of law - Must be read 'ejusdem generis' with preceding words - Not covered by expression - Appeal allowed.
