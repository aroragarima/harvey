Messrs B.P.L. India Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Cochin

Supreme Court of India

07 May 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 317; (2002) 5 SCC 167; AIR 2002 SC 2104; 2003 (1) CalLT(SC) 10; 2002 (81) ECC 449; 2002 (102) ECR 739; 2002 (143) E.L.T. 3; JT 2002 (4) SC 619; 2002 (50) RLT 249; 2002(4) SCALE 462; [2002] 3 S.C.R. 834; 2002 (4) SLT 67; 2002 (4) Supreme 356

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excise & Salt Act, 1944 - Section 2(f) - Held, When a change takes place and a new distinct article comes into existence known to the consumers and the commercial community as a commercial product, which can be no longer regarded as the original commodity, such a change constitutes a process of manufacture for the purpose of levy of excise duty.
