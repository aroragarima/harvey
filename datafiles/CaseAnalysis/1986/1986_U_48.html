Frank Anthony Public School Employees' Association v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 734; (1986) 4 SCC 707; AIR 1987 SC 311; 1987 (2) ATC 35; 1987 (54) FLR 353; JT 1986 SC 861; 1987 LabIC 427; 1987 (1) LLN 53; 1986(2) SCALE 805; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 238; 1987 (1) SLR 133

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Delhi School Education Act, 1973, ss. 8, 10 and 12 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 30(1) - Minority Institution - Salaries and Allowance - Educational Institution - (A) Whether prescription of condition of service violative u/art. 30(1)? - Held, no - Relied on Ahmedabad St. Xaviers College v. State of Gujarat Indlaw SC 268 - Prescription of conditions of service not violative of art. 30(1) as it would not jeopardise right of the management of minority institutions to appoint teachers of their choice - (B) Whether s. 12 violates fundamental rights of minorities u/art. 30(1)? - Held, yes - Makes provisions of Chap. IV inapplicable to unaided minority schools guaranteed by art. 30(1) - Surrender of right u/art. 30(1) is not price which aided minority institutions have to pay to obtain aid from Govt. - (C) Whether suspension against members of staff reasonable? - Held, management of school not to give effect to order of suspension against members of staff - Relied on All Saints High School v. Govt. of A.P, Indlaw SC 328 - Management has right to order suspension of an employee in case of gross misconduct but it is necessary to afford some measure of protection to employees, without interfering with management's right to take disciplinary action - Appeal allowed.
