Mohd. Salimuddin v Misri Lal and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 420; (1986) 2 SCC 378; AIR 1986 SC 1019; 1986 (2) ARBLR 77; 1986 (12) AWC 521; 1986 BLJR 472; 1986 (1) RCJ 472; 1986 (2) RCR(Rent) 149; 1986 (1) RentLR 492; 1986(1) SCALE 362; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 622; 1986 (2) UJ 25

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Bihar Building (Lease Rent and Eviction) Control Act 1977

Summary: Rent Control - Tenancy - Suit for eviction of tenant who had advanced loan to landlord to secure tenancy - Agreement having stipulation as to adjustment of loan towards rent - The amount so advanced by the tenant was sufficient to cover the lanlord's claim of arrears - Held, tenant entitled to adjustment and as such not in arrears of rent - Tenant, not liable to be evicted - Doctrine of Pari-Delicto, not attracted - Appeal dismissed.
