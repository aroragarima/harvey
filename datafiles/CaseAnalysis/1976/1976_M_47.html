State of Orissa v Messrs Dinabandhu Sahu and Sons

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 397; (1976) 4 SCC 431; AIR 1976 SC 1561; 1976 (5) CTR 286; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 966; 1976 (37) STC 583; 1976 TAXLR 1781

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 14(vi) and s. 15 - Orissa Sales Tax Act - Oil seeds - Dhania (coriander seed), Jeera (Cumin seed), Panmohuri, Postak (Poppy seed) and Methi (Fenugreek) are oil seeds within s.14 (vi) of the Act - Determination of tax payable on them under Orissa Sales Tax Act - Held, tax in respect of sale or purchase of these goods inside the State cannot exceed 2 per cent of sale or purchase price thereof - Appeals dismissed.
