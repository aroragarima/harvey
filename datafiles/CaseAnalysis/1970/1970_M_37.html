Mathuralal v Keshar Bai and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, S.M. Sikri, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 347; (1970) 1 SCC 454; AIR 1971 SC 310; 1970 RCR(Rent) 318; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 724

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Final Decree For Sale

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 58 and 60 - CPC, 1908, O. 34, r. 4 - Limitation Act, 1908, s. 28 - Land and Property - Mortgage with possession - Leased back to mortgagor - Preliminary decree on mortgage - But no application for final decree - Mortgagee only loses his right to recover money by sale of property - Otherwise his security remains intact - Remedy , what - Held, mortgagee can sue on rent note - Mortgagor continues to have his right to redeem - Appeal dismissed.
