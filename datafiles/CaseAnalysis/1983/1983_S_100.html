Ashok Service Centre & Another Etc. v State Of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.P. Sen, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 72; (1983) 2 SCC 82; (1983) SCC (Tax) 90; AIR 1983 SC 394; 1983 (55) CLT 509; 1983(1) SCALE 123; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 363; 1983 (53) STC 1; 1983 TAXLR 2861; 1983 UJ 265

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Declared Goods, Statement Of Objects And Reasons, Additional Sales Tax, Orissa Sales Tax Act 1947, Orissa Addittional Sales Tax Act, 1975, Orissa Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979

Summary: Sales Tax - Orissa Additional Sales Tax Act, 1975, s. 3(2) - Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947, s. 8 - Orissa Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979, s. 3(2) - Nature of Tax - Whether the levy of additional tax under the Orissa Additional Sales Tax Act, as amended by 1979 Act is a single point levy or a multi point levy - Acts are not independent - And levy under amending Act is subject to s. 8 of Principal Act - Held, additional tax is not a multipoint levy - Appeal allowed.
