Ajaib Singh v Sirhind Cooperative Marketing-CUM-PROCESSING Service Society Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1315; (1999) 6 SCC 82; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1054; AIR 1999 SC 1351; 1999 (2) AWC 1649; 2001 (2) CGLJ 240; 1999 (82) FLR 137; JT 1999 (3) SC 38; 1999 LabIC 1435; 1999 (1) LLJ 1260; 1999 (2) LLN 674; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 89; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 505; 1999 (4) Supreme 51

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946, Trade Disputes Act, 1929

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 137 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 227 - (A) Determination of applicability of art. 137 of Limitation Act on delay in reference of matter as of back wages - (B) Termination - Writ Petition filed against award of Labour Court - High Court setting aside award on plea of delay which was not raised before Labour Court - Substitutes its opinion even without pleadings - Held, provision do not apply, court can mould relief by refusing back wages or directing payment of part of back wages - Not justified - Appeal allowed.
