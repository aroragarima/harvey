Â Purshottamdas Thakurdas v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Raghubar Dayal, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 237; AIR 1963 SC 1066; [1963] 33 Comp Cas 351; [1963] 48 ITR 206; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 668; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 668

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Advance Payment

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 16(2), 18 A(1), 18 A(6), 18(5) and 49B - Income Tax - 'Dividend income' - Exclusion of it from income - Penal interest u/s. 18A(6) - Whether assesse is liable to penalty u/s. 18A for exclusion of such income - Held, provision of s. 18A not attracted, so not liable to pay, penal interest u/s. 18A(6) - Appeal allowed.
