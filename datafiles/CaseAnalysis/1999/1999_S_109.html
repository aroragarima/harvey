Electronics Corporation of India Limited and Others v Secretary, Revenue Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, B.N. Kirpal, S. Rajendra Babu, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1147; (1999) 4 SCC 458; AIR 1999 SC 1734; [1999] 97 Comp Cas 470; 1999 (3) CompLJ 353; JT 1999 (5) SC 608; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 1078

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Keywords: Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970, Andhra Pradesh Non Agricultural Lands Assessment Tax Act, 1963

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Contract, Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 - Companies Act, 1956 - Andhra Pradesh Non Agricultural Lands Assessment Tax Act, 1963 - In 1963, State of Andhra Pradesh granted a large area of land to Department of Atomic Energy - In 1964, Department leased out land to appellant Company - On 01-10-1978, first respondent issued to appellant notices of demand for non-agricultural assessment on said land under Act - Whether legislature of State of Andhra Pradesh barred by provisions of art. 285 from imposing any tax, including non-agricultural assessment, on property of UOI? - Held, appellant Company is a separate and distinct legal entity that runs its own industry - By virtue of amended definition of 'owner' u/s. 2(j) of Act, appellant Company is owner of said land and by virtue of s. 3, is liable to pay non-agricultural assessment thereon - Art. 285 does not apply when property that is to be taxed is not of Union of India but of a distinct and separate legal entity - Appeal partly allowed.
