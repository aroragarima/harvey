Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat Iii v Ahmedabad Rana Caste Association

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 338; (1982) 2 SCC 542; AIR 1982 SC 32; [1983] 140 ITR 1; 1982 TAXLR 2

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution, art. 133 - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 11 and 256 - Exemption of income of association from tax - High Court in a reference after considering entire matter at length in the light of prevailing case law - HC hold that income derived by association was applied entirely to charitable and religious purposes - Held, there were no reasons for interference by the Supreme Court - Appeals dismissed.
