Smt. Yallawwa v Smt. Shantavva

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1244; (1997) 11 SCC 159; AIR 1997 SC 35; JT 1996 (9) SC 218; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 4; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 268

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Keywords: Rights Of Inheritance, Succession & Inheritance, Minority, Adoption & Guardianship, Debt Laws

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 ; Kerala General Sales Tax Act 1963, s.59A ; Constitution of India, art.14 - Power of Govt. to determine rate of tax was in dispute - s.59-A conferring absolute powers on State Govt., not amenable to appellate or revisional provisions provided by the Act
