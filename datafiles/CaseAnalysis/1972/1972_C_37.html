Ram Pershad v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 183; (1972) 2 SCC 696; AIR 1973 SC 637; [1972] 42 Comp Cas 544; 1972 CTR 327; 1974 (2) ILR(Del) 23; [1972] 86 ITR 122; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 985

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 7 - Income Tax - Salary - Test for determination of whether assessee (appointed as Managing Director) is servant or an agent of the Company - - Whether remuneration payable to the assessee is salary - Renumeration payable held to be salary - Appeal Dismissed.
