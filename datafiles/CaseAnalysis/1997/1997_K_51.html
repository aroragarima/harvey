Kali Charan and Others v H. S. E. Board and Other

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2387; (1997) 10 SCC 230; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1282

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Petitions relate to appointment on posts of a Asst. Linemen and Shift Attendants in Haryana State Electricity Board - Petitioners selected and appointed on these posts - Appointments were challenged - Single Judge set aside entire selection and appointments made on basis of that selection and directed that a fresh selection be made after making fresh advertisements - Present appeal - Held, in pursuance of impugned judgment Board has now formulated policy for making selection for said posts - Selection process is going on and that written examination has been held and that petitioners have participated in fresh selection - Keeping in view facts and circumstances referred to above, not inclined to interfere with impugned judgment - However, Board shall keep in mind limit for reservation laid down in case of Indra Sawhney v. Union of India, 1991 Indlaw SC 735 - Petition disposed of.
