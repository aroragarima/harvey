Ramesh v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 571; (1989) 4 SCC 124; (1989) SCC (Cr) 716; AIR 1989 SC 1881; 1989 (3) Crimes 65; 1989 CRLJ 2094; 1989 CRLR 639; 1989 (25) ECR 479; JT 1989 (3) SC 279; 1989(2) SCALE 168

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Gujarat Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act, 1985, s. 3(2) - Grounds of detention - Validity - Detaining authority considering criminal cases in which detenu was acquitted - Held, that detention order be quashed as grounds were not valid - Appeal allowed.
