Sajjadanashin Sayed Md. B. E. Edr. (D) By LRs v Musa Dadabhai Ummer and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 168; (2000) 3 SCC 350; AIR 2000 SC 1238; 2000 (2) CLT 1; JT 2000 (2) SC 352; 2000 (2) MLJ(SC) 172; 2000(2) SCALE 52; 2000 (2) SCJ 113; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1095; 2000 (3) SLT 21; 2000 (2) Supreme 121

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Res judicata - Waqf property - Held as 'private' in earlier decision - Later held as 'public' after commencement of Bombay Public Trusts Act - Applicability of - 'directly and substantially' and 'collaterally or incidentally' - Test to determine - Held, is whether court considers adjudication of issue material and essential for its decision - As decision was based on law as it then existed - Decision in earlier case will not operate as res judicata.
