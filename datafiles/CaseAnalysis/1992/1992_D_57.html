Governing Council Of Kidwai Memorial Institute Of Oncology, v Dr Pandurang Godwalkar And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 966; (1992) 4 SCC 719; (1993) SCC (L&S) 1; AIR 1993 SC 392; 1993 (23) ATC 389; 1993 (66) FLR 660; 1992 LabIC 2439; 1993 (1) LLJ 308; 1992 (2) LLN 1026; 1992(2) SCALE 861; 1993 (2) SLJ 174; 1992 (5) SLR 661; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 250

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. II, Item 3 - Principle of tearing of veil - Probationer - Termination simpliciter - After some preliminary enquiry - Overall performance and some actions or inactions considered - Held, doesn't amount to removal from service as punishment - Principle of tearing of veil applicable, where direct nexus exists between charges levelled and action taken.
