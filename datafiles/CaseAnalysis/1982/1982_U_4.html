P.P. Entterprises Etc. Etc. v Union Of India & Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 199; (1982) 2 SCC 33; (1982) SCC (Cr) 341; AIR 1982 SC 1016; 1982 (8) ALR 236; 1982 CRLR 590; 1982(1) SCALE 184; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 510; 1982 UJ 236

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Quantity, Sugar Control order 1966

Summary: Constitution, arts. 19(1)(g) and 19(6) - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, ss. 3, 3(1) and 3(2)(d) - Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966, cl. 5 - Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966, cl. 5 - Constitutionality of Central Government Order No. GSR-410-E/Ess. Comm./Sugar D/- 14-07-1960 - Considering ancillary issues of - (A) Vires as to Essential Commodity Act - (B) Restriction under the Order - Reasonable restriction, if - (C) Fixation of limits for storing sugar in Calcutta and other places - Whether artbitrary - (D) If violative of art. 14 - Order is not ultra vires s. 3 - Restrictions put by the Order is not unreasonable - Not arbitrary but is based on reasonable classification - Order not violative of art. 14 - Petition Dismissed.
