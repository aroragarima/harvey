Rolston John v Central Government Industrial Tribunal-Cum-Labour Court and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 870; (1995) SCC (L&S) 142; (1995) Supp4 SCC 549; AIR 1994 SC 131; AIR 1993 SCW 4073; 1994 LabIC 973

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo) and 25 F - Retrenchment - Ground for termination of service - Overstaying of leave period by employee without proper explanation - Amounts of retrenchment - Failure to follow procedure u/s. 25 F of Act - Compensation to be granted - Held, compensation of Rs. 50,000/- Without reinstatement granted - Appeal allowed.
