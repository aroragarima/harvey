Devi Cine Projector Manufacturingco., Etc. Etc. v Commissioner Of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, N.D. Ojha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 890; (1990) 2 SCC 551; AIR 1991 SC 1892; AIR 1991 SCW 2122; 1990 (82) CTR 142; [1990] 183 ITR 19; JT 1990 (1) SC 250; 1990(1) SCALE 217; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 268; 1990 (77) STC 419; [1990] 49 TAXMAN 85; 1991 (2) TLR 230; 1990 (1) UJ 339

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 40(b) - Interest - Deduction of interest paid by Firm to its partner - Held, case remitted to Tribunal for disposal in light of pronouncement in Keshavji Ravji's case - Matter remitted back to tribunal.
