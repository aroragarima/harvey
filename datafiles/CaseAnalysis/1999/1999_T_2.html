Board of Trustees For The Visakhapatnam Port Trust v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 609; (1999) 6 SCC 78; AIR 1999 SC 2552; 1999 (5) ALD(SC) 33; JT 1999 (5) SC 151

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Water

Summary: Municipal & Local Governments - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 285 - Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation Act, 1979, s. 8 - Major Port Trust Act, 1963, s. 110 - Whether Board of Trustees of port is exempted from property tax u/art. 285 of Constitution on grounds of being property of Union of India? - Held, not exempted - Appeal allowed.
