State Of Haryana v Hari Ram Yadav

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1262; (1994) 2 SCC 617; (1994) SCC (L&S) 711; AIR 1994 SC 1262; 1994 (23) ALR 357; 1994 (27) ATC 162; 1994 (1) CLR 298; 1994 (68) FLR 493; JT 1994 (1) SC 126; 1994 (1) LLJ 791; 1994 (1) LLN 151; 1994(1) SCALE 110; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 168; 1995 (1) SLJ 78; 1994 (2) SLR 63; 1994 (1) UJ 581; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 891

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - All India Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1969, r. 3(1)- Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114 - Suspension order - Passing by Govt. - Held, not invalid - Merely because not containing of recital to effect that Governor of State was satisfied - Presumption about fulfillment of condition.
