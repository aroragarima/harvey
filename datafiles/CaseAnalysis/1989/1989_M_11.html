Ambati Narasayya v M. Subba Rao And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 166; (1989) Supp2 SCC 693; AIR 1990 SC 119; 1989 (2) AWC 1461; 1990 (1) BankCLR 459; 1989 (3) CCC 409; 1990 (82) CTR 31; 1989 (59) FLR 705; JT 1989 (4) SC 50; 1989(2) SCALE 806; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 451; 1990 (1) ShimLC 287; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 451

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, r. 64 - Auction sale - Decree - Mandate - Duty u/r. 64 of O. 21 of Code - Only such portion of property as would satisfy decree - Obligation on Court and not just discretion - Tendency to blindfold sell entire property deprecated - Appeal allowed.
