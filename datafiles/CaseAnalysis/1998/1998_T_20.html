Daily Partap v Regional Provident Fund Commissioner, Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1279; (1998) 8 SCC 90; AIR 1999 SC 2015; JT 1998 (7) SC 353; 1999 (1) LLJ 1; 1999 (2) SLJ 1; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 482

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Merit

Summary: Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s. 2(b), exception (ii) - Applicability - Production bonus scheme - Ingredients necessary for getting it covered under exception - Extra payment under scheme made at flat rate and not on sliding scale proportionate to extra output - Held, exemption from payment of provident fund contribution not available - Exception (ii) not attracted.
