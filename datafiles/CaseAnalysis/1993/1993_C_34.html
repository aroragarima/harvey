State of Rajasthan v Chandra Narain Verma

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1005; (1994) SCC (L&S) 734; (1994) 2 SCC 752; 1994 (27) ATC 194; 1994 (2) LLN 58; 1993 (8) SLR 428

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - Service - Compassionate appointment - On father's death, respondent sought appointment in Police Service as Sub-Inspector on compassionate ground - As he had crossed prescribed age limit for Sub-Inspector, authorities offered him post of Lower Division Clerk - Whether respondent may be appointed as Sub-Inspector ignoring prescribed age limit ? - Held, since he had crossed maximum age prescribed, authorities were fully justified in offering him post of Lower Division Clerk, which he should have accepted - Appeal disposed of.
