Goa State Cooperative Bank v Pedne Taluka Prathamik Shikshak Pat Sanstha and Others

01 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1593; (1997) 1 SCC 356; AIR 1997 SC 1801; 1996(8) SCALE 640; 1997 (1) Supreme 28

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Multi-State-Co-Operative Societies Act, 1984, s. 35 - Multi-State-Co-operative Societies(Registration, Membership, Direction and Amendment, Settlement of Disputes, Appeal and Revision) Rules, 1985, rr. 104, sch II - Dispute as to mode of conducting elections of Society - Controversy arising due to amendment of bye-laws - Direction issued by Registrar to conduct elections in accordance with relevant rules and bye-laws applicable as on date of election - Petition disposed of.
