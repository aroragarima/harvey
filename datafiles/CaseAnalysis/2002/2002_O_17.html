Union of India and others v Onkar S. Kanwar and others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1124; (2002) 7 SCC 591; AIR 2002 SC 3563; [2003] 113 Comp Cas 264; 2002 (177) CTR 281; 2002 (83) ECC 731; 2003 (106) ECR 275; 2002 (145) E.L.T. 266; [2002] 258 ITR 761; JT 2002 (7) SC 439; 2003 (1) KHCACJ 86; 2002 (52) RLT 871; 2002(7) SCALE 88; 2002 (5) SLT 481; [2002] Supp2 S.C.R. 675; 2002 (7) Supreme 1; 174 TAXATION 213; [2002] 125 TAXMAN 121

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Kar Vivad Samadhan Scheme, Enactment, Kar Vivad Samadhan Scheme (Removal of Difficulties) Order, Central Excise, 1944

Summary: Central Excise - Kar Vivad Samadhan Scheme (Removal of Difficulties) Order - Held, where a declaration had been made in respect of a tax arrear and where in respect of the same matter a show cause notice had also been issued to any other person, then the settlement in favour of the declarant has to be deemed to be full and final in respect of other persons on whom show cause notices had been issued. -
