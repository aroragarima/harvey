M. Duraiswamy v Murugan Bus Service And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 268; (1986) Supp SCC 1; AIR 1986 SC 1980; JT 1986 SC 518; 1986(1) SCALE 515; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 68; 1986 (2) UJ 220

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 45 and 57 - Motor vehicle - Variation of route of stage carriage - Application for inter regional route, whom to be made - Held, must be made to RTA which originally granted permit - Appeal allowed.
