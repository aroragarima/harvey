Bsi Limited and Another v Gift Holdings Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2687; (2000) SCC (Cr) 538; (2000) 2 SCC 737; AIR 2000 SC 926; 2000 (1) BC 292; 2006 (2) BC 359; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 397; 2000 (1) CCR 226; 2000 (2) CompLJ 13; 2000 (1) Crimes 261; 2000 CRLJ 1424; 2000 (1) JCC 113; JT 2000 (2) SC 127; 2000 (1) RCR(Criminal) 596; 2000(1) SCALE 540; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 815; 2000 (2) SLT 184; 2000 (2) Supreme 41

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, s. 22(1) - Suit - Interpretation of - Dishonour of cheque - Prosecution of Company - Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act not objecting against maintainability and prosecution of complaint - Held, word 'suit' under s. 22 of Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act not includes criminal prosecution.
