K Ajit Babu And Others v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1899; (1997) 6 SCC 473; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1520; AIR 1997 SC 3277; 1998 (1) AWC 155; 1997 (77) FLR 382; JT 1997 (7) SC 24; 1997 (2) LLJ 749; 1997(5) SCALE 82; 1998 (1) SLJ 85; 1997 (4) SLR 775; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 56; 1997 (7) Supreme 76

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, ss.19 and 22 - Doctrine of Precedent - Applicability - Decision of CAT - Challenging earlier decision of Tribunal - By person not party to case but adversely affected by earlier judgment of Tribunal - Held is maintainable - Tribunal necessarily has to take into account the judgment rendered in earlier case as a precedent.
