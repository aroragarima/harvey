Berhampur University & Anr. v Dr. Sailabala Padhi

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 887; (1997) 5 SCC 53; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1113; AIR 1997 SC 2257; JT 1997 (5) SC 171; 1997 LabIC 2114; 1997(4) SCALE 9; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 880; 1998 (1) SLJ 16; 1997 (4) SLR 314; 1997 (5) Supreme 84

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Orissa Universities Act, 1989

Summary: Orissa Universities Act 1989, s.21 - Appointment for the post of professor in Environmental Science - Candidate possessing Master's Degree in Botany with specialisation in Algology but did not have a Master's degree in Environmental Biology - Opinion of Expert body that candidate not fit to be selected - Order of Chancellor directing re-advertisement as per opinion of Expert Committee - However, High Court passed different judgement directing appointment of candidate - Held, High Court was not justified and therefore order of chancellor was made final.
