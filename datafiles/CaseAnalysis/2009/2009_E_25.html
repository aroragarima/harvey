Er. Gurcharan Singh Grewal & Anr v Punjab State Electricity Board & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 11; (2009) 3 SCC 94; (2009) 1 SCC (L&S) 578; 2009 (2) AWC 1611; JT 2009 (1) SC 335; 2009 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 343; 2009 (2) LLJ 543; 2009(1) SCALE 535; 2009 (2) SLR 1; 2009 (1) WLN(SC) 115

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Petition to quash the order withdrawing the benefits of pay fixation already granted to petitioners disposed of as infructuous - Review petition dismissed holding that relief claimed in the review petition is not claimed in the main petition for petitioner No.1 and review petition is not maintainable - Appeal against - Held, reasoning of the High Court was erroneous and the appellant No.1 was also entitled to the same benefits of pay parity with Shri Shori as has been granted to the appellant No.2 - Appeals allowed.
