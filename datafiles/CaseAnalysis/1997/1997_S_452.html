State of Haryana and Others v Ram Atri and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1881; (1998) SCC (L&S) 191; (1998) 1 SCC 195; JT 1998 (4) SC 362

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Conflict between two judge judgment in 1996 Indlaw SC 2947 decided on 8-7-1996 and two judge judgment in 1996 Indlaw SC 2909 decided on 25-5-1996 - In later judgment, earlier judgment (1996 Indlaw SC 2947) has not been noticed - Matter directed to be placed before a three-Judge Bench.
