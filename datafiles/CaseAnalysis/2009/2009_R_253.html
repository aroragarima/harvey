Ravindra Kumar Madhanlal Goenka & Anr v M/S. Rugmini Ram Raghav Spinners P.Ltd

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Mukundakam Sharma, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 478; (2009) 11 SCC 529; AIR 2009 SC 2383; 2009 CRLJ 2852; 2009 (3) JCC 2258; JT 2009 (13) SC 203; 2009 (3) RCR(Criminal) 338; 2009(6) SCALE 162; [2009] 6 S.C.R. 27; 2009 (2) ShimLC 120

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Criminal Remedy

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 406, 420 and 384 - Cr.P.C. 1973, s. 482 - Petition for quashing of criminal proceedings dismissed - Appeal against - Held, while entertaining a petition under s. 482 CrPC, materials furnished by the defence cannot be looked into and the defence materials can be entertained only at the time of trial - When there are prima facie materials available, a petition for quashing the criminal proceedings cannot be entertained - Not one of those extreme cases where criminal prosecution can be quashed by the court at the very threshold - Appeal dismissed.
