Bihar State Electricity Board v Suresh Prasad & Ors

Supreme Court of India

25 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 172; (2004) SCC (L&S) 438; (2004) 2 SCC 681; AIR 2004 SC 1724; 2004 (3) ALD(SC) 148; 2004 (5) AWC 4576; 2004 (2) CLR 263; 2004 (100) FLR 1082; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 209; JT 2004 (3) SC 143; 2004 (2) LLN 48; 2004(2) SCALE 698; 2004 (2) SLR 756; 2004 (3) Supreme 685; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1885

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Whether HC was justified in giving direction to appellant to fill up vacancies which remained unfilled due to candidates not turning up to join post - Shankarsan Dash v Union of India (1991 Indlaw SC 188) - Followed and applied - Impunged orders set aside - Appeals allowed.
