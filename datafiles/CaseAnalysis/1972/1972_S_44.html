Ishwar Chandra v Satyanarain Sinha and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 123; (1972) 3 SCC 383; AIR 1972 SC 1812; 1972 LabIC 909; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 796; 1972 SLR 385

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Saugar University Act, 1946, s. 13(2) - Committee constituted for submitting panel of names of appointment of Vice-Chancellor - Validity of proceedings of committee - For some reason, one of its three members cannot attend its meeting - Absence of any rule for fixing quorum - Effect - Held, proceedings are not illegal - Appeal allowed.
