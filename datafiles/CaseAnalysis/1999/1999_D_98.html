Dr. Ram Sevak Singh v Dr. U. P. Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 925; (1999) 2 SCC 189; (1999) SCC (L&S) 538; AIR 1999 SC 737; 1999 (2) AWC 1039; 1999 (1) CLR 637; 1999 (1) ESC 569; 1999 (81) FLR 651; JT 1999 (1) SC 106; 1999 (3) LLJ 1485; 1999 (4) LLN 352; 1999(1) SCALE 101; 1999 (2) SCJ 114; 2000 (1) SLJ 117; 1999 (1) SLR 705; 1999 (1) SLT 303; 1999 (1) Supreme 199; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 451

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973, s. 31 - Appointment of Principal - Eligibility of - Qualifications required is, Master's Degree or an equivalent degree in one of subjects taught in college or in a subject allied or inter-connected therewith - College imparting education in teaching - Candidate possessing M. Ed. degree - Held, satisfying requirement.
