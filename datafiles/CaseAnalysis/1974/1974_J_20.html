Kuju Collieries Ltd. v Jharkhand Mines Ltd. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 259; (1974) 2 SCC 533; AIR 1974 SC 1892; 1974 (29) FLR 379; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 703

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, ss. 65, 70 and 72 - Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1948, s. 4 - Scope and applicability of s. 65 of 1872 Act to agreement void ab inito - Mining lease contrary to mineral regulations and void - Parties in pari delicto - Held, s.65 does not apply and nor does ss. 70 or 72 - Appeal dismissed.
