Alladi Venkateswarlu and others v Government of Andhra Pradesh and another

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 49; (1978) SCC (Tax) 11; (1978) 2 SCC 552; AIR 1978 SC 945; 1978 (7) CTR 44; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 190; 1978 (41) STC 394; 1978 TAXLR 2049; 1978 UJ 212

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - AP General Sales Tax Act, 1957, ss. 5, ch. I, entry 66(b) - Parched rice and puffed rice which are 'rice' as per entry 66 (b) - Once taxed as paddy - Subsequent taxability u/s. 5 (1) of Act - Held, if once taxed as paddy cannot be further taxed u/s. 5(1) - Appeals allowed.
