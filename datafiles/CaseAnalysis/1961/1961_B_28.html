Â Bhau Ram v B. Baijnath Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, K. Subba Rao, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 385; AIR 1961 SC 1327; 1961 (2) MLJ(SC) 165; 1961 (2) SCJ 601; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 358

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 o. 20 r. 14 - Rewa State Pre-emption Act, 1949 - Appellant withdrew the price of pre-emption - Whether the appellant by withdrawing the pre-emption price can be said to have adopted the decree from which he had already preferred appeal? - Pre-emption price was in the nature of compensation for loss of property and not as benefit under decree - Held, that the appellant by withdrawing pre-emption price had not taken benefit de hors of merits so he is not precluded from continuing appeal - Appeal dismissed.
