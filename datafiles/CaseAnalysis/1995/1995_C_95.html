Rajasthan Spinning and Weaving Mills Limited, Bhilwara, Rajasthan v Collector of Central Excise, Jaipur, Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1214; (1995) 4 SCC 473; AIR 1995 SC 1985; 1995 (58) ECR 569; 1995 (77) E.L.T. 474; JT 1997 (10) SC 419; 1995 (8) RLT 591; 1995(3) SCALE 381; 1996 (102) STC 476; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 112

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, Tariff item No 18 E - Availability of exemption to assessee's blend spun yarn contains 52% polypropylene and 48% Viscose - When notification exempted polypropylene spun yarn u/item 18 E, but - Held, not entitled to exemption as it was blended yarn in which polypropylene predominated though it comes under item 18 E - Appeal dismissed.
