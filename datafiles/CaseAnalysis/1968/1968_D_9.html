Bank of Bihar Limited v Damodar Prasad and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Bachawat, S.M. Sikri, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 80; AIR 1969 SC 297; 1969 ALJ 475; 1969 BLJR 437; [1969] 39 Comp Cas 133; [1969] 1 S.C.R. 620

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure,1908, ss. 151, O. 20, r.11(1) - Banking Company - Nature of surety's liability not deferred until remedies against principal debtor are exhausted - Decree against debtor and surety directing creditor to first exhaust remedies against principal debtor - Whether direction can be justified? - Direction held not justified in light of s. 151 or O 20, r.11(1) of CPC,1908 - Appeal allowed.
