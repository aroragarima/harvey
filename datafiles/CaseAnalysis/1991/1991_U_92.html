Â Indra Sawhney v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Ratnavel Pandian, T.K. Thommen, A.M. Ahmadi, Kuldip Singh, R.M. Sahai, P.B. Sawant, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 280; (1992) Supp3 SCC 215; (1992) SCC (L&S) 482; 1992 (22) ATC 384

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Challenge of notification - Held, Additional Solicitor General states that Govt. definitely expects to be able to fix the economic criteria by 28-1-1992 - All amendment applications filed challenging Notification dt.25-9-1991 are allowed - Regarding stay, no reason to pass any order at this stage as petitions are posted for hearing on 28-1-1992 and in view of economic criterion not being yet determined and other relevant circumstances, no question of immediate implementation of notification arises - Applications disposed of.
