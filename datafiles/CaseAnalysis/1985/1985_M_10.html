Commissioner Of Income Tax, Kanpur Etc. v M/S. Mother India

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Ranganath Misra, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 326; (1985) 4 SCC 1; (1985) SCC (Tax) 493; AIR 1985 SC 1720; 1985 (3) CompLJ 379; 1985 (48) CTR 176; [1985] 155 ITR 711; 1985(2) SCALE 236; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 556; [1985] 23 TAXMAN 8; 1986 (1) UJ 588

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Net Profits

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 10(2)(vi) proviso (b), 24(2) proviso (b) - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 32(2) and 72(2) - Determination of priority between current depreciation and unabsorbed carried forward business loss, that is to say, which should be deducted first while computing the total income of an assessee for the concerned assessment year - Held, depreciation for current year must be deducted first - Appeal allowed.
