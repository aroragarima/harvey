H.C.L. Limited v Collector of Customs, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 436; (2001) 9 SCC 83; 2001 (76) ECC 11; 2001 (130) E.L.T. 405; 2001 (44) RLT 701A; 2001 (4) Supreme 536

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Exemptions - Held, where there are two exemption notifications that cover the goods in question, the assessee is entitled to the benefit of that exemption notification which gives him greater relief - Followed, CCE v. Indian Petro Chemicals [1996 Indlaw SC 78] - Appeals allowed.
