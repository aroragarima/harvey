Messrs Uma Shankar Kamal Narain and Another v Messrss. M.D. Overseas Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 207; (2007) 4 SCC 133; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 521; 2007 (3) ALD(SC) 92; 2007 (67) ALR 309; 2007 (2) AWC 1899; 2007 (2) CHN 156; 2007 (4) MahLJ 615; 2007 (3) MLJ(SC) 465; 2007 (3) MPLJ 293; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 585; 2007(4) SCALE 358; [2007] 3 S.C.R. 1034

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 37 r. 1 - Suit for recovery of an amount Rs.39, 30, 856/- along with interest at the rate of 12% p.a - Order passed by a Division Bench of the Delhi High Court granting conditional leave to the appellants to defend in the summary suit - Appellants were directed to deposit the amount of Rs.39, 30, 856/- to the registry - Appeal against - Held, directed the appellants to deposit a sum of Rs.20, 00, 000/- - Appeal disposed of.
