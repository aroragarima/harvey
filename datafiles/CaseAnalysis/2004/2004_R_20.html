State Bank Of India & Ors. v R.B. Sharma

Supreme Court of India

10 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 595; (2004) 7 SCC 27; (2004) SCC (L&S) 913; AIR 2004 SC 4144; 2004 (3) AWC 2699; 2004 (3) CLR 296; 2004 (113) DLT 25; 2004 (4) ESC 454; 2004 (102) FLR 1027; JT 2004 (6) SC 361; 2004 (3) LLJ 528; 2005 (1) MLJ(SC) 36; 2004(6) SCALE 541; 2004 (5) SLR 429; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 417; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2576

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Suspension - Gross irregularities - Both departmental proceedings and criminal case pending - HC directed stay of departmental proceedings - Whether departmental proceedings should be kept in abeyance during pendency of a criminal case? - Held, for stay it must show substantially that entire matter in departmental proceedings and before criminal court is same - Single Judge not indicated even skeleton basis for his conclusion that matter is substantially same - Matter remitted back to HC - Appeal allowed.
