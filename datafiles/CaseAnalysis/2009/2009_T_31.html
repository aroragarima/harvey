T. S. Ramchandra Shetty v Chairman, Karnataka Housing Board and another

Supreme Court of India

22 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Dalveer Bhandari, H.S. Bedi

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2253; (2009) 14 SCC 334; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1870; 2010 (2) AWC 2006; JT 2009 (4) SC 363; 2009 (4) KarLJ 85; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 360; 2009(3) SCALE 333

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Market

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894-Sections 23 and 24-Compensation -Determination of market value of land acquired-Best evidence of market value of land-Is afforded by transaction of sale in respect of very acquired land-Acquired land purchased in 1986 for Rs. 45,000-Land acquired in 1987-High Court fixed compensation at Rs. 1,30,680 -Same cannot be said to be inadequate by any stretch of imagination-View taken by High Court-Based on settled position of law-Appeals devoid of merits.
