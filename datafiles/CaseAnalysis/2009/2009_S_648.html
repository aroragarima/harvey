Supreme Court of India

03 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

G.S. Singhvi, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2186; (2010) 2 SCC 114; AIR 2010 SC (Supp) 116; 2010 (1) AWC 762; JT 2009 (15) SC 201; 2010 (2) MLJ(SC) 483; 2010 (109) RD 118; 2009(14) SCALE 473; [2009] 16 S.C.R. 111

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: U.P. Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960

Summary: U. P. Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960-Sections 10 (1) and 10 (2)-Surplus land declaration-Efforts to mislead authority and Courts - Have transmitted through three generations -Conduct of appellant and his son to mislead High Court and Supreme Court-Cannot but be termed as reprehensible-They belong to category of persons-Who not only attempt but succeed in polluting course of justice-No justification to interfere with order under challenge
