Collector Of Central Excise, Baroda v Kosan Metal Products Limited

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 254; (1989) Supp1 SCC 135; (1989) SCC (Tax) 153; AIR 1989 SC 265; 1989 (19) ECC 76; 1989 (20) ECR 30; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 573; JT 1988 (4) SC 526; 1988(2) SCALE 1442; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 537; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 537; 1989 (1) UJ 225

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 11A, item, 68 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 8 and 10 - Brass rods - Assessee receiving them, for manufacture of valves - Availing set off of duty under Notification No 178/77 D/- 18-06-1977 - Notice to assessee of availing incorrect duty - Demand for recovery of amount - No fraud or mis-statement on part of assessee - Service of notice beyond six months - Legality - Held, is illegal in view of s. 11A - Appeal dismissed.
