Punjab National Bank and Others v Kunj Behari Misra and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.C. Agrawal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2019; (1998) 7 SCC 84; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1783; AIR 1998 SC 2713; JT 1998 (5) SC 548; 1998 (2) LLJ 809; 1999 (1) SLJ 271; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 22; 1998 (3) UPLBEC 2320

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11 A, chap. 2, item 3 - Banking Companies Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1970, s. 19 - Punjab National Bank Officer Employees (Discipline and Appeal) Regulations, 1977, regn. 7(2) - Natural justice rule in context of disciplinary enquiry - Inquiry Officer's report in favour of delinquent but disciplinary authority proposing to differ - Availability of opportunity of hearing to delinquent before recording its conclusions - Held, must give opportunity, natural justice rule has to be read in regn. 7(2) - Appeal dismissed.
