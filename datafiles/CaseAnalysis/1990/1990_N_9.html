Anil Kumar v Nanak Chandra Verma

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 673; (1990) 3 SCC 603; AIR 1990 SC 1215; 1992 (1) RCJ 1; 1991 (1) RCR(Rent) 61

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Land & Property

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Land & Property - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114, illus.(f) - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 27 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 106 - Notice served on tenant u/s. 106 of Transfer of Property Act - Not personally served - Endorsement of Postman that it was refused - Presumption of service of notice - Bare statement of tenant on oath denying tender and refusal to accept delivery, if sufficient to rebut presumption - Held, not sufficient to rebut presumption - Appeal dismissed.
