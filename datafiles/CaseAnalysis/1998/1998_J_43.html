Dr. Duryodhan Sahu and Others v Jitendra Kumar Mishra and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 235; (1998) 7 SCC 273; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1802; AIR 1999 SC 114; 1998 (3) CLT 179; 1998 (80) FLR 303; JT 1998 (5) SC 645; 1998 (2) KLT 42; 1998 (2) LLJ 1013; 1998 (4) LLN 967; 1998(4) SCALE 643; 1999 (1) SLJ 205; 1998 (7) SLT 108; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 49; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 77; 1998 (6) Supreme 524

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 9 - Jurisdiction of tribunal - Names of two candidate recommended by Public Service Commission - Prior approval by Director of Medical Education as to their eligibility - One appointed - Creation of one more post by Govt. on later finding that department under staffed - Recommendation of name of other candidate - Order by tribunal restraining appointment of candidates - Held, not legal - Public interest litigation - Cannot be entertained by administrative tribunal at instance of total stranger.
