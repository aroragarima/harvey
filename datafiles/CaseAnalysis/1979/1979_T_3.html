Commissioner of Income Tax, A. P v T. N. Aravinda Reddy

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 130; (1979) 4 SCC 721; AIR 1980 SC 96; 1979 (12) CTR 423; [1979] 120 ITR 46; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 872; [1979] 2 TAXMAN 541; 1980 UJ 83

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 54(1) - Purchase of common property by co-parcener - Release deeds - Assessee and his brothers, members of co-parcenary, partitioned their family properties except common house - Later, assessee acquired said common house from his brothers at Rs. 30,000 each who executed release deeds in his favour - Whether release deeds by sharers in favour of one of them whereby joint ownership of all became separate ownership of one amount to purchase of house property within the meaning of s. 54(1) of the Act.

Held, each release, in instant circumstances, is a transfer of the releaser's share for consideration to the release and it is purchase within the meaning of s. 54(1) of the Act. Petition dismissed.
