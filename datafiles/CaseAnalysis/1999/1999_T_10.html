N. S. Giri v Corporation of City of Mangalore and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, D.P. Mohapatra, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 555; (1999) 4 SCC 697; (1999) SCC (L&S) 906; AIR 1999 SC 1958; AIR 1999 SCW 4749; 1999 (3) ESC 2080; JT 1999 (6) SC 538; 1999 LabIC 1982; 1999 (2) LLJ 690; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 771; 1999 (3) SLJ 300; 1999 (5) Supreme 418

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Age Of Superannuation, Madras District Municipalities Act, 1920, Karnataka Municipal Corporation Act, 1976

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 A - Whether award of Industrial Tribunal, that is inconsistent with statutory provisions governing service conditions, can be enforced? - Held, award cannot be enforced - Appeal dismissed.
