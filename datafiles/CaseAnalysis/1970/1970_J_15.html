Â Janapada Sabha, Chhindwara Etc v The Central Provinces Syndicate Ltd. And Anr.Etc

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 345; (1970) 1 SCC 509; AIR 1971 SC 57; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 745

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: M.P. Koyala Upkar (Manyatakaran) Adhiniyam, 1964, s. 3 - Central Provinces Local Self Government Act, 1920, s. 51(2) - (A) Whether the Act validate levy of cess exceeding 3 pies per ton - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Language used in statute not carrying out its object - Power of Court - Act held, does not validate - Court cannot supply deficiencies - Appeal dismissed.
