Kamalam (M) v Dr. V. A. Syed Mohamad

Supreme Court of India

08 March 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 187; (1978) 2 SCC 659; AIR 1978 SC 840; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 446; 1978 UJ 276

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Election

Summary: Election - Representation Of People Act, 1951, ss. 81, 83 and 86 - Election petition - Corrupt practice - True copy of petition - Petioner signed at end of affidavit rather than on election petition - So petition dismissed - Held, no ground for dismissal of petition - Appeal allowed.
