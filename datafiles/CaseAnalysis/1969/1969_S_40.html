Supreme Court of India

16 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 227 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 29 - Dismissal of revision against order, by HC - Order merges with order made in revision - Held, it cannot thereafter be challenged under the Articles - Appeal Allowed.
