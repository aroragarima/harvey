South Gujarat Roofing Tiles Manufacturersassociation And Anr v State Of Gujarat And Another

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 260; (1976) 4 SCC 601; (1977) SCC (L&S) 15; AIR 1977 SC 90; 1976 (33) FLR 394; 1977 (18) GLR 688; 1976 LabIC 1778; 1977 (1) LLN 1; 1976 (33) PLR 394; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 878; 1976 UJ 912

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Minimum Wages Act, 1948 - Interpretation of expression 'potteries industry includes' in s. 27 and sch., Part I, entry 22, expln. -To determine hat whether manufacture of Mangalore pattern roofing tiles if covered - Held, is outside the purview of entry 22 of Part I of sch. and the notification issued by Kerela Government on 12-5-1975 u/s. 27 so far as it applies to such tiles - Appeal Allowed.
