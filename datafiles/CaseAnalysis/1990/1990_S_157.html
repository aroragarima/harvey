Ratilal B. Soni And Ors. v State Of Gujarat And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 707; (1990) Supp SCC 243; (1990) SCC (L&S) 630; AIR 1990 SC 1132; 1991 (15) ATC 857; 1990 (60) FLR 468; 1990 (1) GLH 260; JT 1990 (1) SC 229; 1990 LabIC 889; 1990 (1) LLJ 525; 1990 (1) LLN 492; 1990(1) SCALE 228; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 414; 1990 (1) UJ 548

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Gujarat Panchayat Act, 1961

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311- Whether employees on deputation have right to be absorbed on deputation post? - Held, no such right to be absorbed on deputation post available and such candidates can be reverted to his present cadre at any time - Appeal dismissed.
