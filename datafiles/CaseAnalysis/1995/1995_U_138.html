Hyderabad Industries Limited and Ani v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 469; (1995) 5 SCC 338; 1995 (59) DLT 596; 1995 (60) ECR 4; 1995 (78) E.L.T. 641; JT 1995 (5) SC 594; 1995(4) SCALE 516; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 287

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Indirect Tax - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Whether the Hon'ble Bench had erred in the Khandelwal Metal and Engineering Works Case in holding that the asbestos fibre imported by the appellants was exigible to additional duty regardless of the fact that it was not the result of manufacture and, therefore, not exigible to excise duty? - Held, the decision in the case of Khandelwal Metal & Engineering Works requires the consideration of a larger bench - Papers and proceedings to be placed before the Hon'ble Chief Justice for appropriate administrative directions.
