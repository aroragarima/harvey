Union of India and Another v Shri Baljit Singh

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1429; (1996) 11 SCC 315; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 589; 1996(8) SCALE 216; 1997 (1) SLJ 147; 1997 (1) SLR 98; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 626; 1996 (8) Supreme 87

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Pension Regulations of Army, 1961, r. 173 - Whether High Court rightly directed appellants who is an army personnel to pay him disability pension? - Held, when a disability pension is sought for and made a claim, it must be affirmatively established, as a fact, as to whether injury sustained was to military service or was aggravated which contributed to invalidation for military service - No interference - Appeal dismissed.
