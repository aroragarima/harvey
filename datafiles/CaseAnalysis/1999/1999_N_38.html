Betibai and Others v Nathooram and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 453; (1999) 6 SCC 368; AIR 1999 SC 1767; JT 1999 (3) SC 200; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 1028

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 106 - Madya Pradesh Accommodation Control Act, 1961, s. 3(2) - Whether said property is exempted from being regulated by Act? - Held, property belonging to Religious and Charitable Trust and therefore question of tenancy is determined by Transfer of Property Act, 1882.
