Chaudharana Steels Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Allahabad

Supreme Court of India

08 May 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1024; (2009) 15 SCC 183; 2009 (238) E.L.T. 705; 2009(8) SCALE 16; [2009] 8 S.C.R. 562

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35-G - Appeal - Condonation of delay (COD) - Whether there is power for condonation of delay in filing an appeal u/s. 35-G of Central Excise Act, 1944? - Held, as per SC judgement in Commissioner of Customs and Central Excise v. Hongo India Private Limited 2009 INDLAW SC 800, HC has no power to condone delay in filing 'reference application' filed by Commissioner under unamended s. 35H(1) of Central Excise Act, 1944 beyond prescribed period of 180 days and rightly dismissed reference on ground of limitation - Appeal dismissed.
