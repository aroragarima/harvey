Polammarasetti Varana Venka Satyananarayana v Suddha Apparao Naidu (Dead ) And Ors Respondent

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1084; (1997) 9 SCC 244; AIR 1997 SC 2127; 1997 (3) CLT 1; JT 1997 (4) SC 327; 1997 (1) RCR(Rent) 554; 1997(3) SCALE 193; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 29; 1997 (3) Supreme 363

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 60 - Redemption of Mortgage - Land given on lease and also mortgaged to lessee - There was specific statement in deed that mortgagee would deliver possession to mortgagor on redemption but mode of adjustment of lease rent was not indicated in deed - Findings of Lower Court that lease-hold interest had come to an end, and that mortgagor was entitled to redemption of mortgaged property - Appeal - Held, it clearly indicates the intention to retain only mortgagee's interest and not to continue leasehold rights - It cannot be said that there was holding over by lessee without any payment of rent after expiry of lease - Finding of Lower Court just and proper - Appeal dismissed.
