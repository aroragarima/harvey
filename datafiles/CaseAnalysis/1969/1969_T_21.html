Ramchand (Dead) By Legal Representatives v Thakur Janki Ballabhji Maharaj and Another

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 515; (1969) 2 SCC 313; AIR 1970 SC 532; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 634

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, ss. 9 , 92, O. 41, r. 33, O. 7, r. 4 - Hindu Law - Religious endowment - Private trust temple - Mis-management and misappropriation by pujari - (A) Right to sue on behalf of deity to protect property maintainable - By person making donations for temple maintenance - (B) Suit for possession of temple and its properties by deity - Civil Court - Juristdiction of, to frame scheme for management - Held, can sue on behalf of deity and s. 92 of CPC,1908 has no application - Civil Court has jurisdiction to frame scheme for management - Appeal Dismissed.
