State Of Rajasthan And Ors. v Sajjanlal Panjawat And Others

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.N. Dwivedi, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 214; (1974) 1 SCC 500; AIR 1975 SC 706; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 741

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Religious Denomination

Summary: Constitution, arts. 25, 26 and 372 - Rajasthan Public Trusts Act, 1959, ss. 17(3), 30, 31, 38, 39, 40, 41, 42, 43 52(1)(e) and 53(5) - Temple - Trust - (A) Managemant of Jain temple vesting in Ruler of Udaipur State - Under the provisions of Law made by him before coming into force of Constitution of India - If re-vested after Coonstitution came into force - (B) Constitutional validity of provisions of 1959, Act - Held, it cannot re-vest in the Jain shasan on coming into force of the Constitution - Held, provisions are valid - Appeal No. 1092 and 10 87 of 1967Dismissed; Appeal No.1119 and 1647 of 1967 Allowed.
