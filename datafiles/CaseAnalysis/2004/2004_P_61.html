People’S Union For Civil Liberties & Anr. v U.O.I. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 62; (2004) 2 SCC 476; AIR 2004 SC 1442; 2004 (2) CalLT(SC) 39; JT 2004 (1) SC 152; 2004(1) SCALE 91; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 232

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Freedom Of Speech And Expression

Summary: Constitution - art. 19(1)(a), (2) - Atomic Energy Act, 1962 - s. 18 - Right to information - Right to information on nuclear installations - Held right to information though is a fundamental right, government maintaining secrecy as to atomic energy and nuclear plants, withholding reports of the Atomic Energy Board, balance to be struck, reasonable restriction, security of the country and public safety, aspects considered when restriction imposed under the Atomic Energy Act, s.18 - Intra vires - Legislations providing for non-disclosure in larger public interest - Upheld as reasonable - Appeals Dismissed.
