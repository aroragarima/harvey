Anand Chandra Dash v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1935; (1998) 2 SCC 560; (1998) SCC (L&S) 638; AIR 1998 SC 713; 1998 (78) FLR 423; JT 1998 (1) SC 98; 1998 LabIC 494; 1998 (1) LLN 561; 1998(1) SCALE 72; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 81; 1998 (2) SLJ 285; 1998 (2) SLT 277; 1998 (1) Supreme 224; 1998 (1) UPLBEC 499

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Keywords: Housing

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Seniority - Determination of - Transfer from one department to another department of appellant - Determination of seniority on date of joining new department, condition for transfer - Appellant not agreed on condition and expressed unwillingness to join new department - Request of reversion to parent department - Held, past seniority has to be taken into account, in circumstances.
