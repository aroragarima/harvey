Deo Narain v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, A. Alagiriswami, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 219; (1973) SCC (Cr) 330; (1973) 1 SCC 347; AIR 1973 SC 473; 1973 CRLJ 677; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 57

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure Code 1908

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 102 - Right of private defence - Nature - Whether injury at hands of agressor is required - Held, for exercising right it is not necessary that party exercising it must have actually received some injury at the hands of aggressor - It is a preventive and not punitive right - Appeal allowed.
