D. K. Joshi v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 289; (2000) 5 SCC 80; 2000 (3) AWC 1845; JT 2000 (5) SC 211; 2000(4) SCALE 72; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 525; 2000 (2) SLR 786; 2000 (4) SLT 316; 2000 (3) Supreme 552; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1596

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug

Summary: Health & Drug - Indian Medicine Central Council Act, 1970, ss. 15 and 17 - United Provinces Medical Act, 1917, s. 30 - Whether High Court was correct in dismissing petition praying for issuance of directions to initiate action against persons who are unqualified and unregistered but carrying on medical profession unauthorisedly in district of Agra, Uttar Pradesh? - Held, impugned judgment set aside, unless directions are issued in respect of entire State of Uttar Pradesh problem cannot be solved - Directions issued - Appeal allowed.
