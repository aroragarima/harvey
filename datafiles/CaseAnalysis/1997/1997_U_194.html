Rashtriya Chaturth Shreni Railway Majdoor v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1272; (1997) 11 SCC 1; AIR 1997 SC 3492; JT 1997 (8) SC 357; 1997 LabIC 2896; 1998 (1) LLJ 309; 1997(6) SCALE 340; 1999 (1) SLJ 107; 1997 (8) Supreme 403; 1998 (1) UPLBEC 257

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Regularisation of services - Contract labourers doing job of parcel porters for several years at railway station - Regularisation of services claimed in light of Supreme Court decision in 1995 AIR SCW 2541 - Direction passed by tribunal to avail alternative remedy - Whether proper? - Held, not proper in facts and circumstances - Matter remitted - Appeal allowed.
