General Electric Technical Servicescompany Inc. v Punj Sons (P) Ltd. And Another

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, Veeraswami Ramaswami, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 1036; (1991) 4 SCC 230; AIR 1991 SC 1994; AIR 1991 SCW 2136; 1991 (2) ARBLR 173; 1991 (2) BankCLR 645; 1992 (2) BC 1; 1991 (3) CCC 34; [1992] 74 Comp Cas 624; 1991 (3) CompLJ 95; JT 1991 (3) SC 360; 1991(2) SCALE 272; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 412; 1991 (2) UJ 498

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract Act, 1972 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 - Encashment of bank guarantee - Could be refused on ground that contractor has failed to make a reference to mobilisation advance in letter seeking encashment of bank guarantee - Held, encashment of bank guarantee can not be refused.
