State O Haryana & Ors. v Miss Ajay Walia

Supreme Court of India

07 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3100; (1997) 6 SCC 255; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1445; AIR 1997 SC 3007; JT 1997 (6) SC 592; 1997 LabIC 2886; 1998 (1) LLJ 80; 1997(5) SCALE 48; 1998 (1) SLJ 62; 1997 (4) SLR 638; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 451; 1997 (7) Supreme 189; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1665

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art.16 - Requisition made for filling up of only four vacancies - 28 candidates selected by Selection Board and their names recommended for appointment to various Deptts. - Held, selection and consequent recomendation to by the Deptts. is without jurisdiction and also direction of HC to authority to appoint candidate recommended by Board is illegal - Petition dismissed.
