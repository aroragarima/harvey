Motiram Ghelabhai (Dead) Through L. R. Maniram v Jagan Nagar (Dead) Through Lrs And Others.

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 189; (1985) 2 SCC 279; AIR 1985 SC 709; 1985 (2) Bom.C.R. 553; 1985 (1) RCR(Rent) 538; 1985 (2) RentLR 580; 1985(1) SCALE 360; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 1051; 1985 UJ 691

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, Bombay General Act, 1904

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 50 Proviso, 12(1) -Tenancy - (A) Nature and scope of s. 50 Proviso - Appeal pending when Act made applicable - Applicability - (B) Suit for ejectment - Appeal pending when Act applied - Whether tenant can get benefit of s.12 irrespective of s. 50 - (A) Held, it is an independent provision providing for special savings - Does not merely qualify or create exception to main provisions of s. 50 - Appeal not governed by Act - (B) Held, cannot get benefit and s. 50 is special provision dealing with pending matters and it overrides general provisions of s. 12 - Appeal dismissed.
