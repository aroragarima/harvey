Messrs Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited, Ranipur v Messrs Amar Nath Bhan Prakash

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 269; (1982) 1 SCC 625

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 20 - Whether discharge of the contract by accord and satisfaction or not, is a dispute arising out of the contract and is liable to be referred to arbitration? - Held, High Court finding that there was no accord and satisfaction of the contract set aside - Matters in dispute between the parties, including the question whether or not there was discharge of the contract by accord and satisfaction, referred to arbitration - Appeal dismissed.
