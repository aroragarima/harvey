Tamil Nadu Computer Science, B. Ed. Graduate v Higher Secondary School Computer Teachers

Supreme Court of India

19 November 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Sathasivam, K.G. Balakrishnan, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1560; (2010) 1 SCC 526; AIR 2010 SC 791; 2009 (77) ALR 791; JT 2009 (14) SC 333; 2009(14) SCALE 84; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 1033; 2009 (6) SLR 651

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Recruitment - Post of computer instructors - Amendment in policy decision of Government - Clarification regarding earlier judgment - Petitioner filed applications for certain clarification/directions regarding SC judgement in Tamil Nadu Computer Science, B. Ed. Graduate Teachers Welfare Society v. Higher Secondary School Computer Teachers Association and Others 2009 INDLAW SC 1557 - Held, State Government permitted to (i) conduct examination to candidates who have secured 35% to 49% marks in examination and declare results of candidates who secured more than 50% marks as eligible candidates for appointment (ii) Recruit Vocational Computer Instructors for existing 175 vacancies and future vacancies for post of Computer Instructors through Employment Exchange based on seniority with Employment Exchange - Applications disposed of.
