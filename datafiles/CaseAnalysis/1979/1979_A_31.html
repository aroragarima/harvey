Aribam Tuleshwar Sharma v Aribam Pishak Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 503; (1979) 4 SCC 389; AIR 1979 SC 1047; 1979 UJ 300

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, art.226 - Power of High Court to review it's own order in a writ petition - Held, t High Court can review it's order but under certain limitation such as : (A) on the discovery of new and important matter or evidence which, after the exercise of due diligence was not within the knowledge of the person seeking the review or could not be produced by him at the time when the order was made -

(B) it may be exercised where some mistake or error apparent on the face of the record is found (C) it may also be exercised on any analoguous ground - (D) not be exercised on the ground that the decision was erroneous on merits - Appeal allowed.
