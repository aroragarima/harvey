Workmen of Messrs Bata Shoe Company Private Limited v Messrs Bata Shoe Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 7; (1972) 3 SCC 627; (1973) SCC (L&S) 85; AIR 1972 SC 1436; 1972 (24) FLR 359; 1972 LabIC 847; 1972 (2) LLJ 197; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 450

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965, ss. 32(vii)(a), 2(21), 8, 10, 11 and 17 - Whether amount paid as bonus is general bonus paid under agreement Ext A-5 or an amount paid as production bonus or incentive wages? - Whether said payment as general bonus is annual bonus linked with production or productivity and paid in lieu of bonus based on profits? - Held, essential to find out nature of payment made under Ext A-5 - Art. VI ex gratia payment of bonus (an agreement entered into between parties) - S. 32(vii)(a) of Act operates, employees so long as agreement or settlement is in operation cannot claim bonus on basis of Full Bench Formula or under Act - General bonus paid u/art. VI of agreement Ext A-5, is a payment of annual bonus linked with production or productivity in lieu of bonus based on profits - Agreement has been entered into before 29-05-1965, employees cannot claim any additional bonus under Act for period for which agreement is in operation - Appeal dismissed.
