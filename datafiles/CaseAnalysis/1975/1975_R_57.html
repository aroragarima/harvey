Rikhu Dev, Chela Bawa Harjug Dass v Som Dass (Deceased) Through His Chela Shiamdass

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 392; (1976) 1 SCC 103; AIR 1975 SC 2159; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 487; 1975 UJ 770

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Applicability of O. 22, rr. 3, 4 and 10 - Suit against person in representative capacity - Devolution of interest of representative - Held, rule that is applicable is r. 10 and not r. 3 or r. 4 - Appeal allowed.
