State Of U.P. And Others v Ashok Kumar Singh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1804; (1996) 1 SCC 302; (1996) SCC (L&S) 304; AIR 1996 SC 736; 1996 ALJ 436; 1996 (32) ATC 239; 1996 (1) CLT 40; 1996 LabIC 754; 1995(6) SCALE 465; 1996 (1) SLR 291; 1996 (1) UJ 6

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, art.311 - Removal from service - Multiple absenting from duty without leave by delinquent Police Constable - Order of removal by Tribunal - High Court modifying on ground of disproportionate - Held, delinquent being in service of disciplined force demanding strict adherence to - Not need of interference - Order of removal revived.
