The New India Assurance Co. Ltd. v Smt. Shanti Bai And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 618; (1995) 2 SCC 539; 1995 ACJ 470; AIR 1995 SC 1113; AIR 1995 SCW 1142; 1995 (1) CCC 304; [1995] 83 Comp Cas 71; JT 1995 (2) SC 95; 1995(1) SCALE 472; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 871; 1996 (Supp) AWC 72; 1995 (1) UJ 482

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s.95 (2) (b) - Comprehensive insuranse policy - Third party risk Liability of insurance company - No special contract - What will be legal liability for accidents to passengers - Limit of liability - Tariff of insurance company - Annual Premium per passsanger - Rs.12/- for liability limited to Rs.15000 - Rs.50/- for unlimited liability - Actual Premium Paid @ Rs.12/- - Held, policy covered only statutory liability - Comprehensive policy only entitles - To claim amount up to the estimated value - Not unlimited or higher.
