State of Kerala and Others v V. R. Kalliyanikutty and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, D.P. Mohapatra, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 908; (1999) 3 SCC 657; AIR 1999 SC 1305; [1999] 96 Comp Cas 613; JT 1999 (2) SC 540; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 372

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Kerala Revenue Recovery Act, 1968, s. 71 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Public revenue - Speedy recovery of arrears - Classification of loans viz Bank loan, loan by State Financial Corporation - Constitutionality - Time barred loan - Whether recoverable? - Held, object for classification is advancement of economic well being of people - Not violative of art. 14 - Only legally recoverable loan is permissible and not time barred loan.
