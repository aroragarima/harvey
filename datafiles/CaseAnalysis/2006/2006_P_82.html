Kastha Niwarak Grahnirman Sahakari Sanstha Maryadit, Indore v President, Indore Development Authority

Supreme Court of India

07 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 37; (2006) 2 SCC 604; AIR 2006 SC 1142; 2006 (2) AWC 1139; 2006 (3) JhrCR(SC) 75; JT 2006 (2) SC 259; 2006(2) SCALE 274; 2006 (2) SCJ 361; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 129

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Society

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Urban Land Ceiling Act - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Madhya Pradesh Nagar Tatha Gram Nivesh Adhiniyam, 1973, s. 50(2) - Guidelines allowing Housing Co-operative Societies to utilize land owned by them by making plots for construction of houses for benefit of their members - Benefit of Circular denied to appellant holding that appellant was not owner of land and it only claimed to be potential purchaser having purportedly entered into certain agreements - Appeal against - Held, condition that it must purchase land prior to publication of Declaration of s.50(2) of Authority in respect of scheme was admittedly not fulfilled - Appeal dismissed.
