Smt. Chameli Wati and Another v Municipal Corporation of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 480; (1986) 4 SCC 503; (1986) SCC (Cr) 533; 1985 ACJ 645; AIR 1986 SC 1191

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 110-CC - Effect of enhancement of compensation on interest on compensation - Held, interest must be paid on enhanced amount of compensation from date of application for compensation - Order accordingly.
