Lalita Jalan and Another v Bombay Gas Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1605; (2002) 7 SCC 37; AIR 2002 SC 3129; [2002] 112 Comp Cas 1; 2002 (4) CompLJ 327; 2002 (4) Crimes 239; 2002 (95) FLR 342; JT 2002 (6) SC 588; 2002 (3) LLJ 1080; 2002 (4) RCR(Criminal) 628; 2002(6) SCALE 304; 2002 (5) SLT 178; 2002 (6) Supreme 251

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 630 - Expression 'officer or employee' - U/s. 630 of Act - Interpretation - Held, though expression 'officer or employee of a company' would include past officer or employee of Co. or his legal heirs or representatives but does not include other members of his family - Provision cannot be liberally construed so as to rope in family members - An apparent conflict between two decisions - Abhilash Vinod kumar Jain's case and J K (Bombay) Ltd 's case - Matter referred to larger bench.
