Mehta Ravindrarai Ajitrai (Deceased) Through His Heirs and Lrs and Others v State Of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 682; (1989) 4 SCC 250; AIR 1989 SC 2051; 1989 (3) CCC 15; JT 1989 (3) SC 321; 1989(2) SCALE 296; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 743; 1989 (2) UJ 547

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of market value - Relevancy of following - (A) Sale of adjacent land - Agreement of sale five months after s. 4 notification - Acquisition though for construction of industrial estate, no sharp or speculative rise in price after acquisition - (B) Sale deed over a year prior to notification of acquisition - Sale, distress sale in that it was at Govt. auction - Execution applications pending against vendor - Price appearing in sale, if reliable - (C) Instance of sale - Neither vendor nor purchaser nor any person conversant with sale, examined - Only certified copy produced - Person for whom property was purchased and who was minor at time of execution, examined - Witness not having personal information regarding transaction - Another witness also not having personal knowledge about sale - Held, (A) Sale could not be ignored - (B) Does not furnish reliable guidance - (C) Sale deed cannot be relied upon - Order accordingly.
