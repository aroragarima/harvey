Batala Cooperative Sugar Mills Limited v Sowaran Singh

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 890; (2005) 8 SCC 481; AIR 2006 SC 56; 2006 (1) CLR 23; 2005 (107) FLR 815; JT 2005 (10) SC 79; 2005 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 750; 2006 (1) LLJ 12; 2005(8) SCALE 505; 2005 (6) SLR 413; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 304; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 9

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10(1), 25F - Whether termination of services of workman is justified and in order? - If not, to what relief/exact amount of compensation is he entitled? - Held, workman was employed on casual basis on daily wages for specific work for specific period - Onus to prove 240 days of continuous work lies on workman not employer - Appeal allowed.
