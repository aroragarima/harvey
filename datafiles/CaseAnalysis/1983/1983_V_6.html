Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, Gujarat, Ahmedabad v Vadilal Lallubhai Etc.

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 205; (1983) 4 SCC 697; AIR 1984 SC 157; 1983 (37) CTR 277; [1984] 145 ITR 7; 1983(2) SCALE 821; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 485; [1983] 15 TAXMAN 26; 1984 UJ 100

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss.2(m), 3 and 23 - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 3 and 4 - Gift Tax Act, 1958, ss. 3 and 2(xx) - (A) When liabilities to Wealth Tax, Income Tax or Gift -tax are crystalized or perfected debts - (B) Whether in computing the net wealth of the assessee, the amount deductible in respect of liability of tax for any year for which the assessment is completed after the valuation date is the liability as ascertainable on the valuation date or the actual amount of tax subsequently assessed - Held, relevant date in each case specified - Held, calculated on basis of tax as finally quantified on assessment - Appeals dismissed.
