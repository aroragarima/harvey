Government of A. P. and Another v B. Satyanarayana Rao (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 318; (2000) 4 SCC 262; (2000) SCC (L&S) 486; AIR 2000 SC 1729; AIR 2000 SCW 1561; 2000 (2) CLR 264; 2000 (85) FLR 744; JT 2000 (4) SC 536; 2000 (33) LabIC 1843; 2000 (2) LLJ 545; 2000 (3) LLN 2; 2000(3) SCALE 277; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1009; 2000 (2) SLR 440; 2000 (4) SLT 228; 2000 (3) Supreme 715

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 16 - Andhra Pradesh Transport Services Rules, r. 3(a) - Recruitment - Power of State Govt. - Post of Regional Transport Officer - Held, Govt. exercises specifically overriding power under Para 5(2) of Presidential Order - Can fill vacancies on post in different zones.
