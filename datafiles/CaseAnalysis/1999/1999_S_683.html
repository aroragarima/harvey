Cheriyan Sosamma and Others v Sundaressan Pillai Saraswathy Amma and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 919; (1999) 3 SCC 251; AIR 1999 SC 947; 1999 (2) ALJ 115; 1999 (35) ALR 382; 1999 (2) AWC 971; 1999 (1) CLT 122; 1999 (1) CRC 661; JT 1999 (1) SC 195; 1999 (2) RCJ 197; 1999 (1) RCR(Rent) 199; 1999(1) SCALE 197; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 172; 1999 (1) SLT 394; 2000 (Supp) RD 561; 1999 (1) Supreme 260

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 91 and 58 - Usufructuary mortgage - Prior lease of property in question in favour of husband of mortgagee - Husband and wife living together - Thus lessee and mortgagee were same person - Held, no automatic merger of interest of lessee with that of mortgagee - Deed of mortgage showing that lease had continued even after execution of mortgage - Right to continue in possession of lessee - Would survive after redemption.
