Delhi Cloth and General Mills Company v Ludh Budh Singh

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 402; (1972) 1 SCC 595; AIR 1972 SC 1031; 1972 (25) FLR 1; 1972 LabIC 573; 1972 (1) LLJ 180; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 29

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33(1)(b) - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 - Whether opportunity to adduce additional evidence was denied to appellants? - Held, application filed by management for permission to adduce evidence was highly belated - Appellant did not ask for an opportunity to adduce evidence when proceedings were pending; nor did it avail itself of right given to it in law to adduce evidence before tribunal during pendency of proceedings if such an opportunity had been asked for and refused - Appeal dismissed.
