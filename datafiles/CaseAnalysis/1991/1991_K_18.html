State Of Orissa v K.Rajeshwar Rao

Supreme Court of India

14 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 361; (1992) SCC (Cr) 177; (1992) 1 SCC 365; AIR 1992 SC 240; 1992 (1) CCR 269; 1991 (3) Crimes 868; 1992 CRLJ 300; 1992 CRLR 390; 1992 CrLR(SC) 390; 1991 (45) DLT 627; JT 1991 (4) SC 309; 1991(2) SCALE 988; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 251; 1992 (1) UJ 108

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Criminal - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 7 and 16 - Sale of adulterated food - (A) Punishment for - Sale effected by son of owner - Liability - (B) Sentence - Offence committed before amendment providing imposition of minimum sentence - Fifteen years elapsing from date of offence - Held, (A) He is liable to be convicted - (B) Agony of prosecution undergone sufficient - However, fine in sum of Rs. 500/- Imposed -appeal allowed.
