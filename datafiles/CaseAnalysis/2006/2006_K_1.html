Nautam Prakash Dgsvc, Vadtal and Others v K. K. Thakkar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 May 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 217; (2006) 5 SCC 330; AIR 2006 SC 2075; 2006 (3) Bom.C.R. 883; 2006 (3) G.L.R. 2654; JT 2006 (11) SC 238; 2006(5) SCALE 213; [2006] Supp1 S.C.R. 646

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Bombay Reorganisation Act, 1960

Summary: [A.] Trusts & Associations - Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 - Bombay Reorganisation Act, 1960 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Appellant is a religious trust - Jurisdiction of Assistant Charity Commissioner of Bombay in relation to administration of Appellant-Trust - Whether Assistant Charity Commissioner, Greater Bombay had jurisdiction to interfere with administration of Appellant-trust? - Held, only so much of property which was situate in Maharashtra region would be deemed to be so registered in Bombay - Jurisdiction of Assistant Charity Commissioner, Greater Bombay was, confined to only property which was situate within Maharashtra region and not in relation to entire trust - Appeal allowed.
