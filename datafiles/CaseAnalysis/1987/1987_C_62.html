Hargovind Das K. Joshi and Others v Collector of Customs and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28857; (1987) 2 SCC 230; (1987) SCC (Cr) 343; AIR 1987 SC 1982; 1987 CRLR 543; 1987 (14) ECC 233; 1992 (61) E.L.T. 172

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax; Land & Property

Summary: Custom - Customs Act, 1962, s. 125(1) - Addl. Collector of Customs passed order for absolute confiscation of imported goods, zip fasteners - Validity of order passed by authority, having discretion to give option for redemption by imposing penalty without going into question - Held, it was just, fair and proper that authority addressed himself to question of giving option - Case remitted for determining as to whether or not to give option to redeem on payment of such fine as may be considered appropriate - Appeal dismissed.
