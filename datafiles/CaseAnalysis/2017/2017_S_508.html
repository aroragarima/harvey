Hoshiar Singh (Dead), Through Lrs. and others v State of Punjab and others

Supreme Court of India

11 January 2017

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, Rohinton Fali Nariman

Where Reported

2017 Indlaw SC 436; (2017) 5 SCC 243; 2017 (121) ALR 884; JT 2017 (2) SC 265; 2017 (135) RD 716

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, Land Acquisition Act, 1894

Summary: Land & Property - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O.41 r.27 - Enhancement of compensation - Orders on Additional evidence - Instant appeals by claimants assailing orders of HC contending that they filed application under O.41 r.27 producing awards/orders in case of same nature of land acquired for same purpose in States of Jammu and Kashmir and Himachal Pradesh but HC refused to enhance compensation granted to them.

Held, unless Court passes order on reception or otherwise on additional evidence, Court could not hold that there was no evidence available on circumstances under which compensation was awarded. Hence, impugned orders are set aside and matters are remanded to HC for consideration afresh after passing orders on application filed by appellants under O.41 r.27 of CPC. Matter remanded to HC. Order accordingly.
