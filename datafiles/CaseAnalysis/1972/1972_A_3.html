State of Kerala v A. Pareed Pillai and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 562; (1972) 3 SCC 661; (1972) SCC (Cr) 705; AIR 1973 SC 326; 1972 CRLJ 1243

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code,1860, s. 420 - Cheating - Dishonest intention at time of making promise is necessary - Inference - Held, such a dishonest intention cannot be inferred from mere fact that he could not subsequently fulfil promise - Appeal dismissed.
