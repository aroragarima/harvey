Government Of Tamil Nadu v K.N.Ramamurthy

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2787; (1997) 7 SCC 101; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1749; AIR 1997 SC 3571; JT 1997 (7) SC 401; 1998 LabIC 96; 1998 (1) LLJ 89; 1997(5) SCALE 476; 1997 (5) SLR 232; 1997 (7) Supreme 264

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Services - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - T.N. Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal), Rules, 1953, r. 17(b) - Judicial review - Jurisdiction of Tribunal - Charges framed by Disciplinary Authority - Neither allegation of irregularity nor charges framed contrary to any law - Whether Tribunal can go into correctness or truth of charges - Held, cannot go into correctness or truth of charges - Appeal Disposed of.
