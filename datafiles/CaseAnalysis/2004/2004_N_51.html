Supreme Court of India

06 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 75; (2004) 3 SCC 277; AIR 2004 SC 1761; 2004 (1) AWC 786; 2004 (2) CHN 107; JT 2004 (2) SC 386; 2004 (1) KLT 1082; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 110; 2004(2) SCALE 302; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 202; 2004 (1) SLT 872; 2004 (1) Supreme 867

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Forcible Dispossession

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land and Property - Practice and Procedure - Rent Control - CPC, 1908, O 2,r 2 - Decree passed in favour of the plaintiffs for recovery of possession shall stand affirmed - Courts below erred in not applying rule - Factual adjudication is necessary as to whether appellant acted in good faith or with bona fide belief as envisaged while making improvements to the tenement - Matter remanded - Appeal disposed of.
