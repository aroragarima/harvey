State of Uttar Pradesh and Another v Jaggo Alias Jagdish and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 849; (1971) 2 SCC 42; (1971) SCC (Cr) 401; AIR 1971 SC 1586; 1971 CRLJ 1173

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, s. 286 -Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 154 - Murder - Hostile witness - Prosecution presented an application that some of witness had been won over - Held, in such a case witness can be produced for cross-examination by accused which would elicit correct facts - Mere application that witnesses have been won over wont suffice - Appeal dismissed.
