Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Benami Transactions (Prohibition) Act, 1988, ss. 4(1) and 4(2) - Suit on behalf of person claiming to be real owner of property - To enforce right against property held benami prior to coming into force of s. 4(1) - Held, provisions of s. 4(1) does not apply because s. 4(1) and (2) do not have retrospective effect - Order accordingly.
