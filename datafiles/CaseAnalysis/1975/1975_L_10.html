Municipal Corporation Of Delhi v Laxmi Narain Tandon Etc.

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 303; (1976) SCC (Cr) 76; (1976) 1 SCC 546; AIR 1976 SC 621; 1976 CRLJ 547; 1976 CrLR(SC) 57; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 1050

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Article Of Food

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 2(xiii), 7, 16(1)(a) - (A) Expression 'store' means 'storing for sale' - Liability for storing adultrated food simplicitor - (B) Sale - Supply of food by a detelier to resident customer - Composite charge made for food and other amemities - No rebate allowed if food not takes - Storing adulterated food simpliciter is not offence - Supply of food held amounted to sale - Appeal Allowed.
