Anandji Haridas and Company Private Limited v Engineering Mazdoor Sangh and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 99; (1975) SCC (L&S) 165; (1975) 3 SCC 862; AIR 1975 SC 946; [1975] 45 Comp Cas 386; 1975 (30) FLR 133; [1975] 99 ITR 592; 1975 LabIC 602; 1975 (2) LLJ 12; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 542; 1975 TAXLR 479; 1975 UJ 231

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Finance Act, 1966, Payment of Bonus Act 1954

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Payment Of Bonus Act, 1965, ss. 6(c) and 7(e) - Finance Act, 1966, sch. 1, para. I(A)(2)(1) - Interpretation of statutes - Intention of Legislature - (A) Whether and when external evidence can be used? - Industrial Co. - Concession in rate of income tax granted under Finance Act 1966 - Whether there is a rebate or relief in payment of direct tax, within s. 7(e) of Bonus Act - Held, where a statute is not exhaustive or language is ambiguous, uncertain, clouded or susceptible of more than one meaning or shades of meaning, then external evidence can be used - Not a rebate or relief in payment of direct tax - Order accordingly.
