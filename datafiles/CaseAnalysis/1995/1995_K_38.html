Union Of India And Ors. v Kantilal Hematram Pandya

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1538; (1995) 3 SCC 17; (1995) SCC (L&S) 599; AIR 1995 SC 1349; AIR 1995 SCW 1297; 1995 (29) ATC 522; 1995 (1) CLR 726; 1995 (70) FLR 1023; JT 1995 (2) SC 365; 1995 (2) LLJ 659; 1995 (1) LLN 779; 1995(1) SCALE 708; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 71; 1995 (3) SLJ 130; 1995 (2) SLR 54; 1995 (1) UJ 787

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, art. 311 - Correction of date of birth - Failure of employee to mention alleged correct birth date in option and Provident Fund withdrawal forms - Correctness and genuineness of certificate produced - Doubtful - Delay in filing application - Held, applicant employee not entitled to relief.
