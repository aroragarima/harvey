Rajput Ruda Maha And Ors v State Of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 57; (1980) SCC (Cr) 317; (1980) 1 SCC 677; AIR 1980 SC 1707; 1980 CRLJ 1246; 1980 (21) GLR 411; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 353

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Act, 1970

Summary: CrPC, 1973, s. 384 - Constitution, art. 141 - Precedents - Certain specific case - Neither raised nor argued - Decision by court after 'pondering over the issue in depth' - Whether binding precedent - Held, would not be a binding precedent - And Sita Ram v. State of U.P, 1979 INDLAW SC 509 is not an authority regarding scope of s. 384 of CrPC,1973 - Appeal Dismissed
