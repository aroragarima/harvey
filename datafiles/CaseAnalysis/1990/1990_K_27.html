N.T. Bevin Kath Etc. v Karnataka Public Service Commission And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 780; (1990) 3 SCC 157; (1990) SCC (L&S) 446; AIR 1990 SC 1233; 1990 (14) ATC 688; 1992 (64) FLR 307; JT 1991 (5) SC 282; 1990 LabIC 1009; 1990 (2) LLJ 456; 1990 (2) LLN 1023; 1990(1) SCALE 659; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 239; 1992 (2) SLR 378; 1990 (1) UJ 670

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution - Karnataka Administrative Services Tehsildars)Recruitment (Special) Rules, 1975, r. 10 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Govt. order - Appointment posts of Tehsildars - Notification issued specifying reservations and mode of selection under existing order - Changes made by Govt. Order of 1975 - Para 11 exempting pending selection proceedings from application - Whether Govt. is bound to accept selection made by Commission - Held, Govt. is bound to accept selection - Appeal allowed.
