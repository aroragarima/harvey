Bench

M.M. Dutt, T.K. Thommen

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Oil and Natural Gas Commission

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32, 141 and 226 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Service matter - Decision on question of law relating to fact in issue become final - Effect thereof - Held, decision becoming final would be binding on parties and reopening of it on ground of it being violative of art. 14 is not permissible - Doctrine of res judicata is a universal doctrine laying down finality of litigation between parties - Parties will always be bound by said decision - Order accordingly.
