Board of Technical Education, U.P. and Others v Dhanwantri Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, K.N. Saikia, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 1032; AIR 1991 SC 271; 1990 ALJ 874

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Education - Cancellation of examination results - High Court found that orders impugned were unsustainable for reason that students had not been given proper notices - Whether cancellation of exams invalid for violation of rule of natural justice? - Held, HC was justified in coming to conclusion - Notices served on students were so vague and imprecise that they could not effectively defend themselves in inquiry - Appeal dismissed.
