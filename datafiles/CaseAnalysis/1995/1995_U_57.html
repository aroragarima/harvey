Ram Kishan v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 910; (1995) 6 SCC 157; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1357; AIR 1996 SC 255; 1995 (31) ATC 475; 1996 (1) CLR 2; JT 1995 (7) SC 43; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 251

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Delhi Police Act, 1978, s. 11 - Delhi Police (Appointment and Recruitment) Rules, 1980, r. 4 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 19 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Dismissal by Addl. Deputy Commr. is appointing authorities u/r. 4 - Not without authority of law u/s. 19 of General Clauses Act, 1897.
