Land Acquisition Officer and Sub-Collector, Gadwal v Sreelatha Bhoopal Smt. and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1938; (1997) 9 SCC 628; AIR 1997 SC 2552; 1997 (2) CLT 356; JT 1997 (5) SC 383; 1997(4) SCALE 14; 1997 (2) SCJ 105; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 875; 1998 (5) SLT 581; 1997 (4) Supreme 646

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of compensation for land acquisition of large tract - High Court relying on sale deed relating to small piece of land - Determining compensation per sq. yard - Validity - Held, not proper - Supreme Court deciding compensation per acre - Appeal allowed.
