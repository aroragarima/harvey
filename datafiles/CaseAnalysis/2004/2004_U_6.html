U.P. State Bridge Corporation Ltd. & Ors. v U.P. Rajya Setu Nigam S.Karamchari Sangh

Supreme Court of India

13 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 179; (2004) 4 SCC 268; (2004) SCC (L&S) 637; 2004 (5) ALD(SC) 39; 2004 (1) CLR 857; 2004 (100) FLR 1020; JT 2004 (2) SC 440; 2004 (2) LLJ 9; 2004 (2) LLN 93; 2004(2) SCALE 466; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 391; 2004 (2) SLR 23; 2004 (2) UC 909; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1567

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: [A.] Labour & Industrial Law - Companies Act, 1956, s. 617 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 22 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Dispute Act, 1947, s. 65 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 12, 226, 309, 32 - Illegal strike - Cannot by definition be 'authorised absence' - Specific exclusion of persons on illegal strike plainly means that period a person is on illegal strike does not amount to service - Appeal allowed.
