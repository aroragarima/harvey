Barjure Kaikhosroo Maarfatia v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 136; (1978) SCC (Cr) 149; (1978) 1 SCC 596; AIR 1978 SC 434; 1978 CRLJ 507; 1978 CrLR(SC) 45; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 479

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Tort

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 384 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 471, 468, 408 and 477 - Reappraisal of evidence - Important aspects of case over looked by trial court - Held, High Court justified in having a reappraisal of evidence - Conclusion made contrary to Trial Court - Confirmation of convictions - Appeal dismissed.
