Kilpest Private Limited and Others v Shekhar Mehra

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1700; (1996) 10 SCC 696; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 239

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 397, 398 and 402 - Whether oppression and mismanagement is sufficient for winding up of Company? - A limited company cannot be treated as a quasi-partnership - Regard to wide powers u/s. 402, very rarely would it be necessary to wind up any company in a petition filed u/s. 397 and 398 - Held, report filed by Registrar of Companies which shows that no substance was found in aspects of oppression and mismanagement - Appeals dismissed.
