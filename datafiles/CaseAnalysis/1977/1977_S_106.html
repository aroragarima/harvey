Kalumiya Karimmiya v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 143; (1977) 1 SCC 715; AIR 1977 SC 497; 1983 (11) E.L.T. 1566; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 606; 1977 UJ 153

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.5A, 6 - Constitution, 1950, art.133(1)(c) - Second hearing - Public enquiry - Appellant not furnished with copy of report u/s.5A - Validity of such enquiry - Held, that failure to furnish a copy of the report of such an enquiry cannot vitiate the enquiry unless open to any valid objection - A second hearing is not essential under the law at the stage when the State Govt. is considering the report u/s. 5A for issuing its declaration u/s. 6 - Appeal dismissed.
