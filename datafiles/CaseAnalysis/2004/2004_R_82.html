Medical Council Of India v Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, AR. Lakshmanan, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 406; (2004) 6 SCC 76; AIR 2004 SC 2603; 2004 (4) ALD(SC) 15; 2004 (56) ALR 437; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 49; 2004(4) SCALE 434; 2004 (3) SLR 773; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2278

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Health & Drug

Keywords: Medical Council Act

Summary: [A.] Education - Medical Council Act, s. 10-A - Granting permission by Central Govt. and Medical Council of India for starting or continuing colleges - HC ought not to issue an interim order when for earlier year itself permission had not been granted by Council - HCs should be very cautious in matter of issuing interim orders in such matters.
