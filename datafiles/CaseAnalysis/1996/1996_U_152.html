Union of India and Others v N. R. Banerjee and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2043; (1997) 9 SCC 287; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1194; AIR 1997 SC 3761; JT 1996 (11) SC 605; 1997(1) SCALE 235; 1997 (2) SLJ 103; 1997 (1) SLR 751; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 166; 1997 (5) Supreme 327

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309, Proviso - Ordnance Factories Services Rules, r. 2.1 - Promotion - Preparation of - Panel of successful candidates - By Department Promotion Committee - Procedure laid down by Ministry of Personnel and Training - Employees for posts in question to retire in years 1994 - Direction by tribunal to constitute Departmental Promotion Committee on April, 1994 and to take confidential reports of eligible candidates for year 1993 and to ignore for the year 1994 - Held, action should be taken well in advance in case of clear vacancies - Correct in law.
