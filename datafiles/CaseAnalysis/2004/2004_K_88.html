State Of U.P. v Kishan Chand & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 641; (2004) 7 SCC 629; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2013; AIR 2004 SC 4671; JT 2004 (6) SC 535; 2005 (1) RCR(Criminal) 276; 2004(7) SCALE 75; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 640

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - 1860, ss. 302 r/w 148 - Appeal against acquittal - Held, from facts and circumstances it is clear that accused did form an unlawful assembly and in furtherance of that common object of unlawful assembly, crime has been perpetrated - HC fell in error in acquitting the accused resulting in grave miscarriage of justice - Conviction recorded by Trial Court restored - Appeal allowed.
