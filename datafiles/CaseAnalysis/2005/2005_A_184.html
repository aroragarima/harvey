Bank of India and Another v Avinash D. Mandivikar and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 553; (2005) 7 SCC 690; (2005) SCC (L&S) 1011; AIR 2005 SC 3395; 2005 (6) AWC 5165; 2006 (1) Bom.C.R. 300; 2005 (4) ESC 598; 2005 (107) FLR 610; JT 2005 (8) SC 326; 2005 (4) LLN 56; 2005 (4) MahLJ 409; 2005(7) SCALE 272; 2005 (5) SLR 780; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 170; 2005 (6) Supreme 344

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Reserved category of Scheduled Tribe - Invalidation of caste certificate - Termination - HC set aside termination on ground of delay in making a reference - Appealed herein - Held, mere delayed reference when foundation for same is alleged fraud does not in any way affect legality of reference - Order of termination does not suffer from any infirmity and HC should not have interfered with it - Appeal allowed.
