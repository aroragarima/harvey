Chaman Prakash Puri v Ishwar Dass Rajput and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 782; (1995) Supp4 SCC 445

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(e) - Appellant asked to vacate his residence and, therefore, called upon tenant to vacate premises which has been bequeathed to appellant under his father's Will - Rent Controller held appellant-landlord in bona fide need of premises and application for eviction allowed - In revision proceeding, High Court, re-appreciating evidence, held that landlord was not in requirement of premises - Held, HC was not entitled to do this in revision proceedings - Appeal allowed.
