Resham Singh Pyara Singh v Abdul Sattar

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1941; (1996) 1 SCC 49; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 133; 1995 (4) CCC 350; JT 1995 (8) SC 559; 1995(6) SCALE 672; 1996 (1) SCJ 180; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 483

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 104 and O. 43 r. 1 and O. 39, r. 1- Whether LPA would lie against the order of the Single Judge? - When an appeal was filed against the order of the City Civil Court to the Learned single Judge under O. 43, r. 1(r) as provided in s. 104(1) by operation of s. 104(2), no further appeal shall lie from any order passed in appeal under this Section - Held, s. 104(2) envisages that 'no appeal shall lie from any order passed in appeal under this Section' - Petitions dismissed.
