Balkrishan Gupta and Others v Swadeshi Polytex Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 442; (1985) 2 SCC 167; (1985) SCC (Tax) 215; AIR 1985 SC 520; [1985] 58 Comp Cas 563; 1985 (2) CompLJ 1; 1985(1) SCALE 236; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 854; 1985 TAXLR 2066

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property

Keywords: Companies Act, 1913, Proxy, Companies (Declaration of Beneficial Interest in Shares) Rules, 1975

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 87, 137 and 169 - Uttar Pradesh Land Revenue Act, 1901, ss. 182A and 149 - Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951, s. 18AA(1)(a) - Right of shareholder of company - Appointment of receiver u/s.182 A of 1901 Act - Shares attached u/s.149 - (A) Whether the shareholder lose his title and thereby right to requisition Extra ordinary General meeting -(B) How the shares held by shareholder pledged to another will be effected - (C) Whether the shareholder company lose voting rights if an order u/s. 18AA (1)(a) of 1951Act is passed against it - Held, neither title nor right lost - Rights under the companies act will not be effected - Company does not lose voting rights - Appeal dismissed
