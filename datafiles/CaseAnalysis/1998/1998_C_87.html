Thermax Limited v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1636; (1998) 4 SCC 242; AIR 1998 SC 2894; 1998 (76) ECR 214; 1998 (99) E.L.T. 481; JT 1998 (3) SC 138; 1998 (21) RLT 863; 1998(2) SCALE 711; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 769; 1998 (4) SLT 327; 1998 (3) Supreme 621

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4 - Assessable value of goods for excise duty was to be considered - Assessee, manufacturing high pressure boilers, process heat equipments - Manufacturing process also included erection, installation and commissioning of same for which assessee charged separately - Held, assessee not liable to pay excise duty as installation and commissioning charges could not be included in value of goods.
