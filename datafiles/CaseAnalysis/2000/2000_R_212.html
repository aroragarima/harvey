Ramesh Chandra Acharya v Registrar, High Court of Orissa and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 June 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 695; (2000) 6 SCC 332; (2000) SCC (L&S) 743; AIR 2000 SC 2168; 2000 (3) AWC 2465; 2000 (91) ECR 497; JT 2000 (7) SC 461; 2000 LabIC 2336; 2000 (3) LLN 537; 2000(5) SCALE 128; 2000 (5) SLR 338; 2000 (5) SLT 296; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 456; 2000 (5) Supreme 45

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Orissa Service Code, 1939, r. 71(a) - Superannuation Judicial Officer - Continuation in service thereafter - No specific rule - Entitlement - Held, has no right - Permissible only on positive recommendation of High Court.
