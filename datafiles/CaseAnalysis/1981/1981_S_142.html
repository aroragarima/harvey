Wakil Singh and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 48; (1981) Supp SCC 28; (1981) SCC (Cr) 634; AIR 1981 SC 1392; 1981 CRLJ 1014; 1981 CRLR 319; 1981(1) SCALE 925

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 396 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3, 9 and 134 - Whether conviction can be granted on testimony of a single witness? - Held, conviction cannot be granted only on identification by single witness - Identification was only by one witness after a certain number of days - Appeal allowed.
