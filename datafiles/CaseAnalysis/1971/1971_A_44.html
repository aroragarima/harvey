Commissioner of Income Tax (Central), Calcutta v Asiatic Textiles Limited

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 416; (1971) 3 SCC 536; AIR 1973 SC 2323; 1973 CTR 463; [1971] 82 ITR 816; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 81; 1971 UJ 744

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Distributable Surplus

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 23A(1) - Determination of condition precedent for exercise of power u/s. 23A(1) by ITO - Held, condition 'Directors of a company unjustifiably refraining from declaring dividend' is a condition precedent - Appeals dismissed.
