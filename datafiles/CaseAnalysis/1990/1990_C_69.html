Rathi Alloys and Steel Limited, Alwar and Others v Collector, Central Excise, Jaipur and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 828; (1990) 2 SCC 324; AIR 1990 SC 1020; 1990 CRLR 222; 1990 (28) ECC 7; 1990 (27) ECR 289; 1990 (47) E.L.T. 205; JT 1990 (1) SC 353; 1990(1) SCALE 557

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Tariff Item, Central Excise Rules 1944 (contd)

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, Tariff item 26 Concession of excise duty - Manufacture of Steel ingots with aid of electric arc furnace - Runners and risers obtained during manufacture of steel ingot - Claim for concessional rate same as Steel ingots - Held, in peculiar facts of case concessional rate could be claimed - Order accordingly.
