State of Bombay v Supreme General Films Exchange Limited (with connected appeal)

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 438; AIR 1960 SC 980; 1960 (2) KerLR 306; 1960 KLT 17; 1961 (1) MLJ(SC) 12; 1961 (1) SCJ 119; [1960] 3 S.C.R. 640

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151 - Court Fees Act, 1870, s. 5 - Refund of excess court fees - Held, that an impairment of right of appeal by putting a new restriction thereon or imposing a more onerous condition is not a matter of procedure only; it impairs or imperils a substantive right and an enactment which does so is not retrospective unless it says so expressly or by necessary intendment - Orders of refund of excess court fees by High Court were correct in law - Appeal dismissed.
