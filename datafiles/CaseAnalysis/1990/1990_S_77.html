Gram Panchayat And Anr. v Shree Vallabh Glass Works Limited And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 744; (1990) 2 SCC 440; AIR 1990 SC 1017; 1990 (3) Bom.C.R. 109; [1991] 71 Comp Cas 169; 1992 (3) CompLJ 346; JT 1990 (1) SC 438; 1990(1) SCALE 439; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 966; 1992 (86) STC 41; 1990 (2) UJ 27; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 885

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Corporate - Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, ss. 22(1) and 22(5) - Bombay village Panchayats Act, 1959, s. 129 - Sick industrial company - Proceedings u/ss. 16 and 17 of SICA pending - Proceedings to recover property tax u/s. 129 of 1959 Act - Consent of board under Act, required - What if approval is not granted? - Held, discretionary power - On denial, however, remedy is not extinguished but is postponed - Petition dismissed.
