K. Kalpana Saraswathi v P.S.S. Somasundram Chettiar

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 96; (1980) 1 SCC 630; AIR 1980 SC 512; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 293; 1980 UJ 252

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Special Relief Act, 1963

Summary: Land & Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 28 - Agreement to sell - Immovable property subject to mortgage - Specific performance sought - Equitable relief - Permissibility to extend time to deposit amount - Held, allowed subject to denying unrighteous advantage obtained by plaintiff by taking assignment of mortgage decree - Appeal dismissed.
