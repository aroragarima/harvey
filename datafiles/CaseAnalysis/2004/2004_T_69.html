M/S Parle Biscuits (P) Ltd. v The State Of Bihar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1053; (2005) 9 SCC 669; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 46; JT 2004 (10) SC 449; 2004(10) SCALE 405; 2005 (139) STC 204; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 954

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Sales Tax

Summary: [A.] Sales Tax - Bihar Finance Act, 1981- Bihar Finance Amendment Act, 1985 - Companies Act, 1956 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 - Concessional rate of sale tax for packing of goods such as paper and its products - Whether 'cardboard' and 'cartons can be considered as paper products? - Held, cardboard and cardboard box are commercially different products, observed as in Card Board Products case - Directed Deputy Commissioner of Sales Tax to consider question whether cardboard box used by appellant fits in with definition of carton taking into account product which is packed - Appeal disposed of.
