Mrs Labhkuwar Bhagwani Shaha and Others v Janardhan Mahadeo Kalan and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 223; (1982) 3 SCC 514; AIR 1983 SC 535; 1983 (1) RCJ 587; 1982(2) SCALE 1342; 1983 UJ 187

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art.227 - Nature and scope of power of superintendence - Whether High Court can interfere with the finding of lower courts over a question of fact as to actual date of subletting - Held, the question simply was what was the actual dates of sub-letting and the High Court u/art. 227 could not interfere with the finding recorded by the lower courts on the point - Appeal allowed.
