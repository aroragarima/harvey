Swaika Properties Private Limited and Another v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 148; (2008) 4 SCC 695; AIR 2008 SC 1494; JT 2008 (2) SC 280; 2008 (2) MLJ(SC) 1014; 2008 (2) RCR(Civil) 96; 2008(2) SCALE 271; [2008] 2 S.C.R. 521

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act 1959

Summary: Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act 1959, s. 52(2) - Challenge to acquisition proceedings in respect of land of appellants - Writ petition against dismissed - Division Bench held that since the appellants had filed an application under s. 18 enhancement of the compensation they are not entitled to the relief sought for in the writ petition - Appeal against - held, writ petition challenging the notification for acquisition of land, if filed after the possession having been taken, is not maintainable - Writ petition having been filed after taking over the possession and the award having become final, the same deserves to be dismissed on the ground of delay and laches - Appeal dismissed.
