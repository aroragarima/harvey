State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v T. Suryachandra Rao

Supreme Court of India

25 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 437; (2005) 6 SCC 149; AIR 2005 SC 3110; 2005 (5) ALD(SC) 118; JT 2005 (6) SC 391; 2005 (141) PLR 551; 2005(5) SCALE 621; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 809

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Equitable Doctrine

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Land & Property - Andhra Pradesh Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Act, 1973, s. 10(3), s. 21 - Land Acquisition Act, 1898 - Tribunal passed an order determining ceiling limit of declarant - Tribunal having accepted matter after enquiry - Land which was surrendered had already been acquired - Whether Tribunal has power to reopen matter? - Held, merely because an enquiry was made, Tribunal was not divested of power to correct error when respondent had clearly committed a fraud - Appeal allowed.
