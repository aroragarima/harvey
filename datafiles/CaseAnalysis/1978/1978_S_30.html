Gobind Singh v Shanti Sarup

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 84; (1979) 2 SCC 267; (1979) SCC (Cr) 444; AIR 1979 SC 143; 1979 CRLJ 59; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 806

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006.)

Summary: Criminal- Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 133 and 138 - Usage of oven - Issued conditional order preventing appellant from using oven was and asked to demolish same u/s. 133 of CrPC - Also desisted from further carrying on trade of a baker - Whether Magistrate had gone beyond scope of conditional order in discontinuing trade of baker? - Held, that Magistrate had gone beyond scope of conditional order - Preventing appellant from using oven was certainly within terms of conditional order but not so order requiring him to desist from carrying on trade of a baker at site that -Appeal Dismissed.
