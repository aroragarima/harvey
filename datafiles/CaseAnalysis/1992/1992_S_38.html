Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India , 1950, art. 309 - Madhya Pradesh Public Health Engineering (Gazetted) Service Rules , 1980, sch. IV - Applicability of length of service as criterion for promotion in absence of rule governing aspect - Held, applicable - Appeal allowed.
