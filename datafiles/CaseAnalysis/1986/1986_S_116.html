Mewa Ram (Deceased) By His L.Rs. And Ors v State Of Haryana Through The Landacquisition Collector, Gurg

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 190; (1986) 4 SCC 151; AIR 1987 SC 45; 1986 (12) AWC 1025; JT 1986 SC 246B; 1986(2) SCALE 350; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 660; 1986 (2) UJ 570

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice and Procedure - CPC, 1908, O.17 rr.2, 3 , O.9 r. 13 - Appeal against summary dismissal of the first appeal -Absence of parties to the suit on the date and for him no evidence has been examined till that date - Applicability of r. 3 of O.17 - Held, O. 17, r. 2 applies and Court can proceed ex parte under O. 9, CPC, 1908 - Defendant can thereafter file an application u/r. 13 of O. 9 for setting aside of ex parte decree - Appeal allowed.
