Surajdeo Yadav v State Of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 860; (1997) SCC (Cr) 1070; AIR 1996 SC 3157; 1996 CRLJ 2302; JT 1996 (6) SC 54; 1997 (6) KarLJ 50

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, s. 300 - Murder - Proof - Evidence of eye-witness reliable and unshaken in cross-examination - Being resident of building in precincts of which incident took place, he was natural probable and disinterested witness - Evidence produced in defence wholly unreliable - Whether interference in conviction of accused required - Held, no interference required - Appeal dismissed.
