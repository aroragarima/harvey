Bhawanji Lakhamhi & Ors v Himatlal Jamnadas Dani & Ors

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 489; (1972) 1 SCC 388; AIR 1972 SC 819; 1972 RCJ 529; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 890

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 116 - Calcutta Thika Tenancy Act, 1949 - Tenancy - What constitute holding over - Held, if tenant asserts that landlord accepted the rent not as statutory tenant but only as legal rent (indicating his assent to the tenant's continuing in possession) it is for the tenant to establish it - And in case of failure to so establish it cannot be said that there was holding over by him - Appeal dismissed.
