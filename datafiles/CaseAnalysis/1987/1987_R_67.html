Inder Mohan Lal v Ramesh Khanna

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28222; (1987) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1987 SC 1986; 1987 (32) DLT 354; JT 1987 (3) SC 246; 1987 (2) RCJ 628; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 238; 1987(2) SCALE 196; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 765; 1987 (2) UJ 705

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 21 - Tenancy - Sanction for temporary lease - Validity of sanction, when conditions precedent existing and absence of collusion or fraud etc. - And need of registration - Held, sanction is valid - Further held,once sanction is granted there is no necessity to execute or register lease - Appeal allowed.
