R. Rajagopal v State Of T.N.

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 832; (1994) 6 SCC 632; AIR 1995 SC 264; AIR 1994 SCW 4420; JT 1994 (6) SC 514; 1994(4) SCALE 494; 1995 (2) SCJ 86; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 353; 1994 (Supp) ShimLC 428

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Media & Entertainment

Keywords: Freedom Of Press, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(a) and 21 - Freedom of Press - Right to privacy - Autobiography of condemned prisoner - Publication of - Held, permissible - Based on public records - None can publish anything concerning, without consent - Government cannot impose prior restraint.
