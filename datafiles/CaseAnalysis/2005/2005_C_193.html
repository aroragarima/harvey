Messrs Hindustan Zinc Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Jaipur

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 120; (2005) 2 SCC 662; AIR 2005 SC 1351; 2006 (2) CalLT(SC) 23; 2005 (181) E.L.T. 170; JT 2005 (2) SC 515; 2005 (67) RLT 1; 2005(2) SCALE 373; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 391

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Tariff Item

Summary: Central Excise Act, 1944 - Silver chloride - Whether intermediate product produced in manufacture of zinc in assessee's factory is marketable?; whether product is to be classified under tariff heading 28.43 if it is marketable? - Held, mathematical ratio between total quantity of silver chloride and silver content cannot establish marketability - Department failed to prove test of marketability - Appeal allowed.
