Union of India and Another v Pradeep Kumari and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1758; (1995) 2 SCC 736; AIR 1995 SC 2259; JT 1995 (9) SC 644; 1995 (1) RentLR 610; 1995(2) SCALE 260; 1995 (3) SCJ 203; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 703

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.4 - Punjab Reorganization Act, 1966 - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, ss. 18 to 28, 28-A - Notifications - Acquisition of land - Enhancement of compensation - Notifications issued u/s.4 of 1894 Act in respect of lands in District Kangra - After re-organization of State of Punjab by 1966 Act, lands fall in State of Himachal Pradesh - Proceedings for acquisition of land conducted in State of Himachal Pradesh - Awards made by Land Acquisition Collector - Award by Court was made after enactment of 1894 Act - By 1984 Act, s.28-A was introduced in Act - Respondent No.1 filed application u/s.28-A of Act claimed benefit of award by Court - Collector awarded additional amount of compensation - Writ petition filed by respondent no.2 allowed by HC - Civil Appeal filed by petitioners against judgment of HC dismissed - Hence this petition - Whether HC was right in extending benefit of enhanced compensation as well as enhanced rate of interest and solatium to respondents - Held, benefit of re-determination of amount of compensation u/s.28-A of Act availed of on basis of any one of awards made by court after coming into force of s.28-A of 1894 Act - Application filed u/s.28-A of 1984 Act within prescribed period of three months from making of award on basis of which re-determination was sought - After application submitted u/s.28-A(l) of Act for re-determination of amount-of compensation, process of such re-determination resulted in making of award by Collector - Person not accepted award move Collector to refer matter to Court for determination and such reference was governed by ss.18 to 28 of 1984 Act - HC entitled to direct payment of interest on excess amount awarded as compensation u/s.28-A of 1984 Act - Collector was not competent to award interest on additional amount of compensation under provision - Petition dismissed.
