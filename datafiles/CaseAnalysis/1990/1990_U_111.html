Scheduled Caste Co-Operative Land Owning Society Limited, Bhatinda v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jayachandra Reddy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 448; (1991) 1 SCC 174; AIR 1991 SC 730; 1990 (3) CCC 513; JT 1990 (4) SC 1; 1991 (1) PLR 324; 1990 RD 408; 1990(2) SCALE 557; 1990 (2) ShimLC 323; 1990 (2) UJ 703; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 1202

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 28 A and 23 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151 - Land Acquisition - (A) Applicability of s. 28 A of 1894 Act - Redetermination of compensation - Claimant seeking reference and also preferring appeal to High Court - (B) Compensation for land acquisition - Permission sought by claimants to pay deficit in court-fee to avail benefit of higher compensation - But restrict claim to certain amount - On awarding it applying for permission to pay deficit court-fee to seek higher compensation about six years after awarding decision - HC refused to grant permission under inherent jurisdiction - Held, section does not apply - Proper - Petition dismissed.
