Mohan Meakins Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Kochi

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 894; (2000) 1 SCC 462; AIR 2000 SC 1845; 2000 (88) ECR 5; 2000 (115) E.L.T. 3; JT 1999 (9) SC 635; 2000 (1) KLT 441; 2000 (36) RLT 124; 1999(7) SCALE 486; 1999 (10) SLT 526; 1999 (10) Supreme 191

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 111 - Recovery of duty - Subsequent proceedings u/s. 111 - Proceedings once u/s. 111 adjudicated u/s. 125 - Goods released on payment of redemption fine - Subsequent suit against bona fide purchaser of redeemed goods - Maintainability - Held, is unjust - Not maintainable.
