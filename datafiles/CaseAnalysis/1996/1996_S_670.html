Afzal and Another v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3086; (1996) 7 SCC 397; (1996) SCC (Cr) 424; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 537; AIR 1996 SC 2326; AIR 1996 SCW 824; 1996 (3) CCR 148; 1996 (1) Crimes 58; 1996 CRLJ 1679; JT 1996 (1) SC 328; 1996 (4) KarLJ 189; 1996(1) SCALE 340; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 573; 1996 (1) Supreme 397

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Wrongful Confinement

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 192 - Fabrication of false evidence by accused police officer who took delivery of draft of counter-affidavit from standing counsel for being signed by his superior for filing in Supreme Court - A police official asked to forge signature of his superior on carbon copy of counter-affidavit which he refused - On his refusal, his superior was contacted who forced official to forge his signature - Official acted accordingly - Accused sent carbon copy with others for filing it in Supreme Court - Said affidavit contained false averments - Accused is guilty of offence u/s. 193 - He abetted officer to forge signature of his superior.
