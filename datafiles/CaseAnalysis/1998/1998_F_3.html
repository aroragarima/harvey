Supreme Court of India

17 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, S.C. Agrawal, A.S. Anand, S.P. Bharucha, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 689; (1998) 4 SCC 1; (1998) SCC (L&S) 961; AIR 1998 SC 1767; 1998 (2) AWC 1300; JT 1998 (3) SC 223; 1998 LabIC 1582; 1998 (4) LLN 101; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 845; 1998 (3) SLJ 139

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 15(4), 16(4) and 16(4A) - Bombay Prohibition Act 1949 - Reservation - (A) If a single post can be reserved - (B) Determination of scope of art. 16(4A) of Constitution - Held, cannot be reserved, neither directly nor by device of rotation of roster points - It deals with reservation in promotional post and not with question of reservation in a single cadre post - Appeal allowed.
