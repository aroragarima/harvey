Messrs Hasanali Khanbhai and Sons and Others v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

26 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2008; (1995) 5 SCC 422; 1995 (26) ALR 362; 1995 (3) AWC 1785; JT 1995 (6) SC 92; 1995 (2) RentLR 385; 1995(4) SCALE 786; 1995 (3) SCJ 555; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 363

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4(1)- Whether deduction of 60% of the price determined to the lands under acquisition as developmental charges correct? - The land being far away from the outer municipal limits though situated near about the railway line itself would be a factor to be taken into consideration in deterring the market value along with a possibility of imposition of statutory restrictions to develop the lands for building purposes - Held, deduction of 60% justified - Appeal dismissed.
