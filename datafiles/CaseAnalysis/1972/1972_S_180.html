Gudar Dusadh v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 110; (1972) 3 SCC 118; (1972) SCC (Cr) 438; AIR 1972 SC 952; 1972 CRLJ 587; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 505

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300, cl. 3 and 302 - Murder - Death due to premeditated attack on deceased's head by giving lathi blow resulting in death - Injury sufficient in ordinary course of nature to cause death - Held, accused guilty of offence of murder - Appeal dismissed.
