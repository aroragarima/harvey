Indira Sawhney v Union Of Indian And Others

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1534; (1996) 6 SCC 506; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1477; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 133; AIR 1997 SC 597; 1996 (4) CLT 297; JT 1996 (10) SC 331; 1997 (1) LLN 136; 1996(8) SCALE 209; 1997 (1) SLR 38; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 369; 1996 (8) Supreme 105

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, arts.15 and 29 - Direction issued to specify bases to determine creamy layer amongst the 'other backward classes' - Its non-implementation by State of Kerala in inspite of various extensions given? - Held, request made to learned Chief Justice of the Kerala High Court to appoint a retired judge of the HC to be the Chairman of the High Level Committee who will induct not more than 4 members from various walks of life to identify the 'creamy layer' among the designated other backward classes in Kerala State in the light of the ruling of Apex Court in Mandal case - Directions issued.
