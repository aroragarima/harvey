Kapildeo Prasad Sah and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 671; (1999) 7 SCC 569; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1357; AIR 1999 SC 3215; JT 1999 (6) SC 216; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 725; 2000 (1) UPLBEC 264

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2 - Arrears of salary - Petition against termination of untrained teachers - Appointed on directions of courts against existing vacancies and not against existing those on 01-01-1992 - (A) claim for arrears of salary for period between 30-06-1991 (upto when salaries disbursed) and 04-10-1994 (appointed) - (B) whether initiation of contempt proceedings against State for civil contempt of Court is proper? - Held, claim not tenable, however in view of serious dispute as to existence of vacancy on 1992 matter remitted back to High Court - Not proper - Appeal disposed of.
