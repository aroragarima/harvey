R. B. Ramlingam v R.B. Bhvaneswari

Supreme Court of India

13 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, H.L. Dattu

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 65; 2009 (3) AWC 2443; 2009(2) SCALE 108; [2009] 1 S.C.R. 301; 2009 (1) UC 415

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963 - Held, in each and every case the Court has to examine whether delay in filing the special leave petition stands properly explained - This is the basic test which needs to be applied - The true guide is whether the petitioner has acted with reasonable diligence in the prosecution of his appeal/petition - It cannot be stated as a proposition per se that the prosecution of Review Proceedings would not be a sufficient cause at all for purposes of s. 5 - Order accordingly.
