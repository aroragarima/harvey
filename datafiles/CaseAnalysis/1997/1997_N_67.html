Commissioner of Income Tax v Nirbheram Daluram

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2833; (1997) 10 SCC 373; AIR 1997 SC 1971; 1997 (139) CTR 484; [1997] 224 ITR 610; JT 1997 (3) SC 688; [1997] 91 TAXMAN 181; 1997 (2) TLR 564

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 251(1)(a) - Scope of power of Appellate Asst. Commr. - Held, new source of income which did not come under subject matter of ITO could be considered by Appellate Asst. Commr. - Answered in favour of Revenue.
