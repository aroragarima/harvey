Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Constitution - Writ petition u/art. 226 - Quasi-judicial authority - Inflicted penalty without show causes notice - Rules of natural justice, violation of - Board - Candidate allowed to appear at Intermediate Examination in spite to of shortage in lectures - Examination cancelled - Board - Opportunity to show cause not given by -Held, Order is vitiated - Appeal Dismissed.
