Karam Singh v State of Punjab and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 389; (1996) SCC (L&S) 668; (1996) 7 SCC 748; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 170; AIR 1996 SC 3159; 1996 (33) ATC 361; 1996 (1) CCR 183; 1996 (2) CLR 4; 1996 (72) FLR 765; JT 1996 (2) SC 382; 1996 (4) KarLJ 591; 1996 LabIC 1272; 1996(2) SCALE 211; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 1030; 1996 (1) SLR 795; 1996 (2) Supreme 286; 1996 (1) UJ 727

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Reinstatement - Conviction of member of disciplined Police Force u/s. 323 of Indian Penal Code - Amounts to disqualification even though he was released on probation - Would he be entitled to reinstatement? - Held, he would not be entitled to reinstatement - His penalty of dismissal from service, however, altered to one of removal from service - Petition dismissed.
