Osmania University v V. S. Muthurangam and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2160; (1997) 10 SCC 741; AIR 1997 SC 2758; JT 1997 (5) SC 736; 1997 LabIC 2849; 1997(4) SCALE 416; 1997 (2) SCJ 408; 1997 (4) SLR 597; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 499; 1997 (6) Supreme 264; 1997 (2) UJ 402

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Education Norms

Summary: Education - Osmania University Act, 1959, s. 38(1) - University - Uniformity in service conditions u/s. 38 of Act - Retirement age of teaching staff raised to 60 years in view of recommendations of University Grants Commission - And there is no compulsion to maintain retrial age of non-teaching staff of 58 years - If bringing uniformity in age of retirement is impracticable or undesirable - Held, cannot be said so - Retrial age of non-teaching staff should be raised to 60 years - Appeal dismissed.
