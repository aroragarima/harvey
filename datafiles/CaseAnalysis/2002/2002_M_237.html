Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Bihar Building (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1982

Summary: Bihar Buildings (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1982 - S. 11(1)(c) - decree for eviction - revision u/s. 14(8) allowed by High Court and decree of trial court set aside - appeal - Held, once it has been proved by a landlord that the suit accommodation is required bona fide by him for his own purpose and such satisfaction withstands the test of objective assessment by the Court of facts then choosing of the accommodation which would be reasonable to satisfy such requirement has to be left to the subjective choice of the needy. The Court cannot thrust upon its own choice on the needy. - Further held, the judgment of the trial Court is a detailed and exhaustive judgment which has taken into consideration each and every available piece of evidence and relevant circumstances, assessed with objectivity, consistently with the relevant principles of law and hence the finding is one which could not have been upset by High Court in exercise of its revisional jurisdiction.
