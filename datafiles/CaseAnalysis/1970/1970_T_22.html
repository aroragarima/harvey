The Commissioner Of Gift-Tax, Kerala v Dr. George Kuruvilla

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 63; (1970) 2 SCC 29; AIR 1970 SC 1535; [1970] 77 ITR 746; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 373

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Gift Tax Act, 1958, s. 5(1)(xiv) - Availabiliy of exemption from gift tax - For that gift must be in the course of carrying on business etc. - And for bona fide - But if made by person carrying on business or the property gifted is used for purpose for which it was used by donor, then it is not exempted - Gift by medical practitioner to his son out of love and affection - Held, not exempt - Appeal Allowed.
