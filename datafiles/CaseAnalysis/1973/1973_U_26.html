Parmeshwari Prasad Gupta v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 510; (1973) 2 SCC 543; AIR 1973 SC 2389; [1974] 44 Comp Cas 1; 1973 (2) CTR 396; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 304; 1974 SLJ 486; 1973 TAXLR 2449; 1973 UJ 794

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 286 and 290 - Case before Act come into force - Notice of meeting of Board of Directors - Not given to one director - Validity of resolution passed at meeting - Held, resolution is invalid - But resolution can be validated by regularly constituted meeting - Appeal dismissed.
