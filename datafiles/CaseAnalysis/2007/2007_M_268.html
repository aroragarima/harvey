Hardesh Ores Private Limited and Others v Messrs Hede and Company

Supreme Court of India

15 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, H.S. Bedi

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 674; (2007) 5 SCC 614; 2007 (4) ALD(SC) 89; 2007 (68) ALR 481; 2008 (2) Bom.C.R. 484; JT 2007 (7) SC 150; 2007 (5) MLJ(SC) 187; 2008 (1) MPLJ 30; 2007 (3) RCR(Civil) 157; 2007 (103) RD 371; 2007(7) SCALE 348; 2007 (5) SCJ 677; [2007] 6 S.C.R. 608

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, O. 7 r. 11 - Suit for specific performance of the contract dismissed as time barred - Appeal against - Held, renewal of an agreement or lease requires execution of a document in accordance with law evidencing the renewal - Appellant-plaintiff did exercise their option and claimed renewal. The respondents denied their right to claim renewal in express terms and also unequivocally stated that the agreement did not stand renewed - A cause of action accrued to the appellant- plaintiff when their right of renewal was denied by the respondents - Claim would have been barred by limitation since more than 3 years had elapsed after a categoric denial of their right claiming renewal or automatic renewal by the respondents- defendants - Appeals dismissed.
