Sadhu Singh Ghuman v Food Corporation Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 703; (1990) 2 SCC 68; AIR 1990 SC 893; 1990 (16) ALR 343; 1990 (1) ARBLR 140; 1990 (1) CCC 570; JT 1990 (1) SC 300; 1990 (1) PLR 693; 1990(1) SCALE 236; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 353; 1990 (1) UJ 627

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Stay of suit - Expression step in proceeding - Is not every step taken by him in suit - Whether a step in proceeding would disentitle party from going for arbitration? - Held, right of parties to go for arbitration should not be withheld on technical grounds - Court must go into circumstances and intention of party in step taken.
