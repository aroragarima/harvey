Supreme Court of India

03 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 509; (1998) 3 SCC 88; (1998) SCC (L&S) 730; AIR 1998 SC 1153; 1998 (2) AWC 1111; JT 1998 (1) SC 470; 1998 LabIC 1041; 1998 (2) LLN 99; 1998(1) SCALE 347; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 470; 1998 (2) SLJ 178; 1998 (1) SLR 803; 1998 (3) SLT 75; 1998 (3) Supreme 50

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226, art.16, H.P. University Act, 1970, s.38, s.40, H.P. University Constitution of India, Arts.226 and16; H.P. University Act, 1970, ss.38 and 40; H.P. University Statutes, Statute 11; H.P. University Ordinances, Ordinances 35.8 and 35.11 - Appointment - Teaching post - Mode - Evaluators and Research associates - Performing duties of teacher for long time on ad-hoc basis - Regularisation of services by University on principles of equity, justice and fair play - Held, selection of teacher - Selection of teacher has not to be on minimum eligibility but best available from a larger sphere - And not from the limited sphere of ad hoc or stop gap appointees - Mode of Appointment deprecated.
