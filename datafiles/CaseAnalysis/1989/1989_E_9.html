Rubber House v Excellsior Needle Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 704; (1989) 2 SCC 413; AIR 1989 SC 1160; JT 1989 (1) SC 488; 1989 (1) RCJ 354; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 530; 1989 (1) RentLR 476; 1989(1) SCALE 572; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 986; 1989 (2) UJ 71

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Rules 1976

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, ss. 4, 13, 13(2)(i), Proviso and 23 - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Rules, 1976, rr. 4(c), 5(1) and 6 - Deposit of arrears of rent within 15 days of first hearing - (A) Tenant not depositing full and valid rent due but only part of it - (B) Is it a duty of Rent Controller to determine and calculate arrears of rent - (C) Objection that eviction application cannot be dismissed for non-mention of amount of arrears of rent - Raised for first time before High Court - Maintainability - Held, (a) Deposit not valid - (B) No duty is cast upon - (C) Not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
