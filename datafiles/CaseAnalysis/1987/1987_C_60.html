Lathia Industrial Supplies Company Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise Baroda

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28659; (1987) 3 SCC 557; 1987 (13) ECC 274; 1987 (13) ECR 278; 1987 (29) E.L.T. 751

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 2(f) - Manufacture - Whether re-rubberising and re-lining of old and used vessels amounts to manufacture? - Held, no - Appellant shall be entitled to consequential benefits in matter of refund of duties already collected - Appeal allowed.
