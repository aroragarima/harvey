U O I v K.D. Pandey and Anr

Supreme Court of India

08 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 1076; (2002) 10 SCC 471; (2003) SCC (L&S) 791; 2003 (3) LLJ 557

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Railway Servants(Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1968

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Railway Servants (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1968, r. 25 - Dismissal respondent No. 1 - Industrial Tribunal set aside directed reinstatement of respondent No. 1 with back wages - High Court affirmed the decision of Industrial Tribunal - Challenged - Held, inquiry done in abuse of process of law - HC and Tribunal judgment of reinstatement is just and proper - Appeal dismissed.
