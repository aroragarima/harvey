Jasbir Rani and Others v State of Punjab and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20404; (2002) SCC (L&S) 107; (2002) 1 SCC 124; AIR 2002 SC 60; 2001 (3) CLR 1044; 2001 (91) FLR 1220; JT 2001 (9) SC 351; 2002 (35) LabIC 102; 2002 (1) LLN 682; 2001(7) SCALE 422; 2002 (1) SLJ 329; 2002 (1) SLR 124; 2001 (7) SLT 619; [2001] Supp4 S.C.R. 494; 2001 (8) Supreme 84

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate

Keywords: Punjab Panchayat Secretaries (Recruitment and Conditions of Services) Rules, 1979, Punjab Panchayat Secretaries (Recruitment and Conditions of Services) (First Amendment) Rules, 1993

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 - Punjab Panchayat Secretaries (Recruitment and Conditions of Services) (First Amendment) Rules, 1993 - Punjab Panchayat Secretaries (Recruitment and Conditions of Services) Rules, 1979, r. 5 - Whether there was any rational basis for fixing the same cut-off date in second advertisement as in first advertisement for acquiring qualifications for concerned post? - Held, appointing authority while issuing second advertisement fixed same cut-off date as in first - If this had not been done then there would have been a difference in date by which eligibility qualification pertaining to age was to be complied by male and female candidates - Such action would have exposed authorities to criticism of discrimination - Appeal dismissed.
