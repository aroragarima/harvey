H. B. Gandhi, Excise and Taxation v Messrs Gopi Nath and Sons and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 300; (1992) Supp2 SCC 312; 1990 (77) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, s. 39(5) - Constitution - Whether High Court erred in holding that transactions involved in this case did not constitute sales and were mere transactions of service? - HC was in error in constituting itself into a court of appeal against assessment - It was not open to HC to re-appreciate primary or perceptive facts which have otherwise within domain of fact-finding authority under statute - Judicial review cannot extend to examination of correctness or reasonableness of a decision as a matter of fact - Held, order of HC cannot be allowed to remain undisturbed - Appeal allowed.
