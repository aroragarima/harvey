District Collector Chittoor And Ors. v Chittoor District Groundnut Tradersassociation And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 403; (1989) 2 SCC 58; AIR 1989 SC 989; JT 1989 (1) SC 139; 1989(1) SCALE 146; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 243; 1989 (1) UJ 445

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Essential commodities, Fair Price

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, ss. 3(1)(2)(a), 3(1)(2)(c), 3(1)(2)(d), 3(1)(2)(f) and 5 - Central Govt. Notification No. GSR 800, D/- 0906-1978, u/s. 5 - A P Scheduled Commodities Dealers (Licensing and Distribution) Order, 1982, cl. 12 - State Govt's Orders, D/- 21-06-1985 and 04-12-1985 - Groundnut seed and oil - Export of - To outside State - Imposition of restriction on - Directions for compulsory levy at specified price - Legality - Held, illegal - Appeal dismissed.
