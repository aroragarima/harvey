Kishandas Kanhaiyalal Gandhi v State of Maharashtra and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1408; (1995) Supp2 SCC 3; AIR 1996 SC 185; JT 1997 (10) SC 440; 1995(2) SCALE 259; 1995 (3) SCJ 233; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 423; 1995 (1) UJ 733

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Maharashtra Agricultural Land (Celling on holdings) Act, 1961

Summary: Land & Property - Maharashtra Agricultural Land (Celling on holdings) Act, 1961, s. 5 - Determination of ceiling area - Land owner filed claim statement as to disputed land - Land in possession of tenant - Orders passed in favour of tenant in proceeding (State not made party ) under Tenancy Act become final - Held, land in possession of tenant cannot be excluded from ownership or holding of land owner nor liberty can be given to surrender land - Appeal dismissed.
