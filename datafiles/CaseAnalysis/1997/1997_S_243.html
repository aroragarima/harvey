Pepsi Foods Limited and Another v Special Judicial Magistrate and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 290; (1998) 5 SCC 749; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1400; AIR 1998 SC 128; 1997 ALJ 2406; 1998 (5) Bom.C.R. 426; 1997 (4) CCR 108; 2001 (3) CompLJ 340; 1997 (4) Crimes 212; 1998 CRLJ 1; 1997 (5) CTJ 889; JT 1997 (8) SC 705; 1997(6) SCALE 610; 1998 (6) SLT 102; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 12; 1997 (9) Supreme 279

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal Penal Code, 1974, s. 482 - Complaint - Quashing of - Powers of High Court - Complaint filed u/s. 7/16 of Prevention of Food Adulteration Act - Complainant alleging about adulteration in bottle of beverage sold under brand 'Lehar Pepsi' - Accused neither manufacturer nor holding licence for manufacture of offending beverage - Held, complaint and preliminary evidence making out no case against accused - Complaint liable to be quashed.
