Messrs World Wide Agencies Private Limited v Mrs. Margarat T. Desor And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 348; (1990) 1 SCC 536; AIR 1990 SC 737; [1990] 67 Comp Cas 607; 1990 (1) CompLJ 208; 1990 (40) DLT 430; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 413; 1989(2) SCALE 1473; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 545; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 545; 1990 (1) UJ 448

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 397, 398 and 433(f) - (A) Precedents - Obiter - Point specifically raised and decided - Value - (B) Whether decision of English Courts binding in Indian Courts - Company - Oppression and mismanagement - Petition for removal - Locus standi - (C) Whether LRs. of deceased member are entitled to petition u/ss. 397 and 398 - (D) Joint petition for removal of oppression and mismanagement and winding up - Maintainabilitry - Held, (A) It is obiter for decision of case - (B) Not binding - But observation and reasoning on a pari materia provision of English Act would be valuable guide - (C) LRs. of member whose name is still on register are entitled - (D) A composite petition u/ss. 397, 398 and 433(f) is maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
