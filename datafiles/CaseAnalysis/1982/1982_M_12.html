State of Uttar Pradesh v Major Jitendra Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Amarendra Nath Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 256; (1982) 2 SCC 382; AIR 1982 SC 876; 1982 ALJ 361; 1982(1) SCALE 873

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23 and 54 - Appeal for enhancement of compensation for land acquisition - Powers of Appellate Court to enhance - High Court enhanced it on the basis of sale deed of a date three years later than the Notification u/s.4 of the Act - And when no material produced to suggest any fluctuation in the market rate - Held, no error committed by the HC in relying upon the sale deed - Appeal Dismissed.
