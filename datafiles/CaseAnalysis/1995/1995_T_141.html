U. P. Rashtriya Chini Milladhikari Parishad,Lucknow v The State Of U. P. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 22; (1995) 4 SCC 738; AIR 1995 SC 2148; 1995 ALJ 1599; 1995 (26) ALR 444; 1999 (109) E.L.T. 5; JT 1995 (5) SC 474; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 93; 1995(4) SCALE 265; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 733

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Civil Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Question regarding territorial jurisdiction - Govt. issued notification for conducting sale of sugar mills at Lucknow - Five out of six sugar mills were situated outside Oudh area - Cause of action arose at Lucknow which is within area of Oudh - Therefore, Lucknow Bench has jurisdiction to entertain writ petition - Appeal allowed.
