Gujarat Bottling Company Limited and others v Coca Cola Company and others

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1001; (1995) 5 SCC 545; AIR 1995 SC 2372; AIR 1995 SCW 3521; 1995 (2) ARBLR 249; [1995] 84 Comp Cas 618; 1995 (4) CompLJ 417; JT 1995 (6) SC 3; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 15; 1996 (16) PTC 89; 1995(4) SCALE 635; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 514; 1995 (1) UJ 698

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Intellectual Property; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Unfair Competition

Summary: Indian Contract Act, 1872, ss. 10, 27 and 62 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 42 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 39, rr. 1 and 2 - Multi National Corporation and its Indian Franchise - Novation of Agreement - Licensed to use trade mark - Second agreement - All terms except exercise of right to franchise present in new agreement - (A) Whether subsequent agreement wiped out terms and conditions in previous agreement - (B) Whether Condition restricting franchisee to deal in competing goods be regarded as restraint of trade? - (C) Whether clause restricting transfer of shares in franchisee company without consent of franchiser - Binding on Shareholders - (D) Whether Injunction to enforce negative stipulation - Can be refused - Held, subsequent agreement did not wipe out previous terms and conditions - Such restricting conditions cannot be regarded as in restraint of trade - Shareholders of franchisee not party to agreement - Clause would not bind shareholders - Courts in India, not bound to grant an injunction in every case.
