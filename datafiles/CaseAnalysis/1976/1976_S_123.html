Ram Rattan And Ors. v State Of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 282; (1977) SCC (Cr) 85; (1977) 1 SCC 188; AIR 1977 SC 619; 1977 CRLJ 433; 1977 CrLR(SC) 11; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 232

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, ss. 34, 97, 99, 302, 323, 324 and 326 - (A) When right of private defence of property against trespasser is not available even to true owner -Example - (B) Prosecution - Individual act of main accused causing grievous injuries to deceased -Other accused not having common intention of grievious hurt but that of simple hurt with biro and lathi - Held,if the trespasser has been successful in accomplishing his possession to the knowledge of the true owner - Other accused held liable u/ss. 324/34 and 323/34 - Appeal Partly Allowed.
