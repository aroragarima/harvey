Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras v South Arcot District Co-operative Marketing Society Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 726; AIR 1990 SC 1249; 1989 (80) CTR 83; [1989] 176 ITR 117; [1989] 43 TAXMAN 328; 1990 (2) TLR 389

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 14(3)(iv) - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80P(2)(e) - Co-operative society - Deduction in income-operative society storing goods of State Govt. in its godowns - Society also doing service of transporting goods to godowns - Whether deductable? - Held, service rendered by assessee was only incidental and it was entitled to deduction - Appeal dismissed.
