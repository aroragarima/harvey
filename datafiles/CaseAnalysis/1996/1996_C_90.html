Ramesh Singh & Another v Cinta Devi & Others

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 593; (1996) 3 SCC 142; (1996) SCC (Cr) 467; 1996 (2) ACC 701; 1996 ACJ 730; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 376; AIR 1996 SC 1560; AIR 1996 SCW 1661; 1996 (3) AWC 1408; 1996 (3) CCC 48; 1996 (66) ECR 10; JT 1996 (5) SC 543; 1996(2) SCALE 782; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1036; 1996 (5) Supreme 153; 1996 (2) UJ 61

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s.173, Proviso, s. 217(4) - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 110D - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Application under old Act disposed of after new Act came into effect but right of appeal under old Act still survives - Whether appellant entitled to file appeal without depositing amount - Held, entitled - Appeal allowed.
