J.A. Naiksatam v Prothonotary and Senior Master, High Court of

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 877; (2004) 8 SCC 653; (2004) SCC (L&S) 1190; 2004 (4) AWC 3738; 2005 (5) Bom.C.R. 476; 2004 (5) ESC 45; 2004(8) SCALE 794; 2004 (6) SLR 377; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 287

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Bombay High Court (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, r. 8 - Dismissal from service - Illegal gratification - Held, appellants were given a copy of tentative decision of disciplinary authority and appellants furnished detailed explanation and principles of natural justice have been fully complied with - No interference with findings arrived at by HC as HC considered all aspects before imposing such a penalty on appellants - Appeals dismissed.
