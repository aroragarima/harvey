Government of India and Others v Indian Tobacco Association

Supreme Court of India

23 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 483; (2005) 7 SCC 396; 2005 (187) E.L.T. 162; JT 2005 (7) SC 568; 2005(6) SCALE 683; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 859

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Export And Import Policy

Summary: Customs Tariff Act - Duty Entitlement Pass Book Scheme - Interpretation of expression 'substitute' - Held, registration at inland container depot was to remain valid for a period of 12 months only and in that view of matter too, it cannot be said that Central Govt. intended to deprive Respondents who were agriculturists from benefit of aforementioned notification dated 7/4/1997 only for a limited period, viz, between 7/4/1997 and 27/11/1997 - Appeal dismissed.
