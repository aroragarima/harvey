Mohan Pandey And Another v Smt. Usha Rani Rajgaria And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 793; (1992) 4 SCC 61; AIR 1993 SC 1225; JT 1992 (4) SC 572; 1993 (2) RCJ 90; 1992 (2) RCR(Rent) 431; 1992 (2) RentLR 591; 1992(2) SCALE 220; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 904; 1992 (2) UJ 591

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Dispute between two private persons relating to immovable property - Eviction suit directly covering property in question pending in Civil Court - Writ petition for restraining respondents from disturbing lawful possession of petitioner alleging some complaints made by respondents and action taken by police thereon - Whether maintainable? - Alternative remedy - Held, not maintainable - Remedy for such matter is civil suit or under Criminal Procedure Code against alleged criminal complaints - Appeal allowed.
