Union of India and Others v Messrs Rai Bahadur Shree Ram Durga Prasad Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, R.S. Bachawat, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 356; (1969) 1 SCC 91; AIR 1970 SC 1597; [1971] 41 Comp Cas 864; 1985 ECR 2260; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 113; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 727

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Adjudged

Summary: SERA, 1947, Pre., ss. 12(1), 22, 23 and 23A - Foreign Exchange Regulation Rules, 1952, rr. 3 and 5 - Constitution - Sea Customs Act, 1878 , ss. 19 and 167(8) - (A) Meaning of expression 'restriction imposed by s.12(1) - Export of manganese ore - Exporter making declaration in form prescribed by r. 3 supported by evidence required by r. 5 - Invoice value not representing full export value - Legality - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - FERA, 1947 enacted in interest of national economy - Penal provision of s.12 - Rule of interpretation - Held, it does not constitute contravention of s. 12(1) and also not punishable u/s. 23A r/w s. 167 (8) 1878 Act - Court must construe it as it would construe any other instrument - One hearing fee imposed - Appeals dismissed.
