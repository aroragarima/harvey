Herbertsons Limited v Workmen Of Herbertsons Limited And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 208; (1976) 4 SCC 736; (1977) SCC (L&S) 48; AIR 1977 SC 322; 1976 (33) FLR 398; 1977 LabIC 162; 1977 (1) LLN 24; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 15

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Neutralisation

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 18(1) - Industrial Dispute - Settlement - By recognised union of majority of workers - While pendency of appeal before Supreme Court - Settlement whether just, fair and binding on workers - Matters to be considered by Tribunal in deciding issue sent to it - Matters to be considered by Tribunal - Interference by Supreme Court with finding - Order Accordingly.
