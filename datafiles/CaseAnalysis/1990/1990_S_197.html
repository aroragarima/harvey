J.K. Cotton Spg. And Wvg Mills Co. Limited,Kalpi Road, Kanpur v State Of U.P. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 357; (1990) 4 SCC 27; (1990) SCC (L&S) 570; AIR 1990 SC 1808; 1990 (61) FLR 329; JT 1990 (3) SC 300; 1990 LabIC 1511; 1991 (1) LLJ 39; 1990 (2) LLN 682; 1990(2) SCALE 153; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 523; 1990 (3) SLJ 105; 1990 (5) SLR 642; 1990 (2) UJ 438; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 1066

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(ss), 6N - Industrial Disputes Act 1947, ss. 2(oo), 25F - Voluntary retirement - Resignation - Employee voluntarily tendering resignation - Termination of service by employer by accepting resignation - Whether amounts to 'retrenchment'? - Held, not retrenchment - Case is covered by 'voluntary retirement' u/s. 2(s) - Appeal allowed.
