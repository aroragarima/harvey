Bhagwan Dass Chopra v United Bank Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28898; (1987) Supp SCC 536; AIR 1988 SC 215; 1987 (55) FLR 909; JT 1987 (4) SC 373; 1988 (1) LLJ 427; 1988 (1) LLN 933; 1987(2) SCALE 1107; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1088; 1989 (1) SLJ 92; 1988 (1) UJ 327

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Corporate - Labour and Industrial Law - CPC, 1908, O. 22, r. 10 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, 10, 18 - Takeover of all the rights and liabilities under the agreement of merger - Impleadment of acquiring party while pendency of reference - Not entitled to reopen proceedings as a matter of course - Steps already binding thereon - Held, cannot recall and cross-examine witnesses when evidence was closed before its being impleaded - Appeal allowed.
