Comissioner Of Customs, Chennai v Jayathi Krishna & Co

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3509; (2000) 9 SCC 402; 2000 (91) ECR 768; 2000 (119) E.L.T. 4; 2000 (39) RLT 126

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Deec Scheme, Warehousing Period

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, s. 61(2) - Warehoused goods - Respondent obtained the benefit of the DEEC scheme - DEEC scheme having been made applicable to the goods in question, the question of payment of interest could not arise at all - Tribunal relied upon a decision in Pratibha Processors[1996 Indlaw SC 1849] - Interest on warehousing goods is merely an accessory to the principal - Interest u/s. 61(2) has no independent or separate existence - Appeal dismissed.
