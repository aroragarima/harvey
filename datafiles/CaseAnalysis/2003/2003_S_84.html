General Manager, Kisan Sahkari Chini Mills Limited, Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh v Satrughan Nishad and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 834; (2003) 8 SCC 639; AIR 2003 SC 4531; 2003 (6) AWC 5254; 2003 (3) CLR 972; 2004 (1) ESC 142; 2003 (99) FLR 870; JT 2003 (8) SC 235; 2003 (4) KHCACJ 578; 2003 (3) LLJ 1108; 2003(8) SCALE 343; 2003 (1) SCW 5291; 2004 (1) SLJ 145; 2004 (1) SLR 361; 2003 (6) SLT 435; [2003] Supp4 S.C.R. 349; 2003 (7) Supreme 250; 2004 (1) UPLBEC 7

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - s. 6N - Constitution of India, 1950 - art. 12 - Invoking writ jurisdiction of High Court - Disputed questions of fact regarding the period of working and nature of employment of the workmen - Held, these are disputed questions of facts which cannot be decided in writ jurisdiction and the same can be decided by the courts constituted under the provisions of the Act. The High Court was not justified in entertaining the writ applications.
