Haryana Waqf Board v Shanti Sarup and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 July 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, J.M. Panchal

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1085; (2008) 8 SCC 671; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 616; 2008 (6) AWC 5514B; 2008(10) SCALE 276

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Suit for declaration and injunction - Second appeal had summarily dismissed the appeal on the ground that the second appeal was concluded by the concurrent findings of fact arrived at by the courts below - Appeal against - Held, High Court ought to have considered whether in view of the nature of dispute and in the facts of the present case, whether the Local Commissioner should be appointed for the purpose of demarcation in respect of the suit land - Appeal allowed.
