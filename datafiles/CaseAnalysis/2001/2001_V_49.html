State of Kerala v Vattukalam Chemicals Industries

Supreme Court of India

23 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20008; (2001) 6 SCC 764; AIR 2001 SC 3330; JT 2001 (7) SC 144; 2001 (3) KLT 153; 2001(5) SCALE 409; 2001 (6) SLT 62; 2001 (124) STC 233; 2001 (6) Supreme 319

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Last Purchase

Summary: Sales Tax - Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963 - Notification no 499/90 - Whether exemption given by notification no 499/90 is available to copper scrap purchased by assessee? - Copper scrap as a description of goods is not taxable under act at point of last purchase in state - Held, benefit of notification does not extend to copper scrap purchased by respondent - Appeal allowed.
