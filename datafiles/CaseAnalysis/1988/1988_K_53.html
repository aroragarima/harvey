Collector Of Central Excise, Bombay-Ii v Kiran Spinning Mills, Kolshet Road, Thane

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 864; (1988) 2 SCC 348; AIR 1988 SC 871; 1988 CRLR 201; 1988 CrLR(SC) 201; 1988 (16) ECC 3; 1988 (16) ECR 115; 1988 (34) E.L.T. 5; JT 1988 (1) SC 369; 1988(1) SCALE 356; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 1006; 1988 (69) STC 226

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: All India Services (Death-Cum-Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, s. 35 L(b) - Polyester fibre tow utilised in manufacture of polyester staple fibre - Cutting tow into short length produces staple fibre - Tribunal held that it was not taxable - Held, since there is no manufacture involved it is not liable to tax.
