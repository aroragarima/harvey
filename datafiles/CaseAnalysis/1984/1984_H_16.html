Workmen Employed By Hindustan Lever Limited v Hindustan Lever Limited

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 197; (1984) 4 SCC 392; (1985) SCC (L&S) 6; AIR 1984 SC 1683; 1985 (1) Bom.C.R. 150; 1984 (49) FLR 364; 1984 LabIC 1573; 1984 (2) LLJ 391; 1984 (2) LLN 751; 1984(2) SCALE 265; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 641; 1984 (2) SLJ 364; 1985 (2) UJ 200

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(k), 7A, 10, sch. 3, item 7 - Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946, s. 3(2), sch., item 1 - Industrial Dispute, if - Demand for confirming employees in acting capacity in a grade - Held, an industrial dispute - Demand, not for promotion and not beyond Tribunal's adjudication - Appeal allowed.
