Madhyamic Shiksha Mandal, M.P v Abhilash Shiksha Prasar Samiti and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2290; (1998) 9 SCC 236; JT 1997 (10) SC 363; 1998 (5) Supreme 306

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Decision by Board - Unfair means in examination - Whether High Court was justified in interfering with decision of board where unfair means were used in examinations? - Held, in a situation of use of unfair means Board is left with no alternative but to cancel examination as it is difficult to identify innocent students - This was not a case in which HC should have interfered - Appeal allowed.
