Commissioner of Income Tax v Smt. R. Sharadamma

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2664; (1996) 8 SCC 388; AIR 1996 SC 3199; 1996 (133) CTR 136; [1996] 219 ITR 671; JT 1996 (4) SC 90; 1996(3) SCALE 343B; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 1200; [1996] 85 TAXMAN 679

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 256 and 274(2) - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Deletion of s. 274(2) of 1961 Act by Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act (1970), during pendency of penalty proceedings before Inspecting Asst. Commr. and its effect thereon - Held, Inspecting Asst. Commr. did not lose jurisdiction by virtue of deletion of sub-s. (2) of s. 274 by Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1970 to continue with pending proceedings - Appeal allowed.
