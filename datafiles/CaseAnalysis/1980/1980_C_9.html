Dr. J. P. Kulshreshtha And Ors. v Chancellor, Allahabad University, Raj Bhawan and

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.D. Koshal, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 309; (1980) 3 SCC 418; (1980) SCC (L&S) 436; AIR 1980 SC 2141; 1980 ALJ 571; 1980 (41) FLR 318; 1980 LabIC 692; 1980 (2) LLJ 175; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 902; 1980 SLJ 623

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Ordinances, Allahabad University Act, 1921

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 226 - Allahabad university Act, 1921, s. 32 (2)(f) - Ordinance, O. 9 (2) (i) - Writs against Universities - Powers of administrative body i.e. appointment of Readers - Validity of appointment of candidates obtaining marks less than 54% - Held, illegal as unequal treatment among equals , selection violative of art.14 of Constitution - Appeal allowed .
