State of Uttar Pradesh v Mohammad Musheer Khan and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 315; (1977) 3 SCC 562; (1977) SCC (Cr) 565; AIR 1977 SC 2226; 1977 CRLJ 1897; 1977 CRLR 406; 1977 UJ 546

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution, art. 136 - CrPC, 1898, s. 423 - Trial for offence u/s. 302 - Conviction of one of the accused u/s.302 and sentenced to death - While two other accused convicted u/s.302/34 and sentenced to life imprisonment - High Court acquittal all accused on plea of self defence - Legality - Held, first accuse was alone guilty of murder and the other two must be given benefit of doubt - Appeal Dismissed.
