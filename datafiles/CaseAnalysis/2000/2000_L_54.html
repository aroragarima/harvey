Union of India and Others v Lt. General Rajendra Singh Kadyan and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2852; (2000) 6 SCC 698; (2000) SCC (L&S) 797; AIR 2000 SC 2513; AIR 2000 SCW 2692; JT 2000 (8) SC 276; 2000 (33) LabIC 2664; 2000 (4) LLN 3; 2000(5) SCALE 327; 2000 (5) SLR 228; 2000 (5) SLT 817; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 722; 2000 (5) Supreme 281

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Appointment - Eligibility - Army Commander - Should be commanded corps for at least one year - Waiver of six months - In case of respondent officer - Plea, waiver was granted for considering respondent to post of Army Commander - Falling short of requisite period - Held, appointment cannot be made on basis of seniority alone determined by appraisal report or annual confidential report - Plea, not tenable.
