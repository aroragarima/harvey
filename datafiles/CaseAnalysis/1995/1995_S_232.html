K. Posayya and Others v Special Tahsildar

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1293; (1995) 5 SCC 233; AIR 1995 SC 1641; 1995 (2) CCC 631; JT 1995 (5) SC 174; 1995 (2) MLJ 72; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 72; 1995(3) SCALE 683; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 342

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of market value for land acquisition copmensation - (A) Validity of refusal to place reliance on sale deed of date after notification and possession - (B) Applicablity of doctrine of reinstatement value - (C) Failure of court to apply arm chair test of prudent purchaser and willing vendor or test of realised income on crops - Legality - Held, was proper, as it was a brought up document to inflate market value of lands - Not applicable - Illegal - Appeal dismissed.
