State Of Orissa And Ors. v Mahanadi Coalfields Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, A.M. Ahmadi, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 429; (1995) Supp2 SCC 686; AIR 1995 SC 1868; JT 1995 (3) SC 662; 1995(2) SCALE 900; 1995 (2) SCJ 636

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Mines & Minerals; Parliament & Legislature

Keywords: Coal Bearing Areas (Acquisition And Development) Amendment Act, 1957, Orissa Cess Act, 1962

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Mines and Minerals - Constitution, art. 245 - Orissa Rural Employment, Education and Production Act, 1992, s. 3 - Validity of levy of tax u/s. 3 - Held, levy of tax on 'mineral bearing land' and 'Coal bearing land' u/s. 3 is not within competence of State Legislature and is ultra vires of art. 245 - Appeal allowed.
