Â M/S. Ram Narain Sons Ltd v Asst. Commissioner Of Sales Tax And Others(And Other Cases)

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Syed Jaffer Imam, N. Chandrasekhara Aiyar, B. Jagannadhadas

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 55; AIR 1955 SC 765; 1955 (2) MLJ(SC) 302; 1955 SCJ 808; [1955] 2 S.C.R. 483; 1955 (6) STC 627

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Sales Tax Authorities

Summary: Sales Tax - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 286 (1) (a), expln and art. 286(2), Proviso - Central Province and Berar Sales Tax Act, 1947, s. 2(g), expln II - Sale Tax Continuance Order, 1950 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 - Composite pre and post Constitution assessment, if invalid in toto - Composite assessment of tax for pre-Constitution and post-Constitution period - Whether state in which goods manufactured (MP) can recover sales tax on goods delivered for consumption in another state (UP) - Held, assessment invalid in toto - Explanation to art. 286(1((a) determined State of Uttar Pradesh to be State in which sales took place and which alone was entitled to tax these transactions - Order accordingly.
