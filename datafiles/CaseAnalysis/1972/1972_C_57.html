K. V. A. L. M. Ramanathan Chettiar By L.Rs v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 340; (1973) 3 SCC 351; AIR 1973 SC 2172; 1973 (2) CTR 58; [1973] 88 ITR 169; 1973 (2) MLJ 8; 1973 (2) MLJ(SC) 8; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 650; 1973 TAXLR 1139

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 49 D - Finance Act, 1956 - Construction of expression 'Such doubly taxed income' u/s 49 D of 1922 Act - Held, word 'such' has reference to foreign income which is again being subjected to tax by its inclusion in computation of income under 1922 Act and not same income under an identical head of income under Act - Appeal allowed.
