Rani Laxmibai Kshetriya ,& Gramin Bank Chairman. v Chand Behari Kapoor and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1184; (1998) 7 SCC 469; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1824; AIR 1998 SC 3104; JT 1998 (6) SC 355; 1998 LabIC 3507; 1998 (2) LLJ 1067; 1998 (4) LLN 90; 1998(5) SCALE 226; 1999 (2) SLJ 287; 1998 (5) SLR 770; 1998 (7) SLT 436; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 524; 1998 (7) Supreme 234

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976

Summary: Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, s. 24 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226, 14 and 16 - Appointment Inclusion of name in select list - Existence of vacancies in post - Process of selection for vacancies made in anticipation of opening of new branches - No such branches, in fact, opened - Held, no materials produced by select list candidates to establish existence of vacancies - Conclusion of High Court that vacancies exist, erroneous - Does not confer indefeasible right to be appointed.
