Ramesh Chand Sharma v Udham Singh Kamal and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 885; (1999) 8 SCC 304; AIR 1999 SC 3837; JT 1999 (8) SC 289; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 613

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 21 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Limitation - Petition filed after expiry of 3 years - Challenging promotion of respondent - No condonation of delay before Tribunal - Explanation for delay sought before Supreme Court - Held, as not laid before Tribunal - Not maintainable.
