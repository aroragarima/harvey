Punjab Wakf Board v Gram Panchayat Alias Gram Sabha

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 474; (2000) 2 SCC 121; AIR 2000 SC 3488; JT 1999 (9) SC 544; 2000 (1) RCR(Civil) 283; 1999(7) SCALE 404; 1999 (10) SLT 495; 2000 (Supp) RD 544; [1999] Supp5 S.C.R. 127; 2000 (1) Supreme 17

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Wakf Act, 1954

Summary: Wakf Act, 1954, ss. 5(2) and 6(1), First Proviso - Punjab Village Common Lands (Regulation) Act, 1961, ss. 2(g), 13 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Notification declaring grave - yard as Wakf - Disputed property - 'Shamilat deh' being used for common purposes of Gram Panchayat on revenue records - Dispute between Panchayat, a third party and Wakf - Revenue authorities deciding dispute in favour of Panchayat - Held, not without jurisdiction - Wakf Act not applies as dispute was with stranger.
