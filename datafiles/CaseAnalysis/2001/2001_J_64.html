Rizwan Ahmed Javed Shaikh and Others v Jammal Patel and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20111; (2001) 5 SCC 7; AIR 2001 SC 2198; 2002 AllCriR 1768; 2001 (2) CCR 281; 2002 (1) CRJ 632; 2001 CRLJ 2897; JT 2001 (Supp1) SC 32; 2001 (2) RCR(Criminal) 681; 2001(4) SCALE 205; [2001] 3 S.C.R. 766; 2001 (4) SLT 259; 2001 (4) Supreme 236; 2001 (2) UC 177

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006), Bombay State Reserve Police Force Act, 1951

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 197 - Scope of - Illegal detention - Under police custody - Without producing the detenu before Magistrate within 24 hours of arrest - Held, the sine qua non for the applicability of this section is that the offence charged, be it one of commission or omission, must be one which has been committed by the public servant either in his official capacity or under colour of the office held by him - Act of keeping suspected offenders in custody became illegal when those persons were not produced before a Magistrate within 24 hours of their arrest - Offence alleged was committed by Police Officer in his Official capacity - Appeal dismissed.
