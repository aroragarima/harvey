Om Prakash Garg v Ganga Sahai and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28582; (1987) 3 SCC 553; AIR 1988 SC 108; JT 1987 (2) SC 245

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Decreeholder

Summary: Rent Control - Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 64 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 76 - (A) Mortgagee inducted tenant during subsistence of mortgage - Not act of prudent management - Availability of protection of Rent Act to tenant on redemption of mortgage - (B) Lease created in contravention of s. 64, CPC of property which was under attachment - Effect - Held, not entitled - Voidable at instance of mortgagor and tenant could not claim status as tenant qua mortgagor - Appeal dismissed.
