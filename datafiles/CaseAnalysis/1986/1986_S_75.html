Controller Of Estate Duty, A.P., Hyderabad v Smt. Godavari Bai

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Ranganath Misra, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 336; (1986) 2 SCC 264; (1986) SCC (Tax) 365; AIR 1986 SC 631; 1986 (51) CTR 336; [1986] 158 ITR 683; 1986(1) SCALE 236; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 348; 1986 TAXLR 553; [1986] 24 TAXMAN 804; 1986 (2) TLR 553; 1986 (2) UJ 457

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Estate Duty Act, 1953, s. 10 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 130 and 137 - Partnership - Banking firm - Deceased partner gifted actionable claim valued at Rs. 3, 00, 000 - Out of his right, title and interest as a whole in firm - If covered under his estate - Held, amount not inclusive in estate of deceased that passed on his death and s. 10 not attracted - Appeal dismissed.
