Sabia Khan and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, A.S. Anand, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1816; (1999) 1 SCC 271; AIR 1999 SC 2284; 1999 (3) CalLT(SC) 68; JT 1999 (9) SC 69

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32, 144 and 141 - Writ Petition - Attempting to question - Correctness of orders of Supreme Court - Held, abuse of process of court - Not permissible - Mandate of arts. 144 and 141 cannot be overlooked - Petition dismissed.
