State of Maharashtra and Others v Salvation Army, Western India Territory and another

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, P.N. Bhagwati, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 420; (1975) 1 SCC 509; AIR 1975 SC 846; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 475; 1975 TAXLR 1455

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Family & Personal; Land & Property; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Articles Of Association, Ultra Vires, Charitable or Religious entities

Summary: Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950, s. 58 - Constitution, art. 265 - Bombay Public Trust Rules, 1951, r. 32 - Bombay Public Trusts Act - Contribution - Levy of contribution u/s. 58 r/w r. 32 @ 2 % - Whether it was a tax initially or a fee which assumed character of tax in subsequent years by accumulation of the surplus of income over expenditure - If r. 32 is ultra vires - Tax and fee, distinction - Held, levy of contribution at the rate of 2 per cent of the annual gross income of the trusts became levy of tax after 31st March, 1970 and was without the authority of law - As regards to prayer in the writ Petition to declare r.32 as ultra vires, the SC granted that relief - Appeal allowed.
