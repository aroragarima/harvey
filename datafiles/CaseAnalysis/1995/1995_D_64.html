State Of Orissa v Dhobei Sethi And Another

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1198; (1995) 5 SCC 583; JT 1995 (6) SC 624; 1996 (1) RentLR 48; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 139; 1995 (2) UJ 816

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act - Validity of invoking urgency clause u/s. 17(4) dispensing with enquiry u/s. 5-A - Delay of 7 years - Held, in view of fact that notification was issued as early as on 16-7-1970, writ petition having been filed after 7 years, High Court ought to have dismissed writ petition on ground of laches - Appeals allowed.
