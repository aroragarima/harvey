International Ore And Fertilizers(India) Pvt. Limited v Employees’ State Insurance Corporation

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28373; (1987) 4 SCC 203; (1987) SCC (L&S) 391; AIR 1988 SC 79; 1987 (2) BankCLR 517; 1987 (2) CCC 597; 1987 (55) FLR 480; JT 1987 (3) SC 354; 1988 LabIC 333; 1988 (1) LLJ 235; 1987 (2) LLN 911; 1987(2) SCALE 360; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 981; 1987 (2) UJ 585

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, s. 1(5) - Interpretation - Determination of meaning of word of 'Shop' under A.P. Govt. Notification G.O.M.S. No. 297, D/- 25-03-1975 - Whether establishment is 'shop' and applicability of 1948 Act to it - When actual delivery of goods is not within its premises - Held, establishment employing more than 20 persons and carrying on activities relating to sale of goods - Further held, establishment is 'shop', Act applies to it and actual delivery of goods not necessary - Appeal dismissed.
