Â Sonapur Tea Company, Limited v Must. Mazirunnessa

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 321; AIR 1962 SC 137; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 724

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Socio-economic - Land & Property - Assam Fixation of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1957 - Assam Land and Revenue Regulations, 1836 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19, 31, 31-A and 246 -(A) Does Assam Fixation of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act,1957 falls within protection of art. 31-A of Constitution? - Held, assent of President obtained both for Act as originally passed and for amending Act - Requirement prescribed by proviso to art. 31-A (1)is satisfied - (B)Is Assam Fixation of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1957, a colourable piece of legislation? Further held, Act conforms to pattern followed by several states for giving effect to agrarian reform - When state proceeds to settle lands on tenants,it expects them to pay a fair amount of price for land and puts a ceiling on this price that it shall never exceed amount of compensation payable - State is paying compensation to persons dispossessed - It is a colourable piece of legislation and must fail - Appeal dismissed.
