Banwari Dass v Summer Chand And Ors

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 42; (1974) 4 SCC 817; AIR 1974 SC 1032; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 358

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Any Other Candidate, Purity

Summary: Municipal & Local Govt. - Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, ss. 15, 19(1) and 33(3) - Representation Of People Act, 1951 - Municipal Corporation - Election petition u/s. 15 having additional prayer to declare petitioner duly elected u/s. 19(1)(c) of 1957 Act - Returned candidate can plead corrupt practice by election petitioner - Held, returned candidate is not entitled to recriminate on ground u/s. 17 of Act, in absence of any clear provision - Appeal dismissed.
