Goodyear India Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1034; (1997) 5 SCC 752; AIR 1997 SC 2038; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 14; JT 1997 (3) SC 63; 1997(2) SCALE 376; 1997 (3) SCJ 63; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 445; 1997 (2) Supreme 652

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Finance Act, 1964 ; Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, Sch.1 Item 34(3A) - Scope of motor vehicle was in question - Inclusion of "agricultural tractors'' in item 34 had taken place - Argued it does not have a decisive impact on understanding the scope of words "motor vehicle'' - Held, the argument was valid and fact that heavy movers sometimes traverse on roads will not be enough to say that they were "adopted for use upon roads''.
