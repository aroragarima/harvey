Raj Kumar Khaitan and Others v Bibi Zubaida Khatun and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 826; (1997) 11 SCC 411; AIR 1995 SC 576; 1995 (2) RCJ 568; 1995 (1) RCR(Rent) 495

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Bihar Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1983, s. 11 - Eviction of tenant sought on ground of bona fide need - Clear assertion by landlords as to requirement of premises for setting up business - Precise nature of business - If required to be stated - Held, need not be stated - Appeal allowed.
