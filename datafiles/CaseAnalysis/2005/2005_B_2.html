Secy. Deptt. Of Home Secy.A.P. & Ors v B. Chinnam Naidu

Supreme Court of India

09 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 103; (2005) SCC (L&S) 323; (2005) 2 SCC 746; 2005 (5) AWC 4083; 2005 (2) ESC 170; 2005 (104) FLR 1063; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 18; JT 2005 (2) SC 358; 2005 (2) LLN 43; 2005(2) SCALE 118; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 1147; 2005 (2) SLR 340; 2005 (2) Supreme 73; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1628

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Criminal - Examination - IPC, 1860 - Andhra Pradesh Public Examination Prevention of Malpractice and Unfair Means Act, 1997 - Guilty of suppression of material facts - Denial of appointment to respondent - Whether HC right in holding that authorities were not justified in denying appointment to the respondent?; whether respondent is guilty of any suppression? - Held, conviction by a court or detention under any State/Central Preventive Detention Laws is different from arrest in any case or pendency of a case - Candidate is not required to indicate as to whether he had been arrested in any case or as to whether any case was pending - Respondent cannot be found guilty of any suppression - Appeal dismissed.
