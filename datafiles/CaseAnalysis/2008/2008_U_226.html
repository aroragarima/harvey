Bilkish v United India Insurance Co.Ltd.& Anr

Supreme Court of India

12 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 433; (2008) 4 SCC 259; (2008) 2 SCC (Cr) 781; 2008 (2) ACC 825; 2008 (72) ALR 212; 2008 (2) AWC 2043; 2008 (1) CCC 135; 2008 (2) CPJ(Sc) 37; JT 2008 (4) SC 281; 2008 (5) KarLJ 654; 2008 (151) PLR 270; 2008 (3) RCR(Civil) 82; 2008(4) SCALE 25

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Motor accident claim - Division Bench has ordered the compensation in sum of Rs. 1, 65, 000/- towards dependency and Rs. 10, 000/- for the loss of estate & funeral expenses - Appeal for enhancement of compensation - Held, determining the loss of dependency by 50% was not correct - Loss of dependency was 2/3rd i.e. Rs. 20, 000/- - Multiplier of '11' applied for loss of dependency was not correct and as per schedule appended it should be '12' - Appeal partly allowed.
