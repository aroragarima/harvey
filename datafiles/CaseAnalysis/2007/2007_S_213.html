Y.A. Ajit v Sofana Ajit

Supreme Court of India

07 September 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 881; (2007) 10 SCC 429; AIR 2007 SC 3151; 2007 (69) ALR 148; 2007 (5) ALT 75; 2007 (4) AWC 4036; 2007 (4) CHN 62; 2007 (4) JhrCR(SC) 184; JT 2007 (11) SC 88; 2007 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2826; 2007 (6) MLJ(SC) 1487; 2007(10) SCALE 635; 2007 (6) SCJ 861; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 808

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Respondent sought for transfer of IDOP pending in the Court of District Judge, Kanyakumari at Nagercoil to the Court of Family Judge, Chennai - High Court allowed the transfer petition - Appeal against - Whether any part of the cause of action arose within the jurisdiction of the concerned Court? - Held, in terms of s. 177 of the Code it is the place where the offence was committed - It would be appropriate for the High Court to re-consider the matter - Appeal disposed of.
