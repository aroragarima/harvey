Commissioner Of Income Tax,Bombay And Others v Mahindra And Mahindra Limited & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 69; (1983) SCC (Tax) 336; (1983) 4 SCC 392; AIR 1984 SC 1182; [1983] 54 Comp Cas 651; 1983 (3) CompLJ 30; 1983 (36) CTR 300; [1983] 144 ITR 225; 1983(2) SCALE 222; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 773; 1983 TAXLR 1286

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Companies Act, 1913, Finance Act, 1977

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act,1961, s. 72A - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 394, 370 and 371 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Carry forward and setoff of accumulated loss and unabsorbed depreciation allowance on amalgamation of companies - Whether recommendation of a statutory body u/s. 72 of the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Central Govt's decision based on it-a matter of subjective satisfaction-were open to judicial review and whether the High Court was justified in interfering with the same ? - Interference held to be valid - Appeal dismissed.
