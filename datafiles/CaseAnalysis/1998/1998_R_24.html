State of Punjab v Raj Singh Another

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1386; (1998) 2 SCC 391; (1998) SCC (Cr) 642; AIR 1998 SC 768; 1998 (36) AllCriC 399; 1998 (1) CCR 286; 1998 (1) Crimes 122; 1998 CRLJ 1104; JT 1998 (1) SC 145; 1998 (1) RCR(Criminal) 576; 1998(1) SCALE 130; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 223; 1998 (2) SLT 610; 1998 (1) Supreme 255

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 195 and 154 - Offence relating to proceeding in Court - Power of police to investigate into FIR as to said offence - Held, not controlled or circumscribed by embargo under s. 195 against prosecution of public servant - Court cannot take cognizance after completion of investigation.
