Ragho Singh v Mohan Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3688; (2001) 9 SCC 717; 2000 (41) ALR 113; 2000 (41) ALR 786; 2003 (1) RCR(Civil) 40A; 2000 (91) RD 689

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Whether a time barred appeal can be entertained without condonation of delay? - Board of Revenue before which question of limitation was agitated was of view that though an application for condonation of delay was not filed, delay shall be deemed to have been condoned - Since appeal filed before Additional Collector was beyond time by 10 days and an application u/s. 5 of Limitation Act was not filed for condonation of delay, there was no jurisdiction in Additional Collector to allow that appeal - High Court was right in setting aside judgment of Additional Collector as also of Board of Revenue - Appeal dismissed.
