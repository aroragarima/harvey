Commissioner of Sales Tax, U.P. v S. R. Brothers, Kanpur

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 726; (1973) SCC (Tax) 254; (1973) 3 SCC 496; AIR 1973 SC 78; 1973 (2) CTR 94; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 852; 1973 (31) STC 302

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Sales Tax - UP Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 3 A - Sales Tax notification No ST-905/X, D/- 31-3-1956 - Interpretation of Statutes - Expressions 'dyes and colours and compositions thereof' and 'scents and perfumes' used in entries 10 and 37 respectively - If covers 'food colours' and 'syrup essences' - Held, do not take within their fold 'food colours' and 'syrup essence' - Appeal dismissed.
