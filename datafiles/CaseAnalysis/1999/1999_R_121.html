Rameshwar Prasad v Managing Director, U. P. Rajkiya Nirman Nigam Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1294; (1999) 8 SCC 381; AIR 1999 SC 3443; 1999 ALJ 2220; 1999 (4) AWC 2743; 1999 (83) FLR 442; JT 1999 (7) SC 44; 1999 (4) LLN 700; 1999(5) SCALE 603; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 593; 1999 (3) UPLBEC 2412

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 16 - Uttar Pradesh Rajkiya Nirman Nigam Limited (Engineers and Architects) Service Rules, 1980, r. 16(3) - Uttar Pradesh Absorption of Govt. servants in Public Undertakings Rules, 1984, rr. 4 and 5 - Absorption of deputationist - Picking and choosing - When policy once accepted and rules framed - Held, is arbitrary - Deputationist stands absorbed in service of Nigam.
