Commissioner of Customs and Central Excise v Kumar Cotton Mills Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

13 January 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 154; (2005) 13 SCC 296; 2005 (180) E.L.T. 434

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35 C(2A) - Finance Act, 2002 - Whether Tribunal erred in concluding that amendment did not affect stay orders which were passed prior to date of coming into force of amendment? - Sub-s. (2A) of s. 35C which was introduced in terrorem cannot be construed as punishing assessees for matters which may be completely beyond their control - By reason of other administrative exigencies for which assessee cannot be held liable, stay applications are not disposed within time specified - Reasoning of Tribunal expressed in impugned order cannot be faulted - Appeal disposed of.
