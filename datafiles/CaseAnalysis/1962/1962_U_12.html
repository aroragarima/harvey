Â Bridge and Roof Company (India), Limited, and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah, Syed Jaffer Imam, K. Subba Rao, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 93; AIR 1963 SC 1474; 1962 (5) FLR 423; 1962 (2) LLJ 490; [1963] 3 S.C.R. 978

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution - Employees' Provident Funds Act, ss. 2(b), 6 and 19 A - Bonus - Meaning of - Whether production bonus is included within term 'basic wages' as defined in act? - Whatever is earned as production bonus is payable beyond a base or standard and it cannot form part 'basic wage' - Held, not disputed on behalf of respondents that bonuses other than profit bonus were in force and well known before Act was passed in 1952 - Decision of Central Govt. set aside - Petition allowed.
