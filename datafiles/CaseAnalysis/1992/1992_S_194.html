R.S. Sodhi,Advocate v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1090; (1994) Supp1 SCC 143; (1994) SCC (Cr) 248; AIR 1994 SC 38; 1993 ALJ 1357; 1994 CRLJ 111

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 154 - Constitution, arts.14 and 32 - Allegation of fake Police encounters levelled against local police - Investigation - Whether investigation by State Police will lack credibility - Held, investigation by independent agency desirable - investigation by State Police will lack credibility - Direction to CBI to investigate into matter - Petition allowed.
