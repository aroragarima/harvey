Commissioner Ofincome Tax, Kanpur v Dr. R.S. Gupta

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28635; (1987) 2 SCC 84; AIR 1987 SC 785; 1987 (1) BankCLR 265; [1987] 165 ITR 36; JT 1987 (1) SC 340; 1987(1) SCALE 225; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 121; 1987 TAXLR 457; 1987 (1) UJ 459

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Keywords: Town Planning Act 1882

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 122 and 123 - Wealth Tax - Gift - When gift by book entries is valid - Credit account in non- banking company without overdraft facility - Gift of certain amount made by directing company to debit his account and credit done - But company having insufficient cash - Validity of gift - Inclusiveness in his net wealth - Held, gift made by assessee is not valid - Amount includable - Appeal allowed.
