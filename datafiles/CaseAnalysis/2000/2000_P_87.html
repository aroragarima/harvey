Vasa Chandrasekhar Rao v Ponna Satyanarayana and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 326; (2000) 6 SCC 286; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1104; AIR 2000 SC 2138; 2000 (41) AllCriC 210; 2000 (3) CCR 17; 2000 (2) Crimes 328; 2000 CRLJ 75; 2000 (2) JCC 541; JT 2000 (6) SC 465; 2000 (3) RCR(Criminal) 96; 2000(4) SCALE 635; 2000 (4) SLT 696; 2000 (4) Supreme 173

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Tort

Keywords: Parents

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Evidence Act 1872, s. 6 - Murder of wife and daughter by accused - Circumstantial evidence against accused - Accused suspecting character of wife and beating her - Evidence of doctor stating that death was due to stab injuries - Held, accused providing additional link in chain of circumstances - Acquittal of accused liable to be set aside.
