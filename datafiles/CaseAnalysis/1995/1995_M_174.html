Mool Chand and Others v Dy. Director, Consolidation and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1150; (1995) 5 SCC 631; AIR 1995 SC 2493; JT 1995 (6) SC 683; 1995 RD 490; 1995(5) SCALE 10; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 763; 1995 (2) UJ 799

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Abatement, Effect Of

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 5(2) - U.P. Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1951, s. 176 - Appeal against preliminary decree in partition suit not pending - Proceedings for preparation final decree pending - Decree if abated on publication of notification for consolidation - Held, decree does not abate and proceedings are immaterial - Appeal No 10214 of 1983 dismissed - Appeal No 2635 of 1980 allowed.
