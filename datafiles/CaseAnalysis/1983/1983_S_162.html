Dr. Vinay Rampal v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 351; (1984) SCC (L&S) 103; (1984) 1 SCC 160; AIR 1983 SC 1199; 1983(2) SCALE 336; 1983 (3) SLR 293; 1983 UJ 825

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Education - Admission to PG Course in Government Medical College - Advertisement - Setting out minimum qualification - No reference to Government Order D/- 23-03-1979 therein - Petitioner, eligible as per advertisement - Refused admission - Held, could not be refused on the basis of Order when others have been admitted on the basis of advertisement - Order Accordingly.
