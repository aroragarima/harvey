Patheje Bros. Forgings & Stamping & Anr. v Icici Ltd & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, M.B. Shah, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 375; (2000) 6 SCC 545; AIR 2000 SC 2553; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 49; 2000 (4) Bom.C.R. 13; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 21; 2000 (4) CompLJ 9; JT 2000 (8) SC 252; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 609; 2000(5) SCALE 277; 2000 (5) SLT 665; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 662; 2000 (5) Supreme 257

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - SICA, 1985, s. 22 - Sick company - Whether s. 22 of the Act covers a suit against the guarantor of a loan or advance that has been granted to the industrial company concerned industrial company - Maintainability of suit - Held, suit for the enforcement of the guarantee would not be maintainable or cannot be proceeded with without sanction of Board or Appellate Authority under Act - Appeal allowed.
