Municipal Council, Samrala v Sukhwinder Kaur

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 410; (2006) 6 SCC 516; AIR 2006 SC 2905; 2006 (4) JhrCR(SC) 167; 2006 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2658; 2006(7) SCALE 614; 2006 (6) SCJ 395; 2007 (2) SLR 201; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10, 2(oo)(bb), 25-F - Respondent was engaged on a contractual basis at a fixed pay of Rs. 1000/- Per month - Services being terminated - Labour court opined that termination of respondent from services was not in conformity with provisions of 25-F and directed reinstatement with continuity of service with 25% of back wages - Held there was no fixed period of contract of employment between employer and workman concerned and thus, no question of its renewal on its expiry - Executive Officer has power to dismiss her without issuing any notice - Interest of justice shall be met if a sum of Rs. 30,000/- Is directed to be paid to respondent - Appeals disposed of.
