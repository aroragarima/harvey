Mohanlal Maganlal Bhavsar (Deceased) Through Lrs and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 15; (1986) 1 SCC 122; (1986) SCC (Tax) 186; AIR 1986 SC 401; 1986 (1) CCC 100; 1986 (7) ECR 356; 1986 (23) E.L.T. 3; 1986 TAXLR 1980; 1986 (1) UJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1955, Constitution (Thirtieth Amendment) Act, 1972

Summary: Excise - Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1955, s. 3, sch item 1 - Central Excise and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4 - Excise Duty - (A) Medicinal preparations - Need not be beverage - Taxability of ointment containing alcohol - (B) Determination of value - Medicinal preparations prepared and supplied by firm to alleged distributor (firm) - Common partners except that a son of each of them was admitted to benefits only - There offices situated in same premises - Authorities taking wholesale price of medicines for levy of duty and not price at which medicines were supplied by firm to distributor firm - Validity - Held, ointments are dutiable under Item 1 being medicinal preparations as referred to in item 1 - Held justified - Appeal dismissed.
