Jagdish Chandra Nijhawan v S. K. Saraf

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1319; (1999) SCC (Cr) 20; (1999) 1 SCC 119; AIR 1999 SC 217; 1999 (1) CalLT(SC) 88; 1998 (4) CCR 118; [1999] 95 Comp Cas 48; 1999 (1) CompLJ 1; 1998 (4) Crimes 123; 1999 CRLJ 268; JT 1998 (8) SC 380; 1999 (1) LLJ 295; 1999 (2) RCR(Criminal) 33; 1998(6) SCALE 284; 1998 (9) SLT 477; 1998 (9) Supreme 24

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, s. 227 - Discharge - Appellant appointed as Managing Director of Co. for limited period - Provided to use rent free furnished flat till his service - Expiry of his appointment period - Failure to hand over possession of flat - Co. filing criminal complaint and also civil suit - Held, order by magistrate discharging appellant on ground that dispute between parties was of civil nature - Not illegal.
