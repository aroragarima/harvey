Kanchanlal Maneklal Chokshi v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, P.N. Shingal, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 554; (1979) 4 SCC 14; (1979) SCC (Cr) 897; AIR 1979 SC 1945; 1979 CRLJ 1306; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 54

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1975 - Detention order u/s. 3 - Validity - When grounds of detention containing elaborate statement of facts - Indicating application of mind by detaining authority - No complaint about non-consideration of question of possibility of prosecution - Held, order is not in any manner infirm - Appeal dismissed.
