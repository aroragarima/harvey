Chowgule And Co. Private Limited Etc v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28236; (1987) 1 SCC 730; AIR 1987 SC 1176; 1987 (1) BankCLR 140; 1987 (2) Bom.C.R. 691; 1987 (12) ECC 1; 1987 (11) ECR 481; 1987 (28) E.L.T. 39; JT 1987 (1) SC 496; 1987(1) SCALE 404; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 351; 1987 TAXLR 1894; 1987 (1) UJ 644

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Keywords: Warehoused Goods, Warehouse, Tariff Valuation, Sea Customs Act 1962

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 2(22), 46(1) and 12, chaps. VI and VII - Interpretation of Statutes - Imported Vessels used primarily as transhippers - And incidentally or occasionally go into open sea - (A) If vessel is 'goods' for the purpose of s. 46(1) of the Act - (B) Whether they are 'goods' for home consumption or they are ocean going vessels - Held, vessel is 'goods' for the purpose - Are ' goods' for home consumption and Bills of entry, necessary to be prestented in respect of them - Appeal dismissed.
