State of Bihar and Others v Kameshwar Prasad Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2859; (2000) 9 SCC 94; (2000) SCC (L&S) 845; AIR 2000 SC 2306; JT 2000 (5) SC 389; 2000 (33) LabIC 2379; 2000(4) SCALE 417; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 764; 2000 (4) SLR 8

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 14 - Contempt petition - Fear of - Directions by High Court - To authorities to consider his case for fixing seniority treating date of officiating promotion as relevant date - Not complied with - Held, order cannot be challenged by authorities allegedly due to fear of contempt and various coercive orders - Another officer not be entitled to any relief on analogy of such judgment.
