Collector of Customs, Madras v India Organic Chemicals Limited

Supreme Court of India

03 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 200; (2000) 5 SCC 6; AIR 2000 SC 3373; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 1; 2000 (118) E.L.T. 3; JT 2000 (5) SC 540; 2000 (38) RLT 483; 2000(4) SCALE 321; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 946; 2000 (4) SLT 749; 2000 (4) Supreme 335

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Tariff Act, 1975, tariff item 68 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 19 - Import of diesel engine set - Additional duty charged under item 68 - Claim for refund - Plea, said set comprised of diesel engine and an alternator where additional duty charged separately - Held, s. 19 not applicable for assessment but tariff item 68 - Comprising of two components has no consequence for assessment of additional duty.
