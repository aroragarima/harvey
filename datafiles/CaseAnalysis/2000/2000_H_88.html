Commissioner of Income Tax v Hindustan Electro Graphites Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 616; (2000) 3 SCC 595; AIR 2000 SC 1481; AIR 2000 SCW 1010; 2000 (160) CTR 8; [2000] 243 ITR 48; 2000(2) SCALE 519; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 506; [2000] 109 TAXMAN 342

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Advance Payment, Cash Assistance, Income Tax Act 1961, Finance Act, 1990, False Return, Retrospective Legislation

Summary: Finance Act, 1990, s. 28 (Amended) - Levy of additional tax - Income by way of cash compensatory support - Retrospectively taxable w.e.f. April 1967 by amendment of s. 28 - Assessee filing return on 29 - 12 - 1989 - Non - inclusion of said income - Held, plea of bona fide mistake not tenable - Assessee cannot be imputed with clairvoyance - Levy of additional tax was unwarranted and law prevailing on date when return was filed must be applied.
