Brakes India Limited v Supdt. of Central Excise and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1808; (1997) 10 SCC 717; 1998 (79) ECR 232; 1998 (101) E.L.T. 241

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 2(1) and 2(f) - Character or use test - Manufacture - Whether brake lining blanks purchased by appellant when put to process of drilling, trimming and chamfering can be said to amount to manufacturing? - Held, yes - Single Judge from whose judgment, Division Bench was considering appeal pointed out that brake lining blanks could not be used by owners of motor vehicles without holes and trimming and chamfering - Further held, Single Judge as well as Division Bench applied correct test - No reason to interfere therewith - Appeals dismissed.
