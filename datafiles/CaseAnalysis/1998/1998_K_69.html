Keshav Deo and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1268; (1999) SCC (L&S) 198; (1999) 1 SCC 280; AIR 1999 SC 44; 1999 (1) AWC 789; JT 1998 (7) SC 216; 1998 LabIC 3554; 1998 (4) LLN 985; 1998(5) SCALE 621; 1999 (3) SLJ 10; 1998 (8) SLT 321; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 343; 1998 (8) Supreme 119; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 12

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Uttar Pradesh Service of Engineers (Buildings and Roads Branch) Class II Rules, 1936, r. 23(d) - Seniority - Determination of - Promotion of appellants on ad hoc basis - Well qualified and continued to work uninterruptedly on post till they were selected and approved by Commission - Period of service rendered as ad hoc promotee before appointment - Held, can be counted for purpose of determining seniority - Conformity with rules.
