Dipak K. Ghosh v State of West Bengal and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 86; (2006) 3 SCC 765; AIR 2006 SC 1703; 2006 (63) ALR 480; 2006 (2) BC 308; 2006(3) SCALE 198; 2006 (3) SCJ 618

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Directions by court for public auction of a bungalow - Directions having not been adhered to by respondent No 8 - Application by State of West Bengal seeking directions to Respondent No 8 to cooperate with applicant State of West Bengal in its efforts to implement this Hon'ble Court directions - Plea of respondent no 8 is that order passed is void, a nullity and non est as this Court has no jurisdiction to pass such an order and public auction has been conducted contrary to directions of this Court - Held, perusal of compliance report shows that public auction was conducted consistent with directions of this Court - Directions issued.
