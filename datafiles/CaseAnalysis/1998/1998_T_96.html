T.L.Thathachariar v The Commissioner, H.R.C.E. & Ors

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 624; (1998) 6 SCC 643; AIR 1998 SC 3252; JT 1998 (6) SC 214; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 4; 1998(5) SCALE 108; 1998 (7) SLT 272; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 359; 1998 (7) Supreme 135

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Madras Hindu Religious Endowments Act, 1926, Madras (Tamil Nadu) Hindu religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1959

Summary: Civil Procedure - Trusts & Associations - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1882, s. 539 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s.92 - Madras (Tamil Nadu) Hindu religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1959, s.64(5) - Madras Hindu religious Endowments Act, 1926 (Madras Act II of 1927) - Scheme - Jurisdiction - Modification - Challenged - Appeal pertain to a scheme for administration of Devarajaswamy Temple and Trusteeship of temple - Scheme was originally settled in 1909 in an application made originally before District Court - Application filed u/s. 539 of 1882 Code and scheme was framed u/s.92 of CPC - Deputy Commissioner of Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments initiated proceedings u/s.64(5) of 1959 Act - Deputy Commissioner had no jurisdiction to modify scheme settled by HC - Appellant filed petition before HC challenged order of Deputy Commissioner - Petition dismissed - Hence, instant petition - Whether scheme was deemed under 1927 Act by virtue of could be modified - Held, that Deputy Commissioner had no jurisdiction u/s.64(5) of 1959 Act to modify a scheme originally framed u/s.92 of CPC - Act of 1927 existing scheme could be modified or cancelled by court on an application made by Board - Under provision of s.62(3)(a) of 1951 Act, scheme was deemed to be a scheme under Act of 1927 by virtue of s.103(d) could be modified or cancelled by court u/s. 62(3)(a) of new 1951 Act - No such modification was made - Scheme became a deemed scheme framed under 1951 Act by an appropriate authority under corresponding provisions of 1959 Act - S. 64 5(a) deals with modification or cancellation of a scheme in force - Order accordingly.
