Melepurath Sankunni Ezhuthassan v Thekittil Gopalankutty Nair

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 292; (1986) 1 SCC 118; 1986 (1) ACJ 441; AIR 1986 SC 411; 1986 (1) CCC 377; 1985(2) SCALE 1098; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 805; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 805; 1986 (1) UJ 131

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Succession & Inheritance, Defamatory Material, Defamatory Articles

Summary: Indian Succession Act, 1925; Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Cause of action for defamation - Does not survive death of appellant. - Damages for defamation awarded or not - Held, right does not survive - Damages not awarded - Appeal abated automatically on death of person concerned - Legal representatives acquired no right to be brought on the record in his place.
