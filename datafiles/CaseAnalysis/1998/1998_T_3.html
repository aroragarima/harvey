W. T. Suren and Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1496; (1998) 6 SCC 113; AIR 1998 SC 1575; 1998 (145) CTR 360; [1998] 230 ITR 643; JT 1998 (2) SC 74; 1998(2) SCALE 1; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1069; 1998 (3) SLT 233; 1998 (2) Supreme 200; [1998] 97 TAXMAN 126; 1998 (2) TLR 439

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income - tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Income - tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - Assessee is a subsidiary company closing one of it's units as that business was taken over by parent company staff was surplus so same job was offered by parent company and service continued - The gratuity amount has been paid by subsidiary company to parent company of those employees whose services are continued - Whether amount of gratuity paid by subsidiary company to parent company is deductible? - The amount paid as gratuity is deducted.
