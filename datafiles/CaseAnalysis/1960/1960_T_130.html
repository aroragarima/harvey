Tea Districts Labour Association,Calcutta v Ex-Employees Of Tea Districts Labourassociation And Another

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 229; AIR 1960 SC 815; 1960 (1) LLJ 802; 1960 SCJ 707; [1960] 3 S.C.R. 207

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure, Labour Appellate Tribunal, Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25FFF - Compensation for malafide closure of industrial undertaking - Whether closure of an undertaking is shown to malafide can be deemed to be a fiction? - Held, undertaking in eye of law should be deemed to continue - Appeal allowed.
