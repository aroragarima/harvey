Bhopal Sugar Industries Ltd. v Sales Tax Officer, Bhopal

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 34; (1977) 3 SCC 147; (1977) SCC (Tax) 401; AIR 1977 SC 1275; 1977 (6) CTR 284; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 578; 1977 (40) STC 42

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Sales of Motor Spirit and Lubricants Taxation Act, 1957, Madhya Bharat Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation Act, 1953

Summary: M.P. Sales of Motor Spirit and Lubricants Taxation Act, 1958, s. 2(1) - Determination of 'Sale' for copputation of Tax liability - Contract of agency or contract of sale - Construction of contract - Agreement for supply of HSD Oil and petrol - Part of goods consumed by assessee itself - Held, the consumption by the assessee was not sale eligible to tax - Appeals allowed.
