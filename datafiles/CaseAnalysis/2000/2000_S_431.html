Food Corporation of India v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 147; (2000) 3 SCC 495; AIR 2000 SC 1109; JT 2000 (2) SC 195; 2000(1) SCALE 587; 2000 (119) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Demand Notice

Summary: Haryana General Sales Tax Act 1973, s. 59 - Constitution of India, 1950, sch. 7, List 2, Entry 54 - Interest was levied on belated payment of tax which was in dispute - Demand for payment of sales tax on levy transactions for assessment year validated by Supreme Court - Assessee issued fresh demand notice pursuant to judgment of Supreme Court - Amount demanded was paid by appellant assessee - Held, levy of interest on belated payment of amount which was principal tax due from assessee for assessment year 1975-76 was not sustainable.
