Y.V. Rangaiah and Others v J. Sreenivasa Rao and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 19; (1983) 3 SCC 284; (1983) SCC (L&S) 382; AIR 1983 SC 852; 1983 LabIC 1240; 1983 (2) LLJ 23; 1983 (1) SLR 789

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: A.P. Registration Subordinate Service Special Rules, r. 5 - Service Rules - Transfer or promotions to post of Sub-Registrar Grade II - Determination of Rules to be applicable to the vacancies occurring prior to amendment of 1977 - PRevious or ammended - Held, would be governed by old rules and not by new rules - Held, a panel should have been prepared in 1976 and transfer or promotion should have been made out of that panel - Appeal Dismissed.
