K. C. P. Employees' Association, Madras v Management of K. C. P. Limited, Madras and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 459; (1978) 2 SCC 42; (1978) SCC (L&S) 142; AIR 1978 SC 474; 1978 (36) FLR 217; 1978 LabIC 518; 1978 (1) LLJ 322; 1982 (1) LLN 26; 1978 (2) MLJ 11B; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 608; 1978 UJ 69

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Service - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 - Issue - Applicability of the proviso to s. 3 of the Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 to the present case - There is reasonable doubt on law and facts and the benefit of such doubt must go to the weaker section ie, labour - Held, the appeals are dismissed with no costs.
