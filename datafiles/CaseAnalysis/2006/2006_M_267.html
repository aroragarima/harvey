Seema Arshad Zaheer and others v Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai and others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 537; (2006) 5 SCC 282; 2006 (5) ALD(SC) 1; 2006 (3) AWC 3059; 2006 (4) Bom.C.R. 46; JT 2006 (11) SC 1; 2006 (4) RCR(Civil) 293; 2006(5) SCALE 263; [2006] Supp1 S.C.R. 865

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Municipalities & Local Governments - Mumbai Municipal Corporation Act, 1888, s. 351 - Maharashtra Control Of Organised Crime Act, 1999 - Orders to remove/pull down seven unauthorized and illegal structures - Obtained a temporary injunction restraining Corporation from taking action - Bombay HC vacated temporary injunction - Special leave petition against - Held, there was 'no material' to make out a prima facie case and therefore, HC in its appellate jurisdiction, was justified in interfering in matter and vacating temporary injunction granted by trial court - Appeals allowed.
