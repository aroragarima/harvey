Koli Lakhmanbhai Chanabhai v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 826; (1999) 8 SCC 624; AIR 2000 SC 210; 1999 (4) Crimes 256; 2000 CRLJ 408; 2000 (2) G.L.R. 1151; JT 1999 (9) SC 133; 2000 (1) RCR(Criminal) 26

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 313 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 109 and 120-B - Murder - Eye-witnesses - Appellant acquitted by Trial Court but on appeal by Govt. convicted by High Court - Hence, present appeal - Held, evidence of witnesses and medical evidence fully corroborates prosecution version of events - Recovery of weapon further strengthens prosecution case - Evidence of hostile witness can be relied upon to extent it supports prosecution version - HC rightly reversed acquittal order - Appeal dismissed.
