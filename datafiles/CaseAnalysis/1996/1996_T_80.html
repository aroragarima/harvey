R. Thiruvirkolam v Presiding Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1877; (1997) 1 SCC 9; (1997) SCC (L&S) 65; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 535; AIR 1997 SC 633; 1997 (1) CLR 1; 1997 (75) FLR 136; JT 1996 (10) SC 369; 1997 LabIC 443; 1997 (1) LLJ 400; 1997 (1) LLN 127; 1997 LLR 12; 1996(8) SCALE 297; 1997 (1) SLJ 218; 1997 (1) SLR 238; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 687; 1996 (8) Supreme 89

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11 A, Chap. 2, item 3 - Industrial Disputes - Dismissal - When becomes operative? - Either from date when order was made by employer or from date of confirmation by Labour Court - Held, from date when order was made by employer - Appeal dismissed.
