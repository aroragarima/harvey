Ferozi Lal Jain v Man Mal and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 540; (1970) 3 SCC 181; AIR 1970 SC 794; 1971 RCJ 372; 1970 UJ 458

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Rent Control - Delhi and Ajmer Rent Control Act, 1952, s. 13(1) - Lease of property - Allegation of further sub-lease of property - Compromise decree not making any reference to alleged sub-lease - Whether decree under execution is a nullity as it is in contravention of s. 13? - A decree for recovery of possession can be passed by any court only if that court is satisfied that one more of grounds mentioned in s. 3(1) are established - Without such a satisfaction, Court is incompetent to pass a decree for possession - Held, Court proceeded solely on basis of compromise arrived at between parties - Court was not competent to pass impugned decree - Decree under execution to be a nullity - Appeal dismissed.
