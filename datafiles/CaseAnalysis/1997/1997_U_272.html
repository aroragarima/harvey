University of Cochin and another v N. S. Kanjoonjamma and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1091; (1997) 4 SCC 426; (1997) SCC (L&S) 976; AIR 1997 SC 2083; JT 1997 (5) SC 379; 1997 LabIC 2078; 1997 (2) LLJ 228; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 150; 1997 (2) SLR 606

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 16 - Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, rr. 4 and 17A - Rules, 1958 providing for reservation of posts for SC/ST candidate adopted by University - Advertisement issued to fill up vacant post of Asst. Registrar and Deputy Registrar for recruitment of SC/ST - Omission in advertisement to state that it was special recruitment - Section Committee formed by Vice-Chancellor - Selected candidate, unselected candidate and petitioner participated in the selection process - (A) Whether petitioner having participated in selection is estopped from challenging it - (B) Whether r. 4 would apply - Held, (A) petitioner is estopped from challenging process - Omission in advertisement not material - (B) Recruitment in question being special recruitment, r. 4 has no application - Appeal allowed.
