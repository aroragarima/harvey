Â Barium Chemicals Limited And Another v Sh. A. J. Rana And Others

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, I.D. Dua, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 280; (1972) 1 SCC 240; AIR 1972 SC 591; [1972] 42 Comp Cas 245; 1972 (2) MLJ(SC) 30; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 752

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Corporate; Trade

Summary: FERA, 1947, s. 19(2) - Order requisitioning documents for examination - Some documents have no connection with the purpose u/s. 19(2) of the Act - Particulars of documents not stated - Validity of order questioned - Held, particulars etc. must be stated in the order - Held invalid - Appeal No. 1452/71 Allowed ; Appeal No. 1453/71 Dismissed.
