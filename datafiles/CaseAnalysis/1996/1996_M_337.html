Dr. Buddhi Kota Subbarao v Mr. K. Parasaran & Ors

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 977; (1996) 5 SCC 530; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1038; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 133; AIR 1996 SC 2687; 1996 (4) CCR 20; 1996 (3) Crimes 143; 1996 CRLJ 3983; JT 1996 (7) SC 265; 1996(5) SCALE 797; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 574; 1996 (6) Supreme 120

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Electricity & Energy; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss.191, 192, and193 - Scope and applicability - Applicant, a retired Captain of Defence detained in his way to foreign country - Carrying atomic and defence secret, charged - Prosecution - Had 'Consent' of Attorney General of India and 'Authorisation' of Dept. of Atomic Energy - Held, not amounts to 'False evidence' or 'Fabricating of false evidence' - Sections 191, 192, and193 not applicable in the circumstances.
