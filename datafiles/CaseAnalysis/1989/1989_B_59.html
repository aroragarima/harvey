Dwarkadas Marfatia And Sons v Board Of Trustees Of The Port Of Bombay

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, M.H. Kania, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 601; (1989) 3 SCC 293; AIR 1989 SC 1642; 1990 (1) Bom.C.R. 405; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 146; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 495; 1991 (1) RentLR 23; 1989(1) SCALE 1157; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 751; 1989 (2) UJ 419

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Major Port Trusts (Amendment) Act, 1974, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, Major Port Trusts Act 1889

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 4 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 12, 14 and 226 - Exemption from applicability of rent control legislation to public bodies due to public interest - Allotment of plot by Bombay Port Trust to tenant holding major portion of plot of land, while evicting other tenant - Whether policy decision taken for planned development of area is proper? - Held, proper - Power to evict tenant holding lesser person exercised in public interest - Appeal dismissed.
