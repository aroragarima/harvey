Sher-E-Punjab Silk Stores v Commissioner of Income Tax, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 419; (1973) 4 SCC 206; AIR 1973 SC 2401; 1973 (2) CTR 274; 1975 (1) ITJ 1; [1973] 88 ITR 421; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 76; 1973 TAXLR 1297

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26 A - Income Tax Rules, 1922, rr. 2,3 and 6 - Renewal of registration - Application for renewal - Actual division of profits or losses of firm during previous year - If precondition - Held, must precede application for renewal - Appeal dismissed.
