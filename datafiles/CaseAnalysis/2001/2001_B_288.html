Bhatia International v Bulk Trading S.A. and Anr

Supreme Court of India

11 December 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 18834

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s.9 - International arbitration - Jurisdiction - Dispute with regard to interpretation of s.9 of Act - Whether in case of international arbitration where situs of arbitration proceedings is beyond India, Courts in India will have jurisdiction to grant any interim direction - Held, apart from importance of question involved, SC is not in agreement with each other on question, SC thinks it appropriate to refer matter to three-Judge Bench - Order accordingly.
