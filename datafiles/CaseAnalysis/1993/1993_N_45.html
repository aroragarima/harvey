Messrs Gobind Pershad Jagdish Pershad v New Delhi Municipal Committee

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K. Ramaswamy, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1215; (1993) 4 SCC 69; AIR 1993 SC 2313; JT 1993 (4) SC 233; 1993 (2) RentLR 192; 1993(3) SCALE 208; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 237; 1993 (2) UJ 420

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Land & Property - Punjab Municipal Act, 1911, ss. 3(13)(a) and 171(4) - Validity of declaration of street as public street - Verandah in front of shop used for long by public - Held, it can be declared as 'public street' for exercising regulatory control - Appeal dismissed.
