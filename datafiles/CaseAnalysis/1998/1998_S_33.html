Ram Swarup and Others v S. N. Maira and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1837; (1999) 1 SCC 738; AIR 1999 SC 941; JT 1998 (8) SC 649; 1998 (10) SLT 341; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 617

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Haryana Ceiling on Land Holding Act, 1972

Summary: Haryana Ceiling on Land Holding Act, 1972, s. 12(3) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Necessary party - Order without impleadment of party - Land ceiling proceedings - Determination of surplus land - Allotment of surplus lands to landless persons - Possession given - Confirmation of right on such persons by allotment and delivery of possession - Subsequent writ petition challenging determination of surplus land - Held, allottees are necessary parties - Order in writ petition without impleading them not proper.
