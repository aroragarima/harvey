Rajesh D. Darbar & Ors. v Narasingrao Krishnaji Kulkarni & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 593; (2003) 7 SCC 219; JT 2003 (7) SC 209; 2003(6) SCALE 104; 2003 (4) SLT 849; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 273; 2003 (5) Supreme 455; 2003 (2) UC 1469; 2003 (4) UPLBEC 2881

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950 - s. 72(4) - Societies Registration Act, 1860 -Hindu Religious Institutions and Charitable Endowments Act, 1997 - Legality of membership and eligibility of 38 persons to participate in the elections of the society - Held, as the basic issue revolves around on the question of the legality of the membership and the eligibility of 38 persons to participate in the election held in the year 1996, let the election be held for the Committee under the directions and supervision of the Appellate Authority provided under the Endowments Act.
