Jonnalla Narashimharao and Company and Others v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 896; (1994) Supp3 SCC 688; AIR 1994 SC 1650; 1993 (112) CTR 126; [1993] 200 ITR 588; [1993] 68 TAXMAN 340; 1994 (2) TLR 562

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 28, 36 and 145 - Assessee, commission agent in Jaggery - Dispute of levy of sales tax on sale of Jaggery collected by assessee as Commission agent in Jaggery under name 'Rusum' - Held, since 'Rusum' collected is assessee's business income and levy of sales tax on sale of Jaggery upheld by Supreme Court - Appeal partly allowed.
