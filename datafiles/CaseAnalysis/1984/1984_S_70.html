Ex. Capt. K.C. Arora And Another v State Of Haryana And Others

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 147; (1984) 3 SCC 281; (1984) SCC (L&S) 520; AIR 1987 SC 1858; 1984 (49) FLR 173; 1984 LabIC 1015; 1984 (2) LLJ 362; 1984 (2) LLN 191; 1984(1) SCALE 651; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 623; 1984 (2) SLJ 54; 1984 (2) SLR 97; 1984 UJ 667

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Gujarat Panchayats Act, 1961, Gujarat Panchayats (Third Amendment) Act, 1978, Punjab National Emergency (Concession) Rules, 1965

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, arts. 14, 16, 19, 31, 309 and 311 - Punjab Government National Emergency (Concession) Rules, 1965 (Haryana), rr. 2 and 4(ii) - Constitutionality of amendment - Ex-Army officers commissioned during emergencies - Entitled to get their seniority fixed - Right was taken took away retrospectively - Held, that the amendment are ultra vires the Constitution - Appeal allowed.
