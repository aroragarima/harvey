Union of India v Ranbaxy Laboratories Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1650; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 123; (2008) 7 SCC 502; AIR 2008 SC 2286; 2008 (4) AWC 3490; 2008 DC 214; 2009 (1) MLJ(Crl) 380; 2008(6) SCALE 647; 2008(9) SCALE 371; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 315

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Sale Price, Price List, Bulk Drug, Weights And Measures, Retail Dealer, Drugs (Price Control) Order, 1995

Summary: Health & Drug - Drugs (Price Control) Order, 1995 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940, s. 3(2)(c) - Exemption on Bulk drug - What has been exempted is drug which was manufactured by company and area of exemption is from operation of price control - Whether exemption Notification would apply in respect of drugs which were manufactured upto 31-10-1999 or manufactured and sold upto said date? - Held, exemption Notification would apply only in respect of drugs which were manufactured upto 31-10-1999 - Appeal dismissed.
