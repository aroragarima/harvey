Supreme Court of India

27 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Venereal Disease

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 - Whether a marriage can be dissolved on the ground of irretrievable breakdown? - Held, there are several grounds for granting divorce e.g. cruelty, adultery, desertion etc. but no such ground of irretrievable breakdown of the marriage has been mentioned for granting divorce - Appeal dismissed.
