Â Bachan Singh and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, S.M. Sikri, A.N. Grover, D.G. Palekar, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 130; (1972) 3 SCC 489; AIR 1973 SC 441; 1973 LabIC 315; 1972 (2) LLJ 44; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 898; 1973 SLJ 204; 1972 SLR 397

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Military Engineering Services Class I (Recruitment Promotion and Seniority) Rules, 1951 - Applicability of r. 4 - To matter in which Govt. never infringed quota fixed for direct recruits to Military Engineer Class I Service, either by absorbing direct recruits or by confirming such recruits beyond quota fixed for them - Challenged by departmental promotees to such service - Held, they could not have course for complaint on ground of their seniority against absorption and confirmation of direct recruits - Appeal dismissed.
