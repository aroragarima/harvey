Madan Singh Shekhawat v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Defence Service

Summary: Army Act, 1950, s. 192 - Defence Service Regulations, Regns. 48 and 10 - Disability pension - Entitlement - Disability during casual leave to go home town - Travel undertaken authorisedly - Traveling at own cost - Held, entitled to disability pension.
