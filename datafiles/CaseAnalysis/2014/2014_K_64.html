Kichha Sugar Company Limited Through General Manager v Tarai Chini Mill Majdoor Union, Uttarkhand

Supreme Court of India

06 January 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

Chandramauli Kumar Prasad, Jagdish Singh Khehar

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 8; (2014) 4 SCC 37; AIR 2014 SC 898; 2014 (2) AWC 1504; 2014 (140) FLR 962; JT 2014 (1) SC 278; 2014 (1) LLJ 257; 2014(1) SCALE 106; [2014] 1 S.C.R. 157; 2014 (5) SLR 681; 2014 (2) UPLBEC 1198

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Employment-Hill development allowance-Held-Amount received as leave encashment and overtime wages is not fit to be taken into account for calculating hill development allowance-Impugned award and judgment of High Court affirming award of Industrial Tribunal directing payment of hill development allowance after taking into account amount received as leave encashment and overtime wages-Illegal and set aside.
