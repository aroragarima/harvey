Bharat Coking Coal Limited v Madanlal Agrawal

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1906; (1997) 1 SCC 177; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 415; AIR 1997 SC 1599; JT 1996 (10) SC 584; 1996(8) SCALE 472; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 886

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Mines & Minerals - Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Act, 1973, ss. 2(h), 3(3) and 26 - Mines Act, 1923 - Nationalisation - Properties of mine vesting in Central Govt. - Whether structures adjacent to mine used as office and quarters of staff of mine, vest in Central Govt. - Held, yes, by virtue of extended definition of mine in s. 2(h) of 1973 Act - Appeal allowed.
