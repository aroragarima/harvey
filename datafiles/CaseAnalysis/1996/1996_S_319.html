H.P.M.C. v Shri Suman Behari Sharma

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3466; (1996) 4 SCC 584; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1056; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 617; AIR 1996 SC 1353; 1996 (1) CLR 922; JT 1996 (5) SC 462; 1996 (2) LLJ 665; 1996 (2) LLN 501; 1996(3) SCALE 223; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 1069; 1996 (2) SLR 831; 1996 (3) Supreme 358; 1996 (2) UJ 437

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 16 and 311 - HP Horticultural Produce Marketing and Processing Corporation Ltd. Employees Service Bye-Laws, cl. 3.8, Para 5 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Right to voluntary retirement conferred on employee unde the service Bye-Laws is not the right to retire but only a right to ask for retirement - And permission to retire is required - Whether there is automatic retirement on expiry of notice period in absence of such permission - Held, no - Appeal allowed.
