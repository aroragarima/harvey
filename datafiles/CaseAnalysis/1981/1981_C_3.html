Welath Tax Officer, Calicut v C. K. Mammed Kayi (Since Deceased) Through His L.Rs. T.M.Poc

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 306; (1981) 3 SCC 23; (1981) SCC (Tax) 179; AIR 1981 SC 1269; 1981 (21) CTR 345; [1981] 129 ITR 307; 1981 KLT 451; 1981(1) SCALE 620; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 358; 1981 TAXLR 668; [1981] 6 TAXMAN 30; 1981 UJ 327

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s. 3 - Constitution, art.14 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 13(2) - Constitutionality of s. 3 of wealth tax Act - And scope of term 'individual' - Held, s. 3 is not violative of art. 14 - Term 'individual' includes a group of individuals like a Mapilla Tarwad - Appeal Allowed.
