Rajni Kumari v Raghvinder Sahay @ Lal Babu

Supreme Court of India

12 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2306; (2011) 11 SCC 176; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1065; 2009 (74) ALR 363; 2009 (3) AWC 2495; 2009(1) SCALE 315

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Section 25-Transfer of case-Wife seeking transfer of matrimonial case pending in Family Court at Khagaria, Bihar to Family Court at New Delhi-She is 24 years of age-Having no source of income-And fully dependent on her parents-Distance from Khagaria to Delhi is about 1500 kms.-Difficult for her to attend Court at Khagaria-Hence, case transferred to Family Court at New Delhi.
