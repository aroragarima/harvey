Ratilal Bhanji Mithani v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.P. Sen, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 92; (1979) 2 SCC 179; (1979) SCC (Cr) 405; AIR 1979 SC 94; 1979 CRLJ 41; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 993

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Commercial Documents Evidence Act, 1939

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 253, 254, 258, 403 and 439 - Practice & Procedure - (A) Complaint - Warrant case - Procedure - Magistrate cannot cancel a charge which is once framed or discharge accused - Order of discharge passed after framing of charge - Legality - (B) Revision against illegal order of discharge - Power of High Court to interfere with - Held, is illegal - Even assuming it to be order of acquittal, HC has power to interfere and order retrial and s. 403 is no bar - Appeal dismissed.
