U.P. State Sugar Corporation Ltd. v Jain Construction Co. & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

25 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 680; (2004) 7 SCC 332; AIR 2004 SC 4335; 2004 (3) ARBLR 1; 2004 (4) AWC 3080; [2004] 122 Comp Cas 145; 2004 (4) CompLJ 370; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 38; JT 2004 (7) SC 61; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 248; 2004(7) SCALE 307; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 826

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Foreign Award, Repeal

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 20 - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 21 and 85(2)(a) - Applicability of which Act in facts of case - Disputes and differences between the parties arose in the year 1991 - Respondent filed an application u/s.20 of the 1940 Act on 1/5/1991 - Arbitral proceeding was set in motion - 1940 Act and not 1996 Act applies - Matter remanded to HC - Appeal allowed.
