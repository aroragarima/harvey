Supreme Court of India

05 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 334; (2000) 7 SCC 357; AIR 2000 SC 2957; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 55; 2001 (43) ALR 602; 2001 (1) BC 1; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 53; JT 2000 (10) SC 125; 2001 (1) MLJ(SC) 100; 2001 (1) RCR(Civil) 168; 2000(6) SCALE 283; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 153; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 2394

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Recovery of Debts Due to Banks and Financial Institutions Act, 1993, ss. 31, 18 and 34 - Transfer of pending cases - Suit pending in Court immediately before date of establishment of Tribunal - On crucial date not suit but appeal against decree passed in suit pending - Appeal subsequently allowed and matter remanded to Trial Court - Remand order has effect of reviving suit from date it was originally filed - Held, suit was therefore pending on crucial date - Liable to be transferred.
