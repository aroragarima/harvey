Kulwinder Kaur @ Kulwinder Gurcharan Singh v Kandi Friends Education Trust and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 37; (2008) 3 SCC 659; AIR 2008 SC 1333; 2008 (2) ALD(SC) 112; 2008(1) ALL MR 914; 2008 (70) ALR 471; 2008 (1) AWC 533; JT 2008 (1) SC 371; 2008 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1526; 2008 (3) Law Herald (P&H) 1994; 2008 (5) MahLJ 1; 2008 (3) MLJ(SC) 511; 2008 (4) MPLJ 9; 2008 (150) PLR 22; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 821; 2008 (1) RCR(Rent) 188; 2008 (104) RD 294; 2008(1) SCALE 414; [2008] 1 S.C.R. 556

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Suit for a declaration that all proceedings conducted by the defendants in the Meeting in which the appellant was elected as the Chairperson were illegal, null and void and liable to be set aside - Transferred the suit to Chandigarh - Appeal against - Held, High Court without stating anything whatsoever as to allegations and counter-allegations, without considering the reply submitted by the appellant and without recording any reason/ground passed the impugned order transferring the case - An order of transfer must reflect application of mind by the Court and the circumstances which weighed in taking the action - Appeal allowed.
