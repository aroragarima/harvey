Shree Baidyanath Ayurved Bhawan Pvt. Ltd. v Praveen Bhatia & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 August 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 989; (2009) 8 SCC 779; 2010 (2) AWC 1540; 2009 (4) CompLJ 329; 2009 (4) RCR(Civil) 592; 2009(11) SCALE 256; [2009] 12 S.C.R. 353

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Transfer petition allowed

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss.9 and 34 - Transfer petition - Company entered into contract of carrying and forwarding agency with firm (respondent No. 5 and its partners) - As per arbitration clause in contract jurisdiction of Court to determine dispute between parties was fixed at Jhansi - Dispute aroused between parties - Arbitration clause invoked - Company appointed one Arbitrator 'S' - Firm, however, appointed another arbitrator - Two different awards made by arbitrators - Objections to said awards filed by both parties - Company filed objection in District Court, Jhansi and respondents filed in Court of District Judge, Ludhiana - Firm also filed applications u/s.9 of Act against company in Court of District Judge, Ludhiana - Company filed present petition seeking transfer of cases to Court of Jhansi - Parties hereto are governed by terms of contract - If, in terms of provisions of contract, they by agreement conferred jurisdiction on one of courts which would have otherwise jurisdiction to deal with matter, same should ordinarily be given effect to - Held, cases directed to be transferred to Jhansi - Transfer petition allowed.
