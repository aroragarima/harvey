Govt. Of India, Represented By Secretary,Ministry Of Finance v Dhanalakshmi Paper And Board Millstiruchirupalli

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 296; (1989) Supp1 SCC 596; AIR 1989 SC 665; 1989 CRLR 146; 1989 (19) ECC 192; 1989 (20) ECR 167; 1989 (39) E.L.T. 171; JT 1988 (4) SC 677; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 1051; 1989 (1) ShimLC 352; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 1051

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Constitution - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Notification D/- 01-03-1964, Proviso (3), cl. (a) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Paper board - Exemption from duty - Fixation of particular date - Explanation for choice of that date not given - Clause of notification providing that date - Validity - Held, invalid - Petition dismissed.
