S.S. Bhola and Others v B.D. Sardana and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2703; (1997) 8 SCC 522; AIR 1997 SC 3127; JT 1997 (6) SC 637; 1997(5) SCALE 90; 1997 (5) SLR 1; [1997] Supp2 S.C.R. 507; 1997 (7) Supreme 427

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Fixation of seniority - Promotion - Quota of promotees - Dispute relating to fixation of seniority of promotees and direct recruits in Roads and Buildings Branch arose - First set of seniority list was drawn up - Being aggrieved by said seniority list Writ Petitions filed, was quashed - Seniority list which was drawn up subsequently, took ten incumbents originally constituted service belonging to the quota of promotees since factually all of them were promotees under Punjab Rules - Inter se seniority of direct recruits was determined later basic changes made in Act by altering definition of service by addition of sub clause (c), by providing quota of promotees could exceed beyond 50% - Validity of Act was challenged by the direct recruits - Whether promotion already given on seniority basis under pre-existing rules would be annulled notwithstanding any change in Act - Held, no - Promotion already given on seniority basis under pre-existing rules would not be annulled notwithstanding any change in seniority to be determined under the Act - Impugned judgments of Lower Courts set aside - State Govt. directed to re-consider issue of seniority of employees within six months - Impugned judgment by Lower Court erroneous and could not be sustained - Appeals disposed of.
