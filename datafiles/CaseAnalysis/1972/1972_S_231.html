Krishnamurthi and Company v State of Madras and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 67; (1973) 1 SCC 75; (1973) SCC (Tax) 114; AIR 1972 SC 2455; 1972 (1) CTR 361; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 54; 1973 (31) STC 190; 1973 TAXLR 1601

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: T. N. General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1967, ss. 2 and 4 - Constitution, art. 19(1)(g) - Retrospective operation of provisions u/ss.2 and 4 - Constitutionality of - Held, is not violative of art.19(1)(g) as it does not constitute unreasonable restriction on the right of the dealers to carry on their trade and business - Appeals dismissed.
