Union Of India v Jyoti Chit Fund & Finance & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 119; (1976) 3 SCC 607; AIR 1976 SC 1163; 1976 (2) ALR 470; 1976 (2) ALR 726; 1976 (33) FLR 162; 1976 LabIC 773; 1976 (2) LLJ 69; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 763; 1976 (2) SLR 15; 1976 UJ 379

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Pensions Act, 1871

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 60(1), Proviso (g) and (k) - Provident Funds Act, 1925, s. 2, 2(a), 3 and 4 - Pensions Act, 1871, s. 11 - Service Matter - Amount in hands of Govt. due to employee towards PF and pensionary benefits is not liable to attachment - Objection as to non-attachability - Locus standi - Determination of UOI has locus standi to raise objection as to non-attachability - Appeal Allowed.
