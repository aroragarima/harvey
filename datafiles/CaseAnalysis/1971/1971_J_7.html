Jadunath Singh and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, S.M. Sikri, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 613; (1971) 3 SCC 577; (1971) SCC (Cr) 726; AIR 1972 SC 116; 1972 CRLJ 29; 1971 UJ 789

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 417, 418 and 423 - Provision of s. 423 - Appeal from acquittal - High Court has power to review at large all evidence and concluding that order of acquittal should be reversed - Held, HC has full power to review at large all evidence and to reach conclusion that upon that evidence, order of acquittal should be reversed - Appeal dismissed.
