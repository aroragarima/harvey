Tara and Others v Director, Social Wlfare and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1847; (1998) 8 SCC 671; AIR 1999 SC 1508; 1999 LabIC 228; 1998 (2) LLJ 632

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Dispute Act, 1947, s. 33 - Payment of wages - Rejection of - Instant appeal filed against award of the Labour Court rejecting the applications made by the appellants u/s. 33-C(2) of the Act for payment of wages at the rate claimed by the appellants - Labour Court, placing reliance on the decision of SC in Municipal Corpn. of Delhi v. Ganesh Razak 1994 Indlaw SC 512 held that the applications were not maintainable u/s. 33-C(2) of the Act - Hence, instant appeal.

Held, the status and nature of employment of the appellants is itself disputed and unless there is a prior adjudication on merits of the status which is the foundation for making the claim for wages at the specified rates, the question of moving an application u/s. 33-C(2) for computation of the wages does not arise. Appeal dismissed.
