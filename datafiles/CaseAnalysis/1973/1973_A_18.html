State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v Andhra Provincial Potteries Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 261; (1973) 2 SCC 786; (1973) SCC (Cr) 1062; AIR 1973 SC 2429; [1973] 43 Comp Cas 514; 1973 CRLJ 1616; 1973 CrLR(SC) 593; 1973 (2) CTR 433; 1974 (1) MLJ(SC) 105; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 410

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s.220(3) - (A) Companies Act, 1956 - Failure to file copies of balance sheet and profit and loss account with Registrar - Punishment - Omission to call a general body meeting - Liability of person responsible - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Penal provisions - Interpretation - Held, it although may expose directors to punishment u/ss 76 and 133, no offence can be said to have been committed u/s 134 - In interpreting a penal provision it is not permissible to give an extended meaning to plain words of Section on ground that a principle recognised in respect of certain other provisions of law requires that this section should be interpreted in same way - Appeal dismissed.
