Dolhin Padharo Devi v Indrajeet Tiwary & Anr

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 121; (2008) 14 SCC 508; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 94; 2008 (3) JhrCR(SC) 37; 2008(2) SCALE 154; [2008] 2 S.C.R. 241

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1961

Summary: Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1961, ss. 43, 16(3) - Exclusion of jurisdiction of civil court by statute - Such a bar cannot operate where the plea raised before the Civil Court goes to the root of the matter and could, if upheld, lead to the conclusion that the impugned order is a nullity - not in dispute that the S.B.L.R. and/or the Additional Collector, who passed the order in the ceiling cases and/ or appeal petitions had such jurisdiction to decide the dispute raised under Section 16(3) of the Ceiling Act - Appeals dismissed.
