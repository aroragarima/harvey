Banwasi Sewa Ashram v State Of U.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 258; (1986) 4 SCC 753; AIR 1987 SC 374; 1987 CRLR 319; 1986(2) SCALE 867; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 336

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised OccUttar Pradeshants) Act 1972

Summary: Constitution - Land and Property - Constitution of India, 1950 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Uttar Pradesh Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act 1972 - Forest Act, 1927, ss. 4, 20 - Question that required detailed consideration was relating to the claim of the Adivasis living within forest land as to land and related rights - Part of Forest land declared as reserved forest by State Govt. and notification u/s. 4 issued as regards remaining forest - Writ by occupants, Adivasis and backward people - Direction issued by SC that there should be no dispossession of occupants - State Govt. initiating proceedings for acquisition of lands for construction of super thermal plant, a matter of national importance - Held, SC allowed acquisition - However , directions to safeguard interest of Advisees (Tribals) and backward people occupying the land given - Directions issued.
