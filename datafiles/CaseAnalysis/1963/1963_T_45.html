Tata Oil Mills Company, Limited v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 21; AIR 1966 SC 1672; 1966 (13) FLR 65; 1966 (2) LLJ 602; 1964 (1) SCJ 382; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 125

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Reinstatement - Validity -

Whether Labour court was right in directing appellant to reinstate respondent who was terminated? - Documentary evidence submitted in respect of respondent's conduct was overlooked on ground that no specific instance were cited - Appellant stated in clear terms defects in respondents work and defects are specific - Held, appellant acted bona fide in discharging respondent from service - Respondent's explanation was not satisfactory - Order passed by Labour Court set aside - Appeal allowed.
