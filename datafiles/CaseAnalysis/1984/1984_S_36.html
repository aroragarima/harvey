Eastern Spinning Mills, Shri Virendra Kumar v Shri Rajiv Poddar and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 18; (1989) Supp2 SCC 385; AIR 1985 SC 1668; 1985 CRLJ 1858

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 156 and 482 - Special leave petitions directed against interim orders the High Court - High Court interfering and staying investigation of offences by investigating officer performing statutory duty under the CrPC, 1973 - Permissibility - Held, permissible only if non-interference would result in miscarriage of justice - Petition disposed of.
