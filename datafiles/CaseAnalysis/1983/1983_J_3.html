Â State Of Punjab v Jagdev Singh Talwandi

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen, D.P. Madon, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 249; (1984) SCC (Cr) 135; (1984) 1 SCC 596; AIR 1984 SC 444; 1984 (1) Crimes 224; 1984 CRLJ 177; 1984 CrLR(SC) 409; 1983(2) SCALE 942; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 50

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Attorney-General, Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971 - Appeal challenging the order of detention - Respondents detained on the basis of two provocative speeches made by him - Reports and evidence furnished by CID - Valiity of detention - Held, detention order was proper - Appeal allowed.
