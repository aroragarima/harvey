Nirmal Singh v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 202; (1984) Supp SCC 407; (1985) SCC (L&S) 38; AIR 1984 SC 1619; 1984 LabIC 1312; 1984 (2) LLJ 396; 1984 (2) LLN 526; 1984(2) SCALE 115; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 317

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938, s. 12(5) and 2(s.) - Industrial Disputes - Reference - Labour Commr. declined to refer dispute - Ground, not 'workman' u/s. 2 (s) of Act - No reasons stated in order - Supreme Court directed Labour Commr. to make reference - Appeal allowed.
