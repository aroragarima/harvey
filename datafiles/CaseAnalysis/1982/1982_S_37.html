State Of Andhra Pradesh v Mohd Ashrafuddin

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.D. Koshal, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 126; (1982) 2 SCC 1; AIR 1982 SC 913; 1982(1) SCALE 139; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 482

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Defective Title

Summary: A.P. Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Act, 1973, s. 3(i) - Interpretation of Statutes - Expression 'held' in definition of 'holding' u/s. 3 of the Act - Connotes both ownership as well as possession - Agreement for sale - Land transferred by unregistered sale deed - Srtatus of transferor - Held, transferor is holding as owner, although he is not in possession - Appeal Allowed.
