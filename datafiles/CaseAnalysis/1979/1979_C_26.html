Madhav Prasad Jatia v Commissioner Of Income Tax, U.P., Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 240; (1979) SCC (Tax) 279; (1979) 3 SCC 634; AIR 1979 SC 1291; 1979 (10) CTR 375; [1979] 118 ITR 200; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 745; 1979 TAXLR 977; [1979] 1 TAXMAN 477

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss.10(2)(iii) and 10(2)(xv), 12(2) - Evidence Act, 1872, s.115 - (A) Deduction - Scope for allowing deduction u/ss. 10(2) (iii) and 10(2)(xv) - (B) Whether interest on borrowing completed unrelated to purpose of business and used for charity can be allowed as deduction - Held scope for allowing deduction is wider - Not allowable as deduction either u/ss. 10 (2)(iii) or 10(2)(xv) - Appeal dismissed.
