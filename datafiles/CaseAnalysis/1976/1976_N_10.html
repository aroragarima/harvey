Narandas Karsondas v S.A. Kamtam and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 345; (1977) 3 SCC 247; AIR 1977 SC 774; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 341

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, ss. 54, 60 and 69 - Specific Relief Act, 1963 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 182 - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17 - Property - Mortgagee - Conferment of power to sell without intervention of Court - If he acts as agent of mortgagor while selling - Sale not complete by registration - Mortgagor, if has a right to redeem - Held, mortgagor has a right to redeem unless sale of property was complete by registration - Appeal dismissed.
