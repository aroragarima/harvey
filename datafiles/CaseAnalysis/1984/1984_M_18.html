Supreme Court of India

05 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 149; (1984) 3 SCC 352; AIR 1985 SC 817; 1984 ALJ 102; 1984 (10) ALR 115; 1984 (1) RCR(Rent) 301; 1984 (1) RentLR 250; 1984(1) SCALE 31; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 333; 1984 UJ 778

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 2(2), 20, 39 and 40 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 6, r.17 - Property - Building - On date of suit, not ten years - Completing ten years' pendelite - (A) Provisions of Act, if attracted - (B) Amendment for adding facts, maintainability - Held, provisions attracted - Maintainable - Appeal partly allowed.
