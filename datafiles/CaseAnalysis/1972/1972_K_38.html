State of Assam v Krishna Rao

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 196; (1973) SCC (Cr) 222; (1973) 3 SCC 227; AIR 1973 SC 28; 1973 CRLJ 169; 1973 CrLR(SC) 304; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 239

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Illegal Gratification, Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Criminal - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - Presumption u/s. 4 - Receipt with guilty conscience - If required - Mere factum of receipt of money raises presumption, held receipt with guilty conscience not necessary for raising presumption - Appeal allowed.
