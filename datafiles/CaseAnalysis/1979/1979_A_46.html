Authorised Officer, Thanjavur and Another v S. Naganatha Ayyar

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 224; (1979) 3 SCC 466; AIR 1979 SC 1487; 1980 (1) MLJ 34; 1980 (1) MLJ(SC) 34; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 1121

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Tamil Nadu Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961, s. 22 - Transfer of surplus land - Whether s. 22 operates only in case of transfers which are sham and specifically intended to defeat Act? - Held, if any transfer defeats provisions of Act by reducing extent of surplus land in excess of ceiling available from any person such transaction bona fide or not, is void in matter of computation of permissible area and surplus area - From landless community's angle hungering for allotment, alienation, however necessary for individual, is not bona fide vis-a-vis community - Appeal allowed.
