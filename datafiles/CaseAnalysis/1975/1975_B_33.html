Bharat Iron Works v Bhagubhai Balubhai Patel and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, A. Alagiriswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 381; (1976) SCC (L&S) 92; (1976) 1 SCC 518; AIR 1976 SC 98; 1976 (32) FLR 72; 1976 LabIC 4; 1976 (1) LLN 19; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 280

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Trade Unions (Amendment) Act, 1947

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33 - Constitution - Power of Tribunal to interfere in industrial dispute - Pleading and proof of victimisation - Dismissal of writ application by company in limine - When manifest error of law on part of Tribunal in concluding that management was guilty of victimisation - Held, to be not correct - Appeal allowed.
