National Radio Corporation v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 3; 1963 (6) FLR 8; 1963 (1) LLJ 282

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Transfer - Validity - Whether tribunal right fully held that transfer was invalid and unjustified, and directed retransfer of eight workmen? - Evidence led by employees is more reliable than that led by appellant - First order of transfer was passed before licence for expansion was obtained -Documentary evidence proves that appellants contentions of business running in a loss, is untrue - Appellants frightened employees by undertaking to sell company and adopted unfair labour practice - Held, transfer is not justified and bonafide - Appeal dismissed.
