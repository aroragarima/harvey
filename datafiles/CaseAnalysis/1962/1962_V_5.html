Â S. C. Prashar And The Income Tax Officer, Market Ward, Bombay v Vasantsen Dwarkadas and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Raghubar Dayal, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 313; AIR 1963 SC 1356; [1963] 49 ITR 1; [1964] 1 S.C.R. 29

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Definite Information, Constitutionality, Finance Act, 1956, Income Tax (Amendment) Act, 1953, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Finance Act, 1958, Income-tax and Business Profits-Tax (Amendment) Act, 1948, Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1959

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 34(1)(a), 34(3) - Travancore Income-tax Act, 1947, s.47 - Assessment of Income - Tax Evaders - Escaped Assessment - Violation of Constitution - Appellant found income of respondent escaped assessment while action was not taken against persons contemplated by s. 47 of the 1947 Act while action taken against other tax evaders contemplated by s. 34 (1) of 1922 Act as amended - HC decided in favor of assesse that s. 34 (3) of the 1922 Act violate art. 14 of the Constitution - Hence, instant appeal - Whether s. 34 (3) of the 1922 Act violate art. 14 of the Constitution - Held, persons who came u/s. 34 (1) (a) of the Act after the amendment of 1948 were those in respect of whose income the Income-tax Officer has reason to believe that due to certain conduct on their part their income has escaped assessment while action can be taken against persons contemplated by the second proviso against those persons alone with respect to whose escaped income some authority had given a finding or directions - Latter persons would therefore correspond to persons contemplated by s. 47 of the 1947 Act while other tax evaders contemplated by s. 34 (1) of the 1922 Act as amended in 1948 would correspond to persons contemplated by s. 5 (1) of Investigation Commission Act and hence s. 34 (3) of the 1922 Act did not violate art. 14 of the Constitution - Appeal allowed.
