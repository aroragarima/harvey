Commissioner of Income Tax v Prithipal Singh and Company

Supreme Court of India

21 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, R.C. Lahoti, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 741; 2001 (166) CTR 187; [2001] 249 ITR 670; [2001] 118 TAXMAN 330

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 271(1)(c) - Held, SC have heard counsel and find that on facts of case, no interference is called for. Appeal dismissed.
