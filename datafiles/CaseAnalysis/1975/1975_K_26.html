Khadim Hussain v State Of U.P. & Others

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 308; (1976) 1 SCC 843; AIR 1976 SC 417; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 1; 1976 UJ 302

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: U.P. Town Improvement Act, 1919, ss. 4 and 8 - Land Acquisition (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1967, s. 4(2) - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 6 - (A) Trustees appointed in 1963 - But its term of office started in 1966 (after repeal of U.P. Town Improvement Act) - Framing of Scheme by the Trustees - Validity - (B) Nature of prohibition u/s. 4(2) of 1967 - Held, framing valid - Prohibition is to a declaration made beyond specified period and there is no prohibition to a notification u/s. 4 of the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
