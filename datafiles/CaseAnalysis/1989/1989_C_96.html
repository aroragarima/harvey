Omprakash v Commissioner of Police and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 277; (1989) Supp2 SCC 576; (1990) SCC (Cr) 198; AIR 1990 SC 496; 1989 (2) CCC 203; 1990 (1) Crimes 235; 1990 CRLJ 328; 1990 CRLR 566; JT 1989 (4) SC 177; 1989 (2) UJ 733

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - Gujarat Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act, 1985, ss. 3(1) and 4(3) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Grounds of detention - Appellant a bootlegger - Accused of beating people who did not store his prohibited liquor - Detained for he was becoming a threat to public order - Held, activities of accused do not constitute threat to public order and is therefore, liable to be released - Petition allowed.
