National Buildings Construction Corporation v Pritam Singh Gill And Others

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 381; (1972) 2 SCC 1; AIR 1972 SC 1579; 1972 (25) FLR 248; 1972 LabIC 857; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 40

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, Demotion, Air Force Act, 1950

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947- Provisions of s. 33C(2) - If invoked - By a dismissed workman in respect of benefits and salary due to him for period prior to date of his dismissal - Held, section can be invoked - Appeal dismissed.
