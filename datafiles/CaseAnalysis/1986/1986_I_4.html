Madras Bangalore Transport Company (West) v Inder Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, G.L. Oza, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 748; (1986) 3 SCC 62; AIR 1986 SC 1564; 1986 (2) CCC 771; 1986 (2) RCJ 404; 1986(1) SCALE 989; 1986 (2) UJ 498

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(b) - Subletting - Tenant firm establishing a limited company with its partners as Directors - Company to operate from premises - Whether amount to assignment or parting with possession - Held, although firm and company were separate legal entities there was no subletting or assignment so as to attract s. 14(1)(b) - Appeal allowed.
