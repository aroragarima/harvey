Deputy Commissioner of Sales Tax (Law), Board of Revenue (Taxes), Ernakulam v Coco Fibres

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 229; (1992) Supp1 SCC 290; AIR 1991 SC 378; JT 1990 (4) SC 618; 1991 (80) STC 249; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 419

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963, s. 5A - Purchase tax - Converting coconut husk into coconut fibre - Is it a manufacturing process? - Turnover of purchasing coconut husk - If exigible to tax u/s. 5A - Held, is exigible - Appeal allowed.
