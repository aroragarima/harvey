Jitendra Nath Biswas v Messrs Empire of India and Ceylone Tea Company and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 384; (1989) SCC (L&S) 552; (1989) 3 SCC 582; AIR 1990 SC 255; 1990 (3) CCC 488; 1989 (3) CompLJ 99; 1989 (59) FLR 770; JT 1989 (3) SC 310; 1990 LabIC 308; 1989 (2) LLJ 572; 1989(2) SCALE 158; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 640; 1989 (4) SLR 214; 1989 (2) UJ 612

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10 and 12(5) - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 14 - Relief of reinstatement and back wages is only available only under Act of 1947 - Whether same can be granted by Civil Court? - Held, it cannot be granted by Civil Court - Its jurisdiction, impliedly excluded, in respect of remedies available under Act and for which a complete procedure and machinery has been provided in Act of 1947 - Appeal dismissed.
