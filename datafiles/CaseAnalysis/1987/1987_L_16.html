R.D. Gupta And Ors. Etc. v Lt. Governor. Delhi Admn, And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28301; (1987) SCC (L&S) 470; (1987) 4 SCC 505; AIR 1987 SC 2086; 1987 (5) ATC 65; JT 1987 (3) SC 259; 1987 LabIC 1670; 1987(2) SCALE 226; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 808; 1987 (4) SLR 711

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Ex Gratia Payment

Summary: Punjab Municipal Act, 1911, s. 39 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 39(d) - Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 278 - Ministerial staff of NDMC constitute a unified cadre - But non-technical staff in Electricity wing granted higher pay scales - Whether arbitrary and discriminatory ? - Ancillary questions - Whether civil engineers in electricity wing are not different from electrical engineers ? - Payment of salary or ex gratia amount to staff - Held, arbitrary and discriminatory - They are different - Uniformity in payment of and common date from which Shiv Shankar Committee pay scales and ex gratia amount are to be paid - CA Nos. 2970 of 1983 and 2971 of 1983 and CA No. 6074 of 1983 and WP No. 9266 of 1983 dismissed, CA No. 2969 of 1983 and (SLP No. 11270 of 1982) numbered as CA No. 1688 of 1983 partly allowed.
