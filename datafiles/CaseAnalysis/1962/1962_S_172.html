Sankatha Singh v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Raghubar Dayal, S.K. Das, K. Subba Rao

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 132; AIR 1962 SC 1208; 1963 (2) ALT(SC) 271; 1962 CRLJ 288; [1962] Supp2 S.C.R. 817

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 424 and 369 -Whether Appellate Court is having power to review its order and set it aside?- Held, appellate Court has no power to review or restore an appeal which has been disposed of under ss. 369 and 424 of CrPC, 1898 - Appellate Court is not to alter or review judgment once signed, except for purpose of correcting a clerical error - Appeal Dismissed.
