Â Collector of Central Excise, Jaipur v Man Structurals Limited

Supreme Court of India

19 April 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Shivaraj V. Patil, V.N. Khare, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 319; (2009) 17 SCC 550; 2001 (130) E.L.T. 401; 2001 (44) RLT 113

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff, Tariff Item 73.08 - Structurals - Marketability - Whether structurals exigible to excise duty as they were new, identifiable goods which were produced as a result of manufacture or processes and which were marketable? - Held, Tribunal has failed to consider facts of even a single of appeals before it - Tribunal simply proceeded upon basis that structurals are not exigible to excise duty - Matter remanded back for fresh hearing - Appeal disposed of.
