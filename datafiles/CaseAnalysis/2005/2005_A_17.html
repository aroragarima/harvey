State of Andhra Pradesh and another v A.P. Pensioners Association and others

Supreme Court of India

11 November 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 933; (2005) 13 SCC 161; (2006) SCC (L&S) 666; AIR 2006 SC 407; JT 2005 (10) SC 115; 2005(9) SCALE 323; 2006 (1) SLR 57; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 223

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Commutation Of Pension

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16, 309 - Andra Pradesh Revised Pension Rules, 1980, r. 46 - Andra Pradesh Civil Pension Commutation Rules, 1944, r. 3(d) - Pay Scale Revision came into force on 01-07-1998 with financial benefits from 01-04-1999 - Claim for payment of pensionary benefits in accordance with revised rules by employees retiring between said dates - Monetary benefits were payable from 01-04-1999 - Right to get benefits computed on basis of revised pay scale would be effected from that date only - Intention of State was not to grant any benefit towards payment of gratuity even in relation to those employees who had retired in between 01-07-1998 and 31-03-1999 - Appeal allowed.
