Union of India and Others v N. Y. Apte and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 909; (1998) 6 SCC 741; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1673; AIR 1998 SC 2651; 1998 (2) CLR 634; JT 1998 (5) SC 286; 1998 LabIC 3104; 1998 (4) LLN 645; 1998(4) SCALE 376; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 988; 1999 (1) SLJ 308; 1998 (5) SLR 88; 1998 (6) SLT 571; 1998 (6) Supreme 214

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 16 - Indian Meteorological Service (Group A Posts) Recruitment (Amendment) Rules, 1983, r. 1 - Indian Meteorological Service (Group A Posts) Recruitment Rules, 1978, r. 1 - Equation of posts - On basis of longer experience in lower post - Similar educational qualifications and scales of pay - Held, is rational - 1978 and 1983 rules equating post for widening field of consideration for promotion to Class 1 Gazetted posts - Not void or ultra vires - Constitutional.
