Randhir Singh and Another v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

12 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 859; (2004) 13 SCC 129; (2005) SCC (Cr) 56; AIR 2004 SC 5097; 2004 CRLJ 4985; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 740; 2004(8) SCALE 689; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 351

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - IPC, ss 306 r/w 34 - Abetment of suicide - Trial court imposed sentence of imprisonment for five years - HC maintained sentence so far as appellant no 1 but reduced sentence so far as appellant no 2 is concerned - Appeal against conviction - Held, elaborate analysis of evidence of trial court and HC no infirmity in conclusion arrived at regarding guilt of accused persons - Conviction is, therefore, confirmed - Appeal disposed of.
