Ashadevi, Wife Of Gopal Ghermal Mehta (Detenu) v K. Shiveraj,Addl. Chief Secretary To The Government Ofgujara

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 61; (1979) 1 SCC 222; (1979) SCC (Cr) 262; AIR 1979 SC 447; 1979 CRLJ 203; 1979 CrLR(SC) 9; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 215; 1979 UJ 92

Case Digest

Subject: Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Acts - Detention order u/s. 3(1)(iii) - Non consideration of material or vital facts likely to influence mind of authority - Held, subjective satisfaction of authority is vitiated which renders detention order invalid - Appeal allowed.
