Supreme Court of India

29 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 287; (2002) 4 SCC 503; AIR 2002 SC 2426; 2002 (2) AWC 1598; 2002 (2) CLR 406; 2002 (3) ESC 5; 2002 (93) FLR 971; JT 2002 (4) SC 467; 2002 LabIC 2324; 2002 (2) LLJ 1057; 2002(4) SCALE 228; [2002] 3 S.C.R. 609; 2002 (4) SLR 85; 2002 (4) SLT 93; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1582

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Employment - Termination-Assistant Commissioner of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (K.V.S.) appointed certain L.D.Cs. on 13/15 December, 1997 when his services were already terminated on December 11, 1997-As he had obtained interim order by High Court on writ petition which was ultimately dismissed-High Court itself declaring that orders passed by petitioner-Assistant Commissioner after his termination will be treated as null and void-Whether appellants justified in terminating services of persons appointed by Assistant Commissioner without giving opportunity?-Held, âyesâ.
