S.P. Jain v Krishna Mohan Gupta and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 607; (1987) 1 SCC 191; AIR 1987 SC 222; 1987 ALJ 88; JT 1986 SC 979; 1987 (1) RCJ 651; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 347; 1987 (1) RentLR 75; 1986(2) SCALE 931; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 411; 1987 (1) UJ 182

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Rent Control

Summary: Land and Property - UP Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 24B, 24C - Constitution, art.136 ; Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - First floor of building in cantonment area given on lease, while ground floor in own posession for seprate use - Landlord in military service - Application u/s. 24C ot the 1972 Act , for recovery of possession - Act not applicable to building in question at that time - Order allowing application however passed - Revision application against filed - Act applicable when filed - Dismissed - Ground , Act did not apply when application was made - (A) Sustainability of dismissal - (B) Whether recourse to urgency provisions of s. 24-B of the 1972 Act can be taken - Held, dismissal a futile exercise , as application if now again filed, is bound to succeed under new law , SC refuse to interfere u/art. 136 - Landlord not entitled to take the recourse for getting his premises vacated - Appeal dismissed.
