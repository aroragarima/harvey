Gojer Bros. Pvt. Ltd. v Ratan Lal Singh

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 267; (1974) 2 SCC 453; AIR 1974 SC 1380; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 394

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: W.B. Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, s. 17D - Board Of Revenue Act, 1913 - CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 1 - Availability of benefit u/s. 17D of 1956 Act - Interpretation of expression ' before the commencement of W.B. Premises Tenancy (Amendment) Act 1968' - Decree - Merger of that decree of trial Court (passed in 1958) in that of High Court in second appeal in 1969 - Held, s. 17-D not applicable - Appeal allowed.
