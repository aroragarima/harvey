Vadivelu Thevar v State of Madras

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, B. Jagannadhadas

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 71; AIR 1957 SC 614; 1957 ALJ 898; 1957 CRLJ 1000; 1957 SCJ 527; [1957] 1 S.C.R. 981

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 134 - Oaths Act, 1873 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 109 and 326 - Murder - Conviction based upon testimony of a single witness, depend upon facts of each case - Scope of s. 134, Evidence Act, 1872 discussed - Held, appeal dismissed.
