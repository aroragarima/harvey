V. G. George v Indian Rare Earths Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1407; (1999) 3 SCC 762; AIR 1999 SC 1409; 1999 (2) ARBLR 47; 1999 (2) AWC 1523; 1999 (3) CalLT(SC) 25; JT 1999 (2) SC 629; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 531

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30(a) - Award - Non speaking - Interference by High Court - Award contains sum awarded plus case of parties, issues framed and findings thereon - Non consideration of a clause in tender notice - Awarding escalation in wages of mining workers contrary to self observation - Validity - Held, not non speaking award - HC can interfere - Findings beyond scope of agreement amounting to misconduct of proceedings - Award liable to be set aside.
