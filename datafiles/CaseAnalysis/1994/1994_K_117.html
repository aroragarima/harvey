Kunwar Singh and Others v Sri Thakurji Maharaj

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1660; (1995) Supp4 SCC 125

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedrure - Delay in rendering judgment - Case not listed for judgment pronouncement - Whether long delay and lack of notification of pronouncement affect validity of judgment? - Held, it is not necessary to pronounce finally on these contentions - In facts and circumstances of this case, ends of justice would require that matter be reheard by High Court - Appeal disposed of.
