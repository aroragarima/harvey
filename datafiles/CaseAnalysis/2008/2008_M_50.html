The Apsrtc, Rep. By Its General Manager And Anr. v M. Ramadevi and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 87; (2008) 2 SCC (Cr) 42; (2008) 3 SCC 379; 2008 (1) ACC 356; AIR 2008 SC 1221; 2008 (71) ALR 164; 2008 (1) CCC 101; 2008 (2) JhrCR(SC) 154; 2008 (3) MLJ(SC) 276; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 885; 2008 (3) RLW 2189; 2008(2) SCALE 10; [2008] 2 S.C.R. 22

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Non-Pecuniary Damages

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 166 - Appellant-Corporation questioning correctness of the award made by the Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal - Tribunal awarded compensation of Rs.2, 46, 000/- and to be paid with interest @ 12% p.a - High Court enhanced the amount to Rs.3, 35, 952/- - Appeal against - Whether income has been rightly adopted by the Tribunal High Court was correct?; Whether the correct multiplier was adopted? - Held, considering the figure in the Ex.A/7 the monthly income taken at Rs.3, 000/- after deducting 1/3rd there from the annual contribution is fixed at Rs.24, 000/- - Adopting the multiplier of 10, the amount payable to the claimants comes to Rs.2, 40, 000/- to that shall be added the amount of Rs.20, 000/- fixed by the Tribunal for non- pecuniary damages and consortium as there was no challenge by the Corporation to the award of such amounts - Entitlement of the claimant comes to Rs.2, 60, 000/- - Appeal partly allowed.
