M.C. Mehta v Union of India and Others on behalf of Monitoring

Supreme Court of India

18 September 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 769; (2003) 8 SCC 696; AIR 2003 SC 800; 2003 (3) AllCJ 1825; 2003 (4) AWC 3334; JT 2003 (Supp1) SC 391; 2003 (1) SCW 6997; [2003] Supp3 S.C.R. 925

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Socio-Economic; Water

Summary: Serious irregularities/illegalities committed in carrying out the so-called Taj Heritage Corridor Project - Removal of industries which are polluting the atmosphere in the vicinity of Taj Mahal/removal of the encroachment - Appropriate steps were not taken by the concerned authorities - Directions issued.
