Union of India and Another v T. Sundararaman and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 497; (1997) 4 SCC 664; (1997) SCC (L&S) 988; AIR 1997 SC 2418; JT 1997 (5) SC 48; 1997 LabIC 2342; 1997 (2) LLJ 368; 1997 (3) LLN 72; 1997(3) SCALE 679; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 792; 1997 (4) SLR 253; 1997 (4) Supreme 281

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Short-listing - Validity of - Posts of Asst. Professor, Medicine advertised - Advertisement specifically provided that short-listing would be done if there are more applicants - 37 applications received therefore short-listing done on basis of higher experience proper - Appeal dismissed.
