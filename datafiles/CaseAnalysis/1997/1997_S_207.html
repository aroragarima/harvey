Jaipur Development Authority v Smt Kailashwati Devi

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3094; (1997) 7 SCC 297; AIR 1997 SC 3243; 1997 (31) ALR 678; 1998 (1) AWC 739; 1997 (3) CLT 433; JT 1997 (7) SC 643; 1997 (2) KLT 902; 1997 (88) RD 554; 1997(5) SCALE 658; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 664; 1997 (8) Supreme 255

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Order XLI, Rule 27-Appeal-Additional evidence-Admissibility-Scope of clause (aa) of Rule 27 (1) of Order XLI-Not necessary that party seeking admission of additional evidence under clause (aa) must have adduced some evidence in trial court-Not permissible to restrict benefit of clause (aa) to only those who have adduced some evidence in trial court-Party having produced no evidence in trial court-Entitled to adduce additional evidence if conditions of clause (aa) satisfied.

Judgment of Rajasthan High Court set aside. AIR 1985 Gau 1071, overruled.
