Commissioner of Income Tax, Trivandrum v Relish Goods

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.S.M. Quadri, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 431; (1999) 3 SCC 167; AIR 1999 SC 1640; 1999 (152) CTR 500; [1999] 237 ITR 59; JT 1999 (2) SC 130; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 992; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 392; 1999 (2) TLR 479

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80 HH - Whether assessee's business involves 'production' thereby entitling him to deduction u/s. 80 HH? - Assessee business was not regarded as production on reference to Sterling Foods v State of Karnataka - After process of production raw product should be distinct from end product - Answered in favour of Revenue - Appeal allowed.
