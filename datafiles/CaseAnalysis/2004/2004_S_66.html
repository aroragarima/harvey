State Of Madhya Pradesh v Sanjay Rai

Supreme Court of India

25 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 221; (2004) 10 SCC 570; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1913; AIR 2004 SC 2174; 2004 CRLJ 2006; 2004 (2) JCC 743; JT 2004 (4) SC 73; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 596; 2004(4) SCALE 63; 2004 (2) SLT 971; 2004 (2) Supreme 744

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 201, 302, 304B, 306, 34 - Appeal against acquittal - No error can be said to have been committed by HC - Nor any other infirmity to undermine legality and propriety of findings of HC, warranting interference has been substantiated - Acquittal upheld, appeal dismissed.
