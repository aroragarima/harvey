Messrs Maruti Udyog Limited v Ram Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 46; (2005) SCC (L&S) 308; (2005) 2 SCC 638; AIR 2005 SC 851; 2005 (5) AWC 4185; 2005 (1) CLR 964; 2005 (104) FLR 820; JT 2005 (1) SC 449; 2005 (1) LLJ 853; 2005 (1) LLN 1033; 2005(1) SCALE 585; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 790; 2005 (3) SLR 403; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1470

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Maruti Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1980

Summary: [A.] Corporate - Service - Companies Act, 1956 - Maruti Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1980, s. 6 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10(1)(c), 25F, 25FF, 25FFF, 25H - Termination of respondents - Closure and transfer of an industrial undertaking - Whether respondents entitled to re-employment in view of transfer of undertakings? - Held, in case of closure of an industrial undertaking Act contemplates payment of compensation alone - Statutory scheme does not envisage that even in case of closure of an undertaking, a workman who although had not been retrenched would be re-employed in case of revival thereof by another company - Appeal allowed.
