Â Promod Chandra Deb and Others v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 209; AIR 1962 SC 1288; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 405; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 405

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Indian Independence Act, 1947, Government of India Act 1935, Foreign Jurisdiction Act, 1947, Extra Provincial Jurisdiction Act, 1947

Summary: Constitution - Art. 32 - Extra Provincial Jurisdiction Act, 1947, ss. 4 and 5 - Administration of Orissa States Order 1948 - Whether no khorposh allowance by Orissa Govt. Order u/Extra Provincial Jurisdiction Act, 1947 amount to infringement of fundamental rights under arts. 19(1)(f) and 31 and also discriminatory as violating art. 14 of Constitution of India, 1950? - Held, no infringment of fundamental right - Petition dismissed.
