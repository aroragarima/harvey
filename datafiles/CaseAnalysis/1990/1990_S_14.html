Union of India and Others v S. L. Dutta and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 572; (1991) SCC (L&S) 406; (1991) 1 SCC 505; AIR 1991 SC 363; 1991 (15) ATC 737; 1991 (62) FLR 90; JT 1990 (4) SC 741; 1991 LabIC 290; 1991 (1) LLN 326; 1990(2) SCALE 1051; 1991 (2) SLJ 246; 1991 (1) SLR 564; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 173

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 32, 162 and 311 - (A) Promotion change of policy - What is ? - Chances of Air Vice Marshals to get promoted to post of Air Marshal severely reduced by change - (B) Considerable material in fixing policy is of highly technical or scientific nature - Court should rarely interfere - Change in policy relating to promotional chances of officers in navigation stream in flying branch of Air force - Held, (A) It is change of policy and not change in conditions of service - (B) Court refused to interfere - Appeal allowed.
