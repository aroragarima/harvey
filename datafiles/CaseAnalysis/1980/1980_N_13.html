Vinod Kumar Chowdhry v Narain Devi Taneja

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 333; (1980) 2 SCC 120; AIR 1980 SC 2012; 1980 (17) DLT 313; 1980 (1) RCJ 524; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 746

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Refusing

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 14, 25 A(1), 25 B(10) and 25 B(8) - Interpretation of Statutes - Combined effect of ss. 25 A(1) and 25 B(10) - Order refusing recovery of possession - Specific direction barring execution of orders for prescribed period - Held, when s. 25B depart from procedure prescribed in other chapters of Act, provisions of chap III-A shall prevail otherwise, applications u/s. 25 B(1) shall be treated at par with all other applications - Revisable - Not necessary - Appeal dismissed.
