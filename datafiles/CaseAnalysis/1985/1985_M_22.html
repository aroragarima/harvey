Workmen of American Express International Banking Corporation v Management of American Express International Banking Corporation

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 113; (1985) 4 SCC 71; (1985) SCC (L&S) 940; AIR 1986 SC 458; 1986 (1) CCC 39; 1986 (29) DLT 34; 1985 (51) FLR 481; 1986 LabIC 98; 1985 (2) LLJ 539; 1985 (2) LLN 817; 1985(2) SCALE 1393; 1986 (1) UJ 228

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Service; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 25 F and 25 B(2) - Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946 - Workman in continuous service - Calculation of actual working days - Inclusion of sundays and other paid holidays - Held, can be taken into account - Appeal allowed.
