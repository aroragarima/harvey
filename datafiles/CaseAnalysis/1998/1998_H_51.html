Mr 'X' v Hospital 'Z'

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1218; (1998) 8 SCC 296; AIR 1999 SC 495; 1999 (1) AWC 274; 1999 (2) Bom.C.R. 474; 1999 (1) CompLJ 23; JT 1998 (7) SC 626; 1998(6) SCALE 230; 1998 (9) SLT 418; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 723; 1998 (9) Supreme 220

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service; Women & Children

Keywords: Woman, Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 1964, Parsi Marriage and Divorce Act, 1936

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Right to life - Lady patient found HIV +ve - Disclosure by doctor - To prospective husband - Right to privacy and right to marry invoked - Held, is not absolute - Clash between another person's right to be informed and patient's right of privacy as envisage under right to life - Public morality or public interest considerations.
