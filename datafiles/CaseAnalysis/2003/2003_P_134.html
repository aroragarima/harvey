Messers Gurbax Singh v Punjab Mandi Board

Supreme Court of India

18 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1186; (2005) 10 SCC 575; AIR 2004 SC 1269; 2004 (1) ALD(SC) 118; 2004 (56) ALR 148; 2004 (1) ARBLR 73; JT 2004 (5) SC 587; 2004 (1) RAJ 20; 2003(10) SCALE 1057; 2004 (1) SLT 332; 2003 (8) Supreme 808

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Limitation Act, 1993 - art. 119 - Award - Filing - Limitation - Held, if the appellant succeeds on the question of bar of limitation in filing the objections, in the eye of law there being no objection to the award other questions do not arise for consideration.
