Deputy Director, Social Forestry Division and another v Lakshmi Chandra

Supreme Court of India

02 February 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 105; (2016) 4 SCC 721; 2016 (148) FLR 973; JT 2016 (1) SC 633; 2016(2) SCALE 208

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Regularisation, Allowances, Entitlement of, U.P. Regularisation of Daily Wages (Appointment on Group D Posts) Rules, 2001, Minimum of the pay scale

Summary: Service - Minimum of the pay scale - Entitlement of - Appellants filed instant appeals aggrieved by the proceedings for contempt initiated against them, regarding payment of minimum of the pay-scale to the daily wagers working in the Forest Dept. in Group-D posts - Respondents (Applicants) filed contempt petition before HC, wherein HC directed to pay all the daily rated workers the minimum of the pay scale but without allowances and other benefits, the respondents (applicants) would be entitled to minimum of the pay scale so long they continue to work as daily rated workers in the Forest Dept. - Further directed the payment in terms of orders in State of U.P. & Ors. v. Putti Lal, 2002 Indlaw SC 2387.

Held, Principal Secretary to the Dept. of Forests and Principal Chief Conservator of Forests are directed to file separate affidavits before the HC on the implementation of the orders in contempt petition dt.1-7-2010 and SC order dt.21-2-2002. In case, the workmen have not been paid the amounts as per the orders, they shall see that wages are paid in terms of the orders within a period of 1 month and the affidavit in that regard shall be filed before the HC within 2 weeks thereafter. Appeals disposed of.
