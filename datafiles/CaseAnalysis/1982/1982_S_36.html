Merugu Satyanarayana and another v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Others

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 56; (1982) 3 SCC 301; (1983) SCC (Cr) 18; AIR 1982 SC 1543; 1983 (1) Crimes 267; 1982 CRLJ 2357; 1982 CrLR(SC) 488; 1982(2) SCALE 903; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 635

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: National Security Act, 1980, ss. 3(2) and 3(3) - Person sought to be detained is already confed in jail - Detention order with a view to preventing the from acting in the manner prejudicial to maintenance of public order - If can be passed - Held, can be passed if authority is of view the despite fact that the person is already detained a preventive detention is still necessary - Bu in the instant case not a word is said that the detaining authority was aware - Order clearly discloses a non-application of mind - Petition Allowed.
