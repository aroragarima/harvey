Reserve Bank of India and Another v S. Jayarajan

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 720; (1995) Supp4 SCC 584; 1996 (32) ATC 145; 1996 (2) LLJ 735

Case Digest

Subject: RBI; Service

Keywords: Reserve Bank of India Act

Summary: Service - Constitution - Reserve Bank of India Act (Staff) Regulations, 1948, regn. 25(2) - Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, ss. 7 and 58 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 (1) - Whether regn. 25(2) of Reserve Bank of India Act (Staff) Regulations, 1948 is subject to compliance with arts. 14 and 16(1) of Constitution? - Held, regn. 25(2) are in nature of administrative directions issued u/s. 7(2) and not u/s. 58 of Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 - Mandate of arts. 14 and 16 applies to statutory regulations as well as administrative instructions issued by State, as defined in art. 12 - It is not disputed that Reserve Bank of India is State u/art. 12 - High Court has found that regn. 25(2) is violative of provisions of arts. 14 and 16(1) of Constitution - Referred to Delhi Transport Corporation case - Appeal dismissed.
