Prasad and Others v V. Govindaswami Mudaliar and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 383; (1982) 1 SCC 185; AIR 1982 SC 84; 1983 (1) MLJ(SC) 4; 1981(3) SCALE 1867; [1982] 2 S.C.R. 109; 1982 UJ 326

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Constitution, art. 133 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 53 - (A) Findings of fact - High Court ignored material circumstances - Interference by SC,if - (B) Land and Property - Genuineness of Sale deed which is not supported by adequate consideration - Document executed nominally with a view to stave off creditors - (C) Hindu Law - Antecedent debts of father - Pious duty of sons - Held, SC will interfere - Transaction held vitiated - Pious duty of sons to discharge such debts - Appeals Allowed.
