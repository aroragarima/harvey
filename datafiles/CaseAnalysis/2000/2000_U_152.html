Rajasthan High Court Advocates' Association v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 December 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2707; (2001) 2 SCC 294; AIR 2001 SC 416; JT 2001 (1) SC 287; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 57; 2000(8) SCALE 455; 2001 (1) SCJ 363; 2001 (1) SLT 138; [2000] Supp5 S.C.R. 743; 2001 (3) Supreme 206; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 723

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Parliament & Legislature

Keywords: Rajasthan High Court Ordinance, 1949

Summary: Parliament & Legislature - Miscellaneous - States Reorganisation Act, 1956, ss. 49, 51, 54 and 57 - Rajasthan High Court Ordinance, 1949, s. 44 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Whether explanation inserted in the order of the Acting Chief Justice D/- 23-12-1976 by the subsequent order D/- 12-1-1977 was ultra vires the powers of the Chief Justice? - Held, yes, explanation appended to the order of Chief Justice D/- 23-12-1976 runs counter to the Presidential Order and in a sense it is an inroad into the jurisdiction of the Judges hearing a particular case or cases, preempting a decision to be given in the facts of individual case whether it can be said to have arisen in the territory of a particular district - Appeal dismissed.
