Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Rent control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 21(1)(a) - Whether subsequent developments can be considered into bonafide requirements of landlord? - Crucial date for deciding as to bona fides of requirement of landlord is date of his application for eviction - If every subsequent development during post-petition period is to be taken into account for judging bona fides of requirement pleaded by landlord there would perhaps be no end so long as unfortunate situation in our litigative slow process system subsists - Held, subsequent events pleaded and highlighted by appellant are too insufficient to overshadow bona fide need concurrently found by fact-finding courts - Appeal dismissed.
