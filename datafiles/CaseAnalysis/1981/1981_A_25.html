Aitha Chander Rao v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

08 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 597; (1981) Supp SCC 17; (1981) SCC (Cr) 637; 1982 CRLR 7

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Probation of Offenders Act, ss. 4 and 12 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 304-A - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 361 - Whether appellant may be released on probation? - Held, contributory negligence on part of appellant - Fit case in which appellant may be released on probation of good conduct u/s. 4 and u/s. 361, CrPC, 1973 - May not affect his service and career - Appeal disposed of.
