State Bank of Travancore v Mohammed Mohammed Khan

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 256; (1981) 4 SCC 82; AIR 1981 SC 1744; [1982] 52 Comp Cas 490; 1981(3) SCALE 1253; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 338; 1981 UJ 896

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution

Keywords: Kerala Agricultural Debt Relief Act 1970

Summary: Agriculture & Agri. Products - Kerala Agriculturists Debt Relief Act, 1970, ss. 2(4)(a)(ii) proviso, cl. (b) and 2(4)(1) - State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Act, 1959, s. 2 (k) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Debt releif under agriculture releif Act - Debt owed to subsidiary bank of State Bank of India - Amalgamation of banks - Expression 'debt due before commencement' in s. 2(4)(1) of 1970, Act - Right to recover arising because of take over of assets after specific date - Whether debt under 1970 Act is excluded in light of art. 14 of Constitution and whether provisions of s. 2(4) exhaustive and clauses mutually exclusive? - Held, debt not excluded - Provision is not exhaustive - Cls. of s. 2 (4) are not mutually exclusive - Appeal dismissed.
