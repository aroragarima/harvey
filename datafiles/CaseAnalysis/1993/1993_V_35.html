Mrs. Chandrakala Menon and Another v Vipin Menon (Capt.) and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 847; (1993) 2 SCC 6; (1993) SCC (Cr) 485; 1993 (3) AWC 1746; 1993 (1) BLJR 536; 1993 (1) CCR 53; 1993 (1) Crimes 556; 1993 CRLR 182; JT 1993 (1) SC 229; 1993(1) SCALE 119

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Family & Personal - Women & Children - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 - Custody of minor child of parties in question - Appellant and respondent entered into settlement to get divorce by mutual consent - Alleged by appellant that as per settlement deed custody of child given to grandparents - Respondent filed application for custody of child - Respondent was charged with an allegation that he kidnapped child - Magistrate took cognizance of matter and when child was not produced before court he ordered that custody of child to be given to maternal grandfather - Whether the respondent being the natural guardian cannot be charged with offence of kidnapping? - Held, High Court justified in quashing criminal proceedings against respondent - Order of HC upheld - Marriage between parties has irretrievably broken down and there is no chance of their coming together - Decree of divorce granted - Custody of the child has to be decided on sole and predominant criterion of what would best serve interest and welfare of minor - Custody of minor given to appellant - Appeal disposed of.
