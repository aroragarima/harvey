Ram Chandra Tripathi v U.P. Public Services Tribunal Iv And Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1380; (1994) 5 SCC 180; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1044; 1994 (27) ATC 819; 1994 (68) FLR 931; JT 1994 (2) SC 84; 1994(1) SCALE 738; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 137; 1994 (2) SLJ 137; 1994 (2) SLR 27

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether order terminating services of appellant to comply with injunction of High Court was valid or not? - Since order of confirmation of appellant was made in violation of injunction order, mistake committed in passing order of confirmation was corrected - Held, appellant was not required to be given any opportunity of being heard - It appears that although appellant had remained in service for 16 years but service record of appellant was not at all convincing - Appeal dismissed.
