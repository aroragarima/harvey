Des Raj & Ors v Bhagat Ram (Dead) By Lrs. & Ors

Supreme Court of India

20 February 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 175; (2007) 9 SCC 641; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 512; 2007 (4) ALD(SC) 6; 2007 (2) AWC 1483; JT 2007 (5) SC 299; 2007 (3) MLJ(SC) 803; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 581; 2007(3) SCALE 371; 2007 (8) SCJ 187; [2007] 2 S.C.R. 892

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Copy Of Plaint, Denial Of Title

Summary: Property dispute - Trial Judge opined that the plaintiff had been in exclusive continuous peaceful possession of the suit land to the exclusion of the other co-owners prior to settlement which took place in the year 1953 - Appeal against - Held, plaintiff- respondent had established that he acquired title by ousting the defendants - appellants by declaring hostile title in himself which was to the knowledge of his co-sharers - Appeal disposed of.
