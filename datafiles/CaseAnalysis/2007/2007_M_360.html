Yadvendra Arya & Anr v Mukesh Kumar Gupta

Supreme Court of India

28 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1189; (2008) 2 SCC 144; AIR 2008 SC 773; 2008 (70) ALR 301; 2007 (7) AWC 6981; JT 2007 (13) SC 287; 2007 (2) RCR(Rent) 675; 2007(13) SCALE 502; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 615

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Residential Purpose, Availability Of Alternative Accommodation, Regulation Of Letting, U.P. (Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: U.P. (Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972 - Eviction application allowed - Ground of bonafide needs - Appeal against - Held, considering the factual background inevitable conclusion is that this appeal is without merit - Time is granted to them to vacate the premises - Appeal dismissed.
