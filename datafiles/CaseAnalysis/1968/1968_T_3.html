Nath Singh and Others v Board of Revenue and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 382; AIR 1968 SC 1351; [1968] 3 S.C.R. 498

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Land Revenue Act, 1901 - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1952,ss. 20(b)(i)and 232 - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Rules, r. 183 - Uttar Pradesh Tenancy Act, s. 171 - Whether an entry recording a person as a sub-tenant was equivalent to an entry recording him as occupant for purpose of Zamindari Abolition Act ? - Held, record of rights for year 1356 F had not been corrected afterwards and going by entry in record of rights, no enquiry need be made as to when respondents became sub-tenants after decision in favour of landlord, as between tenant and sub-tenant, entry in record of rights in favour of sub-tenant makes him occupant entitled to adhivasi rights u/s. 20 of Act - Appeals dismissed.
