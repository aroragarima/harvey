Cooperative Sugars (Chittur) Limited v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1072; (1993) Supp4 SCC 42; AIR 1994 SC 1456; 1993 (90) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 3(a) - Sale of Goods Act, 1930 - Constitution of India 1950, sch. 7, list 2, entry 54 - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 3 - (A) Determination of inter State sale for taxation when sugar factory in Kerala purchasing sugarcane from Tamil Nadu and movement of goods occasioned by sale - (B) Legality of tax levied under Tamil Nadu Sales Tax Act treating such sale as inter State sale in view of offices opened by Sugar factory in Tamil Nadu for purchasing - Held, inter-State sale and taxable under Central Sales Tax Act - Further held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
