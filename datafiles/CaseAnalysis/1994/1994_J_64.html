Sub-Committee On Judicial Accountability v Justice V. Ramaswami

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.M. Ahmadi, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 514; (1995) 1 SCC 5; 1994(4) SCALE 634; 1995 (1) SCJ 227; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 639

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Practice & Procedure - Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968 - Criminal contempt - Whether letter to the Enquiry Committee contemptuous? - Subsequent letter - Context in which it was written and the apprehensions about the generality of its sweep stand mitigated - Court declined in the larger interest to suo motu institute any proceedings for contempt against the respondent - Petition dismissed.
