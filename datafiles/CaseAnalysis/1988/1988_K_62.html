Kusheshwar Dubey v Bharat Coking Coal Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 496; (1988) 4 SCC 319; (1988) SCC (L&S) 950; AIR 1988 SC 2118; 1988 (57) FLR 562; JT 1988 (3) SC 576; 1989 LabIC 38; 1988 (2) LLJ 470; 1988 (2) LLN 912; 1988(2) SCALE 641; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 821; 1989 (1) UJ 17

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Trade Union Act, 1926, ss. 3 and 6 - Whether the direction made by the High Court designating the General Manager, North Eastern Railway, as the authority to hold elections of the North Eastern Railway Employees Union, a trade union registered under the Trade Unions Act, 1926, should be substituted by the Registrar of Trade Unions, Uttar Pradesh? SC direct that the election will be held under the supervision of the Registrar of Trade Unions or by an officer designated by him for the purpose - Appeal disposed of.
