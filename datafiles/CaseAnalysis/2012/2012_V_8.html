National Council For Technical Education and others v Vaishnav Institute of Technology and Management

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, H.L. Gokhale

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 164; (2012) 5 SCC 139; AIR 2012 SC 2232; 2012 (93) ALR 435; 2012 (4) AWC 3472; 2012 (2) JabLJ 241; JT 2012 (5) SC 249; 2012 (4) MLJ(SC) 415; 2012(4) SCALE 515; [2012] 2 S.C.R. 856

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Education

Keywords: National Council For Teacher Education Rule, 1997

Summary: Education - Administrative - National Council for Teacher Education Act, 1993, ss. 13 and 17(1) - National Council for Teacher Education Rules, 1997, r. 8 - Where an action was contemplated against recognized institution u/s. 17(1) of the Act by the Regional Committee and inspection of such recognized institution was found necessary, whether such inspection must proceed u/s. 13 of the Act or independent of s. 13, the Regional Committee was empowered to carry out inspection - Held, ss. 17 and 13 of Act were harmoniously construed - In exercise of its powers u/s. 17 of Act, the Regional Committee may feel that inspection of a recognized institution was necessary before it can arrive at the satisfaction as to whether such recognized institution has contravened any of the provisions of the 1993 Act or the rules or the regulations or the orders made thereunder or breached the terms of the recognition - In that event, the route of inspection as provided u/s. 13 of Act has to be followed - If the Regional Committee has been authorized by the Council to perform its function of inspection, the Regional Committee may cause the inspection of recognition institution to be made as provided in s. 13 of Act and prescribed in r.8 of Rules - Where, however, the Regional Committee feels that the inspection of a recognized institution was not necessary for the proposed action u/s. 17of Act, obviously it can proceed in accordance with the law without following the route of inspection as provided u/s. 13 of Act - Appeals disposed of.
