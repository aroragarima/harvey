M/S. Modi Cements Ltd v Shri Kuchil Kumar Nandi

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, M.K. Mukherjee, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 467; (1998) 3 SCC 249; (1999) SCC (Cr) 252; AIR 1998 SC 1057; 1998 (4) AWC 739; 1998 (2) CalLT(SC) 41; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 88; 1998 Crimes 268; 1998 CRLJ 1397; 1998 (146) CTR 36; JT 1998 (2) SC 198; 1998 (1) KLT 582; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1192

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Dishonour of cheques, Legal Notice

Summary: Criminal - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, ss.138,139 - Dishonour of cheque - Presumption u/s. 139 in favour of holder is followed once - Drawer issued cheque - Notice by drawer to drawee or to bank for stoppage of payment - Whether precludes action u/s. 138 by drawee - Held, notice by drawer to drawee or to bank for stoppage of payment - Would not preclude action u/s. 138 by drawee - Appeal allowed.
