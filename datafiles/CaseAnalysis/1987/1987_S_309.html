Mahesh S/O Ram Narain and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28203; (1987) 3 SCC 80; (1987) SCC (Cr) 379; AIR 1987 SC 1346; 1987 CRLJ 1073; 1987 CRLR 329; 1987 CrLR(SC) 329; JT 1987 (1) SC 793; 1987(1) SCALE 594; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 710; 1987 (2) UJ 150

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Appeal for reduction of death penalty - Appellants were accused of five murders - Murder caused merely because daughter of deceased person married a Harijan - Held, death sentence proper - Appeal dismissed.
