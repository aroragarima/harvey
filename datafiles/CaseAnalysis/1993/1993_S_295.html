State of Bihar and Another v Madan Mohan Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 710; (1994) Supp3 SCC 308; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1438; AIR 1994 SC 765; 1994 (28) ATC 392; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 198; 1994 LabIC 469; 1993(4) SCALE 194; 1994 (1) SLJ 153; 1993 (5) SLR 601; 1994 (1) UJ 321

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 233 - Appointment of Addl. District and Sessions Judge - Combined selected list of candidates prepared on basis or ratio of 1:4 i.e., four candidates against each vacancy - Candidates selected and recommended on merit - Whether select list can be kept subsisting? - Select list gets exhausted thereafter and cannot be kept subsisting for filling up other vacancies also - Application disposed of.
