Santosh Gupta v State Bank of Patiala

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 334; (1980) 3 SCC 340; (1980) SCC (L&S) 409; AIR 1980 SC 1219; 1980 (40) FLR 373; 1980 LabIC 687; 1980 (2) LLJ 72; 1980 (2) LLN 170; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 884; 1980 SLJ 661

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Age Of Superannuation, Closure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo) and 25F - Interpretation of Statutes - Scope of word 'Retrenchment' - Held, it includes every kind of termination - And discharge of the workman on the ground-she did not pass the test which would have enabled her to be confirmed was 'retrenchment' within the meaning of s. 2(OO) and, therefore, the requirements of s. 25F had to be complied with - Appeal allowed.
