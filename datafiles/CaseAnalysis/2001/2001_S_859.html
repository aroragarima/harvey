Sajan Abraham v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

07 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20048; (2001) 6 SCC 692; (2001) SCC (Cr) 1217; AIR 2001 SC 3190; 2001 (3) Crimes 377; 2002 (1) CRJ 242; 2001 CRLJ 4002; 2002 (139) E.L.T. 241; 2001 (2) JCC 104; JT 2001 (6) SC 178; 2001 (3) KLT 146; 2001 (3) RCR(Criminal) 808; 2001 (3) RCR(Criminal) 808B; 2001(5) SCALE 91; 2001 (3) SCJ 96; 2001 (5) SLT 641; [2001] Supp1 S.C.R. 335; 2001 (5) Supreme 789B; 2001 (2) UC 445

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Health & Drug; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Mahazar

Summary: Criminal - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 21 and 27 - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Rules, 1985, r. 66 - Possession of psychotropic substance - Quantity thereof - Personal consumption - Whether such possession punishable if evidence is adduced to show that psychotropic substance was for personal consumption? - Held, possession of appellant not liable for punishment u/s. 21 as same was not in breach of r. 66 - Personal consumption substantiated by recovery of syringes which was for his own consumption - Appeal allowed.
