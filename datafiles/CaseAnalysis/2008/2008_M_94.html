M/S Goodearth Steels Pvt. Ltd v Commnr. Of Central Excise, Kanpur

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 187; (2008) 3 SCC 77; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1325; JT 2008 (2) SC 463; 2008(2) SCALE 473

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Appeals Dismissed

Summary: Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35-H - Whether there was power for condonation of delay in seeking a reference? - Held, by a judgment delivered separately in Civil Appeal No. 4647 of 2007, Commissioner of Customs, Central Excise, Noida v Messrs Punjab Fibres Limited, Noida this court held that this is not permissible - Appeals dismissed.
