Supreme Court of India

05 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1676; (1998) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1998 SC 1240; JT 1998 (1) SC 753; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 118; 1998 (1) RCR(Rent) 272; 1998(1) SCALE 613; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 711; 1998 (3) SLT 19; 1998 (2) Supreme 91

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(b) - Eviction sub-letting - Proof of - As to payment of monetary consideration by tenant to tenant - Held, such affirmative evidence not necessary - Letting could be inferred from proof of delivery of exclusive possession.
