Central Bureau Of Investigation v State Of Rajastan

Supreme Court of India

08 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2955; (1996) 9 SCC 735; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1090; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 471; AIR 1996 SC 2402; AIR 1996 SCW 2934; [1997] 88 Comp Cas 326; 1996 CRLJ 3480; 1996 (67) ECR 263; JT 1996 (6) SC 313; 1996(5) SCALE 203; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 127; 1996 (5) Supreme 548

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Fema

Summary: Exchange Control and FDI - FERA , 1947, ss. 56 and 62 - Delhi Special Police Establishment Act, 1946, ss. 3 and 5 - Determinationof jurisdiction to investigate into offence u/s. 56 committed outside India - When notification u/ss. 3 and 5 of 1946 Act authorising members of DSPE to investigate various offences including offences under FERA - But no such notification issued by Central Govt. under FERA - Held, members of Delhi Police Force cannot investigate into offence in question - Appeal dismissed.
