Pooran Singh and Another v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 111; AIR 1965 SC 1583; 1965 CRLJ 1547; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 853

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 112, 124 and 130(1)(b) - (A) Whether magistrate is obliged to make endorsements in complaint u/ss. 124, 112 of Motor Vehicles Act i e offences not specified in Part A of Sch.5 in terms of s. 130(1)(b)? - Held, magistrate is not obliged to make endorsements in complaint u/ss. 124 and 112 of Motor Vehicles Act, 1939 i.e., offences not specified in Part A of Sch.5 in terms of s. 130 (1)(b) - Appeal dismissed.
