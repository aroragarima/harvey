Netai Dutta v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

28 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 138; (2005) SCC (Cr) 543; (2005) 2 SCC 659; AIR 2005 SC 1775; 2005 CRLJ 1737; JT 2005 (3) SC 46; 2005(2) SCALE 452

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, s 482 - Petition to quash criminal proceedings initiated - Offence under, s 306 IPC - Held, case registered against appellant is without any factual foundation - Prosecution initiated against appellant would only result in sheer harassment to appellant without any fruitful result - Single Judge seriously erred in holding that First Information Report against appellant disclosed elements of a cognizable offence - Criminal proceedings initiated against appellant quashed - Appeal allowed.
