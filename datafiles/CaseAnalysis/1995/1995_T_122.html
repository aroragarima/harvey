Karnel Singh v The State Of M.P

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1353; (1995) 5 SCC 518; (1995) SCC (Cr) 977; AIR 1995 SC 2472; AIR 1995 SCW 3644; 1996 ALJ 433; 1995 (3) AWC 1892; 1995 (4) CCR 10; 1995 (3) Crimes 527; 1995 CRLJ 4173; JT 1995 (6) SC 437; 1995(4) SCALE 752; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 629; 1995 (2) UJ 646

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code,1860, s. 375 - Rape - Appreciation of evidence - Prosecutrix, poor labourer - Semen on petticoat and in vagina - Held, in India delay in lodging such complaints is not inordinate - Evidence of prosecutrix need not be tested with same amount of suspicion as that of accomplice.
