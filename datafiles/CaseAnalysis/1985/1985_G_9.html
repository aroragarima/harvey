Shree Gollaleshwar Dev and Others v Gangawwa Kom Shantayya Math and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 376; (1985) 4 SCC 393; AIR 1986 SC 231; 1985(2) SCALE 811; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 646; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 646; 1986 (1) UJ 659

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950- Societies Registration Act, 1860 - Public Trust - Maintaianability of suit by two of trustees with permission of Charity Commissioner - Suit for declaration that certain property belongs to trust and for possession of same from person holding adversely to trust - In light of words 'persons having interest in trust' u/ss 2(10) and 50 - Held, suit maintainable - Further held, words include trustees - Appeal allowed.
