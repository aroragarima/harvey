Mst. Bibi Sayeeda and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.S. Paripoornan, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2856; (1996) 9 SCC 516; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 560; AIR 1996 SC 1936; JT 1996 (4) SC 637; 1996(4) SCALE 232; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 799; 1996 (5) Supreme 81

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Fishing rights

Summary: Bihar Land Reforms Act, 1950, s. 4(a) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 3 - 'Bazar' - Immovable property - Right to hold Bazar - Held, is integral to immovable property - Synonymous to 'market' - Act has no distinction between urban area or rural area - Bazars held by intermediary though in urban area stands vested in State.
