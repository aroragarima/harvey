Zodiac Electricals Private Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 363; (1986) 3 SCC 522; AIR 1986 SC 1918; 1986(1) SCALE 1360; 1986 (2) UJ 576

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Petition u/s 33 - Construction of correspondence among parties disclosing, no concluded contract - Arbitration clause alleged to be in contract - Whether as a result of correspondence a concluded contract came into being between appellants and GOI acting through DGS and D? - Held clause could have no existence - Appeal allowed.
