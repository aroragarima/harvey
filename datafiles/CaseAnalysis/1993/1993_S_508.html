Gandhi Grah Nirman Sahkari Samiti Limited Etc v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 424; (1993) 2 SCC 662; AIR 1994 SC 2329; 1993 (66) E.L.T. 47; JT 1993 (3) SC 194; 1993 (2) RentLR 212; 1993(2) SCALE 342; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 788; 1993 (2) UJ 172

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Rajasthan Urban Improvement (Amendment) Act, 1987

Summary: Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act; 1959, ss.52 and 72; Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.4 - Acquisition of land - Power of State Government - Scope - Held, detailed development plan - And precise specification for use of land not necessary - Court cannot go into comparative utility of public purposes and set aside acquisition.
