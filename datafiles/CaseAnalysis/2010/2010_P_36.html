Goan Real Estate And Construction Ltd. And Anr. v People’S Movement For Civic Action And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Panchal

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 240; (2010) 5 SCC 441; 2010 (4) Bom.C.R. 827; 2010(3) SCALE 508

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Status Quo

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Clarification of order - Instant application filed, seeking clarification that the order dt. 28-8-2008 passed in Civil Appeal No.5282 of 2008 which arose out of SLP (C) No.19767 of 2008 was directed against the order of status quo granted by the HC of Bombay at Goa on 10-7-2008 and was not directed against the order dt. 5-8-2008 by which petitioners were permitted to amend Writ Petition (C) No.403 of 2007 to enable them to challenge the order dt. 30-10-2007 passed by National Coastal Zone Management Authority - It was further prayed that in view of the order dt. 28-8-2008, it be declared that the Transfer Petition No.758 of 2008 and the Writ Petition (C) No.329 of 2008 which, according to the respondents were on the same subject matter, became infructuous and would not come in the way of the HC in deciding the pending writ petition at the earliest - Whether respondents entitled to relief claimed - Held, Writ Petition Civil No.329 of 2008 was instituted seeking a declaration that the building plans sanctioned and constructions made as well as on-going constructions, pursuant to the Coastal Regulation Zone Notification dt. 19-2-1991 as amended by the Notification dt. 16-8-1994 issued by the Central Govt. were valid; by filing Transfer Petition (C) No. 758 of 2008, it was prayed to transfer the proceedings of PIL Writ Petition No.403 of 2007 pending before the HC of Judicature at Bombay, Bench at Goa, Panaji to SC and to hear the said Writ Petition along with Special Leave Petition (Civil) No.16728 of 2008 which was pending before SC - However, subject matters of all the proceedings were different - Hence, having regard to the facts of the case, it could not be said that the Transfer Petition No.758 of 2008 and Writ Petition (C) No.329 of 2008 became infructuous in view of the order dt. 28-8-2008 passed by SC in Civil Appeal No.5281 of 2008 - Further, order dt. 28-8-2008, passed by SC in Civil Appeal No. 5282 of 2008, was recalled and it was clarified that Civil Appeal No. 5281 of 2008 dealt with order dt. 10-7-2008 passed by the HC of Bombay at Goa in Miscellaneous Civil Application No. 866 of 2007 which was filed in writ petition No. 403 of 2007 requiring the parties to maintain status quo whereas Civil Appeal No. 5282 of 2008 dealt with order dt. 5-8-2008 rendered by the HC of Bombay at Goa in Civil Miscellaneous Application No. 595 of 2008, which was filed in writ petition No. 403 of 2007 permitting the original petitioners to amend the Writ Petition No.403 of 2007 so as to enable them to challenge order dt. October 30, 2007 passed by National Coastal Zone Management Authority - Order dt. 28-8-2008 passed in Civil Appeal No.5282 of 2008 was recalled - Application partly allowed.
