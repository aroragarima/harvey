R. B. Desai and Another v S. K. Khanolker and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, A.S. Anand, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 650; (1999) 7 SCC 54; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1216; AIR 1999 SC 3306; 1999 (4) AWC 3375; 1999 (2) CLR 828; 1999 (3) ESC 1744; JT 1999 (6) SC 141; 2000 (3) SLJ 26; 1999 (4) SLR 762; 2000 (1) UPLBEC 425

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Post of Asst. Conservator of Forest - Exclusion of seniority for promotion in Service Rules, rule has to be specific - Rule fixing certain years of qualifying service in feeder post as eligibility criteria - Note appended to rule also requiring eligibility list - Held, rule does not give to ranking in eligibility list any preference over seniority - Appeal allowed.
