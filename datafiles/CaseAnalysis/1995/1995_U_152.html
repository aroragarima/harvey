P. M. Hari Kumar v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1003; (1995) 5 SCC 691; (1995) SCC (Cr) 1013; AIR 1996 SC 70; AIR 1995 SCW 3726; 1995 (3) AWC 1723; 1995 (4) CCR 142; 1995 (3) Crimes 826; 1995 CRLJ 4188; JT 1995 (6) SC 331; 1995(5) SCALE 132; 1995 (3) SCJ 685; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 301

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal -Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Delay in execution of detention order - Plea taken detenu was staying abroad and evading arrest but no passport or an affidavit produced - Effect of unusual delay in serving detention order without satisfactory explaination - Detention vitiated - Petition allowed.
