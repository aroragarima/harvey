Secretary To The Govt. Of Orissa And Anr. v Sarbeswar Rout

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, Kuldip Singh, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 145; (1989) 4 SCC 578; AIR 1989 SC 2259; 1989 (15) ALR 758; 1989 (2) ARBLR 444; 1989 (3) CCC 370; JT 1989 (4) SC 86; 1989(2) SCALE 756; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 366; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 366

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Interest Act, 1978, s. 3 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 29 - Past interest - Arbitrator directed parties to file statements of claim - Arbitration proceedings, commenced therefrom - Grant of past interest for period beyond such date - Validity - Held, improper - Appeal partly allowed.
