Nand Kishore Marwah and Others v Samundri Devi

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28848; (1987) 4 SCC 382; AIR 1987 SC 2284; JT 1987 (3) SC 590; 1987 (2) RCJ 495; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 412; 1987 (2) RentLR 685; 1987(2) SCALE 555; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 158; 1988 (1) UJ 245

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 2(2), 20, 39 and 40 - Tenancy - Eviction suit instituted in period of exemption of building from operation of 1972 Act - Applicability of s. 20 of Act, pendent lite - Held, during pendency of litigation even if period of exemption expired restriction on institution of suit for eviction as provided in s. 20 would not be attracted - Appeal dismissed.
