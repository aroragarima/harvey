Anandilal Bhanwarlal and Another v Smt. Kasturi Devi Ganeriwala and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 356; (1985) 1 SCC 442; AIR 1985 SC 376; 1984(2) SCALE 986; 1985 UJ 532d

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - West Bengal Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, ss. 13(3 A), 13(i)(f), 13(i)(a)and 16 - Suit for eviction - Filed within three years of date of purchase of disputed premises - On grounds of personal needs and subletting by tenant - Maintainability - Held, suit for eviction is not maintainable as it was instituted within three years - Appeal allowed.
