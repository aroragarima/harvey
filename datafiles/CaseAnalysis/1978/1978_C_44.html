Sentinel Rolling Shutters and Engineering Company v Commissioner of Sales Tax

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 55; (1978) SCC (Tax) 214; (1978) 4 SCC 260; AIR 1978 SC 1747; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 644; 1978 (42) STC 409; 1978 UJ 766

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(28) - Sales Tax - Test to determine distinction between sale or contract of work and labour - Special terms showing contract for fabrication, supply and erection of rolling shutters as single and indivisible contract - Held contract was one for work and labour and not a contract for sale - Appeal allowed.
