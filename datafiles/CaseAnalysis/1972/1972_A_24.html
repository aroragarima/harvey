Amar Chand Agarwalla v Shanti Bose and Another

Supreme Court of India

22 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, A. Alagiriswami, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 308; (1973) 4 SCC 10; (1973) SCC (Cr) 651; AIR 1973 SC 799; 1973 CRLJ 577; 1973 CrLR(SC) 159; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 179

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Trade

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 561 A - (A) Exercise of inherent jurisdiction u/s 561 A of Code - (B) Exercise of jurisdiction u/s 439 - Held, High Court was not justified in exercising jurisdiction - Further held, jurisdiction u/s 439, is normally to be exercised only in exceptional cases, when there is a glaring defect in procedure or there is a manifest error of point of law and consequently, there has been a flagrant miscarriage of justice - Appeal allowed.
