Haji Lal Mohd. Biri Works, Allahabad Through Abdul Hamid v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 524; (1974) 3 SCC 137; AIR 1973 SC 2226; 1973 (2) CTR 313; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 25; 1973 (32) STC 496; 1973 TAXLR 2417

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 8(1A) - Uttar Pradesh Bikri-Kar (Dwitiya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1964, Pre - Uttar Pradesh General clauses Act, 1904, s. 5(1)(b) - Local Sales Tax - Provisions of 1948 Act - (A) Need of making an assessment order and issuing a notice of demand for recovery of interest u/s. 8(1A) - (B) Whether stay of order for recovery of sales tax prevent recovery of interest on arrears of sales tax - Held, not necessary - Stay of, does not prevent running of interest - Appeal dismissed.
