Bhudeo Mandal and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Baharul Islam, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 611; (1981) SCC (Cr) 595; (1981) 2 SCC 755; AIR 1981 SC 1219; 1981 BBCJ 104; 1981 BLJR 411; 1981 CRLJ 725; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 291

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 34, 141, 149, 304 Part I, 326, 323 and 325 - Appellant convicted by High Court - Held, no finding given by HC about common object of unlawful assembly - Before recording conviction u/s. 149 of IPC, ingredient of s. 141 of IPC must be established - Further held, there is neither any evidence nor any finding that any of the ingredients of s. 149 have been established by prosecution - Conviction set aside - Appeal allowed.
