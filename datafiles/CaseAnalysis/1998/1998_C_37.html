Ghasi Ram And Others v Chait Ram Saini and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1054; (1998) 6 SCC 200; AIR 1998 SC 2476; 1998 (34) ALR 122; 1998 (3) CLT 88; JT 1998 (5) SC 165; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 311; 1998(4) SCALE 232; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 863; 1998 (6) SLT 293; 1998 (Supp) RD 558; 1998 (6) Supreme 92

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Decree-Holder, Execution Proceeding, Sale Of Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, rr. 98 and 103 - Limitation Act, 1908, s. 14 - Limitation - Exclusion of time spent in revision, while computing limitation for suit subsequently filed u/r. 103 of O. 21 of Code - If allowed, when proceedings in Court without jurisdiction - And order rejecting objection filed by stranger to execution of possessory decree and revision hold to be not maintainable - Held, excludible - Appeal allowed.
