Urban Improvement Trust, Jodhpur v Gokul Narain & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 820; (1996) 4 SCC 178; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 163; AIR 1996 SC 1819; 1996 (2) CCC 356; JT 1996 (4) SC 446; 1996 (1) RentLR 473; 1996(3) SCALE 721; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 148; 1996 (4) Supreme 509

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 47, O. 22, r. 4 - (A) Abatement of appeal in SC - Application in SC for bringing LRs of respondent-claimant in acquisition proceedings within one month of knowledge of death on notice being taken on dasti service - Whether appeal abates - LRs brought on record much earlier in executing Court - Whether material - (B) Execution proceedings - Powers of Court - Decree granted for certain benefits - Court granting benefits not having jurisdiction to entertain claims - Whether decree is valid - Whether question of validity can be set up - Held, (A) Appeal does not abate -Immaterial in view of application being made within one month of knowledge - (B) Decree is nullity - Can be set up at any stage including execution - Decree set aside - Appeal allowed.
