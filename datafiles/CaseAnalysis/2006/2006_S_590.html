K. Madalaimuthu and Anr v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 285; (2006) 6 SCC 558; AIR 2006 SC 2662; JT 2006 (6) SC 190; 2006 (3) MLJ(SC) 161; 2006(6) SCALE 433; 2006 (6) SCJ 87; [2006] Supp3 S.C.R. 176

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: General Rules, Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Services Rules, 1955, Tamil Nadu Registration Services Rules, 1969

Summary: [A.] Service - Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Services Rules, 1955, rr. 4,10(a)(i)(1), 23-a, 39(c) - Tamil Nadu Registration Services Rules, 1969 - Determination of inter se seniority - Temporary appointees vis-a-vis direct recruits - Person temporarily appointed/promoted without recourse to rules of recruitment - Temporary services rendered cannot be counted for reckoning seniority - Reason being, person appointed temporarily dehors rules cannot be said to be in service till such time his appointment is regularized - It is only from date on which his services are regularized that such appointee can count his seniority in the cadre - Appeals allowed.
