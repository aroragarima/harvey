M/S. Oblum Electrical Industries Pvt. Ltd., Hyderabad v Collector Of Customs Bombay

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2746; (1997) 7 SCC 581; AIR 1997 SC 3467; 1997 (72) ECR 535; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 449; JT 1997 (7) SC 688; 1997 (22) RLT 233; 1997(5) SCALE 692; 1997 (3) SCJ 150; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 681; 1997 (8) Supreme 161

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Trade

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s.25 - Notification dated 30-03-1988 - Exemption for duty - Entitlement of - Appellant importing Crystar Beams required for manufacture of Lightening Arrestors - 'Materials required to be imported for the purpose of manufacture of products' as in notification - Interpretation of - Held, not only refers materials are used in manufacture of products - Includes 'materials that are required in order to manufacture the resultant products' - Entitled to exemption for paying customs and additional duty.
