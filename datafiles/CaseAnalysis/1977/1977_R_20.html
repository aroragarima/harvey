State Of Rajasthan v Ramdeen & Others

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 189; (1977) SCC (Cr) 393; (1977) 2 SCC 630; AIR 1977 SC 1328; 1977 CRLJ 997; 1977 CrLR(SC) 282; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 139

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CrPC, 1973, s. 378(1)(3) - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 114 - LImitation period for an appeal by State against order of acquittal with leave of High Court - Determination - Held, appeal can be filed within 90 days from the date of order of acquittal and prayer for leave may be included in that appeal - Appeal Allowed.
