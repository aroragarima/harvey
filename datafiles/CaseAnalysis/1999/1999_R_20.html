Executive Officer, Arthanareswarar Temple v R. Sathyamoorthy and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 388; (1999) 3 SCC 115; AIR 1999 SC 958; 1999 (35) ALR 477; 1999 (2) CLT 38; JT 1999 (1) SC 343; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 39; 1999(1) SCALE 358; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 485; 1999 (2) SLT 203; 2000 (Supp) RD 590; 1999 (1) Supreme 406

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 23, r. 1 - Withdrawal of suit - Grant of permission - Considerations - Application for permission to sell trust property - Withdrawal sought in revisional proceedings on remand - Advantage obtained by applicant from order of remand - Held, withdrawal not permitted.
