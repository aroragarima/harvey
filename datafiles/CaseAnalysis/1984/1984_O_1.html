Sudarsan Chits (I) Limited v O. Sukumaran Pillai and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 317; (1984) 4 SCC 657; AIR 1984 SC 1579; [1985] 58 Comp Cas 633; 1984 (3) CompLJ 40; 1984(2) SCALE 289; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 511; 1984 TAXLR 2181; 1985 UJ 63

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 446(2) - Jurisdiction to entertain claim petition - Order of winding up of company kept in abeyance by appellate Court - Order inoperative but does not cease to exist - Court exercising jurisdiction in appointing official liquidator as provisional liquidator - Whether the court can exercise jurisdiction u/s. 446(2) of the 1956 Act - Court held, will have jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
