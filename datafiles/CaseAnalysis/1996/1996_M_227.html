State Of Bihar And Ors. v Md. Kalimuddin And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1177; (1996) 2 SCC 7; (1996) SCC (L&S) 389; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 568; AIR 1996 SC 1145; 1996 (32) ATC 821; 1996 (72) FLR 529; JT 1996 (1) SC 271; 1996 LabIC 957; 1996 (1) LLN 482; 1996(1) SCALE 281; 1996 (1) SCJ 560; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 314; 1996 (1) SLR 676; 1996 (1) Supreme 371; 1996 (1) UJ 778; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 1474

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art.309 - Assistant Teachers Service Encadrement, Appointment and Transfer Rules, 1975, Assistant Teachers Service Rules, r.1 - Direct recruitment - Life of the select list -- Rule providing that select list shall be valid for one year from date of approval of project by Select Committee - Whether Continuance of list beyond one year is legal? - Whether HC can continue list beyond one year? - Held, Illegal even HC cannot continue list beyond one year unless its Constitutional validity is challenged (B) Constitution of India, arts. 16 and 311 - Appointment - Empanelled/wait listed candidate - Legitimate expectation - Whether halting of appointments with an intention to amend or modify reservation policy , the action of State arbitrary, irrational and mala fide ? - Held, cannot be said to be arbitrary irrational and mala fide especially when select list had lapsed on expiry of one year as contemplated by Rules - Appeal allowed.
