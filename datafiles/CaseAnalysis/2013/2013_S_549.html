Saroj v Sunder Singh and others

Supreme Court of India

25 November 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhansu Jyoti Mukhopadhaya, V. Gopala Gowda

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 756; (2013) 15 SCC 727; 2014 (102) ALR 270; 2014 (2) ALT(SC) 21; 2014 (1) AWC 979; 2014 (1) CLT(SC) 106; 2013 (6) CTC 775; 2014 (3) Law Herald (P&H) 1995; 2014 (1) OLR 528; 2014 (1) RCR(Civil) 227; 2014 (122) RD 386; 2013(14) SCALE 356; 2014 (1) WLN(SC) 24

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Keywords: Denied, Suit for cancellation of sale deed, Minorâs share

Summary: Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956-Sections 8 (2) and 8 (3)-Sale of minors' property without obtaining prior permission of Court-Suit for cancellation of sale deeds-Nothing on record to suggest that previous permission of court obtained by natural guardian before transfer by sale in question-Provision of Section 8 (3) attracted-Sale deeds executed by second respondent in favour of first respondent become voidable at instance of minor, i.e., appellant and proforma-respondent Nos. 4 and 5-Judgments and orders passed by trial court, first appellate court and second appellate court-Set aside-Suit decreed in favour of appellant and proforma respondent Nos. 4 and 5.
