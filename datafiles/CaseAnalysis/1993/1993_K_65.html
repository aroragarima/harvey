Union of India v Kewal Kumar

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1308; (1993) 3 SCC 204; (1993) SCC (L&S) 744; AIR 1993 SC 1585; 1993 (24) ATC 770; 1993 (2) CLR 680; 1993 (67) FLR 228; JT 1993 (2) SC 705; 1993 LabIC 1317; 1993 (2) LLJ 692; 1993 (2) LLN 52; 1993(2) SCALE 551; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 45; 1993 (2) SLR 554; 1993 (2) UJ 340; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 1428; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 898

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Office Memorandum No. 22011/2/86 D/-12-01-1988, c (iv) - Applicability of sealed cover procedure for promotion - Decision to initiate disciplinary proceedings against Govt. servant for imposition of major penalty on basis of FIR - Decision taken prior to meeting of DPC for considering him for promotion - Held, sealed cover procedure attracted - Appeal allowed.
