Union of India and others v West Coast Paper Mills Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

05 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 82; (2004) 2 SCC 747; AIR 2004 SC 1596; 2004 (2) AWC 1830; 2004 (113) ECR 330; 2004 (164) E.L.T. 375; JT 2004 (2) SC 183; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 148; 2004(2) SCALE 285; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 145; 2004 (1) SLT 909; 2004 (135) STC 265; 2004 (1) Supreme 1051

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Civil Procedure - Constitution - Contract and Commercial - Indirect Tax - Practice and Procedure - Sales Tax - Doctrine of Merger - Appeal is continuation of suit - Decision of the Constitution Bench in s.S Rathore (supra) was to be followed in the instant case - P K Kutty Anuja Raja and another vs State of Kerala and another (JT 1996(2) SC 167 : (1996) 2 SCC 496) - Overruled- Held on facts - Order of dismissal made by the Collector did merge into the order of the Divisional Commissioner when the appellant's appeal was dismissed on August 31, 1966 [B]Limitation - Starting point of limitation for filing a suit for the purpose of recovery of the excess amount of freight illegally realised begins from the date of the order passed by this Court - Respondents were entitled to the benefits of s.s 14 and 15 of the Limitations Act, 1963 - Matter remanded - Petition disposed of.
