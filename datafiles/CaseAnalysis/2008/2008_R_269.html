Maharashtra State Judges Association and Others v Registrar General, High Court of Judicature At Bombay and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, R.V. Raveendran, J.M. Panchal

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1946; (2009) 1 SCC (L&S) 208; (2009) 1 SCC 569; AIR 2009 SC 1571; 2009 (2) Bom.C.R. 72; 2009 (1) ESC 98; JT 2009 (1) SC 74; 2008(15) SCALE 320; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 247; 2009 (2) SLR 138; 2009 (1) UPLBEC 616

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Feeder Post, Sources Of Recruitment, Maharashtra Judicial Service (Seniority) Rules, 2007

Summary: Maharashtra Judicial Service (Seniority) Rules 2007 - Whether the caderisation (merger of posts of District Judges/Joint District Judges/City Civil Court Judges/Chief Judge of Small Causes Court/Addl. District Judges/Addl. Chief Judge of Small Cause Court) should be with effect from 31.3.1994 instead of 1.7.1996?; Whether insertion of proviso to r. 4(1) to protect the seniority of persons appointed by direct recruitment or promotion between 1.7.1996 to 31.3.2003 is illegal and discriminatory? - Held, caderisation was rightly given effect from 1.7.1996 under the Rules - All that the proviso to r. 4(1) has done is to provide the protection to which those mentioned in the said proviso were entitled - There is nothing discriminating or illegal in the said proviso - Upheld the validity of the Rules - Petition dismissed.
