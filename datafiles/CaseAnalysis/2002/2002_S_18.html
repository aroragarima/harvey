Supreme Court of India

09 May 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 321; (2002) 5 SCC 229; AIR 2002 SC 2268; 2002 (47) ALR 733; JT 2002 (4) SC 639; 2002 (3) MLJ(SC) 130; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 648; 2002(4) SCALE 521; [2002] 3 S.C.R. 929; 2002 (3) SLT 684; 2002 (4) Supreme 276

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Control) Act, 1960 - Section 14(1)(b) - The High Court has rightly ordered eviction following the law laid down by the Constitution Bench in Vijay Singh & Ors. Case since, the building is an old construction requiring demolition and reconstruction, out of the total area of the property only a part is built up and substantial portion is lying open and vacant, and there is pressure of population on the developing city.
