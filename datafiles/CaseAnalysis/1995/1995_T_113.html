Anil Saran v The State Of Bihar And Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1176; (1995) 6 SCC 142; (1995) SCC (Cr) 1051; AIR 1996 SC 204; AIR 1995 SCW 3937; 1995 (3) AWC 1917; 1995 (4) CCR 59; 1995 (3) Crimes 740; 1996 CRLJ 408; JT 1995 (6) SC 428; 1995(5) SCALE 216; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 58

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 190(1) and 192(1) - Indian Penal Code 1860, s. 406 - Cognizance of offence was in question - Criminal breach of trust - Misappropriation of own's fund by partner - Partner entrusted with property under special contract but property is held by him in fiduciary capacity - Held, magistrate to whom case is transferred not denuded of power under s. 190(1) merely because sanction of transfer of case to his file is not in accordance with law - Misappropriation of funds has taken place.
