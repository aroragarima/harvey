Voltas Limited v Rolta India Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 91; (2014) 4 SCC 516; AIR 2014 SC 1772; 2014 (3) ALD(SC) 14; 2014(5) ALL MR 427; 2014 (2) AWC 1712; 2014 (1) CLT(SC) 339; 2014 (1) CompLJ 431; JT 2014 (3) SC 197; 2014 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1634; 2014 (3) RCR(Civil) 378; 2014(2) SCALE 301; [2014] 2 S.C.R. 797

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Counter claims

Summary: Arbitration-Interim award-Counter-claim lodged-Appellant filed objections about tenability of counter-claim stating same was not maintainable and was barred by limitation-Irresistible conclusion that counter-claim instituted-Within limitation-Interim award passed by Arbitrator as regards rejection of counter-claim in toto stands nullified-Arbitrator directed to deal with counter-claim-Impugned judgment and order of Division Bench of High Court-Modified.
