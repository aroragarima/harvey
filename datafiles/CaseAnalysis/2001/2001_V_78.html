Vijay Kumar Sharma and Others v Chairman, School Service Commission and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20203; (2001) 4 SCC 289; (2001) SCC (L&S) 694; AIR 2001 SC 1691; 2001 (45) ALR 595; 2001 LabIC 1733; 2001(3) SCALE 442; 2001 (2) SLR 795; 2001 (3) SLT 819; 2001 (3) Supreme 451

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Advertisement for filling up vacancies in schools - Appellants applied - Panel prepared - Appointment letters not issued - Writ allowed Appeal - Division Bench set aside - DB dismissed writ petition on ground that vacancies which had been advertised were tentative and, therefore no direction could be issued for filling up vacancies - Held, appellant 2 was not empaneled and, as such, has no right to make any claim to be appointed - Therefore, appeal so far as Appellant 2 is concerned stands dismissed - Appellants 1, 3 and 4 were empaneled - Panel, so far as General Category is concerned, has been extended to 02-02-2002 - Appellants 3 and 4 will be appointed against those vacancies - Appellant 1 belongs to OBC Category - No justification for not appointing Appellant 1 when vacancies were available - No justification for not extending panel life of OBC Category - Direction that Appellant 1 be appointed against vacancies which are available in OBC Category - Order accordingly.
