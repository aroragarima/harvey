Titaghur Paper Mills Co. Limited v State Of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 372; (1983) 2 SCC 433; AIR 1983 SC 603; 1983 (34) CTR 393; [1983] 142 ITR 663; 1983(1) SCALE 437; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 743; 1983 (53) STC 315; 1983 TAXLR 2905; 1983 UJ 503

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Keywords: Investment Company

Summary: Constitution, arts. 32 and 226 - Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947, ss. 23, 24 and 13 - Maintainability of writ petiton - Alternative remedy - By way of appeal and second appeal under the Act - And if they fails, to have the case stated to High Court - Held, not maintainable - Petition Dismissed.
