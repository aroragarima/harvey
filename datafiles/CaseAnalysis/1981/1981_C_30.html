Shiela Kaushish v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 252; (1981) 4 SCC 121; (1981) SCC (Tax) 286; AIR 1981 SC 1729; 1981 (24) CTR 171; [1981] 131 ITR 435; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 246; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 309; 1981 TAXLR 1463; [1981] 7 TAXMAN 1; 1981 UJ 708

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 23(1) - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. (1)(b) - Income Tax - Building let on rent - Determination of Annual Value - Standard rent not determined or date for fixation expired - Held, annual value is value equal to standard rent under 1958 Act and not contractual rent - Factors immaterial - Appeal allowed.
