State of Haryana and Another v Kamal Singh Saharwat and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 732; (1999) 8 SCC 44; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1410; AIR 1999 SC 3775; 1999 (2) CLR 1147; 1999 (83) FLR 587; JT 1999 (7) SC 409; 1999 LabIC 3558; 1999 (4) LLN 1172; 1999(6) SCALE 177; 2000 (1) SCJ 299; 2000 (2) SLJ 19; 1999 (5) SLR 224; 1999 (9) SLT 549; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 67; 1999 (8) Supreme 679

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Haryana State Education Lecturer School Cadre (Group C) Service Rules, 1998

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Haryana State Education Lecturer School Cadre (Group-C) Service Rules, 1998, r. 1 - Punjab Educational Service, Class III, School Cadre Rules, 1955, r. 10 - Teachers - Higher pay - Acquiring academic qualifications prescribed for post of Lecturer - Held, no automatic entitlement for higher pay of lecturer.
