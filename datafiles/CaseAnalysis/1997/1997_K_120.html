Anil Bapurao Kanase v Krishna Sahakari Sakhar Karkhana Limited and

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1561; (1997) 10 SCC 599; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1637; AIR 1997 SC 2698; 1997 (2) CLR 383; 1997 (76) FLR 847; JT 1997 (5) SC 597; 1998 (1) LLJ 343; 1997 (3) LLN 67; 1997 LLR 701; 1997(4) SCALE 338; 1998 (1) SLJ 147; 1997 (4) SLR 586; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 259; 1997 (5) Supreme 646

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo), 25F - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Termination of employee doing seasonal work in Sugar Factory after work was over - If amounts to retrenchment - Held, no - Appeal dismissed.
