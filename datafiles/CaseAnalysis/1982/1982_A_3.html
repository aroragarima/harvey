Abdul Zabbar v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 59; (1984) SCC (Cr) 106; (1984) 1 SCC 443; AIR 1983 SC 505; 1983 CRLJ 853; 1983 CRLR 14; 1983 MLJ 526; 1982(2) SCALE 1114

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Foreign Exchange & FDI - Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Conservation of Foreign exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Detention - Petitioner denied right of representation to petitioner by his friend who was not a legal practitioner - Held, denial of right by advisory board - Not proper - Proceedings vitiated - Consequently, detention of petitioner is illegal - Petition allowed.
