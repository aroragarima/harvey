Government of Andhra Pradesh and Another v Dr. R. Murali Babu Rao and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 753; (1988) 2 SCC 386; (1988) SCC (L&S) 610; AIR 1988 SC 1048; 1988 (7) ATC 316; JT 1988 (1) SC 569; 1988 LabIC 1555; 1988 (2) LLJ 84; 1988(1) SCALE 542; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 173; 1988 (3) SLJ 154; 1988 (2) SLR 182; 1988 (1) UJ 706

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Medical Council Act, 1956, ss. 3, and 19 A - Andhra Pradesh Medical and Health Service Special Rules, 1982 - (A) Interpretation of provisions of r. 5 and Annexture II, Serial No. 17, Column 5, cl (b) for determining eligibility for promotion to post of Professor of Cardiology - (B) Whether recommendation of medical council is mandatory in nature and overrides rules framed u/art. 309 of Constitution? - Held, liberally construed, 'Five year teaching experience' need not be one gained after second PG qualification - Not mandatory and cannot override rules farmed u/art. 309 - Appeal and petition dismissed.
