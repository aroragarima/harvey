Grid Corporation Of Orissa Ltd. v Gajendra Haldea And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 August 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1262; (2008) 13 SCC 414; AIR 2009 SC 304; 2008 (4) JhrCR(SC) 10; JT 2008 (9) SC 184; 2008(11) SCALE 313; [2008] 12 S.C.R. 79

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Central Electricity Regulatory Commission (Fixation of Trading Margin) Regulations, 2006, Central Electricity Rules, 2005

Summary: Electricity Act, 2003, s. 52 r/w 79(1)(g) - Question regarding jurisdiction of the Appellate Tribunal for Electricity - Held, Appellate Tribunal's conclusions regarding nature of transactions are not supportable when various clauses of the agreement are considered - They clearly establish inter-state nature of the transactions - Appellate Tribunal was wrong in interfering with the conclusions of CERC that respondent No.1's petition was not entertainable and/or maintainable - Appeals allowed.
