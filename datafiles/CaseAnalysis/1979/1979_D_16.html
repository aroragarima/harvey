Desh Bandhu Gupta & Co. & Ors. v Delhi Stock Exchange Assn. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Sarkaria, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 265; (1979) 4 SCC 565; AIR 1979 SC 1049; [1980] 50 Comp Cas 84; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 373

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Securities Exchange Board of India

Summary: Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act, 1956, s.16(1) - Notification No. S. O. 2561 D/- 27-06-1969 - Interpretation of Statutes - Principle of 'contemporanea expositio' - Securities - Notification banning all forward trading in shares at all stock exchanges - Scope and ambit of proviso contained in notification , interpreting 'such contracts' in Proviso - Documents can be looked into for finding out true intention of Govt. in issuing Notification - Proviso permitted the closing or liquidating of the existing outstanding transactions in the normal manner by entering into a forward contract - Appeal dismissed.
