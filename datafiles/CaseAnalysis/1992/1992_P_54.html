State of Madhya Pradesh and Another v Pramod Bhartiya and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 548; (1993) SCC (L&S) 221; (1993) 1 SCC 539; AIR 1993 SC 286; 1993 (23) ATC 657; 1992 (65) FLR 991; JT 1992 (5) SC 683; 1992 LabIC 2418; 1993 (1) LLJ 490; 1993 (1) LLN 210; 1992(2) SCALE 791; 1993 (2) SLJ 91; 1992 (5) SLR 643; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 904

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service; Women & Children

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 (1) and 39(d) - Equal Remuneration Act, 1976, s. 2(G) - Enforceable of rule of equal pay for equal work as implicit in arts. 14 and 16(1) in Court of Law - Burden of proof, on whom lies on its infraction considering fact that it is not a mechanical rule - Held, is on one complaining of discrimination - Appeal allowed.
