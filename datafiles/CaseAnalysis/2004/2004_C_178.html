Commissioner of Central Excise, Madras v Systems and Components Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 242; (2004) 10 SCC 764; 2004 (165) E.L.T. 136; 2004 (61) RLT 257

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Tariff Item

Summary: [A.] Excise - Classification of goods - Assessee admitting use of goods for water treatment plant and not disputing nature of such goods - Department not required to prove such use or nature of goods - Water Chilling Plant parts used in manufacture of Water Chilling Plant classified under Heading 84. 19 of Central Excise Tariff by Department as parts of Water Chilling Plant as admitted by assessee whereas assessee claimed classification under Heading 73. 09- Once it is an admitted position by party themselves that these are parts of a Chilling Plant and concerned party not even disputed that such parts have no independent use, no need for department to prove same - Tribunal's order set aside and matter remitted back to Tribunal to decide on merits on basis of admitted position and considering Board's Circular.
