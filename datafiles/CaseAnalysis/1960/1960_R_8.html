Â STATE of ORISSA and ANOTHER v RAM NARAYAN DAS

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, S.K. Das, Mohammad Hidayatullah, K.C. Das Gupta, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 409; AIR 1961 SC 177; 1961 (2) FLR 1; 1961 (1) LLJ 552; 1961 (1) SCJ 209; [1961] 1 S.C.R. 606

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Madhya Bharat Accommodation Control Act, 1950, s. 7(2) - (A) Whether notice given by tenant was sufficient and proper? - Held, u/s. 7(2) of Madhya Bharat Accommodation Control Act, 1950 - No words in section which makes it obligatory that notice should be issued in terms as under Act and be given after Act came into force - Nor has it prescribed any particular form - Notice given by tenant was proper - Appeal allowed.
