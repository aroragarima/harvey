Narayanamma v State of Karnataka

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 480; (1994) 5 SCC 728; (1994) SCC (Cr) 1573; 1995 (1) CCR 53; 1994 (3) Crimes 286; JT 1994 (5) SC 436; 1994(3) SCALE 879; 1995 (1) SCJ 147; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 799

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 376 - Rape - Accused persons were convicted by Trial Court and on appeal acquitted by High Court - Whether HC correct in reversing the conviction order into acquittal? - Held, prosecutrix a reliable witness and HC wrongly doubted her evidence - Her evidence corroborated by medical evidence and other independent witnesses also - Conviction order of trial court restored - Appeal allowed.
