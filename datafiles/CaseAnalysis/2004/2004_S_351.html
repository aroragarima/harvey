N. Bhargavan Pillai (Dead) By Lrs.And Anr. v State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

20 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 283; (2004) 13 SCC 217; (2005) SCC (Cr) 142; AIR 2004 SC 2317; 2004 CRLJ 2494; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 194; 2004 (2) KLT 725; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 641; 2004(4) SCALE 693; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 444; 2004 (2) UC 851

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 19, 5(2) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 409 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, s. 197 - Probation Of Offenders Act, 1958, s. 12 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898 - Sanction for prosecution - Sanction u/s. 197 of Code is not a condition precedent for an offence u/s. 409 IPC - It was not only on basis of undertaking that conviction was recorded, but other evidence on record also unerringly proved entrustment - Therefore, it was for accused to prove as to how property entrusted with him dealt with - No material placed in that regard - Benefit under Probation Act, s 18 of Act rules out application of Probation Act to a case covered u/s. 5(2) of Act - Appeal dismissed.
