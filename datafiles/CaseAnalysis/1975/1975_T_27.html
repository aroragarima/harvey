Sales Tax Officer, Special Circle, Ernakulam and v Tata Oil Mills Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, M. Hameedullah Beg, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 206; (1975) 2 SCC 394; AIR 1975 SC 1991; 1975 (4) CTR 211; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 152; 1975 (36) STC 232; 1975 TAXLR 1958; 1975 UJ 665

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Keywords: Collected, Railway Freight

Summary: Constitution, arts. 245, 246, sch.7, list 2, entry 54 - Kerala Genral Sales Tax Act, 1963, s. 22(3) - Competency of State Legislature to enact s. 22 (3) of 1963 Act - State Legislature held, is not competent to enact law contained in s. 22 (3) of 1963 Act in so far as it related to payment of an amount collected as tax on transactions not liable to tax under the Act or in excess of the tax leviable under the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
