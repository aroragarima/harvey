Union Of India v State Of Gujarat And Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Dalveer Bhandari, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2755; (2010) 15 SCC 664

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Encroachment on public space - Suo moto action - News daily published a news item stating that 1200 temples and 260 Islamic shrines had encroached on public space - HC, by suo moto, issued notice to fourteen persons/authorities as respondents - HC, on interim, directed to take immediate steps for removal of encroachment of religious structures on the public space - Petitioner UOI contended that this interim direction is in the nature of final direction which could be issued only after hearing respondents and no such direction could be issued at a preliminary stage without hearing respondents - Held, SC already stayed operation of impugned order - Petitioner states that Central Govt. after convening a meeting of all the concerned Secretaries of respective States would try to take a consensual decision to deal with the problems such as in present case all over country and seeks time for this purpose - Listed on future date - Order accordingly.
