Principal, J. D. Patil Sangludkar and Another v Ganesh

Supreme Court of India

10 March 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1440; (2003) 9 SCC 164

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Service - Indian Penal Code, ss. 498-A and 302 - Whether High Court correct in directing payment of full salary to respondent, pending his suspension on account of criminal proceedings? - Charge of burning his wife - Held, pending actual consideration of issue and a final decision on same, HC ought not to have allowed full salary - Further held, in a case of nature where accused is charged with a serious criminal offence and is facing prosecution for same at instance of police before competent criminal courts and adjudication in respect of same by competent criminal court which is seized of matter has to be awaited as a matter of necessity, and service rule does not permit as such, court cannot allow full subsistence allowance amounting to full pay and allowances - Appeal allowed.
