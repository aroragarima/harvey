Renu Mullick v Union Of India (Kuldip Singh, J.)

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1662; (1994) 1 SCC 373; (1994) SCC (L&S) 570; AIR 1994 SC 1152; 1994 (26) ATC 602; 1994 (2) CLR 12; 1994 (50) ECR 481; 1994 (68) FLR 215; JT 1993 (6) SC 527; 1994 (1) LLN 511; 1993(4) SCALE 556; 1994 (1) SLR 262; 1994 (1) UJ 154

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Central Excise and Land Customs Dept Group 'C' Posts Recruitment Rules, 1976, r. 4 - Instruction D/- 20-05-1980, Para 2(ii) - Appointment of Upper Division Clerk by promotion - Whether past service in previous collectorate from which she transferred on her request can be considered for determination of seniority and thereby eligible for promotion - Held, cannot be ignored - Appeal allowed.
