Anna Bhau Magdum, Since Deceased By His Legal Representatives v Babasaheb Anandrao Desai.

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1698; (1995) 5 SCC 243; AIR 1995 SC 2164; JT 1995 (5) SC 519; 1995(4) SCALE 538; 1995 (3) SCJ 411; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 259

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948 - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, ss. 32 F(1 A)and 32 P - Whether tenant can be said to fail to exercise his right to purchase - Held, tenant failed to give intimation of his intention to purchase land within period contemplated by original Act and not even in further period of two years from date of commencement of Amendment Act - Tenant failed to exercise his right of purchase and he is liable to summary eviction u/s. 32 P(1) - Appeal dismissed.
