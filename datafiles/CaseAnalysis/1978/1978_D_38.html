Badri Prasad v Dy. Director Of Consolidation And Ors

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 147; (1978) 3 SCC 527; AIR 1978 SC 1557; 1978 ALJ 1010; 1978 (4) ALR 681; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1; 1978 UJ 545

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114 -Presumption and proof as to factum of marriage - Where a man and a woman living as husband and wife for about 50 years - Held, strong presumption arises in favour of wedlock - Proof as to factum of marriage by examining the priest and other witnesses not necessary - Petition Dismissed.
