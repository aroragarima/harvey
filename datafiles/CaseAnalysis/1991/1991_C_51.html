Delhi Cold Storage Pvt. Limited v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Delhi-I, New Delhi.

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, Kuldip Singh, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 744; (1991) 4 SCC 239; AIR 1991 SC 2125; [1991] 72 Comp Cas 723; 1991 (3) CompLJ 131; 1991 (98) CTR 165; 1991 (45) DLT 227; [1991] 191 ITR 656; JT 1991 (3) SC 449; 1991(2) SCALE 346; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 589; [1991] 59 TAXMAN 144

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Finance Act ,1973, s. 2(7)(c) - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 4 - A company running cold store - 'processing' - Whether a company running a cold storage is an 'industrial company' within the meaning of s. 2(7)(c) of the Finance Act, 1973 and it First Schedule - Company can not be considered as an industrial company - Appeal dismissed.
