S. J. Ebenezer v Velayudhan and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1205; (1998) 1 SCC 633; AIR 1998 SC 746; JT 1997 (10) SC 135; 1998 (2) MLJ 50; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 50; 1998 (1) RCJ 138; 1998 (1) RCR(Rent) 86; 1997(7) SCALE 635; 1998 (1) SLT 452; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 441

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 227 - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 11(3) - Eviction - Ground, bona fide need of landlord - Held, mere desire of landlord not sufficient for bona fide need - To be tested objectively - Burden also lies upon landlord to establish fact.
