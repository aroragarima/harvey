Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Fixation

Summary: Fixation of quota in respect of unaided professional institutions and to the holding of examinations for admission and to holding of examinations for admission - Held, controversy involved should be referred to a larger Bench for final determination - Interim order granted
