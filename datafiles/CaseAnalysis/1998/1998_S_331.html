Dhan Raj and Others v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 652; (1998) 4 SCC 30; (1998) SCC (L&S) 982; AIR 1998 SC 1747; JT 1998 (2) SC 590; 1998 LabIC 1577; 1999 (1) LLJ 633; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 404; 1998 (3) SLJ 124; 1998 (2) UPLBEC 1525

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: State Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Constitution of Jammu and Kashmir, s. 124, Proviso - Jammu and Kashmir Civil Service Regulations, art. 177, 3rd Proviso - Service Rules - Eligibility of temporary drivers and conductors in transport corporation to claim pension when they were retiring prior to date of amendment of art. 177 of Regulations - Constitutionality of wriggle out of its own order by State to give benefit to its employees - Held, it is opposed to constitutional goal - Appeal allowed.
