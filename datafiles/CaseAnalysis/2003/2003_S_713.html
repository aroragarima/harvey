Darpan Kumar Sharma @ Dharban Kumar Sharma v State of Tamil Nadu and others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 14; (2003) 2 SCC 313; (2003) SCC (Cr) 537; AIR 2003 SC 971; 2003 (1) CCR 169; 2003 (1) Crimes 446; 2003 (3) CRJ 237; 2003 CRLJ 1222; JT 2003 (1) SC 176; 2003 (1) RCR(Criminal) 514; 2003(1) SCALE 325; [2003] 1 S.C.R. 442; 2003 (1) SLT 431; 2003 (1) Supreme 508

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: Drug offenders

Summary: Constitution of India - Article 32 - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Bootleggers, Drug-offenders Forest offenders, Goondas, Immoral traffic offenders and Slum grabbers Act, 1982 - S. 3 - plea to quash the order of preventive detention - only a solitary instance wherein the detenu is alleged to have robbed in a public place - Held, the question whether a man has only committed a breach of law and order or has acted in a manner likely to cause disturbance of the public order is a question of degree and the extent of the reach of the act upon the society; that a solitary assault on one individual can hardly be said to disturb public or place public order in jeopardy so as to bring the case within the purview of the Act providing for preventive detention.
