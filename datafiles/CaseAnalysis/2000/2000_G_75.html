Narsingh Das Tapadia v Goverdhan Das Partani and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 342; (2000) 7 SCC 183; AIR 2000 SC 2946; AIR 2000 SCW 3268; 2001 (1) BC 113; 2000 (1) CLC 2028; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 146; JT 2000 (10) SC 141; 2000 (3) KLT 605; 2000 (4) RCR(Criminal) 39; 2000(6) SCALE 299; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 171; 2001 (1) UC 50

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Trade

Summary: Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, ss. 138 and 142 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 190 - Dishonour of cheque - Filing of complaint before expiry of statutory period of notice - Held, cannot be dismissed as premature - Taking cognizance and filing of complaint are distinct.
