Krishna District Co-Operative Marketing Society Limited, Vijayawada v N. V. Purnachandra Rao and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28165; (1987) 4 SCC 99; (1987) SCC (L&S) 366; AIR 1987 SC 1960; 1987 (2) BankCLR 465; 1987 (2) CCC 617; 1987 (55) FLR 498; JT 1987 (3) SC 197; 1987 LabIC 1651; 1987 (2) LLJ 365; 1987 (2) LLN 671; 1987(2) SCALE 175; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 728; 1987 (3) SLJ 68; 1987 (2) UJ 663

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946, AP State and Subordinate Service Rules 1962

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 254 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 1, chap. VA, 25 J - Andhra Pradesh Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1966, ss. 40(1) and 40(3) - Industrial Dispute (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, s. 4 - Service matter - Retrenchment - Establishment governed by State Act - (A) Applicability of central and state legislation - (B) Illegal termination of service - Held, chap. V-A of Central Act will have to be complied if establishment is 'industry' and employee is 'Workman' as defined in Central Act and State Act does not prevail over provisions of chap. V-A of Central Act - Amendment of Industrial Disputes Act making it possible to individual workman to seek redress, suggested by Supreme Court - Petition dismissed.
