Union of India and another v Delhi Cloth and General Mills Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2215; (1997) 4 SCC 303; AIR 1998 SC 2917; 1997 (70) ECR 3; 1997 (91) E.L.T. 23; JT 1997 (4) SC 324; 1997 (19) RLT 475; 1997(2) SCALE 609; 1998 (109) STC 113; 1997 (3) Supreme 274; 1997 (1) UJ 699

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, s. 4 - Levy of excise duty on calcium carbide manufactured by assessee company which was entirely consumed in manufacture of acetylene gas produced by same company in same factory premises - Argument was that calcium carbide was not 'goods' and hence not capable of being sold as commercial commodity nor could be subject to excise duty - Held, matter directed to be placed before larger bench.
