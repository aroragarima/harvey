Supreme Court of India

05 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: Office Of Profit Under, Assam Basic Education Act, 1954, Assam Elementary Education Act, 1968, Assam, Elementary Education Act, 1962, Assam Elementary Education (Provincialisation) Act, 1974, Assam Elementary Education (Provincialisation) Service and Conduct Rules, 1981

Summary: Election - Representation of the People Act, 1951 - s. 100 - Disqualification - Holding post of profit under the state - Employer-employee relationship required - Appellant's services terminated - Termination accepted - No employer-employee relationship - HC order set aside - No disqualification under Act or the Constitution - Appeal allowed.
