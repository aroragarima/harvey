Gema Coutinho Rodrigues Smt v Bricio Francisco Pereira and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 881; (1993) 2 SCC 620; AIR 1994 SC 1199; JT 1993 (3) SC 544

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Application for substitution - Whether judgment of Punaji Bench, Goa of Bombay High Court D/- 11-01-1983 whereby Learned Single Judge of HC upheld order D/- 19-03-1979 passed by Trial Court is correct? - Held, it appears that gift deeds were made by deceased brother's brother-in-law in pursuance of power of attorney in his favour - So long as one of heirs has been brought on record who substantially represented estate of deceased plaintiff, application could not be dismissed on ground that suit has abated or it could not proceed - Trial Court should have directed appellant to implead other heirs if any, of deceased mother who was also party to suit by way of defendants - But application for being brought on record by appellant could not have been rejected - Set aside order of Trial Court D/- 19-03-1979 as well as order of HC D/- 11-01-1983 and direct Trial Court to bring appellant on record as legal heir of deceased plaintiffs and permit appellant to implead any other heirs as co-defendants - Appeal allowed.
