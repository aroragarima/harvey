Uttaranchal Transport Corporation Limited v Vimla Devi and others

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2267; AIR 2009 SC 2146; 2009 (3) AWC 2986; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 468; [2009] 2 S.C.R. 504

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Sections 166 and 168 - Compensation for death - Compensation for death - Enhancement - Tribunal awarded compensation at Rs. 1,84,500 with 9% Interest - High Court fixed notional Income at Rs. 30,000 p.a. - Applied multiplier of 15 since deceased was aged about 43 years - And fixed compensation at Rs. 3,60,000 with 9% Interest - Whether calls for Interference? - Held, "yes" - In view of age of deceased, multiplier appears to be on higher side - Multiplier should be 10 - And rate of Interest 6% - Accordingly, compensation fixed at Rs. 2,40,000.
