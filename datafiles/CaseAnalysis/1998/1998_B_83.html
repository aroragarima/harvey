Commissioner of Income Tax v Bokaro Steel Limited

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1885; (1999) 1 SCC 645; AIR 1999 SC 387; 1999 (151) CTR 276; [1999] 236 ITR 315; JT 1998 (8) SC 615; 1998(6) SCALE 611; 1999 (1) SLT 1; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 680; 1998 (9) Supreme 383; 1999 TAXLR 69; [1999] 102 TAXMAN 94; 1999 (2) TLR 69

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 28 - Business or capital receipt - Assessee-company in process of erecting its plant - Receiving income by permitting its contractor to use various assets of company - Held, receipts directly related with process of setting up of its plant - Royalty received by assessee - Are capital receipts.
