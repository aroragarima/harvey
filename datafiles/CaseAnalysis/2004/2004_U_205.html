New Moga Transport Company, Through Its v United India Insurance Co. Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 312; (2004) 4 SCC 677; 2004 (2) ACC 19; AIR 2004 SC 2154; 2004 (3) ALD(SC) 143; 2004 (56) ALR 245; 2004 (3) AWC 2230; [2004] 121 Comp Cas 180; [2004] 121 Comp Cas 187; 2004 (98) CutLT 81; 2004 (3) JhrCR(SC) 63; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 294; 2005 (139) PLR 61; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 141; 2004(5) SCALE 73; 2004 (3) SLT 240; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 623

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: CPC, s.20 - Whether Civil Court at Barnala had jurisdiction to try suit - Consignment note indicated that Court at Udaipur alone had jurisdiction - Once parties bound themselves as such it is not open for them to choose a different jurisdiction - Appeal allowed
