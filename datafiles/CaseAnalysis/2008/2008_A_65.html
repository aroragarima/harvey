Abhishek Malviya v Additional Welfare Commissioner and Another

Supreme Court of India

23 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 62; (2008) 3 SCC 108; 2008 (1) ACC 376; AIR 2008 SC 1222; 2008(1) SCALE 635

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Bhopal Gas Leak, Bhopal Gas Leak Disaster (Processing Of Claims) Act, 1985

Summary: The Bhopal Gas Leak Disaster (Registration and Processing of Claims) Scheme, 1985 - Appeal relates to compensation payable to a Bhopal gas tragedy victim - Application for payment of compensation under the scheme for payment of compensation to the gas victims alleging that the appellant's pregnant mother was affected by the leaked gas and consequently, the appellant, who was in her womb, was also affected - Rs. 55, 000 awarded as compensation - Claim for enhancement - Held, order dated 13.3.1997 having attained finality, his efforts to re-agitate the issue again and again is an exercise in futility - Even on merits appellant was unable to demonstrate how the appellant was entitled to be placed in a higher category under the scheme so as to become entitled for higher compensation - Appeal dismissed.
