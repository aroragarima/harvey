C. L. Verma v State of Madhya Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.N. Venkatachaliah, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 274; (1989) Supp2 SCC 437; AIR 1990 SC 463; 1991 (17) ATC 217; 1989 (59) FLR 786; JT 1989 (4) SC 182; 1989 (2) LLN 965; 1989 MPLJ 764; 1989(2) SCALE 894; 1990 (1) SLR 134; 1990 (1) UJ 174

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: State Municipal Service (Executive) rules 1973

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Service - Madhya Pradesh State Municipal Service (Executive) Rules, 1973, r. 29 - Superannuation - Order of dismissal - Issued two days after employee attained age of 58 i.e., of superannuation as per r. 29 - Justification sought - Ground, superannuation would be effective on last day of month in which employee completed his 58 years in view of administrative instructions - Held, dismissal invalid, as statutory rule would prevail over notification it being in nature of administrative instruction - Appeal allowed.
