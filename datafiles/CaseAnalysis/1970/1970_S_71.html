Mohammad Mustafa v Sri Abu Bakar and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 395; (1970) 3 SCC 891; AIR 1971 SC 361; 1971 UJ 109

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 20, r. 4, O. 6, r. 17 - Constitution, art. 136 - (A) Provisions of O. 20, r. 4 of the Code - Contents of judgment - Finding without proper pleadings and necessary issues - Whether binding - (B) Provisions of O. 6, r. 17of the Code - Application for amendment by defendant appellant - In appeal arising from suit for partition - High Court rejecting application - Taking highly technical view in granting relief - Held, not binding on parties to suit - On appeal to Supreme Court application was allowed in the interest of justice - Order Accordingly.
