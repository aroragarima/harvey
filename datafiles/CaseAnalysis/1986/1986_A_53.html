Yudhishter v Ashok Kumar

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 603; (1987) 1 SCC 204; AIR 1987 SC 558; JT 1986 SC 1021; 1987 (2) RCJ 238; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 225; 1987 (1) RentLR 29; 1986(2) SCALE 1044; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 516

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, Hindu Succession Act, 1956, Income tax Act, 1922, Income Tax Act, 1961 - Suit for eviction - Admission of additional evidence warranted by facts and pleadings of case - Held, appellate authority was not in error in admitting such additional evidence - The need of landlord was bona fide under s.13 (3) (a) (i).
