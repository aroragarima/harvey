Rajesh Kumar Verma v State Of M.P.

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1914; (1995) 2 SCC 129; AIR 1995 SC 1421; AIR 1995 SCW 2102; 1995(2) SCALE 448

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Health & Drug; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Date

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226; Rules by M.P. State Government for admission to medical colleges, r.3.5.2 - Education - Admission to medical college - Reservation for SC/ ST candidates - Eligible candidates not available - Relaxation of minimum qualifying for reserved category by State Government - Held, not illegal.
