Arun Ghosh v State Of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 59; (1970) SCC (Cr) 67; (1970) 1 SCC 98; AIR 1970 SC 1228; 1970 CRLJ 1136; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 288

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Preventive Detention Act, 1950, s. 3(2) - Grounds for detention - When individual acts can be subversive of public order - Distinction between public order and law and order -Held, it is always a question of degree of the harm and its effect upon the community - Appeal Dismissed.
