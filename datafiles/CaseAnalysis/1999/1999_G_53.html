Budhia Swain and Others v Gopinath Deb and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1167; (1999) 4 SCC 396; AIR 1999 SC 2089; JT 1999 (3) SC 548; 1999 (3) MLJ(SC) 132; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 1189

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Orissa Estate Abolition Act, 1951

Summary: Land & Prperty - Orissa Estate Abolition Act, 1951, s. 38A - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151 - Estate abolition - Power of Estate Abolition Collector - (A) Recalling of order of settlement in light of fact that public notice of claim was not served in locality - (B) Whether an Order made on time barred application, can be recalled on ground of jurisdiction or legality? - Held, mere irregularity does not occasion failure of justice, recalling of order on said ground, not warranted - Order is not without jurisdiction but an illegal order, cannot be recalled - Appeal dismissed.
