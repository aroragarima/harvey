Union of India and Others v Orient Enterprises and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1623; (1998) 3 SCC 501; AIR 1998 SC 1729; 1998 (146) CTR 546; 1998 (75) ECR 687; 1998 (99) E.L.T. 193; JT 1998 (2) SC 630; 1998(2) SCALE 467; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 143; 1998 (4) SLT 19; 1998 (3) Supreme 343

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Customs Act 1962, s. 27A - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Refund of customs duty - Right to claim interest on delayed refund was provided only after introduction of s. 27-A in 1995 - Claims for interest on delayed refund made earlier to 1995 are not in exercise of statutory right - Writ petition to claim interest therefore not maintainable.
