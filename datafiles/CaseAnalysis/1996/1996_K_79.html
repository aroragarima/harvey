Karan Singh and Others v Bhagwan Singh (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3141; (1996) 7 SCC 559; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 917; AIR 1996 SC 2249; 1996 (1) CLT 321; JT 1996 (1) SC 618; 1996(1) SCALE 594; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 924; 1996 (1) Supreme 739; 1996 (1) UJ 419

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Family & Personal - Punjab Pre - emption Act, 1913, s. 15 - Availability of right of preemption - Joint family property - Co-owner selling land in specie from coparcenary property to strangers - Subsequent sale of that land by strangers to appellant - Whether co-owner can claim right of pre-emption from said strangers - Held, cannot claim right of pre-emption - Even otherwise under amended s. 15 only tenant could exercise right of pre-emption - Appeal allowed.
