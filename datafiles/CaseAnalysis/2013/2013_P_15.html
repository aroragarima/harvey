State of Uttar Pradesh and others v Pankaj Kumar Vishnoi and another

Supreme Court of India

25 July 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra, Vikramajit Sen

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 634; (2013) 11 SCC 178; AIR 2013 SC (Supp) 80; 2013 (5) AWC 5062; JT 2013 (11) SC 408; 2013(10) SCALE 422; [2013] 7 S.C.R. 787; 2013 (5) SLR 477

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: (1) U. P. Recruitment of Dependents of Government Servants (Dying-in-Harness) Rules, 1974-Rules 5 and 8 (2)-Employment-Compassionate appointment-On higher post in violation of norms and procedure-Appointment on compassionate basis on post of Constable-Claim for grant of compassionate appointment on post of Sub-Inspector (Civil Police) without subjecting respondent to appear in any physical efficiency test and interview-Claim put forth by respondent accepted by Division Bench of High Court-Entitlement of respondent for particular post on compassionate basis-Respondent participated in physical examination test but could not become successful as result of which his candidature for post of Sub-Inspector rejected-Once respondent did not qualify physical test, High Court could not have asked department to give him opportunity to hold another test to extend him benefit of compassionate appointment on post of Sub-Inspector solely on ground of efflux of time-Even though person is considered eligible for appointment in place of employee dying in harness yet minimum standard of working and efficiency required to be considered-Candidate to maintain minimum standard of work and efficiency expected of post-Respondent after being disqualified in physical test could not claim as matter of right and demand for appointment of particular post-High Court could not have granted further opportunity after crisis of bereaved family was over-Impugned order of Division Bench of High Court-Wholly unsustainable and set aside-Open to respondent to compete in normal course if eligible for post of Sub-Inspector for promotion in accordance with rules prescribed for promotion.

(2) Employment-Compassionate appointment-Primary object is to save bereaved family from sudden financial crisis occurring due to death of sole bread winner-Compassionate appointment is exception to general rule of equality and not another independent and parallel source of employment.

(3) Employment-Compassionate appointment-Claim for compassionate appointment is traceable only to scheme framed by employer for such employment and there is no right whatsoever outside such scheme.
