Shadi Singh v Rakha

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 382; (1992) 3 SCC 55; AIR 1994 SC 800; JT 1992 (3) SC 152; 1992 (1) RCJ 502; 1992 (2) RCR(Rent) 7; 1992(1) SCALE 918; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 726; 1992 (1) UJ 718

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, ss. 13(3)(a)(iii) and 12 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 23 - Houses and rents - Eviction on ground of reconstruction - Tenant repaired premises - Held, no requirement of building of reconstruction subsists - No eviction.
