Molar Mal (Dead) Through Lrs v Kay Iron Works Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 208; (2000) 4 SCC 285; AIR 2000 SC 1261; 2000 (3) ALD(SC) 72; JT 2000 (3) SC 122; 2000 (125) PLR 678; 2000 (1) RCJ 624; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 354; 2000(2) SCALE 334; 2000 (2) SLT 726; 2000 (2) Supreme 284

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act 1973, ss. 13 and 15(6) - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 115 - Eviction petition - Revisionary power - Want of material particulars as required u/r. 4 - Held, is not mandatory - Landlord giving all material particulars in regard to its requirement for additional space though not in original petition, but in rejoinder application - Eviction petition cannot be rejected - Finding of appellate authority based on no evidence - Can be reversed by High Court.
