Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 54; (2002) 2 SCC 678; AIR 2002 SC 829; AIR 2002 SCW 494; 2002 (46) ALR 704; 2002 (2) AWC 912; JT 2002 (2) SC 9; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 146; 2002(1) SCALE 425; 2002 (1) SCJ 467; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 605; 2002 (1) SLT 517; 2002 (1) Supreme 348; 2002 (1) UC 567B

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: (1) U. P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972-Sections 20 (4), 30 (2) and 30 (6)-Suit for eviction on ground of arrears of rent-Deposit of rent by tenant at first hearing of suit-Property in question on lease granted by Municipal Board to landlord-On expiry of period of lease, Municipal Board issuing notice demanding rent from defendant petitioner-Petitioner deposit rent under Section 30 (2) in compliance therewith-Also depositing rent on first date of hearing-Whether deposit made under Section 30 (2) to be taken into account to relieve petitioner from eviction in terms of Section 20 (4)?-Held, âyesâ-If not so, it would render sub-section (2) of Section 30 devoid of meaning and sub-section (6) of Section 30 otiose-This is so notwithstanding non-mention of sub-section (2) of Section 30 in Section 20 (4)-Judgments of High Court and revisional court set aside-Order of trial court dismissing suit for eviction restored.

(2) Interpretation of statutes-Provision in statute-Not to be read in isolation-Has to be read with other related provisions.
