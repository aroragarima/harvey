Collector Of Central Excise, Bombay-I And Anr. v Parle Exports (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 831; (1989) SCC (Tax) 84; (1989) 1 SCC 345; AIR 1989 SC 644; 1989 (19) ECC 49; 1989 (20) ECR 1; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 741; [1990] 183 ITR 624; JT 1988 (4) SC 454; 1988(2) SCALE 1381; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 933; 1989 (1) ShimLC 322; 1989 (75) STC 105; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 933; 1989 (1) UJ 228

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 37 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(10) - Notification No. 55/75 C E D/- 01-04-1975 of CG - Exemption of Excise Duty - Notification of - (A) Expressions 'Food products' and 'Food preparations' - Whether covers non-alcoholic beverage Bases - (B) Interpretation of fiscal statute - Principles of - Held, do not include - Principles for interpretation specified - Appeals allowed.
