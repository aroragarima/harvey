Indian Express Private Limited v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 122; 1987 (67) STC 474

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(d) - Sale of surplus copies of newspaper - Whether surplus copies of newspapers sold by appellant can be treated as sale of newspaper and thus exempt from tax? - Business of selling surplus copies as waste paper attracted sales tax, having regard to terms of s. 2(d) of Act, which defined expression 'business', as it stood at relevant time - Held, what is exempt from sales tax is turnover of newspapers and not turnover of old newspapers or waste paper - Transactions of sale of surplus copies must be regarded as a business carried on by appellant - It was an activity which he pursued regularly, and motive was to earn a profit - Appeal dismissed.
