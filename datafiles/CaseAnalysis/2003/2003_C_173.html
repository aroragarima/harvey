Dabur India Ltd. v Commissioner Of Central Excise, Meerut

Supreme Court of India

28 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1118; (2004) 13 SCC 107; 2003 (157) E.L.T. 129; 2003 (58) RLT 833

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Trade - Central Excise Act - Whether herbal extracts were goods for purpose of Central Excise Act and if so, whether they were classifiable under Chap. 13 or 30 of Act? - Both Commr. and Tribunal have held that extracts had 'medicinal value' - Held, liquid extracts used by appellant in manufacture of medicines had therapeutic value, then they can, according to CBEC circular be classifiable only under tariff entry 30.30 and not under 13.03 - Further held, decisions of Tribunal and Commissioner are set aside and impugned demands quashed - Appeal allowed.
