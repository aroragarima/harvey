Rajendra Roy v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 742; (1993) SCC (L&S) 138; (1993) 1 SCC 148; AIR 1993 SC 1236; 1993 (23) ATC 426; 1993 (1) CLR 5; JT 1992 (6) SC 732; 1993 LabIC 446; 1993 (1) LLN 237; 1992(3) SCALE 218; 1993 (1) SLJ 93; 1993 (1) SLR 126; 1993 (1) UJ 73

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16, 226 and 309 - Transfer - Order not passed mala fide or in violation of Service Rules or without proper justification - If can be quashed - Proof of malice - Held, cannot be quashed - Cannot be established on basis of insinuation and vague suggestions - Appeal dismissed.
