Sihor Nagar Palika Bureau v Bhabhlubhai Virabhai and Company

Supreme Court of India

21 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 339; (2005) 4 SCC 1; 2005 (3) ALD(SC) 125; 2005 (60) ALR 152; 2005 (4) Bom.C.R. 395; JT 2005 (4) SC 528; 2005 (3) MLJ(SC) 63; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 672; 2005(4) SCALE 281; 2005(4) SCALE 284

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Furnishing Of Security

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41 r. 1(3), O. 41 r. 5(5), O.41 r. 5 - Decree for recovery of damages allowed - Appeal against this order admitted and granted a stay subject to condition that appellant shall deposit in court an amount of Rs. 8,78,925/- With 8 per cent interest - Prayer to modify its earlier order so as to dispense with requirement as to deposit of amount and instead permit solvent security being furnished - Whether to insist on deposit in cash or permit a security being furnished? - Held, HC ought to have permitted furnishing of security instead of insisting on deposit in cash of amount as directed by HC - Appeals allowed.
