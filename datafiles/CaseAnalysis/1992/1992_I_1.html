Addl. Commissioner Of Income Tax, Gujarat v I.M.Patel And Co.

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1002; (1993) Supp1 SCC 621; AIR 1992 SC 1762; 1992 (105) CTR 195; [1992] 196 ITR 297; JT 1992 (3) SC 614; 1992(1) SCALE 1313; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 914; 1992 (86) STC 185; [1992] 62 TAXMAN 497

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 271(1)(a) - Is tribunal justified in cancelling penalty levied on assessee u/s. 271(1)(a) for three assessment years? - Held that mens rea need not be proved before imposition of penalty u/s. 271(1)(a) - Appeal allowed.
