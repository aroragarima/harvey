State Bank Of India And Ors. v Mohd. Mynuddin

Supreme Court of India

17 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28863; (1987) SCC (L&S) 464; (1987) 4 SCC 486; AIR 1987 SC 1889; 1987 (5) ATC 59; 1987 (2) BankCLR 342; 1987 (55) FLR 301; JT 1987 (3) SC 89; 1987 LabIC 1627; 1988 (1) LLJ 142; 1988 (1) LLN 317; 1987(2) SCALE 53; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 532; 1988 (1) ShimLC 71; 1987 (3) SLJ 161; 1987 (4) SLR 383; 1987 (2) UJ 339

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 311 - Promotion - Merit basis - Denial to certain officer - Selection not vitiated by bias or mala fides - Directions sought - Direction to promote officer, who was denied promotion, cannot be given - Appeal disposed of.
