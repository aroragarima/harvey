Depot Manager, A.P. State Road Transport Corporation v Mohd. Yousuf Miya and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1923; (1997) SCC (L&S) 548; (1997) 2 SCC 699; AIR 1997 SC 2232; 1997 (1) ALT 12; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 28; 1997 (2) LLJ 902; 1996(9) SCALE 65; 1997 (1) SLJ 241B; 1996 (6) SLR 629; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 941; 1997 (1) Supreme 565

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 3 - Simultaneous criminal proceeding and departmental enquiry - Departmental proceeding stayed - Need of, when criminal prosecution for offence punishable u/ss. 304A and 338, Indian Penal Code, 1860 - And departmental enquiry on charge of failure to anticipate accident and prevention thereof, has nothing to do with culpability of offence u/ss. 304A and 338 - Held, not warranted - Enquiry to be stated only if it causes serious prejudice to delinquent's defence in criminal trial - Appeal allowed.
