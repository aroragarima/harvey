Director, Central Rice Research Institution v Khetra Mohan Das

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, A.M. Ahmadi, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 808; (1994) Supp3 SCC 595; (1995) SCC (L&S) 179; AIR 1995 SC 122; 1994 (28) ATC 588; 1995 (1) CLR 5; JT 1994 (6) SC 482; 1994 (2) LLN 1318; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 301

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research

Summary: Service - Promotion - By way of fitment - Whether order of Central Administrative Tribunal, Cuttack Bench, Cuttack holding that respondent Khetra Mohan Das should be held to have been appointed in Category II Grade TII-3 in scale of pay of Rs. 425-700 with effect from date Technical Service Rules of Indian Council of Agricultural Research came into force is correct? - Held, Tribunal, however, observed that in meantime respondent has been promoted to Grade T-I-3 of Category I carrying same scale as Grade T-3 of Category II, therefore he should be deemed to have been inducted into Grade T-3 Category II - This reasoning is erroneous - Tribunal also made a reference to r. 81 which applies only to direct recruits and it has no relevance so far as case of respondent is concerned - Finding of Tribunal that respondent should be held to have been appointed in Grade T-3 of Category II in pay scale of Rs. 425-700 with effect from 1-10-1975 is set aside - Appeal allowed.
