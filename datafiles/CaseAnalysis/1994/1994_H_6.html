H.S. Atwal v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 919; (1994) 5 SCC 341; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1091; AIR 1994 SC 2531; 1994 (27) ATC 873; 1994 (69) FLR 725; JT 1994 (5) SC 346; 1994 (2) LLN 1036; 1994(3) SCALE 555; 1995 (1) SLJ 226; 1994 (4) SLR 559; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 319; 1994 (2) UJ 726; 1994 (3) UPLBEC 1882

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - De mobilised Indian Armed Forces Personnel (Reservation of Vacancies) in Himachal Pradesh Administrative Rules, 1974, r. 4(1) - Armed Force Service - Seniority - (A) Legality of denial of benefit of military service as no opportunity of entering Administrative Service available to them - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Scope of fiction created by legal provision - Held, not illegal - Further held, such fiction cannot be carried beyond purpose for which it was created - Appeal dismissed.
