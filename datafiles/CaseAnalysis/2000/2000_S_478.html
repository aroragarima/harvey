Nathpa Jhakri Joint Venture v State of Himachal Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 207; (2000) 3 SCC 319; AIR 2000 SC 1268; JT 2000 (3) SC 189; 2000(2) SCALE 352; 2000 (118) STC 306

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 245 and 14 - Himachal Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1968, ss. 12A - Himachal Pradesh General Sale Tax Rules, 1970, r. 31A - Deduction of tax at source provided by said provision obligating deduction, ignoring fact whether sale involved was outside domain of state Act or exempted under State Act - Held, impugned tax rule is liable to be struck down as being beyond state's legislative capacity and as conferring arbitrary powers on it.
