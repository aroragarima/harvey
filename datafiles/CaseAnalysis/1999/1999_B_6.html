State of Orissa v B. K. Routray

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1420; (1999) 2 SCC 58; AIR 1999 SC 1101; 1999 (1) ARBLR 314; 1999 (3) Bom.C.R. 70; 1999 (1) CLT 93; [1999] 95 Comp Cas 808; JT 1999 (1) SC 58; 1999 (1) RAJ 376; 1999(1) SCALE 96; 1999 (1) SCJ 529; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 71; 1999 (1) SLT 228; 1999 (1) Supreme 159

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 29 - Interest Act, 1978, ss. 3 and 6(2) - Arbitral award as to interest for pre-reference period which itself was prior to commencement of Interest Act - Proceeding covered by expression 'legal proceedings' in s. 6(2) of Act - Validity of award when interest Act as per s. 6(2) does not apply to proceedings pending at commencement of Act - Hence, interest for pre-reference period cannot be granted - Appeal allowed.
