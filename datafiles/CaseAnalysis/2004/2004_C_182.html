Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai v Messrs Mahindra and Mahindra Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 631; (2005) 10 SCC 251; 2004 (171) E.L.T. 159; 2004 (64) RLT 232

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Modvat Scheme

Summary: Excise - Suppression of duty - Inference drawn by Tribunal as to that there could not by any suppression of duty on ground that credit was available to the respondents - Finding of Tribunal based on decision of SC in 'AMCO Batteries Limited vs. Collector of Central Excise, Bangalore' - Whether finding of Tribunal proper? - If not whether decision in AMCO to be sustained? - Held, law laid down in AMCO's case not correct - As question as to whether there is suppression or not is a question to be decided on facts of each case and has nothing to do with available credit or a party having benefit of MODVAT scheme - Matter to be referred to larger Bench - Papers placed before CJI for proper orders.
