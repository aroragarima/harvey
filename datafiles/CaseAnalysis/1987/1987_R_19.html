State of Orissa v Radheshyam Gudakhu Factory (And Others Appeals)

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28117; 1988 (68) STC 92

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Additional Duties of Excise (Goods of Special Importance) Act, 1957, s. 2 (c) - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 - Oriss Sales Tax Act, 1947 - Whether, 'gudakhu' is covered by expression 'tobacco' as defined in s. 2 (c) Act, 1957 which was substituted w.e.f. 01-07-1967, by Notification No 21278-F D/- 6-06-1967 and is exempted from tax under Act, 1947? - 'Gudakhu' is a product of tobacco and that although a major part of molasses and other constituents are added to tobacco essential and effective ingredient remains tobacco, and therefore 'gudakhu' is known as a product of tobacco in common parlance - Held, 'gudakhu' is a product which falls within exemption covered by serial No. 35 of Schedule to Orissa Sales Tax Act - Appeals dismissed.
