Additional Commissioner of Income Tax and Another v A.L.N. Rao Charitable Trust

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1313; (1995) 6 SCC 625; AIR 1996 SC 344; 1995 (129) CTR 205; [1995] 216 ITR 697; JT 1995 (7) SC 339; 1995(5) SCALE 742; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 348

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Charitable or Religious Purposes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 11(2) - Charitable trust - Accumulated income - Exemption u/s. 11(2) - For benefit of said exemption whether investment of entire accumulated income is necessary - Held, not necessary - Investment of portion of accumulated income would also be sufficient - Appeal dismissed.
