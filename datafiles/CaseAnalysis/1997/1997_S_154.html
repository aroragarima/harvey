Commissioner of Income Tax, Bangalore v Shree Manjunatheaware Packing Products and Camphor Works

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1296; (1998) 1 SCC 598; AIR 1998 SC 1478; 1997 (143) CTR 406; [1998] 231 ITR 53; JT 1997 (9) SC 583; 1997(7) SCALE 390; 1998 (1) SLT 346; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 109; 1997 (10) Supreme 368; [1998] 96 TAXMAN 1; 1998 (2) TLR 198

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Land & Property

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984 - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 263 - Revisional powers of Commr. as to consideration of all records relating to proceedings available - Valuation report submitted after passing of assessment order - Notice u/s. 263 issued on ground of investment not accounted for - Consideration of report and notice under judicial consideration - Held, legal and proper - Appeal allowed.
