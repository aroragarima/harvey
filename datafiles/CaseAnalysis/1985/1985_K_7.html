Supreme Court of India

08 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 410; (1985) Supp SCC 714; AIR 1985 SC 1495; JT 1985 (4) SC 773A; 1985(1) SCALE 832; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 352

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Affirmative Action, Employment Under The State

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 15(4), 16 and 340 - Constitution (First Amendment) Act, 1951 - Reservation - Question of reservation of seats/posts u/art.15 (4) and art.16 (4) of the Constitution - Opinion of Supreme Court sought, which may serve as a guide line to the Commission for examining the question of affording better employment and educational opportunities to SC, STs and OBC - Guidelines for, indicated - Order accordingly.
