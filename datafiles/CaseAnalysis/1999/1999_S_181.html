Badni (Dead) By Lrs. and Others v Siri Chand (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 955; (1999) 2 SCC 448; 1999 (1) ACJ 776; AIR 1999 SC 1077; 1999 (2) AWC 1280; 1999 (2) CLT 46; 1999 (1) CRC 502; JT 1999 (1) SC 519; 1999 RD 205; 1999(1) SCALE 458; 1999 (2) SLT 217; 1999 (2) Supreme 104

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 22, r. 3 - Abatement of appeal - Effect - Decree of suit for possession by way of redemption - Death of one of appellants pending various appeals against decree before High Court - Decree based on common issue against appellants in all appeals - Held, failure to bring LRs. of one of deceased - appellants in one appeal would result in abatement of other appeals - Thus order of HC dismissing all appeals as abated so as to avoid conflicting decrees is proper.
