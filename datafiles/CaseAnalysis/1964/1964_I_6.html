Â Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation v Income Tax Officer, B-1 B-Ward, Hyderabad and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, J.C. Shah, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 308; AIR 1964 SC 1486; [1964] 34 Comp Cas 473; [1964] 52 ITR 524; 1964 (2) MLJ(SC) 33; 1964 (2) SCJ 167; [1964] 7 S.C.R. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Constitution of India, 1950, art.289 - Income Tax Act, 1922, s.3 - Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950, ss.28, 29, 30 and 38 - Taxation - Andhra Pradesh Road Transport Corporation established under statute - (A) Whether income of corporation is exempt from Union taxation in light of art.289 - (B) Whether ss.29 and 30 of 1950 Act are repugnant to s.3 of Income Tax Act in light of art.254 - Held, not exempt from Union taxation - Not repugnant to s.3 - Appeals dismissed.
