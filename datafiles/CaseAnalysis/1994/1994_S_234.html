Pritpal Singh v State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S. Mohan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1108; (1994) 5 SCC 695; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1239; AIR 1995 SC 414; 1994 (28) ATC 169; 1994 (2) CLR 639; 1994 (69) FLR 651; JT 1994 (5) SC 245; 1994(3) SCALE 627; 1995 (1) SLJ 23; 1994 (4) SLR 567; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 326; 1994 (2) UJ 359

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Written examination for post of Asst. Sub-Inspector of Police - Selection Board destroyed - Answer papers - Written examination - Before declaration of selection - Since shortage of space to keep them - Suspicious - Final marks of interview - Not produced - Mark-list found manipulated - Validity of appointments questioned - Held, selection of candidates as made by board - Quashed.
