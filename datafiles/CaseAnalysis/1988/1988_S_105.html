Dr. Chakradhar Paswan v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 883; (1988) 2 SCC 214; (1988) SCC (L&S) 516; AIR 1988 SC 959; 1988 (7) ATC 104; JT 1988 (1) SC 496; 1988 LabIC 619; 1988 (2) LLJ 66; 1988 (1) LLN 940; 1988 MLJ 565; 1988(1) SCALE 459; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 130; 1988 (3) SLJ 110; 1988 (2) SLR 119; 1988 (1) UJ 747

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Possibility of reservation initially or any future vacancy regarding only post in cadre - Held, can be no reservation with reference to that post either for recruitment at initial stage or for filling up future vacancy in respect of that post - Order accordingly.
