Supreme Court of India

18 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 227 - Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 21(1)(a), 21(1)(b) - Appellate court finding that shop was an old construction which needed to be demolished - Reversed by HC in proceedings under art.226, 227 - Clearly exceeded its jurisdiction in setting aside judgment of Appellate Court - Exercised its certiorari jurisdiction for correcting judgment of Appellate Court - On basis of evidence - HC order set aside - Surya Dev Rai vs Ram Chander Rai and Ors - (2003 Indlaw SC 598), referred to and followed.
