Member Board Of Revenue, West Bengal v M/S. Swaika Oil Mills

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 240; (1977) 4 SCC 286; AIR 1977 SC 2008; 1977 (6) CTR 333; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 270; 1977 (40) STC 367; 1977 UJ 507

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 5(1) - Taxability of sale - Sale whether took place in course of export - Sale effected in favour of Netherlands Selling Organisation - Held sale was not a sale in the course of export and was, therefore, exigible to tax - Appeal Allowed.
