Bhoja Alias Bhoja Ram Gupta v Rameshwar Agarwala and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 956; (1993) 2 SCC 443; AIR 1993 SC 1498; 1993 (2) BLJR 745; JT 1993 (2) SC 375; 1993 (1) RCJ 476; 1993 (1) RCR(Rent) 484; 1993 (1) RentLR 510; 1993(2) SCALE 58; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 369; 1993 (1) UJ 784

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Bihar Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1947, ss. 4 and 11 - Payment of excess rent due to illegal enhancement - Act not providing for its automatic adjustment towards default if made in payment of rent - And tenant also not claiming adjustment earlier - Effect - Held, tenant will be liable to eviction for default in payment of rent - Appeal dismissed.
