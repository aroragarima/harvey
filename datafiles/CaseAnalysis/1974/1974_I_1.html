Iftikhar Ahmed And Others v Syed Meharban Ali And Others

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A. Alagiriswami

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 212; (1974) 2 SCC 151; AIR 1974 SC 749; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 464; 1974 UJ 257

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, s. 11 - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 16 and 30 - (A) Applicability of res judicata among co-plaintiffs - (B) Effect of error in arbitral award - When it is apparent on face of it - Held, operates between co-plaintiffs as well - Apparent illegality vitiates the award - Appeal Allowed.
