Darshan Singh and Others v Ram Pal Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, T.K. Thommen, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 1016; (1992) Supp1 SCC 191; AIR 1991 SC 1654; JT 1990 (4) SC 561; 1990(2) SCALE 1114; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 212

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: Land & Property - Punjab Customs (Power to Contest) Act, 1920, ss. 4 and 7 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 133 - Interpretation of Statutes - Retroactivity of Law - Ancestral property - Alienation - Right to contest - On ground that alienation was contrary to custom - Bar to contest placed by s. 7 of Act - If applies - Held, applies to pending proceedings - Order accordingly.
