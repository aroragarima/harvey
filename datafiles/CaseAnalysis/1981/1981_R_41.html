Pandurang Jivaji v Ramchandra Gangadhar Ashtekar (Dead) By Lrs. and

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.D. Koshal, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 321; (1981) 4 SCC 569; AIR 1981 SC 2235; 1982 (2) Bom.C.R. 88; 1981(3) SCALE 1652; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 1020

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal

Keywords: Garnishee

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114 - CPC, 1908, s. 100 - (A) Party to the suit - Failure to appear in court - When adverse inference can be drawn - (B) Concurrent findings of facts - If set aside by second appellate court - Question as to would arise only when there is no other evidence on record on the point in issue - Normally not set aside by second appellate court - Appeal Allowed.
