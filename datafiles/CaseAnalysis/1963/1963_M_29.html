Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - S.33 of The Industrial Act NO. XIV of 1947 - The employee was dismissed due to the mistakes made by him in the preparation of of load sheet and balance chart which might have led to serious accident - Whether the dismissal was justified? - Held, that the dismissal was justified as there was no unfair labour practice nor victimisation - Appeal dismissed.
