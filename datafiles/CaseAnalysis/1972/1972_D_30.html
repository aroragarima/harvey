Devidas Vithaldas and Company v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City I

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, Hans Raj Khanna, S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 241; (1972) 3 SCC 457; AIR 1973 SC 318; 1972 CTR 28; [1972] 84 ITR 277; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 215

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2) - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Acquisition of goodwill of a business by assessee firm - Payments whether deductible - Held, transaction was a licence and not a sale of the goodwill, therefore disbursements were in the nature of royalty and must be treated as admissible deductions - Appeals allowed.
