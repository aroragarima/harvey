Rajesh K. Gupta v Ram Gopal Agarwala and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 333; (2005) 5 SCC 359; AIR 2005 SC 2426; 2005 (3) ALD(SC) 75; 2005 CRLJ 2581; 2005 (119) DLT 430; JT 2005 (5) SC 1; 2005 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 771; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 578; 2005(4) SCALE 448; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 946

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: [A.] Women & Children - Family & Personal - Habeas corpus petition - Custody of daughter - Ground that on account of mental ailment with which his wife was suffering, custody of child should be given to petitioner rejected by HC - Held, appellant is in profession of law being an Advocate-on-Record in SC - A lawyer's profession is very exacting and busy profession and requires lot of time - No ground to take a contrary view and disturb present custody of child and give her custody to the appellant - Appeal dismissed.
