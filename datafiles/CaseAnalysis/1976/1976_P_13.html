C.I.T., Punjab, Haryana, J & K, H.P. & Union Territory Ofcha v Panipat Woollen & General Mills Co. Ltd.Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

02 January 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 93; (1976) 2 SCC 5; AIR 1976 SC 640; 1976 CTR 317; [1976] 103 ITR 66; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 186

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 10(2)(xv) and 66 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 10 - (A) Income Tax - (1) Claim for deduction u/s.10(2)(xv) of the Act - Condition precedent - Applicability of test commercial expediency - Applicability - Contract of agency with assessee company, a sort of partnership - (2) Reference u/s. 66 - Power of High Court - (C) Interpretation of Statutes - Construction of contract - Rule of - Payments in question, held not deductible - HC is not entitled to go behind the statement of the case - Court in order to construe an agreement has to look to the substance or the essence of it rather than to its form - Appeal Allowed.
