State Of Tamil Nadu v Kodaianal Motor Union (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 131; (1986) 3 SCC 91; AIR 1986 SC 1973; 1986 (8) ECR 241; 1986(1) SCALE 922; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 927; 1986 (62) STC 272; 1986 TAXLR 2363; 1986 (2) UJ 631

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 10A - Determination of penalty in lieu of prosecution - Assessee committing offences u/s. 10(d) - Held, penalty should be 1 and 1/2 times of normal tax rates and not of concessional rates of tax - Appeals allowed.
