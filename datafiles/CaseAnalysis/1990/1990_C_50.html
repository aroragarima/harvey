Seshasayee Paper And Boards Limited, Erode v Collector Of Central Excise, Coimbatore

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 700; (1990) 2 SCC 146; AIR 1990 SC 974; 1990 (27) ECC 5; 1990 (47) E.L.T. 202; JT 1990 (1) SC 197; 1990(1) SCALE 164; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 320

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Trade Discount

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 4 and 35 - Fixation of normal price - Trade discount given to selling agent - Manufacturer claiming deduction - Ground, selling agent is real purchaser though name of third person shown as purchaser - Plea raised for first time before Supreme Court - Permissibility - Held, could not be allowed, Tribunal being final fact finding authority - Appeal dismissed.
