Asharfilal v Koili Smt. Dead By Lrs

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, A.M. Ahmadi, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1036; (1995) 4 SCC 163; AIR 1995 SC 1440; 1995 ALJ 1154; JT 1995 (5) SC 496; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 102; 1995 RD 390; 1995 (2) RentLR 144; 1995(2) SCALE 827; 1995 (2) UJ 282

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 32, r. 3 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 44 - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition And Land Reforms Act, 1951, s. 229-B - Decree against minor because of gross negligence on part of his next friend - (A) Whether minor can file suit to set aside decree ? - (B) Whether s. 44 applicable ? - Held, (A) minor can file suit to set aside decree - (B) S. 44 would be attracted only if inference of fraud or collusion could be drawn from gross negligence of next friend of minor in conducting earlier declaratory suit - Appeal allowed.
