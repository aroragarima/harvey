Airports Authority of India v Satyagopal Roy and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, S.N. Variava, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 171; (2002) 3 SCC 527; AIR 2002 SC 1423; 2002 (47) ALR 374; 2002 (5) AWC 4356; JT 2002 (3) SC 58; 2002 (3) RCR(Civil) 404; 2002(2) SCALE 663; 2002 (2) SCJ 479; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 505; 2002 (2) SLT 537; 2002 (2) Supreme 453; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1185

Case Digest

Subject: Air Law; Carriers & Transportation; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Capitalising

Summary: Damages-Compensation for cutting of trees-Market value on basis of yield from trees or plantation required to be determined by appropriate multiplier-Multiplier of 8 years already considered appropriate by Supreme Court-High Court having applied multiplier of 18 years-Applying of such multiplier not justified-However, considering amount to be small no interference is made.
