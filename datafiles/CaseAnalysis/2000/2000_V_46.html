Vijay L. Mehrotra v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2895; (2001) 9 SCC 687; (2002) SCC (L&S) 278; AIR 2000 SC 3513; 2000 (1) CLR 416; JT 2000 (5) SC 171; 2000 LabIC 2663; 2000 (2) LLJ 253; 2000 (3) LLN 1; 2000 (2) SLR 686; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1599

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Retiral benefits - Granting of interest - Payments of the retiral benefits long after she retired - Held, payment of retiral benefits should be paid on the date of retirement or soon thereafter if for some unforeseen circumstances the payments could not be made on the date of retirement - Respondent to pay to the appellant simple interest at the rate of 18 per cent with effect from the date of her retirement - Appeal allowed.
