Dr. Uma Agrawal v State Of U. P. & Another

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 276; 1999 (1) LLJ 133; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 1006

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Retiral benefit - Entitlement of - Held, case is a clear example of departmental delay which is not excusable - Petitioner retired on April 30, 1993 and it was only alter February 12, 1996 when an interim order was passed in this writ petition that respondents woke up and started work by sending a special messenger to various places where petitioner had worked - Such an exercise should have started at least in 1991, two years before retirement - Amounts due to petitioner were computed and payments were made only during 1997-98 - Exercise which was to be completed long before retirement was in fact started long after petitioner's retirement - This is a fit case for awarding interest to petitioner - Petition disposed of.
