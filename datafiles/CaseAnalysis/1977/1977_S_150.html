Smt. Basavva Kom Dyamangouda Patil v State of Mysore and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 327; (1977) 4 SCC 358; (1977) SCC (Cr) 598; AIR 1977 SC 1749; 1977 BBCJ 168; 1977 CRLJ 1141; 1977 CRLR 257; 1977 MLJ 486

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CrPC, 1898, s. 517 - (A) Power of Court regarding property lost or destroyed during pendency of trial - (B) Property custodia legis - Determination - Production before the Court does not mean physical custody or possession - Property produce and directed to be kept in custody of police - Court has power to order payment of value of property - Property is produced before the Court and becomes custodia legis - Appeal Allowed.
