P. Lal v Union of India and others

Supreme Court of India

05 February 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 465; (2003) SCC (L&S) 289; (2003) 3 SCC 393; AIR 2003 SC 1499; 2003 (2) AWC 1024; 2003 (2) ESC 138; 2003 (96) FLR 1188; JT 2003 (1) SC 649; 2003 LabIC 762; 2003 (2) LLJ 164; 2003 (1) LLN 854; 2003(1) SCALE 644; [2003] 1 S.C.R. 846; 2003 (2) SLJ 1; 2003 (2) SLR 369; 2003 (2) SLT 208; 2003 (1) Supreme 961; 2003 (1) UPLBEC 878

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship, Right To Promotion

Summary: All India Services (Death-cum-Retirement) Rules, 1958 - rr.16(2) and 16(2A) - Termination of master and servant relationship - Voluntary retirement - Under rr.16(2) and 16(2A), when a notice is given for voluntary retirement, it merely has to be accepted by the Government of India and when it is accepted, the retirement becomes effective - Where the member has abandoned service, the severance of the relationship of master and servant takes place immediately on acceptance of notice - Communication of acceptance arises in cases where the member continues to work/perform his duties after notice
