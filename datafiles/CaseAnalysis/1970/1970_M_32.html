Ch. Surat Singh and Others v Manohar Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 462; (1971) 3 SCC 889; AIR 1971 SC 240; 1970 UJ 793

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 20 - Practice and Procedure - Effect of non-joinder - Appeal to Supreme Court - Failure to implead necessary party - Effect - Held, appeal was liable to be dismissed for want of necessary party to the appeal - An application filed on the date of decision to implead him as party(respondent), held being highly belated could not be entertained - Appeal Dismissed.
