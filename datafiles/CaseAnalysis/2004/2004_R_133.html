Ramesh Singh @ Photti v State Of A.P.

Supreme Court of India

25 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 226; (2004) 11 SCC 305; AIR 2004 SC 4545; 2004 CRLJ 3354; JT 2004 (4) SC 62; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 732; 2004(4) SCALE 16; 2004 (2) UC 815

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 34 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, ss. 164, 313 - Evidence of witnesses whose statements are recorded u/s. 164 CrPC to be considered with caution - If there are other circumstances on record which might support truth of evidence of such witnesses - Can be acted upon - Mere fact that statement of witness was recorded u/s. 164 cannot be a ground to reject their evidence - Held on facts and in totallty of circumstances, lower courts and HC committed no error - Appeal dismissed.
