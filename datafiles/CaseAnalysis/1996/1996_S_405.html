Bai Shakriben (Dead) By Natwar Melsingh and Others v Special Land Acquisition Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3924; (1996) 4 SCC 533; AIR 1996 SC 3323; 1996 (3) CCC 52; JT 1996 (5) SC 597; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 13; 1996(4) SCALE 636; 1996 (2) SCJ 607; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 369

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Propcedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23 and 28 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, ss. 151, O. 47, r. 1 - Amendment of decree - Decree allowed to become final - Cannot be amended by executing Court or reference Court by exercising powers under O. 47, r. 1 and s. 151 - Ommission to award additional amounts u/s. 23 (1-A), enhanced interest u/s. 28 and solatium u/s. 23(2) of 1894 Act - (A) Whether are clerical or arithmetical mistake crept in award passed by reference court? - (B) Entertaining application for amendment of decree by reference court - If within power and jurisdiction - Held, (A) Are not clerical or arithmetical mistake but amounts to non-award - (B) Is devoid of power and jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
