State of Uttar Pradesh v Tipper Chand

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 270; (1980) 2 SCC 341; AIR 1980 SC 1522; 1980 ALJ 749; 1980 UJ 686

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act , 1940, s. 34 - Contract - Interpretation of clause therein to determine bar on suit as to Works contract with Government - Held, relevant clause did not amount to arbitration clause - Did not contain any express arbitration agreement nor could it be implied - The clause vested the Superintending Engineer only with supervision and administrative control over the work - Appeal Dismissed.
