Indian Piston Limited v Collector Of Central Excise, Madras

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 675; (1990) 1 SCC 470; AIR 1990 SC 977; 1990 (27) ECC 1; 1990 (27) ECR 292; 1990 (46) E.L.T. 3; JT 1990 (1) SC 99; 1990(1) SCALE 79; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 157; 1990 (1) UJ 363

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Normal Price

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 233B - Payment of excise duty - Department's view that distributors of assessee were related persons - Protest - Form in which protest should be made - Not prescribed by rules - Held, facts encompassed all types of sale - Appeal allowed.
