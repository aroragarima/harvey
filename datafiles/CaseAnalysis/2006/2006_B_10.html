Union of India and Others v B. Valluvan and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 716; (2006) 8 SCC 693; AIR 2007 SC 210; 2007 (1) AWC 829; JT 2006 (9) SC 385; 2006(10) SCALE 607; 2007 (1) SLR 5; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 755

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 114 - Challenge to select list - Held, HC, despite noticing argument advanced on behalf of Union of India that 1 st Respondent had no legal right to be appointed, proceeded to opine that panel prepared for filling up of future vacancies should be given effect to - Review of HC was not only contrary to circular letter issued by Union of India, but also contrary to general principles of law - Appeal allowed.
