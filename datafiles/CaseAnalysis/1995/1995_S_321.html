Habibulla Khan v State Of Orissa And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1517; (1995) SCC (Cr) 382; (1995) 2 SCC 437; AIR 1995 SC 1124; 1995 (1) CCR 117; 1995 (1) Crimes 567; 1995 CRLJ 2071; JT 1995 (2) SC 1; 1995 (2) LLN 245; 1995(1) SCALE 419; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 819; 1995 (1) UJ 536

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 13 and 19 - Accused persons - Prosecuted for criminal misconduct allegedly committed during their tenure as ministers - Not in their capacity as MLAs - Held, prosecution done after they ceased to be ministers, thus sanction is not necessary.
