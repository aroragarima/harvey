Messrs Vikram Cement v Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore

Supreme Court of India

18 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, B.N. Srikrishna, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 76; (2006) 2 SCC 351; 2006 (194) E.L.T. 3; JT 2006 (1) SC 385; 2006 (72) RLT 609; 2006(1) SCALE 327; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 465

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Modvat Scheme, CENVAT Rules, 2000

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - CENVAT Rules, 2000 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 57A, 57J - Whether decision in Jaypee Rewa Cement Vs CCE (2001 Indlaw SC 20009) where it was held that MODVAT was allowable on use of explosives in manufacture of cement irrespective of fact that explosives were used directly in mines and not in factory would apply to CENVAT Rules 2000 framed under Central Excise Tariff Act? - Held, CENVAT Rules in effect substitute MODVAT Rules - Decision in Jaypee Rewa Cement would continue to apply - Decision in Commissioner of Central Excise, Jaipur Vs J K Udaipur Udyog Limited (2004 Indlaw SC 741) holding to contrary is not good law - Reference answered accordingly.
