Zila Singh and Others v Hazari and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 264; (1979) 3 SCC 265; AIR 1979 SC 1066; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 222

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss.146, O.20,r.14, O.21,r.16 - Nature of right of preemption - Decree in favour of pre- Sale by pre-emptor - Whether purchaser can apply for execution of decree? - Held, application for execution would certainly be maintainable u/s. 146 - Appeals allowed.
