(1) Messrs Popcorn Entertainment and Another v City Industrial Development Corporation and

Supreme Court of India

23 February 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 650; (2007) 9 SCC 593; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 152; 2007 (2) Bom.C.R. 910; JT 2007 (4) SC 70; 2007 (4) MLJ(SC) 890; 2007(3) SCALE 465; 2007 (6) SCJ 947; [2007] 3 S.C.R. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: New Bombay Land Disposal Rules, 1975

Summary: Cancellation of plot - Writ petition against - High Court held that appellants have an equally efficacious remedy of filing a civil suit and thus the writ jurisdiction cannot be invoked - Appeal against - Held, High Court has committed a grave mistake by relegating the appellant to the alternative remedy when clearly in terms of the law laid down by this Court, this was a fit case in which the High Court should have exercised its jurisdiction in order to consider and grant relief to the respective parties - Allotment made in favour of the appellants cannot be faulted with and this Court will accordingly set aside the orders of CIDCO seeking to resile from a concluded contract in favour of the appellants - Appeal disposed of.
