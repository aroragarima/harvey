Union of India and another v Swiss Garnier Life Sciences and others

Supreme Court of India

04 July 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhansu Jyoti Mukhopadhaya, G.S. Singhvi

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 405; (2013) 8 SCC 615; AIR 2013 SC 2885; 2014(1) ALL MR 933; JT 2013 (10) SC 20; 2013 (4) RCR(Criminal) 82; 2013(8) SCALE 329

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Health & Drug

Keywords: Bulk Drug, Formulations, Drugs (Prices Control) Order, 1995

Summary: Health & Drug - Constitution - Drugs (Prices Control) Order, 1995 - Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 - (A) Whether Doxofylline was a bulk drug within the meaning of para 2(a) of Order - Held, if any pharmaceutical, chemical, biological or plant product conforms to pharmacopeial or other standards accepted under Act and thus comes within the meaning of bulk drug, as defined in para 2(a) of Order, all salts, esters, stereo-isomers and derivatives of such bulk drug were, ipso facto, deemed to be conforming to the pharmacopoeial or other standards accepted under the Act and were deemed to be bulk drug within the meaning of para 2(a) of Order - As Doxofylline was a derivative of Theophylline and admittedly, Theophylline was a bulk drug shown in First Schedule (Item No.34) of Order and was conforming to pharmacopoeial and other standards specified in the Act , Doxofylline was deemed to be a bulk drug within the meaning of para 2(a) of Order conforming to pharmacopoeial and other standards specified in the Second Schedule to the Act - (B) Whether Doxofylline was a schedule bulk drug within the meaning of para 2(u) of Order - Held, scheduled bulk drug means a bulk drug specified in the First Schedule of Para 2(u) of Order - Theophylline had been shown as one of the scheduled drug at Serial No.34 of the First Schedule of Para 2(u) of Order - As Doxofylline was a derivative of Theophylline, Doxofylline comes within the meaning of bulk drug as defined in para 2(a) of Order and also within the meaning of scheduled bulk drug as defined in para 2(u) of Order - (C) Whether Doxofylline was a scheduled formulation within the meaning of para 2(v) of Order - Held, scheduled formulation was defined in para 2(v) of Order, means a formulation containing any bulk drug specified in the First Schedule of para 2(v) of Order, either individually or in combination with other drugs, including one or more than one drug or drugs not specified in the First Schedule of para 2(v) of Order - As Doxofylline being the derivative of Theophylline, a bulk drug, and Doxofylline in any formulation having held to be a bulk drug within the meaning of para 2(a) of order, Doxofylline also comes within the definition of scheduled formulation under para 2(v) of Order - (D) Whether the appellant had power to fix the ceiling price or revise the price of Doxofylline under paras 9 and 10 of Order - Held, though the appellant called for details from manufacturers of Doxofylline formulations by letters dt. 22-7-2008, 16-9-2008, they failed to furnish information as required under Order, within the time specified therein - In view of such refusal to furnish the detailed information, it was well within the jurisdiction of the Government to fix price under para 11 of Order on the basis of information as available with it, by order fixing a price in respect of Doxofylline or its formulation - Therefore, interference with Notifications both dt. 30-4-2009 and 17-11-2009 was uncalled for - Appeals allowed.
