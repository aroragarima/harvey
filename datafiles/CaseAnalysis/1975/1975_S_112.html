Â Mohd. Serajuddin and Others v State Of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Hans Raj Khanna, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 170; (1975) 2 SCC 47; (1975) SCC (Tax) 269; AIR 1975 SC 1564; 1975 (4) CTR 139; 1975 (36) STC 136; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 169; 1975 TAXLR 1800

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Second Sale, Coffee Act, 1942, Sale In The Course Of Export, Transfer Of Documents Of Title, Tea Act, 1953

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 5(1) - Constitution, art. 286(1)(b) - Constitution (Sixth Amendment) Act, 1956 - What constitute sale in the course of export - Assessee entered into contracts with STC for sale of mineral ore - STC contracted with foreign buyer for sale of identical goods purchased by it from assessee - Exigiblity as to Sales Tax - Sales by assessee to corporation, held were not in the course of export - And exigible to tax under CST Act - Appeal Allowed.
