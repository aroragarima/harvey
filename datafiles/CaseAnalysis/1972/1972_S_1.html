S. Abdul Khader Saheb v Mysore Revenue Appellate Tribunal, Bangalore and

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.K. Mukherjea

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 195; (1973) 1 SCC 357; AIR 1973 SC 534; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 925; 1973 UJ 456

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Nationalisation

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 68D - Motor Vehicles - Prevailace , Scheme of Nationalisation and Inter state agreement - Nationalisation of Intra-State routes, portion of it overlaps the Inter-State route - Held, scheme approved u/s. 68-D would prevail over an Inter-State agreement in respect of an Inter-State route - Govt. can nationalise Intra-State route even though a portion of it overlaps the Inter-State route - Appeal Dismissed
