Indian Overseas Bank v I. O. B. Staff Canteen Workers' Union and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 511; (2000) 4 SCC 245; (2000) SCC (L&S) 471; AIR 2000 SC 1508; 2000 (2) AWC 1571; 2000 (2) CLR 268; JT 2000 (4) SC 503; 2000 LabIC 1495; 2000 (1) LLJ 1618; 2000 (2) LLN 930; 2000(3) SCALE 255; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1212; 2000 (3) SLJ 258; 2000 (2) SLR 408; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 1920

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(5) - Workmen - Canteen employees - Working hours and days strictly same as Bank's working - Since decades - Infrastructure given by Bank - Day to day administration in hand of promoters - Held, canteen service contribute to good efficiency of Bank's employee and is necessary - Administration in different hand does not make a difference - Are employees of Bank.
