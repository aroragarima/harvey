Union of India and Others v Muneesh Suneja

Supreme Court of India

30 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20320; (2001) 3 SCC 92; (2001) SCC (Cr) 433; AIR 2001 SC 854; AIR 2001 SCW 463; 2001 (1) AWC 831; 2001 (1) CCR 178; 2001 (1) Crimes 255; 2001 CRLJ 1069; 2001 (1) JCC 150; JT 2001 (2) SC 416; 2001 (1) RCR(Criminal) 689; 2001(1) SCALE 510; 2001 (1) SCJ 518; [2001] 1 S.C.R. 683; 2001 (1) SLT 746; 2001 (1) Supreme 674

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1973, Foreign Exchange Maintenance Act

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1999 - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1973, s. 3 - FERA, 1973 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 21, 22 and 226 - Respondent found in possession of currency notes worth 3 lakhs and gold biscuits - High Court quashed detention order on ground that detention order was made after a long delay and even after that no steps were taken to execute detention order - Challenged - If there are good reasons for delay in passing order or in not giving effect to it, same could be explained and those are not such grounds which could be made basis for quashing order of detention at a predetention stage - Held, HC wrong in quashing detention order at pre-'Govt of India v Alka Subash Gadia' 2000 Indlaw SC 2842 followed - Appeal allowed.
