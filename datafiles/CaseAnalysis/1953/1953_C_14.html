Supreme Court of India

08 October 1953

Case Analysis

Bench

Ghulam Hasan, M. Patanjali Sastri, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Vivian Bose, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 23; AIR 1953 SC 501; 1954 (56) BomLR 742; [1953] 23 Comp Cas 462; 1981 (25) CTR 186; [1953] 24 ITR 499; 1953 SCJ 651; [1954] 1 S.C.R. 203

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Business Profits Tax Act, 1947, Sch. 2, rr. 2 (1) and 2 (1) - Companies Act, 1913, ss. 131 (a) and 132, Chap. 1, Table A, regn. 99 - Business Profits Tax Act - Computing abatement u/s. 2(l) - Interpretation - 'Reserve - Undistributed profits on crucial date' - Held, profits lying unutilized and not specially set apart for any purpose cannot be treated as reserve - Appeal No. 158 dismissed and Appeal No. 157 allowed.
