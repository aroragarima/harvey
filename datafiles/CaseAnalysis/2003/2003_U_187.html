Macson Marbles Private Limited v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

11 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1201; (2008) 15 SCC 481; AIR 2004 SC 4927; 2003 (158) E.L.T. 424; 2003 (59) RLT 502

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Enactment

Summary: [A]State Act vis-a-vis Central Act - State Financial Corporation Act, 1951 though a special enactment not to prevail upon the Central Excise Act because Excise Act is also a special enactment [B] Recovery of excise dues - Liability to pay excise dues - Transfer of industrial unit - Recovery of dues of previous owner from the auction purchaser - Rule 230(2) of erstwhile Central Excise Rules, 1944 indicates that it is a mode of recovery of excise dues from the assets owned by a predecessor and on his liabilities being assessed could be recovered even from the successor - r.230(2) held applicable.
