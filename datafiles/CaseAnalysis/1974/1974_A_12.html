State Of Mysore v Allum Karibasappa And Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 104; (1974) 2 SCC 498; AIR 1974 SC 1863; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 601; 1974 UJ 51

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Mysore Cooperative Societies Act 1959

Summary: Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 - Scope of s. 54 - Validity of notifications u/s. 54 superseding management of Co-operative Bank - Including President and Vice-President - Considering the meaning of 'Control' - Held, action of Govt. was ultra vires the Act and in violation of the principles of natural justice and the notifications must be set aside - Appeal Dismissed.
