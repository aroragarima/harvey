Â Hari Singh and Others v Military Estate Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua, D.G. Palekar, Hans Raj Khanna, M. Hameedullah Beg, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 372; (1972) 2 SCC 239; AIR 1972 SC 2205; 1972 RCJ 955; 1972 RCR(Rent) 508; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 515

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Constitution (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1955, Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, City of Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, Assam Acquisition of Land For Flood Control and Prevention of Erosion Act, 1955, Gujarat Imposition Of Taxes By Municipalities (Validation) Act, 1963, Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorized Occupation) Act, 1958, Bangalore Acquisition of Lands (Validation) Act, 1962, Constitution (Fifth Amendment) Act,1955

Summary: Constitution, arts.14 and 245 - Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971, ss. 2(c), 15 and 20 - Punjab Tenancy Act, 1887, s. 43 - (A) Act retrospectively removing discrimination resulting from two procedures provided under the 1958 Act - (B) Word 'premises' u/s. 2(c) - Applicability - Scope of - (C) Constitutional validity of s. 15 - Held, it is within legislative competence to enact such a validating Act - Would apply to agricultural land also - Section is not violative of art.14 of because of the procedure of application to Revenue Officer for ejectment being available u/s. 43 of Punjab Tenancy Act, 1887 - Appeals dismissed.
