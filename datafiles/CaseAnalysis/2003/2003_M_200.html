National Aluminium Company Limited v Messrs Pressteel & Fabrications Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1162; (2004) 1 SCC 540; 2004 (2) ALD(SC) 17; 2004 (1) ARBLR 67; 2004 (2) CompLJ 28; 2004 (98) CutLT 167; JT 2004 (5) SC 582; 2004 (1) MLJ(SC) 167; 2004 (2) MPLJ 400; 2004 (1) RAJ 1; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 511; 2003(10) SCALE 1062; 2004 (1) SLT 336; 2003 (8) Supreme 876

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Corporate; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - ss. 15, 17, 29 - Arbitration & Conciliation Act, 1996 - Appointment of arbitrator - Unilateral appointment questioned - Whether the proceedings in which an impugned award has come to be made, are governed by the 1940 Act or the 1996 Act; (2) whether the appropriate court for the purpose of challenging the said award or seeking modification of the said award is this Court, being the court which appointed the arbitrator - Held, in view of the provisions of s. 34 r/w s. 2(e) of the 1996 Act that it is not Court which has the jurisdiction to entertain an application for modification of the award and it could only be the principle civil court of original jurisdiction as contemplated under s. 2(e) of the Act.
