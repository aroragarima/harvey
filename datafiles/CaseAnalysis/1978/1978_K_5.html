K. N. Bahl v State Of Punjab & Ors

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, N.L. Untwalia, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 64; (1978) 4 SCC 336; (1979) SCC (L&S) 1; AIR 1979 SC 220; 1978 LabIC 1765; 1979 (1) LLN 14; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1089; 1979 SLJ 105; 1979 (1) SLR 288

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 166, 226 and 311 - (A) When Order of Chief Minister confirming a public servant becomes operative - Reversion from temporary post on abolition thereof Challenged - (B) Burden of proof as to alleged mala fides - Held, employee cannot challenge his reversion to his substantive post on basis of such order - A person seeking to invalidate an administrative order on charge of mala fides has to establish charge of bad faith by furnishing necessary particulars - Appeal dismissed.
