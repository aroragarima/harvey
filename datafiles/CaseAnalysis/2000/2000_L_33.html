Kamla Devi v Laxmi Devi

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 527; (2000) 5 SCC 646; AIR 2000 SC 1640; 2000 (40) ALR 213; JT 2000 (6) SC 498; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 126; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 516; 2000(4) SCALE 768; 2000 (4) SLT 488; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 330; 2000 (4) Supreme 627

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 2(i)and 50 - Tenancy under separate agreement - Landlord filed suit for demolition of construction made by tenant on impugned land adjacent to buildings ended in compromise - And open plot was let out to him, which is not 'premises' u/s. 2(1)(i) - Suit for eviction form plot - Legality - Held, not barred by s. 50 of Act and said suit plot cannot be treated as part of building as it was let out separately - Appeal allowed.
