Wazir Chand v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2980; (2001) 6 SCC 596; (2001) SCC (L&S) 1038; 2000 (3) CLR 849; JT 2000 (Supp1) SC 515

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Appellant, a retired railway employee continued to occupy the Government quarter even after superannuation - Penal rent - Adjustment against gratuity - Withholding of gratuity amount - Held, appellant having unauthorisedly occupied the Government quarter, was liable to pay the penal rent in accordance with Rules, and therefore, there is no illegality in those dues being adjusted against the death-cum-retirement dues of the appellant - Appeals dismissed.
