Sardarkhan Rajadarkhan v The Charity Commissioner And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 850; (1996) 7 SCC 5; AIR 1996 SC 575; 1996 (2) Bom.C.R. 663; 1996 (1) CLT 65; JT 1995 (9) SC 201; 1995(6) SCALE 551

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 - Mutawalli and Mujawars not discharging duties property and not prepared to cooperate and carry out duties - Directions of High Court modifying scheme - Held, HC not justified in taking away right of Mutawalli to receive remuneration hence, directions set aside - And Original scheme restored - Appeal allowed.
