Sheoratan Agarwal and Another v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 350; (1984) 4 SCC 352; (1984) SCC (Cr) 620; AIR 1984 SC 1824; 1985 (1) ALT(SC) 115; 1984 (3) CompLJ 245; 1984 (2) Crimes 566; 1984 CRLR 388; 1984 CrLR(SC) 388; 1984(2) SCALE 362; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 719

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Consumer Protection - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, ss. 3, 10 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Offences by companies - Appeal under art.136 of Constitution - Permissibility of separate prosecution of person-in-charge or Officer of Company without prosecuting company - Held, is permissible - There is nothing in s.10 which bars such prosecutions - Appeals dismissed.
