Anand Regional Company Op Oil S. Union Limited v Shaileshkumar Harshadbhai Shah

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 390; (2006) 6 SCC 548; 2006 (3) AWC 2959; 2007 (2) G.L.R. 1333; JT 2006 (7) SC 362; 2006(7) SCALE 603; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 370

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Air Force Act, 1950, Navy Act, 1957

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(s) - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Misconduct - Punishment of dismissal - Held, Presiding Officer, Labour Court in his award did not assign any reason as to how findings of Enquiry Officer were perverse - There may or may not be any specific allegation - Single Judge of HC also without any material on record opined that Respondent workman was not involved in incident - Similarly placed persons were allowed to take benefit of voluntary retirement scheme - First Respondent shall also be given benefit of voluntary retirement scheme - Appeal partly allowed.
