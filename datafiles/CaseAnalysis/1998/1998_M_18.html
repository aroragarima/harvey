Mahabir Prasad Singh v Jacks Aviation Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1297; (1999) 1 SCC 37; AIR 1999 SC 287; 1999 (1) ALT(SC) 27; 1999 (1) CLT 1; JT 1998 (7) SC 579; 1999 (1) KarLJ 530; 1998(6) SCALE 127; 1998 (9) SLT 252; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 1; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 675; 1998 (8) Supreme 378

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Advocates Act, 1961, s. 35 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 24 and 115(1) - Transfer of case - Ground, inability to attend court - In pursuance of Bar Association resolution to boycott particular court - But retaining brief - No objection by other party - Held, a change of court is not allowable merely because other side too has no objection for such change - Conduct deprecated, as unprofessional and unbecoming of status of advocate - No ground to allow transfer of case.
