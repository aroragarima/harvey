Kuljit Singh v Lt. Governor of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A.P. Sen, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 205; (1982) SCC (Cr) 234; (1982) 1 SCC 417; AIR 1982 SC 774; 1982 CRLJ 624; 1982 CrLR(SC) 59; 1982(1) SCALE 1; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 58

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 72 and 161 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 433 - Constituional power to commute punishment - President of India - Guidelines to follow discretion - Held, no guidelines - Left open - Petition dismissed.
