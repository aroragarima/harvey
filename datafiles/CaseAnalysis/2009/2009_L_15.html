Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 446; (2009) 4 SCC 437; 2009 (3) ADJ 322; 2010 ALL MR (Cri) 2030; 2009 (3) JCC 2180; JT 2009 (7) SC 327; 2009 (4) MLJ(Crl) 990; 2009 (3) RCR(Criminal) 401; 2009(4) SCALE 77; [2009] 4 S.C.R. 1027

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 467, 468, 471, 420, 409, 218 - Quashing of F.I.R. - Held, since, charge sheet has been filed and cognizance has been taken, and on the facts of this case, this is not a fit case for quashing the first information report - Arrest is not a must whenever an F.I.R. of a cognizable offence is lodged - In appropriate cases interim bail should be granted pending disposal of the final bail application, since arrest and detention of a person can cause irreparable loss to a person's reputation - Appeal dismissed.
