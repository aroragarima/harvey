Om Prakash Sud Etc. Etc. v State Of J & K & Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Baharul Islam, R.S. Pathak, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 208; (1981) 2 SCC 270; AIR 1981 SC 1001; 1981(1) SCALE 314; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 841

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Allotment of resin and raw material

Summary: Administrative - Practice & Procedure - Allotment of resin and raw material - Denial - Legality - Petitioners industries were small scale industries for manufacture of resin and turpentine oil - Petitioners applied to Govt. for allotment of resin as well as raw material for their industries but Govt. refused to make any allotment - Petitioners approached respondent authorities for allotment of raw-materials but to no avail - Petitioners alleged that while they were refused allotment of supply of raw-materials, respondent authorities made allotments to respondents Nos. 4 to 16/allottee respondents although most of them were not even formally registered at time of making impugned orders of allotment - Aggrieved, petitioners preferred instant petition - Whether respondent authorities were justified in allotting the resin as well as raw material to allottee respondents - Held, respondent authorities stated that they had considered all 110 applications including those of petitioners coming from industrialists of different parts of the country - Respondent authorities had not explained as to how and on what basis, if any, allotments were made by impugned orders in favour of respondent allottees - Respondent authorities had taken reliance on State Industrial Policy decision, they did not appeared to have followed it in practice, except in cases of respondents - In fact no reasonable basis had adopted in making allotments in favour of respondent allottees and denying allotments to petitioners - Impugned orders of allotments in favour of respondent allottees were quashed - Respondent authorities were not justified in allotting the resin as well as raw material to allottee respondents - Petitions partly allowed.
