Miss Maneck Gustedji Burjarji v Sarafazali Nawabali Mirza

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 533; (1977) 1 SCC 227; AIR 1976 SC 2446

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, art. 227 - CPC, 1908 - Altermnate remendy - Others adequate and comprehensive remedy by way of appeal to High Court itself - Not availed of - Extraordinatry circumstances - Interference u/art. 227 in favour of petitioner - Validity - Interference held, was erroneous and quashed - Application Dismissed.
