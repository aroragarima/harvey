State of M.P. and another v Devkinandan Maheshwari

Supreme Court of India

03 February 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 143; (2003) 3 SCC 183; AIR 2003 SC 1136; 2003 (2) JhrCR(SC) 48; JT 2003 (1) SC 604; 2003(1) SCALE 588; 2003 (2) SLR 392; 2003 (1) SLT 804; 2003 (1) Supreme 875

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Swatantra Sangram Sainik Samman Nidhi Niyam, 1972

Summary: M.P. Swatantra Sangram Sainik Samman Nidhi Niyam, 1972 - Issue- Whether the respondents are entitled to claim pension (Samman Nidhi) under the M.P.S.S.S.S.N.N. Rules, 1972 from the date of application or from the date of sanction of the pension? Held-Mukundlal Bhandari case not applicable. Freedom Fighter is entitled to pension from the date of order and not from the date of application.
