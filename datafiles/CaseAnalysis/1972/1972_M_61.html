Commissioner of Income Tax, U.P v Messrs Mohd. Shakoor Mohd. Bashir

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 233; (1973) 4 SCC 107; (1973) SCC (Tax) 393; AIR 1973 SC 2359; 1973 (2) CTR 286; [1973] 89 ITR 57; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 87; 1973 TAXLR 1306

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Practice & Procedure - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 34 - Whether Appellate Asst. Commr. in dealing with appeals of asssociation of persons could give a direction u/s 34 (3) to take action against assessee? - Appellate Asst. Commr. did not give any finding as contemplated in proviso to s. 34 (3) - Held, direction given by Appellate Asst. Commr. is not one that falls within scope of second proviso to s. 34 (3) - Appeal dismissed.
