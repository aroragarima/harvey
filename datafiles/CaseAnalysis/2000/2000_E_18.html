Christian Medical College v Employee's State Insurance Corporation

Supreme Court of India

23 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2768; (2001) SCC (L&S) 220; (2001) 1 SCC 256; AIR 2001 SC 373; 2001 (88) FLR 267; JT 2000 (Supp3) SC 162; 2001 LabIC 93; 2001 (1) LLJ 18; 2001 (1) LLN 762; 2001 (1) MLJ(SC) 112; 2000(7) SCALE 575; 2001 (1) SCJ 53; 2000 (8) SLT 341; [2000] Supp5 S.C.R. 24; 2000 (7) Supreme 639; 2001 (1) UC 157; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 181

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Printing, Visva-Bharati Act, 1951, Employee's Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act 1952

Summary: Labour & Industrial - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, s. 2(12) and 75 - Employee's Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 - Factories Act, 1948, s. 2 - Maintenance works under Factories Act - Whether ESI Act are applicable to the Equipment Maintenance Department of the appellant? - U/s. 2(k) of the Factories Act, amongst other things, if any repairing takes place with a view to use the equipment then it amounts to manufacturing process - Equipment Maintenance Department maintains and repairs their equipment for the efficient use of the equipment in the Hospital - Held, the provisions of the ESI Act are applicable to the Equipment Maintenance Department of the appellant - Appeal dismissed.
