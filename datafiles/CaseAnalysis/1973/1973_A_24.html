Â Gwalior Rayon Silk Manufacturing (Weaving) v Assistant Commissioner of Sales Tax and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A. Alagiriswami, Hans Raj Khanna, P.N. Bhagwati, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 245; (1974) 4 SCC 98; (1974) SCC (Tax) 226; AIR 1974 SC 1660; [1974] 94 ITR 204; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 879; 1974 (33) STC 219; 1974 TAXLR 2017

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Keywords: Legislative Powers, Delegated Legislation

Summary: Constitution, art. 245 - Central Sales Tax ST Act, 1956 - Subordinate legislation - Delegation of Legislative powers to subordinate authority - Validity of s. 8(2)(b) of CST Act - Held, provision not suffer from abdication or excessive delegation of legislative power - Appeals Dismissed.
