M. K. Shah Engineers and Contractors and another v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1135; (1999) 2 SCC 594; AIR 1999 SC 950; 1999 (1) ARBLR 646; 1999 (2) CLT 10; JT 1999 (1) SC 315; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 19; 1999 (1) RAJ 437; 1999(1) SCALE 272; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 419; 1999 (1) SLT 483; 1999 (1) Supreme 344

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 20 - Reference to arbitration - Pre-requisite obligation - To exclude applicability of arbitration clause - Non-performance of - Held, cannot be permitted to plead invalidity or non-maintainability of arbitration - As party at fault.
