State Of Himachal Pradesh, Through Thesecretary Agriculture v Nodha Ram And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1147; (1998) SCC (L&S) 478; AIR 1997 SC 1445; 1996 (72) FLR 668; JT 1996 (1) SC 220; 1996 (1) LLJ 1248; 1996(1) SCALE 253; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 54; 1996 (1) SLR 648B

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial - Constitution, art.16 - Status of Temporary employees after closure of project for which they were employed - Held, temporary employees have to go along with closure of project and no vested right created - Directions by High Court to regularise would amount to creating of posts and continuing them despite non availability of work and therefore such directions are not proper - Appeal allowed.
