B.P.L. Limited v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

09 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Mrs. Justice Ruma Pal, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 456; (2001) 2 SCC 139; AIR 2001 SC 665; 2001 (1) ALD(SC) 145; 2001 (127) E.L.T. 655; JT 2001 (1) SC 599; 2001(1) SCALE 225; 2001 (1) SCJ 688; [2001] 1 S.C.R. 195; 2001 (1) SLT 636; 2001 (121) STC 450; 2001 (1) Supreme 196

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Rate

Summary: Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957 - Whether fully automatic washing machine can be regarded as 'electronic goods' so as to attract a lower rate of sales tax? - Even though washing machine is not specifically mentioned as one of items, it would fall under category of 'consumer electronic product' - Like a record player or radio, it works with aid of micro processor and falls within definition of 'electronic goods' as contained in said Notification D/-20-07-1988 - An electronic washing machine would come under entry 38(v) and Notification D/-20-07-1988 would apply to all electronic goods including automatic washing machines of appellants - Appeal allowed.
