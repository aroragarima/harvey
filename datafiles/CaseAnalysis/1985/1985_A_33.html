Workmen Employed in Associated Rubber Industry Limited, Bhavnagar v Associated Rubber Industry Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 328; (1985) 4 SCC 114; (1985) SCC (L&S) 957; AIR 1986 SC 1; 1985 (2) CCC 847; 1985 (48) CTR 355; 1985 (51) FLR 478; [1986] 157 ITR 77; 1986 LabIC 37; 1985 (2) LLN 848; 1985(2) SCALE 321; 1986 (1) SLJ 218; 1986 (1) UJ 235

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Payment Of Bonus Act, 1965, s. 4 - Calculation of gross profits - Duty of Court in case where transfer of asset to reduce profit - Held, in every case where ingenuity is expended to avoid taxing and welfare legislation, Court has to get behind smoke-screen and discover true state of affairs - Court is not to be satisfied with form and leave well alone substance of a transaction - Appeal allowed.
