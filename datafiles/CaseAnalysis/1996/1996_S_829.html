Narayan Dattatraya Ramteerthakhar v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1913; (1997) SCC (L&S) 152; (1997) 1 SCC 299; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 305; AIR 1997 SC 2148; 1997 (76) FLR 976; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 262; 1998 (3) LLJ 168; 1997 (2) LLN 1004; 1996(9) SCALE 1; 1997 (2) SLJ 91; 1996 (3) SLR 778; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 939; 1997 (6) Supreme 337

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Misappropriation of public money by delinquent officer - Removal from service - Appeal - Held, full - fledged enquiry was conducted after issuance of charge-sheet, therefore, plea that preliminary enquiry was not properly conducted is untenable - Punishment imposed is appropriate - Appeal dismissed.
