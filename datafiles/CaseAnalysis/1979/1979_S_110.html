Tamil Nadu Education Department Ministerial & Generalsubordi v State Of Tamil Nadu & Anr

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 81; (1980) 3 SCC 97; (1980) SCC (L&S) 294; AIR 1980 SC 379; 1980 LabIC 211; 1980 (1) LLN 324; 1980 (2) MLJ(SC) 25; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 1026; 1980 (1) SLR 159; 1980 UJ 282

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 16 and 309 - Tamil Nadu G. O. No. 1968 - Integration of District Board staff with Govt. servants - Promotion and seniority - Discrimination, if any - Held, neither ratio between District Board staff and Govt. servants in the matter of promotion nor provision for consideration of service of District Board staff under District Board while drawing up common seniority lists is discriminatory - Petition dismissed.
