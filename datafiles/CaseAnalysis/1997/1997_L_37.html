Lokshikshan Prasarak Mandal and Another v Rajendrakumar Ajabrao Mahalle and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2009; (1997) 10 SCC 62; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1273; AIR 1997 SC 2779; 1997 (76) FLR 895; JT 1997 (5) SC 598; 1997 LabIC 2865; 1997 (3) LLN 87; 1998 (1) SLJ 150; 1997 (4) SLR 596; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 89; 1997 (6) Supreme 5

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 226 - Stay of proceedings - Teacher appointed for particular session but not in following year due to letter of Deputy Director of Education - Tribunal held in appeal that headmaster had no authority to pass order of termination - Writ petition file for order of suspension - Interim order by HC vacating stay on order of Tribunal - If proper - Held, not proper - Order of Tribunal liable to be stayed pending disposal of writ petition - Appeal allowed.
