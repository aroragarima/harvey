Des Raj (Deceased) Through L.Rs. and Others v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 796; (2004) 7 SCC 753; AIR 2004 SC 5003; 2004 (6) ALD(SC) 120; 2005 (58) ALR 468; 2004 (4) AWC 3197; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 111; JT 2004 (8) SC 202; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 450; 2004(8) SCALE 433; 2005 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 461; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 934

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Land and Property - Practice and Procedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 28-A - Land acquired - Compensation - Enhanced compensation - Whether appellants entitled to enhanced compensation?; Whether benefit of s 28-A available to appellants? - Held, benefit of s 28-A is available only to parties who had not sought reference under s 18 of Act for enhancement of compensation - This provision is not available to persons who seek for reference under s 18 of Act for enhancement of compensation and do not challenge judgment of reference court or judgment of HC thereafter - Appeals Dismissed.
