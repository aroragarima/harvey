Anil Kumar Mitra and Others v Ganendra Nath Mitra and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1988; (1997) 9 SCC 725; AIR 1997 SC 3767; JT 1996 (11) SC 20; 1996(9) SCALE 255; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 338; 1997 (2) Supreme 27

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Hindu Law - Suit for partition of joint family property when there was termination of joint family status and partition in earlier suit - But parties continuing to be members of joint family by their acts even after partition - However no proof of reunion of their respective shares and treating it as joint family property - Maintainability of subsequent claim for partition - Held, not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
