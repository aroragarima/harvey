K. Venkatachalam v A. Swamickan and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1113; (1999) 4 SCC 526; AIR 1999 SC 1723; JT 1999 (3) SC 242; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 857

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Sovereignty And Integrity Of India

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 329(b), 226, 191 and 193 - Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 5 - Electoral matters - Bar to courts jurisdiction - Person disqualified and re-elected - Sitting and voting in House - High Court u/art. 226 declaring of disentitlement to sit in state legislative assembly - Validity - Held, bar to jurisdiction does not apply under situation - Proper.
