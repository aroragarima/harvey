P. Mahendran and Others v State Of Karnataka

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 161; (1990) SCC (L&S) 163; (1990) 1 SCC 411; AIR 1990 SC 405; 1990 (12) ATC 727; 1990 (60) FLR 103; JT 1989 (4) SC 459; 1990 LabIC 369; 1990 (1) LLJ 337; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 385; 1990 (1) SLJ 48; 1990 (1) SLR 307; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 385

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921, Karnataka General Service (Motor Vehicles Branch) (Recruitment) Rules 1976 (as amended in 1987)

Summary: Service - Constitution - Karnataka General Service (Motor Vehicles Branch) (Recruitment) Rules, 1962, r. 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Appointment of Motor Vehicles Inspectors - Commencement of process of selection - Effect of prospective amendment of Rules - Held, election process is to be completed in accordance with law as it stood at its commencement - Amended Rule would not invalidate selection already made - Appeal allowed.
