C. K. Subramonia Iyer & Ors. v T. Kunhikuttan Nair And 6 Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 200; (1969) 3 SCC 64; 1970 ACJ 110; AIR 1970 SC 376; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 688; 1970 UJ 57

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Labour & Industrial Law; Tort

Keywords: Wrongful

Summary: Fatal Accidents Act, 1855, - CPC, 1908, ss. 100 and 101 - Constitution, art. 133(1) - (A) Damages u/ss. 1A and 2 of 1855 Act - Assessment of - (B) Ascertainment of damages under Fatal Accidents Act - Second appeal - Principles stated - Appellate Court should be slow in disturbing findings reached by lower Courts, if they have taken all relevant facts into consideration - Appeal Dismissed.
