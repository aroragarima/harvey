Paluru Ramkrishnaiah And Ors. Etc. v Union Of India And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, L.M. Sharma, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 530; (1989) SCC (L&S) 375; (1989) 2 SCC 541; AIR 1990 SC 166; 1989 (10) ATC 378; 1989 (1) CLR 712; 1989 (58) FLR 713; JT 1989 (1) SC 595; 1989 (2) LLJ 47; 1989(1) SCALE 830; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 92; 1990 (2) SLJ 136; 1989 (2) SLR 202; 1989 (2) UJ 618

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Right To Promotion, Eligibility - Seniority

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16, 73 and 309 - Ordinance Factories (Recruitment and Conditions of Service of Class III Personnel) Rules, rr. 8 and 12 - Service matter - (A) Whether executive instruction can override provision of rule framed u/art. 309 ? - Held, executive instruction could make a provision only with regard to service matter which was not covered by Rules and such executive instruction could not override any provision of Rule framed u/art. 309 - (B) Applicability of precedents - Supreme Court granted promotion to supervisors on completion of two years' service - Decision become final - HC relying on Supreme Court decision granted similar promotion and HC judgment stood approved by Supreme Court - HC did not give benefit of back wages and granting limited relief - Whether supervisors who governed by Supreme Court decision entitled to same limited relief ? - Further held, entitled to same limited relief - Petition dismissed.
