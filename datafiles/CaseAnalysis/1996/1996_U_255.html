Shaheen Welfare Association v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2638; (1996) SCC (Cr) 366; (1996) 2 SCC 616; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 290; AIR 1996 SC 2957; 1996 (1) CCR 207; 1996 (1) Crimes 166; 1996 CRLJ 1866; 1996 JCC 283; JT 1996 (2) SC 719; 1996(2) SCALE 481; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1123; 1996 (2) Supreme 469

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Summary: Terrorist And Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987, ss. 20, 5 and 28 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Delay in trial was in question - Inadequate number of designated Courts causing under - trials languishing in jails - Supreme Court divided under-trials into different categories - Held, guidelines issued as one time measure for their release on bail.
