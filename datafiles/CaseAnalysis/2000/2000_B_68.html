Bijoyo Kumar Pattanaik v Basanta Kumar Patnaik and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3643; (2000) 9 SCC 335; AIR 2000 SC 3587; JT 2000 (9) SC 35

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family & Personal - Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure , 1908, O. 1, r. 10, O. 7, r. 10 - Partition suit - Appellant prosecuted application u/O. 1, r. 10, CPC, but Munsif came to conclusion that as he had no pecuniary jurisdiction to entertain this suit as held by appellate Court he could not grant permission to plaintiff to transpose remaining plaintiffs as defendants - Revision before High Court rejected - Correctness of HC order challenged - Held, to serve ends of justice u/art. 142 of Constitution sustaining order of Trial Court as confirmed by HC, appellant is permitted to transpose original plaintiffs Nos. 2 to 5 as defendants Nos. 4 to 7 and plaint in O.S. No. 67 of 1975, stood amended - Appeal allowed.
