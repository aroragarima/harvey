Tvl, Ramco Cement Distribution Co. Pvt.Limited,Tamil Nadu Etc. v State Of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 685; (1993) 1 SCC 192; AIR 1993 SC 123; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 729; 1994 (38) KarLJ 79; 1992(2) SCALE 850; 1993 (88) STC 151; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 78

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Sale Price, Railway Freight, Packing Charges, Packing Materials, Additional Sales Tax, Tamil Nadu Additional Sales, Packing Charges And Excise Duty

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 2(h), 2(j) - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(p) - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Rules, 1959, r. 6(C) - (A) Deduction from Sales Tax - Taxable turnover of Sales of Cement Selling Agent - Freight independently charged in invoice, paid on behalf of purchaser on behalf of assessee and credit given therefor and packing charges and excise duty thereon - Whether should be included in arriving at taxable turnover under both Acts? - (B) Deduction from taxable turnover - Words 'without including them in price of goods sold' - Whether freight charges are to be deducted from'taxable turnover'? - Held, (A) Should be included - (B) Are not to be deducted from 'taxable turnover' - Appeal dismissed.
