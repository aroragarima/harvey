Ashok Kumar Bhattacharya v Ajoy Biswas and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 359; (1985) 1 SCC 151; AIR 1985 SC 211; 1985 (1) CCC 339; 1984(2) SCALE 760; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 50; 1984 (3) SLR 216; 1985 UJ 7

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 102(1)(a) - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 3(31) - Bengal Municipal Act, 1932, ss. 66 and 63 - (A) Whether Agartala Municipality is local authority - (B) Disqualification for membership of Parliament - Holder of office of profit under Govt. - Whether Accountant-in-charge of Agartala Municipality is disqualified - Not disqualified from membership - He does not hold office of profit under Govt. of Tripura - Appeal dismissed.
