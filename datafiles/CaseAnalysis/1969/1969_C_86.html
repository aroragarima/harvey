Sudhir Kumar Saha v Commissioner of Police, Calcutta and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 235; (1970) SCC (Cr) 71; (1970) 1 SCC 149; AIR 1970 SC 814; 1970 CRLJ 843; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 360

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Preventive Detention Act, 1950 - Detention u/s. 3(1)(a)(ii) - Legality - Grounds - Incidents mentioned in grounds of detention actually not amounting to anything more than commission of certain breaches of law - Whether prejudicial to maintenance of 'public order' or subversive of 'public order' - Detemination of - Held, they can be said prejudicial to 'law and order' and not prejudicial to maintenance of 'public order' or subversive of 'public order' - Detention held, is illegal - Petition Allowed.
