Fenner (India) Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Madurai

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 608; (1995) Supp2 SCC 567; 1995 (58) ECR 202; 1995 (77) E.L.T. 8; JT 1995 (3) SC 388; 1995(2) SCALE 508; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Whether product manufactured by appellant was classifiable under Tariff Entry 3922.90 or Tariff Entries 3920.11/3920.12 as claimed by Excise Authorities? - An article which is over 100 metres but only upto 1200 millimeters in width cannot be described as a 'strip' - Tariff Entries 39.20.11 and 39.20.12 cannot, therefore, be made applicable to the belting made by the appellants - For later period, Tariff Heading 39.26 must be read as applicable to it - Held, judgment and order under appeal is set aside - Respondents directed to classify the appellants' conveyor belts under Tariff Heading 39.22.90 for the period December 1986 to February 1987 and under Tariff Heading 39.26.90 for period February 1987 to June 1987 - Appeals allowed.
