Achal Misra v Rama Shanker Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Balasubramanyan, R.C. Lahoti, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 267; (2005) 5 SCC 531; 2005 (59) ALR 591; 2005 (1) ARC 877; 2005 (2) AWC 1585; JT 2005 (4) SC 236; 2005 (2) LCD 1141; 2005 (141) PLR 356; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 598; 2005 (1) RCR(Rent) 558; 2005(4) SCALE 32; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 439

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s.97 , O. 43 r. (1A) - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss.11 , 12 , 16 , 18, 16(9) , 105(1) , 105(2) , 12(3-A) - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Rules, 1972,r. 10 , r. 8(2) - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, s.109 , - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 ,art. 227 - Vacancy not challenged - Whether owner of building can challenge order declaring vacancy in subsequent revision against order of allotment? - Held, since order notifying a vacancy is only a step in passing final order in a proceeding regarding allotment, it is clear that same could be challenged while challenging final order, unless there is anything in Act precluding such a challenge or conferring a finality to order notifying a vacancy - Appeal allowed.
