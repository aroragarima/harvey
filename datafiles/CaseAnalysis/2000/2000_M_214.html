Union of India and Others v Messrs Ganesh Das Bhojraj

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 672; (2000) 9 SCC 461; AIR 2000 SC 1102; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 294; 2000 (1) CLC 815; 2000 (159) CTR 178; 2000 (89) ECR 228; 2000 (116) E.L.T. 431; [2000] 244 ITR 691; JT 2000 (2) SC 314; 2000 (2) MLJ(SC) 193; 2000(2) SCALE 17; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1081; 2000 (119) STC 293

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Keywords: Jaipur Laws Act, 1923

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1947 - Jaipur Laws Act, 1923 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Notification No. 129/76 - Cus D/- 02-08-1976 - Whether duty can be levied on basis of a Notification which was not made available to public? - There is a conflict in ratio of decisions of this Court in M/s Pankaj Jain Agencies v Union of India, Collector of Central Excise v New Tobacco Co and I T C Limited v Collector of Central Excise Bombay - Held, it is appropriate that this appeal is to be heard by a larger Bench - Order accordingly.
