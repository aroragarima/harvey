Ashwani Kumar & Anr. v Regional Transport Authority Bikaner And Another

Supreme Court of India

28 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 750; (1999) 8 SCC 364; AIR 1999 SC 3888; 1999 (6) ALD(SC) 56; JT 1999 (7) SC 609; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 125; 2000 (124) PLR 287; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 211

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Social Welfare Legislation

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 88(5) - Existence of inter-state route is condition precedent for grant of permit - Opening of new inter-state route by one State alone and grant of permits, when it is to be opened by two States - Legality - Held, grant of permit on opening of route is illegal - Appeal dismissed.
