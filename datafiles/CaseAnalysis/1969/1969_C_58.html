Commissioner of Income Tax, Banglore v D. C. Shah

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 297; (1969) 1 SCC 550; AIR 1969 SC 927; [1969] 73 ITR 692; [1969] 3 S.C.R. 586

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Family & Personal - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Whether remuneration earned by karta of a Hindu Undivided Family as a partner of firm is assessable as income of HUF - Karta was partner of firm on basis of his personal qualifications - No real or sufficient connection between investment of joint family funds and remuneration paid - Held, under circumstances of case income of Karta not assessable as income of HUF - Appeal dismissed.
