Bibi Salma Khatoon v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20012; (2001) 7 SCC 197; AIR 2001 SC 3596; 2001 (3) JhrCR(SC) 304; JT 2001 (7) SC 109; 2001 (4) RCR(Civil) 299; 2001(5) SCALE 474; 2001 (6) SLT 127; [2001] Supp2 S.C.R. 22; 2001 (6) Supreme 405

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1961, Bihar and Orissa General Clauses Act, 1917, Board Of Revenue Act, 1913

Summary: Land & Property - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1908, s. 16, O. 21, r. 34 - Board of Revenue Act, 1913 - Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1961, s. 16(3) - Bihar and Orissa General Clauses Act, 1917, ss. 4(34) and 11 - Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 4 to 14 - Land in dispute in this appeal was sold by respondent 6 in favour of respondent 5 and sale deed was registered on 30-04-1988 - Appellant filed an application u/s. 16(3) of Act for transfer of land in dispute in his favour to enforce right of preemption before deputy Collector on 30-04-1988, which was allowed by Asst. Collector on 16-08-1988 - Appellant approached board of revenue in revision but it was dismissed on 31-1-1994 - High Court dismissed writ petition - Whether application u/s. 16(3) of Act was filed within period of limitation and that courts below as well as HC erred in coming to conclusion that application was barred by limitation? - Held, it is concerned with compliance with requirement of making application within prescribed period of three months - Date from which limitation commences has to be excluded in computing period of limitation of three months - Application filed by appellant on 30-04-1988 is within limitation, a period of three months of date of registered sale deed D/- 30-04-1988 - Appeal allowed.
