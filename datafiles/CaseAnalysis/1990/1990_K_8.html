Director of Technical Education and Another v K. Sitadevi Smt

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 367; (1991) Supp2 SCC 387; (1992) SCC (L&S) 78; AIR 1991 SC 308; 1992 (19) ATC 287; 1991 LabIC 212; 1991 (1) LLN 578

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Administrative - Service - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 19 - Dispute as to correct date of birth of Govt. servant - Finding of Tribunal on basis of certificate and other evidence - Whether date of birth as finding of fact can be interfered with in appeal? - Held, finding being one of fact cannot be interfered with in appeal - Appeal dismissed.
