Satendra Prasad Jain and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Venkatachala, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 630; (1993) 4 SCC 369; AIR 1993 SC 2517; AIR 1993 SCW 3184; 1993 (22) ALR 500; 1993 (3) CCC 249; JT 1993 (5) SC 385; 1993 (2) RentLR 433; 1993(3) SCALE 787; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 336; 1993 (2) UJ 748

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1984, ss. 5, 11 A and 17 - Award - Acquisition of land by invoking urgency clause u/s. 17 - Land vested in Govt. - (A) Whether provisions of s. 11 A, regarding passing of award within two years is applicable? - Possession was taken illegally i.e., without making payment of estimated compensation - (B) Whether Govt. is liable to make award? - Held, (A) s. 11 A, not applicable? - (B) Fact of illegal possession would not absolve Govt. from making award - Appeal allowed.
