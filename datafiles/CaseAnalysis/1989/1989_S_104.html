V. Markendeya And Ors v State Of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 826; (1989) 3 SCC 191; (1989) SCC (L&S) 454; AIR 1989 SC 1308; 1989 (11) ATC 3; 1989 (58) FLR 871; JT 1989 (2) SC 108; 1989 LabIC 1556; 1989 (2) LLJ 169; 1989 (1) LLN 815; 1989(1) SCALE 860; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 422; 1989 (3) SLJ 34; 1989 (3) SLR 37

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 39(d), 14 and 16 - Pay scale - Parity - Claim as to - Not integration of graduate supervisors and non graduate supervisors in Andhra Pradesh Engineering Subordinate Service into one class of service - (A) Whether different pay scales violative of arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution? - (B) Applicability of 'equal pay, equal work' - Enforced only when discrimination is practised amongst equals - Failure to demonstrate discrimination - Held, not violative - Principle of equal pay for equal work cannot be enforced by court in abstract - Petition dismissed.
