Messrs O.K. Play (India) Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi-iii, Gurgaon

Supreme Court of India

03 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 82; (2005) 2 SCC 460; AIR 2005 SC 1023; 2005 (180) E.L.T. 300; JT 2005 (1) SC 648; 2005 (66) RLT 657; 2005(1) SCALE 732; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 1069

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, ss. 11A(1), 35L(b) - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985, s. 20 - Activity Desks and Chairs, Fun Fliers, Play Table, Play Pool, Rockers, Slides and Swings - Whether assessee was right in classifying articles as toys under Heading 95.03 and clearing same without payment of duty? - Held, play pool cannot be said to be a toy under Heading 95.03 - Swings, Slides, Fun Fillers and Rockers are not equipments for general physical exercise - They are toys under Heading 95.03 - Play table, activity desks and chairs constitute 'furniture' in terms of Heading 94.01/94.03 - No infirmity in impugned judgment of Tribunal - Appeals dismissed.
