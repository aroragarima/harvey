Kishori Lal and Another v Madan Gopal (Dead) By L.Rs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 754; (2005) 9 SCC 243; JT 2004 (8) SC 422; 2004(8) SCALE 104

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Jammu and Kashmir Common Lands (Regulation) Act, 1956, Common Land (Regulation) Act

Summary: Civil Procedure - Injunction suit - Injunction granted - Second appeal - HC did not frame substantial question of law arising in second appeal - HC did consider matter elaborately - HC did not consider whether suit property, is joint undivided property of plaintiff and defendants 1 to 4, as held by first appellate Court or it is exclusive property of appellants - Remitted second appeal to HC for fresh consideration after framing appropriate substantial questions of law - Appeals allowed.
