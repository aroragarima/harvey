Chairman, Indore Vikas Pradhikaran' v Messrs Pure Industrial Cock and Chem. Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 554; (2007) 8 SCC 705; AIR 2007 SC 2458; JT 2007 (7) SC 352; 2007(8) SCALE 110; 2007 (6) SCJ 552; [2007] 6 S.C.R. 799

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Gram Panchayat Act, Himachal Pradesh Town and Country Planning Act, 1977, Madhya Pradesh Nagar Tatha Gram Nivesh Adhiniyam, Madhya Pradesh Bhumi Vikas Niyam, 1984

Summary: Interpretation of the provisions of Madhya Pradesh Nagar Tatha Gram Nivesh Adhiniyam - Appellant published a draft development plan - High Court held that unless a development plan for an area is published and comes into operation, a draft development scheme cannot be published by the Town and Country Development Authority under s. 50(2) - Appeal against - Whether having regard to notification dated 13.02.1974 vis-'-vis the expansion of the Indore Development Plan, the District Committee in exercise of its delegated power can automatically extend the area of operation of the appellant despite the notification constituting it by the State whereby and whereunder its area of operation was limited to the one covered by the notification dated 13.02.1974?; Whether the appellant authority can declare its intention in terms of s. 50 before the development attained finality? - Held, villages in question had been included by the State in its notification issued on 28.10.2005 - Prior thereto, the said villages having not been included within the area of operation of the appellant authority, any action taken either by way of its intention to frame a town planning scheme or otherwise shall be wholly illegal and without jurisdiction - Appeals dismissed.
