Messrs Jayant Vitamins Limited v Chaitanyakumar and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 189; (1992) 4 SCC 15; (1992) SCC (Cr) 793; AIR 1992 SC 1930; AIR 1992 SCW 2187; 1992 (2) AnWR 41; 1993 (3) Crimes 990; 1992 CRLJ 3450; 1992 CRLR 611; JT 1992 (4) SC 487; 1992(2) SCALE 166; 1992 (2) UJ 595

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 173 and 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 420, 415, 408 and 34 - Investigation closed as matter found to be internal dispute of Company - Investigation restarted on directions of superintendent - High Court quashed further investigation - Appeal - Investigation into an offence is a statutory function of police and superintendence thereof is vested in state Govt. and court is not justified without any compelling and justifiable reason to interfere with investigation - Held, further investigation legally permissible - HC wrongly quashed investigation - Appeal allowed.
