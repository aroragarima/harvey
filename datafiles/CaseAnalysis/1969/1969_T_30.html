Ranganatha Reddiar v The State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 104; (1969) 2 SCC 457; AIR 1970 SC 520; 1970 CRLJ 599; 1969 KLT 737; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 864

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Adulteration, Food Adulteration

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 14 and 19(2) - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules, 1955, r. 12A, proviso - CPC, 1908, Pre. - Object underlying Act achieved by giving reasonable interpretation - Court must do so - Whether use of popular language in warranty under proviso to r. 12A defy the objects of the Act - Held, object of Act is not defeated - Appeal Allowed.
