Bijlee Cotton Mills Private Limited and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 561; (1986) 4 SCC 184; AIR 1987 SC 244; [1987] 62 Comp Cas 129; JT 1986 SC 362; 1986(2) SCALE 393; 1986 (2) UJ 594

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Keywords: Sick Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1972

Summary: Corporate - Sick Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1972, s. 2(d)(ii) - Principal submission of petitioners was that undertaking was not a 'Sick Textile Undertaking' within meaning of above act as it had not remained closed for a period of not less than three months immediately before appointed day - Material showing that owner made some effort to restart mill but mill had not resumed working till appointed day and it had been closed for more than three months before date - Held, undertaking as sick - Petition dismissed.

Legislation Cited

Sick Textile Undertakings(Taking Over Of Management) Act, 1972

Sick Textile Undertakings(Taking Over Of Management) Act, 1972 19(2)
