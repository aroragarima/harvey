Suman Gupta And Others Etc v State Of J & K And Others

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 139; (1983) 4 SCC 339; AIR 1983 SC 1235; 1983(2) SCALE 305; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 985; 1983 UJ 897

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution, arts.14, 226, 32, 133 and 136 - Education - Admission to Medical Colleges - Nomination by State Government to seats reserved in other states - (A) Jurisdiction - (B) Validity - Held, absolute power does not vest in Government in choosing candidates for nomination - Interference by Supreme Court - Appeals Partly Allowed.
