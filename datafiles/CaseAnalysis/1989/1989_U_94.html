Smt. Sushma Gosain and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 207; (1989) 4 SCC 468; (1989) SCC (L&S) 662; AIR 1989 SC 1976; AIR 1994 SCW 2305; 1989 (15) ALR 855; 1989 (11) ATC 878; 1989 (59) FLR 626; JT 1989 (3) SC 570; 1989 LabIC 2014; 1990 (1) LLJ 169; 1992 (2) LLN 847; 1989(2) SCALE 473; 1989 (4) SLR 327; 1990 (1) UJ 24

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 and 309 - Compassionate appointment - Application by widow for appointment - Trade test passed - Delay in appointment - Effect - Rejection of application in view of ban subsequently imposed on appointment of ladies to post - Constitutionality - Held, denial of appointment is patently arbitrary - Appeal allowed.
