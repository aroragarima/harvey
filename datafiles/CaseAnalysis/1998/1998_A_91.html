Ashok Chaturvedi and Others v Shitul H. Chanchani and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1134; (1998) 7 SCC 698; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1704; AIR 1998 SC 2796; [1998] 94 Comp Cas 401; 1998 CRLJ 4091; JT 1998 (5) SC 452

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 190 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 463 - Forgery - Quashment of order taking cognizance - Alleged forged signature on share transfer document - Non production of materials showing involvement of accused on alleged forgery - Not even supported by witness - Held, allegations baseless - Cognizance improper - Quashed.
