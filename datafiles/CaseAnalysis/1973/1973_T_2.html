Municipal Corporation of Greater Bombay v B.E.S.T. Workers' Union

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, A. Alagiriswami, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 339; (1973) SCC (L&S) 177; (1973) 3 SCC 546; AIR 1973 SC 883; 1973 (26) FLR 123; 1973 LabIC 466; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 285

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Bombay Municipal Act, 1888, Bombay Industrial Relation Act, 1946

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1947, s. 78(1)(D)(i) - Employee's Union sought adjournment of enquiries - Employee sick - Delaying enquiry - Prescription of 6 months within which to punish - If mandatory - Held, six months time to dismiss is not mandatory - Appeals allowed.
