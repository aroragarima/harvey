Commissioner Of Income Tax, Delhi Central v Modi Spinning And Weaving Mills Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 920; (1992) Supp1 SCC 32; AIR 1991 SC 2033; 1990 (90) CTR 58; [1991] 187 ITR 51; JT 1991 (1) SC 29; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 461; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 461; [1990] 53 TAXMAN 584; 1991 (2) TLR 753

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2) - Development rebate - Obligation to create statutory reserve - Total income computed before allowing development allowance, a loss - Held, no legal obligation to create reserve - Further, no deliberate contravention of provisions - ITO may condone genuine deficiencies - However, removal of deficiencies by creating additional reserve in current year's books in which assessment is framed essential - Answered in favour of assessee.
