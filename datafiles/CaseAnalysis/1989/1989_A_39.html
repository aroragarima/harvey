Capt. B. V. D'SOUZA v Antonio Fausto Fernandes

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 383; (1989) 3 SCC 574; AIR 1989 SC 1816; 1989 CRLJ 1816; JT 1989 (3) SC 265; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 186; 1990 (2) RentLR 381; 1989(2) SCALE 197; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 626; 1989 (2) UJ 605

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Practice & Procedure - Goa Daman and Diu Buildings (Lease, rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1968 - Issue - Whether the document in question is a lease or a licence? - Held, the terms in the document are not consistent with the respondent's case of licence and indicate that an interest in the property was created in favour of the appellant in pursuance of which he was put in possession with a right of renewal - Appeal allowed.
