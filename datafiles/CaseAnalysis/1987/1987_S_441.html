Gaya Prasad v Surendra Bahadur Singh, (Dead) By L.Rs. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28657; (1987) 2 SCC 383; AIR 1987 SC 925; JT 1987 (1) SC 652; 1987(1) SCALE 514; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 542; 1987 (2) UJ 89

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Special Relief Act, 1963, Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, Uttar Pradesh Town Areas Act, 1914, Uttar Pradesh Encumbered Estates Act, 1934

Summary: Land & Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 10 - Uttar Pradesh Encumbered Estates Act, 1934, ss. 7, 14, 18 and 24 - Suit for specific performance of contract for sale of house - When suit filed, house exempted from sale u/s. 24 of 1934 Act, and free from all encumbrances - Decree of specific performance, if can be passed - Held, in circumstances, decree could be passed - Appeal allowed.
