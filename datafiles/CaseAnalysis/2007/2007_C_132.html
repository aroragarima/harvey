Commissioner Of Income Tax v Shirke Construction Equipment Limited

Supreme Court of India

17 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 847; (2007) 14 SCC 787; AIR 2007 SC 2089; [2007] 291 ITR 380; JT 2007 (8) SC 356; 2007(8) SCALE 105; 2007 (5) SCJ 267; [2007] 7 S.C.R. 19; [2007] 161 TAXMAN 212

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax - Sections 72, 80AB, 80HHC, Income Tax Act, 1961 - (1) Whether s. 80AB can be applied to s. 80HHC; (2) Whether, in determination of business profit under s. 80HHC, the unabsorbed business losses of the earlier years under s. 72 should be set off - Held, s. 80HHC is not independent of s. 80AB and would be governed by s. 80AB; and (2) losses were to be set off against the profits earned from export of self-manufactured goods - Impugned judgment set aside.
