Suresh Mahato v District Magistrate, Burdwan and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 305; (1975) SCC (Cr) 120; (1975) 3 SCC 554; AIR 1975 SC 728; 1975 CRLJ 607; 1975 CRLR 122; 1975 UJ 116

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 3(1) - Detention - (A) Delay in arrest pursuant to detention order - Whether amounts to negation of subjective satisfaction - (B) Detention order - Validity of - Held, a delay of about one month cannot be regarded as so unreasonable as to support such an inference negativing the genuineness of the satisfaction of the D.M. - Held on facts that the order of detention in the circumstances cannot be said to be vitiated - Rule Discharged.
