Ganesh Trading Company, Karnal v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 444; (1974) SCC (Tax) 100; (1974) 3 SCC 620; AIR 1974 SC 1362; 1974 (3) CTR 6; 1973 (32) STC 623; 1974 TAXLR 2142

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 5(2)(a)(vi), sch. B and C, item (2) - (A) Sales Tax - Deduction - Paddy and rice - Not identical goodsfor tax purpose - Dealer purchasing paddy and after dehusking selling rice - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Taxing Statutes - Interpretation of enteries in Sales Tax Act - Held, no deduction for turnover after sale of rice - What is relevant is not the dictionery meaning but how those entries are understood in common parlance, specially in commercial circles - Appeal Dismissed.
