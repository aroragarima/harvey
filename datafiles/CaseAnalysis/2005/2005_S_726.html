State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Vijay Shanker Tripathi

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 442; (2005) 6 SCC 135; (2005) SCC (L&S) 817; 2005 (6) AWC 5367; 2005 (106) FLR 919; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 112; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 271; JT 2005 (6) SC 465; 2005(5) SCALE 655; 2005 (5) SLR 382; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 749; 2005 (3) UPLBEC 2991

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311(2) - Order of termination set aside by HC - Appealed herein - Held, HC did not consider question of stigma or effect of any enquiry held before order of termination was passed - Question whether enquiry purportedly held was motive or foundation was required to be considered by HC in detail - Remitted matter to HC for fresh consideration - Appeal disposed of.
