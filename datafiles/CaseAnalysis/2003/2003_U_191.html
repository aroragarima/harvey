Sri Sathya Sai Inst., High. Medi. Sciences v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

12 September 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Arun Kumar, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1214; 2003 (158) E.L.T. 675

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Import Duty, Exemption From Payment

Summary: Customs - Indirect Tax - Customs Act, 1962, s. 25 - Import duty - Exemption from payment - Petitioner contended that fulfillment of such conditions would entail huge expenditure - Held, when it was prerogative of Govt. to grant exemption, it was for them to impose appropriate conditions for same - If that was so, Court need not have interposed by reason of order - Govt. may issue appropriate conditions for fulfillment of exemption - Petition disposed of.
