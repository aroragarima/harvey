State Of Maharashtra v Ramdas Shrinivas Nayak And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 36; (1982) 2 SCC 463; (1982) SCC (Cr) 478; AIR 1982 SC 1249; 1983 (1) Crimes 335; 1982 CRLJ 1581; 1982 CrLR(SC) 328; 1982(1) SCALE 554; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 8

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution, arts. 136 and 163 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114, Illus. (e) - CrPC, 1973, s. 401 - Complaint against Cheif Minister - Charges of corrupt practice - Dismissed for want of Government sanction - High Court noticing in revision that an application was made to Governor for sanctioning prosecution - On basis of concession of Government counsel HC while dismissing revision directing that Governor should use his own discretion in granting sanction - But in SLP counsel denying to have made any concession - Whether SC can inquire into submission made before HC - Held, cannot inquire into submission - Application Dismissed.
