State of Tamil Nadu v Thiru K. S. Murugesan and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 175; (1995) 3 SCC 273; (1995) SCC (L&S) 668; 1995 (29) ATC 555; 1995 (71) FLR 319; JT 1995 (4) SC 307; 1995 LabIC 1753; 1996 (3) LLJ 333; 1995(2) SCALE 310; 1995 (63) SCJ 229; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 386; 1995 (3) SLJ 237; 1995 (2) SLR 392

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether non consideration of respondent's promotion is in accordance with law? - For promotion to a selection post, least that is expected of an employee is to have an unblemished record - When promotion is under consideration, previous record forms basis - Held, promotion denied on account of bad record is not illegal and unjustified - Appeals allowed.
