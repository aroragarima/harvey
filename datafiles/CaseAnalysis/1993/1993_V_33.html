Vinodanand Yadav and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 717; (1994) Supp2 SCC 44; (1994) SCC (L&S) 789; 1994 (27) ATC 319

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Appellants directly recruited as Asst. Engineers (Mechanical) in Public Health Engineering Department - Respondents on basis of 8.33 per cent quota fixed for them for promotion to next higher post of Asst. Engineers were promoted on different dates, but were given retrospective promotion - On basis of retrospective promotion respondents shown as seniors to appellants in gradation list - Whether respondents entitled to seniority on basis of promotion? - No person could be promoted with retrospective effect from a date when he was not borne on cadre so as to adversely affect others - Held, promotees not borne on cadre at time when direct recruits came to be appointed cannot be given seniority in service over direct recruits - State of Bihar v. Akhouri Sachindra Nath 1991 Indlaw SC 264 followed - Appeal allowed.
