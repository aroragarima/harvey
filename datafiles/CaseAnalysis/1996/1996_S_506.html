State Of Himachal Pradesh, Through The Secretary (Ruraldevel v Ashwani Kumar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1145; (1996) SCC (L&S) 383; (1996) 1 SCC 773; AIR 1997 SC 352; 1996 (32) ATC 820; 1996 (72) FLR 540; JT 1996 (1) SC 214; 1996 (1) LLJ 869; 1996(1) SCALE 237A; 1996 (1) SCJ 412; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 56; 1996 (1) SLR 647; 1996 (1) Supreme 358

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution, arts. 16 and 311 - Temporary employee employed in Govt. project - Whether termination on closure of project is proper - No vested right is created in temporary employees- Whether directions by High Court to regularise them or to continue them in other places proper - Held, directions by HC not proper - Appeal allowed.
