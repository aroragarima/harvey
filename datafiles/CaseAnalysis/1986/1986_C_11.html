C.I.T. (Central), Madras v Canara Workshops (P) Ltd., Kodialball, Mangalore

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 347; (1986) 3 SCC 538; AIR 1986 SC 1727; 1986 (58) CTR 108; [1986] 161 ITR 320; JT 1986 SC 302; 1986(2) SCALE 29; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 166; 1986 TAXLR 1133; [1986] 27 TAXMAN 262; 1986 (2) TLR 1133; 1986 (2) UJ 667

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80E - Whether, in computing profits for purpose of deduction u/s. 80E, loss incurred by assessee in manufacture of alloy steels could not be set off against profits of manufacture of automobile ancillaries - Held, assessee cannot set off loss - Appeals dismissed.
