Madanlal Fakirchand Dudhediya v Shree Changdeo Sugar Mills Limited

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 154; AIR 1962 SC 1543; 1963 (65) BomLR 162; [1962] 32 Comp Cas 604; 1962 (5) FLR 35; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 973; [1962] Supp3 S.C.R. 973

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 76(1) and 76(2) and Proviso - Limitation Act, 1908 - Interpretation of Statutes - Interpretation of sub-sections to section i.e., s. 76 - Agreement as to payment of commission prior to Act - Agreement if inconsistent with s. 76 does not prevail - Payment out of profits - Whether payment is within purview of s. 76 - Held, sub section must be construed as parts of an integral whole - Provisos are often inserted to allay fears or to remove apprehensions - Payment, not outside provisions of s. 76 - Appeal dismissed.
