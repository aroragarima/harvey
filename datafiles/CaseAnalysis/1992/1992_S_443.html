Mrs. U. Vijayalakshmi v State of Tamil Nadu and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 64; (1995) SCC (Cr) 176; AIR 1994 SC 165; 1993 CRLJ 3913

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot-leggers, Drug-Offenders, Forest-Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, and Slum Grabbers Act, 1982, ss. 3(1) and 2(9), Expln - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 22(5),32 and 226 - Preventive detention - Alleged ground recognised by sub-section (1) of section 3 - Held, Court will not probe into truth of alleged facts.
