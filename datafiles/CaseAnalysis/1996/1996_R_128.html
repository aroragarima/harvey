State of Rajasthan v Ram Narain and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3132; (1996) 8 SCC 64; (1996) SCC (Cr) 529; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 1037; AIR 1996 SC 2987; 1996 (1) CCR 184; 1996 CRLJ 1882; JT 1996 (2) SC 396; 1996(2) SCALE 34; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 874; 1996 (5) Supreme 231

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 376 and 366 - Abduction and rape of minor girl - Evidence of victim factual - Prosecution case corroborated by medical and circumstantial evidence - Guilt of accused established by prosecution beyond doubt - High Court while confirming conviction, reduced sentence to period already undergone - Held, judgement of HC is erroneous - Accused was sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment of 5 years.
