Commissioner of Income Tax v Anand Theatres Etc

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 624; (2000) 5 SCC 393; AIR 2000 SC 2356; 2000 (160) CTR 492; [2000] 244 ITR 192; JT 2000 (6) SC 407; 2000(4) SCALE 741; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 338; [2000] 110 TAXMAN 338

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Depreciation Allowance, Depreciation And Development Rebate, Ejusdem Generis, Industrial Undertaking, Investment Allowance, Manufacture Or Production, Stock-In-Trade, Club, Ordered accordingly, Plant And Machinery, Rebate, Actual Cost, Profits And Gains Of Business, Income Tax Act 1961, Know-How, Technical Know-How, Depreciation On Building, Factory Building, Higher Rate, Rate Of Depreciation, Cinema Theatre, Component Parts, Grant Of Depreciation, New Machinery, Office Appliance, Finance Act, 1971, Cinema Business, Extra Shift Allowance, Guest House, Additional Depreciation, Renovation, Appeal By Special Leave, Luxury, Depreciation Applicable

Summary: Income - tax Act, s. 32 - 'Plant' - Depreciation - Building used as hotel or a cinema theatre - Held, s. 32 envisages clearly separate depreciation for a building, machinery and plant, furniture and fittings etc - 'Plant' has inclusive meaning under s. 43(3) which not includes buildings - No depreciation.
