Â Killick Nixon Limited v Killick and Allied Companies Employees Union

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Hans Raj Khanna, P.N. Bhagwati, M. Hameedullah Beg, A. Alagiriswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 615; (1975) 2 SCC 260; (1975) SCC (L&S) 316; AIR 1975 SC 1778; 1975 (30) FLR 401; 1975 LabIC 1315; 1975 (2) LLJ 53; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 453

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Fair Wage

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.3, item 2 - Service - Salary - Dearness Allowance - imposition of ceiling on DA - Matters to be considered by Tribunal - Held, it cannot be laid down as an invariable rule that in all cases there should be ceiling on DA but it laid down certain aspects from which the problem will have to be viewed - Appeal Partly Allowed.
