The Commercial Tax Officer & Ors. v M/S. Biswanath Jhunjhunwalla & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1054; (1996) 5 SCC 626; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 616; AIR 1997 SC 357; JT 1996 (7) SC 600; 1996(6) SCALE 211; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 286; 1996 (6) Supreme 436

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Sales Tax - Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941, s. 26 - Bengal Sales Tax Rules, 1941, r. 80(5)(ii) - Effect of extension of time limit to exercise powers of suo motu revision by 1974 amendment retrospectively from 01-11-71 - Whether assessments falling within increased period whether incomplete or final would be revisable - Held, assessments falling within increased period would be revisable - Appeal allowed.
