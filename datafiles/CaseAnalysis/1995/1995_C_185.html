Supreme Court of India

21 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 395; (1995) SCC (Cr) 590; (1995) 3 SCC 732; AIR 1995 SC 1592; 1995 (4) Bom.C.R. 351; 1996 (1) CCR 162; [1995] 84 Comp Cas 28; JT 1995 (3) SC 528; 1996 (3) LLJ 35; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 873

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 630 - Wrongful withholding of premises by heirs of deceased Officer/ employee allotee - Maintainablity of petition u/s. 630 covering Legal heirs of employee or past employee against them - Held, maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
