Gujarat Nermada Valley Fert. Company Limited v Collector of Ex. and Customs

Supreme Court of India

26 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 838; (2005) 7 SCC 94; 2005 (184) E.L.T. 128; 2005 (68) RLT 705; 2005(5) SCALE 269

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 2(F) and 3 - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Whether intermediate chemicals, Diethyl Chloro Acetanilide and Chloro Methyl Butyl Ether which are formed in process of manufacture of Butachlor are liable to tax under Act, 1944? - Unless product is capable of being marketed and is known to those who are in market as having an identity as a distinct identifiable commodity that article is not subject to excise duty - Held, revenue failed to prove this - Decision of CEGAT is set aside and order of Collector (Appeals) is restored - Appeal allowed.
