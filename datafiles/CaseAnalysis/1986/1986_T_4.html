Director General, Telecommunication And Anr v T.N. Peethambaram

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 197; (1986) 4 SCC 348; (1986) SCC (L&S) 780; AIR 1987 SC 162; 1986 ATC 552; 1987 (1) CCC 223; JT 1986 SC 496; 1987 LabIC 31; 1987 (1) LLJ 438; 1986 (2) LLN 1067; 1986(2) SCALE 471; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 828; 1987 (1) SLJ 190; 1986 (3) SLR 396; 1986 (2) UJ 747

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Telegraph Engineering Service (Group B) Recruitment Rules 1981

Summary: Service - Telegraph Engineering Service (Group B) Recruitment Rules 1981,appendix iii r 2 - 'Fails' in one subject, but 'passes' the examination - Interpretation of r.2, App.III of the Service rules for determining minimum passing marks in the departmental qualifying Examination - Held, minimum pass marks must be obtained in each subject or item - Appeal allowed.
