Christian Medical College Hospital Employees’ Union Andanr. v Christian Medical College Vellore Association And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28443; (1987) 4 SCC 691; (1988) SCC (L&S) 53; AIR 1988 SC 37; 1988 (2) CCC 309; JT 1987 (4) SC 129; 1988 LabIC 225; 1988 (1) LLJ 263; 1988 (1) LLN 9; 1987(2) SCALE 798; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 546; 1989 (1) SLJ 130; 1988 (1) SLR 259

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Educational Institution, National Tribunal, Gujarat University Act, 1949

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 30(1), sch. 7, list 3 entries 22, 23 and 24 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 9 A, 10, 11 A, 12 and 33 - Right of minority to establish and administer minority institution - Applicability of provisions of Industrial Dispute Act to minority institutions which are protected by cl. (1) of art. 30 of Constitution - Provisions of 1947 Act do not interfere with such right - And ss. 9 A, 10, 11 A, 12, 33 of 1947 Act apply to minority institution - Appeal allowed.
