Miss Raj Soni v Air Officer Incharge Administration And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 863; (1990) 3 SCC 261; (1990) SCC (L&S) 466; AIR 1990 SC 1305; 1990 (41) DLT 136; 1990 (61) FLR 5; JT 1990 (2) SC 173; 1990 LabIC 1161; 1990(1) SCALE 711; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 412; 1990 (4) SLR 240; 1990 (1) UJ 683

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Delhi School Education Act, 1973, s. 208 - Delhi Education Rules, 1973, r. 110 - Age of retirement - (A) Teacher appointed prior to enforcement of Act - Governed by Delhi Education Code providing 60 years age of superannuation - Order by Management retiring teacher on completion of 58 years - Validity - (B) If can be fixed by employers at their own discretion - Held, (A) Liable to be quashed - (B) Cannot be left to whims of employer to enable him to retire different employees at different ages - Petition allowed.
