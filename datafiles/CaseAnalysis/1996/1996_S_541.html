Balmokand Khatri Educational Andindustrial Trust, Amritsar v State Of Punjab And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 549; (1996) 4 SCC 212; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 50; AIR 1996 SC 1239; 1996 (1) CCC 319; 1996 (3) CLT 58; JT 1996 (3) SC 60; 1996 (1) RentLR 748; 1996(2) SCALE 577; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 643; 1996 (2) Supreme 638; 1996 (2) UJ 102

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.17 - Land acquisition for public purpose invoking urgency clause - Validity of urgency clause in the light of fact that land holder retained possession of acquired land even after passing of the award - Held, sufficient reason for invoking urgency clause, as his possession is illegal - Appeal dismissed.
