Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 14(1)(e) and 25 B(8) - Eviction - Revisional power - Bona fide requirement - Availability of suitable alternative accommodation to landlord - Dismissal of eviction petition by Rent Controller - As availability of alternative accommodation not disclosed - Revision - Re-examination on question regarding availability of alternative accommodation - Validity - Held, is justified.
