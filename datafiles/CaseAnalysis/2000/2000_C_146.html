V.M. Salgaocar And Bros. Pvt. Ltd. Etc. Etc. v Commissioner Of Income Tax Etc.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 681; (2000) 5 SCC 373; AIR 2000 SC 1623; 2000 (1) CLC 1181; 2000 (160) CTR 225; [2000] 243 ITR 383; JT 2000 (4) SC 473; 2000(3) SCALE 240; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1169; 2000 (119) STC 483; [2000] 110 TAXMAN 67

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Income-Tax Act, Finance Act, 1985, Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984, Income-tax Rules

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 261 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 133 and 136 - Company - (A) Whether interest on debit balance of Directors not charged amounts to perquisite? - (B) Order in this regard upheld, by High Court in case of AY 1980 - 81 - Effect of dismissal of appeal to Supreme Court u/art. 136 of Constitution - Held, is not perquisite - Supreme Court cannot take different view in respect of AY 1979 - 80 - Appeal No. 4012 - 13 of 1998 dismissed - Appeal No. 657 of 1994 allowed.
