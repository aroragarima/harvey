Mangalore Electric Supply Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Pathak, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 235; (1978) 3 SCC 248; AIR 1978 SC 1272; 1978 (7) CTR 61; [1978] 113 ITR 155; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 913; 1978 TAXLR 792; 1978 UJ 503

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 12B - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 66 and 66A - Income Tax Act - (A) Capital gains - Scope of expression 'transfer' - (B) Mixed question of law and fact - Raised for the first tome in appeal to Supreme Court - If allowed - Held, includes with its sweep both voluntary and involuntary transfers - Questions not allowed to be raised on facts - Appeals Dismissed.
