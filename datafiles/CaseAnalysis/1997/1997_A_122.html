Anukul Chandra Pradhan, Advocate, Supreme Court v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, Sujata V. Manohar, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2541; (1997) 6 SCC 1; AIR 1997 SC 2814; JT 1997 (6) SC 86; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 641

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Representation of the People Act, 1951, s. 62(5) - Constitution, arts. 14, 21, 32 and 226 - (A) Constitutionality of debarment of persons in prison or police custody , but not of persons under preventive detention - (B) If provision of s.62(5) of the Act can be challenged with reference to fundamental rights - Held, not discriminatory or violative of art. 21 - Cannot be challenged - Petition dismissed.
