Prakash Cotton Mills (P) Ltd. v B. Sen & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 286; (1979) 2 SCC 174; AIR 1979 SC 675; 1980 (17) DLT 74; 1979 (3) E.L.T. 241; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 1142; 1979 TAXLR 2438

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Documents Of Title

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, s.15(1)(b) - FERA, 1947 - Whether requirement of amended s. 15 can be ignored on ground that goods were imported before it came into force - Held, could not be ignored simply because the goods were imported before it came into force, or that their bills of lading or bills of entry were lodged before that date - Appeals dismissed.
