Dharmarathmakara Raibahadur Arcot Ramaswamy Mudaliar Educational Institution v Educational Appellate Tribunal and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 659; (1999) 7 SCC 332; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1288; AIR 1999 SC 3219; 1999 (3) ESC 1748; JT 1999 (6) SC 60; 1999 LabIC 3237; 1999(5) SCALE 138; 1999 (7) Supreme 274

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Karnataka Private Educational Institutions (Discipline and Control) Act, 1975, s. 6 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Leave to pursue particular course granted to Lecturer - Joining other course of larger duration - Need for enquiry on termination of service when allegations already admitted - Held, enquiry unnecessary as facts speak for itself and no interference with termination - Appeal allowed.
