M/S. Premier Fabricators, Allahabad v Heavy Engineering Corpn. Limited, Ranchi

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1103; (1997) 4 SCC 319; AIR 1997 SC 3603; 1997 (1) ARBLR 517; 1997 (3) CLT 57; JT 1997 (4) SC 220; 1997(3) SCALE 208; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 161; 1997 (3) Supreme 409

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & Adr - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 33 - Award - Setting aside - Dispute including the question whether certain claims made are arbitrable referred to umpire - Umpire awarding consolidated sum by non-speaking award - Whether award considered question of arbitrability - Whether award is legal - Held, it cannot be construed to have considered arbitrability by implication - Umpire before adjudicating claims on merits should give his finding on arbitrability of claims and reasons therefor - Is illegal - Appeal allowed.
