Raja Bahadur K. C. Deo Bhanj v Raghunath Misra and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

Syed Jaffer Imam, S.K. Das, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 96; AIR 1959 SC 589; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 952; [1959] Supp1 S.C.R. 952

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: Companies Act, misa, Orissa Gram Panchayats, Act, 1948

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 123(7) and 123(8) - Orissa Gram Panchayats Act, 1948, ss. 8 and 31 - Elections - Gram Panchayat - Sarpanch - Interpretation of 'In service of Govt' u/s. 128(7)(f) of 1951 Act - Considering provisions of Orissa Gram Panchayats Act - Held, Sarpanch does not exercise functions as one in service of Govt., he is neither revenue officer nor village accountant - Appeal allowed.
