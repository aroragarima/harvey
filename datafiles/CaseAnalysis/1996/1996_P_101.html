Surendra Kumar v Phoolchand (Dead) Through LRs. and Another

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3197; (1996) 2 SCC 491; AIR 1996 SC 1148; 1996 (1) CCC 186B; 1996 (1) CLT 492; JT 1996 (2) SC 127; 1996(1) SCALE 644; 1996 (2) SCJ 282; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 15; 1996 (1) Supreme 777

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Civil procedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 30 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Apportionment of compensation - Dispute as to non-inclusion of disputed property in earlier partition suit - Whether fatal to reference proceedings u/s. 30 - Whether 0. 2, r. 2 of CPC, 1908 providing for inclusion of whole claim in suit, applicable - Held, it is not fatal to reference proceedings - S. 30 is not applicable - (B) Whether property was joint family property or self acquired property? - Further held, no material showing that consideration for disputed property was paid by appellant from out of his separate funds - Concurrent findings of courts that property is joint family property - No interference - Appeal dismissed.
