Usman Gani J. Khatri Of Bombay Etc. Etc. v Cantonment Board and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1068; (1992) 3 SCC 455; AIR 1994 SC 233; 1993 (1) Bom.C.R. 443; JT 1992 (4) SC 538; 1992(1) SCALE 1068; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 1; 1992 (2) UJ 441

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Cantonment Land Administration Rules, 1937

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Land & Property - Cantonments Act 1924, s. 181 - Pune Cantonment (Building) Bye-laws, 1988, Bye-laws 21, 23, 24 and 25 - Buildings plans - Sanction as to - New schemes of building restrictions issued before sanction was given - Building plans as originally submitted - Whether can be approved ? - Held, cannot be approved - Fresh plans if submitted would be governed by such new schemes - Appeal dismissed.
