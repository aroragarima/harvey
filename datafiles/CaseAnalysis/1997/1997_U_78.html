J. N. Srivastava v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1528; (1998) 9 SCC 559; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1251; AIR 1999 SC 1571; 1999 (1) LLJ 546

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Voluntary retirement - Withdrawal of notice of voluntary retirement - Employer accepts - Subsequently withdrawal by employee - Held, employee has locus standi - May withdraw proposal before his date of retirement.
