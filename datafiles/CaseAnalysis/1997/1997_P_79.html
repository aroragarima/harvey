Vinita M. Khanolkar v Pragna M. Pai and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1726; (1998) 1 SCC 500; AIR 1998 SC 424; AIR 1997 SCW 4415; 1998 (4) Bom.C.R. 321; 1998 (1) CLT 99; JT 1997 (9) SC 490; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 48; 1997(7) SCALE 356; 1998 (2) SLT 169; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 593

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 6(3) - Bar to appeal/revision - Applicability - Order passed by single judge u/s. 6 decreeing suit - Letters patent appeal against - Maintainability - Held, bar to appeal and revision contained in s. 6(3) - Does not apply to Letters patent appeal - Is maintainable.
