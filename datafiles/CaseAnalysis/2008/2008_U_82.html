Union Of India v Y.S. Sadhu, Ex-Inspector

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1516; (2008) 12 SCC 30; AIR 2009 SC 161; 2009(1) ALL MR 455; 2008 (6) AWC 6443; 2008 (5) CTC 285; 2008 (5) ESC 689; 2008 (119) FLR 395; JT 2008 (10) SC 521; 2009 (1) LLJ 609; 2008(12) SCALE 748; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 784

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Dismissal - Single Judge directing re-instatement of writ petitioner in service without payment of back wages on the ground that the witnesses examined earlier were not produced for cross examination - Appeal against - Held, Court/Tribunal should not mechanically set aside the order of punishment - There shall not be any reinstatement but the proceedings shall continue from the stage where it stood before the alleged vulnerability surfaced - Appeal partly allowed.
