Sutlej Cotton Mills Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 224; (1978) 4 SCC 358; AIR 1979 SC 5; 1978 CTR 155; [1979] 116 ITR 1; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 976

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(1)(2) - Income Tax - Business Loss - Determination - Transfer of profits by assessee having income in Pakistan, to India - High Court holding loss arising out of devaluation of Rupee by Pakistan as not amounting to business loss - Challenged - Held, HC order set aside - Case Remanded.
