State of Andhra Pradesh v Dar-U-Salam Education Trust and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 July 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 665; (1990) Supp SCC 3

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Medical college - Provisional permission - Whether students already admitted on basis of provisional permission granted by State Govt. to college should be permitted to appear at university examinations to be held shortly? - Held, exigencies of situation warrant students being permitted to appear at university examinations to be held shortly - Liberty for mentioning for early appearing granted - Order accordingly.
