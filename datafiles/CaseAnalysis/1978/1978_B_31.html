Mranalini B. Shah and Another v Bapalal Mohanlal Shah

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 423; (1980) 4 SCC 251; AIR 1980 SC 954

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 12(3)(b) - Tenancy - Payment of rent and permitted increases regularly during pendency of suit or appeal - Mature of term 'regularly' - For compliance with the proisions of s. 12(3)(b) - Held, is mandatory and not directory - In case of monthly tenancy Court has no discretion to treat payments made at irregular intervals as sufficient compliance with s.12(3)(b) - Order Accordingly.
