M. G. Pandke and Others v Municipal Council Hinganghat, District Wardha and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 588; (1993) SCC (L&S) 240; (1993) Supp1 SCC 708; AIR 1993 SC 142; 1993 (23) ATC 778; 1993 (3) Bom.C.R. 162; 1993 (66) FLR 981; JT 1992 (5) SC 408; 1992 LabIC 2430; 1992(2) SCALE 491; 1993 (2) SLJ 61; 1992 (5) SLR 358; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 464; 1993 (1) UPLBEC 48

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Maharashtra Municipalities Act, 1965

Summary: Maharashtra Secondary Education Boards Act, 1965, s. 37 - Maharashtra Secondary Education Boards Regulations, 1966, Regn. 19(7)(xvi) - Maharashtra Secondary Schools Code, 1963, r. 82(1), Exception -Constitution of India, 1950, art. 245 - Delegated legislation - Occupied field - Retirement age - Teacher - Recognised Municipal schools - Entitled to be in service till 60 years of age as per Secondary Schools Code - Held, Municipal Council cannot make Bye-laws to contrary - As legislative field qua retirement age is already occupied by statutorily recognised school code - Bye-law violative of art. 16 of Constitution.
