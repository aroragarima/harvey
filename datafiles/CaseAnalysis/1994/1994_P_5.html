Commissioner of Income Tax, Hyderabad v P. J. Chemicals Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1025; (1994) Supp3 SCC 535; AIR 1994 SC 2727; 1994 (121) CTR 201; [1994] 210 ITR 830; JT 1994 (6) SC 330; 1994(4) SCALE 337; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 561; [1994] 76 TAXMAN 611

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 32 and 43(1) - Govt. subsidy - Interpretation of Statutes - Interpretation of expression 'actual cost' to determine whether subsidies are or not deductible from 'actual cost' u/s. 43(1) Act - It is not partake of character of a payment intended either directly or indirectly to meet 'actual cost' - Held, to to be interpreted liberally - Order accordingly.
