Aligarh Muslim Univiersity Enterprises (P) v Vinay Engineering Entreprises (P)

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.M. Punchhi, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 672; (1994) 4 SCC 710

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Arbitration & ADR - Contracts in question were executed at Aligarh - Construction work was to be carried out at Aligarh - Dispute resolution clause provided that in event of dispute Aligarh Court alone will have jurisdiction - Held, case of abuse of jurisdiction - Proceedings initiated in High Court of Calcutta shall be returned to respondent for presentation in proper court - Litigation filed in Calcutta HC was thoroughly unsustainable - Appeal allowed.
