Gurupreet Singh v Chatur Bhuj Goel

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28132; (1988) 1 SCC 270; AIR 1988 SC 400; 1988 (1) CCC 135; JT 1987 (4) SC 665; 1988 MPLJ 1; 1987(2) SCALE 1338; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 401

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Code of Civil Procedure 1908, O. 23, r. 3 - Compromise of suit - Compromise was entered into during hearing of appeal - Held, court must insist that compromise should be reduced in writing and signed by parties - Appeal dismissed
