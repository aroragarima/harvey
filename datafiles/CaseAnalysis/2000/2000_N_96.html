Neelam Kanwar v Devinder Singh Kanwar

Supreme Court of India

25 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3908; (2000) 10 SCC 589; (2001) SCC (Cr) 1507; JT 2000 (10) SC 190

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 500 - Transfer of Criminal Complaint from Chandigarh to Mumbai - Held, petitioner is a lady and first respondent is a male and therefore convenience wise a transfer to place where lady is residing would be preferred by this court unless it is shown that there are special reasons not to do so - No such special reason is shown - Criminal Complaint No. 68 of 1994 on file of Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class UT Chandigarh is transferred to file of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Mumbai - Transfer Petition is disposed of.
