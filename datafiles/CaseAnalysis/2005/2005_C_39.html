Dilip Kumar Ghosh & Ors v Chairman & Ors

Supreme Court of India

12 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 550; (2005) 7 SCC 567; (2005) SCC (L&S) 986; AIR 2005 SC 3485; JT 2005 (8) SC 271; 2005(7) SCALE 263; 2005 (5) SLR 795; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 158

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Education - Bengal (Rural) Primary Education Act, 1930 - West Bengal Urban Primary Education Act, 1963 - West Bengal (Rural) Primary Education (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1969 - West Bengal Primary Education Act, 1973 - Recruitment and Leave of Teachers in Primary Schools in West Bengal Rules of 1991, rr. 2(n), 35, 6, 6(d), 9(e) - Whether appellants who have obtained B A/B Ed /Ph Ed degrees can be equated with candidates who are holders of Junior Basic Training/Primary Teacher Training Certificate for purpose of appointment to post of Primary School Teacher? - Held, for teaching in primary school, one must know child psychology and development of a child at tender age - Appellants who are trained in B Ed degree are not necessarily equipped to teach students of primary class - They are not trained and equipped to understand psychology of a child of tender age - Appeal dismissed.
