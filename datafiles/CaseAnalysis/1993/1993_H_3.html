Delhi Development Authority v H.C. Khurana

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1188; (1993) 3 SCC 196; (1993) SCC (L&S) 736; AIR 1993 SC 1488; 1993 (24) ATC 763; 1993 (2) CLR 683; 1993 (67) FLR 303; JT 1993 (2) SC 695; 1993 LabIC 1115; 1993 (2) LLJ 303; 1993 (2) LLN 34; 1993(2) SCALE 464; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 1033; 1993 (2) SLR 509; 1993 (2) UJ 342; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 1421

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Govt. servant - When decision to initiate disciplinary proceedings was taken? - Charge-sheet to issued and despatched - Held, issue of chargesheet indicates proceedings was taken as actual service is not part of decision making process - Appeal allowed.
