V. Balasubramaniam Etc. Etc v Tamil.Nadu Housing Board And Ors Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28413; (1987) 4 SCC 738; (1988) SCC (L&S) 82; AIR 1988 SC 6; 1987 (5) ATC 183; 1988 (1) CCC 17; JT 1987 (3) SC 617; 1988 LabIC 213; 1988 (2) LLJ 435; 1988 (2) LLN 961; 1987(2) SCALE 625; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 228; 1988 (1) SLJ 90; 1988 (3) SLR 753; 1988 (1) UJ 48

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Tamil Nadu State Housing Board Act, 1961, Madras Housing Board Service regulation

Summary: Service - Tamil Nadu State Housing Board Act, 1961, ss. 16, 17, 18, 19 and 161 - Tamil Nadu State Housing Board Service Regulations, Regn. 28(d) - Constitution, art. 162 - T.N. General Clauses Act 1891, s. 3(19A) - Promotion to post of A.E. - 5 years service fixed for J.E. - (A) Resolution passed by Board for it and sent to Govt. - During pendency of matter fresh resolution passed and period reduced to 3 years - Subsequent resolution going back on 5 years' service - Order passed by Govt. in terms of resolution but mentioning 3 years' qualifying service for promotion - Nature and scope of subsequent order passed by way of errata to correct it to five years - (B) 5 years service for J.E. prescribed under Regulations - Board cannot depart from regulations though they are not published in Gazette - Promotions made without following Regulations - Held, it was only an errata and it did not modify order - Held, are illegal - Appeal allowed.
