State Of Maharashtra v Kaliar Koil Subrahmaniam Ramaswamy

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 94; (1977) 3 SCC 525; (1977) SCC (Cr) 528; AIR 1977 SC 2091; 1977 CRLJ 1740; 1977 CrLR(SC) 372; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 274; 1977 UJ 597

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Prevention of Corruption Act 1964

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 5(1)(e) and 5(30) - Constitution, art. 20(1) - Criminal misconduct - Possession of disproportionate pecuniary resources or property - Mere possession made offence u/cl. (e) - No evidence possession after provision comme into existence - Efect - Accused is entitled to protection u/art. 20(1) of Constitution - Appeal Dismissed.
