Special Land Acquisition Officer, Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Board, Dharwad, Karnataka v Revanasiddappa

Supreme Court of India

18 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2436; (2008) 15 SCC 257; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1024; 2009 (75) ALR 495; 2009 (2) AWC 1279; 2009(1) SCALE 153

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Land acquisition - Compensation - Petitioner/State acquired land of respondents/claimants - HC directed petitioner shall deposit half of amount awarded by HC in Reference Court - Claimants filed Interlocutory applications seeking release of pre-deposit amount - Petitioner prayed for stay against above order - Hence, instant petitions.

Held, 50% of amount awarded by HC in Reference case has already been deposited. Such being position and considering total amount of compensation payable on basis of order of HC. Court disposes of applications for release of pre-deposit amount. Claimants are entitled to withdraw 50% of amount already deposited by petitioner in compliance with our interim order without furnishing any security and balance 50% of amount deposited in compliance with our order can be withdrawn by claimants after furnishing security to satisfaction of Reference Court, where money has already been deposited. In event it is found that amount deposited by petitioner in compliance with order. Petitions disposed of.
