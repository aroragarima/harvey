Committee of Management, Arya Nagar Inter v Sree Kumar Tiwary & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1133; (1997) 4 SCC 388; (1997) SCC (L&S) 967; AIR 1997 SC 3071; 1997 ALJ 1900; 1997 (30) ALR 208; JT 1997 (4) SC 572; 1997 LabIC 2903; 1997 (2) LLJ 797; 1997(3) SCALE 509; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 467; 1997 (4) SLR 11; 1997 (4) Supreme 31

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Ad Hoc Appointment

Summary: U.P. Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Board Act, 1982, s.33B(1)(a)(i); U.P. Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Board (Removal of Difficulties) (Second) Order, 1981, Para 2 - Ad hoc appointment - Act amended for regularisation of candidate appointed on substantive vacancy - Ad hoc appointee were terminated before that cut-off date - continued in the office with permission of the management and stay order of High Court - Held, continuance was not by virtue of his own right - Temporary vacancy deemed to have been converted into a substantive vacancy - Stay order of High Court and regularisation directed by management subject to result of writ petition - Immaterial.
