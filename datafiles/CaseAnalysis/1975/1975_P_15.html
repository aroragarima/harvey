Pasupuleti Venkateswarlu v Motor and General Traders

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 408; (1975) 1 SCC 770; AIR 1975 SC 1409; 1975 RCR(Rent) 486; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 958; 1975 UJ 327

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, O. 7, r. 7, s. 115, O. 41, r. 25 - Practice and Procedure - (A) Power of Revisional Court to take cognisance of subsequent events - Eviction proceeding under Rent Control Act - Subsequent event disabling the landlord from seeking eviction - (B) Remittal of whole case by appellate - Court where a finding on a specific point was required - High Court in revision is bound to take note of subsequent event, in disposing of proceeding - Held, is illegal - Appeal partly allowed.
