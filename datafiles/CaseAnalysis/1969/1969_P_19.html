Â Chandramouleshwar Prasad v Patna High Court and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.M. Sikri, A.N. Ray, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 473; (1969) 3 SCC 56; AIR 1970 SC 370; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 666; 1970 SLR 825; 1970 UJ 6

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Bihar Superior Judicial Service Rules, 1951, rr. 5, 16(b), 16(d) - Constitution, arts. 309, 233, 234 and 235 - Civil Services - (A) Power to fix gradation of judicial officers - High Court has no power -Seniority, how fixed - (B) Appointment of District Judge by Gov without consultation of HC - Validity - (C) Right of HC to transfer District Judges - Held, to be determined when the persons appointed either temporarily or on an officiating basis are given substantive appointment - Held, is not valid - Right to transfer judicial officers including District Judges after their first appointment and posting rests with the HC - Order Accordingly.
