Economic Transport Organization v Dharwad Distt. Khadi Gramodyog Sangh

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 243; (2000) 5 SCC 78; AIR 2000 SC 1635; 2000 (1) CPJ 41; 2000 (1) CPJ(Sc) 41; 2000 (2) CPR 33; 2000 (3) CTC 73; JT 2000 (4) SC 327; 2000(3) SCALE 153; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 888; 2000 (3) SLT 537; 2000 (3) Supreme 232

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Consumer Protection

Summary: Carriers Act, 1865, s. 9 - Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 14 - Complaint against carrier in Consumer Forum - Principle underlying s. 9 of Carriers Act imposing burden on carrier to prove absence of negligence applies to such proceedings - Initial onus of proof cast on complainant by s. 14(1)(a) of 1986 Act - Held, can be discharged by complainant by relying on s. 9 of Carriers Act.
