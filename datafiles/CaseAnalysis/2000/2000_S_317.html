Federation of A. P. Chambers of Commerce and v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 260; (2000) 6 SCC 550; AIR 2000 SC 2905; 2001 (165) CTR 672; [2001] 247 ITR 36; JT 2000 (8) SC 516; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 151; [2001] 115 TAXMAN 145

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Non Agricultural Lands Assessment Tax Act, 1963

Summary: AP Non-Agricultural Land Assessment Act, 1963, ss. 3, 2(d) and 2(g) - Appeal against the view of Five Judges Bench of High Court which interpreted the word 'used' in the Andhra Pradesh Non-Agricultural Lands Assessment Act, 1963 to mean 'non-agricultural lands not only 'actually used' but are 'meant to be used' or 'set apart for being used' '- Assessment of land used for industrial purpose - Interpretation of Statutes to determine the meaning of word 'used' in s. 3 - Held, means land which is 'actually in use' for industrial purpose and not land 'meant to be used' or 'set apart for being used' - Appeal allowed.
