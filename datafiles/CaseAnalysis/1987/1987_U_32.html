O.P. Gupta v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28178; (1987) 4 SCC 328; (1987) SCC (L&S) 400; AIR 1987 SC 2257; 1987 (5) ATC 14; 1987 (2) CCC 755; JT 1987 (3) SC 532; 1987 LabIC 1904; 1988 (1) LLJ 453; 1987 (2) LLN 847; 1987(2) SCALE 457; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 27; 1988 (1) SLJ 121; 1987 (5) SLR 288

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 13, 21, 226, 309 and 311 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 34 - Central Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1965, r. 12(2) - Service Rules - Suspension continued for 11 years pending departmental inquiry, as proceedings kept pending for over 20 years - Revocation and reinstatement order withholding increments at efficiency bar that too without being heard - (A) Legality of Order - (B) Interest on delayed payment pension - Order is illegal, court can review order - Settled practice, to allow interest at 12% - Appeal allowed.
