Indtel Technical Services Private Limited v W.S. Atkins Rail Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 August 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1363; (2008) 10 SCC 308; AIR 2009 SC 1132; 2008 (73) ALR 302; 2008 (6) AWC 6427; JT 2008 (9) SC 354; 2009 (1) MLJ(SC) 320; 2008 (4) RCR(Civil) 212; 2008(11) SCALE 735; [2008] 12 S.C.R. 673

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Curial Law, Legal Process, International Commercial Arbitrations

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1996, s. 11(9) - Application for appointment of arbitrator - Held, when an arbitration agreement is silent as to the law and procedure to be followed in implementing the arbitration agreement, the law governing the said agreement would ordinarily be the same as the law governing the contract itself - From the wording of clause 13.2 and clause 13.3, for the purpose of this application, that the parties to the Memorandum intended to have their disputes resolved by arbitration - Petition allowed.
