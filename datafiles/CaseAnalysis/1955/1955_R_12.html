Jugalkishore Saraf v Rao Cotton Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhi Ranjan Das, Syed Jaffer Imam, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 20; AIR 1955 SC 376; 1956 (58) BomLR 517; 1955 (1) MLJ(SC) 220; 1955 MWN 421; 1955 SCJ 371; [1955] 1 S.C.R. 1369

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Copyright Act, 1911

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 21, r. 16 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 8 - Respondent company prayed to declare them assignees of decree as decrement debt along with other debts had been transferred to them by a deed of assignment - Whether respondent can claim to have become transferees of decree by deed of assignment, if so whether they entitle for execution? - Held, respondent company were after transfer, owners of debt and entitled u/s. 146 to make application for execution - Appeal dismissed.
