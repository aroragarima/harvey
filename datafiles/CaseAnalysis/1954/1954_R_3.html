Â Raj Krushna Bose v Binod Kanungo and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1954

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhi Ranjan Das, Mehr Chand Mahajan, Bijan Kumar Mukherjea, Vivian Bose, Ghulam Hasan

Where Reported

1954 Indlaw SC 31; AIR 1954 SC 202; 1954 (1) MLJ(SC) 489; 1954 SCJ 286; [1954] 1 S.C.R. 913

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Representation of People Act, 1950

Summary: Election - Constitution of India , 1950, art. 136 and 226 - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 33(2) and 123(8) - Whether an election to a State Legislative Assembly is invalidated when member's nomination was either proposed or seconded, or both, by a Govt. servant or servants? - Held, no - Proposing or seconding by a Govt. servant cannot be regarded as 'assistance for furtherance of prospects of candidate's election' - Directions issued - Matter remitted back to tribunal.
