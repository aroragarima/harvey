Bashir Ahmed v Mehmood Hussain Shah

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1596; (1995) 3 SCC 529; AIR 1995 SC 1857; 1996 (2) BC 416; 1995(2) SCALE 566; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 812; 1995 (2) UJ 59

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 17, r. 1(2) - Grant of adjournment - Matter posted for cross-examination of plaintiff - Defendant seeking adjournment on ground that counsel is not well - Court granted adjournment and also ordered forfeiture of right to cross-examine - Whether said order justified - Held, said order was not justified - Court should have adjourned case for next date so as to enable defendant to engage another Counsel - Appeal allowed.
