Harihar Prasad Dubey v Tulsi Das Mundhra and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 426; (1980) 4 SCC 120; (1980) SCC (Cr) 931; 1980 (17) ACC 294; AIR 1981 SC 81; 1980 CRLJ 1329

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s. 406 - W.B. Board of Secondary Education Act, 1963, ss. 27 and 45 - Misappropriation of Provident Fund money of school - In the absence of any statutory rules in this regard, proceedings were quashed - Absence of statutory rules held is no ground to quashing - Appeal Allowed.
