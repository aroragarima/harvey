Sukra Mahto v Basdeo Kumar Mahto &. Anr

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 134; (1971) SCC (Cr) 372; (1971) 1 SCC 885; AIR 1971 SC 1567; 1971 CRLJ 1168; [1971] S.C.R. 329; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 329

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: IPC, 1860, s. 499, Exception 9 - CrPC, 1898, s.144 - (A) Prosecution for defamation - Claim as to good faith - Accused to show imputation he had made enquiry with due care and attention and that he was satisfied about the truth of that imputation - (B) Question of title is involved in proceeding u/s. 144, CrPC, 1898 - If it per se authorise a party to make defamatory statement against other party and then claim that it was for protection of interest - Emphasis is on enquiry, care and objective satisfaction - Protection of interest of person making imputation will have to be established by showing that the imputation was itself the protection of interest of the person making - Appeal Dismissed.
