S. Banerjee v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 84; (1989) Supp2 SCC 486; (1990) SCC (L&S) 160; AIR 1990 SC 285; 1991 (17) ATC 197; 1990 (60) FLR 140; JT 1989 (4) SC 547; 1990 LabIC 298; 1990 (2) LLJ 440; 1989(2) SCALE 941; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 562; 1990 (1) SLJ 95; 1990 (1) SLR 855; [1989] Supp1 S.C.R. 562

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 5(2) - Effective date for availing benefit of revised pension - Govt. employees - Benefit available to employees who retire during 1-1-1986 to 30-9-1986 - Petitioner who was allowed to voluntarily retire with effect from forenoon of 1986 also entitled to benefit of revised pension - Petition allowed.
