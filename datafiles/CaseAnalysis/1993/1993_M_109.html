Morvi Municipality and another v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, N.M. Kasliwal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1760; (1993) 2 SCC 520; AIR 1993 SC 1508; AIR 1993 SCW 1476; 1993 (112) CTR 448; JT 1993 (2) SC 529; 1993 (1) RCR(Rent) 572; 1993(2) SCALE 380; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 803

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Land & Property - Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1964, ss. 99, 99(1)(i) and 112 - Property Tax - (A) Determination of annual letting value of property to which Rent Restriction Act applies - (B) Limitation for authentication of assessment list - (C) Validity of r. 5 of Junagadh Municipality Rules, r. 5 as far as it providing for value to be actual rent received - Held, letting value cannot exceed standard rent, fair rent etc. - No limitation - Further held, permissible - Appeal allowed.
