Harish Pahwa v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 44; (1981) SCC (Cr) 589; (1981) 2 SCC 710; AIR 1981 SC 1126; 1981 BBCJ 205; 1981 CRLJ 750; 1981 CRLR 384; 1981 CrLR(SC) 384; 1981(1) SCALE 704; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 276

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution, arts. 22 and 136 - CoFEPOSA Act, 1974, s. 3 - Preventive detention - High Court dismissed writ petition for habeas corpus - Appeal - Plea that detention was illegal for non deciding plea in reasonable time - (A) Point not raised in writ petition - (B) Delay - Held, that in view of its importance and the fact that material necessary for its determination being available on record the point could be allowed - Delay invalidates detention - Appeal Allowed.
