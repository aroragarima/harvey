Gudur Kishan Rao and Others v Sutirtha Bhattacharya and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1495; (1998) 4 SCC 189; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1064; AIR 1998 SC 1242; 1998 (2) AWC 1094; JT 1998 (2) SC 90; 1998 LabIC 1065; 1998 (2) LLN 439; 1998(2) SCALE 14; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1053; 1998 (2) SLJ 294; 1998 (2) SLR 233; 1998 (3) SLT 263; 1998 (2) Supreme 185

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Year Of Allotment, All India Services (Conditions of Service Residuary Matters) Rules, 1960, Widows Welfare Bill, 2007

Summary: All India Services Act, 1951, s. 3 - Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, rr. 3 and 4 - Indian Administrative Service (Fixation of Cadre Strength) Regulation, regn. 1 - Notification of Central Govt. D/ -15-12-93 - Amending cadre strength regulations for State of AP by notification - Held, not have status of Act or rule - Amended cadre strength regulation increasing only number of posts for state civil service officers to be promoted - Contravenes r. 9 of Recruitment Rules.
