Bal Niketan Nursery School v Kesari Prasad

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28886; (1987) 3 SCC 587; AIR 1987 SC 1970; JT 1987 (3) SC 93; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 4144; 1987 (2) RentLR 612; 1987(2) SCALE 67; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 510; 1987 (2) UJ 501

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111 - Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Act, 1972 - Tenancy - Suit for eviction of tenants by recognised school, from land (with superstructures) purchased in its own name - Maintainability of suit filed through Manager who was also the Secretary of Registered Society - Held, maintainable - Appeal allowed.
