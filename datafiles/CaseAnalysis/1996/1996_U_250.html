S. R. Bhanrale v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2215; (1996) 10 SCC 172; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1384; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 195; AIR 1997 SC 27; 1996 LabIC 2756; 1997 (1) LLN 147; 1996(5) SCALE 693; 1997 (1) SLJ 14; 1996 (4) SLR 717; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 763; 1996 (6) Supreme 258

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 35 - Defaulted in making payments promptly inspite of consistent demands for payments towards encashment of leaves, certain increment arrears etc - Govt. pleaded limitation - If allowed - Held, can not he pleaded - Court directed Department to pay interest and compensation and litigation expenses also - Appeal disposed of.
