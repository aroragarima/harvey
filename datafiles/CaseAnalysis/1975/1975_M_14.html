Roshan Lal v Madan Lal

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 250; (1975) 2 SCC 785; AIR 1975 SC 2130; 1975 RCJ 675; 1976 RCR(Rent) 112; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 878; 1975 UJ 936

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: M.P. Accommodation Control Act, 1961, s. 12(1) - CPC, 1908, O. 23, r. 3 - Tenancy - Interpretation - Construction - O. 23, r. 3 of CPC, 1908, applicable to eviction suits governed by special statutes - Decree on basis of compromise - Duty of Court - Held, Court is bound to record the compromise and pass a decree in accordance therewith - Appeal Dismissed.
