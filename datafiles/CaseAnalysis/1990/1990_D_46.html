Subodhaya Chit Fund Private Limited v Director of Chits, Madras, and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 948; (1991) Supp2 SCC 131; AIR 1991 SC 998; [1993] 76 Comp Cas 873

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate

Summary: Parliament & Legislature - Tamil Nadu Chit Funds Act, 1961, s. 12 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(g), 301 and 304(b) - Legislative competence - State legislature - Sanction of President - When not necessary ? - 1961 Act repealed - Amended by Amendment Act 14 of 1975, s. 12 - Validity - Amended provision only varied form of restriction which was already inherent in original Act - Principal Act having sanction of President - Amended Act, whether invalid for want of sanction of President - Held, not invalid - Appeals dismissed.
