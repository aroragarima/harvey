Hindustan Shipyard Limited and Others v Dr. P. Sambasiva Rao Etc

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3441; (1996) 7 SCC 499; (1996) SCC (L&S) 619; AIR 1996 SC 3230; 1996 (33) ATC 309; 1996 (73) FLR 1050; JT 1996 (2) SC 481; 1996 LabIC 1606; 1996 (2) LLJ 807; 1996(1) SCALE 639; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 1037; 1996 (1) SLR 805; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 836

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2 item 6 - Recruitment Rules providing appointment on post of Medical Officers only after selection by duly constituted Selection Committee - Direction for regularisation given by the High Court - Requirement in the Rules regarding selection by a Selection Committee for the purpose of regular appointment on the post of medical officer had been dispensed with - Held, directions liable to be set aside - Appeal partly allowed.
