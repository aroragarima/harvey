State Of Maharashtra & Anr. v Messrs National Construction Company, Bombay and

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1169; (1996) 1 SCC 735; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 458; AIR 1996 SC 2367; AIR 1996 SCW 895; 1996 (28) ALR 26; 1996 (1) CCC 61; 1996 (1) CLT 315; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 21; JT 1996 (1) SC 156; 1996(1) SCALE 176; 1996 (2) SCJ 58; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 293; 1996 (1) Supreme 264

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 11, O. 2, r. 2 - (A) Res judicata - Applicability - Dismissal of suit and subsequent appeal on technical grounds - No decision on merits of case - Whether such dismissal of suit and appeal would operate as bar to subsequent suit - (B) Suit to include whole claim - First suit filed to enforce bank guarantee - Second suit to claim damages for breach of contract relating to which bank guarantee was given - Whether relief sought in first suit based on different cause of action from that upon which relief in subsequent suit was founded - Whether subsequent suit hit by O. 2, r. 2 - Held, (A) Dismissed without any adjudication on the matter in issue merely on a technical ground of non joinder, cannot operate as res judicata - (B) Based on different cause of action - Not hit by O. 2, r. 2 - Appeal allowed
