Mukesh Kumar Aggarwal And Ors. v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28809; (1988) Supp SCC 232; AIR 1988 SC 563; 1988 (14) ECR 353; 2004 (178) E.L.T. 3; JT 1988 (1) SC 50; 1988(1) SCALE 1; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 501; 1988 (68) STC 324

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1959, sch. 2, Part II, Entry 32 A - Expression 'Timber' - Interpretation - Stacks of 'eucalyptus-wood' after separating 'ballies' and 'poles' - Whether timber? - Held, not timber - Order accordingly.
