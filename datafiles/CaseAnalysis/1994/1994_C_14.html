Hico Products Limited v C.C.E.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1499; (1994) 4 SCC 578; AIR 1995 SC 400; 1994 (47) ECC 63; 1994 (52) ECR 321; 1994 (71) E.L.T. 339; JT 1994 (3) SC 309; 1994(2) SCALE 644; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 625

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Tariff Item, Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1955, Tariff Act, 1934

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3,ch. 1, items 68 and 15 A - Trade and Merchandise Marks Act, 1958 - Excise Duty - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Exemption - Notification specified goods by schedule annexed thereto and falling under residuary item, Item 68 - Exemption for Medicinal Silicone bearing names Dimethicone and Simethicone on ground that they were 'bulk drugs' as specified in schedule to notification - Whether can be claimed as Silicone falls under specific Items No. 15 A and not under Item No. 68 ? - Held cannot be claimed - Appeal dismissed.
