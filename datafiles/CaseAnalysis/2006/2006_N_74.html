Nizam Sugar Factory v Collector of Central Excise, A.P

Supreme Court of India

20 April 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 671; (2006) 11 SCC 573; 2006 (197) E.L.T. 465

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Limitation - Extended period - Suppression of facts - Show cause notice has been issued for the earlier period on certain set of facts, then, on the same set of facts another SCN based on the same/similar set of facts invoking the extended period of limitation on the plea of suppression of facts by the assessee was issued - Held, when the first SCN was issued all the relevant facts were in the knowledge of the authorities - While issuing the second and third show cause notices, the same/similar facts could not be taken as suppression of facts on the part of the assessee as these facts were already in the knowledge of the authorities - No suppression of facts - Impugned order set aside - Appeals disposed of.
