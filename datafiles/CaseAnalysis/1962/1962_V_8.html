Virupaxappa Veerappa Kadampur v The State Of Mysore

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, Syed Jaffer Imam, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 263; AIR 1963 SC 849; 1963 CRLJ 814; 1964 (1) SCJ 160; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 6; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 6

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Defense & Security Forces - Bombay Police Act, 1951, s. 161(1) - Bombay General Clauses Act - (A) Is s. 161(1) of Bombay Police Act, 1951 applicable to offences under Indian Penal Code, 1860 as well? - Held, definition of 'Offence' in Bombay General Clauses Act includes an offence under Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Legislature deliberately gave protection of s. 161(1) to offences against any law - No justification for limiting that protection to offences under Police Act only - Hence, applicable to IPC offences as well - Appeal allowed.
