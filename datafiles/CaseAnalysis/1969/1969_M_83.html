Smt. Gunwant Kaur and Others v Municipal Committee, Bhatinda and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 431; (1969) 3 SCC 769; AIR 1970 SC 802; 1970 ALJ 178

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 5A and 17(A) - Constitution of India arts. 226 and 227 - Land - Acquisition - (A) Notification seeking to acquire certain land without demarcation - Objections that it did not show correct demarcation and landholders had no opportunity to file objections - Collector's order stating that collector was satisfied that land demarcated corresponded to the area notified - But Order u/s. 17(4) not passed - Writ petition against also dismissed in limine by the High Court on ground that it could not go into disputed question of fact - (B) Constitution - Writ jurisdiction - (1) Disputed questions of fact - If can be determined - (2)Dismissal of petition in limine - Order held, was bad - Disputed questions of fact can be determined - Consideration of - Appeal Allowed.
