Arun Tewari and Others v Zila Mansavi Shikshak Sangh and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1276; (1998) 2 SCC 332; (1998) SCC (L&S) 541; AIR 1998 SC 331; JT 1997 (9) SC 593; 1998 LabIC 414; 1998 (1) LLN 368; 1997(7) SCALE 461; 1998 (1) SLR 219; 1998 (1) SLT 229; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 604; 1997 (10) Supreme 281

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Tort

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Non-gazetted Class III Education Service (Non-Collegiate Service) Recruitment and Promotion Rules, 1973, rr. 10(3), proviso (inserted in 1993), Sch. 3 - Recruitment of Asst. teachers - Prescribing higher qualification - Basic Training Certificate or a B. Ed degree prescribed qualification - Held, since Montessory and Mahila Bal Sevika Prasikshan Pramanpatras and Diploma T are not equivalent qualifications - Not discriminatory and does not violate arts. 14 and 16.
