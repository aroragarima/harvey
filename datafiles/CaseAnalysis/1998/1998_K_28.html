Sankaranarayanan Potti (Dead) By Lrs v K. Sreedevi and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1583; (1998) 3 SCC 751; AIR 1998 SC 1808; JT 1998 (2) SC 637; 1998(2) SCALE 476; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 444; 1998 (4) SLT 48; 1998 (3) Supreme 320

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Land & Property - Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964, s.2(22) and 125(1), Proviso - Code of Civil Procedure,1908, ss.9 and 11 - Determination of status of tenancy in regard to civil suit pending on or before coming into force of Amending - Tribunal ordered issuing of purchase certificate to party - Jurisdiction and res judicata questioned - Held, without jurisdiction and nullity - Further held that question of res judicata does not arises - Appeal allowed.
