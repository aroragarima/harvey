Nirma Industries Ltd. v Director General Of Investigation & Registration

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1411; (1997) 5 SCC 279; AIR 1997 SC 2382; [1997] 89 Comp Cas 537; 1997 (2) CPJ 10; 1997 (5) CTJ 161; JT 1997 (5) SC 481; 1997(4) SCALE 114; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 137

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Media & Entertainment; Tort

Summary: Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1969, s.36 - Restrictive Trade Practice - Proof - Conclusion of Commission against company merely based on complaint of D. G. Stating that company while offering prize scheme raised prices in transaction as whole - Denial by company stating rise in price otherwise is justified - Held, opportunity of being heard be given once to company - To determine whether any loss or injury caused to consumers by eliminating or restricting competition or otherwise - Remitted to Commission.
