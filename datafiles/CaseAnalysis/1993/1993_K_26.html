K.R. Raveendranathan v State of Kerala and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 550; (1996) 10 SCC 35

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Jurisdiction of arbitrator - Held, in view of conflict between ratio in Sudarsan Trading Co v. Govt. of Kerala [1989 Indlaw SC 463] and case of Associated Engg. Co. case [1991 Indlaw SC 1020] it is expedient that matter may be referred to a three-Judge Bench for decision - Order accordingly.
