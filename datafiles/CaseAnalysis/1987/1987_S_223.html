Prabhakaran Nair, Etc. v State Of Tamil Nadu And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28180; (1987) 4 SCC 238; AIR 1987 SC 2117; JT 1987 (3) SC 492; 1988 (1) RCJ 113; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 384; 1987 (2) RentLR 743; 1987(2) SCALE 469; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 21 and 32 - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, ss. 14(a), 14(b) and 16 - Tenancy - Eviction of tenants for repairs and for reconstruction of property - Provision for tenant's re-induction made in case of repairs, but not in case reconstruction - (A) Whether it render s. 14 (1)(b) of Act, discriminatory - (B) Consideration of Rent control legislation in light of fundamental right to shelter - Held, not discriminatory as comparison with Rent Acts of other States - Urgent need of National Housing Policy emphasised and examination of idea of National Rent Tribunal with quicker procedure, suggested - Petition dismissed.
