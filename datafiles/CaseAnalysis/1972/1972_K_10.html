Senior Superintendent, R.M.S Cochin and Another v K. V. Gopinath, Sorter

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 112; (1973) SCC (L&S) 277; (1973) 3 SCC 867; AIR 1972 SC 1487; 1973 (26) FLR 178; 1972 KLT 315; 1972 LabIC 826; 1972 (1) LLJ 486; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 530; 1972 SLR 390

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Central Civil Services (Temporary Service) Rules, 1965, r. 5(1), Proviso to sub-r.(b) - Service matter - Termination of temporary Govt. servant - Non-payment of amount due to him for notice period - Held, termination is ineffective - Appeal dismissed.
