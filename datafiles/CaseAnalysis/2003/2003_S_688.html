Ganga Retreat and Towers Limited and another v State of Rajasthan and others

Supreme Court of India

19 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1505; (2003) 12 SCC 91; 2004 (2) AWC 1117; JT 2003 (10) SC 232; 2003(10) SCALE 898; [2003] Supp6 S.C.R. 1134

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Frustration, Promisee, Promisor, Rajasthan Municipalities Act, 1959

Summary: Land & Property - Contract & Commercial - Constitution - Urban Land Ceiling and Regulation Act, ss. 4, 6, 8, 15 and 38 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, ss. 2, 10, 18, 19, 20, 21, 22 and 56 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Respondent No. 1 issued advertisement for auction of two prime properties describing them as freehold, ceiling free and vacant - Appellants were declared as the successful bidder in the auction held - Payment was made, thereafter, the sale deed was executed and registered
