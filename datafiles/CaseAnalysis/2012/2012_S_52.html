Secretary, Ministry of Defence and others v Prabhash Chandra Mirdha

Supreme Court of India

29 May 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Balbir Singh Chauhan, Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 636; 2012 (4) AWC 3989; 2012 (4) SLR 722

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Constitution of India-Article 311-Employment-Disciplinary proceedings-Initiation-Quashability of charge-sheet-Authority subordinate to appointing authority can initiate disciplinary proceedings or issue charge memo-Law does not permit quashing of charge-sheet in routine manner-Unless delay created prejudice to delinquent employee-Disciplinary proceedings not liable to be quashed on ground of having been initiated at belated stage-On facts, held appeal not to be proceeded further on merit.
