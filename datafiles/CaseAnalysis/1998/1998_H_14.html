Supreme Court of India

22 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 781; (1998) 6 SCC 328; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1485; AIR 1998 SC 2483; 1998 (3) AWC 1880; JT 1998 (5) SC 127; 1998 (2) LLJ 821; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 854; 1999 (1) SLJ 162; 1998 (4) SLR 621

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 16 and 309 - U P Palika (Centralised) Service Retirement Benefit Rules, 1981, rr. 3(2) and 3(3) - Whether Rules can be called discriminatory on the ground that retiral benefits thereunder are not available to employees retiring prior to coming into force of Rules - Held, not discriminatory - Appeal dismissed.
