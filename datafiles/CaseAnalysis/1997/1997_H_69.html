Tayabbhai M. Bagasarwalla & Another v Hind Rubber Industries Private Limited Etc.

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 842; (1997) 3 SCC 443; AIR 1997 SC 1240; 1997 (4) Bom.C.R. 312; 1997 (2) CLT 1; JT 1997 (2) SC 699; 1997 (2) MahLJ 1; 1997 (1) RentLR 399; 1997(2) SCALE 205; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 152; 1997 (2) Supreme 395

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ex-Parte, Court Of Limited Jurisdiction, Ex-Parte Order

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O. 39, r. 2A, s. 9A - Disobedience of Interim order of injunction passed in suit - Liability for violation of orders in suit which lateron Civil Court found to have no jurisdiction to entertain - Held, still they can be punished - Appeal allowed.
