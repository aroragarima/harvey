Mahendra Nath Das Alias Gobinda Das v State of Assam

Supreme Court of India

14 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 550; (1999) 5 SCC 102; (1999) SCC (Cr) 664; AIR 1999 SC 1926; 1999 (39) AllCriC 270; 1999 CRLJ 2873; JT 1999 (4) SC 35; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 729

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 354(3) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Whether accused is liable to capital punishment as case is suitable to fall in category of rarest of rare case? - Held, in view of fact and circumstances of case, murder committed by accused is cold - blooded, gruesome and heinous and justifiably falls in category of rarest of rare case, hence he is liable to be awarded death sentence.
