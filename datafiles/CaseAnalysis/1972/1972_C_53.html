M. K. Brothers Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Kanpur

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 265; (1973) 3 SCC 30; AIR 1973 SC 524; 1972 CTR 357; 1973 (1) ITJ 382; [1972] 86 ITR 38; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 1077; 1973 TAXLR 371

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Sole Selling Agency

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss.4 and 10 - Expenditure incurred for the purpose of acquiring the sole selling agency right - Nature of - Capital or revenue - Payment is made periodically - Held, constitutes capital expenditure even though payment is made periodically - Appeal dismissed.
