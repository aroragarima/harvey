Haryana State Electricity Board v Naresh Tanwar and Another

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3191; (1996) 8 SCC 23; (1996) SCC (L&S) 816; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 349; 1996 (72) FLR 819; JT 1996 (2) SC 542; 1996 (1) LLJ 1066; 1996(2) SCALE 374; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 69; 1996 (2) SLR 11; 1996 (2) Supreme 552; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 843

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether respondent was eligible to be appointed on compassionate ground? - Compassionate appointment cannot be granted after a long lapse of reasonable period - Its object is to relieve immediate hardship and distress caused to family by sudden demise of earning member of family and such consideration cannot be kept binding for years - Held, Haryana State Electricity Board will consider application of respondent with such sympathy as applicant may deserve in facts of case - Appeals allowed.
