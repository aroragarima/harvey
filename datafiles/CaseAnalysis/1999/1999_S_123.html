Municipal Council, Ahmednagar and Another v Shah Hyder Beig and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 625; (2000) 2 SCC 48; AIR 2000 SC 671; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 678; 2000 (1) CLT 261; JT 1999 (10) SC 336; 2000(1) SCALE 124; 2000 (1) SLT 255; 1999 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 37; [1999] Supp5 S.C.R. 197; 2000 (1) Supreme 146

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Equitable Doctrine

Summary: Land & Property - Limitation Act, 1908 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6 - Maharashtra Regional Town Planning Act, 1966, s. 126(4) - Land acquisition - Writ petition challenging the acquisition - After 21 years from the date of notification and 16 years from the date of making over possession and award - Sustainability of - Held, High Court has dealt with matter on a totally different perspective and as such clearly fell into an error in passing order as impugned in the present appeal - Not only length of time but concept of approbation and reprobation has totally been ignored at HC stage - Appeal allowed.
