Haryana Urban Development Authority v Darsh Kumar, Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 589; (2005) 9 SCC 449; 2004 CCC 126; 2004 (3) CPJ(Sc) 34; JT 2004 (6) SC 400; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 780; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 43

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Land & Property

Summary: Consumer Protection - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Allotment of plots - Not offered possession due to some reason or other - National Commission, granting interest at rate of 18% per annum irrespective of fact of each case - Fact of case was that allotment was made far back and possession was given very late, whether compensation is to be granted? - Held, considering very long period during which no possession was given, on an ad hoc basis, directed that for mental agony/harassment and for increase in costs of construction, compensation at rate of 12% from date of deposit till date of possession be awarded - Appeals Disposed Of.
