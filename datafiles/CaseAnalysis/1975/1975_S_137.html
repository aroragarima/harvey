State of Assam and Another v J. N. Roy Biswas

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 359; (1976) SCC (L&S) 10; (1976) 1 SCC 234; AIR 1975 SC 2277; 1975 (31) FLR 355; 1975 LabIC 1681; 1976 (2) LLJ 17; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 128; 1976 SLJ 1; 1975 UJ 901

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 - Delinquent exculpated after disciplinary enquiry and reinstated - When power regarding re-opening of proceedings is avilable? - Held, Govt. can re-open proceedings only if rules vest some such revisory power - Appeal dismissed.
