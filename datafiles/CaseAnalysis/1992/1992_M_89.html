Modern Food Industries (India) Limited and Another v Sachidanand Dass and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 625; (1995) Supp4 SCC 465

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Reinstatement - Legality - Single Judge of High Court quashed order of termination and directed reinstatement and payment of back salary - Division Bench did not take up appeal of appellant against reinstatement nor considered prayer for interlocutory stay - Meanwhile, respondent filed a contempt - Under threat of proceedings of contempt, appellants had to comply with order of Single Judge notwithstanding pendency of their appeal and application for stay - Petitioners are confronted with a position where their stay application is virtually rendered infructuous by steps they had to take on threat of contempt - Held, directed that all further proceedings in contempt proceedings be stayed - It will be appropriate for High Court to take up and dispose of application for stay without reference to developments in interregnum, namely, that respondent had to obey order of learned Single Judge under pain of proceedings of contempt - Depending upon outcome of appellants' application for stay, further question whether or not reinstatement should be reversed would arise - Appeal disposed of.
