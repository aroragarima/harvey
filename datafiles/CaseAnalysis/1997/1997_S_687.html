State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ajay Kumar

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 833; (1997) 4 SCC 88; (1997) SCC (L&S) 902; 1997 (3) AWC 1656; 1997 (76) FLR 85; JT 1997 (3) SC 219; 1997 (1) LLJ 1204; 1998 (1) LLN 320; 1997(2) SCALE 340; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 102; 1998 (1) SLJ 164; 1997 (2) SLR 234; 1997 (2) Supreme 756

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Daily wager - Regularisation - Single Bench held no statutory rule under which respondent could be regularised - Held, Division Bench was erred in directing appellant to regularise service of respondent to post as and when vacancy arises and to continue him until then - Order of Single Judge upheld - Appeal allowed.
