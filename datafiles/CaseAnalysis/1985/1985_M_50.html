State of Punjab v Mohinder Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 478; (1986) 1 SCC 365; AIR 1987 SC 758; 1985(1) SCALE 1319

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.23(2) and 28 - Maintainability of appeal for determination of interest on compensation for acquisition of land - Held, no merit in appeal so regards to quantum of compensation - Owner of land acquired is entitled to 30% solatium from date of publication of notification and interest at rate of 9% and to interest at 9% towards which he already got 6% - Appeal dismissed.
