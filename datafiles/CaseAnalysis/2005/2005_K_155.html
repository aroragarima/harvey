Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, o.5, rr. 1, 5, o. 8, r. 1, s. 122 - CPC(Amendment) Act, 1999 - CPC(Amendment) Act, 1976 - Scope for granting extension of time beyond period of 90 days to file written statement - Court granted time to file written statement by 19-02-2004 and that being a holiday written statement was filed on 20-02-2004 - Refused to accept - Held, unless compelled by express and specific language of Statute, provisions of CPC or any other procedural enactment ought not to be construed in a manner which would leave court helpless to meet extraordinary situations to do justice - Provision does not deal with power of court and also does not specifically take away power of court to take written statement on record filed beyond time as provided for - Appeals allowed.
