Burn Standard Company Limited And Anr. v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 200; (1991) 3 SCC 467; AIR 1991 SC 1784; 1991 CRLR 573; 1993 CrLR(SC) 573; 1991 (44) DLT 645; 1991 (35) ECR 289; 1992 (60) E.L.T. 671; JT 1991 (3) SC 108; 1991(2) SCALE 58; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 960; 1991 (2) UJ 260

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 3 and 4 - Levy of excise duty - Wagon manufactured and supplied to Railways - Free supply of certain components by purchaser - Held, excise duty leviable on normal value of wagon, which included value of free supply items - Appeal dismissed.
