State of Rajasthan and Others v Khemraj and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3544; (2000) 9 SCC 241; AIR 2000 SC 1759

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 65 and 75 - Secondary evidence in respect of a map - Application seeking permission to lead secondary evidence u/s. 65 of the Evidence Act was not supported by any affidavit - Averments vague - Trial Court rejected the application filed u/s. 65 - No fault can be found with the orders of the Trial Court or High Court - Appellant permitted to file a fresh application to lead secondary evidence supported by a proper affidavit - Appeal disposed of.
