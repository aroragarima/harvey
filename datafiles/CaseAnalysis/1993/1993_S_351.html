Laxmi Video Theatres and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1225; (1993) 3 SCC 715; AIR 1993 SC 2328; 1993 (2) CCC 604; JT 1993 (4) SC 218; 1993(3) SCALE 103; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 159; 1993 (2) UJ 313

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Media & Entertainment

Keywords: Punjab Entertainment Duty Act, 1955, Punjab Cinemas (Regulations) Act, 1952

Summary: Media & Entertainment - Punjab Cinemas (Regulations) Act, 1952, s. 2(a) - Cinematograph Act, 1952, s. 2(c) - Interpretation of expression 'Cinematograph' in Central Act and State Act for licencing to run video parlour which is necessary under State Act and Rules - Held, includes Video Cassette Recorder and Video Cassette Player - Appeal dismissed.
