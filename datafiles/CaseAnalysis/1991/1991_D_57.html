State Of Sikkim v Dorjee Tshering Bhutia And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 1035; (1991) 4 SCC 243; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1095; AIR 1991 SC 1933; 1991 (17) ATC 332; JT 1991 (3) SC 456; 1991 LabIC 1934; 1993 (3) LLJ 47; 1991 (2) LLN 818; 1991(2) SCALE 378; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 633; 1991 (5) SLR 82; 1992 (1) UJ 68

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 162, 166(3), 246 and 309 - Sikkim State Civil Service Rules, 1977, r. 4(3) - Scope of executive power of Govt. as to recruitment to civil services when statutory provisions were unworkable and inoperative - Held, Govt. can exercise its executive power - Appeal allowed.
