National Insurance Co. Ltd. v Sujir Ganesh Nayak And Company And Anr

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1108; (1997) 4 SCC 366; AIR 1997 SC 2049; [1997] 89 Comp Cas 131; 1997 CPJ 1; JT 1997 (4) SC 179; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 17; 1997(3) SCALE 228; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 202

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s.28 - Contract of insurance - Clause in policy - Providing shorter period contrary to law for filing of claim from occurrence - Covers losses due to riot and strike - Information to insurer by insured about strike - Filing suit after limitation period, whether - Held, not hit by s.28 - Clause not curtails limitation - Suit time barred.
