N. Mohanan v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1564; (1997) 2 SCC 556; (1997) SCC (L&S) 745; AIR 1997 SC 1896; 1997 (2) ESC 1075; JT 1997 (1) SC 475; 1997(1) SCALE 278; 1997 (1) SCJ 161; 1997 (2) SLJ 76; 1997 (1) SLR 522; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 920; 1997 (4) Supreme 26

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16, 226 and 309 - Kerala Secretariat Subordinate Service Special Rules, r. 7 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Service Rules - Post of Legal Asst. - Whether mere inclusion of name in list of selected candidates or appointment by interim order which is subject to outcome of final adjudication, confers any right to be appointed - Held, does not confer/ create any right to be appointed - Petition dismissed.
