Â Indrapuri Griha Nirman Sahakari Samiti Ltd. v The State Of Rajasthan & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, Y.V. Chandrachud, A. Alagiriswami, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 145; (1975) 4 SCC 296; AIR 1974 SC 2085; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 68; 1974 UJ 589

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Rajasthan Land Acquisition Act, 1953 - Effect of laches - Writ petition - Challenging notifications u/ss. 4, 6 and 9 of 1953 Act - Which were filed after 9 years from the date of the declaration of s. 6 - Held, rightly dismissed on the ground of delay as in such cases length of the delay is an important circumstance - Appeal Dismissed.
