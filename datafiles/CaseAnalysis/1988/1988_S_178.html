P. Ganeshwar Rao and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 480; (1988) Supp SCC 740; (1989) SCC (L&S) 123; AIR 1988 SC 2068; 1988 (8) ATC 957; JT 1988 (3) SC 570; 1989 LabIC 46; 1988 (2) LLN 991; 1988(2) SCALE 588; 1988 (4) SLR 548; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 805; 1988 (2) UJ 697

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: AP Panchayat Raj Engineering Service (Special) Rules 1963

Summary: Service - Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Engineering Service (Special) Rules 1963 - Constitution of India, 1950 - Service Rules - Appeal by special leave against Tribunal's direction to the State Govt. to refrain from making any direct recruitment against temporary vacancies in the cadre of Assistant Engineers under the 1963 Rules - Determination of vacancies prior to amendment made on 28-04-1980 which amended r. 2 Expln.(C) is prospective - Held, direct recruitment of 371/2% can be both against temporary and substantive vacancies - Appeal disposed of.
