State Of Tamil Nadu v M/S Burmah Shell Oil Storage & Distributingco. Of India Ltd

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 205; (1973) SCC (Tax) 269; (1973) 3 SCC 511; AIR 1973 SC 1045; 1973 (2) CTR 20; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 636; 1973 (31) STC 426; 1973 TAXLR 1739

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 2(e),2(g) and 2(d) - Assessee Oil Co. - Sale of advertisement materials, Canteen sales and periodical sale of scrap - (A) Taxability - Transactions covered by Act prior to 1964 amendnment, which has no retrospective operation - (B) Determination of materiality of motive to make gain or profit - Held, are not liable to Sales Tax - Further held, is immaterial - Appeal partly allowed.
