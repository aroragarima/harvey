Executive Engineer, Bhadrak and Others v Rangadhar Mallik

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1189; (1993) SCC (L&S) 276; (1993) Supp1 SCC 763; 1993 (23) ATC 807; 1993 (1) GLH 393; JT 1992 (5) SC 364; 1993 LabIC 425; 1993 (1) UPLBEC 58

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Service - Orissa General Financial Rules, r. 65 - Date of birth - Correction - Whether respondent was not afforded an opportunity of personal hearing before rejecting his representation? - R. 65 stipulates that representations made for correction of date of birth near about time of superannuation shall not be admitted - Held, representation as well as documents furnished by respondent were considered by Governor and thereafter his representation was rejected - It cannot be said that such action taken by Govt. was in any manner illegal or against any principles of natural justice - There was no requirement of any law to give any personal hearing to respondent before dismissing his representation - Appeal allowed.
