Kondiba Rama Papal @ Shirke (Dead) By His Heirs and LRs. and Another v Narayan Kondiba Papal

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 470; (1991) 2 SCC 218; AIR 1991 SC 1180; 1991 (1) CCC 399; JT 1991 (5) SC 121; 1991 (1) UJ 480

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956.

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, s. 10 - Adoption - Bombay State - Custom in concerned area permitted adoption of child at any age - Whether adoption can be challenged on ground that child was above 15 years at time of adoption? - Held, once factum of adoption is proved it cannot be challenged on that ground - Appeal dismissed.
