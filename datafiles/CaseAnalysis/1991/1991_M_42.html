Workmen Represented By Secretary v Management Of Reptakos Brett.And Co. Ltd. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 343; (1992) SCC (L&S) 271; (1992) 1 SCC 290; AIR 1992 SC 504; 1992 (1) CompLJ 340; 1991 (63) FLR 928; JT 1991 (4) SC 243; 1992 LabIC 289; 1992 (1) LLJ 340; 1991 (2) LLN 1166; 1992 (2) MLJ(SC) 16; 1991(2) SCALE 940; 1992 (1) SLJ 34; 1992 (1) SLR 219; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 129; 1992 (1) UJ 308

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 3, item 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Minimum Wages Act, 1948, Pre - Fixation of wage structure - (A) Norms fo fixation of minimum wages - (B) Validity of calculations based on 30 to 40 years old wage structure - (C) Validity of restructuring scheme to prejudice of workmen - Held, minimum wage is not just contract, must be tested on anvil of social justice - Not proper, must be in context of present situation - Cannot be allowed - Supreme Court can interfere - Appeal allowed.
