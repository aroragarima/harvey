State Bank of Hyderabad v Town Municipal Council

Supreme Court of India

01 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 892; (2007) 1 SCC 765; 2006 (68) ALR 332; 2007 (1) ALT 23; 2007 (1) AWC 627; 2007 (145) PLR 332; 2007 (1) RCR(Civil) 415; 2006(13) SCALE 332; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 919

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure - Suit related to ownership of a plot - Application for amendment of plaint - Whether the proviso appended to O. 6 r. 17 is applicable in the instant case? - Held, High Court relied upon the said proviso and opined that having regard thereto the plaintiff was obligated to establish that in spite of due diligence it could not have raised the matter before commencement of the trial of the suit - High Court evidently committed an illegality in relying upon the said provision - Matter is remitted to the High Court - Appeal allowed.
