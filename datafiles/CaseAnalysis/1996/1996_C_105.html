Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited v Collector of Central Excise Ahmedabad

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 964; (1996) 5 SCC 488; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 61; AIR 1996 SC 2542; 1996 (66) ECR 1; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 177; JT 1996 (7) SC 44; 1996 (15) RLT 498; 1996(5) SCALE 501; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 183; 1996 (5) Supreme 689

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, item 6 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - Notification No. 187/61 - Availability of exemption from excise duty on raw naphtha used for production of ammonia and fertilisers - Held, raw naphtha used to produce ammonia is entitled to exemption - Appeal allowed.
