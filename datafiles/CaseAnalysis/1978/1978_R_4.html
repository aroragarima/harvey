R.G Anand v Messrs Delux Films and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.S. Pathak, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 294; (1978) 4 SCC 118; AIR 1978 SC 1613; 22 PTC 802; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 218

Case Digest

Subject: Intellectual Property

Keywords: Infringement Of Copyright

Summary: Intellectual Property - Copy Right Act, 1911, ss. 1 and 2 - Violation of copy right - Validity of the principles enunciated by High Court challenged - Held, two courts of fact, having considered entire evidence, circumstances and materials before them have come to a finding of fact that defendants committed no violation of the copyright, therefore not to disturb findings of fact - Appeal dismissed.
