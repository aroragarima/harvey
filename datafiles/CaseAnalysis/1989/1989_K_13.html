N. Abdul Basheer And Ors. Etc. Etc. v K.K. Karunakaran And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 620; (1989) Supp2 SCC 344; (1990) SCC (L&S) 153; AIR 1989 SC 1624; 1991 (17) ATC 160; 1989 (59) FLR 148; JT 1989 (2) SC 449; 1989 LabIC 1540; 1989 (2) LLJ 67; 1989 (2) LLN 14; 1989(1) SCALE 1473; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 201; 1989 (3) SLJ 91; 1989 (2) UJ 353

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Kerala Excise and Prohibition subordinate Service Rules

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 and 311 - Special Rules for Kerala Excise and Prohibition Subordinate Service, r. 2 - Promotion - Quota - Constitutionality of (Discriminatory, if ) - Prescription of ratio dividing quota between graduate and non graduate for promotion to post of Second Grade Excise Inspectors - Held, invalid as violative of arts. 14 and 16 - Appeal dismissed.
