Sat Pal v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 101; (1982) 1 SCC 12; (1982) SCC (Cr) 46; AIR 1981 SC 2230; 1981 CRLJ 1867; 1981 CRLR 610; 1981(3) SCALE 1760; 1982 UJ 35

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: COFEPOSA Act, 1974, ss. 8 and 11 - Constitution, art. 22(5) - Revocation of preventive detention - (A) Simultaneous represention by detenu to State and Central Government - Former is duty bound to forward it to to latter - Delay in forwarding - Effect - (B) Nature of power of Central Government - Held, not sufficient to invalidate detention - Held, is independent of power of confirming or setting aside an order of detention u/s. 8(f) - Petition Dismissed.
