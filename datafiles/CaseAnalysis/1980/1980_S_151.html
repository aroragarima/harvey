Kadra Pehadiya and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 48; (1981) 3 SCC 671; (1981) SCC (Cr) 791; AIR 1981 SC 939; 1981 (29) BLJR 300; 1981 CRLJ 481; 1981 CRLR 109

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Delay and procrastination of justicing process - Under-trial prisoners - Requirement of speedy trial - Petitioners have been a Pakud sub-jail for a period of about eight years without their trial having made any progress - Directions issued to High Court of Patna to make an inquiry and find out why it should have taken a period of 20 months for case of petitioners to be committed to Sessions Court and to submit a report - Directions to Sessions Judge to report immediately after disposal of case stating as to when he took up case for hearing and when he completed it - Directions issued to State of Bihar and Superintendent of Police also - Order accordingly.
