Asom Rastrabhasa Prachar Samiti v State Of Assam And Others

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, Kuldip Singh, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 234; (1989) 4 SCC 496; AIR 1989 SC 2126; JT 1989 (3) SC 699; 1989(2) SCALE 632; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 160; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 160

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Emergency Provisions, Assam Rashtrabhasa Prachar Samiti (Taking Over of Management & Control) Act 1984

Summary: Miscellaneous - Asom Rashtrabhasha Prachar Samiti (Taking over Management and Control) Act, 1984, s. 3 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 19(1)(c) - Asom Rashtrabhasha Prachar Samiti (Taking over Management and Control) Act, 1984 - Constitutional validity of Act - Notification taking over management of Prachar Samiti - Held, Act is ultra vires of Constitution - Liable to be quashed - Petition allowed.
