State of Tamil Nadu and Another v Adhiyaman Educational and Research Institute and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1631; (1995) 4 SCC 104; AIR 1995 SCW 2179; JT 1995 (3) SC 136; 1995(2) SCALE 401; 1995 (2) SCJ 367; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 1075; 1995 (3) SLR 752; 1995 (2) UPLBEC 937

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Madras University Act, 1923, Gujarat University Act, 1949, Tamil Nadu Private Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976

Summary: Education - Constitution (Forty-Second Amendment) Act, 1976 - All India Council For Technical Education Act, 1987 - University Grants Commission Act, 1956 - Gujarat University Act, 1949 - Madras University Act, 1923 - Tamil Nadu Private Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976 - Whether after the coming into force of the All India Council for Technical Education Act, 1987 (hereinafter referred to as the "Central Act") the State Govt. has power to grant and withdraw permission to start a technical institution
