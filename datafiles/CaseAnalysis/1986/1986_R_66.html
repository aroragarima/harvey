Rojasara Ramjibhai Dahyabhai v Jani Narottamdas Lallubhai (Dead)By Lrs. and

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 644; (1986) 3 SCC 300; AIR 1986 SC 1912; 1986 (2) CCC 557; 1986(1) SCALE 566; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 447

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Contingent Contract, Saurashtra Land Reforms Act, 1951

Summary: Contract and Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1877, s. 13 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 113 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 31 - Agreement for sale of land - Execution of sale deed - Precondition, permission to use it as village site - Suit for specific performance filed within 3 years after obtaining permission - If amounts to contingent contract or barred by limitation - Held, contract not contingent but specifically enforceable - Not barred by limitation - Appeal dismissed.
