Textile Machinery Corporation Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, P.S. Kailasam, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 225; (1977) 2 SCC 368; AIR 1977 SC 1134; 1977 CTR 151; 1977 (1) ITJ 337; [1977] 107 ITR 195; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 762

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 15C - New industrial undertaking - Company starting manufacture of goods - Purchased from outside through establishing new divisions - Major portion used by itself- Rest sold to outsiders - Held, new divisions were entitled to exemption u/s.15-C - Appeals Allowed.
