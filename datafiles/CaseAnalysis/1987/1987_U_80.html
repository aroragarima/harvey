Catering Cleaners of Southern Railway v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28478; (1987) SCC (L&S) 77; (1987) 1 SCC 700; AIR 1987 SC 777; 1987 (1) CCC 591; 1987 (54) FLR 476; JT 1987 (1) SC 376; 1987 LabIC 619; 1987 (1) LLJ 345; 1987 (1) LLN 480; 1987(1) SCALE 240; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 164; 1987 (2) SLJ 23; 1987 (1) UJ 560

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Hours Of Work, Catering, Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970

Summary: Practice & Procedure- Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, s. 10 - Contract labour - Employment of - Cleaners by Southern Railway through contractors - Satisfies all requirements to attract s. 10(2) of Act - Held, to restrain from employing contract labour - Directions to Govt. for taking appropriate action - Petition disposed of.
