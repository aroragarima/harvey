Messrs Variety Emporium v V. R. M. Mohd. Ibrahim Naina

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 243; (1985) 1 SCC 251; AIR 1985 SC 207; 1985 (1) CCC 459; 1985 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1985 (1) RCR(Rent) 185; 1984(2) SCALE 829; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 102; 1985 UJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution, art.136 - TN Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 10 - (A) Interference sought in an appeal by special leave to Supreme Court - In concurrent findings of lower courts which were shown to be manifestly unjust - (B) Tenancy - Eviction petition - Gound, bona fide need - Consideration of subsequent events, if - Held, it is not only the right but the duty of SC to remedy injustice - Held, shall be taken into consideration - Appeal allowed.
