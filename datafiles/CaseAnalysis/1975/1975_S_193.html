V. B. Badami and Others v State of Mysore and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 51; (1976) SCC (L&S) 353; (1976) 2 SCC 901; AIR 1980 SC 1561; 1980 LabIC 986; 1975 (2) LLJ 466; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 815; 1976 SLJ 244; 1975 (2) SLR 626

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Karnataka Administrative Service (Recruitment) Rules, 1957, r. 17(h) - Constitution, art. 309 - Service Rules - Computation of seniority - Assistant Commissioners Class-I (Junior Scale) - Principles to be followed as regard to quota rules for appointment by direct recruitment and by promotion - Held, principles given by court - Appeal Dismissed.
