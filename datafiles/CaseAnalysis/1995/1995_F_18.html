U.P. Avas Evam Vikas Parishad And Anr. v Friends Coop. Housing Societyltd. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 430; (1995) Supp3 SCC 456; AIR 1996 SC 114; 1995 (2) RentLR 33; 1995(3) SCALE 604; 1995 (2) SCJ 255; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 729; 1995 (2) UJ 274

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Socio Economic - UP Urban Planning and Development Act, 1973, s.59(1)(a) - Constitution, arts. 19(1)(e), 21 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - (A) Whether approval of State Govt. prior to notification u/ss. 28 or 32 of Adhiniyam is needed in regards to housing or improvement scheme - (B) Where does right to shelter springs from - Held, not needed - From the right to residence assured in art. 19 (1) (e) and right to life u/art. 21 of the Constitution - Appeal dismissed.
