Collector of Central Excise v Usha Martin Industries

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, B.N. Kirpal, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2986; (1997) 7 SCC 47; AIR 1997 SC 3871; AIR 1997 SCW 3795; 1997 (72) ECR 257; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 460; JT 1997 (7) SC 557; 1997 (22) RLT 221; 1997(5) SCALE 600; 1998 (111) STC 254; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 601; 1997 (8) Supreme 19

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 3 and 37 B - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1986, Tariff item 26 AA - Exemption by notification from excise duty which was already paid on raw material - Notifications before insertion of s. 37-B have same binding effect on Dept. - If manufacturers can claim benefit in respect of goods referred in Notifications - Held, entitled to claim - Appeal dismissed.
