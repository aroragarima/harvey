Alkapuri Co-Operative Housing Society Limited v Jayantibhai Naginbhai (Deceased) Thr.Lrs

Supreme Court of India

09 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 66; (2009) 3 SCC 467; AIR 2009 SC 1948; 2009 (76) ALR 601; 2009 (2) AWC 1607; 2009 (2) Bom.C.R. 42; 2009 (2) G.L.R. 1579; JT 2009 (2) SC 300; 2009 (2) RCR(Rent) 26; 2009(1) SCALE 642; [2009] 1 S.C.R. 157; 2009 (1) UC 411

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Amendment Of Pleading, Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act

Summary: Civil Procedure - Suit claiming to be an allottee in respect of plot by respondent - Application amendment to the plaint allowed by High Court - Appeal against - Held, an application for amendment of the plaint seeking to introduce a cause of action which had arisen during the pendency of the suit stands on a different footing than the one which had arisen prior to the date of institution of the suit - Plaintiff-respondents in their application for amendment of the plaint themselves accepted the fact that the appellant herein not only had filed a suit prior in point of time to the suit filed by the deceased respondent but had also obtained an injunction as a result whereof they did not obtain effective possession of the suit land - Plaintiff-respondents in effect and substance are seeking to alter the basic structure of the suit - Appeal allowed.
