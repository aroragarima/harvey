State Bank Of Saurashtra v Chitranjan Rangnath Raja And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.C. Gupta, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 150; (1980) 4 SCC 516; AIR 1980 SC 1528; 1980 ALJ 654; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 618; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 915; 1980 UJ 647

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Principal Debtor, Contract Of Guarantee

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, ss. 139 and 141 - CPC, 1908, s. 144, expln. - Commercial Contracts - CC facility by bank - On pledged goods and surety - Pledged goods lost due to bank's negligence - Suit - Decree varied or reversed in appeal by HC - Only court of first instance is competent to order restitution - Appeal to Supreme Court - For determination of surety's position - And for grant of restitution - Surety, held discharged to the extent of security lost - Reversal decree of HC affirmed - Restitution cannot be granted by SC - Appeal Dismissed.
