Land Acquisition And Anr. Etc. v Smt. Jasti Rohini And Anr. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1151; (1995) 1 SCC 717; AIR 1995 SCW 823; JT 1995 (2) SC 339; 1995 (1) RentLR 215; 1994(5) SCALE 75; 1995 (1) SCJ 139; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 232

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4(1), 23(1) and 24 - Stamp Act, s. 47 A - (A) Whether for award of compensation, reliance upon basic valuation register maintained by municipalities on basis of notification issued by Govt. u/s. 47 A correct? - (B) What are basis for determination of market value of land acquired? - (C) Whether market value should be determined on basis of its potentiality for use for building purposes? - Held, (A) No - Notification u/s. 47 A is meant to be a guide for collection of revenue and cannot form basis for determination of market value of land u/s. 23(1) and fixation of market value on that basis for basic valuation register is illegal and unsustainable - (B) reasonable method to determine market value of acquired land is on evidence of transactions of bona fide sales of acquired land and market value prevailing as on date of notification u/s. 4(1) alone should be considered - (C) existence or absence of building activities towards acquired land or in neighbourhood thereof should be taken into consideration in determining compensation on basis of potential value of land - Appeals allowed.
