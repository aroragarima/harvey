H. M. T. Limited v Labour Court, Ernakulam and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 661; (1994) 2 SCC 38; (1994) SCC (L&S) 639; 1994 (26) ATC 904; 1994 (1) LLN 514; 1993 (8) SLR 539

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Dismissal of workman more than 14 years ago - High Court reinstated workman with full back wages - Held, no party should suffer on account of delay in decision by court - Further held, instead of full back wages, workman is to be given 60 per cent of back wages till reinstatement - Appeal disposed of.
