S.K.Saha v Prem Prakesh Agarwal

Supreme Court of India

23 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, R.M. Sahai, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1389; (1994) 1 SCC 431; (1994) SCC (L&S) 574; AIR 1994 SC 745; 1994 (26) ATC 607; 1994 (1) CLR 121; 1994 (68) FLR 90; JT 1993 (6) SC 441; 1994 (1) LLJ 577; 1993(4) SCALE 503; 1994 (1) SLJ 100; 1994 (1) SLR 37; 1994 (1) UJ 109; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 1082

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 311(1) - Service matter - Notional seniority from retrospective date - Illegal - Transfer of function of Deptt. of Govt. to Govt. Corporation - Option given - Employee joined Corporation - Non-gazetted post on ad hoc basis - But again appointed in service of Govt. - Period during which he was serving in Corporation - Whether it can be counted towards his seniority - Held, that period cannot be counted - Appeal Dismissed.
