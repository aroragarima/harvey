Medical Council of India v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K.T. Thomas, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1037; (1998) 6 SCC 131; AIR 1998 SC 2423; AIR 1998 SCW 2418; 1998 (3) CLT 58; JT 1998 (5) SC 40; 1998(4) SCALE 161; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 740; 1998 (5) SLR 4; 1998 (6) SLT 140; 1998 (6) Supreme 20

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 1993, University Grants Commission

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 254 - Medical Council Act, 1956, ss. 10A, 10B and 33 - Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976, s. 53(10) - Karnataka Educational Institutions (Prohibitions of Capitation Fee) Act, 1984, s. 4 - Dentist Act, 1948, s. 10A - Education - Admission - Medical College - Held, Medical council only competent for fixation of admission capacity - State Govt. and Universities cannot interfere with.
