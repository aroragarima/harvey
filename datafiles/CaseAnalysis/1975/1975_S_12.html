Uttar Pradesh Government v Sabir Hussain

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 182; (1975) SCC (L&S) 401; (1975) 4 SCC 703; AIR 1975 SC 2045; 1975 LabIC 1493; 1975 (2) LLJ 93; 1975 SLJ 525; 1975 (2) SLR 267; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 354; 1975 UJ 446

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Government of India Act 1935

Summary: Government of India Act, 1935, s. 240(3) - Constitution, art. 311(2) - Service Rules - (A) Protection afforded by s. 240(3) and art. 311(2) are substantially same - Reference to dismissal in s. 240 - Inference - (B) 'Reasonable opportunity' - Test - Report, findings and 'comments' of Enquiring Officer not supplied to delinquent employee - Effect - Held, includes removal - Order of removal held illegal - Appeal Dismissed.
