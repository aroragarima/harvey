(1) Meerut Development Authority; (2) Pawan Kumar v (1) Association of Management Studies and Another;

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

B. SUDARSHAN REDDY, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 527; (2009) 6 SCC 171; 2009 (5) ADJ 157; AIR 2009 SC 2894; 2009 (5) ALJ 506; 2009 (3) AWC 2558; 2009 (2) BC 551; 2009 (4) MLJ(SC) 818; 2009(6) SCALE 49; [2009] 6 S.C.R. 663

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Appeals Disposed Of, Uttar Pardesh Urban Planning and Development Act, 1973

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Tender - Uttar Pardesh Urban Planning and Development Act, 1973, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Meerut Development Authority - Allotment of lands by invitation of tender - Association of Management Studies(AMS), a society for managing educational institutions, had been allotted certain land in one Residential Scheme at a specific rate for educational purpose - Request of additional lands of two specific measurements for its educational establishments - Tenders invited - Reserved prices fixed, for such two areas which were at variance - Tender submitted by AMS - For one of the area for which reserved price was higher, tender for it was submitted at lower price by AMS - One of the tender was accepted and for other MDA sent a letter giving one week's time for reconsideration - AMS in clear and categorical terms stated that, '..the other land may be deleted from the offer as the cost of that land is not viable for..' - Allotment of the other land to AMS - After some time MDA got issued advertisement inviting applications for allotment of said plot inviting bids from foreign direct investors, building developers etc. for housing purposes with more higher reserved price per sq/m. - Invitation challenged by AMS on various grounds - Challenges made whether acceptable? - Held, No - Court observed that in all fairness matter should have been ended at the time when AMS has specifically and categorically asked for deletion of the said land and accepted the allotment of other land - Appropriate directions issued - Appeals disposed of.
