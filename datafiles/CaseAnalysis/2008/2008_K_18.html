K. M. Mishra v Central Bank Of India & Ors

Supreme Court of India

16 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Aftab Alam, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1475; (2008) 9 SCC 120; AIR 2009 SC 1114; JT 2008 (10) SC 357; 2008(12) SCALE 565; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 534; 2009 (1) SLR 1; 2009 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 392; 2009 (1) UPLBEC 69

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Promotion to the post of Chief Manager, Rajbhasha in Senior Manager Grade, Scale IV in the Central Bank of India - Held, Clause 3 of the Guidelines provides that in case the concerned officer does not submit his self-appraisal within one month of receiving the format, the Reporting Authority would have the right to submit his report concerning the officer to the Reviewing Authority - No substance or merit in the appellant's objections regarding his Performance Appraisal Ratings for the three years in question - Appeal dismissed.
