Jai Singh B. Chauhan v Punjab National Bank and others

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 420; (2005) 6 SCC 262; (2005) SCC (L&S) 830; AIR 2005 SC 3134; 2005 (3) AWC 2961; 2006 (3) Bom.C.R. 467; 2005 (4) ESC 557; 2005 (106) FLR 814; JT 2005 (6) SC 272; 2005(5) SCALE 531; 2005 (5) SLR 288; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 743; 2005 (5) Supreme 113; 2005 (3) UPLBEC 2427

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Service

Keywords: Bank (Employees) Pension Regulations, 1995

Summary: [A.] Banking & Finance - Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1970, s. 19(2) - Bank (Employees) Pension Regulations, 1995, regns. 3(3)(b), 3(3)(c), 5 - Punjab National Bank (Employees) Pension Regulations, 1995 - Bank's Pension Scheme - Appellant had not exercised option within time stipulated in scheme - Belated application rejected - Appeal against rejection - Held, appellant not entitled to get benefits of scheme - Appeal dismissed.
