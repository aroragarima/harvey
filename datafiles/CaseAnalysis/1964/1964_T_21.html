Tata Oil Mills Company Limited v Its Workmen

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 139; AIR 1965 SC 155; 1964 (9) FLR 142; 1964 (2) LLJ 113; 1965 (1) SCJ 281; [1964] 7 S.C.R. 555

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Workman was charged with assault charges, which were not private in nature - He was dismissed - On appeal, Industrial Tribunal directed reinstatement - Challenged - Held, domestic enquiry was properly held and fairly conducted and conclusions of fact reached by Enquiry Officer are based on evidence which he accepted as true - Appeal allowed.
