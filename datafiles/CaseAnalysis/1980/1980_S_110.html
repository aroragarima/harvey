Gopalanachari v State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 234; (1980) Supp SCC 649; (1981) SCC (Cr) 546; AIR 1981 SC 674; 1981 CRLJ 337; 1981 CrLR(SC) 94; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 1271

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Tort

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 110 - Constitution, 1950, arts. 21 and 39A - Appellant illegally detained u/s. 110 of CrPC - Held, appellant be released and any such victim should be defended at State expense and isued show cause notice to respondent to furnish the total number of prisoners kept in custody u/s. 110 CrPC - Petition allowed.
