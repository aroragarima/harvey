Union of India and Another v N. P. Thomas

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 629; (1993) SCC (L&S) 237; (1993) Supp1 SCC 704; AIR 1993 SC 1605; 1993 (23) ATC 775; 1993 (1) CLR 901; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 220; 1993 LabIC 89; 1993 (1) LLJ 1063; 1993 (1) LLN 3; 1992(2) SCALE 657; 1992 (5) SLR 600; 1992 (2) UJ 684

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 proviso, 311 - Telegraphic Engineering Service (Group B Post) Recruitment Rules, 1981, r. 1 - Sustainability of Order of transfer on Promotion - Authorities stating that accommodation of employee to original circle not possible as several officers who have completed tenure postings, were waiting to be posted there - Held, order not invalid, cannot be quashed - Appeal allowed.
