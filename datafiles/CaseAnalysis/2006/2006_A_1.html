A. Geetha v State of Tamil Nadu and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 465; (2006) 7 SCC 603; AIR 2006 SC 3053; JT 2006 (8) SC 389; 2006 (4) RCR(Criminal) 300; 2006(9) SCALE 6; [2006] Supp5 S.C.R. 724

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Human Rights; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Bootleggers, Drug Offenders, Forest Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, Slum Grabbers and Video Pirates Act, 1982, s. 3(2) - IPC, 1860, s. 366 - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 22 - Detention order challenged - Held, on basis of materials before detaining authority came to conclusion that there is likelihood of detenu being released on bail - That is his subjective satisfaction based on materials - Detaining authority has indicated as to why he was of opinion that there is likelihood of detenu being released on bail - Appeal dismissed.
