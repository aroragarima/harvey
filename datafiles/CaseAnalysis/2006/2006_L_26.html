Leader Engineering Works v Commissioner of Central Excise, Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

02 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 672; (2006) 9 SCC 290; 2006 (197) E.L.T. 469; 2007 (212) E.L.T. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35 L(b) - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Additional Duties of Excise (Goods of Special Importance) Act, 1957 - Notification Nos. 70/77 CE and 64/95 CE - Whether appellant is entitled to benefit of exemption under Notification Nos 70/77 C E and 64/95 C E? - Held, leaving question of law open and in peculiar facts of present case - Appeals allowed to avoid passing of contradictory and inconsistent orders arising out of same/similar show cause notices based on similar set of materials.
