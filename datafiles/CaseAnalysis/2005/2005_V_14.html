Binny Limited and Another v V. Sadasivan and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 501; (2005) 6 SCC 657; (2005) SCC (L&S) 881; AIR 2005 SC 3202; 2005 (3) AWC 2806; 2005 (3) CLR 218; 2005 (4) ESC 563; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 257; JT 2005 (7) SC 214; 2005 (4) KLT 315; 2005(6) SCALE 435; 2006 (2) SLR 25; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 421; 2005 (6) Supreme 214

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Public Sector, Gujarat University Act, 1949, Central Inland Water Transport Corporation Limited (Service, Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25F, s. 25N - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 32 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 23 - Writ of Mandamus - Termination of service from a private company - Whether writ can be issued against a private body? - Held, a writ of mandamus can be issued against a private body - There must be a public law element and it cannot be exercised to enforce purely private contracts entered into between parties - Unable to perceive any public element in termination of employees by appellant - Remedy available to respondents is to seek redressal of their grievance in civil law - Appeal allowed.
