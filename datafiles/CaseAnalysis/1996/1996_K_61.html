M.C. Mehta v Kamal Nath and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1476; (1997) 1 SCC 388; 1999 (4) CompLJ 44; JT 1996 (11) SC 467; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 12

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Enviornment, Wildlife & Animal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Whether the lease deed granted in favour of Span Motel can be quashed on the ground that activities of Motel resulted in degradation of environment? - Held, Span Motels interfered with the natural flow of the river by trying to block the natural relief or spill channel of river - Lease deed granted in favour of Motel should be quashed and State Govt. to take over the area to restore it to its original natural conditions - Motel to pay the compensation by way of costs for restitution of environment and ecology of the area - Petition disposed of.
