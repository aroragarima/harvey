S. Prakash and Another v K. M. Kurian and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1285; (1999) 5 SCC 624; (1999) SCC (L&S) 997; AIR 1999 SC 2094; 1999 (2) ESC 1531; 1999 (82) FLR 856; JT 1999 (3) SC 607; 1999 (3) LLN 424; 1999(3) SCALE 540; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 610; 1999 (6) SLT 65; 1999 (5) Supreme 479

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, r. 5, Note (3) (inserted by Amendment) Rules, 1992 - Kerala Agricultural Income Tax and Sales Tax Services (Special) Rules, r. 2 - Appointment - Post of Sales Tax Officer - Special Rules providing for filling up posts on basis of vacancies existing at time - Computing or calculating vacancies - Method of, not provided - Amendment provides for that - On basis of cadre strength - Held, special rule must give way to general rule.
