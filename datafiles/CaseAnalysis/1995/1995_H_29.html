Housing Board Of Havana v Havana Housing Board Employees Union And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1836; (1996) 1 SCC 95; (1996) SCC (L&S) 278; AIR 1996 SC 434; 1996 (1) CLR 399; 1996 (72) FLR 300; JT 1995 (8) SC 37; 1996 (1) LLJ 833; 1996 (1) LLN 1; 1995(6) SCALE 139; 1995 (5) SLR 729; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 533

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Maharashtra Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1963, Local Authorities Loans Act, 1914

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 - Whether the Haryana Housing Board is a 'Local Authority' within the meaning of s. 32(iv) of the Act.

Held, functions as are indicated in a Housing Scheme are essentially performed by Municipal Boards or Municipal Council which, undoubtedly, are 'Local Authorities' but on that analogy the Haryana Housing Board cannot be treated to be a 'local authority' as the extent of control of the State Govt. under which the Board has to function is so prominently pervasive that it is almost destructive of its independence which will also be apparent from the fact that in the matter of settlement of its Annual Programmes, Budget and Establishment schedule, the Board has to obtain the sanction of the State Govt. u/s. 24 of the Act. The supplementary budget and programme, if any, has also to be sanctioned by the State Govt. Appeal dismissed
