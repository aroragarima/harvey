Putti Kondala Rao & Ors v Vellamanchili Sitarattamma & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 299; (1976) 1 SCC 712; AIR 1976 SC 737; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 998; 1975 UJ 165

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 21, r. 90, Proviso - Petition for setting aside sale - Substantial injury not alledged - Trial court and the first appellate court addressed themselves at length on it - Held, allegations in petition could be read to imply substantial injury to judgment debtor - Appeal Dismissed.
