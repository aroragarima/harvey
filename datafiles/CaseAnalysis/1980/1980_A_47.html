Nasir Ahemed v Assistant Custodian General, Evacuee Property U.P., Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 136; (1980) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1980 SC 1157; 1980 ALJ 546; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 248; 1980 UJ 418

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Land & Property - Administration of Evacuee Property (Central) Rules, 1950, r. 6 - Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950, ss. 2, 7, 19 and 24 - Administration of Evacuee Property Ordinance, 1949 - Whether notice u/s. 7 and subsequent proceedings based on it was justified in law? - Held, it was essential to state particulars to enable appellant to answer case against him, hence ,notice did not comply with r. 6 - Therefore, could not provide a foundation for proceedings that followed - Appeal allowed.
