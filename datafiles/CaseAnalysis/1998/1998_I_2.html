Raunaq International Limited v I. V. R. Construction Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1819; (1999) 1 SCC 492; AIR 1999 SC 393; 1999 (1) ARBLR 431; JT 1998 (8) SC 411; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 421

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 226 - Govt. contract - Grant of - Court intervening and passing of interim orders - Petitioner, tenderers either having no requisite minimum qualification or having higher offer - Validity - Held, interim order to be moulded to provide for restitution - Petitioner should be bona fide - Court must carefully weigh conflicting public interests before interfering and passing interim orders - Not proper.
