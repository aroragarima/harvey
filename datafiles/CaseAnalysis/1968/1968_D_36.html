Durga Prashad v Chief Controller of Imports and Exports and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 352; (1969) 1 SCC 185; AIR 1970 SC 769; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 861

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 227 - Writ of mandamus and Certiorari - Laches - Even where there is an alleged breach of fundamental rights the grant of relief is discretionary - Exercise of discretion - Held, such discretion has to be exercised judiciously and reasonably - Appeal dismissed.
