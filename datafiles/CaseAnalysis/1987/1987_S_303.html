Beena Tiwari and Another v State of Madhya Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28140; (1988) Supp SCC 213; (1988) SCC (L&S) 667; AIR 1988 SC 488; JT 1987 (4) SC 686; 1988 LabIC 954; 1988 (2) LLN 168; 1987(2) SCALE 1371; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 492; 1988 (1) SLR 783

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 235 - Madhya Pradesh Government Service (Temporary and Quasi-Permanent Service) Rules, 1960, r. 3 A - Madhya Pradesh Judicial Service (Classification, Recruitment and Condition of Services) Rules, 1955, r. 16(5) - Member of MP Subordinate Judicial Service - Not governed by r. 3 A of 1960 Rules - Absolute power vests in High Court, whether to confirm him or not - Held, on not being confirmed his services can be terminated u/ r. 16(5) of 1955 Rules - Order accordingly.
