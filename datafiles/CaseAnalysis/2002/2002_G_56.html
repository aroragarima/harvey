Konda Lakshmana Bapuji v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 92; (2002) 3 SCC 258; AIR 2002 SC 1012; 2002 (2) ALD(SC) 56; JT 2002 (2) SC 253; 2002(1) SCALE 584; 2002 (1) SCJ 508; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 651; 2002 (1) SLT 590; 2002 (1) Supreme 551

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Occupiers, Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972, Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Abolition of Inams Act, 1955, Land Encroachment Act

Summary: The appellant falls within the mischief of the definition of the expression "land grabber" under the A.P. Land Grabbing (Prohibition) Act, 1982, if, the activity of grabbing of land has been done without any lawful entitlement and also with a view to illegally take possession of such lands.
