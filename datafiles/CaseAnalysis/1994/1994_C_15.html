Mac Laboratories v C.C.E.

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

Mr.Justice R.M. Sahai, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1197; (1995) 2 SCC 56; AIR 1995 SC 510; 1995 (49) ECC 18; 1994 (74) E.L.T. 769; JT 1994 (7) SC 496; 1994(4) SCALE 1018; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 621

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Interpretation of statues - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 5 A - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Exemption from excise duty on kemicetine vaginal suppositories used for local absorption - Medicine falling in category of parenteral medicine - Availability of benefit under notification no 116/69 CE D/- 03-05-1969 as amended by notification no 106/80 CE D/- 19-06-1980 - Held, benefit cannot be denied because it falls in category of medicines which are applied locally - Is covered under notification - Appeal allowed.
