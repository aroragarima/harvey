Union of India and another v National Federation of the Blind and others

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Sathasivam, Ranjana Prakash Desai, Ranjan Gogoi

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 674; (2013) 10 SCC 772; 2014 (1) AWC 700; 2014 (1) Bom.C.R. 527; 2013 (139) FLR 811; 2013(12) SCALE 588; [2013] 9 S.C.R. 1023; 2013 (6) SLR 395; 2014 (1) UPLBEC 42

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Draft Rights of Persons with Disabilities Bill, 2012

Summary: Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995-Section 33-Reservation for persons with disabilities-Computation of reservation for persons with disabilities has to be computed in case of Groups A, B, C and D posts in identical manner viz., "computing 3% reservation on total number of vacancies in cadre strength" which is intention of Legislature-Finding of High Court that in terms of Section 33, 3% reservation for disabled persons has to be computed on basis of total strength of cadre, i.e., both identified as well as unidentified posts-Erroneous-Certain clauses in impugned office memorandum-Struck down-Appropriate Government to issue new office memorandum in consistent with decision rendered by Apex Court in instant case-In order to ensure proper implementation of reservation policy for disabled and to protect their rights-Necessary directions issued.
