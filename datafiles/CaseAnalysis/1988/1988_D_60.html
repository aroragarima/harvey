State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v Dr. Mohanjit Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 858; (1988) Supp SCC 562; (1988) SCC (L&S) 906; 1988 (8) ATC 151; 1989 (1) LLN 601

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Selection - Validity - Whether there can be a valid selection by body constituted under Govt. order when there is no quorum? - Presence of at least one representative of University and representative of Director of Higher Education in Selection Committee meeting should be regarded as essential for completing quorum - Once a decision is taken and absentee member is called upon to ratify conclusion already reached, it becomes a very different type of activity - Held, not acceptable decision of Administrative Tribunal on principle that subsequent ratification constituted valid selection - Answered in favour of respondent.
