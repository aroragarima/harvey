Â Rabindranath Bose and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Ray, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 467; (1970) 1 SCC 84; AIR 1970 SC 470; 1970 LabIC 402; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 697

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Board Of Revenue Act, 1913

Summary: Constitution, arts. 13, 14, 13 and 32 - Service Rules - (A) The art. 13 has no retrospective operation - Appointments to posts of ITO Class I Grade II - Preparation of Seniority Lists under Seniority Rules of 1949 and 1950 - Whether there is any violation of arts. 14 and 16 - (B) Inordinate delay - Changes affected in seniority Lists on 01-08-1953 due to change in Seniority Rules of 1952 - Petition challenging changes as violative of art. 14 and 16 , file after 15 years - Maintainability - Held, fact that seniority list of the Officers was finally settled after the Constitution came into force, would not enable the petitioners to appeal to arts.14 and 16 - Petition held, not maintainable - Petition Dismissed.
