Ramdas Bhikaji Chaudhari v Sadanand and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 570; (1980) SCC (Cr) 268; (1980) 1 SCC 550; 1979 (16) ACC 311; AIR 1980 SC 126; 1980 CRLJ 111; 1980 CrLR(SC) 41; 1980 MahLJ 1; 1980 MLJ 352; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 849; 1980 UJ 63

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 141 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 7 and 16 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules, 1955, r. 22 - Appeal against acquittal - Food Adulteration - (A) High Court acquitted on basis of Supreme Court decision - Supreme Court decision subsequently overruled - Legality of order - (B) Sentence - Accused prosecuted in 1971 and ultimately acquitted by HC in 1976 - Acquittal set aside by Supreme Court - Acquittal was clearly wrong - Ends of Justice did not require that accused be sent to jail so accused sentenced only to fine of Rs. 2,000 - Appeal allowed.
