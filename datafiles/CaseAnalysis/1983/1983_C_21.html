D. P. Sharma v Commissioner and Returning Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 302; (1984) Supp SCC 157; AIR 1984 SC 654; 1983(2) SCALE 867

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Election - Conduct of Election Rules, 1967, rr. 45 and 56 - Discrepancies and irregularities in records required to be maintained u/rr. 45 and 56 - Which showed that the entire counting process was defective necessitating a re-count of the votes - Effect on election - Held, insignificant discrepancies, could be attributed to clerical or arithmetical mistakes - No case for recount of votes - Appeal dismissed.
