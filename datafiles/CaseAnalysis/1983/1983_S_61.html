Kailash Sonkar v Smt. Maya Devi

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.B. Misra, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 295; (1984) 2 SCC 91; AIR 1984 SC 600; 1983(2) SCALE 1211; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 176; 1984 UJ 262

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act, 1976, Christianity

Summary: Family & Personal - Constitution (Scheduled Castes) Order, 1950, Part 9, Serial No. 29 - Reconversion to Hinduism - Tests to determine revival of Scheduled Caste - Held, it is intention of convertee which would determine revival of caste - Appeal dismissed.
