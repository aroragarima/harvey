Punjab & Sind Bank & Anr. v S. Ranveer Singh Bawa & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 329; (2004) 4 SCC 484; (2004) SCC (L&S) 657; AIR 2004 SC 2334; 2004 (3) AWC 2223; 2004 (111) DLT 226; 2004 (4) ESC 509; 2004 (101) FLR 989; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 594; 2004 (2) LLJ 573; 2004 (2) LLN 868; 2004(4) SCALE 816; 2004 (4) SLR 9; 2004 (3) SLT 254; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 524; 2004 (3) Supreme 369; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1991

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Voluntary retirement scheme - Whether respondent who earlier opted for voluntary retirement scheme could be permitted to withdraw therefrom after having received payments under scheme - Conduct of respondent no 1 indicates his knowledge about payments under scheme in his accounts; that he never objected to such payments and that he had appropriated amounts for his benefit - He cannot resile from scheme.
