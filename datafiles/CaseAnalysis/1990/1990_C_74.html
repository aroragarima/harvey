Poonjabhai Varmalidas v Commissioner Of Income Tax Rajasthan,Jaipur And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 893; (1990) 4 SCC 98; AIR 1991 SC 236; 1990 (86) CTR 173; [1990] 185 ITR 56; JT 1990 (3) SC 287; 1990(2) SCALE 121; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 393; 1990 TAXLR 1022; [1990] 51 TAXMAN 300

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Undisclosed Income, Authorisation For Search

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 132(5) - Income Tax Raid - Return of valuables seized in raid - Notice u/s. 132 for search and seizure issued against wife carrying on certain business - Search and seizure declared illegal - In proceedings u/s. 132(5) husband claiming that same belonged to him - High Court refusing to return valuables to wife - Held, in view of dispute about ownership, refusal cannot be faulted - Application dismissed.
