Peddireddy Subbareddi and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 38; AIR 1991 SC 1356; 1991 CRLJ 1391

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Delay in lodging First Information Report of Murder - Eyewitness not reporting incidence to villagers and moreover his testimony clouded with suspicion - Possibility of false implication also not completely ruled out - Held, accused entitled to acquittal - Appeal allowed.
