K.C. Sharma & Ors. v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S.P. Bharucha, M.M. Punchhi, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1889; (1997) 6 SCC 721; AIR 1997 SC 3588; JT 1997 (7) SC 58; 1998 (1) LLJ 57; 1998 (2) LLN 69; 1997(5) SCALE 223; 1998 (1) SLJ 54; 1997 (4) SLR 774; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 87; 1997 (7) Supreme 40

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Service - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 21 - Condonation of delay - Notification D/ - 05-12-1988 amending r. 2544 of Railway Establishment Code for calculation of average emolument - Said notification so far as it gave retrospective effect to amendment held invalid by Full Bench of Tribunal in its earlier decision - Application seeking benefit of said decision by appellant who were adversely affected by impugned amendments - Whether to be dismissed on grounds of delay - Held, cannot be dismissed - Moreso, when earlier decision given by Full Bench of Tribunal is affirmed by SC in civil appeal - Appeal dismissed.
