Â State of Assam and Another v S. N. Sen and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

Subimal Chandra Roy, G.K. Mitter, J.M. Shelat, I.D. Dua, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 470; (1971) 2 SCC 889; AIR 1972 SC 1028; 1972 LabIC 571; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 251; 1972 SLR 1; 1972 UJ 191

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Keywords: Assam Judicial Service (Junior) Rules 1954

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 235 - Assam Judicial Service (Junior) Rules, 1954, r. 5(iv) - Constitutionality of r. 5(iv) of 1954 Rules - Power to confirm promotion of subordinate Judge vests in High Court - Held, r. 5(iv) is in conflict with art. 235 and is unconstitutional - Appeal dismissed.
