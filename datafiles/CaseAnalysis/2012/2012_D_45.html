Vinayak Kashinath Shilkar v Dy. Collector & Competent Authority And Others

Supreme Court of India

29 February 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, H.L. Gokhale

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 88; 2012(4) ALL MR 461; 2012 (92) ALR 300; 2012 (2) AWC 2123; 2012 (5) Bom.C.R. 869; 2012 (5) MahLJ 122; 2012 (5) MLJ(SC) 450; 2012 (167) PLR 744; 2012 (2) RCR(Civil) 650; 2012(3) SCALE 385; [2012] 2 S.C.R. 219

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Urban Land (Ceiling & Regulation) Act, 1976, s. 10 - Land Ceiling - Repealed - Abated - Validity - Appellant filed Writ petition before HC to declare proceedings under Act as abated in view of repeal of Act - Respondent contended that notice u/s. 10 (5) of Act was issued to appellant to hand over possession of land within 30 days from, thus subject land had vested with State Government - HC dismissed petition, DB also dismissed observing that possession of property had already been taken by Government under Act - Aggrieved instant appeal - Appellant contended that finding of HC that possession of property had been taken by Government was factually incorrect - Respondents submitted that there was nothing on record to indicate that actual possession of subject land had been taken over by respondents from appellant - Whether proceedings under Act for property should be abated - Held, legal position enunciated by SC earlier and factual situation that possession of subject land had not been taken by Government, SC was satisfied that appellant was entitled to relief and HC ought to have declared that proceedings under Act in relation to subject property stood abated - Appeal allowed.
