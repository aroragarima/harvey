Sri Venkata Satyanarayna Rice Mill Contractors Company v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

25 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 4041; (1996) 6 SCC 611; (1997) SCC (L&S) 763; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 95; AIR 1997 SC 182; 1997 (137) CTR 267; [1997] 223 ITR 101; JT 1996 (9) SC 586; 1996(8) SCALE 343; 1997 (1) SLJ 162; 1996 (6) SLR 617; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 767; 1997 (1) Supreme 104; [1996] 89 TAXMAN 92; 1997 (2) TLR 40; 1997 (1) UJ 174

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Allowable deduction - Business expenditure - Contribution to PWF directly related to business - Contribution to District Welfare fund evolved by rice millers' association with District Collector - Each member proposing to export rice required to deposit certain amount - If entitled to deduction of amount deposited u/s. 37(1), in light of fact that in application for export permit, member required to state amount and particulars of bank etc - Assessee held was entitled to claim deduction - Order accordingly.
