11 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Punjab Civil Services (Premature retirement) Rules 1975

Summary: Service - Punjab Civil Services (Premature retirement) Rules, 1975, rr. 2 and 3 - Premature retirement - Whether retiring appellant prematurely was arbitrary and unreasonable as appellant's service record has all along been good? - Held, appellant had not earned any adverse remarks during last five years of service, on other hand he had earned 'good' and 'very good' entries during those years - Therefore Govt's decision to retire appellant prematurely in exercise of power u/r. 3 is not sustainable in law - Appeal allowed.
