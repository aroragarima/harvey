Supreme Court of India

26 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25 F, chap. 2, item 3 - Retrenchment - Daily wage employees - Appointment made on basis of need of work - Termination of their services - (A) Whether retrenchment? - (B) Whether same is arbitrary? - Held, (A) cannot be construed to be retrenchment - (B) Same is also not arbitrary as they were not entitled to post - Petition dismissed.
