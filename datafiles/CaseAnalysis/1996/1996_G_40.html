Union of India and Others v Garware Nylons Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2404; (1996) 10 SCC 413; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 125; AIR 1996 SC 3509; 1996 (66) ECR 644; 1996 (87) E.L.T. 12; 1996 (16) RLT 619; 1996(6) SCALE 667; 1996 (3) SCJ 323; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 629; 1996 (8) Supreme 467

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, Item 18 - Excise Duty - Nylon Twine - Is considered as a kind of 'Nylon Yarn' in particular trade by persons conversant with subject-matter? - If it is to be classified as item 18 of sch. 1 of Act - Held, it is to be classified under item 18 - Appeal dismissed.
