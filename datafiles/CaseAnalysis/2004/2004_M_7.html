Printers (Mysore) Limited v M. A. Rasheed and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1470; (2004) 4 SCC 460; JT 2004 (4) SC 158; 2004 (3) MLJ(SC) 132; 2004(4) SCALE 192; 2004 (3) Supreme 97

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Land & Property - Bangalore Development Authority Act, s. 38 - Rules thereunder - Allotment of plot in question to appellant - Company for establishing industry - Execution of sale deed - Public interest litigation filed against such allotment on ground that it has been allotted without any publication or advertisement - Whether Bangalore Development Authority(BDA) was authorised under rules to make such allotment? - Provisions under Act and rules made thereunder perused - Transaction in question not found to be covered by any rules framed under Act - Held, BDA has power to lease, sell or otherwise transfer any movable or immovable property belonging to it subject to such restrictions, conditions and limitations, as may be prescribed - Petition was filed at a belated stage and court observed that High Court should have dismissed petition on that ground - Impugned judgment set aside - Appeal allowed.
