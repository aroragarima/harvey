S.D.S. Shipping Pvt. Ltd. v Jay Container Services Co. Pvt. Ltd. and others

Supreme Court of India

08 May 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 436; (2003) 9 SCC 439; AIR 2003 SC 2186; 2003 (51) ALR 631; 2003(4) SCALE 604; 2003 (1) SCW 2621; 2003 (3) SLT 670; [2003] Supp1 S.C.R. 101; 2003 (4) Supreme 44

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India - art. 136 - claim for non-return of containers for the ships and outstanding rental - Held, it does not appear to be a case where interference under art. 136 of the Constitution of India is called for. That power is exercised only on showing substantial injustice, and not for merely technical flaws in a proceeding.
