New Standard Engineering Company Limited v N. L. Abhyankar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, P.N. Bhagwati, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 129; (1978) 2 SCC 133; (1978) SCC (L&S) 183; AIR 1978 SC 982; 1978 (36) FLR 190; 1978 LabIC 712; 1978 (1) LLJ 487; 1978 (1) LLN 542; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 798; 1978 UJ 158

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(p)and 18 - Settlement of industrial dispute - How fairness of direct negotiations to be judged? - Held, question should be examined with reference to situation as it stood on date on which it was arrived at - Appeal allowed.
