State of Rajasthan v Tarachand Jain

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 494; (1973) SCC (Cr) 774; (1974) 3 SCC 72; AIR 1973 SC 2131; 1973 CRLJ 1396; 1973 CrLR(SC) 499; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 146

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Prevention Of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 6 - (A) Precedents - Judgment of earlier Division Bench of High Court - Effect upon subsequent proceedings of same case - (B) Prevention Of Corruption Act, 1947 - Sanction for prosecution - Validity of - Burden of proof - Held, is binding in subsequent proceedings of same case - Burden of proof that requisite sanction had been obtained rest upon prosecution - Appeal allowed.
