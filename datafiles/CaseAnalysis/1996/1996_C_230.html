Commissioner of Income Tax v B. Posetty and Company

Supreme Court of India

08 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3504; (1996) 1 SCC 767; AIR 1996 SC 1091; 1996 (134) CTR 596; [1996] 220 ITR 216; JT 1996 (1) SC 153

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 184 - Assessment Year 1967-68 - Registration of partnership firms doing Abkari business - One of partners entering into sub-partnership - Whether a sub partnership entered into by one of partner of firm carrying on business of vending liquor or abkari business, governed by provisions of s. 14 of Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Abkari Act, 1316F, during relevant Assessment Year could be registered under provisions of Income Tax Act, 1961 Act? - Held, not interferred - Matter referred to a larger bench.
