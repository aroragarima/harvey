Maharashtra State Electricity Board, Bombay v Official Liquidator, High Court, Ernakulam

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 135; (1982) 3 SCC 358; AIR 1982 SC 1497; 1983 ARBLR 438; [1983] 53 Comp Cas 248; 1982 (3) CompLJ 205; 1982(2) SCALE 875; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 561; 1982 UJ 839

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract Act , 1872, ss. 128 and 134 - Liability of surety under Contract - Bank guarantee on behalf of a company to Electricity Board - Company went into liquidation - Impact on right of Board to recover guarantee amount from Bank - Held, a discharge which the principal debtor may secure by operation of law in bankruptcy (or in liquidation proceedings in the case of a company) does not absolve the surety of his liability - Appeal Allowed.
