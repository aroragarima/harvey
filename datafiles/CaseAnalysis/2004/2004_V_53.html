Held, in view of fact that two other cases between parties are already pending in Courts at Patna, it is appropriate to allowed claim of petitioner. Respondent also has no objection to adopt such course, having regard to fact. Under circumstances, Matrimonial Case pending in Court of Vth Additional District Judge, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, shall stand transferred to District Judge, Patna, Bihar, who may either hear case himself or transfer it to Court of competent jurisdiction. Petition allowed.

Ratio - For convenience of parties cases shall be transfer from one Court to other.
