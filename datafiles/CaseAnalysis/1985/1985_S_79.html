Chiranjit Lal Anand v State Of Assam And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, V.D. Tulzapurkar, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 137; (1985) Supp SCC 392; (1985) SCC (Tax) 532; AIR 1985 SC 1387; 1985(2) SCALE 113; 1985 (60) STC 89; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 385

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Keywords: Assam Sales Tax Act, 1947

Summary: Sales Tax - Assam Sales Tax Act, 1947, ss.7, 19, 2(4) and sch.III item 11- Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Taxability of supplies for army ration - Sale of 'Meat on hoof' pursuant to tender to supply various items of ratio in Army Scale - Whether liable to sales tax, in light of the fact that it was supplied by assessee to Army for ration to personnel of army - Held, transactions in question were not subject to sales tax - Appeal allowed.
