Commissioner of Income Tax, Kerala v H. A. Mohamed Haneef

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 418; (1972) 4 SCC 375; 1974 (3) CTR 129; [1972] 83 ITR 215; 1972 TAXLR 446

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922 - Penalty on assessee on ground that he had deliberately submitted a wrong return - Held, in view of decision of Supreme Court in CIT v Anwar Ali, decision of High Court correct as there is no basis for coming to a firm conclusion that assessee deliberately supplied wrong particulars - Appeal dismissed.
