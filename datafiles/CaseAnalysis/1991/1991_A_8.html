Government Of Andhra Pradesh And Anr v A. Suryanarayanarao and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 890; (1991) Supp2 SCC 367; AIR 1991 SC 2113; 1992 (19) ATC 272; 1992 LabIC 60; 1991(2) SCALE 548; 1991 (6) SLR 56; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 27; 1991 (2) UJ 623

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Criteria For Promotion, Eligibility - Seniority - Cadre

Summary: Service - Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 371 D - Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Organisation of Local Cadres and Regulation of Direct Recruitment) Order, 1975, Para 5, Para 13 - Special Rules for Andhra Pradesh Panchayati Raj Engineering Service, r. 2 A (introduced in 1979) - (A) Whether expression in matter of public employment in art. 371 D(1) includes both direct recruitment and promotion? - (B) Whether r. 2 A operates retrospectively - (C) Whether promotions made prior to introduction of r. 2 A on basis of State wise list to be treated as provisional? - (D) Whether Zonal seniority lists or State wise seniority list would amount to discrimination - Held, (A) it includes both, public employment can be by way of direct recruitment or by promotion - (B) said rule operates retrospectively - (C) promotions have to be treated as provisional and should be reviewed and readjusted and it should be on basis of zonal seniority lists and not State wise seniority list as it has nexus with object to be achieved - (D) possibility of some anomaly would not amount to discrimination - Order accordingly.
