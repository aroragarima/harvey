Lachminarayan Madan Lal v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 354; (1973) 3 SCC 76; AIR 1973 SC 2330; 1972 CTR 418; [1972] 86 ITR 439; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 207; 1972 TAXLR 1171

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 37 and 256 - Business expenditure - Selling agency commission - Whether deductible as business expenditure - Scope of enquiry - Refused to state case - Refused to state case involving the question held justified - Appeal dismissed.
