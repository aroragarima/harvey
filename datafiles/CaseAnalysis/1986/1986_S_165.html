State Government Pensioners’ Association And Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 743; (1986) 3 SCC 501; (1986) SCC (L&S) 676; AIR 1986 SC 1907; 1986 (53) FLR 317; JT 1986 SC 31; 1986 LabIC 1803; 1986 (2) LLN 1059; 1986(2) SCALE 138; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 383; 1986 (2) UJ 473

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 311 - Gratuity - Payment of larger amount with prospective effect from specified date - Whether provision offends art. 14? - Held, it does not offend art. 14 unless it also provides for payment on revised basis to all those who have already retired between date of commencement of Constitution in 1950 and date of upward revision - Petition dismissed.
