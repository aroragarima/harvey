Surath Chandra Chakrabarty v State Of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 197; (1970) 3 SCC 548; AIR 1971 SC 752; 1971 LabIC 456; 1971 (1) LLJ 293; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 1; 1971 (2) SLR 103

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1930, r. 55 - Constitution, art. 311 - Civil Services - Vague and indefinite charges - Also failure to supply statement of allegations - Held, would render removal of Govt. servant void and inoperative - Appeal Allowed.
