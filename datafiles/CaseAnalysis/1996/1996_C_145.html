Messrs Mittal Engineering Works Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Meerut

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1904; (1997) 1 SCC 203; 1997 (68) ECR 13; 1996 (88) E.L.T. 622; JT 1996 (10) SC 722; 1996 (17) RLT 612; 1996(8) SCALE 452; 1997 (106) STC 201; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 796

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Mono vertical crystallizers are used in sugar factories - Whether mono vertical crystallizers are 'goods' upon which excise duty under provisions of Act can be levied? - Mono vertical crystallizers have to assembled, erected and attached to earth by a foundation at site of sugar factory - Not capable of being sold as it is, without anything more - Held, mono vertical crystallizers are not 'goods' within meaning of Act and therefore, not exigible to excise duty - Appeal allowed.
