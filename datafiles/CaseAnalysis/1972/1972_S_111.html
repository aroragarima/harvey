Messrs Sachalmal Parasram v Smt. Ratnabai and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 670; (1973) 3 SCC 198; AIR 1972 SC 637; 1972 MPLJ 427; 1972 RCJ 328; 1972 UJ 559

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 76(a) and 111(c) - (A) Lease created by mortgagee in possession - Redemption of mortgage - (B) Lease by mortgagee in possession - Effect of redemption - Held, terminates on redemption of mortgage - Tenancy created by mortgagee in possession does not survive termination of mortgagee's interest - Appeal dismissed.
