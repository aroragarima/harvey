Workmen of Messrs Rohtas Industries v Rohtas Industries and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1823; (1995) Supp4 SCC 5; (1996) SCC (L&S) 125; AIR 1996 SC 467; [1996] 86 Comp Cas 1; JT 1995 (7) SC 629; 1996 (2) LLN 56; 1995(6) SCALE 58; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 459

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Winding up proceedings - Against large industrial undertaking - Writ petition by workers - Apex Court entertaining petition with object of revival of undertaking and rehabilitation of workers - Held, adopting unprecedented course of assuming direct control over functioning undertaking - In spite of best efforts revival of undertaking is not feasible - Direction for resumption of winding up proceedings.
