Sant Lal Jain v Avtar Singh

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 435; (1985) 2 SCC 332; AIR 1985 SC 857; 1985 (1) CCC 1078; 1985 (1) RCR(Rent) 696; 1985 (1) RentLR 736; 1985(1) SCALE 423; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 184; 1985 UJ 609

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 6, 39 - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 13 -Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s.108 - Tenancy - Lessee of plot of land licenced part of it , for certain period - Filed suit for mandatory injunction on expiry of period - But licensee claimed that he purchased entire land from owner - Maintainability of suit for possession filed by lesssee - Held, suit is maintainable - Appeal allowed .
