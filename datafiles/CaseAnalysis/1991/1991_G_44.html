Mohinder Singh v Gulwant Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, S. Ratnavel Pandian, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 425; (1992) 2 SCC 213; (1992) SCC (Cr) 361; AIR 1992 SC 1894; 1992 (1) CCR 674; 1992 (1) Crimes 603; 1992 CRLJ 3161; 1992 CrLR(SC) 136; JT 1992 (1) SC 542; 1992(1) SCALE 43; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 478

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 202 and 482 - Enquiry u/s. 202 - Scope - Complaint u/s. 494 Indian Penal Code not specific to mention performance of second marriage - Criminal proceedings - Quashed by High Court - Validity - Held, is liable to be set aside.
