Ayyaswami Gounder and Others v Munnuswamy Gounder and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 311; (1984) 4 SCC 376; AIR 1984 SC 1789; 1985 (1) CCC 93; 1985 (1) MLJ(SC) 36; 1984(2) SCALE 437; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 808; 1985 UJ 247

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 38 - Easements Act, 1882, s. 8, Illus.(c) - Partition of joint property which was meant for common enjoyment including channel running from well - Whether one of the co-owner can connect different water channel (from his exclusive well) with common channel - Held, in absence of any specific pleading regarding prejudice or detriment to the defendants-respondents the plaintiffs had every right to use the common land and the common channel - Appeal allowed.
