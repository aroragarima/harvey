Roshan Lal and Others v International Airport Authority of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 80; (1980) Supp SCC 449; (1981) SCC (L&S) 303; AIR 1981 SC 597; 1981 LabIC 1; 1981 (1) LLJ 272

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art.32 - International Airport Authority Act, 1971, Pre. - Circulars Nos.17/31/74-Ops. D/- 30-8-1974 and 17/31/74-Ops. D/- 08-04-1974 - Service Airport Officers - (A) Writ petition filed in 1976 against appointments of certain Air Force Officers made in 1975 of certain Airport Officers - (B) Airport Officrers - Post was superior to Terminal Managers - Reorganisation - Still placed above Terminal Managers in seniority list - Challenged - Supreme Court refused to reopen question of legality of appointment on ground that it would not be justified in doing so several years after the appointments -Is not open to challenge - Appeal Dismissed.
