Taherakhatoon (D) By Lrs v Salambin Mohammad

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 983; (1999) 2 SCC 635; AIR 1999 SC 1104; 1999 (4) ALD(SC) 1; 1999 (2) CLT 91; JT 1999 (2) SC 45; 1999 (90) RD 268; 1999(1) SCALE 634; 1999 (1) SCJ 660; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 901; 1999 (2) SLT 441; 1999 (2) Supreme 446

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Award - Setting aside of - Ground, violation of principle of natural justice - Plea, additional statement and documents filed by opposite party were considered without affording opportunity to party to contest them - Record showing that no substantive statement or documents were filed by opposite party - Held, question of affording opportunity does not arise - More, no objection was taken by party before arbitrator at relevant time.
