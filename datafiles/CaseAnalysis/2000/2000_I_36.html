Commissioner of Sales Tax, U. P v Indra Industries

Supreme Court of India

12 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3070; (2000) 9 SCC 66; AIR 2000 SC 3442; 2001 (168) CTR 50; [2001] 248 ITR 338; JT 2000 (2) SC 441; 2000(6) SCALE 392; 2001 (122) STC 100

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 42(2) - Departmental circular - Issued by Commr. of Sales Tax - Binding on courts and assessee, whether - Held, binding on sales tax authorities unless circular has been withdrawn - But not courts and assessee.
