Dr. Partap Singh and Another v Director of Enforcement Foreign Exchange

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 419; (1985) 3 SCC 72; (1985) SCC (Cr) 312; (1989) SCC (Tax) 469; AIR 1985 SC 989; 1985 (2) CCC 599; [1985] 58 Comp Cas 477; 1986 CRLJ 824; 1985 CRLR 589; 1985 CrLR(SC) 589; 1985 (44) CTR 208; 1985 (46) CTR 319; [1985] 155 ITR 166; 1985(1) SCALE 1208; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 969; [1985] 22 TAXMAN 30; 1986 (2) TLR 275

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Illegal Search

Summary: Exchange Control and FDI - FERA, 1973, ss. 37, 38 - Income Tax Act, 1961 - CrPC, 1973, s.165(1) - Appeal against dismissal of WP praying for quashing of a search warrant and for a direction to return articles seized during the search of his house - High Court found nothing illegal in the issuance of search warrant - Scope of s. 37 of FERA in the light of s. 165(1) of the CrPC, 1973 - Issuance of search warrant or the search found to be not illegal or invalid - Held, s. 165(1) of CrPC, 1973 is not incorporated by pen and ink in s. 37(2) - Return of seized documents / articles cannot be ordered - Appeal dismissed.
