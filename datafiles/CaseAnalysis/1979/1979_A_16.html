State (Spe Hyderabad) v Air Commodore Kailash Chand

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 111; (1980) SCC (Cr) 323; (1980) 1 SCC 667; AIR 1980 SC 522; 1980 CRLJ 393; 1980 CrLR(SC) 86; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 697; 1980 (4) ShimLC 123

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Air Force, Reserve and Auxiliary Air Force Act 1953

Summary: Criminal - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 5(2) and 6 - IPC, 1860, s. 21 - CrPC, 1973, s. 197 - Sanction to prosecute public servant for offence u/s. 5(2) of 1947 Act - Officer retired but re-employed by Indian Air Force - Prosecution without sanction - Continuing as member of Auxiliary Air Force at the time of taking cognizance - Held, prosecution without sanction of competent authority is invalid - Appeal dismissed.
