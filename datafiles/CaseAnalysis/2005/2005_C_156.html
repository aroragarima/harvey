International Auto Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Bihar

Supreme Court of India

17 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 394; (2005) 12 SCC 129; 2005 (183) E.L.T. 239

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 57F(2)(b) - Valuation - Excise duty - Demand and penalty - Held, manufacturer of final product is permitted to remove inputs to a place outside factory for purpose of manufacture of intermediate products so that they are returned to factory for further use in manufacture of final products - Appellant is not liable to pay duty on inputs supplied by TELCO since it had not taken credit for Modvat in respect of inputs - Appeal allowed.
