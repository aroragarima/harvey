Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Patanjali Sastri, Ghulam Hasan, Mehr Chand Mahajan, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Vivian Bose

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 543; AIR 1954 SC 119; 1954 (56) BomLR 681; [1954] 24 Comp Cas 103; 1954 (1) MLJ(SC) 355; 1954 SCJ 175; [1954] 1 S.C.R. 674

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate

Keywords: Specific Relief, Insurance Amendment Act, 1950

Summary: Textiles - Sholapur Spinning and Weaving Company (Emergency Provisions) Ordinance, 1950 - Sholapur Spinning and Weaving Company (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1950 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 13, 19, 31 and 141 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 - Constitutionality of 1950, Ordinance - Considering - Vires, Duty of Court, clauses deal with same topic, Who can challenge, Meaning of 'Property' and 'Acquisition', Fundamental rights, Construction, Precedents, Supreme Court if bound by its own decisions - Held, Act unconstitutional and void - Appeal allowed.
