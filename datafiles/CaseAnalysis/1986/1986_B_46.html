Commissioner of Income Tax, Calcutta v Biju Patnaik

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 637; (1986) 3 SCC 310; AIR 1986 SC 1428; 1986 (58) CTR 65; [1986] 160 ITR 674; 1986(1) SCALE 885; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 26; 1986 TAXLR 744; [1986] 26 TAXMAN 324; 1986 (2) TLR 744

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 256(2) - Whether, question of law referable to the High Court, arises out of the order of the Appellate Income Tax Tribunal in this case - High Court not directing a reference on the specified questions to the High Court u/s. 256(2) of the Act - Held, HC was in error in not directing a reference - Appeals allowed.
