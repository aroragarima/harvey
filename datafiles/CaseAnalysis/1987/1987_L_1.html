L/Naik Mahabir Singh v Chief of Army Staff

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28439; (1990) Supp SCC 89A

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Justifiability, Dismissal from services

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - Army Act, 1950, s. 162 - Dismissal from services - Justifiability - Held, there was summary court martial in which petitioner was found guilty and was sentenced to 6 months imprisonment and dismissed from service. Abstract of proceeding produced as to petition shows that proceedings were subject to review u/s. 162 of the Act. Decision of summary court martial is subject to review, authorities shall exercise jurisdiction of review and pass appropriate order or directions. Petition disposed of.
