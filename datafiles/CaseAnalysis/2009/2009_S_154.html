Satwati Deswal v State Of Haryana & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 November 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, R.M. Lodha

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1506; (2010) 1 SCC 126; 2009 (4) AWC 3996; 2010 (1) AWC 314; 2009 (4) JhrCR(SC) 159; JT 2009 (14) SC 1; 2009 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2933; 2009 (8) MLJ(SC) 988; 2010 (157) PLR 387; 2009(13) SCALE 599; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 697; 2009 (6) SLR 441

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Termination - Challenged - HC dismissed writ petition filed by appellant challenging his termination from service on ground of maintainability and relegated appellant to take statutory remedy of appeal - Whether said order justified? - Held, order of termination was passed without issuing any show cause notice to appellant and without initiating any disciplinary proceedings by authorities and without affording any opportunity of hearing - Writ petition was maintainable in law and HC erred in holding that in view of availability of alternative remedy to challenge order of termination, writ petition was not maintainable in law - Impugned judgment of HC set aside - Order of termination passed against appellant quashed - Appeal allowed.
