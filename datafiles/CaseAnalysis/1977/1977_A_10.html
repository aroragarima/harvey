Bhavnagar Municipality v Alibhai Karimbhai and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 16; (1977) SCC (L&S) 264; (1977) 2 SCC 350; AIR 1977 SC 1229; 1977 (34) FLR 229; 1977 LabIC 834; 1977 (1) LLJ 407; 1977 (2) LLN 1; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 932; 1977 UJ 208

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 33(1)(a), 33A - Service - (A) Retrenchment during pendency of industrial dispute - If s.33 (1) (a) contravened - (B) Complaint u/s. 33A - (1) Maintainability and - (2) Nature of complaint - Duty of Tribunal - Held, there was contravention of s.33(1)(a) on the part of the Municipality - Complaint u/s.33A at the instance of such workers was maintainable - Tribunal has to pronounce upon the merits of the dispute between the partie - Appeal Allowed.
