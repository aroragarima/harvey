Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City-III, Bombay v Shantilal Private Limited, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 269; (1983) 3 SCC 561; AIR 1983 SC 952; 1983 (35) CTR 395; [1983] 144 ITR 57; 1983(2) SCALE 37; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 470; 1983 TAXLR 1190; [1983] 14 TAXMAN 1

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s.43(5) - Contract Act, 1872, s. 73 - Income Tax - Breach of contract and on a dispute between the parties damages are awarded as compensation by an arbitration award - Whether speculative transaction - Held, not a speculative transaction - Order Accordingly.
