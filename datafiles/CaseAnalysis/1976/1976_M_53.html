Messrs Modi Spinning and Weaving Mills Company v Messrs Ladha Ram and Company

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 465; (1976) 4 SCC 320; AIR 1977 SC 680; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 728

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 17 - Ammendment of pleadings - Application for ammendment of written statement by substituting certain paragraphs - To the effect of entirely different new case and seeking to displace the plaintiff completely from admissions made by defendants in WS - Maintainability - Application, held, was liable to be rejected - Appeal Dismissed.
