Dhanna v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2240; (1996) 10 SCC 79; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1192; AIR 1996 SC 2478; AIR 1996 SCW 3066; 1996 (3) Crimes 139; 1996 CRLJ 3516; JT 1996 (6) SC 652; 1996(5) SCALE 467; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 28; 1996 (6) Supreme 114

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 34 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 378 and 386 - Proof of murder - Prosecution witness making improvement at time of trial, to statement given in investigation making no reference to any role played by accused - Applicability of s. 34 in absence of special mentioned in charge - Held, Court can take recourse to s. 34 even if said section was not specifically mentioned in charge - Appeal No. 252 of 1984 dismissed - Appeal No. 170 - A of 1984 allowed.
