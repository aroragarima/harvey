Aligarh Muslim University and Others v Mansoor Ali Khan

Supreme Court of India

28 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 429; (2000) 7 SCC 529; AIR 2000 SC 2783; AIR 2000 SCW 3826; 2000 ALJ 2436; 2000 (4) AWC 2993; JT 2000 (9) SC 502; 2000(6) SCALE 125; 2000 (5) SLR 67; 2000 (6) SLT 415; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 684; 2000 (6) Supreme 1; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 2234

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Aligarh Muslim University Act, 1920, s. 36 - Aligarh University Revised Leave Rules 1969, r. 5(8) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Unauthorized absence or overstaying of leave - Deeming vacation of post - Forewarning notice to delinquent - Not resuming duty within given time - Deemed vacated - Constitutionality - Held, no prejudice caused to delinquent even if no show cause notice - Not arbitrary.
