National Textile Corporation Limited And Ors. v Sitaram Mills Ltd. And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

04 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, R.S. Pathak, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 289; (1986) Supp SCC 117; AIR 1986 SC 1234; [1987] 61 Comp Cas 373; 1986 (2) CompLJ 261; 1986(1) SCALE 657; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 187

Case Digest

Subject: Textiles

Keywords: Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983, Sick Textile Undertakings(Taking Over Of Management) Act, 1972

Summary: Textiles - Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983, s. 3(2) - Constitution, art. 39(b) and (c) - Interpretation of statute - Textile undertaking taken over under the Act - A division dealing in real estate plough back proceeds from the sale of land and galas built on them in the textile mill - (A) Whether the lands are assets of textile undertaking within s. 3(2) of the Act - (B) How a legislation in furtherance of directive principles contained in art. 39(b), (c) of Constitution is to be construed - Held, lands are assets of textile undertaking - Construction which would sub serve and carry out purpose and object of Act should be adopted - Petition dismissed.
