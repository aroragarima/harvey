State Of Karnataka And Anr. v Dr. Praveen Bhai Thogadia

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 244; (2004) 4 SCC 684; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1387; AIR 2004 SC 2081; 2004 (2) Crimes 107; 2004 CRLJ 1825; 2004 (3) G.L.R. 2242; JT 2004 (4) SC 129; 2004 (2) KLT 342; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 457; 2004(4) SCALE 115; 2004 (3) SLT 357; 2004 (3) Supreme 8

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Defence of India Act, 1962

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Defence Of India Act, 1962, ss. 29, 40 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Prohibitory orders - Orders passed by Additional District Magistrate restraining respondent u/s. 144 of CrPC - HC holding that ADM had no jurisdiction - Held, courts should not normally interfere with matters relating to law and order - Primarily domain of concerned administrative authorities - By and large best to assess and to handle situation depending upon peculiar needs and necessities within their special knowledge - Notifications dated 27/3/1974 and 6/7/1974 passed by Govt. confers powers on ADM - HC order set aside.
