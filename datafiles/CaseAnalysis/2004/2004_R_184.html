Government of A. P. and Another v Road Rollers Owners Welfare Association and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1107; (2004) 6 SCC 210; 2004(9) SCALE 34

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1963

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1963 - Whether road rollers were not motor vehicles under Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 and hence no tax could be levied on road rollers? - Held, a road roller is specifically included in definition of a light motor vehicle - If a road roller is a light motor vehicle then it is a motor vehicle - It is settled law that if intention of legislature is clear and unambiguous, then courts cannot ignore clear wording and hold to contrary - As Act categorically provides that a road roller is a motor vehicle, we fail to understand how High Court, even after noticing definition, could have held that road roller was not a motor vehicle - Appeals allowed.
