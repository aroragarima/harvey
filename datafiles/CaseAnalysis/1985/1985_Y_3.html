Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, Punjab, J And K, Chandigarh, Patia v Yuvraj Amrinder Singh Etc.

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 324; (1985) 4 SCC 608; AIR 1986 SC 959; 1985 (49) CTR 211; [1985] 156 ITR 525; 1985(2) SCALE 687; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 565; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 565; [1985] 23 TAXMAN 25; 1986 (2) TLR 23; 1986 (1) UJ 674

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Exempted Assets

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Insurance Act, 1938, s. 2(11) - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 5(1)(vi), 2(e) and 2(m) - Expression 'any policy of insurance' in s. 5 (1)(iv) - Right or interest in commutable annuity policy is covered by expression 'policy of insurance' - Exegibility - Held, right or interest therein is exempt from wealth tax - Appeals dismissed.
