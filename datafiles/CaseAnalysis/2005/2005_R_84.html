Raju S. Jethmalani and Others v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 352; (2005) 11 SCC 222; JT 2005 (5) SC 244; 2005(4) SCALE 688; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966, s. 26(1) - De-reservation of land by impugned notification - Whether Govt. can prepare a development plan or/and without acquiring the land and deprive owner of land from using that land? - Held, there is no prohibition for preparing development plan comprising of private land but that plan cannot be implemented unless private land is acquired by Govt. for development purpose - Order of HC set aside - Appeals allowed .
