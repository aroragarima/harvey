Shreeram Finance Corporation v Yasin Khan And Others

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 657; (1989) 3 SCC 476; AIR 1989 SC 1769; 1989 (2) BankCLR 499; 1989 (2) CCC 640; 1989 (2) GLH 301; JT 1989 (3) SC 146; 1989 MahLJ 849; 1989 MPLJ 665; 1989(2) SCALE 51; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 484; 1989 (2) UJ 505

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Partnership Act, 1932 - Maintainability of suit - Change in constitution of registered firm but current partners not shown as partners in Register of Firms - Held, suit not maintainable in view of provisions of s. 69(2) of Partnership Act - Appeal fails.
