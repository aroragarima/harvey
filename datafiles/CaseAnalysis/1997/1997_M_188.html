Bihar State Electricity Board and Another v Messrs Usha Martin Industries and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2086; (1997) 5 SCC 289; AIR 1997 SC 2489; 1997 (3) CLT 15; JT 1997 (5) SC 431; 1997(4) SCALE 139; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 279; 1997 (5) Supreme 158; 1997 (2) UJ 238

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Excise

Summary: Electricity and Energy - Excise - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, ss. 49 and 59 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, ss. 9, 64A - Electricity tariff - (A) Electricity Board to decide electricity tariff as its fixation is a policy matter Whether court can interfere - (B) Plea for reduction of tariff when excise duty on Board withdrawn, on the basis on s. 64A of 1930 Act - Tenanbility of plea - Held, Court cannot lay down what should be the tariff - Held on facts, not tenable - Appeal allowed.
