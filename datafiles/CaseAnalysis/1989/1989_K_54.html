Krishna Pillai v T. A. Rajendran and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, P.B. Sawant, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 313; (1990) Supp SCC 121; (1990) SCC (Cr) 646

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Keywords: Marriage & Divorce

Summary: Family & Personal - Women & Children - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 482 - Child Marriage Restraint Act, 1929, s. 9 - Whether cognizance was barred u/s. 9 of Act? - Held, magisterial action in this case was beyond period of one year from date of commission of offence - Magistrate was not competent to take cognizance when he did not view of bar u/s. 9 of Act - Prosecution quashed - Appeal allowed.
