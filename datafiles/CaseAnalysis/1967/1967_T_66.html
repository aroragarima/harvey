Â Bhagat Raja v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, Vishishtha Bhargava, K. Subba Rao, J.C. Shah, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 453; AIR 1967 SC 1606; 1968 (1) MLJ(SC) 77; 1968 (1) SCJ 431; [1967] 3 S.C.R. 302

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, s. 13 - Mineral Concession Rules, 1960, r. 55 - Companies Act, 1956 - Order passed by public Authority - Revision under - Simply 'dismmissed' or 'allowed' - Central Govt, held, must make a speaking order - Appeals allowed.
