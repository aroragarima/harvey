Kulwant Rai v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 492; (1981) 4 SCC 245; (1981) SCC (Cr) 826; AIR 1982 SC 126; 1982 CRLR 23

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code,1860 - Applicability of provisions of s. 300 cl. 3, s. 304, Part II - Quarrel followed by assault - One blow with a dagger in epigastrium area of deceased - Who succumbing to injury - Absence of prior enmity or intention of accused - Held, cl. 3 of s. 300 would not be attracted - Case would fall u/s. 304, Part II - Order Accordingly.
