Nanhoo Mal and Others v Hira Mal and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 506; (1976) 3 SCC 211; AIR 1975 SC 2140; 1976 (2) ALR 162; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 809; 1975 UJ 805

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Municipal & Local Govt. - Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, s. 43 B - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Election to office of President of Municipal Board challenged - Allowed only by election petition under Act - Writ jurisdiction of High Court - Held, writ jurisdiction of HC is excluded - Appeal allowed.
