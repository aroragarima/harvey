Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.269UD(1) , 269UE - Income Tax rules, 1962, r. 48l - Constitution art.14 , form 37-i - Validity of the provisions of Chapter XX-C inserted in the 1961 Act by the Finance Act of 1986 - Reckoning of statutory period of two months u/s. 269 UD of the Act i n case of Acquisition of Property - Held, in cases other than this pending before SC and HCs, period to be reckoned from date of decision of the concerned cases and in case of vacation of stay, from date of such vacation - As regards cases pending before Income Tax Authorities, Form 371 to be deemed to have been filed on date of judgment - This order issued as supplemental to and part of main judgment in the above case - Order accordingly.
