State of Jharkhand and Others v Ambay Cements and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, S.N. Variava, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 974; (2005) 1 SCC 368; 2004 (178) E.L.T. 55; 2006 (3) JhrCR(SC) 183; JT 2004 (10) SC 93; 2004(9) SCALE 501; 2005 (139) STC 74; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 125

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Industrial Policy of 1995 of Govt. of Bihar, cls. 16.1 and 16.2 - For giving certain incentives to the newly set up industrial units in the small scale sector - Exemption from Sales Tax on purchase of raw material and exemption of Sales Tax on sale of finished products - Whether conditions prescribed by Authorities for grant of exemption are mandatory for availing same?; whether HC can in exercise of writ jurisdiction can direct grant of exemption contrary to terms thereof and overlooking statutory conditions prescribed for such grant in absence of any challenge to validity of such conditions? - Held, grant of temporary registration certificate was not sufficient and same was not equivalent to prior permission - Condition prescribed by authorities for grant of exemption are mandatory for availing exemption and HC exercising writ jurisdiction cannot direct grant of exemption - Appeal allowed.
