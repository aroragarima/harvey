Bahadul Alias Ghanshyam Padhan v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 527; (1979) 4 SCC 346; (1979) SCC (Cr) 982; AIR 1979 SC 1262; 1979 CRLJ 1075; 1979 CRLR 177; 1979 (48) CutLT 456; 1979 UJ 348

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 8 and 27 - Admissibility of evidence - Recovery of weapon - Weapon merely taken out by accused from beneath his cot and handed over to police without any statement - That too from a place accessible to all - Held, factum of recovery is barred u/s. 27 and weapon cannot be relied on as admissible u/s. 8 - Appeal allowed.
