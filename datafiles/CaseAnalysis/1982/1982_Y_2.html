Food Corporation of India and Another v Yadav Engineer and Contractor

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 181; (1982) 2 SCC 499; AIR 1982 SC 1302; 1983 ARBLR 123; 1982 MahLJ 803; 1982(1) SCALE 591; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 95; 1982 UJ 605

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure

Keywords: Arbitration Act

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether 'taking any other steps in proceedings' includes each and every step - Plea that application did not contain averment and that applicant was ready and willing to do necessary things for proper conduct or arbitration - Held, it does not includes each and every step - Plea cannot be raised for first time in revision.
