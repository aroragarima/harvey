H.L. Trehan And Ors. Etc. v Union Of India And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, N.D. Ojha, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 284; (1989) SCC (L&S) 246; (1989) 1 SCC 764; AIR 1989 SC 568; 1989 (9) ATC 651; [1989] 65 Comp Cas 673; 1989 (1) CompLJ 191; JT 1988 (4) SC 464; 1989 LabIC 1031; 1994 (3) LLJ 1113; 1989 (1) LLN 294; 1988(2) SCALE 1376; 1989 (1) SLR 7; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 925; 1989 (1) UJ 54

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Caltex Acquisition of Shares of Caltex Oil Refining (India) Limited and of Undertakings in India of Caltex (India) Limited Act, 1977

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Corporate - Caltex (Acquisition of Shares of Caltex Oil Refining (India) Ltd and Undertakings in India of Caltex (India) Ltd ) Act, 1977, ss. 11(1) and 11(2) - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 9 A, sch 2 item 6 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 226 - Service conditions - (A) Change of - By management of CORIL - To disadvantage of staff of CIL - Opportunity of being heard, given only after alterations - Validity - (B) Whether power to alteration u/s. 11(1) is unguided or arbitrary - Held, (A) alteration order set aside - (B) Not unguided or arbitrary and alterations to be made 'duly' as provided u/s. 11(2) of Act - Appeal dismissed.
