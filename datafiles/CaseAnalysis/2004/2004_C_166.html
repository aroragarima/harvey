Escorts Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi - Ii

Supreme Court of India

25 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 883; (2004) 8 SCC 335; 2004 (173) E.L.T. 113; JT 2004 (9) SC 265; 2004 (65) RLT 111; 2004(9) SCALE 6; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 603

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944 - Central Excise and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4(1) - Central Excise Valuation Rules, 1975, r. 6(b) - Levy of duty - Value of goods captively consumed - Whether principles laid down in Ashok Leyland's case applicable in present case? - Held, CEGAT did not consider applicability of Ashok Leyland's case in background of r. 6 of Valuation Rules though it has substantial bearing on dispute - Remitted matter to CEGAT for considering factual aspect and applicability of Ashok Leyland's case to facts of present case - Appeals allowed.
