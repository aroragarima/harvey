Asit Kumar Kar v State Of West Bengal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, R.M. Lodha

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 79; (2009) 2 SCC 703; (2009) 1 SCC (Cr) 851; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 282; 2009 (2) AWC 1628; 2009 (2) CalLT(SC) 73; 2009(1) SCALE 745; [2009] 1 S.C.R. 469

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Excise

Keywords: Principle Of Natural Justice

Summary: Excise - Policy of the State of West Bengal of granting additional licences for foreign liquor and country made spirit - Directed cancellation of the licences for the auctions held on 20.03.2005, 21.03.2005 and 22.03.2005 - Held, cancellation order was passed without hearing the persons whose licences were ordered to be cancelled - No adverse orders should be passed against a party without hearing him - Recalled the directions of the judgment - Petitions disposed of.
