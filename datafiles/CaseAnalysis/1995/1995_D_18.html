Angile Insulations v Davy Ashmore India Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1018; (1995) 4 SCC 153; AIR 1995 SC 1766; AIR 1995 SCW 2763; 1995 (26) ALR 302; 1995 (2) BC 372; 1995 (60) DLT 522; JT 1995 (5) SC 179; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 74; 1995 (3) PLR 275; 1995(3) SCALE 203; 1995 (1) SCJ 540; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 443

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 23 and 26 - Ousting of jurisdiction of court by agreement among parties when there are two or more competent courts to entertain a suit - And parties agreeing to vest jurisdiction in one such court through contract - Legality of contract in absence of unambiguity - Held, it is not hit by s. 23 or s. 28 of Contract Act - Appeal dismissed.
