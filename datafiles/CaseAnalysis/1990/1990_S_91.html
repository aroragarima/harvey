Life Insurance Corporation of India v Smt G. M. Channabasamma

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 954; (1991) 1 SCC 357; 1991 (1) ACJ 303; AIR 1991 SC 392; 1991 ALR 118; 1991 (1) CCC 133; [1991] 70 Comp Cas 635; JT 1991 (5) SC 73; 1990(2) SCALE 1191; 1991 (1) UJ 218

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Summary: Insurance Act, 1938 - Pre-Life Insurance - Contract thereof - Assured is under solemn obligation to make full disclosure of material facts - Allegation of insured being guilty of making false representations and suppressing material facts - Burden of proof - Held, it is on corporation - Appeal dismissed.
