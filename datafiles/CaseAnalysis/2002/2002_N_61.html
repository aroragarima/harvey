Supe Dei others v National Insurance Company Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

16 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Brijesh Kumar, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2275; (2009) 4 SCC 513; (2009) 2 SCC (Cr) 528; 2005 (1) ACC 63; 2002 ACJ 1166; 2002 (48) ALR 237; 2002 (4) AWC 2734; JT 2002 (Supp1) SC 451

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988-Sections 163A, 166 and Second Schedule - Motor accident compensation-Multiplier-Rate of interest-Age of victim on date of accident 32 years-Claim not under Section 163A but under Section 166-No reason why multiplier under Second Schedule cannot be taken as guide for determining compensation under Section 166-Hence multiplier of 17 to be applied as against 15 applied by Tribunal and High Court-9% is appropriate rate of interest instead of 6%-Order of Tribunal and judgment of High Court modified accordingly.
