Â Lala Ram Swarup And Others v Shikar Chand And Another

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, P. Satyanarayana Raju

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 218; AIR 1966 SC 893; 1966 ALJ 360; [1966] 2 S.C.R. 553

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ejectment Suit

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s.115 - Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947, ss. 3 (1), 3 (2), 3 (4), 7-E, 7-F and 16 - Appellant got permission from Commr. for eviction of respondent tenant from premises - Whether permission granted by Commr. without jurisdiction and as such, a nullity ? - Held, relevant provisions of Act did not intend, even prior to amendment of 1954, to limit jurisdiction of Commr. only to cases where irregularity or illegality bad been committed by District Magistrate in granting or refusing, to grant permission - Appeal allowed.
