U.P. State Road Transport Corporation and Others v A. K. Parul

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1357; (1998) 9 SCC 416; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1194; AIR 1999 SC 1552; 1998 (4) AWC 67; 1999 (1) AWC 632; 1999 (1) CalLT(SC) 77; 1998 (2) CLR 1070; 1998 (80) FLR 939; JT 1998 (7) SC 203; 1999 (3) LLJ 1093; 1999(1) SCALE 138; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 201

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Road Transport Corporations Act,1950, s. 1 -Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11A - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Punishment - Quantum - Judicial review - Bus conductor charged for taking passengers without ticket - Enquiry held - Charges proved - Held, order of removal commensurate with gravity of charge - Interference with by High Court - Not justified.
