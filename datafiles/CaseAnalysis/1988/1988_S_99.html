Desoola Rama Rao And Anr. v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 374; (1988) Supp SCC 221; (1988) SCC (L&S) 659; AIR 1988 SC 857; 1988 (7) ATC 441; 1988 (56) FLR 772; JT 1988 (1) SC 412; 1988 LabIC 994; 1988(1) SCALE 384; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 24; 1988 (3) SLJ 101; 1988 (1) SLR 690; 1988 (1) UJ 568

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: AP State and Subordinate Service Rules 1962, Andhra Pradesh (Roads and Buildings) Engineering Service Rules 1965

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - A.P. State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1966, r.23(a) - Service rules - (A) Regularisation of service - Retrospectively - Date of regularisation - Not anterior to date of appointment - Constitutinality of - (B) Inter se seniority - Fixation - Absence of any rule - Held, regularisation would not be vitiated on account of arbitrariness - In absence of rule, length of service is basis for fixing seniority -

Appeal dismissed.
