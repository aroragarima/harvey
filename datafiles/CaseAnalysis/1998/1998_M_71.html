Sadanandan Bhadran v Madhavan Sunil Kumar

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1179; (1998) 6 SCC 514; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1471; AIR 1998 SC 3043; AIR 1998 SCW 2902; 1998 (34) ALR 313; 1998 (4) AWC 753; [1998] 94 Comp Cas 812; 1998 CRLJ 4066; JT 1998 (6) SC 48; 1998 (2) KLT 765; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 178

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, ss. 138 and 142 - Dishonour of cheque - Complaint - Cause of action - Multiple presentation of cheque during its validity period - Held, accrue each time a fresh right in favour of payee - But not cause of action as it arise only once.
