Municipal Corporation Chandigarh and Others v Messrs Shantikunj Investment Private Limited Etc

Supreme Court of India

28 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 73; (2006) 4 SCC 109; AIR 2006 SC 1270; JT 2006 (3) SC 1; 2006 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 980; 2006 (2) RCR(Civil) 26; 2006 (1) RCR(Rent) 238; 2006(2) SCALE 712; 2006 (3) SCJ 558; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 768

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Punjab Urban Estates (Development and Regulation)Act, 1964, Punjab Urban Estates (Sales of Sites) Rules, 1965, Haryana Urban Development Authority ( Disposal of Land and Buildings) Regulations, 1978

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Capital of Punjab (Development and Regulation) Act, 1952, ss. 2(b), 8 - Punjab Municipal Act, 1911 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 2(a) - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, ss. 105, 108 - Punjab Urban Estates (Development and Regulation)Act, 1964 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 67 - Chandigarh Lease-hold of Sites and Buildings Rules, 1973, r. 3(2) - Punjab Urban Estates (Sales of Sites) Rules, 1965 - Whether grant of amenities is a condition precedent or not? - Held, though it was not a condition precedent but there is obligation on part of Administration to provide necessary facilities for full enjoyment of same by allottees - Remitted matter to HC to see that in cases where facilities like kutcha road, drainage, drinking water, sewerage, street lighting have not been provided - HC shall examine each individual case and consider grant of proportionate relief - Matter remitted to HC.
