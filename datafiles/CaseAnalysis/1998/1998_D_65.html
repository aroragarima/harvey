Ganesh Shet v Dr. C.S.G.K. Setty & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 741; (1998) 5 SCC 381; AIR 1998 SC 2216; 1998 (33) ALR 512; 1998 (3) CLT 35; JT 1998 (4) SC 181; 1998(3) SCALE 643; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 479; 1998 (5) SLT 346; 1998 (5) Supreme 56

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s.20 - 'Concluded contract' - Sale of property - Property owned jointly by three brothers - Purchaser clearly stated that while meeting one of the brothers, he told that he is yet to consult his two brothers about sale consideration - Tenor of several letters between parties showing that sale consideration was not finalised in their meeting - Held, there was no concluded contract between parties and decree for specific performance could not be passed.
