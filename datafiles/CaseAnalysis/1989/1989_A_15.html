Shubhlaxmi Mills Limited v Additional Commissioner Of Income-Tax,Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 739; (1989) 2 SCC 465; AIR 1989 SC 1406; 1989 (3) CompLJ 94; 1989 (77) CTR 31; 1989 (77) CTR 33; [1989] 177 ITR 193; JT 1989 (2) SC 1; 1989(1) SCALE 724; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 86; [1989] 43 TAXMAN 267

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 33 and 34 - Whether benefit of carrying forward of development rebate is available to assessee ? - Development rebate contemplated by sub-s. (1) of s. 33 of Act, 1961 cannot be allowed as deduction unless a reserve account has been created in previous year in which installation or first use occurs - Held, reserve account has not been created in previous year in which installation or first use of machinery etc. occurs as required u/s. 33(1) - Answered in favour of Revenue.
