Thakur Das (Dead) By LRs v State of Madhya Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, N.L. Untwalia, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 396; (1978) SCC (Cr) 21; (1978) 1 SCC 27; AIR 1978 SC 1; 1977 (3) ALR 720; 1978 (1) ALT 81; 1978 (1) ALT(SC) 81; 1978 CRLJ 1; 1977 CrLR(SC) 484; 1978 MLJ 255; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 732

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 423, 435 and 439 - Essential commodities Act, 1955, ss. 3 and 6 - Appellant did not maintain the records of daily accounts - License of appellant was cancelled by collector of district - Cancellation order was set aside by Sessions Court but in revision High Court restored the collector's order of cancellation - Whether HC correct in restoring the cancellation order of collector in its revisional powers? - Held, HC exceeded its revisional powers as the matter was decided by Sessions Court in appellate jurisdiction - Jurisdiction u/s. 439 CrPC, 1973 can be only exercised in exceptional cases where there is glaring defect in the procedure - Appeal allowed, Sessions Court order restored.
