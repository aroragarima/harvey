V. Karnal Durai v District Collector, Tuticorin and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 872; (1999) 1 SCC 475; JT 1998 (8) SC 301; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 94; 1998(6) SCALE 350; 1999 (1) SCJ 217; 1998 (10) SLT 25; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 206; 1998 (9) Supreme 99

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Summary: Mines & Minerals - Commercial - Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, r. 8(6) - Whether lease granted to appellant would be for whole three years or for remaining period - Held,that if rules are amended during pendency of application for mining lease, Amended Rule is to be applied while disposing of application - Court opines that it makes no difference whether delay has occurred on account of keeping applications pending or on account of appeal being filed - Court are of view that amended r. 8(8)(a) which came into force on 19-12-1996 applied to appeal which came to be disposed of on 19-12-1996 - Leave granted.
