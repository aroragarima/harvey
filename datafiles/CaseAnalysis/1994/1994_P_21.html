P.V. Papanna v K. Padmanabhaiah

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1449; (1994) 2 SCC 316; AIR 1994 SC 1577; 1994 (23) ALR 363; JT 1994 (1) SC 371; 1994 (2) MLJ 44; 1994 (2) MLJ(SC) 44; 1994 (1) RCJ 191; 1994 (1) RCR(Rent) 504; 1994 (1) RentLR 226; 1994(1) SCALE 386; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 642; 1994 (1) UJ 311

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 21(1) - Tenancy - Eviction Decree on ground of bona fide requirement becoming final in highest court - Effect of subsequent (event) death of landlord before execution - Held, decree cannot be reopened on plea that it has become infructuous - LR entitled to execute decree - Appeal allowed.
