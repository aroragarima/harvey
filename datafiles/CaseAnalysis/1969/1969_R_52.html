Rampur Distillery Company Ltd. v Company Law Board & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover, S.M. Sikri, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 98; (1969) 2 SCC 774; AIR 1970 SC 1789; [1970] 40 Comp Cas 916; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 177

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Companies Act - Provisons of s. 326 - Appointment of managing agent - For approval , Central Government must give reference to both present conduct and the past record of the person - Satisfaction is the objective - To consider whether Central Government acts judicially - Held, that court is not duty bound to consider sufficiency of grounds but to consider existence of conditions required by the section - Appeal Dismissed
