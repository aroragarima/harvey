Motilal Jain v Ramdasi Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 370; (2000) 6 SCC 420; AIR 2000 SC 2408; AIR 2000 SCW 2554; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 33; 2001 (43) ALR 98; JT 2000 (8) SC 59; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 202; 2001 (127) PLR 231; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 545; 2001 (92) RD 59; 2000(5) SCALE 232; 2000 (5) SLT 534; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 615; 2000 (5) Supreme 165

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 16 and 21 - Contract for purchase of property - Stipulating for execution of sale deed within 5 months - Suit for specific performance - Filed 9 months after last notice - Delay - Denial of relief - Held, not belated - Plaint clearly indicate readiness and willingness of plaintiff - Alternative relief cannot be ground to refuse main relief of specific performance to plaintiff.
