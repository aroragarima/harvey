P.Kasilingam And Ors v P.S.G. College Of Technology And Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 397; (1995) Supp2 SCC 348; AIR 1995 SC 1395; AIR 1999 SCW 2065; JT 1995 (3) SC 193; 1995(2) SCALE 387; 1995 (2) SCJ 155; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 1061; 1995 (3) SLR 59

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Education Norms

Summary: T.N. Private Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976, ss.2(8) and 53; T.N. Private Colleges (Regulation) Rules, 1976, r.2(b) - 'College' - 'Means and includes' - Held, indicates definition is exhaustive and not extensive - college would cover only the educational institutions falling under the categories.
