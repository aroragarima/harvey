Kapur Chand Jain (Dead) and Others v State Government of H. P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1242; (1999) 2 SCC 89; AIR 1999 SC 3470

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Administrative - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 28 - Acquisition of land - Compensation - Interest on solatium - Validity - Petitioners referred to a three-Judge Bench judgment of SC in Union of India v. Ram Mehar, 1972 Indlaw SC 458 and also later two decisions of two-Judge Benches of SC in Periyar and Pareekanni Rubbers Ltd. v. State of Kerala, 1990 Indlaw SC 989 and Narain Das Jain v. Agra Nagar Mahapalika, 1991 Indlaw SC 1055 - Relying on these judgments, petitioner submitted that for applicability of s. 28 of the Act solatium had to be considered as a component of compensation and interest could be paid thereon; and that the HC wrongly not granted interest on solatium.

Held, in view of this conflict of decisions and also in view of the further fact that the three-Judge Bench of SC in Prem Nath Kapur, 1995 Indlaw SC 1987, had no opportunity to refer to the earlier decision of a three-Judge Bench in Ram Mehar, 1972 Indlaw SC 458, it is directed that instant special leave petitions be placed for decision before a three-Judge Bench of SC. Order accordingly.
