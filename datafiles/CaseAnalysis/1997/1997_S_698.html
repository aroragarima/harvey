Hira Man v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1431; (1997) 11 SCC 630; AIR 1997 SC 3288; 1997 ALJ 1912; 1997 (77) FLR 257; JT 1997 (7) SC 324; 1997 LabIC 3335; 1997(5) SCALE 430; 1997 (5) SLR 224; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 396; 1997 (7) Supreme 621; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1531

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Government servant

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Uttar Pradesh Recruitment of Dependents of Govt. Servants Dying in Harness Rules, 1974, rr. 4, 5 and 8 - Compassionate appointment - S. 4 of Act - Gives an overriding effect - Held, cannot be made contrary to quota rule - Reading rr. 4, 5 and 8 together - Only a limited overriding effect.
