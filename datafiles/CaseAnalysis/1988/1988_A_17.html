National Agricultural Cooperative Marketingfederatlon Of Ind v Alimenta S.A.

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 255; (1989) Supp1 SCC 308; AIR 1989 SC 818; 1989 (2) ARBLR 22; 1989 (1) CompLJ 147; JT 1988 (4) SC 721; 1988(2) SCALE 1612; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 548; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 548; 1989 (1) UJ 207

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Two claims arising out of two contracts - Supreme Court to save bar of limitation permitting claimants to continue legal proceedings in suit - Held, claimant can give up part of its claims related to one of contract - Merely because of that proceeding of arbitration, suit cannot be stayed.
