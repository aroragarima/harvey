Baijnath Mahton and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1207; (1993) Supp3 SCC 1; (1993) SCC (Cr) 849; AIR 1993 SC 2323; 1993 (2) Crimes 944; 1993 CRLJ 2833; 1993 CRLR 495; JT 1993 (4) SC 95; 1993(3) SCALE 53; 1993 (2) UJ 315

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 100 and 302 - Validity of conviction u/s. 394, Part I when accused exceeded right of private defence and killed deceased working with him in field 24 years ago - Held, proper but sentence reduced to two years RI - Appeal dismissed.
