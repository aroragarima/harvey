Assistant Sales Tax Officer and Others v B. C. Kame, Proprietor Kame Photo Studio

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.C. Gupta, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 341; (1977) 1 SCC 634; AIR 1977 SC 1642; 1977 (6) CTR 67; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 435; 1977 (39) STC 237

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: M.P. General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(n) - Contract of work and labour and contract for sale - Determination - Photographer - Preparing and supplying to the customer positive prints of photos taken for him - Held, undertakes a contract of work and labour and does not enter into a sale transaction - Appeal dismissed.
