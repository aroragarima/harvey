Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1973 - Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 - Violation of - Show cause notice - Legality of - Held, it is desirable and proper for HC to indicate reasons which has weighed with it in granting an extra ordinary relief in form of an interim protection - Further held, proceedings emanating from show cause notice shall be continued.
