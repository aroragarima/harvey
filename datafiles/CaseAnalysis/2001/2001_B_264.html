Bongaigaon Refinary and Petrochemicals Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

24 July 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20817; (2001) 10 SCC 289; 2001 (170) CTR 257; [2001] 251 ITR 329; JT 2001 (Supp2) SC 46; 2001 (8) Supreme 551; [2001] 119 TAXMAN 488

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, ss. 4,56 - Formative period - Investments - Whether income derived by assessee during formation period was taxable income - Held, Tribunal was justified in holding items of income derived by assessee during formation period for main business, was not taxable income but were to be adjusted against project cost for main business for which company was set up - Appeal allowed.
