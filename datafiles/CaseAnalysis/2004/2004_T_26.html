Eagle Flask Industries Limited v Talegaon Dabhade Municipal Council and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 956; (2004) 8 SCC 640; 2005 (1) BC 79; [2005] 125 Comp Cas 36; 2004(9) SCALE 16; 2005 (140) STC 280; 2005 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 628; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 137

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Octroi - Maharashtra Municipal Council Nagar Panchayat and Industrial Townships Act, 1965 - Maharashtra Municipalities (Octroi) Rules, 1968, r. 15, Entry 86 and 53(c) - Levy of octroi - Challenging levy of octroi - Held, goods are clearly covered under Entry 56 - It appears that reference was not made to Entry 56 before HC - Confirm HC's view though on a different basis i.e. with reference to Entry 56 - Appeal disposed of.
