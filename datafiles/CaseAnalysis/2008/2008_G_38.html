Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Hindu Marriage Act - Whether a marriage which is otherwise dead emotionally and practically should be continued for name sake - Held, since the marriage between the parties is dead for all practical purposes and there is no chance of it being retrieved, the continuance of such marriage would itself amount to cruelty - Appeal disposed of.
