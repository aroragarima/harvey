Government of A.P v P.B. Vijayakumar and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1374; (1995) 4 SCC 520; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1056; AIR 1995 SC 1648; 1995 (30) ATC 576; 1995 (2) CLR 112; JT 1995 (7) SC 489; 1995 LabIC 2236; 1995 (2) LLN 647; 1995(3) SCALE 613; 1995 (3) SLJ 89; 1995 (4) SLR 45; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 462; 1995 (2) UJ 480

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh State and Subordinate Service Rules

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 15, 15(3), 16, 16(4) and 309 - Andhra Pradesh State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1966, r. 22 A - Service Rules - Reservation of post for women - (A) Permissibility u/arts. 15 and 16 of Constitution - (B) What constitutes reservation? - (C) Validity of r. 22 A (preference to women in direct recruitment) in light of art. 15(3) of Constitution - Held, permissible u/arts. 15(3) and 16 - Separate quota, reserved for a special category of persons, and within that category appointments to reserved posts may be made in order of merit, but not to compete on equal terms with open category, to protect this weak category against competition - Rule held to be within ambit of art. 15(3) and hence valid - Appeal allowed.
