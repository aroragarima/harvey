Rajesh Jagdamba Avasthi v State of Goa

Supreme Court of India

03 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1009; (2005) 9 SCC 773; AIR 2005 SC 1389; 2005 (1) RCR(Criminal) 406; 2004(9) SCALE 539

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 20(b)(ii) - Trial Court and HC convicted accused and sentenced to rigorous imprisonment of ten years - Appeal against conviction - Held, there is justification for argument that since seal as well as packets were in custody of same person, there was every possibility of seized substance being tampered with, and that is only hypothesis on which discrepancy in weight can be explained -There is serious doubt about truthfulness of prosecution case - Conviction set aside.
