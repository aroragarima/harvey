Narcotics Control Bureau v Dilip Pralhad Namade

Supreme Court of India

18 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 212; (2004) 3 SCC 619; (2004) SCC (Cr) 916; AIR 2004 SC 2950; 2004 (1) Crimes 429; 2004 CRLJ 1815; 2004 (92) ECC 705; 2004 (114) ECR 151; 2004 (2) JCC 689; JT 2004 (4) SC 386; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 320; 2004 (61) RLT 722; 2004(3) SCALE 474; 2004 (1) ShimLC 438; 2004 (2) SLT 772; 2004 (2) Supreme 456

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Non-Supply, Narcotics Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985

Summary: NDPS Act - granting of bail - there is a prohibition on the grant of bail in terms of Section 37 of the NDPS Act - only under the specified conditions in the section can bail can be granted - non-supply of documents pursuant to the court's order is not one of the grounds - High Court in error while granting bail on this ground - has overlooked the purpose behind s. 37 - bail cancelled - appeal allowed.
