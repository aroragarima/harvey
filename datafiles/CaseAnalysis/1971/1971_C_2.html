C. A. P. Andiappan v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 415; (1971) 3 SCC 659; AIR 1972 SC 1682; [1971] 82 ITR 876; 1973 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 88; 1971 UJ 747

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 49A - International law - Agreement for relief or for Avoidance of Double Taxation in India and Ceylon - Effect of - Held, amount of tax attributable to the Ceylonese law is that which was ultimately levied on the assessee and not one leviable in Ceylon on a non-resident - Appeals dismissed.
