Supreme Court of India

15 January 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 418; AIR 1963 SC 1405; 1963 CurLJ 25; 1964 (1) MLJ(SC) 60; 1964 (1) SCJ 187; [1964] 1 S.C.R. 515

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Indian Contract Act, 1872 - s. 74 - Compensation for breach of contract and mesne profits - Issues arise - (A) Whether the plaintiff or defendant has committed breach of the contract? - Held, defendant committed breach of contract - At the time of execution of agreement the defendant had inspected the sale deed, lease and the sketch plan provided by plaintiff - Plaintiff had put the defendant in possession of the property - The defendant despite repeated requests failed to pay the balance remaining due by him and to obtain the sale deed in his favour - Held, plaintiff carried out his part of the contract, but the defendant failed to pay the balance price and honour his obligations - (B) Whether s. 74 applies to the stipulations for forfeiture of amounts deposited or paid under the contract - Held, s. 74 of the Indian Contract Act deals with the measure of damages in two classes of cases (i) where the contract names a sum to be paid in case of breach and (ii) where the contract contains any other stipulation by way of penalty - The covenant for forfeiture of price paid on delivery of possession is a stipulation by way of penalty - Held, the quantum of damages by way of penalty for breach of a stipulation is reasonable compensation not exceeding the penalty stipulated - Held, jurisdiction of the Court to award compensation in case of breach of contract is unqualified except as to the maximum stipulated, but compensation has to be reasonable, and that imposes upon the Court duty to award compensation according to settled principles - Plaintiff allowed to retain only earnest money and entitled to compensation @ 6% as it accrues month after month from 1-06-1949 till delivery of possession - Appeal dismissed.
