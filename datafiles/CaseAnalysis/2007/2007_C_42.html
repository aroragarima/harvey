D. Dwarakanantha Reddy v Chaitnya Bharathi Educational Society And Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 457; (2007) 6 SCC 130; AIR 2007 SC 1794; JT 2007 (6) SC 224; 2007 (6) MahLJ 19; 2007 (4) MPLJ 365; 2007(6) SCALE 256; 2007 (5) SCJ 726; [2007] 5 S.C.R. 775

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Residential House, Andhra Pradesh (Talengana Area) Public Societies Registration Act, 1350

Summary: Appeal against the judgement of High Court of Andhra Pradesh dismissing the petition filed to challenge the judgment of Additional Chief Judge - Petition filed for a declaration that appellants were legally inducted members and were entitled to participate in the management and administration of the Society - Contention of the Society that an amount ought to be paid by a person before he is admitted as Patron Member - Order passed by Civil Court that no prima facie case could be made out confirmed by High Court - Hence appeals -- Held, since no payment was made by the persons claiming Promoter-Members, their membership was 'void' - Appeal dismissed.
