S.V.R. Mudaliar (Dead) By Lrs. And Ors. v Mrs. Rajabu F. Buhari (Dead) By Lrs. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1015; (1995) 4 SCC 15; AIR 1995 SC 1607; AIR 1995 SCW 2528; JT 1995 (3) SC 614; 1995(2) SCALE 720; 1995 (2) SCJ 660; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 312

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, ss. 41 and 54 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 100 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 10 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 20, 15 - Finding of fact - Reversal by Appellate Court - Contract - Enforceability - Genuineness of document - Sale deeds of two properties - Subsequent deed for reconveyance - Sale deeds not having stipulation about recoveyance - Reconveyance deed signed by agent of purchaser - Existence of reconveyance - Supported by letter from seller to purchaser - Held, reconveyance deed genuine - Reasons on findings of Trial Court must be considered by Appellate Court - Enforceability of contract not dependent on existence of intention to create legal relations - Refusal of relief risen of specific performance - Not merely on ground of hike in price of property during pendency of litigation.
