Ranjan Dwivedi v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 276; (1983) 3 SCC 307; (1983) SCC (Cr) 581; AIR 1983 SC 624; 1983 (1) Crimes 1170; 1983 CRLJ 1052; 1983(1) SCALE 487; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 982

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Summary: Constitution, arts. 22, 39A and 32 - CrPC, 1973, s. 304(1) - Right to be supplied with lawer by state in trial for murder before Sessions Court - Entitlement as to writ of mandamus for the enforcement of the Directive Principle enshrined in art. 39A of Constitution - Held, mandamus cannot be issued by Supreme Court u/art. 32 - Remedy lies u/s. 304, CrPC, 1973 - Petition Dismissed.
