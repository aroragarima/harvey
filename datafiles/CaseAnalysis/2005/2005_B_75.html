State Bank of India and another v Bela Bagchi and others

Supreme Court of India

31 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 522; (2005) 7 SCC 435; (2005) SCC (L&S) 940; AIR 2005 SC 3272; 2005 (6) AWC 5523; 2005 (3) CLR 420; 2005 (4) ESC 551; 2005 (107) FLR 258; JT 2005 (8) SC 96; 2005 (4) LLN 1; 2005(7) SCALE 60; 2006 (3) SLR 184; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1084; 2005 (6) Supreme 87

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - State Bank Of India Act, 1955, ss. 43, 50 - State Bank of India (Supervising Staff) Service Rules, 1975, rr. 20-A, 20-B - Continuation of proceedings after superannuation - Order of dismissal passed during extended period of service - Held illegal by HC - Appealed herein - Held, in terms of rr.20-A, 20-B bank had discretion to continue service of an employee for purpose of continuance and conclusions of departmental proceedings - HC was, in error in holding order of dismissal from service to be bad - Appeal allowed.
