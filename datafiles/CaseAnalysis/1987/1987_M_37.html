Mahabir Prasad Santuka And Ors v Collector, Cuttack And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28724; (1987) 1 SCC 587; AIR 1987 SC 720; JT 1987 (1) SC 401; 1987(1) SCALE 301; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 289

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.23 - Land Acquisition - High Court reducing the compensation awarded by the Sub-ordinate - Compensation for adjacent land acquired by the same notification was determined at higher rate - Land near the vicinity of industrial area is bound to rise - Offer to industrial entrepreneurs at concessional rate, if reflect the prevailing market value - Held, not a market value - Appeal allowed.
