H. M. Bags Manufacturer v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 99; (1997) 11 SCC 696; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 3; 1997 (22) RLT 7

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, ss. 37B, 11A - Classification - Reclassification - Trade notice No. 29 issued by the Board on 5-11-1992 - Effective date for raising the demand - Held, the effective date for raising the demand by the revenue cannot be any date prior to 05-11-1992 - Board's order excluded the intention of making the same effective from any earlier date - Tribunal order set aside - Appeals allowed.
