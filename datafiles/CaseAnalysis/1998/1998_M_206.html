Municipal Corporation of Delhi v Dharam Prakash Sharma and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1089; (1998) 7 SCC 221; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1800; AIR 1999 SC 293; JT 1998 (9) SC 175; 1998 (2) LLJ 625; 1998 (3) LLN 898; 1998 (7) SLT 118

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Payment Of Gratuity Act, 1972, s. 14 - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 1 - Gratuity - Employees of Municipal Corporation - Held, are entitled to payment of gratuity - Notwithstanding of fact that provisions of Pension Rules which provide for gratuity are made applicable to them.
