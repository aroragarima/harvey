Hari Kishan And Anr. v Sukhbir Singh And Ors

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 27; (1988) 4 SCC 551; (1988) SCC (Cr) 984; AIR 1988 SC 2127; 1988 BBCJ 161; 1988 (3) Crimes 541; 1989 CRLJ 116; 1989 CRLR 43; JT 1988 (3) SC 711; 1988(2) SCALE 426; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 571

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, s. 357(3) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 307 and 149 - Attempt to murder - Intention - Accused persons - Fight on sudden flare up - Not using cutting edge of Ballam - Fact, accused were armed with Barchha - Not proved - No incised wound on victim - Held, no intention to murder - Participation of two accused doubtful - Acquittal is proper.
