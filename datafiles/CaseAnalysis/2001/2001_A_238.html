Arun Agarwal v Nagreeka Exports Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21181; (2002) 10 SCC 101

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Letter Patent Rules, Chap. XIII-A - Jurisdiction - Summary disposal of the suit - Appellant filed a petition for revocation of leave on the ground that the Court has no jurisdiction to entertain and decide the matter - High Court directed the appellant to furnish security by way of bank guarantee failing which there will be a decree for the principal amount - Question regarding the jurisdiction of the Court was required to be decided as a preliminary issue - Matter remanded to decide the question of jurisdiction of the Court as a preliminary issue - Appeal disposed of.
