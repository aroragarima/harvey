Visakhapatnam Urban Development Authority v S. S. Naidu and others

Supreme Court of India

29 June 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Anil R. Dave, Adarsh Kumar Goel

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 459; AIR 2016 SC 3024; 2016(5) ALL MR 973; 2016 (117) ALR 723; 2016 (5) AWC 4476; 2016 (3) CLT(SC) 1; JT 2016 (6) SC 185; 2016 (3) RCR(Civil) 842; 2016 (133) RD 114; 2016(6) SCALE 54; [2016] 4 S.C.R. 1; 2016 (4) WLN(SC) 53

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Keywords: Public Purpose, Land Acquisition Act, 1894

Summary: Land & Property - Administrative - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 4(1), 48, 48(1), 6 - Land acquisition - Withdrawal - Challenged - Appellants/authorities issued notification for land acquisition from respondents/land-owners - Respondents made a request to appellants for withdrawal of acquisition - Acquisition proceedings was withdrawn, but subsequently withdrawal of acquisition proceedings was cancelled and notification which was initially issued remained in force - Respondents filed writ petitions before HC - HC directed authorities to reconsider the issue with regard to acquisition of land in question - Hence, instant appeal.

Held, after taking possession of land in question from land-owners, s. 48(1) of the Act does not permit withdrawal of acquisition proceedings. It was not just and proper for Court to show undue sympathy towards land-owners by asking them to make a representation when it was against legal provisions to withdraw land from acquisition in view of provisions of s. 48 of the Act. Such undue sympathy had relegated land-owners to this long drawn litigation which had not helped them at all.

Further, acquisition proceedings should not be quashed by HC, especially when Govt. had not acted in accordance with law while withdrawing land in question from acquisition proceedings. Impugned judgment of HC was set aside. Appeal allowed.

Ratio - After taking possession of land in question from land-owners, power u/s.48(1) of the Act does not permit withdrawal of acquisition proceedings.
