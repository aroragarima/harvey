Babu v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 784; (1999) 8 SCC 499; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1493; AIR 1999 SC 3861; 1999 (4) CCR 222; JT 1999 (8) SC 412; 1999 (4) RCR(Criminal) 804; 1999(6) SCALE 634; 1999 (9) SLT 69; 1999 (8) Supreme 728

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 50 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 134 - Whether right of accused is violated in terms of s. 50 of Act? - Held, conviction of accused is liable to be set aside with a view that he had not been informed of having right to be searched in presence of Magistrate or any Gazzetted Officer and therefore s. 50 of Act is violated.
