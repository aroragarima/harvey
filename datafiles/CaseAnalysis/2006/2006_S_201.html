Patel Joitaram Kalidas & Ors. v Spl. Land Acquisition Officer & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

13 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 972; (2007) 2 SCC 341; 2007 (66) ALR 833; 2007 (5) AWC 4960; 2007 (2) G.L.R. 1773; 2007 (3) JhrCR(SC) 92; JT 2007 (1) SC 424; 2007 (2) MPLJ 321; 2006(13) SCALE 420; 2006 (7) SCJ 131; 2006 (Supp) RD 211; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 922; 2007 (1) UPLBEC 417

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Plea that High Court ought to have awarded interest on solatium payable under s. 23 (2) - Whether appellants are entitled to maintain an application for special leave before this Court impugning the judgment and order of the High Court which affirmed the findings of the Reference Court under s. 18? - Held, not necessary for the appellant- claimant to have filed separate appeal/cross-objections before the High Court for the purposes of claiming interest - Appellants are entitled to interest on the amounts payable to them under s. 23 (1A) and s. 23 (2) - Appeals allowed.
