State Through Inspector of Police v K. Narasimhachary

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 606; (2005) 8 SCC 364; AIR 2006 SC 628; 2006 CRLJ 518; JT 2005 (12) SC 553; 2005(8) SCALE 266; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 197

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service; Socio-Economic

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, ss. 7, 11, 13(1)(d), 13(2) - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 47, 76, 78 - Appeal against acquittal - Held, two views are possible and view of HC cannot be said to be wholly improbable - It cannot be said, in view of discussions made that materials brought on records would lead to only one conclusion, i.e., guilt of accused - Appeal dismissed.
