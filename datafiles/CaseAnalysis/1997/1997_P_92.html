Smt. Sujata Mukherjee v Prashant Kumar Mukherjee

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1190; (1997) 5 SCC 30; (1997) SCC (Cr) 673; AIR 1997 SC 2465; 1997 (35) AllCriC 108; 1997 (2) CCR 117; 1997 CRLJ 2985; JT 1997 (5) SC 478; 1997(4) SCALE 112; 1997 (2) SCJ 97; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 1127; 1997 (4) Supreme 448

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss.178(b) and 178(c) - Criminal complaint - Territorial jurisdiction - Mal-treatment by in-laws and husband - Some occasions all accused had taken part in one local area and on other occasion, only by husband in another local area where complaint was lodged - Held, clause (c) of s.178 clearly attracted - Magistrate has territorial jurisdiction to proceed against all.
