Hari Dev Sharma v State (Delhi Administration)

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 514; (1977) 3 SCC 352; (1977) SCC (Cr) 516; AIR 1976 SC 1489; 1976 CRLJ 1176; 1976 CRLR 264; 1976 UJ 602

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Service

Keywords: Illegal Gratification

Summary: IPC, 1860, s. 161 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - (A) Provision of s.5(1)(d) r/w s. 5(2) of 1947 Act - Vital part of prosecution case disbelieved by High Court - Confirming acquittal - Validity of - Order for conviction passed by trial Court, held, could not be affirmed - Appeal Allowed.
