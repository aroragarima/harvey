Hansa Industries Private Limited and Others v Kidarsons Industries Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

13 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 685; (2006) 8 SCC 531; AIR 2007 SC 18; 2007 (4) CompLJ 42; JT 2006 (9) SC 100; 2006(10) SCALE 170; 2006 (8) SCJ 191; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 235

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Corporate - Registration Act, 1908, ss. 17(1)(b), 17(2) - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 100, 104 - Single Judge dismissed appellant's objections to report of Chartered Accountants who had valued share of Respondent No 1 Company, and directing implementation of settlement arrived at between parties - Whether portion of Golf Links property which at time of settlement was occupied by appellant no 2 be not allotted to him? - Whether appropriate directions be given so that appellants be made liable for payment of capital gains tax, if any, levied in future which levy shall be challenged by Respondent No 1 Company, provided funds are made available to it by appellants for purpose? - Held, appellant No 2, shall be allotted portion of Golf Links house which was in his occupation on date of settlement - No deduction shall be made from value of assets of anticipated capital gains tax liability on hypothetical sale under settlement - Appeal partly allowed.
