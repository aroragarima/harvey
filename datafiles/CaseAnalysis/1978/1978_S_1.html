In Re: Shri S. Mulgaokar v Na

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 323; (1978) 3 SCC 339; (1978) SCC (Cr) 402; AIR 1978 SC 727; 1978 MLJ 593; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 162

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2(1)(c) - Constitution, arts. 19(1)(a) and 129 - (A) Whether criticism of judiciary by press amounts to contempt - (B) Desirability of formulation of Code of judicial ethics and etiquette - Held, relevant considerations stated - Desirability pointed out - Order Accordingly.
