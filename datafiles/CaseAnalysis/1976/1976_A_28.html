Amritlal Nathubhai Shah and Others v Union Government of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, A.N. Ray, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 375; (1976) 4 SCC 108; AIR 1976 SC 2591; 1977 (34) FLR 340; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 372; 1976 UJ 783

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, ss. 4, 10 and 17 - Mineral Concession Rules, 1960, rr. 58, 59 and 60 - Validity of rejection of application for grant of mining lease for Bauxite in reserved area - State Govt. held, was well within its rights in rejecting the applications of the appellants u/r. 60 as premature - Appeal dismissed.
