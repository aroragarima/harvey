Union Of India And Another v Kunisetty Satyanarayana

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 833; (2006) 12 SCC 28; AIR 2007 SC 906; 2007 (5) AWC 4945; 2006(12) SCALE 262; 2007 (7) SLR 146; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 257

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - CCS (Conduct) Rules, 1964, rr. 3(1)(i), 3(1)(iii) - Respondent has been promoted as an Upper Division Clerk under Scheduled Tribe category - Allegation of forged caste-certificate - Showcause notice - WP against notice allowed - Appeal against - Held, no writ lies against a charge sheet or show- A mere charge-sheet or show-cause notice does not give rise to any cause of action, because it does not amount to an adverse order which affects rights of any party unless same has been issued by a person having no jurisdiction to do so - Respondent is now directed to submit his reply to Charge Memo - Appeal allowed.
