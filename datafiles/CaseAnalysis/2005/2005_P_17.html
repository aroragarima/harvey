Devasahayam (D) By Lrs v P. Savithramma and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 586; (2005) 7 SCC 653; AIR 2006 SC 779; 2006 (1) ALD(SC) 73; 2005 (61) ALR 637; JT 2005 (8) SC 361; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 306; 2006 (100) RD 60; 2006 (2) RLW 969; 2005(7) SCALE 322; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 255

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Court fee and Suit Valuation Act, Andhra Pradesh Building (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1960

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Land & Property - Andhra Pradesh Court fee and Suit Valuation Act, s. 24-A - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 7, r. 7 - Andhra Pradesh Building (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1960, ss. 10, 10 (1), 10(2)(vi), 12, 13 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, ss. 53-A, 54 - Specific performance of oral agreement of sale - Not allowed - Eviction on ground of denial of landlord-tenant relationship granted; counter claim granted - Held, Civil Court had no jurisdiction to pass a decree for eviction only on basis that tenant has denied their title - Dismissal of Appellant's suit for specific performance of contract upheld - Appeal is allowed in part so far as it relates to counter-claim made by Respondents - Appeal partly allowed.
