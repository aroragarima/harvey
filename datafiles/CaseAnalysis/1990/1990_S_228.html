State of Gujarat v Mohan Bhai Raghbhai Patel and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 816; (1992) Supp3 SCC 87; (1992) SCC (Cr) 954; AIR 1990 SC 1379; 1990 (2) Crimes 691; 1990 CRLJ 1462; 1990 CRLR 313; 1990 SCALE 106

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Offence of murder - Circumstantial evidence - Conduct of accused throwing mattress over deceased, creates doubt about prosecution version of his having poured kerosene and setting her on fire - Absence of motive - Acquittal - Held, justified - Appeal dismissed.
