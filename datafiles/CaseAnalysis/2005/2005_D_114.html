Dr. Subramanian Swamy v Director, CBI and others

Supreme Court of India

04 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, D.M. Dharmadhikari, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 98; (2005) SCC (L&S) 241; (2005) 2 SCC 317; 2005 (1) AWC 797; 2005 CRLJ 1413; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 273; JT 2005 (2) SC 382; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 38; 2005(1) SCALE 757

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Delhi Special Police Establishment Act, 1946, s. 6-A - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Security Interest Act, 2002 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 11(1), s. 12(3), s. 5(10)B - Constitutional validity of s. 6-A - Whether arbitrariness and unreasonableness or manifest arbitrariness and unreasonableness, being facets of art.14 of Constitution of India are available or not as grounds to invalidate a legislation? - Held, matters deserve to be heard by a larger-Bench.
