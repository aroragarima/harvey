Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay v Smt. Kasturbai Walchand Trust, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Vishishtha Bhargava, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 225; AIR 1967 SC 844; 1967 (1) ITJ 328; [1967] 63 ITR 656; [1967] 2 S.C.R. 7

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.4(3)(i), 41(1) - Indian Trusts Act, 1882 - Whether exemption of income from trust u/s.4(3)(i) is available after execution of deed by trustee for surrender of all rights? - Income earned by Trust is taxable in hands of trustees whether received or not by beneficiaries subject to exemption u/s.4(3)(i) - Held, effect of deed of surrender was that entire properties were held by trustees for charitable purposes - Exemption u/s.4(3)(i) is available even when income of trust is accumulated for application to charitable purpose - Appeal dismissed.
