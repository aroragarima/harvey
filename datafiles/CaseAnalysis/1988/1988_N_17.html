Nathi Lal and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 519; (1990) Supp SCC 145; (1990) SCC (Cr) 638

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Cross cases - Fair procedure - Directions - Held, after recording of evidence in one case is completed, he must hear arguments but he must reserve judgment - Must hear cross case and after recording all evidence he must hear arguments - In deciding each of case, he can rely only on evidence recorded in that particular case - Evidence recorded in cross case cannot be looked into - Trial in both matter to be heard in light of directions given - Appeal disposed of.
