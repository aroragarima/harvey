T.P. Moideen Koya v Government of Kerala and others

Supreme Court of India

30 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 791; (2004) 8 SCC 106; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2120; AIR 2004 SC 4733; 2004 (114) DLT 106; JT 2004 (8) SC 383; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 758; 2004(8) SCALE 364; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 904

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950, Uttar Pradesh Tenancy Act, 1939

Summary: Criminal - COFEPOSA, 1974, s. 3(1)(iv) - Detention Order - Special Leave Petition filed by wife of petitioner dismissed by this court - Whether this is a bar for present petition filed by petitioner under art 32 of Constitution? - Held, only plea raised in present petition also been raised in SLP which had been filed earlier seeking quashing of detention order and release of petitioner - It is neither a subsequent development nor a new plea which may not have been available at earlier stage - Petition not maintainable - Petition dismissed.
