Phoolwati Smt v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 247; (1991) Supp2 SCC 689; (1992) SCC (L&S) 135; AIR 1991 SC 469; 1991 (17) ATC 937; 1991 LabIC 392

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Printing

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Appointment on compassionate basis - Petition filed by widow of Govt. employee - Direction sought to allow her to stay in quarters and to employ her son according to his educational qualification - Held, directed that immediate steps be taken for employing applicant's son in a suitable post and she be allowed to stay in quarters - Appeal allowed.
