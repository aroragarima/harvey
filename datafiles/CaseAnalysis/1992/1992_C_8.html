P.M.C. Kunhiraman Nair v C.R. Naganatha Iyer And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 481; (1992) 4 SCC 254; AIR 1993 SC 307; 1992 (2) CCC 514; JT 1992 (4) SC 235; 1993 (1) MLJ(SC) 22; 1992 (2) RCJ 137; 1993 (1) RCR(Rent) 127; 1992 (2) RentLR 584; 1992(1) SCALE 1216; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 371; 1992 (2) UJ 193

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(F) - (A) Plaintiff, lessee of land starting Mill while defendant is subsequent assignee of Mill - Whether plea by defendant that he is tenant barred in suit for possession - On ground that it was not raised in earlier suit between parties when earlier suit was confined to Mill - Held, not barred - Appeal allowed.
