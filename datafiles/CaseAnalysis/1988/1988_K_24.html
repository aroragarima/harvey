Modula India v Kamakshya Singh Deo

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 239; (1988) 4 SCC 619; AIR 1989 SC 162; 1989 (1) CalLT(SC) 15; JT 1988 (4) SC 214; 1988 (2) RCJ 525; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 530; 1988(2) SCALE 1163; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 333; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 333

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - West Bengal Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, s. 17(3) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 8, r. 1, r. 5, r. 10, O. 11, r. 21, O. 15, r. 5 (U.P.), O. 15 A, r. 1 (Bom ) - Tenancy - Eviction suit - Striking off of defence - Practice and Procedure - Whether tenant is still entitled to cross-examine plaintiff's witnesses and address argument on basis of plaintiff's case - Held, entitled - Appeal allowed.
