Smt. J. Tiwari v Smt. Jwala Devi Vidya Mandir and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 539; (1979) 4 SCC 160; (1979) SCC (L&S) 356; AIR 1981 SC 122; 1980 ALJ 1070; 1979 (27) BLJR 122; 1979 (1) SLR 614; 1979 UJ 234

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 34 - Legality of termianation of Principal of Education institution registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 - Termination though unlawful, principal entitled only to decree for damages and not to a declaration of continuing in service - Appeal Dismissed.
