H. H. Maharaj Rana Hemant Singhji, Dholpur v Commissloner Of Income-Tax, Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 101; (1976) 1 SCC 996; AIR 1976 SC 662; 1976 CTR 188; [1976] 103 ITR 61; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 423; 1976 TAXLR 353; 1976 UJ 406

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Land Acquisition, Capital Gains

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 2(4-A)(ii) and 12B - Personal effects - Intimate connection of effects with person of assessee - Essential to render them 'personal effects' - Treasure (gold sovereigns, silver coins and bullion) placed before deity while performing Puja - Whether included in it - Held, not covered by the expression - Appeal Dismissed.
