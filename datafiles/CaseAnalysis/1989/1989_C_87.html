Rama Bai and Others v Commissioner of Income Tax, Andhra Pradesh Hyderabad and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, S. Ranganathan, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 184; (1990) Supp SCC 699; 1990 (84) CTR 164; [1990] 181 ITR 400; [1991] 54 TAXMAN 496

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 18, 28 and 34 - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 257 - References made u/s. 257 - Question relating to when does interest payable u/ss. 28 and 34 of Act, accrues or arises, where such interest is paid on enhanced compensation awarded on a reference u/s. 18 or further appeal to High Court and/or Supreme Court - Held, question of accrual of interest will have to be determined in accordance with case of CIT v. Govindarajulu Chetty (TNK), 1987 Indlaw SC 1117 - Interest cannot be taken to have accrued on date of order of court granting enhanced compensation but has to be taken as having accrued year after year from date of delivery of possession of lands till date of such order - Petition disposed of.
