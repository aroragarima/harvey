State of Kerala and Others v O. C. Kuttan and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 961; (1999) SCC (Cr) 304; (1999) 2 SCC 651; AIR 1999 SC 1044; 1999 (1) CCR 110; 1999 (1) Crimes 104; 1999 CRLJ 1623; 1999 (1) JCC 144; JT 1999 (1) SC 486; 1999 (1) KLT 747; 1999 (1) RCR(Criminal) 831; 1999(1) SCALE 505; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 696; 1999 (2) SLT 224; 1999 (2) Supreme 182

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: Abuse Of Process Of Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 376 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 154 - Quashing of FIR and investigation - Whether quashing of FIR and investigation by High Court is illegal? - Held, order of HC was illegal as it exceeded its jurisdiction while quashing FIR and investigation against accused persons.
