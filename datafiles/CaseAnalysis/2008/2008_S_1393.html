Super Cassettes Industries Ltd v Commissioner Of Customs, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

13 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, J.M. Panchal

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1512; 2008 (225) E.L.T. 401

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Importation

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 2(23), 20 and 130 E - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3(1) - Notification no. 94/96-Cus D/- 16-12-1996 - Whether tribunal had erred in holding that in view of s. 20 of Customs Act, on re-importation of goods, duty leviable would be same as would have been levied on importation of goods for first time? - A reading of s. 20 makes it abundantly clear that goods re-imported after exportation would be liable to duty and be subject to all conditions and restrictions to which goods of like kind and value are liable or subject on importation thereof - Held, findings recorded by tribunal cannot be faulted with - Appeals dismissed.
