Collector of Central Excise, Pune v Dai Ichi Karkaria Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 105; (1999) 7 SCC 448; AIR 1999 SC 3234; 1999 (156) CTR 172; 1999 (84) ECR 4; 1999 (112) E.L.T. 353; JT 1999 (5) SC 595; 1999 (33) RLT 899; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 360

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 4 and 37 - Central Excises (Valuation) Rules, 1975, r. 6 - Excise Duty - Determining cost of an excisable product covered by Modvat scheme u/s. 4(1)(b) of Act r/w r. 6 of Valuation Rules - Whether excise duty paid on raw material also covered by Modvat scheme can be included? - Held, it is price of raw material less excise duty thereon, which has been paid by seller for which manufacturer is entitled to credit under MODVAT Scheme - Hence, it is not to be included - Appeal dismissed.
