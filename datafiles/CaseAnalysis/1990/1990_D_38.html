Gitarani Paul v Dibyendra Kundu Alias Dibyendra Kumar Kundu

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 282; (1991) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1991 SC 395; 1992 (2) CalLT(SC) 5; 1991 (1) GLH 419; JT 1990 (4) SC 702; 1991 RD 32; 1990(2) SCALE 1198; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 464; 1991 (1) UJ 232

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Procedure - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 6 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 7, r. 7 - Suit for title and possession of land - Concurrent findings of lower Courts that plaintiff had title to suit lands - Held, title of appellant-plaintiff to land in dispute having been proved therefore, she was entitled to a decree for possession - Appeal allowed.
