Commissioner of Income Tax v Madras Auto Service Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1878; (1998) 6 SCC 404; AIR 1998 SC 2667; 1998 (148) CTR 398; [1998] 233 ITR 468; JT 1998 (5) SC 507; 1998(4) SCALE 537; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 1121; 1998 (6) SLT 632; 1998 (6) Supreme 359; [1998] 99 TAXMAN 575

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Revenue expenditure - Lease deed for period of 39 years - Assessee made substantial savings in monthly rent for a period of 39 years by expending these amounts - Saving in expenditure was a saving in revenue expenditure in form of rent - Held, assessee did not get any capital asset by spending - No depreciation could be claimed - Appeals dismissed.
