Orissa State Warehousing Corporation v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1182; (1999) 4 SCC 197; AIR 1999 SC 1388; 1999 (153) CTR 177; [1999] 237 ITR 589; JT 1999 (2) SC 527; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 383; 1999 TAXLR 456; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 623

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Warehousing Corporation Act, 1962, ss. 16 and 24 - Interpretation of s. 10(29) of Income Tax Act, 1961 for determining 'income exempt' - Interest received by State Warehousing Corporation from banks on fixed deposits - Whether exempt u/s. 10(29) of Income Tax Act - Held, not exempt - Appeal dismissed.
