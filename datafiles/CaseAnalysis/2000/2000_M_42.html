Kush Saigal and Others v M. C. Mitter and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 229; (2000) 4 SCC 526; AIR 2000 SC 1390; 2000 ALJ 949; 2000 (39) ALR 367; 2000 (2) AWC 1490; JT 2000 (3) SC 563; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 408; 2000(2) SCALE 593; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 648; 2000 (3) SLT 589; 2000 (3) Supreme 125

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, United Provinces (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947 (ENG), Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent & Eviction) (Amendment) Act, 1976, Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws Amendment Act, 1972, Indian Soldiers (Litigation) Act, 1925

Summary: U. P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972 (new Act)-Sections 21 and 43 (2) (r) and (rr)-U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947-Sections 3 and 7F-Eviction-Application under Section 3 of old Act for permission for instituting suit for eviction of tenant on ground of running nursing home-Application allowed and suit instituted accordingly-Ultimately suit withdrawn without liberty to file fresh suit-Application under Section 43 (2) (rr) filed for eviction of tenant-Scope of Section 43 (2) (rr) of new Act-If ground of permission under Section 3 of old Act covered by any of grounds mentioned in Section 21 (1) or Section 21 (2) of old Act-It will be executable under clause (rr) of Section 43 (2)-Permission under Section 3 of old Act granted for eviction of tenant from residential portion of building for commercial purpose of running nursing home- Such ground not one within meaning of Section 21 (1) or Section 21 (2)- Hence application under Section 43 (2) (rr) not maintainable-Application rightly rejected by Prescribed Authority and District Judge-High Court wrong in allowing application- Application dismissed.

Judgment of Allahabad High Court, reversed.
