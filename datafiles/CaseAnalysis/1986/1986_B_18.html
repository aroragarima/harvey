Girdhari Lal and Sons v Balbir Nath Mathur and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 655; (1986) 2 SCC 237; AIR 1986 SC 1499; 1986 (1) CCC 1070; 1986 (30) DLT 68; 1986 (2) RCJ 199; 1986 (2) RCR(Rent) 361; 1986 (1) RentLR 314; 1986(1) SCALE 272; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 383

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 17 and 18 - (A) Interpretation of statutes - Object of legislation - Held, court must interpret to promote object and purpose and may even depart from normal rule of plain meaning to achieve aforesaid purpose - (B) An exceptional case with facts peculiar to its own where agreement of sub-tenancy attested by landlord himself - Protection against eviction to sub tenant in such a case - Further held, normally, s. 17 should be strictly complied with, for sub-tenant to get benefit u/s. 18 - Twin requirement is satisfied - Appeal allowed.
