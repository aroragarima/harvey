Bank Of Bihar v State Of Bihar And Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 466; (1972) 3 SCC 196; AIR 1971 SC 1210; [1971] 41 Comp Cas 591; [1971] S.C.R. 299; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 299; 1971 UJ 638

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s. 180 and 176 - Pledge - Pawnee's special property and lien on the goods - Other creditor - Right - Pawnee has special property and lien on the goods and so long as his claim is not satisfied, no other creditor of pawnor has any right to take away the goods or its price - Appeal Allowed.
