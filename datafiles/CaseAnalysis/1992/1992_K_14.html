State Of Andhra Pradesh And Anr. v K.S. Muralidhar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 857; (1992) 2 SCC 241; (1992) SCC (L&S) 539; AIR 1992 SC 922; AIR 1992 SCW 734; 1992 (20) ATC 226; 1992 (1) CLR 394; JT 1992 (1) SC 360; 1992 LabIC 855; 1992 (2) LLN 13; 1992(1) SCALE 176; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 295; 1992 (1) SLR 652

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Supervisor, AP State and Subordinate Service Rules 1962

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 16 and 309 - Andhra Pradesh G O Ms 559(I and P) D/-18-07-1977 - Junior Engineers - Appointment by upgrading supervisors acquiring degree qualification and by direct selection of degree holders in temporary service - Inter se seniority - Computation - Weightage under G O Ms 559 - Held, can be given only from date of appointment - Order accordingly.
