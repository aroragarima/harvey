Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: [A.] Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-Tax Act, 1961, ss. 10(3), 14, 45, 48, 55(2), 56 - Whether amount received by respondent-assessee on surrender of tenancy rights is liable to capital gains tax u/s.45? - Held, there is no dispute that a tenancy right is a capital asset surrender of which would attract s.45 so that value received would be a capital receipt and assessable if at all only under Item E of s. 14 - That being so, it cannot be treated as a casual or non-recurring receipt u/s. 10(3) and be subjected to tax u/s. 56 - If income cannot be taxed u/s.45, it cannot be taxed at all - Appeal dismissed.
