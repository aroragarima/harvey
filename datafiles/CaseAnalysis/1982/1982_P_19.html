Padma Srinivasan v Premier Insurance Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 106; (1982) SCC (Cr) 330; (1982) 1 SCC 613; 1982 ACJ 191; AIR 1982 SC 836; [1983] 53 Comp Cas 333; 1982 (44) FLR 271; 1982(2) SCALE 107; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 244; 1982 UJ 248

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 95(2) and 95(a) - Insurer undertaking 'liability as one under Chap VII of Act' in third party insurance of motor vehicles - Accident - Determination of liability - Held, liability of insurer would be extended to legal provision as it stood on date of accident - Appeal allowed.
