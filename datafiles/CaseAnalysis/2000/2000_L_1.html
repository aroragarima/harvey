L. Muthukumar and Another v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 479; (2000) 7 SCC 618; AIR 2000 SC 3084; 2001 (42) ALR 226; JT 2000 (Supp1) SC 85; 2000(6) SCALE 536; 2000 (7) SLT 169; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 462; 2000 (6) Supreme 407

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Tamil Nadu Recognised Private Schools (Regulation) Act, 1974, s. 12 - Diplomas or certificates in Teachers Training - Passing of public examination - Subsequent de - recognition of Institute - Writ of mandamus - For issuance of marks - sheet, diploma or certificates - Held, not expected that respondents would issue diplomas/certificates with endorsement to other candidates - Mistakes occurred earlier could not be repeated - Detrimental to interest of nation - Writ cannot be granted.
