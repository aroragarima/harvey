Shiv Shankar and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 January 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 130; (1985) 2 SCC 30; (1985) SCC (L&S) 387; AIR 1985 SC 514; 1985 (50) FLR 200; 1985 LabIC 731; 1985 (1) LLJ 437; 1985 (1) LLN 588; 1985(1) SCALE 106; 1985 (1) SLJ 403; 1985 UJ 304

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Railway Establishment Manual, paragraph 1301 and 1304 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 311 - Order of forfeiture of past service for participation in illegal strike - Denial of opportunity to show cause - Held, order is liable to be quashed on ground of non-observance of principles of natural justice - Petition allowed.
