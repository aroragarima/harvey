Sayeed Ahmed and Company v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 July 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 31(7)(b)- Arbitration Act, 1940 - Interest

, 1978, s. 3(i) - Interest - Award thereof - Whether the arbitrator can award interest for pre-reference period and pendente lite, when the contract prohibits the employer from entertaining any claim for interest? - Held, bar under contract can only operate till date of award, and arbitrator can award interest for the period starting from the date of award - On rejection of claims put up by appellant/contractors for the work done, disputes were arose which were referred to arbitration - Arbitrator awarded certain sums with interest at different rates for different periods - High Court after considering bar contained in respective clause of the contract as to claim of interest held that arbitrator had no power to award interest and thus it set aside that part of the award under which interest was awarded for the period till date of award and for the period after date of award it reduced the rate of interest from 18% to 6% - Respective clause of contract perused, provisions esp. s. 31(7)(b) of 1996 Act further perused, decisions referred and contentions heard - Any provision in the contract barring interest, shall operate only till the date of award and not thereafter - Arbitrator had awarded interest at three different rates on three different amounts which were all less than 18% p.a. - Thus award of interest by arbitrator fount not to be contrary to s. 31(7)(b) of Act, 1996 - No reasons assigned by HC for reducing the rate of interest to 6% p.a. - Reduction unsustainable - Judgment of HC setting aside the award of interest upto the date of award, affirmed - Rate of interest on the amounts due and payable under the award, from the date of award till date of payment to be in terms of the award of the arbitrator - Appeal partly allowed.
