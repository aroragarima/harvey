Dev Kumar (Died) Through Lrs. v Smt. Swaran Lata And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1806; (1996) 1 SCC 25; AIR 1996 SC 510; 1996 (1) CLT 113; JT 1995 (9) SC 331; 1995 (2) RCJ 531; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 40; 1995 (2) RentLR 669; 1995(6) SCALE 316; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 89

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, ss. 13(2)(ii)(a) and 15(5) - (A) Revision - Powers of HC - Absence of perversity in matter of appreciation of evidence or unreasonable conclusion by Appellate authority - Whether High Court can interfere with same - (B) Eviction - Ground of sub-letting - Proof - Report as well as statement of local commissioner not sufficient to show that tenant parted possession in favour of sub tenant and sub tenant was in exclusive possession of demised premises - Failure of land lady to establish subletting vitiated - Finding of subletting - Whether order of eviction top be set aside - Held, (A) Cannot be equated with appellate jurisdiction - Cannot interfere - (B) Order eviction set aside - Appeal allowed.
