Nagar Mahapalika (Now Municipal Corporation ) v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 May 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 218; (2006) 5 SCC 127; AIR 2006 SC 2113; 2006 (7) AWC 6799; JT 2006 (5) SC 318; 2006(5) SCALE 145; 2006 (7) SLR 637; [2006] Supp1 S.C.R. 681

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act

Summary: [A.] Service - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, s. 6N - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo)(bb) - Apprentices Act, 1961 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14, art. 16 - U P Nagar Mahapalika Adhiniyam, 1959 - Appointment on an adhoc basis - Order of termination - Reinstatement ordered - Appeal against - Held, appointments of Respondents have been made in violation of provisions of Adhiniyam and is void - Municipal Corporation deals with public money - Appointments of Respondents were made for carrying out work of assessment - Such assessments are done periodically - Their services, should not have been directed to be continued despite requirements therefor having come to an end - Compensation of Rs. 30, 000/- Per person is directed to be paid - Appeal partly allowed.
