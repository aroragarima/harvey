Kingsway Model Town Company and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 792; (1995) Supp2 SCC 46; 1994 (3) CCC 492; 1995 (1) RentLR 580; 1994(4) SCALE 711A

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4(1) and 54 - Appeal for further enhancement of compensation - Claimants sought to rely on a document post-notification sale deed - Held, mutation proceedings are inadmissible in evidence - No justification to enhance market value - Appeal dismissed.
