Dr. Umakant Saran v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 56; (1973) 1 SCC 485; AIR 1973 SC 964; 1973 LabIC 229; 1972 (2) LLJ 580; 1973 SLJ 14; 1973 (1) SLR 204; 1973 UJ 410

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Mandamus for setting aside appointments to civil services of respondents - Who is aggrieved person in case? - Held, as petitioner-appellant was not eligible for appointment, he could not be regarded as aggrieved for purpose of issue of mandamus for setting aside appointments - Appeal dismissed.
