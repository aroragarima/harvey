T.J. Stephen & Ors. v Parle Bottling Co.(P) Ltd. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 914; (1988) Supp SCC 458; (1988) SCC (Cr) 690; AIR 1988 SC 994; 1988 (3) Bom.C.R. 425; [1988] 64 Comp Cas 151; 1988 (2) CompLJ 53; 1988 CRLJ 1095; 1988 CrLR(SC) 231; 1988 (16) ECC 185; 1988 (34) E.L.T. 409; JT 1988 (1) SC 606B; 1988(1) SCALE 562; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 296

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal - Import and Export (Control) Act. 1947, s. 5 - CrPC, 1973, s. 200 - Imports - Offence u/s. 5.- Prosecution of Pvt. Ltd. Company and its M.D. - (A) Application for dismissal of complaint - Dismissed by Magistrate and affirmed by HC and SC - Application thereafter that M.D. be discharged as company is prepared to admit its guilt - Maintainability - (B) Delay of about 20 years for which M.D. was responsible - Held, not maintainable - Prosecution at such delayed stage dropped but M.D. saddled with exemplary costs - Case closed and exemplary costs imposed.
