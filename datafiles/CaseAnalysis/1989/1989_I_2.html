I.T.C. Limited v George Joseph Fernandes And Another

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 634; (1989) 2 SCC 1; AIR 1989 SC 839; 1989 (2) ARBLR 49; 1990 (1) CalLT(SC) 1; 1989 (1) CCC 589; 1989 (1) CompLJ 260; JT 1989 (1) SC 552; 1989(1) SCALE 283; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 469; 1989 (1) UJ 711

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Contract Act, 1972 - Court can decide validity of contract containing arbitration clause - A dispute as to binding nature of contract cannot be determined by arbitrator - Arbitration clause itself stands or falls according to determination of question in dispute - Held, it could not be said that validity or otherwise of charter party was covered by arbitration clause - There being no mutual mistake, contract could not be avoided - Arbitration clause independent of contract.
