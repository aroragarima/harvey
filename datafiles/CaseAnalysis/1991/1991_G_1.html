Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Different Intention, Law Reforms (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 1934

Summary: Interest Act, 1978 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Pendente lite interest - Arbitrator's power to grant - Agreement silent as to grant of interest - It has to be presumed as implied term of agreement - Whether arbitrator has power to grant interest pendente lite? - Held, Arbitrator can grant interest pendente lite but it cannot be claimed as a matter of right.
