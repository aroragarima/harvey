Mohinder Kumar v State, Panaji, Goa

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N.P. Singh, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1478; (1998) 8 SCC 655; (1999) SCC (Cr) 79; AIR 1995 SC 1157; 1995 CRLJ 2074

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 42 and 50 - Procedure - Not followed - Police officer suddenly reaching accused's house - Patrolling duty - Search and seizure of charas from accused - No grounds of belief recorded - No copy forwarded to superior officer - Not informed to person to be searched that if he would like to be taken to a gazetted officer or a magistrate - Held, accused would be entitled to be acquitted as mandatory provisions of ss. 42 and 50 of Act not followed.
