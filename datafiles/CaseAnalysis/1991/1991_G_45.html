Sri Chand Gupta v Gulzar Singh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

22 October 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 316; (1992) 1 SCC 143; AIR 1992 SC 123; AIR 1991 SCW 2813; 1992 (19) ALR 242; JT 1991 (6) SC 532; 1991 (2) RCJ 557; 1991 (2) RCR(Rent) 702; 1992 (1) RentLR 5; 1991(2) SCALE 949; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 538; 1992 (1) UJ 90

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Rent Control

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(b) - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 18 and 114 - Lease - Proof of subletting - Tenant's brother making statement before Income tax authorities that he is sole tenant - And there is no pecuniary or joint interest of both brothers - Binding effect of admission to prove sub-brother's admission held, could not bind tenant u/s. 18 of 1872 Act - And sub-letting could not be inferred - Appeal dismissed.
