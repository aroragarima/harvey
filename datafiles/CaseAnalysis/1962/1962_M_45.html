Kapur Chand Godha v Mir Nawab Himayatalikhan Azamjah

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 207; AIR 1963 SC 250; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 41 and 63, illustration (c) - Creditor accepting lesser amount in full satisfaction of his claim from third party instead of debtor - Whether creditor can later claim balance amount from principal debtor? - Held, cannot recover - Appeal dismissed.
