P. D'Souza v Shondrilo Naidu

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 508; (2004) 6 SCC 649; AIR 2004 SC 4472; 2004 (4) AWC 3629; 2004 (2) CGLJ 245; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 123B; JT 2004 (6) SC 126; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 668; 2004(6) SCALE 364; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 186

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Trusts & Associations

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Income-Tax Act, 1961, s. 230A - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 20, 20(2), 23 Specific performance of contract - Agreement for sale - Whether plaintiffs-tenant ready and willing to perform their part of contract; Whether court should exercise its discretionary jurisdiction in view of hardship caused to defendant-landlord - Escalation of prices - Held, upon consideration of materials on records HC arrived at a finding that plaintiffs ready and willing to perform contract - HC Judgment confirmed.
