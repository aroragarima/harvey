Om Prakash (D) By Lrs. & Ors. v Union Of India & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

05 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 567; (2004) 10 SCC 627; AIR 2004 SC 4135; 2004 (3) AWC 2726; 2004 (112) DLT 891; JT 2004 (6) SC 288; 2004 (138) PLR 727; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 726; 2004(6) SCALE 455; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2359

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Delhi Land Reforms Act, 1954 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Compensation - Increased potentiality of lands - Assessment of market value of acquired lands as on date of notification - Held, HC justified in assessing market value at a higher rate on account of some increased potentiality of lands - Appeals Dismissed.
