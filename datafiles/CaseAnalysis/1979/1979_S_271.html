Hindustan Sugar Mills v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 39; (1980) 1 SCC 599; AIR 1981 SC 1681; 1980 (45) STC 194; 1981 TAXLR 1125; 1980 UJ 250

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954

Summary: Supreme Court Rules, 1966, O.40, r. 1 - Constitution, art. 299 - CPC, 1908, O. 47, r. 1

- (A) Review of Supreme Court decision - Reimbursement of sales tax on sale of cement to Central Government - On assumption of absence of term in contract - Review application against - Term in contract found to be in fact existing - (B) Obligation arising from contract - Observations on such assumption deleted - Duty of Central Government - Application Aallowed.
