Supreme Court of India

17 January 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 39 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 3 item 1 - Applicability of doctrine of equal pay for equal work - Two persons employed on a daily wage basis - Held, they are entitled to same wages as are paid to similarly employed employees - Cost imposed - Petition allowed.
