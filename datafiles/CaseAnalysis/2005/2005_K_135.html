Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others v Kollutla Obi Reddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 458; (2005) 6 SCC 493; AIR 2006 SC 642; 2005 (60) ALR 877; 2005 (4) AWC 3860K; JT 2005 (7) SC 472; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 6; 2005(6) SCALE 394; 2005 (Supp) RD 208; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 513

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 6, 11, 18, 23, 23(1) - Nagarjuna Sagar Project (Acquisition of Lands) Act, 1956, s. 4(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 31-A - High Court set aside orders/awards and directing Land Acquisition Officer to pass fresh awards - Appealed herein - Maintainability of writ petition filed after long passage of time - Held, HC was moved in these matters by writ petitioners long after s.4(1) Notification and s. 6 declarations came - References in terms of s. 18 were pending - HC not even indicated any reason as to why writ petitions were being entertained when references were pending - Set aside judgment of HC - Appeals allowed.
