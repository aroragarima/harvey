K. George Thomas v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Kerala

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 308; (1986) Supp SCC 269; AIR 1986 SC 1661; 1986 (2) CCC 678; 1986 (58) CTR 43; [1986] 159 ITR 851; 1986(1) SCALE 1296; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 874; 1986 TAXLR 845; [1986] 26 TAXMAN 136; 1986 (2) TLR 845

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s.10(3) - Income - Assessee receiving various foreign remittances as Vice- President of India Gospel Mission - Used it for his newspaper and personal expenses -Taxability of amount of remittances - Held could be taxed as income of assessee - Appeal dismissed.
