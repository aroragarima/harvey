S.K. Kale v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 293; (1977) SCC (Cr) 356; (1977) 2 SCC 394; AIR 1977 SC 822; 1977 CRLJ 604; 1977 CrLR(SC) 318; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 533

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5(1)(d) - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Charge of abuse of position - Charges based upon on circumstantial evidence - Wrong inference from proved facts and misplacing of onus of proof - Conviction - Validity - Conviction, held, not sustainable - Appeal allowed.
