Sitaram Ramcharan and Others v Nagrashna M. N. (Authority Under The Payment of Wages Act For Ahmedabad Area) and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 42; AIR 1960 SC 260; 1960 (1) LLJ 29; 1960 (1) MLJ(SC) 60; 1960 MPLJ 487; 1960 SCJ 183; [1960] 1 S.C.R. 875

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948, s. 50 - Workmen's Compensation Act, ss. 10 and 10 (1) - Factories Act, 1948, ss.2(6), 59 and 70 - Payment of Wages Act, 1936, s. 15 (2) - Limitation Act, 1963, ss.2(1)and 5 - Whether delay in filing application can be condoned? - Limitation of six months prescribed by s. 2(1) applies to notice of claim which a workman has to give to his employer - Notice of claim had to be served on employer within six months after date of accident and proceedings had to be initiated before tribunal claiming compensation - Held, appellants have failed to establish sufficient cause for their inaction - Appeals dismissed.
