Bharat Electronics Limited, Bangalore v Industrial Tribunal, Karnataka,Bangalore And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K. Jayachandra Reddy, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 745; (1990) SCC (L&S) 255; (1990) 2 SCC 314; AIR 1990 SC 1080; 1990 (60) FLR 622; JT 1990 (1) SC 431; 1990 LabIC 854; 1990 (2) LLJ 32; 1990(1) SCALE 443; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 971; 1990 (2) SLR 711; 1990 (2) UJ 32; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 822

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Telephone, Night Shift, Working Journalists (Conditions of Service) and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1955

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(rr)and 33(2)(b) - Wages - Night shift allowance - It is not part of wages - It becomes due only in event of working - Rejection of application u/s. 33(2)(b) for non-payment of night shift allowance - Legality - Held, is illegal - Appeal allowed.
