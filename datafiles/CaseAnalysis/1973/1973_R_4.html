Collector of Estate Duty v R. Kanakasabai and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 256; (1973) 4 SCC 169; (1973) SCC (Tax) 379; AIR 1973 SC 1214; 1973 (2) CTR 227; [1973] 89 ITR 251; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 747; 1973 TAXLR 1042

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Land and Property - Estate Duty Act, 1953, ss. 10 and 12 - (A) Whether properties settled by the deceased by the six deeds of settlement valued at Rs. 7, 38, 656 or any part thereof was not liable for inclusion in the estate of the deceased as property deemed to pass on his death? - (B) Settlement without reservation - If s.12 is attracted - Held, the question answered affirmative and in favour of the assessee - Held, not attracted - Civil appeal No. 474 of 1973 allowed; Civil Appeal No. 1770 Of 1970 dismissed.
