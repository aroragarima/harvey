P.K. Singh And Others v Presiding Officer And Others

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 513; (1988) 3 SCC 457; (1988) SCC (L&S) 817; AIR 1988 SC 1618; 1988 (57) FLR 239; JT 1988 (3) SC 62; 1989 LabIC 1256; 1988 (2) LLJ 363; 1988 (2) LLN 537; 1988(2) SCALE 27; 1989 (1) SLJ 122B; 1988 (3) SLR 352; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 471; 1988 (2) UJ 340

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 33 C(2) and 10 - Appeal by special leave is filed by 16 workmen of Small Arms Factory against common passed by Central Govt. Industrial Tribunal-cum-Labour Court, in applications u/s. 33 C(2) of 1947 Act rejecting their claims - Relief as to re-classification of workmen claimed u/s. 33 C(2) of Act - Held, cannot be claimed - No ground to set aside order of Tribunal - Appeal dismissed.
