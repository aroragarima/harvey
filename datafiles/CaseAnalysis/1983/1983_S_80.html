Motor General Traders And Another Etc. Etc. v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Others Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 256; (1984) 1 SCC 222; AIR 1984 SC 121; 1984 (1) RCJ 145; 1983 (2) RCR(Rent) 579; 1983 (2) RentLR 713; 1983(2) SCALE 513; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 594

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Andhra State Act, 1953

Summary: Rent Control - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, ss. 32(b) and 26 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - States Reorganisation Act, 1956 - Tenancy - Provisions of s. 32(b) of 1960 Act - Exempting buildings constructed on or after 26-08-1957 from operation of Act - Challenge to its validity - Held, violative of art. 14 of Constitution - Challenge at very late stage, not improper, though it was in existence for 23 years and held valid by High Court previously - Petitions allowed.
