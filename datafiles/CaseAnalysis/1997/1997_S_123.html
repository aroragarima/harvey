Raj Kumar and Others v Shakti Raj and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2670; (1997) 9 SCC 527; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1029; AIR 1997 SC 2110; JT 1997 (2) SC 688; 1997 LabIC 2088; 1997(2) SCALE 228; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 1159; 1997 (2) SLR 130; 1997 (3) Supreme 572; 1997 (2) UJ 6

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 309, Proviso - Punjab Public Works Department (Irrigation Branch) Patwaris State Service Class III Rules, 1955, r. 6 - Appointment of canal patwaris - Written examinations of candidates for selection conducted under 1955 Rules - Posts taken out of purview of Board by modification in 1955 Rules and Selection Committee constituted in exercise of power under proviso to r. 6 - Names of successful candidates in exam called from employment exchange and interview taken by committee and appointments made - Held, Govt. should give wide publicity in media inviting applications from qualified persons for selection - Whole procedure arbitrary and illegal.
