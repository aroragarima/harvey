Poonam Chand Jain and another v Fazru

Supreme Court of India

15 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 870; (2004) 13 SCC 269; (2005) SCC (Cr) 190; AIR 2005 SC 38; 2005 CRLJ 100; JT 2004 (9) SC 208; 2005 (139) PLR 40; 2005 (1) RCR(Criminal) 600; 2004(8) SCALE 833; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 525

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 200 - Effect of dismissal of a complaint filed u/s. 200 of Code and whether second complaint can be filed? - Held, second complaint on same facts would be entertained only in exceptional circumstances, namely, where previous order was passed on an incomplete record or on misunderstanding of complaint or it was manifestly absurd or unjust - HC has not considered legality of order directing issuance of process keeping in view law laid down by this Court remitted matter to HC to record positive findings on relevant issues - Appeal disposed of.
