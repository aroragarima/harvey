Jai Kishan v Commissioner of Police and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1288; (1995) Supp3 SCC 364; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1233; AIR 1996 SC 660; 1995 (31) ATC 148; JT 1997 (10) SC 536; 1996 LabIC 589; 1995(3) SCALE 195; 1995 (2) SCJ 245; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 268; 1995 (3) SLJ 51; 1995 (2) SLR 706; 1995 (2) UJ 531

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Central Civil Services (Temporary Service) Rules, 1965, r. 5(e)(iii) - Service Rules - Validity of termination of police service on failure to improve when successful completion of probation was a condition precedent for confirmation - Held, termination of service is proper - Appeal dismissed.
