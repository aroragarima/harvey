Phool Patti & Anr v Ram Singh (Dead) Through Lrs. & Anr

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1001; (2009) 13 SCC 22; 2009 (3) AWC 2294; 2009 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1153; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 805; 2009 (107) RD 246; 2009(4) SCALE 694; [2009] 5 S.C.R. 362

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Matter referred to Larger Bench

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Registration Act, 1908 - Interpretation to the exception in s. 17(2) (vi) - Held, if a suit is filed in respect of property A but the decree is in respect of immovable property B, then the decree so far as it relates to immovable property B will require registration - Since there is no mention of any pre- existing right in the exception in clause (vi) it is difficult to accept the views in Bhoop Singh vs. Ram Singh Major 1995(5) SCC 709 - Inconsistency in the decisions in Bhoop Singh vs. Ram Singh Major 1995(5) SCC 709 and K. Raghunandan & Ors. vs. Ali Hussain Sabir & Ors. 2008(9) Scale 215 - Matter referred to Larger Bench.
