Santosh De v Archna Guha and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 106; (1994) Supp3 SCC 735; AIR 1994 SC 1229; AIR 1994 SCW 1480; 1994 (2) BLJ 348; 1994 (2) BLJR 1271; 1994 CRLJ 1975

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 386 and 482 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Delayed trial, infringing right to speedy trial - During pendency of prosecution for 14 years, not a single witnesss examined and charge sheet framed after lapse of about eight years - Proceedings quashed - Interference sought - Order not liable to be interfered - Order accordingly.
