Madan Naik (Dead) By Legal Representatives and v Mst. Hansubala Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 373; (1983) 3 SCC 15; AIR 1983 SC 676; 1983(1) SCALE 382; 1983 UJ 428

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908 O.22 rr. 4, 9(1), 9(2), O.43 r. 1(k), ss. 2(2), 100 - Letters Patent (Pat.), cl. 10 - (A) Abatement of proceeding - Whent no specific order is envisaged - (B) Abatment of appeal for non-substitution does not imply adjudication on merits and there is no decree - Legality of second appeal against such order - (C) Orders which are not judgments - High Court decision u/r. 1(k) of O. 43 refusing to set aside abatement - Held, abatement takes place on its own force by passage of time - Held, second appeal is incompetent - Letters Patent Appeal does not lie -Appeal Allowed
