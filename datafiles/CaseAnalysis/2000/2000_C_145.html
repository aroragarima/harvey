J.M. Baxi & Co v Commissioner Of Customs, New Kandla

Supreme Court of India

03 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3510; (2001) 9 SCC 275; AIR 2000 SC 3407; 2000 (120) E.L.T. 29; JT 2000 (1) SC 540; 2000 (39) RLT 627A

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Writ, In Accordance With Law, Barred By Time, High Court, Interim Order, Demand

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Demand was raised in writ - Same had been challenged on ground that it was barred by time - Held, High Court ought to have exercised its jurisdiction and determined questions which were raised in writ petition on merits - On facts, HC would necessarily have to consider contentions of respondents as well - Matter remitted - Appeal allowed.
