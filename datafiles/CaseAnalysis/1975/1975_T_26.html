Union of India and Others v Tata Iron and Steel Company Limited, Jamshedpur

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 436; (1976) 2 SCC 123; AIR 1976 SC 599; 1976 (5) CTR 328; 1977 (1) E.L.T. 61; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 1044; 1976 UJ 178

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Notification No. 30/60 D/- 01-03-1960granted exemption form excise duty - Its applicability to duty-paid pig iron - When duty-paid pig iron mixed with non-duty-paid pig iron - Exemption held applied to former - Appeal Dismissed.
