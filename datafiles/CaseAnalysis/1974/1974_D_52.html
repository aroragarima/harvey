Smt. Asha Devi v Dukhi Sao and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, M. Hameedullah Beg, A. Alagiriswami

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 374; (1974) 2 SCC 492; AIR 1974 SC 2048; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 611

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Letters Patent (Pat.), cl. 10 - CPC, 1908, ss. 100 and 101 - Letters Patent (Pat.), cl. 10 - Powers of Letters Patent Bench - Scope of - Not limited to question of law as u/s.100, CPC, 1908 - Decree - Held, whole decree lies open before the Court - Appeal Allowed.
