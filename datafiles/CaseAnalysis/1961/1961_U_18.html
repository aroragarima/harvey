Jawahar Lal Burman v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 337; AIR 1962 SC 378; 1962 ALJ 411; 1962 (1) MLJ(SC) 269; 1962 (1) SCJ 654; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 769

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Indian Contract Act, 1872 - Constitution of India, 1950 - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 32 and 33 - What remedy is open to a party to adopt in order to obtain an appropriate declaration about existence or validity of an arbitration agreement and is suit barred by s. 32? - Held, petition made by respondent was competent u/s. 32 of Act and has been properly entertained by Trial Court - Appeal dismissed.
