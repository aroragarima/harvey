M. Dayanand Reddy v A. P. Industrial Infrastructure Corporation Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 997; (1993) 3 SCC 137; AIR 1993 SC 2268; 1993 (2) ARBLR 251; JT 1993 (3) SC 566; 1993(2) SCALE 270; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 629; 1993 (2) UJ 180

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Absence of arbitration clause precludes one from appointing arbitrator - Fact that arbitration terms contained in copy of agreement sent to applicant - Not relevant - Signed agreement between parties was binding on parties - Written terms in original agreement signed by parties to be considered - Held, only original agreement and not copy is binding on parties regarding arbitration.
