Industrial Credit and Development Syndicate Now Called I. C. D. S. Limited v Smithaben H. Patel Smt. and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 409; (1999) 3 SCC 80; AIR 1999 SC 1036; [1999] 96 Comp Cas 1; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 114; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 555

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, r. 1(2) - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 59, 60 and 61 - Payment of decretal amount - Mode for - No direction regarding mode of payment by Trial Court - No agreement between parties specifying such mode - Debtor indicating for mode while making payment in which appropriation is to be made - Held, creditor not bound to accept such mode and should follow general rule of adjustment firstly towards interests and costs and then to principal amount - Debtor cannot take refuge of ss. 59 to 61 of Contract Act in such case.
