Abhishek Kumar v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1250; (2006) 12 SCC 44; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 308; 2007 (112) FLR 700; 2006(13) SCALE 658; 2007 (3) SLR 836; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 37

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Haryana Compassionate Assistance to Dependents of Deceased Government Employees Rules, 2003, r. 9 - Appointment - On compassionate grounds - Whether case required to be considered in terms of Rules existing in the year 2001? - Held, yes - Appointment sought for compassionate grounds when 2003 Rules were not in existence - Appellant's case, therefore, was required to be considered in terms of the Rules which were in existence in the year 2001 - Appellant was entitled to obtain an appointment on compassionate grounds - Appeal allowed.
