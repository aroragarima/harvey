Vinay Krishna v Keshav Chandra and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1019; (1993) Supp3 SCC 129; AIR 1993 SC 957

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 34 and 42 - Applicability of - Suit for declaration - Share in property - Plaintiff in possession of property with two other persons - Failure of plaintiff to claim relief of possession - Held, discretion of Court in granting decree for declaration barred - Prayer in plaint without specific plea for possession - Not sufficient.
