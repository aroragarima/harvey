India Household and Healthcare Ltd v Lg Household and Healthcare Ltd

Supreme Court of India

08 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 327; (2007) 5 SCC 510; AIR 2007 SC 1376; 2007 (2) AWC 1442; [2007] 136 Comp Cas 621; 2007 (2) CompLJ 425; JT 2007 (4) SC 325; 2007 (5) MLJ(SC) 1010; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 362; 2007(4) SCALE 209; [2007] 3 S.C.R. 726

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 11(5), 11(6) - Application for appointing an arbitrator on the respondent's purported failure to do so - Held, as and when a question in regard to the validity or otherwise of the arbitration agreement arises, a judicial authority would have the jurisdiction under certain circumstances to go into the said question - An application for appointment of an arbitrator, therefore, is not maintainable unless the procedure and mechanism agreed to by and between the parties is complied with - Application dismissed.
