Gajanan Fabrics Distributors, Sangli and Others v Collector of Central Excise, Pune

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2057; (1997) 11 SCC 66; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 451

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Whether Central Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal erred in its finding that show - cause notices issued to assessees (appellants) did not clearly indicate how demands for excise duty had been worked out nor was there any indication of source from which figures had been compiled? - Held, facts of these cases require to be reconsidered, having regard also to law that has now been settled by judgments of Supreme Court and High Court subsequent to passing of order under challenge - Order of Tribunal under appeal set aside - Matter remitted back to Collector - Appeals allowed.
