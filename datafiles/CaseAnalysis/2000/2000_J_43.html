Janatha Bazar (South Kanara Central Cooperative v Secretary, Sahakari Noukarara Sangha and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 472; (2000) 7 SCC 517; AIR 2000 SC 3129; 2000 (4) AWC 3309; JT 2000 (10) SC 589; 2000 (2) LLJ 1395; 2000(6) SCALE 446; 2000 (8) SLR 744; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 367

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Karnataka Cooperative Societies Act, 1959

Summary: Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 3, s. 11A - Reinstating the employee in service despite charges being established - Justification and relevancy of quantum of misappropriation, past record - Held, unjustified and irrelevant - Appeal allowed.
