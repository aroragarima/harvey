Khetrabasi Samal Etc v State Of Orissa Etc

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, S.M. Sikri, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 106; (1969) 2 SCC 571; AIR 1970 SC 272; 1970 CRLJ 369; 1970 (36) CutLT 85; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 880

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 239, 417(1), 417(3) and 479 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Practice and Procedure - Crimianal case - (A) Accused charged of offences committed in the course of same transaction - Case instituted against some of the accused upon complaint - Case clubbed u/s. 239 along with case instituted against other accused upon police report - Acquittal of all accused - Appeal against acquittal of accused against whom cognizance is taken on police report - Maintainability - (B) Acquittal of accused - Revision at the instance of private complainant - Revisional jurisdiction of High Court - Held, maintainable only at the instance of the State and not at the instance of complaint and remedy of complainant is u/s. 439 - HC cannot re-appraise the evidence and upset the findings of Magistrate - Appeal Allowed.
