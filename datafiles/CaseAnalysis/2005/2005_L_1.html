Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut-II v L.H. Sugar Factories Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 545; (2005) 13 SCC 245; 2006 (200) CTR 687; 2005 (187) E.L.T. 5; 2006 (3) S.T.R. 715

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Excise - Finance Act, ss. 70, 71A, 73 - Show cause notices issued to appellants invoking s.73 - Held, s.73 takes-in only case of assessees who are liable to file return u/s. 70 - Liability to file return is cast on respondent only u/s. 71 A - Class of persons come u/s. 71A is not brought under net of s.73 - Show cause notices not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
