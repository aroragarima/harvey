Smt. Dularia Devi v Janardan Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 687; (1990) Supp SCC 216; AIR 1990 SC 1173; 1990 ALJ 245; 1990 (1) AWC 471; JT 1990 (1) SC 417; 1990 RD 193; 1990(1) SCALE 431; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 799; 1990 (2) UJ 74

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908, Fraudulent

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Contract & Commercial - Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 49 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 20 - Bar to civil suit - Misrepresentation - Suit to set aside sale deed - Executant illiterate woman - Thumb impression obtained on sale deed by making her believe that she was executing gift deed in favour of her daughter - Misrepresentation is as to character of document - Sale deed - Void or voidable - Maintainability of suit - Held, sale deed is totally void and not voidable - Suit not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
