Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras v M. K. K. R. Muthukaruppan Chettiar

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 298; (1970) 2 SCC 546; AIR 1970 SC 486; [1970] 78 ITR 69

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 22(2), 22(3) and 34 - Assessee filing returns in his individual capacity in response to notice u/s. 22(2) and (3) - ITO issuing notice u/s. 34 without disposing of returns - Validity - Notice u/s. 34 and consequent assessments are invalid - Appeals Dismissed.
