V. Radhakrishnan v S.N. Loganatha Mudaliar

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, B.N. Kirpal, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 555; (1998) 6 SCC 431; AIR 1998 SC 2696; JT 1998 (5) SC 494; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1998 (2) RCJ 349; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 219; 1998(4) SCALE 478; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 1029; 1998 (6) SLT 597; 1998 (6) Supreme 293

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 10(3)(a)(iii) - Eviction of tenant on ground of bonafide requirement for setting up son's business - Landlord in occupation of non-residential building where he was carrying on his business - If operate as bar for seeking eviction - Held, would not operate - Appeal dismissed.
