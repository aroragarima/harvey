Nutan Arvind Smt v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3069; (1996) 2 SCC 488; (1996) SCC (L&S) 529; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 981; AIR 1996 SC 3352; 1996 (33) ATC 228; 1996 (1) CLR 427; 1996 (72) FLR 652; JT 1996 (1) SC 699; 1996 LabIC 1838; 1996(1) SCALE 656; 1996 (2) SCJ 296; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 491; 1996 (1) SLR 774; 1996 (1) Supreme 790

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Appeal - Promotion of candidates - On merits - After consideration by Department Promotion Committee - Candidates not found fit for promotion - Supreme Court - Held, Supreme Court decline to interfere - Cannot sit over assessment made by Department Promotion Committee as an appellate authority.
