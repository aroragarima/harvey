A. P. Aboobaker Musaliar v Distt. Registrar (G), Kozhikode and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1513; (2004) 11 SCC 247

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Societies Registration Act, 1860, s. 4 - Appellant filed list of governing body members accepted by District Registrar - Whether District Registrar was right in accepting list of governing body members filed by appellant? - Order of District Registrar did not become final - District Registrar did not adjudicate any dispute as such and only prima facie accepted list - Held, appellant could establish his claim in a competent forum - Appeal dismissed.
