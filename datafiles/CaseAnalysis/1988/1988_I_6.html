Supreme Court of India

06 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 487; (1988) 3 SCC 36; AIR 1988 SC 1340; 1988 (1) ARBLR 394; 1988 (3) Bom.C.R. 688; JT 1988 (2) SC 212; 1988 (1) KarLJ 797; 1988(1) SCALE 965; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 426; 1988 (2) UJ 91

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Reason for passing arbitration award stated - Award challenged on ground that detailed reasons are not given - When arbitrator has set out the history in the interim award - Held, cannot be set aside on that ground and arbitrator is not bound to give detailed reasons - Petition dismissed.
