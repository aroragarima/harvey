Karnataka Board of Wakf v Government of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 295; (2004) 10 SCC 779; 2004 (4) ALD(SC) 124; 2004 (56) ALR 19; 2004 (5) AWC 3878; 2004 (4) CHN 106; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 702; 2004(4) SCALE 856; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 255; 2004 (2) UC 1044

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Ancient Monuments Preservation Act, 1904, Old Wakf Act

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Ancient Monuments Preservation Act, 1904 - Ancient Monuments And Archaeological Sites And Remains Act, 1958 - Wakf Act, 1995, s. 26 - Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964, s. 67 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 27 - Old Wakf Act, s. 4, s. 56 - Question for resolution is factum of ownership, possession and title over suit property - In copy of Register of Ancient Protected Monuments, Govt. is referred to as owner - Suit property is Govt. property and not of a wakf character - Adverse possession by respondent is unsustainable - Appeals dismissed.
