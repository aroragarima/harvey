Som Prakash Rekhi v Union Of India & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 227; (1981) SCC (L&S) 200; (1981) 1 SCC 449; AIR 1981 SC 212; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 71; 1981 (42) FLR 13; 1981 (1) LLJ 79; 1981 (1) LLN 322; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 111; 1981 (1) SLR 154

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Public Sector, Controlled, Oil and Natural Gas Commission, Personality, Industrial Finance Corporation (Transfer of Undertaking and Repeal) Act, 1993

Summary: Labour and Industrial Laws - Constitution, arts. 12 - Burmah Shell (Acquisition of Undertakings in India) Act , 1976, ss.7, 9, 10, 11, 12 - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, s. 14 - Employees Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s.12 - Acquisition - What is the criteria for determining Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited as 'State' within art. 12 of constiution - Determination of the validity of the deduction made from PF and Gratuity under regulations made by Burmah shell - Corporation held to be 'State' within the enlarged meaning of art.12 - Petitioner held to be entitled to full pension - Petition dismissed.
