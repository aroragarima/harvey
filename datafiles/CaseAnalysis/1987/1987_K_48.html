Haji T.M. Hassan Rawther v Kerala Financial Corporation

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28796; (1988) 1 SCC 166; AIR 1988 SC 157; JT 1987 (4) SC 368; 1987(2) SCALE 1067; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1079

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 299 - Sale of tea plantation for recovery of its dues by State or instrumentality of State (Kerala Financial Corporation) - Departure from general rule of public auction or inviting of tenders and second highest bidder - Held, must be justified by compulsions and not by compromise - Appeal dismissed.
