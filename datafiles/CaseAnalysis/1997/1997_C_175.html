Baroda Electric Meters Limited v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Venkataswami, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 96; (1997) 11 SCC 697; 1998 (74) ECR 721; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 13; 1997 (22) RLT 5

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Assessable value - Whether, the excess freight would be a part of the assessable value? - Followed Indian Oxygen Ltd. case [1988 Indlaw SC 799] - Duty of excise is a tax on the manufacturer and not a tax on the profits made by a dealer on transportation - Tribunal order set aside - Appeal allowed.
