State Of Kerala v M.K. Kunhikannan Nambiar Manjerimanikoth, Naduvil

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1930; (1996) 1 SCC 435; AIR 1996 SC 906; AIR 1996 SCW 301; JT 1995 (8) SC 533; 1995(6) SCALE 734; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 139

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964, s.83 - Ceiling area - Determination of - Application for impleadment - By persons claiming to be tenants of land in question - Rejection of, by Taluk Land Board holding it collusive - High Court confirming - Becoming final - Subsequent order of High Court in another petition by landholder against Determination of Ceiling area holding that ceiling proceedings were illegal - Effect of previous order of Board rejecting impleadment - Held, not invalid thereby - Void orders - Would be effective inter parties till set aside by higher forum.
