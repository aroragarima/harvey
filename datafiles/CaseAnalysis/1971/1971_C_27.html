Chhugamal Rajpal v S. P. Chaliha and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 335; (1971) 1 SCC 453; AIR 1971 SC 730; 1973 CTR 484; [1971] 79 ITR 603; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 342; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 442; [2002] 124 TAXMAN 82

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 148 and 151(2) - ITO issued notice u/s. 148 r/w s. 151(2) - On feeling that certain transactions by assessees with creditors were bogus and there was case for investigating truth of alleged transaction - Validity - Held, is invalid as not complying with requirements of s. 151(2) of the Act - Appeal Allowed.
