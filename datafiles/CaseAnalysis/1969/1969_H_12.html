Sushila Kashinath Dhonde and Others v Harilal Govindji Bhogani and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, I.D. Dua, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 363; (1969) 3 SCC 223; AIR 1971 SC 1495; 1970 RCJ 138; 1969 RCR(Rent) 996; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 950

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Land & Property - Contract & Commercial - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Bombay Rent, Hotel And lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 18(3) and 28 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 - Registration Act, 1908 - Registered deed of charge for construction - Loan executed by defendants in favour of plaintiff - Deed was complying with all requirements of s. 18(3) of Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 - Defendants failed to let out premises to plaintiff contrary to deed of charge - Suit by plaintiff for enforcing charge for recovery of loan - Issue of Jurisdiction - Held, Special Court under s. 28 of Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 alone has jurisdiction in suits related to claims or questions arising out of Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 or any of its provisions within s. 28(1) - Plaintiff's rights flowed from Act and not from contract, namely, deed of charge - Appeal dismissed.
