Debu Mahato v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, D.G. Palekar, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 444; (1974) 4 SCC 135; (1974) SCC (Cr) 274; AIR 1974 SC 816; 1974 CRLJ 699; 1974 CRLR 226; 1974 UJ 182

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Single Act

Summary: Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, ss. 3(1) and 88(1) - Detention order - (A) Grounds - Single act of wagon breaking - Whether it can form the basis for making of order of detention - (B) Communication of grounds of detention to detenu - One of the grounds not communicated - Effect - Held, no satisfaction at all or was in any event colourable and it could not form the basis for the making of the order of detention - Order is invalid - Petition Allowed.
