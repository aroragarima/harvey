Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, A.M. Ahmadi, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1984, Life Insurance Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1981

Summary: Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, s. 48(2)(cc), Sub s. 2(c) - Life Insurance Corporation of India (Staff) Regulations, 1960, Regn. 14 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo) and 25 F - Retrenchment - Termination of service - Probationer - Failing to get stipulated target under contract of employment - Without notice - Held, not retrenchment - No application of s. 25-F.
