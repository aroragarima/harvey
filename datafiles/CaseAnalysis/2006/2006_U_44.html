Mathura Prasad v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 734; (2007) 1 SCC (L&S) 292; (2007) 1 SCC 437; AIR 2007 SC 381; 2006 (6) AWC 5418; 2007 (2) CLR 154; 2007 (111) FLR 1211; 2007 (3) G.L.R. 2042; 2007 (1) LLJ 1021; 2007 (1) LLN 592; 2006(11) SCALE 368; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 264

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 309 - Railway Servants (Discipline & Appeal) Rules, 1968, rr. 10(2), 10(3) - Application of - Charge that service card was fake - Disciplinary authority differed with findings of Inquiry Officer and punishment of removal from service was imposed - Held, if sufficient materials are not available on record, a direction for holding a further inquiry may be issued in terms of r 10(2) so as to enable department to lead further evidence before him - No reasons recorded - Matter is remitted to disciplinary authority It may pass an appropriate order upon application of his mind afresh - Appeal allowed.
