Babulal Narottamdas and Others v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 625; (1991) Supp2 SCC 618; AIR 1991 SC 513; 1991 (91) CTR 127; [1991] 187 ITR 473; JT 1990 (4) SC 784; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 541; [1991] 55 TAXMAN 3; 1991 (2) TLR 26

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 10 and 13 - Accrual of income - Assessee, a Managing agent following mercantile system of accounting - Resolution by company for payment of additional remuneration to assessee - Litigation Pending against him - When accrual of income occurs - Whether, on date of resolution or of judgment upholding resolution? - Held, income accrues to assessee on date of resolution and not on date of judgment - Appeal dismissed.
