Employees’ State Insurance Corporation And Anr v Tata Engineering & Co. Locomotive Co.Ltd. And Anr

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, A. Alagiriswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 213; (1975) 2 SCC 835; (1976) SCC (L&S) 41; AIR 1976 SC 66; 1975 (31) FLR 387; 1976 LabIC 1; 1976 (1) LLJ 81; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 199; 1975 UJ 879

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Employees State Insurance Act, 1948, s. 2(9) - Apprentices Act, 1961, s. 2(a) - Employees state insurance - Apprentice - If an employee within s. 2 (9) of Employees' State Insurance Act - Held, an employee - Appeal dismissed.
