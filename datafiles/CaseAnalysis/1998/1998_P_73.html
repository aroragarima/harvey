Director of Education (Secondary) and Another v Pushpendra Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M. Srinivasan, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 981; (1998) 5 SCC 192; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1302; AIR 1998 SC 2230; 1998 ALJ 1525; 1998 (33) ALR 468; 1998 (3) AWC 1772; 1998 (2) CLR 223; 1998 (2) CLT 118; JT 1998 (4) SC 155; 1998 LabIC 2123; 1998 (3) LLN 47; 1998(3) SCALE 590; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 432; 1999 (1) SLJ 32; 1998 (4) SLR 386; 1998 (5) SLT 42; 1998 (5) Supreme 1; 1998 (2) UPLBEC 1310

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921, s. 9(4) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Regulations framed under, Regn. 106 - Compassionate appointment is an exception to general provisions for appointment - Dependent of employee of non - Govt., recognised aided school - Regn. 106 providing for Compassionate appointment - Held, rule for compassionate appointment cannot therefore be so construed so as to subsume main provision - Cannot be construed as compelling authorities to make appointment to Class III posts by creating supernumerary posts.
