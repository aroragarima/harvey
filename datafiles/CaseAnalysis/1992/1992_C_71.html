Commissioner of Income Tax Gujarat v Bababhai Pitamberdas (Huf)

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 584; (1993) Supp3 SCC 530; 1993 (112) CTR 130; [1993] 200 ITR 563; [1993] 68 TAXMAN 342

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 34, 236 (2) - Whether Tribunal was right in law in holding that notice served on April 10, 1962 u/s. 34 and proceedings commenced in pursuance of said notices and orders passed therein were invalid and bad in law? - Held, High Court as well as Tribunal were in error in taking view that question of law is concluded by decision in Banarsi Debi in view of subsequent decision of Supreme Court in R. K. Upadhyaya v Shanabhai P. Patel - HC is required to direct Tribunal to state a case and refer to it aforesaid question of law for its decision - Appeals allowed.
