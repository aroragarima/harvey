Sushila Devi And Anr. v Hari Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 188; (1971) 2 SCC 288; AIR 1971 SC 1756; [1971] S.C.R. 671; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 671

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Frustration

Summary: Contract Act, 1872 - Provision of s. 56 - (A) Law of frustration - Embodied in the provision - Applicability to lease - (B) Impossibility contemplated by the provision - Scope - Held, is not applicable to leases as it applies only to agreement - Is not confined to something which is not humanly possible - Appeal Dismissed.
