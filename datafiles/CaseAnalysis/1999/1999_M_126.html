Mgmt Of Thanjavur Textiles Ltd v B. Purushotham And Ors

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 997; (1999) 3 SCC 316; (1999) SCC (L&S) 677; AIR 1999 SC 1290; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 646; 1999 (1) CLR 1023; JT 1999 (2) SC 364; 1999 (1) LLJ 104; 1999 (1) LLJ 1049; 1999 (3) MLJ(SC) 35; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 1150; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 1036

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.2, item 3, sch.2, item 1 - Domestic enquiry - Advocate as Enquiry Officer by Management - Not company's employee - Entitlement - Held, no distinction applicable on ground of employee or outsider - And reference of the enquiry to an advocate was valid - Appeal allowed.
