Union of India Others v Rabia Bikaner Etc

Supreme Court of India

07 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1728; (1997) 6 SCC 580; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1524; AIR 1997 SC 2843; 1997 (77) FLR 68; JT 1997 (6) SC 95; 1997 LabIC 2858; 1997 (3) LLN 265; 1997(4) SCALE 625; 1998 (1) SLJ 181; 1997 (4) SLR 717; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 442; 1997 (6) Supreme 382

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Railway Establishment Manual, Para 2511 - Family pension benefits for casual labourer was in question - Family pension scheme is applicable only on their absorption against regular temporary posts which is after putting in six months of temporary service and further one years minimum service on appointment to temporary post - Widow of casual labour who dies after putting in six months service but before appointment to temporary post - Held, she is not entitled to family pension.
