Harpal Kaur Chahal Smt v Director, Punjab Instructions, Punjab and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 967; (1995) Supp4 SCC 706; (1996) SCC (L&S) 226; 1996 (32) ATC 172; 1995 (8) SLR 828

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Whether appointment of appellant was illegal? - Held, last date for receipt of applications was fixed for purpose of recruitment - Only those candidates who possessed all qualifications on that date were eligible to apply and to be recruited according to rules - Appellant did not possess qualifications on that day - Her appointment by Board is illegal - Art. 14 cannot be extended to legalise illegal orders - Appeal dismissed.
