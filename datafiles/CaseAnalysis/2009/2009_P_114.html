Ram Suresh Singh v Prabhat Singh @ Chhotu Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 592; (2009) 6 SCC 681; AIR 2009 SC 2805; 2009 ALL MR (Cri) 3155; 2009 CRLJ 3997; JT 2009 (7) SC 459; 2010 (1) RCR(Criminal) 244; 2009(8) SCALE 512; [2009] 7 S.C.R. 451

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Women & Children

Keywords: Public Document, Date Of Occurrence, School Register, Juvenile Justice (Care & Protection of Children) Rules, 2001

Summary: Criminal - Women & Children - Juvenile Justice (Care & Protection of Children) Act, 2000 - Juvenile Justice (Care & Protection of Children) Rules, 2001 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Murder - Determination of the age of a person - Whether High Court is right in relying upon the entries made in the School register in preference to the opinion of the Medical Board? - Held, opinion of the Medical Board is to be preferred only when a date of birth certificate from the school first attended is not available - Admission register of the school having been proved in accordance with law no reason as to why the same should not be taken into consideration - Appeal dismissed.
