Union Of India v Shantaram Gajanan Kanekar

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 762; (1994) Supp2 SCC 571; (1994) SCC (Cr) 1496

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3(1) and 9(1) - Detenu, did not know English and was conversant with Marathi language - High Court found that translated version of declaration u/s. 9(1) was defective and opined that it was not possible for detenu to make an effective representation - Order of detention was, therefore, quashed - Held, view taken by HC is unexceptionable and no reason to interfere - Appeal dismissed.
