Mahendra Kumar And Anr. v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28219; (1987) 3 SCC 265; AIR 1987 SC 1395; 1987 (1) CCC 1100; JT 1987 (2) SC 524; 1987(1) SCALE 1257; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 155; 1987 (2) UJ 274

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O.8 r.6A(1) - Limitation Act, 1963 - Treasure Trouve Act 1878, ss. 8, 13 and 14 - (A) Appeal by special leave - Whether counter-claim in suit u/s. 8 can be dismissed on the ground that it was barred by s.14 of the Indian Treasure Trove Act, 1878 - Held, in instant case s.14, has no manner of application to a suit filed u/s.8 - (B) Whether r.6A(1) of O.8 of the CPC, 1908 bars the filing of a counter-claim after the filing of a written statement - Not barred - Appeal allowed.
