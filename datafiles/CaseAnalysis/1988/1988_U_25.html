Accountant and Secretarial Services Private v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 522; (1988) 4 SCC 324; AIR 1988 SC 1708; 1988 (2) BankCLR 397; 1989 (2) CalLT(SC) 1; 1988 (2) CCC 445; JT 1988 (3) SC 78; 1988 (2) RCJ 433; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 278; 1988(2) SCALE 53; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 493; 1988 (2) UJ 471

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Agra Tenancy Act, 1926, Punjab Restitution of Mortgaged Lands Act, 1938, Public Premises Act 1971

Summary: Land and Property - Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971, s. 2(e)(2) - W.B. Public Land (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1962, s.1 - W.B. Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, s.1 - Constitution of India, 1950, sch. 7, list 1, entry 32, list 2, entry 18, list 3, entry 6, entry 7, entry 13 - Premises owned by a nationalised bank (a statutory Corporation) - The central act of 1971 and state acts of 1956 and 1962 were the legislation - Which provides for housing Eviction of unauthorised occupant from statutory corporation like nationalised bank - Questions raises to the validity , vires of the 1971 Act in regard to the W.B. state acts - Considering the premises to be public premises the provision of the central act of 1971 held to be intravires - But the question whether the 1971 Act prevails over the State Acts , found to be unnecessary to be decided - Order accordingly.
