Ram Sarup v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 12; (1979) SCC (L&S) 35; (1979) 1 SCC 168; AIR 1978 SC 1536; 1978 (37) FLR 229; 1978 LabIC 1535; 1978 (2) LLJ 409; 1979 (1) LLN 1; 1978 (2) SLR 836; 1978 UJ 773

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Punjab Labour Services (Class I and II) Rules, 1955 - Service Rules - Appointment as Labour-cum-Conciliation Officer u/r. 4 (1) of 1955 Rules Holding post for 9 years without having the necessary experience - Appointment held irregular but not void - Employee must be deemed to have been validly appointed on completion of necessary period of experience - Appeal Allowed.
