Commissioner Of Income Tax, Bombay v H. Holck Larsen

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 367; (1986) 3 SCC 364; AIR 1986 SC 1695; 1986 (2) CompLJ 219; 1986 (58) CTR 53; [1986] 160 ITR 67; JT 1986 SC 343; 1986(1) SCALE 1340; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 1072; 1986 TAXLR 904; [1986] 26 TAXMAN 305; 1986 (2) TLR 904; 1986 (2) UJ 264

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Tax - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 66 and 10 - Whether in respect of certain transactions in respective years, assessee was a dealer or an investor and consequentially whether income arising from sale of shares by assessee was to be taxed on revenue account or capital account ? - Held, assessee was not a dealer but an investor and fact was immaterial - Appeal dismissed.
