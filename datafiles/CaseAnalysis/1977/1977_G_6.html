Mohd. Salamatullah and Others v Government of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 374; (1977) 3 SCC 590; AIR 1977 SC 1481

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 33 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 73 - Constitution, art. 133 - (A) Breach of contract - Interference with finding of fact by appellate Court - Assessment of damages for breach based on estimated profits - (B) Constitution - Question of damages - Held, not justified in absence of reason based on fact - Supreme Court in appeal was not inclined to investigate the matter de novo when no ground had been raised in the memorandum of appeal to the HC - Appeal Allowed.
