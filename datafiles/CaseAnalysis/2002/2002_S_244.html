Association of Industrial Electricity Users v State of Andhra Pradesh and others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Shivaraj V. Patil, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 174; (2002) 3 SCC 711; AIR 2002 SC 1361; 2002 (3) ALD(SC) 26; JT 2002 (2) SC 595; 2002(2) SCALE 569; 2002 (2) SCJ 324; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 273; 2002 (2) SLT 460; 2002 (2) Supreme 287

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Financial Principles And Their Application

Summary: Fixing a tariff and providing for cross-subsidy by the Regulatory Commission constituted under the Andhra Pradesh Electricity Reforms Act, 1998 is essentially a matter of policy and normally a court would refrain from interfering with a policy decision unless the power exercised is arbitrary or ex facie bad in law.
