Kathiawar Industries Limited v Jaffrabad Municipality

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 203; (1979) 4 SCC 56; AIR 1979 SC 1721; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 243; 1979 UJ 732

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Indirect Tax - Saurashtra Terminal Tax and Octroi Ordinance, 1949, s. 3 - Saurashtra Octroi and Terminal Tax Rules, 1949 - Bombay District Municipal Act, 1901- Item 23 of sch.1, r.3 of 1949 Rules - Connotion of 'Consumption or use', 'Salt for factory' - Uncrushed salt brought to Octroi area and crushed - Whether octroi is leviable - Held, is leviable - Appeals dismissed.
