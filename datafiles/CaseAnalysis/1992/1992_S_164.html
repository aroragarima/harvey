Lilasons Breweries (Pvt.) Limited v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 974; (1992) 3 SCC 293; AIR 1992 SC 1393; JT 1992 (3) SC 236; 1992(1) SCALE 834; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 595; 1992 (2) UJ 141

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Madhya Pradesh Excise Act, 1915, s. 62 - Madhya Pradesh Brewery Rules, 1970, r. 22 - Validity of recovery of duty from particular unit - Not sufficient to meet expenses of officers - R. 22 permitting raising of demand of additional excise duty, without its being actually due - Whether ultra vires? - Held, is ultra vires - Appeal allowed.
