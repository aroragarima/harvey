Messrs Kapoor Nilokheri Co-Operative Dairy Farm Society Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 465; (1973) 1 SCC 708; AIR 1973 SC 1338

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30 - Arbitral award - Setting aside of award - Question of Law (question of interpreting agreement) - Interference - Held, where an arbitrator is called upon to decide effect of agreement, he has really to decide a question of law and his decision is not open to challenge - Appeal dismissed.
