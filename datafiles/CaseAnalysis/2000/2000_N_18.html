Mafatlal Industries Limited v Nadiad Nagar Palika and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, Ruma Pal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 565; (2000) 3 SCC 1; AIR 2000 SC 1223; 2000 (1) CLT 368; 2000 (117) E.L.T. 290; 2000 (2) G.L.R. 1496; JT 2000 (2) SC 564; 2000(2) SCALE 152; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 52; 2000 (2) SLT 528; 2000 (118) STC 494; 2000 (2) Supreme 53

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1963

Summary: Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1964, ss. 99(1)(iv) and 2(16) - Constitution of India,1950, Sch. 7, List 2, entry 52 - Octroi duty - Levy of - Cloth of 100 meters brought into octroi limits - Cutting into smaller lengths and exporting - Consumption or sale or use, whether - Held, not in for use or consumption or sale - No octroi.
