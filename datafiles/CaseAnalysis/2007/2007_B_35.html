Sandeep Polymers Pvt. Ltd v Bajaj Auto Ltd. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 735; (2007) 7 SCC 148; AIR 2007 SC 2656; 2007 (5) Bom.C.R. 744; 2007 (4) JhrCR(SC) 176; JT 2007 (9) SC 363; 2007 (6) MLJ(SC) 67; 2007 (4) RCR(Civil) 422; 2007(9) SCALE 304; 2007 (8) SCJ 168; [2007] 8 S.C.R. 437

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 7 r. 10-A - Suit for recovery of Rs.79, 63, 99, 736/- as damages for breach of contract - Single Judge of the High Court of Bombay, Nagpur Bench, directing return of the plaint, as according to the High Court the Court at Nagpur had no jurisdiction to entertain a part of the claims made in the suit - Appeal against - Held, it shall be open to the appellant to file a separate suit in relation to cause of action if any relating for the purchase orders, at Pune as was submitted by counsel for the appellant - If the appellant is so advised it may move for amendment of the suit at Nagpur - It shall be open to the respondents-defendants to raise all objections and take such pleas as are available in law - Appeal disposed of.
