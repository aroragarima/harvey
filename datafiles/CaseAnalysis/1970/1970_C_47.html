Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Madhya Pradesh v Sir Hukumchand Mannalal & Co.

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 141; (1970) 2 SCC 352; AIR 1971 SC 383; [1970] 78 ITR 18; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 646

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26A - Contract Act, 1872, s. 11 - Partnership Act, 1932, s. 4 - Registration of firms - Permissibility - Joint Hindu family - Two or more coparceners entering into partnership - Held, firm can be granted registration - Appeal Dismissed.
