Dulichand Lakshminarayan v Commissioner of Income Tax, Nagpur

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1956

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhi Ranjan Das, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, T.L. Venkatarama Aiyyar

Where Reported

1956 Indlaw SC 13; AIR 1956 SC 354; [1956] 29 ITR 535; 1956 (1) MLJ 164; 1956 (1) MLJ(SC) 164; 1956 SCJ 317; [1956] 1 S.C.R. 154

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: General Clauses Act

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26 A - Partnership Act, 1932, s. 4 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 3(42) - Interpretation of word and phrases 'Firm' and 'Person' - Partnership between firms and individuals - Registration u/s 26 A of Partnership Act - Held, firm is not a 'person' - Definition u/s 3(42), General Clauses Act, cannot be imported to s. 4, Partnership Act - Partnership cannot be among firms but individual members, personally - Appeal dismissed.
