Central Coal Fields Ltd v State Of Orissa And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1083; (1992) Supp3 SCC 133; 1992 (2) ACC 271; AIR 1992 SC 1371; AIR 1992 SCW 1366; 1992 (74) CLT 171; JT 1992 (3) SC 77; 1992(1) SCALE 984; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 982; 1992 (2) UJ 230

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation Orissa - Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1975, s. 2(b) - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 2(18) - Motor Vehicle - Adopted for use of roads - Dumpers and Rockers using rubber tyres - They are vehicles adapted for use on road - Are motor vehicles liable to taxation - Held, entitled to a regulatory and compensatory tax - Appeal dismissed.
