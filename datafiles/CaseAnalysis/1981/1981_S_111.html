Lila Dhar v State Of Rajasthan & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 253; (1981) 4 SCC 159; (1981) SCC (L&S) 588; AIR 1981 SC 1777; 1981 (43) FLR 347; 1981 LabIC 1515; 1981 (2) LLJ 297; 1982 (1) LLN 8; 1981(3) SCALE 1235; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 320; 1981 (2) SLJ 266; 1981 (3) SLR 56; 1981 UJ 719

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Tort

Summary: Constitution, arts. 14, 16 and 309, Proviso - Rajasthan Judicial Service Rules, 1955, Pre. and sch. 3 - Selection for posts of Munsifs - Constitutionality of allocation of 25% of marks to viva voce under 1955 Rules - Held, does not render selection arbitrary and violative of arts. 14 and 16 - Observation in 1980 INDLAW SC 244 relating to public employment held per incuriam - Petition Dismissed.
