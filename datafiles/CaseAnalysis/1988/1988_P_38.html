Collector Of Central Excise. Coimbatore v Protein Products Of India Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 290; (1989) Supp1 SCC 729; AIR 1989 SC 627; 1989 (19) ECC 73; 1989 (20) ECR 134; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 749; [1990] 183 ITR 655; JT 1988 (4) SC 517; 1988(2) SCALE 1404; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 993; 1989 (1) ShimLC 299; 1989 (74) STC 98; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 993; 1989 (1) UJ 108

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 35L and 37 - Notification of Govt. of India D/- 30-06-1979 - Excise Duty - Exemption - Interpretation of words by rule of ejusdem generis - Expression 'bone products' - Whether include ossein and gelatine manufactured from bones by chemical process - Held, it includes - Appeal dismissed.
