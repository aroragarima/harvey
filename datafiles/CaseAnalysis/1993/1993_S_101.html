Sharwan Kumar and Others v Director General of Health Services and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1120; (1993) 3 SCC 332; AIR 1994 SC 1448; AIR 1994 SCW 887; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 624

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Admission to medical college after obtaining eligibility certificate from Registrar of University - Subsequently certificate withdrawn and admission cancelled - Validity - Held, withdrawal held as improper - Directions given to grant admission to student - Order accordingly.
