Central Inland Water Transportcorporation Ltd. v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 142; (1975) SCC (L&S) 304; (1975) 4 SCC 348; AIR 1975 SC 1639; 1975 (30) FLR 391; 1975 LabIC 1083; 1975 (2) LLJ 117; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 443

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Payment Of Bonus Act, 1965, s. 16(1) - Bonus - New establishment tests - Central Inland Water Transport Corporation set up and properties of River Steam Navigation Co. transferred to it - Thereby Rajabagan dockyard owned by company coming into hands of corporation - Whether Dockyard was a new establishment u/s. 16(1) of Act - Held, no - Appeal dismissed.
