Balwant Narayan Bhagde v M. D. Bhagwat & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 177; (1976) 1 SCC 700; AIR 1975 SC 1767; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 250

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.16, 17(1) and 48(1) - CPC, 1908, O. 21, rr. 35, 36, 95 and 96 - Mode of taking possession under 'Waste and arable land' - When Government or Commissioner can withdraw from acquisition of land u/s. 48(1) of 1894 Act - When possession of land has been taken in accordance with ss. 16 or 17(1) of the Act - Held, neither Govt. nor Commissioner could thereafter withdraw from the acquisition of any portion of this land u/s. 48(1) of the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
