R. Radhakrishnan v Director General of Police and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, H.S. Bedi

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1041; (2008) 1 SCC 660; AIR 2008 SC 578; 2008 (5) AWC 4845; JT 2007 (13) SC 225; 2007(12) SCALE 539; 2007 (8) SCJ 137; [2007] 11 S.C.R. 456; 2008 (1) SLR 655; 2008 (1) WLN(SC) 81

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Application for appointment to the post of Fireman - Involvement in a criminal case - As petitioner made false statement in verification roll he was not selected - Held, appellant had suppressed a material fact - Question of exercising an equitable jurisdiction in his favour would not arise - Appeals dismissed.
