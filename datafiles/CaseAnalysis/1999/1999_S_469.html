Yoginath D. Bagde v State of Maharashtra and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1318; (1999) 7 SCC 739; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1385; AIR 1999 SC 3734; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 658; 1999 (2) CLR 971; 1999 (3) ESC 2344; JT 1999 (6) SC 62; 2000 (1) SLJ 174; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 490; 1999 (8) Supreme 129; 1999 (3) UPLBEC 2419

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 32, 235, 309 and 311(2), Proviso - Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, r. 8(25) - Whether Disciplinary Committee, can dismiss officer of subordinate judiciary from service - Whether dismissal where disciplinary authority disagrees with findings of enquiry officer in favour of officer, is violative of principles of natural justice? - Held, committee can dismiss - Principles of natural justice violated - Appeal allowed.
