Swamy Atmananda and Others v Sri Ramakrishna Tapovanam and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 302; (2005) 10 SCC 51; AIR 2005 SC 2392; 2005 (2) ESC 272; JT 2005 (4) SC 472; 2005 (3) MLJ(SC) 65; 2005 (3) RCR(Civil) 404; 2005(4) SCALE 117; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 556; 2005 (3) Supreme 396; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 2070

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Recognized Private Schools (Regulation) Act, 1973

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Education - Tamil Nadu Recognized Private Schools (Regulation) Act, 1973, ss. 53, 53A - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 31 r. 2, s. 11 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 33 - Societies Registration Act, 1860 - Jurisdiction of civil Court - Whether jurisdiction of Civil Court stands ousted in terms of ss.53, 53 A of Tamil Nadu Recognized Private Schools (Regulation) Act, 1973? - Held, there are principles relating to exclusion of jurisdiction of civil courts - This case does not fulfil conditions and jurisdiction of Civil Court was not excluded by reason of ss.53, 53 A - Appeal dismissed.
