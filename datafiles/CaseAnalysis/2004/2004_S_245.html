Supreme Court of India

13 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 227 - Transfer of postal employees from one division to another - HC quashed transfer order - Transfer is a matter for employer to consider depending upon administrative necessities and extent of solution for problems faced by administration - Court cannot direct one way or other - Judgment of HC set aside.
