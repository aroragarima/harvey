Tribhuvandas Bhimji Zaveri and Another v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2502; (1997) 11 SCC 276; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 467

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Socio-Economic

Summary: Excise - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Gold (Control) Act, 1968, s. 55 - Gold Control (Forms, Fees and Miscellaneous Matters) Rules, 1968, r. 13 - Whether appellant has contravened provisions of s. 55 of Act r/w r. 13 of Rules in relation to gold ornaments found short in stock and their acts of omission and commission rendered themselves liable to penal action under Act? - Held, tribunal, based its decision on accounts which did not properly reflect correct state of affairs as far as shortfall is concerned - Failure of department to supply certified copy of check list prepared at time of raid to appellants to enable them to check and verify particulars has prejudiced appellants - Failure to supply this important piece of information to appellants has prejudiced appellants - Impugned order is set aside and penalty paid shall be refunded - Appeal allowed.
