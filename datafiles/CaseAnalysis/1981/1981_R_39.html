Ayodhya Dube and Others v Ram Sumer Singh

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 63; (1981) Supp SCC 83; (1982) SCC (Cr) 471; AIR 1981 SC 1415; 1981 CRLJ 1016; 1981 CRLR 430; 1981(1) SCALE 811

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 401 - Order of retrial by setting aside acquittal - Valid or not - High Court justified, directing retrial by setting aside acquittal - Sessions Judge acquitted accused - Ignoring probative value of FIR and reliable testimony of eyewitnesses and without considering material evidence on record - Judgment full of inconsistencies - Faulty reasoning - High Court justified in directing retrial and setting aside acquittal - Appeal dismissed.
