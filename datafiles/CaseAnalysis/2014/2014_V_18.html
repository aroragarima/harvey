Union of India and others v Vasavi Co-operative Housing Society Limited and others

Supreme Court of India

07 January 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Panicker Radhakrishnan, A.K. Sikri

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 13; (2014) 2 SCC 269; AIR 2014 SC 937; 2014 (2) ALD(SC) 157; 2014(2) ALL MR 415; 2014 (102) ALR 735; 2014 (2) AWC 1507; 2014 (1) CLT(SC) 313; 2014 (4) CTC 471; JT 2014 (1) SC 385; 2014 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1201; 2014 (3) MahLJ 244; 2014(3) M.P.H.T. 367; 2014 (2) MPLJ 486; 2014 (2) RCR(Civil) 76; 2014 (122) RD 634; 2014 (2) RLW 1453; 2014(1) SCALE 126; [2014] 1 S.C.R. 180; 2014 (3) WLN(SC) 6

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Land Revenue Act, 1317, A.P. Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923, Cantonment Land Administration Rules, 1937, Cantonment Land Administration Rules, 1925, Secunderabad and Aurangabad Cantonment Land Administration Rules, 1930, Hyderabad Record of Rights in Land Regulation, 1358, Hyderabad Record of Rights Rules, 1956

Summary: Suit-For declaration of title and possession-Plaintiff has to succeed only on strength of his case and not on weakness of case set up by defendants in suit for declaration of title and possession-Plaintiff not succeeded in establishing his title and possession of suit land in question-Judgment of trial court, affirmed by High Court-Set aside.
