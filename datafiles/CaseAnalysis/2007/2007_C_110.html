Commissioner Of Central Excise, Bangalore v Messrs Brindavan Beverages Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 June 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 709; (2007) 5 SCC 388; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1212; 2007 (213) E.L.T. 487; 2007(8) SCALE 549; 2007 (5) SCJ 422; [2007] 7 S.C.R. 1033

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Intellectual Property

Keywords: Brand Names, Appeals dismissed., Exemption Notification, Aerated Water, Holding Company, Mis- Statement

Summary: Excise - Show Cause Notice - Allegation - Nothing specific as to the role of the respondents mentioned in the SCN - Arrangements as alleged have not been shown to be within the knowledge or at the behest or with the connivance of the respondents - Held, if the allegations in the show cause notice are not specific and are on the contrary vague, lack details and/or unintelligible, that is sufficient to hold that the noticee was not given proper opportunity to meet the allegations indicated in the show cause notice - Impugned order upheld.
