Mitter Sen and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 515; (1976) SCC (Cr) 190; (1976) 1 SCC 723; AIR 1976 SC 1156; 1976 CRLJ 857; 1975 CRLR 563; 1975 UJ 735

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1973, s. 154 - IPC, 1860, ss. 34 and 323 - Evidentiary value of FIR - Effect of discrepancy in FIR and prosecution case - (B) Proof of common intention - Common intention to cause simple hurt - Held, evidence of the prosecution withnesses cannot, be accepted at its face value and be relied upon implicitly - Held not proved on facts - Appeal Allowed.
