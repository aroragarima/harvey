Ram Ekbal Sharma v State Of Bihar And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 808; (1990) SCC (L&S) 491; (1990) 3 SCC 504; AIR 1990 SC 1368; 1990 (14) ATC 741; 1990 LabIC 1188; 1990 (2) LLJ 601; 1991 (1) LLN 56; 1990(1) SCALE 12; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 679; 1990 (2) SLJ 98; 1992 (6) SLR 673; 1990 (2) UJ 284

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, Bihar Service Code, 1979

Summary: Service - Constitution - Bihar Service Code, 1979, rr. 74(b)(i), 74(b)(ii) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14, 309 and 311 - Compulsory retirement - Civil servant - In public interest - Order passed in terms of rule - State averred that order was passed in view of financial irregularities and loss thereby - Penal nature of order - Constitutionality - Held, order is in violation of art. 311 - Appeal allowed.
