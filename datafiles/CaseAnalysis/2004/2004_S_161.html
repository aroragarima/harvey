Bihari Nath Goswami v Shiv Kumar Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 164; (2004) 9 SCC 186; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1435; 2004 (1) CCR 370; 2004 (2) Crimes 209; 2004 (1) JCC 668; 2004(2) SCALE 671; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 623; 2004 (2) SLT 357; 2004 (2) Supreme 243

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 149, 302, 364 - Appeal against acquittal by special leave - Though some of reasons given by HC may not be approved and on facts - No interference warranted - View taken to direct acquittal is a possible view - Merely because on evidence a different view is available to be taken - Cannot be a ground to upset acquittal - Appeal dismissed.
