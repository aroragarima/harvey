John Tinson and Company Private Limited and Others v Surjeet Malhan (Mrs) and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2480; (1997) 9 SCC 651; AIR 1997 SC 1411; 1997 (2) AWC 652; 1997 (2) CLT 263; [1997] 88 Comp Cas 750; 1997 (3) CompLJ 40; JT 1997 (2) SC 457; 1997(2) SCALE 99; 1997 (1) SCJ 388; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 842; 1997 (2) Supreme 610; 1997 (1) UJ 487

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Blank Transfer

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, s. 108 - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 10 and 25 - Share - Transfer of - Contract not concluded between shareholder and broker - Transfer contemplated for Re 1/- Non authorisation to transfer Share by shareholder to her husband or broker - Entrustment of blank forms - Held, not amounts to concluded contract of transfer - Invalid as no prior sanction of directors according to requirement of articles of association - Void for absence of consideration also.
