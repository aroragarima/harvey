K. Channegowda and others; v Karnataka Public Service Commission and others

Supreme Court of India

06 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 882; (2005) 12 SCC 688; JT 2005 (12) SC 390; 2005(8) SCALE 455; 2005 (6) SLR 44; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 126

Case Digest

Subject: Competition; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Conduct of competitive examination by Karnataka Public Service Commission for recruitment to post of Gazetted Probationers - Tribunal directed Karnataka Public Service Commission to get all answer scripts evaluated afresh - HC directed for re-evaluation of some of answer scripts - Appeal against by unsuccessful candidates - Held, this is not a case where examination deserves to be cancelled - Finding recorded by HC that there is really no allegation imputing unfairness in matter of examination of answer scripts by examiners, is justified - Order of HC upheld - Appeals dismissed.
