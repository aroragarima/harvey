Supreme Court of India

06 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Defence of India Act, 1962

Summary: Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable property Act, 1952, s.8 - Acquisition of land-Valuation - Determination - Evidence of sales relating to acquired land or land in vicinity - Court resorts - Parable sales method adopted for Valuation of acquired land - Held, building potentiality of land at time of acquisition - Has no significance.
