Government of Kerala and others v Sudhir Kumar Sharma and others

Supreme Court of India

02 September 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 554; (2013) 10 SCC 178; 2013 (101) ALR 237; 2013 (6) ALT(SC) 33; 2013 (6) AWC 5751; 2013 (5) Bom.C.R. 586; 2013 (4) CLT(SC) 210; 2013 (5) CTC 458; JT 2013 (12) SC 1; 2013 (3) KLT 1005; 2013 (3) MahLJ 121; 2013 (6) MLJ 731; 2015(2) M.P.H.T. 54; 2014 (2) MPLJ 373; 2013 (4) RCR(Civil) 664; 2013 (121) RD 481; 2013(11) SCALE 107; [2013] 10 S.C.R. 62; 2013 (3) WLN(SC) 152

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Tort

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Section 80 and Order VII, Rule 11-Suit against State-Notice under Section 80 (1)-Absence of compliance with Section 80 (1)-Maintainability of suit-Suit filed without serving notice as required by Section 80 (1)-Suit may be filed against Government or public officer without serving notice as required by Section 80 (1) only with leave of Court-Court has to be satisfied after hearing parties that there was some grave urgency which required some urgent relief and, therefore, plaintiff was constrained to file suit without issuance of notice under Section 80 (1)-Admitted fact that no order had been passed on application filed under Section 80 (2) whereby leave of Court had been sought for filing suit without complying with provisions of Section 80 (1)-Trial court wrongly rejected applications for rejection of plaint filed under provisions of Order VII, Rule 11 by appellants-Trial court ought to have heard and decided application filed under Section 80 (2) before hearing applications under Order VII, Rule 11-Order of trial court rejecting applications under Order VII, Rule 11 quashed and set aside-Trial court directed that first of all decide application filed by respondent No. 1 (plaintiff) under Section 80 (2) and only after disposal of said application, applications filed by appellants under Order VII, Rule 11 to be decided-Impugned judgment of High Court confirming order of trial court-Quashed and set aside.
