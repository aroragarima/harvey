Celina Coelho Pereira and Others v Ulhas Mahabaleshwar Kholkar and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 October 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1477; (2010) 1 SCC 217; AIR 2010 SC 603; 2009 (6) Bom.C.R. 903; JT 2009 (13) SC 602; 2009 (8) MLJ(SC) 675; 2009 (2) RCR(Rent) 456; 2009(13) SCALE 487; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 558

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Change Of User, Rent Control Authorities, Goa, Daman & Diu Buildings (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1968, Goa, Daman and Diu the Mamlatdars Court Act, 1966

Summary: Rent Control - Goa, Daman & Diu Buildings (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1968 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 227 - Sub-letting - Eviction - Landlord filed application for eviction of tenant on grounds of subletting and change of user - Additional Rent Controller held that ground of sub-letting was established and directed tenant to vacate subject premises - Eviction order upheld by Administrative Tribunal - Petition filed u/art. 227 of Constitution allowed by HC - Whether HC justified in interfering with concurrent orders of eviction passed by Additional Rent Controller and Administrative Tribunal? - Held, HC acted too technically in construing pleadings of case overlooking fact that plea of sub-letting was set up by landlord in plaint and there was full and critical examination of evidence by Additional Rent Controller as well as Administrative Tribunal - Findings recorded by Administrative Tribunal as well as Additional Rent Controller were based on consideration of evidence on record - HC was not justified in interfering with concurrent orders of eviction based on ground of sub-letting in exercise of its power u/art. 227 of Constitution - Appeal allowed.
