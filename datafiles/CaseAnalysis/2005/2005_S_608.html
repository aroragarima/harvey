Vishnu @ Undrya v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

24 November 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 935; (2006) 1 SCC 283; AIR 2006 SC 508; 2006 CRLJ 299; 2006 CRLJ 303; 2006 (1) JCC 78; JT 2005 (10) SC 174; 2006 (1) RCR(Criminal) 201; 2005(9) SCALE 510; 2006 (1) SCJ 299; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 474

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Women & Children - Criminal - Bombay Children Act, s. 57 - IPC, 1860, ss. 366, 375, 376 - CrPC, 1973, s. 313 - Appellant was convicted by Trial Court and sentenced to two years RI on each count - HC enhanced sentence to 5 years RI - Appeal against conviction - Held, no infirmity in well-merited concurrent findings recorded by two courts below - Evidence of facts shall prevail over expert opinion based on ossification test - Conviction confirmed - Appeal dismissed.
