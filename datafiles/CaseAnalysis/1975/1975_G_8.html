Gandhi Faizeam College Shahajahanpur v University Of Agra And Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 116; (1975) 2 SCC 283; AIR 1975 SC 1821; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 810

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education

Summary: Education - Constitution - University of Agra, Statute 14A - Gujarat University Act, 1949 - Kerala University Act, 1969 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 30 - Infringement of right - University statute requiring a managing committee for minority educational institution - Held, provision is valid as it intends to secure better administration of an institution - Art. 30 does not invalidate provisions of a regulatory character which are intended to secure better administration of an institution - Appeal dismissed.
