Dhanyalakshmi Rice Mills and Others v Commissioner of Civil Supplies and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 405; (1976) 4 SCC 723; AIR 1976 SC 2243; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 387; 1976 UJ 266

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Socio-Economic

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, ss. 21 and 72 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Constitution, arts. 133 and 226 - Export of rice - Amount paid by licence to Govt. - Towards administrative surcharges to obtain permits - Claim for refund - Ground, it was paid under mistake - High Court refusing mandamus - Interference by Supreme Court - Appeals dismissed.
