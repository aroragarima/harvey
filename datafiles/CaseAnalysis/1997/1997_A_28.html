Vishweshwaraiah Iron & Steel Ltd. v Abdul Gani & Ors

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 684; (1997) 8 SCC 713; AIR 1998 SC 185; 1997 (77) FLR 871; JT 1997 (9) SC 94; 1998 (1) LLJ 929; 1997(7) SCALE 11; 1998 (1) SLR 13; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 119; 1997 (9) Supreme 383

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10, 10 A, and 33 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Termination without domestic enquiry - Management lead evidence before Court or Tribunal - Relation back of order to date of management order - Rule regarding proceedings u/s. 33 rendered in precedent - Whether applies to proceedings u/ss. 10 and 10 A of Act? - Question referred to larger Bench.
