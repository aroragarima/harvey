Marine Container Services South Private Limited v Go Go Garments

Supreme Court of India

23 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 113; (1998) 3 SCC 247; AIR 1999 SC 80; 1998 (33) ALR 590; 1998 (3) AWC 1962; 1998 (6) CTJ 481; JT 1998 (4) SC 322; 1998 (2) MLJ 65; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 65; 1998 (8) Supreme 544

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Indirect Tax

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 2(c) - Contract Act, 1872, s. 230 - Protection to agent/consumer - Complaint against deficient services of contractor - Complaint under Consumer Protection Act - Held, Contract Act applicable - Agent has protection available under Act and his defence based thereon cannot be brushed aside.
