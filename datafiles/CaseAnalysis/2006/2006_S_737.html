Syed Ibrahim v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

27 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 348; (2006) 10 SCC 601; (2007) 1 SCC (Cr) 34; AIR 2006 SC 2908; 2006 CRLJ 4087; 2006 (3) JCC 1308; JT 2006 (6) SC 597; 2006 (3) RCR(Criminal) 864; 2006(7) SCALE 399; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 105

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - IPC, 1860, s. 302 - CrPC, 1973, s. 16 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 134 - Trial court had found appellant guilty of murdering his wife and was sentenced to undergo imprisonment for life - HC confirmed conviction and sentence - Appeal against - Held, HC was not right in lightly brushing aside apparent inconsistencies and discrepancies by making a general observation that PW1 is an illiterate person - Appeal allowed.
