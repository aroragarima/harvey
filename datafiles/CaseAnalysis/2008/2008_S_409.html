D. Krishnan and Another v Special Officer, Vellore Coop. S.M. and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

H.S. Bedi, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 968; (2008) 7 SCC 22; AIR 2009 SC 395; 2008 (6) AWC 6165; 2008 (3) CLR 111; 2008 (106) CutLT 376; 2008 (3) ESC 492; 2008 (118) FLR 1196; JT 2008 (7) SC 401; 2008 (3) LLN 498; 2008(9) SCALE 399; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 1239; 2008 (6) SLR 786

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Whether disputed claim could come within the purview of s.33C(2) of ID Act, 1947? - Held, proceedings u/s. 33C(2) are in the nature of execution proceedings, and such proceedings presuppose some adjudication leading to the determination of a right, which has to be enforced - In the absence of any supporting oral evidence by the workmen which would also result in their cross-examination, a mere reliance on the documents filed by them is insufficient for determining the factual basis of the issues involved, in proceedings u/s. 33-C(2) of the Act - Appeal dismissed.
