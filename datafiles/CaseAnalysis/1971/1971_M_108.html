Mst. Karmi v Amru and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 772; (1972) 4 SCC 86; AIR 1971 SC 745; 1971 UJ 140

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Constitution, art. 136 - Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 14(2) (A) - Concurrent finding as to genuineness of will - Questioned in Special leave petition - (B) Hindu Law - Widow succeeded to properties of her deceased husband through will - Claim her rights - Scope - Held, cannot be questioned in special leave appeal - Cannot claim any rights in the properties other than those conferred by the will - Appeal Dismissed.
