Dr Surinder Singh Jamwal and Another v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2213; (1996) 9 SCC 619; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1296; AIR 1996 SC 2775; AIR 1996 SCW 3481; 1996 (2) CLR 956; 1996 (74) FLR 2080; JT 1996 (6) SC 725; 1996 LabIC 2444; 1996 (2) LLJ 795; 1996(5) SCALE 528; 1996 (5) SLR 170; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 663; 1996 (6) Supreme 141; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2755

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Claim for regularization by ad hoc employees (appointed dehors service rules) after 13 years of service - Not entitled but can continue till and apply for selection according to Rules subject to age barred - Held, Court directed State to relax necessary age qualification - Appeal disposed of.
