Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and Adr - Arbitration Act, 1940, s.30 - Setting aside of arbitration award - Only when error of law apparent on face of record - Mistake of fact - Held, is not amenable to correction - However workers' objections are disposed of by stating that there are sufficient provisions in the award to meet to he claim of the gratuity of the workers and they should have the right to be met out of the award -
