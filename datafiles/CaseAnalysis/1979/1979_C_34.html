Sassoon J. David and Company Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 120; (1979) 3 SCC 524; AIR 1979 SC 1441; 1979 (10) CTR 383; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 878; 1979 TAXLR 1078; [1979] 1 TAXMAN 485; 1979 UJ 566

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Annuity

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - Tests for determination of business expenditure - Remuneration - 'Wholly and exclusively used' - Payment of retrenchment compensation by company to all employees on sale of all its shares - Payment of annuity to ex-director - If allowed u/s. 10(2)(xv) - Held, expenditure held 'business expenditure' and allowable u/s. 10(2)(xv) - Appeals allowed.
