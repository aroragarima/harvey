C. Kasturi and Others v Secretary, Regional Transport Authority and Another

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3181; (1996) 8 SCC 314; 1996 (1) ACC 551; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 241; AIR 1996 SC 1664; JT 1996 (3) SC 458; 1996(2) SCALE 900; 1996 (2) SCJ 170; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 1085

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Existing Stage Carriage Permits

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 68D and 68I - Town service not extendible up to 8 Kms beyond municipal limits on notified route - Scope of r. 282(2) (ii) of AP Motor Vehicles Rules, 1964 - Held, cannot be read into notified scheme - Appeal dismissed.
