Ishwarlal Premchand Shah and Others Etc v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3621; (1996) 4 SCC 174; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 657; AIR 1996 SC 1616; 1996 (3) CCC 62; 1996 (2) CLT 230; JT 1996 (4) SC 208; 1996 (1) RentLR 508; 1996(3) SCALE 495; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 510; 1996 (5) Supreme 234

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.11(2), 23(1A) and 55 - Agreement between owners and body for which land is to be acquired - (A) In the absence of terms in agreement, If owners are entitled to payment of additional compensation, solatium or interest - (B) Need for it being in prescribed Form No. 14 as specified by the Rules framed u/s. 55 - Held, not entitled - Agreement need not be in prescribed Form - Appeal dismissed.
