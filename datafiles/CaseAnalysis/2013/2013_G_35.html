Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988-Sections 166 and 168-Compensation-Death case-Enhancement-Claimants Parents-Accident occurred in year 2003-Deceased 19 years old and was student of Engineering course-Tribunal awarded total compensation of R 1,92,000 to appellants-Meagre enhancement of R 8,000 granted by Division Bench of High Court-Whether appellants entitled to higher compensation-Held, âyesâ-End of justice will be served by awarding lump sum compensation of R 7,00,000 to appellants-Appellants entitled to compensation of R 7,00,000 with interest @ 6% p.a. on enhanced amount with effect from date of filing petition under Section 166-Insurance company (respondent No. 3) directed to pay amount of enhanced compensation and interest to appellants-Open to insurance company to recover from owner and driver (respondent Nos. 1 and 2) their respective shares of compensation-Impugned judgment of High Court -Modified.
