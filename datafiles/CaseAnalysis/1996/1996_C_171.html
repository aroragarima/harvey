Decorative Laminates (India) Private Limited v Collector of Central, Excise, Bangalore

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2250; (1996) 10 SCC 46; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 173; AIR 1996 SC 2588; 1996 (66) ECR 221; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 186; JT 1996 (7) SC 627; 1996 (15) RLT 679; 1996(5) SCALE 582; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 209; 1996 (6) Supreme 69

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1986, Sch., item 44 - Slip - proof commercial plywood - Made after carrying out processing work on commercial plywood - (A) If new commercial product - (B) Liability to excise duty - Whether it can be denied on ground that duty was paid for plywood before its processing? - Held, (A) Is new commercial product acquiring different identity - (B)Cannot be denied on this ground - Appeal dismissed.
