Dharmadeepti, Alwaye, Kerala v The Commissioner Of Income Tax, Kerala

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Y.V. Chandrachud, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 183; (1978) SCC (Tax) 193; (1978) 3 SCC 499; AIR 1978 SC 1417; 1978 (7) CTR 120; [1988] 114 ITR 454; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 1038; 1978 TAXLR 1000; 1978 UJ 531

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(15) and 11 - Income from the kurie - Derived from property held under trust for charitable purpose - Association constituted for promotion of charity and education - Whether Income of Association from the kurie is exempt from tax - Income held to be exempt - Appeal Allowed.
