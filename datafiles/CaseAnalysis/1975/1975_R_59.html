Rohtas Industries Limited and Another v Rohtas Industries Staff Union and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Y.V. Chandrachud, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 307; (1976) 2 SCC 82; (1976) SCC (L&S) 200; AIR 1976 SC 425; 1976 (32) FLR 50; 1976 LabIC 303; 1976 (1) LLJ 274; 1976 (1) LLN 165; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 12

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Trade Union Act, 1926

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(k), 10A, 24 and 26 - Illegal strike by workmen - Award granting compensation to employer for loss of business through illegal strike - Held, award is illegal - Such compensation is not a dispute - Not consistent with purpose of Act.
