Assistant Collector Ofcustoms (Preventive) Bombay v Babu Miya Sheikh Imam And Ors. Etc

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 119; (1983) 3 SCC 447; (1983) SCC (Cr) 662; AIR 1983 SC 974; [1984] 56 Comp Cas 365; 1983 (2) CompLJ 340; 1983 (2) Crimes 281; 1983 CRLJ 1494; 1983 CrLR(SC) 434; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1666; 1983(2) SCALE 33; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 500; 1983 TAXLR 2815

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 135(1)(a)(ii) - Export Trade Control Order , 1968 - Prohibition on export of silver without licence - Evasion or attempt of evasion - Held, it amounts to offence u/s. 135 (1) (a) (ii) although no duty is chargeable on export of silver - Appeal Allowed.
