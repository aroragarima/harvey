Haryana State Adhyapak Sangh And Ors v State Of Haryana & Ors

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 721; (1990) Supp SCC 306; (1991) SCC (L&S) 307; AIR 1990 SC 968; 1991 (15) ATC 873; 1990 (60) FLR 503; JT 1990 (1) SC 293; 1990 LabIC 698; 1990(1) SCALE 247; 1990 (4) SLR 226; 1990 (1) UJ 355; 1990 (3) UPLBEC 1563

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Reimbursement, Parity

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Pay scale and allowances - Directions by Supreme Court in certain case to remove disparity in emoluments of teachers employed in various Govt. aided private schools and those in Govt. schools - Implementation - Held, further directions issued by Supreme Court to implement directions - Order accordingly.
