03 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963 , s.5 - Condonation of delay - Sufficient cause - Application for setting aside ex parte decree - Delay of 883 days in filing - Due to failure of advocate to inform appellant as well as his failure to take action - Found to be satisfactory by trial Court - Held, order of HC in revision setting aside same is not proper - Court should compensate opposite party in such cases - Appellant had secured compensation from delinquent advocate - Appeal disposed of.
