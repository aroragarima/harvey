State (Delhi Administration) v Pali Ram

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 90; (1979) 2 SCC 158; (1979) SCC (Cr) 389; AIR 1979 SC 14; 1979 CRLJ 17; 1979 MLJ 250; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 931

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Power Of The Court, Sample Writing, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872., s. 73, second para and s. 45 - Power of court to direct accused to give his specimen handwriting - Comparision by expert - Permissibility - Held, direction can be given to enabling it to be compared by handwriting expert - Appeal Allowed.
