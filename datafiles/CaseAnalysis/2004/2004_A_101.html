Andhra Bank v B. Satyanarayana and others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 122; (2004) SCC (L&S) 433; (2004) 2 SCC 657; AIR 2004 SC 4007; 2004 (3) ESC 387; 2004 (100) FLR 1011; JT 2004 (2) SC 312; 2004 (2) LLJ 5; 2004 (2) LLN 43; 2004(2) SCALE 446; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 304; 2004 (2) SLR 8; 2004 (2) SLT 671; 2004 (2) Supreme 1; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 2109

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Andhra Bank (Officers') Service Regulations, 1982

Summary: [A.] Service - Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1980, ss. 12(2), 13, 14, 19, 8 - Atomic Energy Act, 1962 , ss. 18, 3 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Andhra Bank (Officers') Service Regulations, 1982, reg. 17(1) - Banking - Service regulations - Andhra Bank (officers') Service Regulation 1982, Regulation 17(1) does not confer any unguided, uncanalised and arbitrary power - Issued in conformity with guidelines issued by Central Govt. - HC erred in its judgment - Appeal allowed.
