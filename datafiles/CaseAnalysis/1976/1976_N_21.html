Nawn Estates (P) Ltd v C.I.T., West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 362; (1977) 1 SCC 7; AIR 1977 SC 153; 1977 CTR 19; [1977] 106 ITR 45; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 798

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Distributable Income

Summary: Finance Act, 1968 - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 23A and expln. 2(i) - Intepretation - Taxing statutes - Income Tax Act - Reference to English authorities - The meaning of expression 'holding of investments' u/s. 23A and expln. 2(i) of 1922 Act - Held, the expression has to be understood as the company whose primary or principal income is admittedly derived from house properties which it leases out to tenants and capital gains as well - Appeal dismissed.
