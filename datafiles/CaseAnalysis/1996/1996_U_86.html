Akhil Bhartiya Soshit Karamchari Sangh, Through Its Secretary and Another v Union of India Through Its Secretary, Ministry of Railway and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2554; (1996) 6 SCC 65; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1346; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 434; AIR 1996 SC 3534; 1996 (74) FLR 2365; JT 1996 (8) SC 274; 1996 LabIC 2527; 1996 (2) LLN 1074; 1996(6) SCALE 629; 1996 (3) SCJ 314; 1996 (5) SLR 687; [1996] Supp6 S.C.R. 165; 1996 (7) Supreme 566; 1997 (1) UJ 103; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 127

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Seniority - Accelerated promotion of reserved category candidate - Confers on him seniority in promotion cadre - Held, further Promotion of reserved candidate against vacant post in general category on basis of his normal category prior to specified Judgment of Supreme Court - No interference - Petition fails.
