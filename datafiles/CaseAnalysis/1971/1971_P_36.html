Prof. Khaidem Ibocha Singh v State of Manipur

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 583; (1972) 2 SCC 576; (1972) SCC (Cr) 894; AIR 1972 SC 438; 1972 (8) DLT 99; 1972 (74) PLR 338; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 1022

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Orissa Preventive Detention Act, 1970, ss. 3(1) and 3(2) - Detention order u/s. 3(1)(2) of 1970 Act - Earliest opportunity of making a representation against - Unexplained delay of 17 days in passing orders on representations - Effect - Held, is by itself a sufficient ground for holding that orders of their detention are illegal - Order accordingly.
