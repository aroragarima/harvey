Ballarpur Industries Limited v Collector of Customs (Appeals), Customs House

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, Mr.Justice R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 738; (1995) Supp1 SCC 446; 1995 (75) E.L.T. 6; JT 1995 (1) SC 164; 1994(5) SCALE 263; [1994] Supp6 S.C.R. 615

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Warrant

Summary: Customs - Indiirect Tax - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, Item Nos. 68.01/16(1) and 84.31 - Granite Press Roll - Whether the article 'Granite Press Roll' could be classified under Import Tariff Item No. 68.01/16(1) of the Ist Schedule to the Act as it stood prior to its amendment on 28-2-1986, as against its classification under Import Tariff Item No. 84.31 of the Ist Schedule to the Act.

Held, since 'Granite Press Roll' is an imported article, which is classified as Tariff item 84.31 of the 1st Schedule to the Act as it stood prior to its amendment on 28.2. 1986, the import duty payable thereon is only 40% as provided thereunder. Appeal allowed.
