Brijendra Singh v State of Madhya Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 50; (2008) 13 SCC 161; AIR 2008 SC 1056; 2008 (2) ALD(SC) 105; 2008(2) ALL MR 294; 2008 (70) ALR 465; 2008 (5) AWC 4357; 2008 (5) Bom.C.R. 362; 2008 (1) CTC 856; 2008 (2) JhrCR(SC) 91; JT 2008 (1) SC 443; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 1083; 2008 (2) RCR(Civil) 277; 2008 (3) RLW 2517; 2008(1) SCALE 372; [2008] 1 S.C.R. 593; 2008 (2) WLN(SC) 12

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: M.P. Ceiling On Agricultural Holdings Act, 1960

Summary: M.P. Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings Act, 1960; Hindu Adoption and Maintenance Act, 1956 - Suit seeking a declaration that appellant is an adopted son - Held, proviso to s. 7 imposes a restriction in the right of male Hindu to take in adoption - As per the proviso to s. 7 the wife's consent must be obtained prior to adoption and cannot be subsequent to the act of adoption - Proviso lays down consent as a condition precedent to an adoption which is mandatory and adoption without wife's consent would be void - Appeal dismissed.
