Mohan Lal (Deceased) Throughhis Lrs. Kachru and v Mirza Abdul Gaffar & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1899; (1996) 1 SCC 639; AIR 1996 SC 910; 1996 (2) CalLT(SC) 14; 1996 (1) CLT 210; JT 1995 (9) SC 436; 1996(1) SCALE 5; 1996 (1) SCJ 290; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 638

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s.53-A; Limitation Act, 1963, Arts.64 and 65; Specific Relief Act, 1963, s.16(C) - Part performance of contract - Willingness of transferee - Agreement of sale - Party in possession of suit land pursuant to said agreement - Dismissal of suit for - Order becoming final - Suit for possession by subsequent purchaser - Held, plea of title by adverse possession not available to party - Neither pleaded nor proved his willingness to perform his part of contract - Not entitled to retain possession.
