Yogiraj Charity Trust v Commissioner of Income Tax, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 398; (1976) 3 SCC 378; AIR 1976 SC 1836; 1976 CTR 211; [1976] 103 ITR 777; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 947; 1976 UJ 436

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 4(3)(i) - Trust - Objects - Some objects charitable and some non-charitable - Claim for exemption - Test - Held, test is that if one of the objects of the trust deed is not of a religious or charitable nature and the trust deed confers full discretion on the trustees to spend the trust funds for an object other than of a religious or charitable nature, exemption u/s. 4 (3) (i) not available - Appeals dismissed.
