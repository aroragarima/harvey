Narasingh Charan Mohanty v Surendra Mohanty

Supreme Court of India

12 October 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.N. Dwivedi, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 144; (1974) 3 SCC 680; AIR 1974 SC 47; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 39

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Election

Keywords: Dismissed, Notification, Any Other Person, Religious Symbol

Summary: Criminal - Elections - Corrupt Practice - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 100 and 123 - Respondent was editor in an Oriya Newspaper named 'Kalinga' - Certain statements were made in his newspaper regarding personal character of contestant - Certain imputations were also made against personal character and conduct of candidate - Whether statement made regarding personal character and conduct of contestant, amounted to corrupt practice u/s. 123? - Held, no - Statements were made regarding public conduct of deceased and not his personal character - No intention to prejudice prospects of said candidate - Appeal dismissed.
