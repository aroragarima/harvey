(1) Charan Dass (Dead) By L.Rs.; (2) Kishan Singh; v Himachal Pradesh Housing and Urban Development

Supreme Court of India

07 September 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

D.K. Jain, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1096; (2010) 13 SCC 398; 2009 (4) AWC 3625; 2009 (4) RCR(Civil) 740; 2009(12) SCALE 293; [2009] 14 S.C.R. 163

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Valuation Of Land

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1984 - Land acquisition - Compensation - Enhancement of - 3rd respondent determined market value of appellants' acquired land - Appellants sought enhancement by filing reference to District Court - District Court enhanced compensation - 2nd respondent filed appeal before HC - HC allowed appeal and reduced 40% from award amount on account of development charges - Legality - Held, HC re-evaluated entire evidence - Sale deeds of adjacent lands relied upon by HC, were best evidence to ascertain market value - Location and date of such sales were considered by HC - No legal or factual infirmity in approach of HC - However, deduction of 30% towards development charges from market value arrived at by HC, would be met ends of justice - Further, appellants were entitled to a solatium of 30% and interest as per Act - Appeal partly allowed.
