Commissioner Of Gift Tax Gujarat v Chhotalal Mohanlal

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28745; (1987) SCC (Tax) 217; (1987) 2 SCC 612; AIR 1987 SC 1412; 1987 (2) BankCLR 299; 1987 (61) CTR 263; [1987] 166 ITR 124; JT 1987 (3) SC 8; 1987(1) SCALE 823; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1042; 1987 TAXLR 776; [1987] 31 TAXMAN 512; 1987 TLR 776; 1987 (2) UJ 471

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Trade

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Gift Tax Act, 1958, s. 2(xii) - Gift - Minors admitted to a fixed share in benefit of partnership - Right to money value of goodwill also transfered thereby - Whether it amounts to gift of goodwill of the firm - Held, goodwill of firm being property, admission of minors to benefit as aforesaid, constitutes gift - Appeal allowed.
