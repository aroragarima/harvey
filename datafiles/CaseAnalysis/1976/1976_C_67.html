Corporation of Calcutta v Md. Omer Ali and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 490; (1976) 4 SCC 527; (1977) SCC (Cr) 6; AIR 1977 SC 912; 1977 CRLJ 778; 1977 CRLR 332; 1978 UJ 54

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Summary: Prevention Of Food Adulteration Act, 1954 - Compliane of mandatory requirement of s. 20(1) - Determination - Complaint u/s. 16(1)(a)(i) r/ w s. 7 by Food Inspector - Filed with written consent of Health Officer ( authorised by Corporation ) - Held, no breach of requirement - Appeal Allowed.
