Collector Of Central Excise, Vadodara v Dhiren Chemical Industries

Supreme Court of India

21 February 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1351; (2002) 2 SCC 127; AIR 2002 SC 453; 2002 (172) CTR 670; 2002 (79) ECC 1; 2002 (101) ECR 1; 2002 (143) E.L.T. 19; [2002] 254 ITR 554; 2002 (51) RLT 260; 2002 (126) STC 122

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Departmental clarification - CBEC circulars binding on Revenue even if placing different interpretation then given by Supreme Court - Followed, Dhiren Chemical Industries [2001 Indlaw SC 21373] - Regardless of the interpretation that the court have placed on the phrase 'on which the appropriate amount of duty has already been paid' - Appeal dismissed
