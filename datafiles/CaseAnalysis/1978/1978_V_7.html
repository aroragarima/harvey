State of Kerala v Vijaya Stores

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 214; (1978) 4 SCC 41; AIR 1979 SC 355; [1979] 116 ITR 15; 1978 KLT 802; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 538; 1978 (42) STC 418; 1978 UJ 728

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Kerala General Sales Tax Act,1963, s. 39(4) - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 33(4) - Income Tax and Sales Tax - Provision u/s. 39(4) of 1963 Act , pari materia with s. 33(4) of the Income Tax Act - Enhancement of assessment - Whether can be made in absence of appeal or cross-objection by Revenue - Held, cannot be made in absence of appeal or cross-objection - Appeal Dismissed.
