Sevi and Another, Koodakkal Karian and Others v State of Tamil Nadu and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 36; (1981) Supp SCC 43; (1981) SCC (Cr) 679; AIR 1981 SC 1230; 1981 CRLJ 736; 1981 CRLR 222; 1982 (1) MLJ(SC) 7

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 154 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Non production of original FIR and General diary by police - Even on direction of Court - Effect of - Murder trial - Partition eye witnesses - Witnesses themselves admitting of being assaulted simultaneously - Explaining minute of incidents on each victim also - Credibility - Held, inference of suppression of FIR - Partisan eye witnesses cannot be accepted when impartial investigation found to be tainted.
