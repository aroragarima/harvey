Commissioner of Income Tax v Mysore Minerals Limited

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 792; (2001) 9 SCC 381; 2000 (162) CTR 485; [2001] 247 ITR 301; JT 2000 (10) SC 557; 2000 (6) SLT 816A; [2001] 115 TAXMAN 685

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 256(2) - Whether investment allowance allowable on machinery employed in process of extraction of granite from quarry, cutting same into various sizes and polishing them? - High Court declined to call for a reference in view of Mysore Minerals judgment [1992 Indlaw KAR 130] - Held, a question of law arises that requires consideration of HC - Tribunal directed to refer to HC - Appeal allowed.
