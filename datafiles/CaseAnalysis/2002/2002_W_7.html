State of West Bengal and Another v West Bengal Government Pensioners Associations and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 3; (2002) 2 SCC 179; (2002) SCC (L&S) 234; AIR 2002 SC 538; AIR 2002 SCW 112; 2002 (94) FLR 1078; JT 2002 (Supp1) SC 73; 2002 (35) LabIC 523; 2002 (1) LLJ 1081; 2002 (1) LLN 628; 2002(1) SCALE 1; 2002 (1) SCJ 184; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 1; 2002 (1) SLT 73; 2002 (1) Supreme 13

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Computation Of Pension, West Bengal Services (Revision of Pay and Allowances) Rules, 1990, West Bengal Services (Death-cum-Retirement) Rules 1971

Summary: Parity in the amount of pension cannot be granted if, the difference between pre-1986 pensioners and the post-1986 pensioners is only on account of the revision of pay scales and not on account of failure of State Government to equitably apply the liberalised pension scheme formula.
