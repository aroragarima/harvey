Mahadeo Prasad Bais (Dead) v Income-Tax Officer ’A’ Ward, Gorakhpur And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, N.D. Ojha, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 254; (1991) 4 SCC 560; AIR 1991 SC 2278; 1991 (98) CTR 230; [1991] 192 ITR 402; JT 1991 (6) SC 150; 1991(2) SCALE 541; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 9; [1992] 60 TAXMAN 388; 1991 (2) TLR 985

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Partial Partition

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 148, 149, 150(1) and 297(2) - Re-assessment - Proceedings relating to assessment years covered by 1922, Income Tax Act Re-assessment sought to give effect to order of High Court passed under 1922 Act -Assessment proceedings initiated after commencement of 1961 Act - Limitation -

Held, saved from limitation by virtue of s. 150(1) - Appeal dismissed.
