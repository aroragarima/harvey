Â A. Thangal Kunju Musaliar v M. Venkatachalam Potti, Authorised Official and Income Tax Officer, and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Vivian Bose, B. Jagannadhadas

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 45; AIR 1956 SC 246; [1956] 29 ITR 349; 1956 SCJ 323; [1955] 2 S.C.R. 1196

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Travancore Act, 1124, ss. 3,5(1),47(1) - Notification - Assessment - Discriminatory - Investigation - Petitioner was Managing Director of 'A' and had been assessed to income-tax for years 1942-43 and final orders in his assessment - U/s.3 of 1124 Act, an Investigation Commission was to be constituted to investigate in accordance with provisions of Act cases referred to it u/s.5 of Act and report to Govt. - Govt. issued orders referring cases of petitioner for years 1942 and 1943 for investigation by Income-tax Investigation Commission - Hence instant appeal - Whether s.47 of 1121 Act could assumed to be discriminatory and violative of fundamental right guaranteed u/art. 14 of Constitution - Held, s.47(1) of 1121 Act, was directed only against those persons concerning whom definite information came into possession of Income tax Officer and in consequence of which Income Tax Officer discovered that income of those persons had escaped or been under-assessed or assessed at too low a rate or had been subject of excessive relief - Action to be taken under it was not confined to escapement from assessment of income made during war period - S.5(1) of 1124 Act, had to be read for that purpose in juxtaposition with s.47 of 1121 Act, could not be assumed to be discriminatory and violative of fundamental right guaranteed u/art.14 of Constitution - Proceedings which took place in course of investigation by Commission were valid and so were proceedings during course of investigation which took place after inauguration of Constitution under which petitioner acquired guarantee of fundamental right u/art.14 of Constitution - Appeal disposed of.
