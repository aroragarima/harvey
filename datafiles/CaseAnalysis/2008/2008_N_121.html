Speech and Software Technologies (India) Private v Neos Interactive Ltd

Supreme Court of India

05 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Panchal

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1917; (2009) 1 SCC 475; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 814; 2009 (75) ALR 162; 2009 (2) AWC 1375; 2009 (1) CompLJ 21; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 153; JT 2009 (1) SC 157; 2009 (3) MPLJ 1; 2009 (1) RCR(Civil) 605; 2008(16) SCALE 102; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 62

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11(6) - Held, exercise of power under Section 11(6) of the Act is judicial power - Existence of arbitration agreement is a condition precedent before exercise of powers under s. 11(6) - Preliminary matters to be considered by the court are (1) existence of arbitration agreement, (2) territorial jurisdiction, (3) whether there are live issues to be referred to the arbitrator, and (4) whether application is filed within the period of limitation prescribed by the law - Disputes are existing between the parties relating to the execution of the Services Agreement - Application allowed.
