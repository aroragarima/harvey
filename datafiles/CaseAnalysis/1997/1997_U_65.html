Union of India and Another v Food Specialities Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2973; (1998) 9 SCC 423; 1998 (97) E.L.T. 402; JT 1998 (8) SC 30; 1998 (24) RLT 685

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Customs - Indirect Tax - If the decision in the assessee's favour has become final, can the question of the consequential order about quantum made by the Tribunal in favour of the assessee be challenged alone, by the department? - Held, in view of the statement made by the learned counsel for the assessee-respondent, and it not having been shown to the court that the department has challenged the tribunal's decision on the question of classification reported in Dalmia Industries case this appeal must fails.
