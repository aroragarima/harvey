Vankamamidi Venkata Subba Rao v Chatlapalli Seetharamaratharanganayakamma

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1769; (1997) 5 SCC 460; AIR 1997 SC 3082; 1997 (3) CLT 346; JT 1997 (4) SC 713; 1997(3) SCALE 664; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 530; 1997 (5) Supreme 94

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Succession & Inheritance, Marriage & Divorce

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 14 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Widow obtained properties from husband - Prior to Act - As maintenance - Subsequently acknowledged it as a limited right with provision that property would revert to her son - (A) Applicability of s. 14 of Act - (B) CPC, 1908 - Jurisdiction of Court in suits of civil nature covered under land reform laws - Held, in circumstances provisions u/s. 14(2) and not u/s. 14(1) would be attracted and her right would not be enlarged into absolute estate - Jurisdiction stands excluded, court cannot unsettle patta which has become final in decree - Appeal allowed.
