Management Of Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation,Ban v M. Boraiah and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 59; (1984) SCC (L&S) 117; (1984) 1 SCC 244; AIR 1983 SC 1320; 1984 (48) FLR 89; 1984 (1) LLJ 110; 1984 (1) LLN 230; 1983(2) SCALE 652; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 783; 1984 (1) SLJ 142; 1984 UJ 27

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Age Of Superannuation, Closure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo) and 25F - Industrial Dispute - Retrenchment - Discharge of probationer - If requirements of s. 25F have to be complied with - Held, it amounts to retrenchment and s. 25F to be complied with - Appeal dismissed.
