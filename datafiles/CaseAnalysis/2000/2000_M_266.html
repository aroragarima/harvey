State of Madhya Pradesh v Mohanlal Soni

Supreme Court of India

19 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 364; (2000) 6 SCC 338; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1110; AIR 2000 SC 2583; AIR 2000 SCW 2674; 2000 (3) CCR 86; 2000 (3) Crimes 105; 2000 CRLJ 3504; 2000 (2) JCC 676; JT 2000 (8) SC 333; 2000(3) M.P.H.T. 467; 2000 (3) RCR(Criminal) 452; 2000(5) SCALE 197; 2000 (5) SLT 554; 2000 (5) Supreme 139

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Socio-Economic

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, s. 228 - Framing of charge - Documents made available by accused during investigation - Trial Court not considering said documents in spite of directions of High Court - Held, such documents should also be considered - Order framing charge against accused - Liable to be set aside.
