Mrs. Y. Theclamma v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28233; (1987) 2 SCC 516; AIR 1987 SC 1210; 1987 (54) FLR 618; JT 1987 (2) SC 165; 1987 LabIC 907; 1987(1) SCALE 781; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 974; 1987 (3) SLJ 20; 1987 (2) SLR 253

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Kerala University Act, 1957, Kerala Education Bill, 1957

Summary: Service - Delhi School Education Act, 1973, s. 8(4) - Right of minorities - Petitioner a teacher in a minority school - Suspended - Institution did not consult education officer before taking permission - Held, that before suspending a teacher it is incumbent on management of a minority institution to obtain prior permission of education officer - Petition dismissed.
