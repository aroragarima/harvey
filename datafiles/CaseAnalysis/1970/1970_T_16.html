Â State of Bihar and Another v Tata Engineering and Locomotive Company, Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 50; (1970) 3 SCC 697; AIR 1971 SC 477; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 849; 1971 (27) STC 127

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Constitution, art. 286(2) - Trade - When sale in course of inter-state trade is effected - When contract of sale requiring buyer to remove goods from State in which goods are purchased to another State - Held, goods so removed must be considered as sale in course of inter-state trade - Appeal Dismissed.
