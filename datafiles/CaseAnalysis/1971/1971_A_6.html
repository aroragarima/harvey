Railway Board v A. Pitchumani

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 318; (1972) 4 SCC 608; AIR 1972 SC 508; 1972 LabIC 305; 1972 (1) LLJ 112; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 187; 1972 SLR 165

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Service - Railway Establishment Code - Bombay Prohibition Act, 1949 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Civil Services - Constitutionality of r. 2046, cl. (b), Note of Railway Code as substituted on 23-12-1967 - Held, part of Note to cl. (b) as substituted on 23-12-1967 namely, 'if rules of company or State had a provision similar to cl. (b) above' is violative of art. 14 of Constitution - And must be struck down - Appeal dismissed.
