Janba (Dead) Through Lrs v Gopikabai Smt

Supreme Court of India

06 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 340; (2000) 4 SCC 1; AIR 2000 SC 1771; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 826; JT 2000 (4) SC 280; 2000(3) SCALE 111; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1035; 2000 (4) SLT 67

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands, (Vidarbha Region) Act, 1958, ss. 50(1) and 41(2) - Right of tenant to purchase land - Provision favours to exercise power within one year from date of tenancy - Postponement for indefinite period after cessation of disability of widow landlady - Validity - Held, to 'such purchase' ss. 41 to 44 mutatis mutandis shall apply - Period of one year cannot be changed.
