Shankar Finance and Investments v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 June 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2178; (2008) 8 SCC 536; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 558; AIR 2009 SC 422; 2009(4) ALL MR 480; 2008 (4) BC 523; 2008 (3) JCC (NI) 345; 2008(10) SCALE 654; [2008] 10 S.C.R. 905

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Banking and Finance - Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 200, 482 - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, ss.138, 142 - Whether a complaint u/s. 138 of Act signed by a Attorney holder maintainable? - Held, payee of cheque is the petitioner - Complaint is filed by Attorney Holder - Therefore, it is evident that complaint is in name of and on behalf of payee - Once complaint is in name of 'payee' and is in writing, requirements of s. 142 are fulfilled - Hence, complaint u/s. 138 of Act signed by Attorney holder is maintainable - Appeal allowed.
