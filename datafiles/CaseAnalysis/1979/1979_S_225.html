J. A. Naidu, Etc. v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 1; (1980) SCC (Cr) 192; (1980) 1 SCC 206; AIR 1979 SC 1537; 1979 CRLJ 962; 1979 CRLR 730; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1611

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 120 B, 161, 165 A and 218 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 5(1)(a) r/ w 5(2) - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 135 and 136 - Conviction - Entitlement to aquittal, when documentary and oral evidence found to be not reliable - Accused, held, entitled to acquittal - Appeal allowed.
