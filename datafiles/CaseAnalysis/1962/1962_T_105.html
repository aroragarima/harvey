Messrs L. H. Sugar Factories and Oil Mills Private Limited v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 146; AIR 1967 SC 161; 1962 (5) FLR 292; 1963 (1) LLJ 677; [1963] 3 S.C.R. 571

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure, Seasonal

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Entitlement of closure holidays - Whether crushing season deemed to have been ended when entire machinery was at a standstill or when actual crushing of sugar cane stopped? - Crushing season ended on date on which crushing operations in factory came to an end and not on date on which manufacturing processes in factory came to an end - Held, seasonal workers who were borne on muster roll would be entitled to three days closure holidays - Appeal dismissed.
