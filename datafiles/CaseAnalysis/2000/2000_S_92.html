State of Haryana and Others v Santra Smt

Supreme Court of India

24 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 288; (2000) 5 SCC 182; AIR 2000 SC 1888; AIR 2000 SCW 1491; 2000 (40) ALR 470; 2000 (3) AWC 1817; 2000 (4) Bom.C.R. 209; 2000 (2) CLT 152; 2000 (1) CPJ 53; 2000 (1) CPJ(Sc) 53; 2000 (3) G.L.R. 2309; JT 2000 (5) SC 34; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 98; 2000(3) M.P.H.T. 150; 2000 (125) PLR 790; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 739; 2000(3) SCALE 417; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 195; 2000 (4) SLT 93; 2000 (3) Supreme 520

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Family & Personal; Tort

Keywords: Breach Of Duty, Degree Of Care

Summary: Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, ss. 20 and 23 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 125 - Obligation to maintain minor children - Unwanted child - Damages against - Liabilities of doctor and state - Whether provision bars claim? - Held, no statutory bar in claiming damages - Entitled to claim full damages from State Govt. and doctor.
