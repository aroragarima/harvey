Phillippa Anne Duke v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 May 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 32; (1982) 2 SCC 389; (1982) SCC (Cr) 444; AIR 1982 SC 1178; 1982 CRLJ 1389; 1982 CrLR(SC) 295; 1982(1) SCALE 496; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 769; 1982 UJ 585

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Banking & Finance - Foreign Exchange Management Act - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 11 - Detention - Smuggling - Two British nationals under detention - Habeas Corpus - Petitioner detained by order of magistrate for smuggling - Whether detention of petitioner illegal? - Held, detention orders and grounds were duly served on petitioner - Advisory Board was constituted of three judges and representations of petitioner was sufficiently considered - Petition dismissed.
