State of Uttar Pradesh v Dr. Dina Nath Shukla and Another

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3051; (1997) 9 SCC 662; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1231; AIR 1997 SC 1095; 1997 (2) AWC 689; 1997 (2) ESC 1222; 1997 (75) FLR 962; JT 1997 (2) SC 467; 1997 LabIC 1441; 1997 (2) LLN 526; 1997(2) SCALE 103; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 750; 1997 (1) SLR 715; 1997 (3) Supreme 386; 1997 (1) UJ 494; 1997 (2) UPLBEC 964

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: University, Equality of opportunity, Equality

Summary: Service - Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Reservation for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes) Act, 1994, s. 3 - (A) Whether rule of reservation applicable in appointment to post of professor, reader or lecturer in University - (B) Whether faculty wise, discipline wise, reservation should be indicated in advertisement - Held, (A) rule of reservation applicable - (B) faculty wise and discipline wise reservation should be indicated - Appeal disposed of.
