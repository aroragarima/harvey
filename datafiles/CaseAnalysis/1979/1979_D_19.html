Hasinuddin Khan and Others v Deputy Director of Consolidation and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 364; (1980) 3 SCC 285; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1207; 1980 UJ 412

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - U.P. High Court (Abolition of Letters Patent Appeals) Act, 1962 - U.P. High Court (Abolition of Letters Patent Appeals) (Amendment) Act, Act 33 of 1972 (amendment) - Constitutionality of Act - In the instant case constitutionality of the Act and amendment was challenged - And an application filed seeking leave of instant court for urging additional grounds and an application for condonation of delay in filing the special leave petition - Whether, appellant should be granted with relief - Held, the petition for permission to urge additional grounds, except on Constitutional points, shall be treated as having been filed in the special leave petition - Order accordingly.
