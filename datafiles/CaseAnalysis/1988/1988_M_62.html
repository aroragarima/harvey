Jaywant S. Kulkarni & Ors. v Minochar Dosabhai Shroff & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, L.M. Sharma, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 613; (1988) 4 SCC 108; AIR 1988 SC 1817; 1988 (3) Bom.C.R. 531; JT 1988 (3) SC 360; 1988 MahLJ 828; 1988 (2) RCJ 312; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 513; 1988 (2) RentLR 761; 1988(2) SCALE 384; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 296; 1988 (2) UJ 494

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 - Eviction of tenants on ground of arrears of rent - Tenant neglecting to make payment - Until expiration of period of one months notice u/s.12(2) - If decree of eviction can be passed - Held, Court can pass the decree for eviction - But in order to cause less hardship, six months time to vacate the premises granted - Appeal dismissed.
