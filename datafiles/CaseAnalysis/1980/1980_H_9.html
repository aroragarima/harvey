Hari Obul Reddy and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 407; (1981) 3 SCC 675; (1981) SCC (Cr) 795; AIR 1981 SC 82; 1980 CRLJ 1330; 1980 CRLR 627

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Reliability of interested and partisan witness - Whether interested evidence can form basis of conviction - Interested evidence held is not necessarily unreliable - Even partisanship by itself is not a valid ground for discrediting of rejecting sworn testimony - Appeal Dismissed
