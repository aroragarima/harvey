Newspapers, Limited, Allahabad v State Industrial Tribunal, Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 78; AIR 1960 SC 1328; 1960 (2) LLJ 37

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 3, 4 and 8 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10 and 36 - Whether only a registered body can sponsor a workman's case to make it an industrial dispute? - Held, no - It is not necessary that a registered body should sponsor a workman's case to make it an industrial dispute - Once it is shown that a body of workmen, either acting through their union or otherwise had sponsored a workman's case - It becomes an industrial dispute - Appeal dismissed.
