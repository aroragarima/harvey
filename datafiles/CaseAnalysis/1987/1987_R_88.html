Richpal Singh And Others Etc. v Dalip

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28394; (1987) 4 SCC 410; AIR 1987 SC 2205; JT 1987 (3) SC 516; 1987 (2) RCJ 436; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 351; 1987(2) SCALE 527; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 93

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Punjab Tenancy Act, 1887, s. 77 - Applicability of principle of Res judicata - Whether order directing eviction of a person by Revenue Court u/s. 77(3) of 1887 Act, operates as Res judicata for a civil suit for restoration of possession filed by that person claiming to be a mortgagee and not a tenant - Held, does not bar civil suit - Appeal dismissed.
