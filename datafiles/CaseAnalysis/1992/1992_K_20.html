Rukmini Amma Saradamma v Kalyani Sulochana and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, A.S. Anand, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 787; (1993) 1 SCC 499; AIR 1993 SC 1616; AIR 1993 SCW 317; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 314; 1993 (1) RCJ 27; 1993 (1) RCR(Rent) 346; 1993 (1) RentLR 1; 1992(3) SCALE 580; [1992] Supp3 S.C.R. 579; 1993 (1) UJ 273

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 20 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 115 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - (A) Scope of revisional powers of High Court - Re-appreciation of oral and documentary evidence while setting aside eviction - (B) Revision u/s. 115 of CPC, 1908 of order of eviction- Party taking chance by participating in proceedings before Rent Controller pursuant to order of remit and taking up matter in appeal - Whether remit order can be questioned? - Held, not justified - Cannot be questioned - Appeal allowed.
