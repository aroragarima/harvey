Babu Lal v Hazari Lal Kishori Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 204; (1982) 1 SCC 525; AIR 1982 SC 818; 1982 ALJ 345; 1982 (8) ALR 258; 1982(1) SCALE 79; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 94; 1982 UJ 493

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 22, 21 and 28 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 47 and O. 2, r. 2 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 55 - Contract - Agreement to sell - Property sold to another in defiance of previous agreement - Suit for specific performance - Possession at appellate stage - Held, relief of possession can be granted at appellate stage - Petition dismissed.
