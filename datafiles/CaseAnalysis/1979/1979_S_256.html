Gurdial Singh Fijji v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 255; (1979) SCC (L&S) 197; (1979) 2 SCC 368; AIR 1979 SC 1622; 1979 LabIC 1186; 1980 (2) LLN 143; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 518; 1979 SLJ 299; 1979 (1) SLR 804

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 311- Indian Administrative Service (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations, 1955, Regns. 4, 5 and 7 - All India Service Manual Part II, Resolution 11 and 12 - Service Rules - IAS - (A) If resolutions 11 and 12 of Manual are ultra vires 1955, Regulations - (B) Adverse report in confidential roll - Effect of non-consideration of explanation offered by servant - Held, resolutions not ultra vires regulations - Held, non-issuance of integrity certificate, could not be upheld - Appeal allowed.
