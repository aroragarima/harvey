Supreme Court of India

06 February 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956.

Summary: Family & Personal - Guardians and Wards Act, 1890, ss. 7, 8, 9 and 11 - Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 15, 39 and 24 - Inter-country adoption - Held, court laid down a normative and procedural safeguards to be followed as there isn't any statutory enactment - Petition disposed.
