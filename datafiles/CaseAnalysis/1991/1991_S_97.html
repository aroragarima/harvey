A. Viswanatha Pillai And Ors. v Special Tahsildar For Land Acquisitionno. Iv And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 242; (1991) 4 SCC 17; AIR 1991 SC 1966; AIR 1991 SCW 2192; 1991 (3) CCC 108; JT 1991 (3) SC 575B; 1991 (2) KLT 444; 1992 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1991(2) SCALE 286; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 465; 1991 (2) UJ 470

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 18 and 23 - Enhancement of compensation - Reference sought by one of co- Owners of land but not by other - Other co-owners not expressly seeking reference - If entitled to compensation - Held, would also be entitled to enhanced compensation pro-rata as per their shares - Appeal allowed.
