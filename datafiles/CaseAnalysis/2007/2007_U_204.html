Unipatch Rubber Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Bhopal

Supreme Court of India

13 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1717; 2011 (272) E.L.T. 340

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985, headings 4008.21 and 4016.99 - Criss-cross patches of vulcanized rubber - Whether the criss-cross patches of vulcanized rubber manufactured by the assessee was classifiable under sub-heading 4008.21 of the CETA, 1985, as claimed by the Revenue or under sub-heading 4016.99 thereof, as claimed by the assessee? - Held, matter remanded to the Commissioner to determine whether the process being followed by the assessee is the same as being followed by the appellant herein who is relying upon the said decisions - Appeals allowed.
