Living Media Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21067; (2002) 10 SCC 405; 2002 (175) CTR 299; [2002] 255 ITR 268; [2002] 123 TAXMAN 53

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Practice & Procedure - Time barred assessment - Held, no justification for interfering with order of HC that summarily dismissed appellant's writ petition - Appeal dismissed.
