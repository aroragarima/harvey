Chandulal v Management of Messrs Pan American World Airways

Supreme Court of India

19 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 93; (1985) SCC (L&S) 535; (1985) 2 SCC 727; AIR 1985 SC 1128; 1985 (28) DLT 329; 1985 (51) FLR 142; 1985 LabIC 1225; 1985 (2) LLJ 181; 1985 (2) LLN 582; 1985(1) SCALE 764; 1985 (2) SLR 335

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.2, item 3, ss. 2(oo), 25F - Industrial Dispute - Termination of workmen in Airline on the ground of loss of confidence - Not retrenched - Out of employment for more than eleven years - (A) Whether domestic enquiry is a pre-condition - (B) Legality of termination and tenability of stand on loss of confidence as a defence to reinstatement - (C) Quantum of compensation has to be ascertained - Compensation in lieu of reinstatement - Consideration of periods of past unemployment and future term - Held, not a condition precedent - Termination held illegal; workman entitled to compensation (as loss of confidence) and tenability of defence of loss of confidence not decided - Held, considering the facts of the case direction issued to pay Rs. 2 lakhs as and by way of back wages as also in lieu of relief of reinstatement - Appeal partly allowed
