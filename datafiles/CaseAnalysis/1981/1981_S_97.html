R.K. Garg, Advocate v State Of Himachal Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 299; (1981) 3 SCC 166; (1981) SCC (Cr) 663; AIR 1981 SC 1382; 1981 CRLJ 1029; 1981 KLT 406; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 536

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, ss. 2(c) and 12 - Contempt committed by Advocate by throwing shoe at Trial Court Judge - Appellant's vesion include 'Judge remarked: 'You rascal, I will set you right'. ' - Behaviour most reprehensible - Unconditional apology tendered - Held, sentence altered - Appeal Partly Allowed.
