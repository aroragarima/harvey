State of Uttar Pradesh and Another v Shiv Narain Upadhyaya

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 430; (2005) 6 SCC 49; (2005) SCC (L&S) 794; 2005 (6) AWC 5363; 2005 (3) ESC 451; 2005 (106) FLR 910; JT 2005 (6) SC 444; 2005 (3) KLT 754; 2005(5) SCALE 678; 2005 (8) SLR 197; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 847; 2005 (5) Supreme 194; 2005 (3) UPLBEC 2440

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Births, Deaths And Marriages Registration Act, 1886 - Issue relating to date of birth - Held, whenever any such question arises, onus is on applicant, to prove about wrong recording of his date of birth, in his service book - High Court has clearly erred in holding that service book was not produced as records reveal along with the affidavit a copy of original service book was filed - Appeal allowed.
