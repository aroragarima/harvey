Â Controller Of Estate Duty, Kerala v V. Venugopala Varma Rajah

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 178; (1976) 4 SCC 3; AIR 1977 SC 121; 1976 CTR 423; [1976] 105 ITR 593; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 346

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Estate Duty Act, 1953, ss. 5 and 34 - Land and Property - Whether forest land, is 'agricultural land' - Burden of establishing exemption - Value of such land if to be included in total value of estate of deceased - Held that the assessee had not discharged his onus of proof - The forest land could not be said to be 'agricultural land' - Hence, value of this land must be included in the total value of the estate of the deceased - Appeal Allowed.
