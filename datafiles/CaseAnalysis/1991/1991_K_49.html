Narcotics Control Bureau v Krishan Lal And Others

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 486; (1991) SCC (Cr) 265; (1991) 1 SCC 705; AIR 1991 SC 558; AIR 1991 SCW 339; 1991 (1) Crimes 467; 1991 CRLJ 654; 1991 CRLR 178; 1991 CrLR(SC) 178; 1991 (52) E.L.T. 328; JT 1991 (1) SC 258; 1991 (1) KLT 547; 1991(1) SCALE 97; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 139; 1991 (2) ShimLC 36; 1991 (2) UJ 121

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: TADA Act, Fetters, Defence And Internal Security Of India Act, 1971

Summary: Criminal - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 37 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 4 and 439 - Scope - High Courts power u/s. 439 CrPC to grant bail - If subject to limitations u/s. 37 of 1985 Act - Whether limitations placed u/s. 37 of 1985 Act on power to grant bail are applicable to HC? - Held, is subject to limitations of s. 37 of of 1985 Act - Are applicable - S. 37 of 1985 Act prevails over s. 439 CrPC - Appeal disposed of.
