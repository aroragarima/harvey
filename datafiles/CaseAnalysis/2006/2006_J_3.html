United India Insurance Co. Ltd. v J.A.Infra Structure Pvt.Ltd

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1495; (2006) 8 SCC 21; 2006 (65) ALR 482; 2006 (4) AWC 3581; 2008 (6) CompLJ 35; 2006 (4) JhrCR(SC) 123; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 936; 2007 (1) MPLJ 232; 2006 (4) RCR(Civil) 331; 2006(8) SCALE 707; [2006] Supp5 S.C.R. 638

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Insurance

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Insurance - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 34 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 14 - Applicability of s. 14 - Respondent had taken policy from appellant (Insurance Company) and subsequently, invoked arbitration as per Condition No. 7 of the Policy - Arbitrator awarded Rs. 2,12,49,336/- with future interest @ 18% - HC dismissed the petition filed against said order for want of jurisdiction - Appellant thereafter filed a petition u/s. 34 of 1996 Act before the District Court alongwith the application u/s. 14 of the 1963 Act - District Court dismissed said application - HC also dismissed appeal filed against said order - Hence, the instant appeal - Held, s. 14 of the Limitation Act, 1963 was applicable in the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 and accordingly order of the lower court set aside and matter remanded back to the District Court for deciding the application u/s. 14 of the 1963 Act - Order accordingly.
