M/S Voltas Limited, Bombay. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, A.M. Ahmadi, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 453; (1995) Supp2 SCC 498; AIR 1995 SC 1881; [1995] 83 Comp Cas 228; 1995 (3) CTJ 49; JT 1995 (2) SC 261; 1995(1) SCALE 455; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 947

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Summary: Trade - MRTP - MRTP Act , 1969, ss. 33(1), 2(O), 37and 38 908 - Restrictive trade practice - (A) Object of deeming clause is to avoid such practices beyond controversy - Agreement containing condition covered by clauses of s. 33(1) - (B) Finding and order to cease and desist from restrictive trade practice - Recording finding that objectionable clauses of agreement were prejudicial to public interest, necessary - Unreasoned finding by Commission - Held, would be a deemed trade restrictive practice and Commission or Court, cannot test and examine such trade practices mentioned in clauses (a) to (l) of s. 33, in light of s. 2(0), of the Act - Fresh examination of question involved, directed - Appeal allowed.
