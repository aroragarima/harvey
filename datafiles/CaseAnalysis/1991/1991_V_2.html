V. B. Rangaraj v V. B. Gopalakrishnan and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 395; (1992) 1 SCC 160; AIR 1992 SC 453; [1992] 73 Comp Cas 201; 1992 (1) CompLJ 11; JT 1991 (4) SC 430; 1992 (2) MLJ 11; 1992 (2) MLJ(SC) 11; 1991(2) SCALE 11352; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Practice and Procedure - Limitation Act, 1908 - Companies Act, 1956, s. 82 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 5 - Shares are transferable - As per s. 82 of 1956 Act and s. 5 of Transfer of Property Act - Restriction on tansfer can imposed by Article of Association of the company - No such restriction in the AOA - But a private agreement among Shareholders - Imposing restriction that a living member can transfer his shareholding only to the branch of family to which he belongs - Whether such agreement binding either on shareholders or on Company - Held, agreement imposing additional restrictions on the member's right to transfer his shares in contravention of provisions of the AOA is not binding on the shareholders or company - Appeal allowed. .
