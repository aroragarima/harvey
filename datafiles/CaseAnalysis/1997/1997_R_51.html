R.S.E.B.Accountants Association, Jaipur v Rajasthan State Electricity Board And Another

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2508; (1997) 3 SCC 103; (1997) SCC (L&S) 644; AIR 1997 SC 882; JT 1997 (2) SC 342; 1997 LabIC 863; 1997 (2) LLN 43; 1997 SCALE 668; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 868; 1997 (2) SLR 138

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Qualifications

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Rajasthan State Electricity Board Officers (Recruitment, Promotion and Seniority) Regulations, 1974, sch. 1, entry 19 - Promotion to post of Asst. Accounts Officer, Asst. Audit Officer, Asst. Revenue Officer - Classification on basis of educational qualification for promotion is permissible - Constitutionality of entry 19 in service regulation prescribing lesser service experience to accountants holding intermediate ICWA or Diploma in Cost and Works and fixing quota of 25% for them - Held, not violative of arts. 14 and 16 - Appeal dismissed.
