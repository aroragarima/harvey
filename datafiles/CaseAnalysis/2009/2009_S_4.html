(1) Haryana State Industrial Development v (1) Shakuntla and Others; (2) Raj Bala and Others;

Supreme Court of India

22 October 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1465; (2010) 12 SCC 448; AIR 2010 SC 468; 2010 (1) AWC 1000; JT 2009 (13) SC 498; 2009 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2938; 2009 (6) Law Herald (P&H) 3810; 2010 (109) RD 651; 2009(13) SCALE 410; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 413

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Appeals Disposed Of, Punjab Scheduled Roads & Controlled Areas (Restriction of Unregulated Development) Act, 1963

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 6 - Punjab Scheduled Roads & Controlled Areas (Restriction of Unregulated Development) Act, 1963, s. 3 - Land Acquisition proceedings - Release of lands from acquisition - Legality - Notification issued for purpose of acquisition of lands for public purpose - Respondent No.1, being one of the owners of notified acquired lands, filed objections u/s. 5 of Act, for exclusion of their lands - HC set aside notifications issued u/ss. 4 and 6 of Act in respect of acquired lands of respondents, subject to the condition that respondent will maintain the green belt essentially required to lay the infrastructure - Whether said order justified? - Held, nothing wrong in decision of HC to release lands of respondents from acquisition - However, burden to maintain a green belt should have been placed on Municipal Corporation or State Government and not on the individuals - Conditions imposed by HC modified - Appeals disposed of.
