Bhagwan Dass v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 531; (1976) 3 SCC 784; AIR 1976 SC 1393; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 869; 1976 UJ 418

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Summary: U.P. Minor Minerals (Concession) Rules, 1963, rr. 2(5)(7), 3 and 67 - Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, s. 3(e) - (A) Sand, gravel etc., deposited on lands abutting on rivers - Due to fluvial action - Whether they are minor minerals - (B) Auction of right to conduct mining operation - Considerations while determining compensation to owner of land - Held, is minor mineral - District Officer would be having due regard to all relevant factors, particularly the length of deprivation entitled by the conduct of mining operations - Appeal Dismissed.
