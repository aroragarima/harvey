Gangubai Bablya Chaudhary and Others v Sitaram Bhalchandra Sukhtankar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 356; (1983) 4 SCC 31; AIR 1983 SC 742; 1983(1) SCALE 775; 1983 UJ 609

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 39, r. 1 - Validity of interim injunction restraining defendant from putting up construction on entire land - Both parties in possession of about half portion of disputed land - Held, injunction justified - As situation might become irreversible by the time dispute is decided if injunction is not granted - Appeal Allowed.
