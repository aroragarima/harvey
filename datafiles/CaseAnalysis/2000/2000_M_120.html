L. R. Ferror Alloys Limited v Mahavir Mahto and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2956; (2002) 9 SCC 450; (2002) SCC (L&S) 1117; 2001 (1) ACC 326; 2001 (1) LLJ 181; 2000 (8) SLT 693

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Workmen's Compensation Act - Whether entire liability including penalty and interest will have to be reimbursed by Insurance Company? - Payment of interest and penalty are two distinct liabilities arising under Act, penalty imposed on insured employer is on account of his personal fault and Insurance Company cannot be made liable to reimburse penalty - Held, compensation with interest is payable by Insurance Company but not penalty - Appeal allowed.
