Davenport and Company Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Ii

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 331; (1975) 2 SCC 399; AIR 1975 SC 1996; 1975 CTR 235; 1975 (2) ITJ 310; [1975] 100 ITR 715; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 180; 1975 TAXLR 908; 1975 UJ 621

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922 - Loss in speculative business - What is 'speculative transaction' u/s. 24(1) expln. 2 of the Act - Held, transactions entered into by the assessee, involving mere transfer of delivery notes and not actual delivery of the goods, were speculative transactions within the meaning of expln. 2 to s. 24(1) of the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
