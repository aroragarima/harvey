Estate of Late Rangalal Jajodia v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 253; (1970) 3 SCC 371; AIR 1971 SC 147; [1971] 79 ITR 505; 1971 (2) MLJ(SC) 80; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 807

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income- Tax Act, 1922, ss. 34(3), Second Proviso and 24B(3) - Income Tax returns filed by a certain person - Completion of assessment completed after his death - Assessment described assessee as estate of that person by heirs and his LRs - Fresh assessments ordered in accordance with provisions of s. 24B - Held, ss. 34 (3) Second Proviso and 24B (3) applied to the facts of the case - Appeal Dismissed.
