Khardah and Company, Limited v Its Workmen

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 32; AIR 1964 SC 719; 1963 (7) FLR 274; 1963 (2) LLJ 452; [1964] 3 S.C.R. 506

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Guilt, Charge - Sheet

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Whether said dismissal was malafide with motive of victimizing? - Incitement for strike - Version given by appellant was untrue - Held, appellant in dismissing respondent showed lack of good faith - Tribunal cannot allow evidence to be led by one party in absence of other - Tribunal merely send for authenticated record to see whether respondent was organizing secretary of union or not - Appeal dismissed.
