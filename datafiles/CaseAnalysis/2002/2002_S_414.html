H.S. Jayanna and Bros and Others v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 164; (2002) 4 SCC 125; AIR 2002 SC 1285; JT 2002 (2) SC 549; 2002(2) SCALE 476; 2002 (2) SCJ 316; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 261; 2002 (2) SLT 410; 2002 (128) STC 226; 2002 (2) Supreme 330

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Agricultural Produce Markets, Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966, Market Fee, Bihar Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1981, Rice Milling Industries (Regulation) Act, 1958, Bihar Agriculture Produce Markets Act, 1960, Karnataka Rice Procurement (Levy) Order, 1984

Summary: There is no conflict between the provisions of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 and the Karnataka Rice Procurement (Levy) Order, 1984 since they do not cover the same field though they do deal with the same subject, and therefore, the question of any inconsistency, repugnancy or the Marketing Act being ineffectual in terms of Section 6 of the Essential Commodities Act in view of the Control Order issued under Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act would not arise.
