Shyamal Kanti Danda v Chunilal Choudhary

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 376; (1984) 4 SCC 345; AIR 1984 SC 1732; 1984(2) SCALE 271; 1985 UJ 17

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 5 and 14 - CPC, 1908, s. 96 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Delay - Application for condonation of delay of 386 days in filing appeal against ex parte decree in eviction suit - On the ground that he was prosecuting under legal advice a remedy in another civil court, for setting aside the ex-parte decree and that time spent must excluded - Court declined to condone the delay and after an unsuccessful revision petition in the High Court, the petitioner has approached Supreme Court u/art.136 of the Constitution - Held, application allowed but subject to full compliance with condition of depositing arrears of use and occupation charges by tenants - Petition dismissed.
