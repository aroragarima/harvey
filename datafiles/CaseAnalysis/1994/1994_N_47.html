M.P. Public Service Commission v Navnit Kumar Potdar

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 492; (1994) 6 SCC 293; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1377; AIR 1995 SC 77; 1994 (28) ATC 286; 1995 (2) CLR 20; 1994 (69) FLR 1098; JT 1994 (6) SC 302; 1995 (1) LLJ 80; 1995 (2) LLN 314; 1994(4) SCALE 251; 1995 (1) SLJ 134; 1994 (5) SLR 273; 1995 (1) UJ 115

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Madhya Pradesh Industrial Relations Act, 1960, s. 8(1)(c); Constitution Of India,195, arts. 16 and 309 - Short-listing - Interview - Criteria - Presiding Officer of Labour Courts - Candidates with seven and half years experience only were called for interview - Held, does not amount to change in statutory criterion of 5 years experience by administrative decision - Appeals allowed.
