Corporation of The City of Bangalore v M. Papaiah and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 381; (1989) 3 SCC 612; AIR 1989 SC 1809; JT 1989 (3) SC 294; 1989(2) SCALE 182; 1989 (2) UJ 472

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Procedure - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 34 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 100 - (A) Suit for decree of perpetual injunction restraining defendant from interfering with possession - Relief of declaration of title not specifically mentioned in relief portion of plaint - Dismissed - Validity - (B) Question of law - Revenue record is not document of title - Interpretation of revenue record is not question of law - Finding of fact rendered by lower court on basis of interpretation of revenue record - Interference with finding by HC - Held, (A) Suit could not be dismissed on that ground - (B) Is illegal.
