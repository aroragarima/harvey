Niadar and Another v Delhi Administration and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 244; (1992) 4 SCC 112; (1992) SCC (L&S) 819; 1992 (21) ATC 398; 1992 (2) LLN 11A

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Regularisation of services - Payment of minimum salary payable to person regularly employed in a comparable posts in department - Petitioners worked for nearly 20 years as casual labourers - Direction issued to Delhi Administration to prepare a scheme for absorbing casual labourers who have worked for one year and more in Soil Conservation Department as regular employees - Writ petition disposed of.
