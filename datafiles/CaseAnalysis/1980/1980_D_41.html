Nirod Baran Banerjee v Dy. Commissioner Of Hazari Bagh

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 119; (1980) 3 SCC 5; AIR 1980 SC 1109; 1980 (28) BLJR 238; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1043; 1980 UJ 425

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Constitution, 19, 50, art. 133 - CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 8, O. 8, r. 2 - Arbitration agreement - Not in accordance with the provisions of art.299 of the Constitution - Question raised for first time before Supreme Court - Held, that in view of the provisions of O. VI r. 8 and O. VIII r. 2 of CPC, the appellant would be debarred from raising the point for the first time - Appeal dismissed.
