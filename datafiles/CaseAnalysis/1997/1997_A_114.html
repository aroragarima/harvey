State Rep. By The C.B.I v Anil Sharma

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1313; (1997) 7 SCC 187; (1997) SCC (Cr) 1039; AIR 1997 SC 3806; 1997 (4) CCR 10; 1997 (3) Crimes 252; 1997 CRLJ 4414; JT 1997 (7) SC 651; 1997(5) SCALE 689; 1997 (3) SCJ 32; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 737; 1997 (7) Supreme 670

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 438 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 - Anticipatory Bail - Case against respondent MLA/ former Minister for offences u/s. 13(2) of 1988 Act - Validity of grant of anticipatory bail despite of apprehension by CBI that he would influence witnesses - In light of fact that consideration for anticipatory bail need not be same as for an application to release on bail after arrest - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
