Ram Bali v State Of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

16 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 265; (2004) 10 SCC 598; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2045; AIR 2004 SC 2329; 2004 CRLJ 2490; 2004 (3) JhrCR(SC) 53; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 211; 2004(4) SCALE 611; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 195; 2004 (2) UC 1034

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ballistics

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Reappraisal of evidence and interference with concurrent findings of fact - Interference is permissible only when exceptional and special circumstances exist which resulted in injustice - Not a case of that nature and evidence seems creditworthy and conclusions arrived at are well merited and sufficiently supported by overwhelming material on record - Appeal dismissed.
