Nand Lal Bajaj v State of Punjab and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 92; (1981) 4 SCC 327; (1981) SCC (Cr) 841; AIR 1981 SC 2041; 1981 CRLJ 1501; 1981 CrLR(SC) 535; 1981(3) SCALE 1393; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 718

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Deem, Prevention of Blackmarketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities Act, 1980

Summary: Prevention of Black-marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities Act, 1980, ss. 3, 11 and 12 - Constitution, arts. 14, 21 and 22 - Detention - Legal assistance to detenu in proceedings before Advisory Board - Detenu denied legal assistance given by Detaining Authority - Legality - Procedure is arbitrary and illegal - Petition Allowed.
