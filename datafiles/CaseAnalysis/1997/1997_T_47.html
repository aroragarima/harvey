P. Virudhachalam and Others v Management of Lotus Mills and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1186; (1998) SCC (L&S) 342; (1998) 1 SCC 650; AIR 1998 SC 554; 1998 (1) CLR 1; 1998 (78) FLR 107; JT 1997 (9) SC 734; 1998 LabIC 834; 1998 (1) LLJ 389; 1998 (1) LLN 977; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 54; 1997(7) SCALE 520; 1998 (1) SLT 311; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 263; 1997 (10) Supreme 389; 1998 (1) UPLBEC 68

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Binding Effect, Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25C, Proviso,ss. 25 - J and 18(3) - Statutory compensation - Settlement - Adversely affecting statutory rights of workmen - Binding to - Extent of - Held, is also binding to non signatory unions to settlement - Provisions of s. 25 - J cannot be applied for isolating binding effect of settlement.
