Gokak Patel Volkart Limited v Collector Of Central Excise, Belgaum

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28062; (1987) 2 SCC 93; (1987) SCC (Tax) 165; AIR 1987 SC 1161; 1987 (2) BankCLR 264; 1987 (1) CCC 755; 1987 (11) ECR 363; 1987 (28) E.L.T. 53; JT 1987 (1) SC 469; 1987(1) SCALE 361; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 309; 1987 (1) UJ 627

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 11 A and 11 A(1) explanation - Limitation - Notice proceedings for recovery of excise duty short levied - Exclusion of period of stay of service of notice - High Court did not give any interim direction staying service of notice or levy of duty - (A) Computation of limitation - (B) If issuance of notice to assessee before raising demand is condition precedent - Held, benefit of exclusion is not available - Notice is a condition precedent to a demand u/s. 11A(2) - Appeal allowed.
