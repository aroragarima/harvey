Sher Mohammad @ Seru v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

08 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 127; (1975) 2 SCC 2; (1975) SCC (Cr) 360; AIR 1975 SC 2049; 1975 CRLJ 1751; 1975 CrLR(SC) 161; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 154; 1975 UJ 166

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Maintenance Of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 3(4) - Detention valid or not? - Infringement of procedural safeguard - Communication of order to Central Govt. beyond period of seven days and State Govts. approval - Held, order invalid - No sufficient compliance with statutory requirement - Infringement of procedural safeguard - Petition allowed.
