Messrs Vishal Properties Private Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 October 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1023; (2007) 11 SCC 172; AIR 2008 SC 183; 2008 (5) AWC 4822; JT 2007 (12) SC 56; 2008 (2) RCR(Civil) 700; 2007(12) SCALE 37; 2007 (7) SCJ 766; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 910; 2008 (1) UPLBEC 554

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Finance Commission, Architectural, U.P. Industrial Development Act, 1976

Summary: U.P. Industrial Development Act, 1976 - Notice was issued for unauthorized additional construction and change of user of land - Plea of discrimination - Writ petition against dismissed - Appeal against - Held, any action/order contrary to law does not confer any right upon any person for similar treatment - Art. 14 is not meant to perpetuate an illegality - It provides for positive equality and not negative equality - Not bound to direct any Authority to repeat the wrong action done by it earlier - Appeal dismissed.
