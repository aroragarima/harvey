Nepc Micon Limited and Others v Magma Leasing Limited

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1406; (1999) 4 SCC 253; (1999) SCC (Cr) 524; AIR 1999 SC 1952; 1999 (36) ALR 306B; 2006 (2) BC 316; [1999] 96 Comp Cas 822; 2001 (2) CompLJ 278; 1999 CRLJ 2883; JT 1999 (3) SC 374; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 932

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - Dishonoured cheque returned by bank with endorsement 'account closed' - Interpretation of penal statute by considering legislative intent - Held, is offence by drawer of cheque as envisaged in s. 138 - Appeal dismissed.
