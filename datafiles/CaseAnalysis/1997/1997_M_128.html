State of Orissa and Others v Mamtarani Sahoo and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1305; (1998) 8 SCC 753; AIR 1999 SC 3370; 1999 LabIC 2621

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Ad Hoc Appointment

Summary: Orissa Education Act, 1969, ss. 10(3) and 10(2) (amendment of 1989) - Appointment - Teachers of private schools - Service Rules u/s. 10(3) silent on question of Constitution of District Selection Boards and procedure for inviting applications and selecting candidates by District Selection Boards - Held, District Selection Board constituted vide Notification issued u/s. 10(2) applicable under executive instructions.
