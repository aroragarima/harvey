Zahoor and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 505; (1991) Supp1 SCC 372; (1991) SCC (Cr) 678; AIR 1991 SC 40; 1990 (3) Crimes 556; 1991 CRLJ 56; 1991 CRLR 46; JT 1990 (4) SC 207; 1990(2) SCALE 813; 1991 (1) UJ 159

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 154 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Delayed First Information Report - By itself sufficient to reject prosecution case, whether - Material particulars mentioned in FIR were substantiated by testimony of eye-witnesses - Corroborated by medical evidence - Found reliable - Held, no clear indications of fabrication - Delay in itself not ground to reject.
