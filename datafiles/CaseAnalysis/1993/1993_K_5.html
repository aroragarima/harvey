Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, L.M. Sharma, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: All India Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1955

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 & 311 - Central Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1964, r. 3(1)(i)(ii)(iii) - Service Rules - Validity of initiation of disciplinary proceedings against Govt. servant for violation of Conduct Rules - Materiality of his discharging quasi-judicial functions - Held, proceedings can be initiated and discharging immaterial - Appeal allowed.
