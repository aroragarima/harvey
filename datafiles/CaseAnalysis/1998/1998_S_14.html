Land Acquisition Officer Revenue Divisional Officer, Chittor v L. Kamalamma Smt. Dead By LRs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1939; (1998) 2 SCC 385; AIR 1998 SC 781; 1998 (1) CLT 130; JT 1998 (1) SC 141; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 92; 1998(1) SCALE 133; 1998 (2) SLT 482; 1998 (1) Supreme 251

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Comparable sales method for determining compensation for land acquisition - Large chunks of land acquired - (A) Whether sale transactions in respect of small extent of land can be relied on? - (B) Applicability of 'Belting method' to land acquired having potentiality of being developed as urban land - Held, can be relied upon but only after making appropriate deductions - Cannot be applied - Appeal allowed.
