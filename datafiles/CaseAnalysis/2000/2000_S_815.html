Commissioner of Income Tax v Steller Investment Limited

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3176; 2000 (164) CTR 287; [2001] 251 ITR 263; [2001] 115 TAXMAN 99

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, ss. 256(2), 68 - Question of Fact - Tribunal concluded facts and HC answered against Revenue - Hence, instant appeal - Whether this Court to interfere in Tribunal's decision - Held, Tribunal came to a conclusion on facts and no interference is called for - Appeal dismissed.
