Â Shyam Behari and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, K.C. Das Gupta, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 113; AIR 1965 SC 427; [1964] 34 Comp Cas 430; 1964 (2) SCJ 226; [1964] 6 S.C.R. 636

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6 - Land required for company - Acquisition - Entire compensation to be paid by Company - Validity of notification, declaring that land was needed for public purpose - Held, invalid - Such notification must state that land is needed for Company - Appeal allowed.
