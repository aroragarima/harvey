Mediquip Systems Private Limited v Proxima Medical System Gmbh

Supreme Court of India

17 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 203; (2005) 7 SCC 42; 2005 (2) BC 366; [2005] 124 Comp Cas 473; 2005 (4) CompLJ 6; JT 2005 (3) SC 380; 2005(3) SCALE 357; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 1015

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Civil Procedure - Corporate - Winding up petition - Whether dismissal of connecting stay petition could be justified reason alone for dismissing appeal summarily which was based on cogent grounds? - Held, Division Bench is not justified in dismissing appeal summarily holding that appellant was not entitled to stay of operation of order passed by Company Judge under appeal - Respondent is not a creditor and appellant is not a debtor in so far as US $ 11000 is concerned - There is no prima facie dispute as to debt - No justification whatsoever for admitting winding up petition - Appeal allowed .
