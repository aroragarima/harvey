Khanjan Lal Sewak Ram v Commissioner of Income Tax, Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 414; (1971) 3 SCC 662; AIR 1972 SC 61; [1972] 83 ITR 175; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 502

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26A - Income Tax Rules, 1922 , r. 6, Para 3 - Renewal of registration - Rejection - Jurisdiction as to - Determination - Held, ITO was within his powers in rejecting the renewal of registration - Appeal dismissed.
