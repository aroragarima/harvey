Mohinuddin @ Moin Master v District Magistrate, Beed & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28885; (1987) 4 SCC 58; (1987) SCC (Cr) 674; AIR 1987 SC 1977; 1987 (3) Crimes 1; 1987 CRLR 560; 1987 CrLR(SC) 560; JT 1987 (3) SC 173; 1987(2) SCALE 128; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 668

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - National Security Act, 1980 - Detention u/s. 3(2) - Wholly unexplained and unduly long delay in disposal of representation by State Govt. - No reason why representation could not be dealt with by Chief Minister with all reasonable promptitude and diligence and explanation that he remained away from Bombay - Held, explanation is certainly not a reasonable explanation, and further detention of appellant held illegal - Appeal allowed.
