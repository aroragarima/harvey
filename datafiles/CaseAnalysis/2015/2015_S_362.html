Sudhir and another v State of Maharashtra and another

Supreme Court of India

01 October 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

Prafulla C. Pant, Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 653; (2016) 1 SCC 146; AIR 2015 SC 3665; JT 2015 (9) SC 120; 2015 (4) MLJ(Crl) 195; 2015(10) SCALE 280; [2015] 12 S.C.R. 387

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Cancellation Of Bail, Anticipatory Bail, Misappropriation

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 120B, 34, 380, 406, 409, 420, 448, 456, 457, 467, 468, 471,477A - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, ss. 13, 13(1)(d), 13(2) - Alleged Misappropriation of Funds - Cancellation of anticipatory bail - Justification - It was alleged that appellants had misappropriated funds while acting as public servant - FIR was registered against appellant u/ss. 409,420, 467, 468, 477A read with s. 34 of IPC and s. 13(1)(d) and s. 13(2) of Act - Trial Court granted anticipatory bail to appellant, however, HC cancelled anticipatory bail granted - Hence, present appeal.

Held, after considering gravity of offence, circumstances of case, particularly, allegations of corruption and misappropriation of public funds released for rural development, and further considering conduct of appellants and fact that investigation is held up as custodial interrogation of appellants could not be done due to anticipatory bail. Hence, SC held that HC has rightly cancelled anticipatory bail granted to appellants by Trial Court. Order passed by HC is confirmed. Appeals dismissed.

Ratio - Anticipatory bail cannot be granted in favor of persons when there are serious allegations involving corruption, misappropriation of public funds.
