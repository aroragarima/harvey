Mrs. Winifred Ross and Another v Mrs. Ivy Fonseca and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 251; (1984) 1 SCC 288; AIR 1984 SC 458; 1984 (1) Bom.C.R. 385; 1983(2) SCALE 900; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 1005; 1984 UJ 860

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 13A1, 13(1)(g) - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - WB Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, s. 29B - Whether retired defence personnel acquring possession of premises from tenanat is entitled to benefit u/s. 13-A1of the 1947 Act - Held, not entitled - Appeal dismissed .
