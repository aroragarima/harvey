Dedh Nathu Raja (Dead) By L. Rs. v L. Angha Nathu Jamal (Dead) By L. Rs & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 82; (1969) 3 SCC 813; AIR 1971 SC 300; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 434

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Bombay Reorganisation Act, 1960

Summary: States Reorganisation Act, 1956, ss. 52 and 57 - Constitutuon, 1950 - Saurashtra High Court abolished - Its jurisdiction conferred on Bombay HC - Appeal again transferred to Gujarat HC under Bombay Reorganisation Act, 1960 - Governing provisons - Determination - Held, appeal from single Judge to Division Bench was governed by Letters Patent cl. 15 (Bom.) and not by s.22A of Saurashtra Ordinance 2 of 1948 - Appeal Allowed.
