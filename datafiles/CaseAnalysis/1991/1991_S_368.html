Sushilabai Laxminarayan Mudliyar and Others v Nihalchand Waghajibhai Shaha and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 718; (1993) Supp1 SCC 11; AIR 1992 SC 185

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Letters Patent (Bom), cl. 15 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Maintainability of appeal - Petition u/arts. 226 and 227 - Grounds however unmistakably showing that it was u/art. 226 - Order passed by single Judge of High Court was also u/art. 226 - Letters Patent Appeal against order - Held, is maintainable - Appeal partly allowed.
