Siddappa Vasappa Kuri and Another v Special Land Acquisition Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Brijesh Kumar, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 19934; (2002) 1 SCC 142; AIR 2001 SC 2951; 2001 (6) ALD(SC) 138; 2001 (45) ALR 688; 2002 (1) AWC 106; 2002 (1) CRC 244; JT 2001 (9) SC 176; 2002 (1) MLJ(SC) 82; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 805; 2001(7) SCALE 380; 2001 (7) SLT 513; [2001] Supp4 S.C.R. 236; 2001 (8) Supreme 89; 2002 (1) UC 133B; 2002 (1) UPLBEC 109

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 11 and 23 (1-A) - Possession of Land - Market value - Possession of land was taken by respondents from appellants, who were owners thereof for purposes of construction of an irrigation channel - Compensation was awarded to appellants at rate of Rs. 10,000 per acre - District judge enhanced compensation to Rs. 50,000 per acre - On appeal, High Court reduced compensation to Rs. 41,400 per acre - Executing court granted to appellants additional compensation u/s. 23(1-A) from date on which possession of land was taken, namely, 01-06-1977 to date on which s. 4 notification was issued, namely, 08-03-1991 - High Court, before whom a revision petition was presented by Land Acquisition Officer, took view that appellants were entitled to additional compensation from date of s. 4 notification, namely, 08-03-1991, to date of award, namely, 06-02-1992 - Whether appellants that appellants are entitled to compensation for period 01-06-1977 to 08-03-1991? - Held, it is clear from s. 23(1-A) that starting point for purposes of calculating amount to be awarded there under, at rate of 12 per cent per annum on market value, is date of publication of s. 4 notification - Terminal point for purpose is either date of award or date of taking possession, whichever is earlier - In present case, possession of land having been taken prior to publication of s. 4 notification, that terminal is not available - Only available terminal is date of award - HC, therefore, was in no error in holding that appellants were entitled to additional compensation u/s. 23(1-A) for period 08-03-1991 to 06-02-1993 - Appeal dismissed.
