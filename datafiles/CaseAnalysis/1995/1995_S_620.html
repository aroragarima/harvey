Shambhu Singh Meena and Others v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1031; (1995) Supp2 SCC 431; (1995) SCC (L&S) 874; 1995 (30) ATC 140; 1995 (71) FLR 750; JT 1997 (10) SC 441; 1995 LabIC 1774; 1995 (2) LLN 20; 1995(2) SCALE 741; 1995 (2) SLR 616; 1995 (2) UJ 49

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Rajasthan Administrative Services Rules, 1954, r. 28-B - Whether petitioner were promoted in accordance with Rajasthan Administrative Services Rules ? - Held, ACRs./APARS of officers should either be 'Very Good' or 'Outstanding' to be selected on basis of merit for entire period under consideration - Tribunal and High Court correctly construed explanation and no interference is called for - Petitions dismissed.
