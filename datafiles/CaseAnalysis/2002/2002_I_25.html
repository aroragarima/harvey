Subject: Service

Keywords: Food Corporations Act, 1964, Statutory Bodies, International Airports Authority Act, 1971

Summary: Constitution of India - Article 12 - Held, Council of Scientific and Industrial Research is well within the range of Article 12 of the Constitution of India, as it is financially, functionally and administratively dominated by or under the control of the Government. - Decision in Sabhajit Tewari vs. Union of India, (1975) 3 SCR 616 case overruled.
