Oriental Insurance Company Limited v Samayanallur Primary Agricultural Co-Operative Bank

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1332; (1999) 8 SCC 543; AIR 2000 SC 10; 1999 (37) ALR 762; 2000 (1) AWC 395; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 7; [2000] 99 Comp Cas 99; JT 1999 (9) SC 9; 2000 (124) PLR 187; [1999] Supp4 S.C.R. 329

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Consumer Protection - Insurance - Whether cashiers box can be equated with a safe within meaning of insurance policy? - Insurance policy has to be construed having reference only to stipulations contained in it and no artificial far - fetched meaning could be given to words appearing in it - Held, alleged burglary and removal of cash box containing jewellery and cash was not covered by insurance policy between parties - Appeal allowed.
