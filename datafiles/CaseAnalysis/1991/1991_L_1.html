Amir Shad Khan Aziz Ahmedkhan @ Azizmohd. Khan v L. Hmingliana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Veeraswami Ramaswami, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 241; (1991) 4 SCC 39; (1991) SCC (Cr) 946; AIR 1991 SC 1983; 1992 (1) CCR 233; 1993 (3) Crimes 275; 1991 CRLJ 2713; 1993 CrLR(SC) 647; JT 1993 (3) SC 367; 1991(2) SCALE 277; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 443

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Specially Empowered

Summary: Criminal - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 21 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 11 - Preventive detention - (A) Who can exercise power as to revocation of order of detention? - (B) Constitutionality of not acceding detenu's request to take out and send copies of his representation to State and Central Govt. - Held, State Govt. or Central Govt. or their officer ordering detention - There is denial of right u/art. 22(5) of constitution and s. 11 of COFEPOSA Act - Appeal allowed.
