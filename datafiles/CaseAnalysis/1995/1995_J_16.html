Nagar Palika, Jind. v Jagat Singh, Advocate.

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 403; (1995) 3 SCC 426; AIR 1995 SC 1377; JT 1995 (3) SC 281; 1995 (3) PLR 224; 1995 (2) RentLR 125; 1995(2) SCALE 512; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 9; 1995 (2) UJ 241

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s.6; Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s.96 - Suit for injunction - Restraining defendant against possession of plaintiff - No allegation of Plaintiff about dispossession by defendant - But, claimed to be owner and possessor of land in question - Held, principles under s.6 would not be applicable in facts and circumstances.
