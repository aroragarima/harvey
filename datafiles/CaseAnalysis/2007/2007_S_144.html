Union of India and Another v Shardindu

Supreme Court of India

16 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 537; (2007) 6 SCC 276; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 456; AIR 2007 SC 2204; 2007(6) ALL MR 970; 2007 (6) AWC 5929; JT 2007 (7) SC 439; 2007 (4) LLN 180; 2007(7) SCALE 575; 2007 (5) SCJ 328; [2007] 6 S.C.R. 1039; 2007 (4) SLR 816

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Prasar Bharati (Broadcasting Corporation of India) Act, 1990, National Council For Teacher Education Rule, 1997, Cable Television Networks Rules, 1994

Summary: National Council for Teacher Education Act, 1993 - Order passed by the Union of India dated 18.11.2005 purporting to terminate the deputation of the respondent as Chairperson, National Council for Teacher Education set aside - Appeal against - Held, appointment of the Chairperson of the NCTE is a tenure post for a period of four years or any person attaining the age of sixty years whichever is earlier. Section 5 deals with disqualification and none of the disqualifications mentioned in that section has been incurred by the respondent - View taken by the High Court of Delhi is correct and there is no ground to interfere with the same - Appeal dismissed.
