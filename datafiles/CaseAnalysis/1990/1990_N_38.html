P.K. Unni v Nirmala Industries & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, K.N. Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 718; (1990) 2 SCC 378; AIR 1990 SC 933; 1990 (1) AWC 509; 1990 BBCJ 84; 1991 (2) BC 146; 1990 (1) CCC 706; JT 1990 (1) SC 423; 1990 (1) KLT 903; 1990 (1) MLJ 36; 1990(1) SCALE 316; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 483

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O 21, rr. 89 and 92(2) - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 127 - Sale of immovable property in execution - Application for setting aside sale - Deposits - Limitation - Held, deposit has to be made within 30 days from date of sale u/r. 92(2) and not within 60 days u/art. 127 of Limitation Act - Appeal allowed.
