Kadra Pahadiya and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 507; (1983) 2 SCC 104; (1983) SCC (Cr) 361; AIR 1982 SC 1167; 1981 CRLJ 481

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution, arts. 21 and 32 - Right to speedy trial - Remedy in the event of denial of such right - Held, he can approach the SC for the purpose of enforcing such right - SC in discharge of its constitutional obligation has the power to give necessary direction - Appeal Allowed.
