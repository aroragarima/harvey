State Of Rajasthan And Ors. v Swaika Properties And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 229; (1985) 3 SCC 217; AIR 1985 SC 1289; 1985 (2) CCC 651; 1985(1) SCALE 1181; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 598; 1985 UJ 785

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Land and Property - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act, 1959, s. 52 - Territorial jurisdiction - Cause of action - Whether the service of notice u/s. 52(2)of 1959 Act served at registered office of owner company in Calcutta was an integral part of the cause of action and was sufficient to invest Calcutta High Court with jurisdiction to entertain a petition u/s. 226 of the Constitution challenging the validity of a notification u/s.5 (21) of the Act for the acquisition of certain (in another State) for a public purpose - Held, has no jurisdiction to entertain - Appeal allowed.
