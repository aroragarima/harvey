Balwant Kaur v Union Territory Of Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28079; (1988) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1988 SC 139; 1988 CRLJ 398; JT 1987 (4) SC 239; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 745

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal- Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 120 B - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 133 - Corroboration of evidence of Approver in murder case was in question - Approver's evidence with regard to complicity of accused in conspiracy lacked corroboration on certain material particulars necessary to connect accused - Held, accused entitled to benefit of doubt.
