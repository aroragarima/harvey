Bhagwandas Fatechand Daswani and Others v Hpa International and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 583; (2000) 2 SCC 13; AIR 2000 SC 775; AIR 2000 SCW 321; 2000 (41) ALR 774; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 685; 2000 (1) CLT 268; JT 2000 (1) SC 266; 2000 (2) MLJ(SC) 24; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 207A; 2001 (92) RD 71; 2000(1) SCALE 146; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 254; 2000 (1) SLT 370; 2000 (1) Supreme 209

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 41, rr. 31 and 33 - Appeal - Five years delay in judgment delivery - Held, it gives impression in mind of appellant that arguments raised at bar have not been reflected or appreciated - Deprecated - Judgment set aside - Matter remitted for deciding appeal afresh.
