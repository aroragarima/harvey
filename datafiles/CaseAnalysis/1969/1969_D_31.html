Digyadarsan Rajendra Ramdassji Varu v State Of Andhra Pradesh & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 167; (1969) 1 SCC 844; AIR 1970 SC 181; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 86; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 103

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Religious Denomination, Constitutionality, Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966

Summary: Constitution, arts. 14, 19(1)(f), 25 and 226 - A.P. Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966, ss. 46, cls.(a) - (h), 47 - Constitutionality of - (A) Provisions of ss. 46 and 47of 1966 Act - (B) Mathadhipathi - Suspension of, pending enquiry into serious charges against him - Assistant Commissioner appointed as day to day administrator - Held, s. 46 is not violtive of art.19(1)(f) or art.14 of Constitution and ss. 46 and 47 do not contravene art. 26 of Constitution - There is no violation of art. 25 - Appeal Dismissed.
