Lakhi Ram v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 573; (1981) SCC (L&S) 438; (1981) 2 SCC 674; AIR 1981 SC 1655; 1981 (43) FLR 296; 1981 LabIC 1116; 1981 (2) LLN 497; 1981 (2) SLJ 408; 1981 (3) SLR 110

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Keywords: Disposal on Merits

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 311 - Service Matter - Government employee - Expungement of adverse remarks in confidential report - Remarks by an Officer, affecting chances of promotion of another Officer - Writ petition - Locus - standi of officer - Held, he can challenge expungement - Appeal Allowed.
