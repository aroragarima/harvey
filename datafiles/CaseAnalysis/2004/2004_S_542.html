Dharam Pal and others v State of Haryana and another

Supreme Court of India

01 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, Tarun Chatterjee, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1938; (2004) 13 SCC 9; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 75

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice and Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 193 - Accused cannot be tried while exercising power u/s. 193 of CrPC - Sessions Court whhile framing of charge concluded that offence was made out against even those whowere shown and had no power to proceed u/s. 319 of CrPC - Held, SC law was correctly enunciated in Kishun Singh v. State of Bihar 1993 Indlaw SC 197 - Decision in Ranjit Singh v. State of Punjab 1998 Indlaw SC 1220 is of three-Judge Bench SC directed matter may be placed before Hon'ble Chief Justice for placing same before a larger Bench. Order accordingly.
