Cloth Traders Private Limited and Others v Additional Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat-I and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 229; (1979) 3 SCC 538; AIR 1979 SC 1691; 1979 (10) CTR 393; [1979] 118 ITR 243; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 984; 1979 TAXLR 1170; [1979] 1 TAXMAN 335

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Finance Bill, Finance Act

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961- Finance Act, 1968 - Interpretation of ss. 85A and 80M of 1961 Act - Availability of rebate - Held , rebate is admissible on the actual amount of dividend received by company, from an Indian Company, and not only dividend income as per the Act i.e. after deductions u/s. 57 - Appeals allowed.
