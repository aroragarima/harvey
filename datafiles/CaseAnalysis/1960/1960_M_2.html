Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay North v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay North

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 419; AIR 1960 SC 703; [1960] 30 Comp Cas 286; [1960] 39 ITR 1; 1960 SCJ 743; [1960] 3 S.C.R. 50

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 4 - Income - Accrual - Agreement with managing agents - Commission on sales - Variable - Payment only after finalisation of company's accounts - Rate reduced - When commission accrued and whether comission lost due to reduction in rate is included in total income - Held, accrued when accounts were made up - Amount foregone, not income - Appeal dismissed.
