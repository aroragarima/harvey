Saraspur Mills Company Limited v Ramanlal Chimanlal and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 143; (1973) SCC (L&S) 410; (1974) 3 SCC 66; AIR 1973 SC 2297; 1973 (26) FLR 294; 1973 LabIC 1040; 1973 (2) LLJ 130; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 967; 1973 UJ 672

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Bombay Industrial Relation Act, 1946

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1947, s. 3(13)and 3(14)(e) - 'Employee' and 'Employer' relation - Employer (Mill) required by statute to maintain canteen - Canteen run by Co-operative Society - Status of employees - Employees of that society are employees of Mill - Appeal dismissed.
