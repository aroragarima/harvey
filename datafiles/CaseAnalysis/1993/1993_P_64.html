Siraj Ahmad Siddiqui v Prem Nath Kapoor

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, M.M. Punchhi, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 606; (1993) 4 SCC 406; AIR 1993 SC 2525; 1993 (22) ALR 375; JT 1993 (5) SC 300; 1994 (1) RCR(Rent) 153; 1993 (2) RentLR 443; 1993(3) SCALE 771; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 254; 1993 (2) UJ 741

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Framing Of Issues

Summary: Land & Property - U. P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 20(4) - (A) Meaning of expression, 'date of first hearing' - Suo motu appearance of defendant before Court - Court fixing date for filing written statement and still further date for hearing - Tenant paying full amount of arrears on date prior to date fixed for filing written statement - (B) Whether there is compliance with provisions of s. 20(4) - Held, (A) It is date on which Court proposes to apply its mind to determine points in controversy between parties to suit - (B) Compliance is there - Appeal allowed.
