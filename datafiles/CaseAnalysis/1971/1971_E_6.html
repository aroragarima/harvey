Messrs Bharat Barrel and Drum Manufacturing Company Limited and Another v Employees' State Insurance Corporation

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 41; (1971) 2 SCC 860; AIR 1972 SC 1935; 1972 (24) FLR 75; 1972 LabIC 753; 1971 (2) LLJ 647; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 867

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Insurance - Employees State Insurance Act, 1948, ss. 75 and 96(1)(b) - Rule making power - But s. 96(1)(b) does not empower Govt. to prescribe by rules a period of limitation for claim u/s 75 - Held, r. 17 framed by Bombay Govt. prescribing limitation for filing application u/s 75 is ultra vires s. 96(1)(b) of 1948 Act - Appeal dismissed.
