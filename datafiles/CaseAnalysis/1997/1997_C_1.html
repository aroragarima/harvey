Brooke Bond India Limited v Brooke Bond India Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2811; (1997) 10 SCC 362; AIR 1997 SC 1336; 1997 (140) CTR 598; [1997] 224 ITR 798; JT 1997 (3) SC 375; 1997(2) SCALE 448; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 524; 1997 (2) Supreme 728; [1997] 91 TAXMAN 26; 1997 (2) TLR 378

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 28 and 261 - Capital expenditure - Assesee issued shares to increase capital of company - It amounts to capital expenditure and not revenue expenditure - Held, increase in capital results in increase in capital base and also helps in business and profit making of company - Expenses incurred in that connection still retain its character of capital expenditure - Answered in favour of Revenue - Appeal dismissed.
