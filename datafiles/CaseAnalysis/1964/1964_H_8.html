Central Bank of India Limited v Hartford Fire Insurance Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 323; AIR 1965 SC 1288; [1965] 35 Comp Cas 378

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Contract Act, 1872, s.10 - Principles of construction of Deed - Insurance contract - Term therunder enabling company to terminate insurance of at any time - Determination of effect of surrounding circumstances on interpretation of provisions - Held, Company can terminate it at its will - Plain language cannot be changed by surrounding circumstances - Appeal dismissed.
