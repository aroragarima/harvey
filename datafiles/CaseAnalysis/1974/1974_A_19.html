Arvind Mohan Sinha v Amulya Kumar Biswas and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S.N. Dwivedi

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 492; (1974) 4 SCC 222; (1974) SCC (Cr) 391; AIR 1974 SC 1818; 1974 CRLJ 885; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 133

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Probation Of Offenders Act, 1958, s. 4(1) - Defence of India Rules, 1962, Part XIIA - Customs Act, 1962 - Gold Control (Licensing of Dealers) Rules, 1969, rr. 126A(d) and 126P - (A) Applicability 1958 Act to offences under Customs Act and those under Part XII-A of Defence of India Rules - (B) Scope and ambit of word 'Gold' u/r. 126-A (d) of 1969 Rules - Held, applicable - Covers not only gold which is lawfully possessed but gold in any form or shape and whether possessed lawfully or otherwise - Order accordingly.
