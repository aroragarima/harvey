Supreme Court of India

12 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 858; (1996) 3 SCC 15; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 1081; AIR 1996 SC 2384; AIR 1996 SCW 926; JT 1996 (2) SC 692; 1996 (2) MLJ 10; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 10; 1996(2) SCALE 103; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 422; 1996 (2) Supreme 567

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education

Keywords: Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 1993

Summary: Constitution of India, art.254; Medical Council Act, 1956, s.10A; Dr. M.G.R. Medical University Act, 1987, s.5(5), Proviso - Repugnancy between Central Act and State Act -Permission for establishment of college granted by Central Govt. - Further permission from State Government - Held, is not necessary.
