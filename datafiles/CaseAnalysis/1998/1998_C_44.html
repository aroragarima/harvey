U. P. State Cooperative Land Development Bank Limited v Chandra Bhan Dubey and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2070; (1999) SCC (L&S) 389; (1999) 1 SCC 741; AIR 1999 SC 753; AIR 1999 SCW 364; 1999 ALJ 463; 1998 (4) AWC 579; 1999 (1) ESC 325; JT 1998 (9) SC 81; 1999 (1) LLN 1081; 1998(6) SCALE 670; 1999 (2) SLR 576; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 659; 1998 (9) Supreme 535; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 296

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 12 and 226 - Uttar Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1966, s. 122 - Operative Land Development Bank Act, 1964, s. 30 - Bank - Amenability to writ jurisdiction - Petitioner Bank, though functioning as Society under Societies Act is constituted under 1964 Act - Provisions of Both Acts Provisions indicate state control - If amenable to writ jurisdiction - Held, bank is, instrumentality of State and employees had statutory protection - Amenable to writ jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
