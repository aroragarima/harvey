Municipal Corporation, Bilaspur and Another v Veer Singh Rajput and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2985; (1998) 9 SCC 258; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1178; 1999 (1) AWC 701; 1999 (1) CLR 16; 1998 (80) FLR 847; JT 1998 (7) SC 390; 1998 (2) LLJ 627

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Municipalities & Local Governments - Whether respondents are eligible for regularisation of services? - Held, if casual workers or daily-rated workers are now being appointed by appellant-Corporation, in fairness to respondents whose services were terminated on principle of 'last come first go', they should be considered for appointment on daily wages in preference to others by waiving age bar (if necessary) if they are otherwise qualified and eligible for post - Appeal allowed.
