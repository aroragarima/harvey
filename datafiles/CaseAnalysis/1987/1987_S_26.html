Helper Girdhiarbhai v Saiyed Mohmad Mirasaheb Kadri And Others.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28281; (1987) 3 SCC 538; AIR 1987 SC 1782; JT 1987 (2) SC 599; 1987 (1) RCJ 691; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 124; 1987 (2) RentLR 269; 1987(1) SCALE 1235B; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 289

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Family & Personal; Labour & Industrial Law; Rent Control

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel And Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 29(2) and 13(1)(e) - Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 4 and 6 - Tenancy - Subletting - Partnership firm - Certain partner bringing in as asset his tenancy of premises in which business carried on - (A) If amounts to subletting - (B) Only sharing of profit and no right to operate bank accounts put question on genuineness of partnership - (C) Revision - Powers of High Court - Lower court taking one of two views on evidence possible after evaluating evidence - Held, there is no subletting - No indication that partnership is not genuine - HC cannot substitute its own view - Petition dismissed.
