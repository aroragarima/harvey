State of Karnataka v Muniyalla

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 14; (1985) SCC (Cr) 50; (1985) 1 SCC 196; AIR 1985 SC 470; 1985 BBCJ 43; 1985 CRLJ 751; 1985 CRLR 2; 1984(2) SCALE 1015

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Wrong Section

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Transfer of Sessions case by Principal City Civil and Sessions Judge in exercise of power under s. 409 instead of s. 194 - Transfer not invalid - Recital of wrong section does not invalidate an order which is otherwise within power of authority making it - Appeal allowed.
