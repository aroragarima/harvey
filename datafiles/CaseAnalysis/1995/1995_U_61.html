Jai Narain And Ors. Etc. Etc. v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1986; (1996) 1 SCC 9; AIR 1996 SC 697; 1995 (4) CCC 356; 1996 (1) CLT 251; JT 1995 (9) SC 323; 1995(6) SCALE 664; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 769

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Emergency Provisions

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.4 and 17 - Acquisition of Land - Notification of - Invoking of urgency clause - Ground, for setting up sewage treatment plant on war-footing by the order of Supreme Court to control pollution - 'likely to be needed' - Held, means 'Certainly' and 'urgently' - Invoking the clause not illegal.
