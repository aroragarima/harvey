Union of India v B. S. Agarwal

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1556; (1997) 8 SCC 89; AIR 1998 SC 1537; JT 1997 (8) SC 290; 1998 (3) SLJ 50; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 327; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 2055

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Appointment - Requirement of residual length of service of two years - Interpretation of scheme for filling up posts of General Manager - Held, should be calculated from date of accrual of vacancy and not from date of appointment - Appeal disposed of.
