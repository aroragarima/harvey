State of Tamil Nadu and Others v Messrs Sanjeetha Trading Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 641; (1993) 1 SCC 236; AIR 1993 SC 237; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 695; 1992(2) SCALE 635; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 840

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Madras Essential Articles Control And Requisitioning (Temporary Powers) Act, Mysore Forest Act, 1900

Summary: Socio Economic - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 19(1)(g), 301 and 304(b) - Tamil Nadu Essential Articles Control and Requisitioning Act (Temporary Powers), 1949, s. 3 - Tamil Nadu Timber (Movement Control) Order, 1982, cl. 3 - Constitutionality of total ban imposed on movement of timber outside State u/cl. 3 of 1982 Order - To satisfy local requirement - Held, being a regulatory measure it is not violative of art. 19(1)(g), 301 and 304(b) - Appeal allowed.
