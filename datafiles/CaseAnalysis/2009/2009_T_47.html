Sr. Divisional Manager, O. I. Company Limited and v Target Plywood Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

07 July 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Mukundakam Sharma, Balbir Singh Chauhan

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 882; (2009) 7 SCC 759; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1388; 2009 (4) AWC 3295; 2009 (1) CCC 404; JT 2009 (9) SC 42; 2009(9) SCALE 147; [2009] 10 S.C.R. 356

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Insurance

Keywords: Investigator, Chartered Accountant, Re Insurance, Excess Amount Paid

Summary: Consumer Protection - Insurance - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Fire Insurance Claim - Stocks lying in godown destroyed in accidental fire - Claim of Rs. 81.33 lakhs made, including a sum of Rs. 79, 08, 314/- towards loss of stock - Surveyor appointed; final survey and assessment report; loss assessed at Rs. 62, 38, 655.76/- from which salvage value, and thereafter 50% of the amount was deducted for various alleged deficiencies, and assessed the loss of Rs. 28, 64, 560.38/- - Being unsatisfied, insurer appointed an investigator - Loss assessed as Rs. 61, 50, 990.15/-; deduction of Rs. 32, 08, 624/- made on ground that the purchases were not genuine, and further sum of Rs. 1, 61, 746/- was deducted on ground that these are purchases for which payment was not made/or the bill is tampered with etc.; thus stock position which was destroyed was of the value of Rs. 27, 80, 620.15/-; therefrom an amount of Rs. 5, 09, 535.00/- was deducted towards salvage of leftover stock - Thus total loss of stock was Rs. 22, 71, 085.15/- according to Investigator's report - Appellant/company approved the claim for an amount of Rs. 22, 46, 536/- - Aggrieved thereagainst, complaint filed before National Commission - Complaint was allowed, and held value of the stock destroyed by fire, to be Rs. 57, 29, 120.76 - Deduction of 50% by Surveyor was rejected as unjustified; Surveyor's assessment was relied upon by the Commission - Whether judgment of Commission, proper and justified? - Held, No - Relevant material/evidence on record, ignored - Held further, total loss of stock to be Rs. 57, 29, 120.76 out of which 35% of the amount to be deducted; thus total loss assessed at Rs. 37, 23, 928.49/- - Interest awarded - Directions issued - Appeal disposed of.
