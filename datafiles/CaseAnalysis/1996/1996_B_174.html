State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Bridge and Roof Company (India) Limited

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2303; (1996) 6 SCC 22; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 621; AIR 1996 SC 3515; AIR 1996 SCW 3626; 1996 ALJ 1805; 1996 (3) AWC 1708; JT 1996 (7) SC 395; 1996(6) SCALE 168; 1997 (104) STC 78; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 762; 1996 (6) Supreme 586

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Contract - Constitution - Arbitration and Concilliation Act, 1996 - Indian Contract Act - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Writ jurisdiction - Interference in contractual matters - Contract of improvement of road - Non-statutory - Arbitration clause - Stipulations for deduction of sales tax at time of payment and for price adjustment in event of change in tax rate - Govt. deducting certain amount on ground that tax rule has been reduced by composition order passed by Sales Tax Commr. - Writ petition to restrain Govt. from making deduction - Held, it is not tenable as contract was non - statutory and contained arbitration clause - Fact that question of interpretation of composition order is involved does not make writ petition tenable - Appeal dismissed.
