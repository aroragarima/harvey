Sukhbir Singh & Ors. v Brij Pal Singh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3006; (1997) 2 SCC 200; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 72; AIR 1996 SC 2510; 1996 (28) ALR 331; 1996 (3) CLT 116; JT 1996 (6) SC 389; 1996 (2) RentLR 393; 1996(5) SCALE 342; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 863; 1998 (5) Supreme 118

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 16(C) - 'Ready and willing to perform' - Plea is to and proof by way of Sub-Registrar's endorsement to effect that buyer was present in his office for having sale deeds executed and registered by seller - Buyer was ready and willing to perform his part of contract having necessary funds to pass on consideration - Held, such averments are in accordance with forms 47 and 48 of CPC, 1908 - Proof or averments that he also had ready cash with him - Not necessary.
