Badri Prasad and others v Union of India and others

Supreme Court of India

06 May 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 369; (2005) 11 SCC 304; AIR 2005 SC 2531; 2005 (2) CLR 899; 2005 (120) DLT 55; 2005 (105) FLR 1023; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 241; JT 2005 (11) SC 304; 2005 (3) LLN 19; 2005(4) SCALE 725; 2005 (5) SLR 62

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Labour & Industrial Law - Service - Reversion from Group 'C' post of Store man to Group 'D' post of Khalasi - Administrative Tribunal directed consideration for regularization after ascertaining minimum eligibility criteria for Group 'C' post and subjecting them to qualifying tests - Whether HC right in setting aside order of tribunal? - Held, appellants cannot be granted relief of regularizing their services on post of Store man/Clerk merely on basis of their ad hoc promotion from open line to higher post in Project or construction side - Entitled to claim age relaxation and advantage of experience for long period spent by them on a higher group 'C' post - Pay last drawn by them in group 'C' post shall be protected even after their repatriation to group 'D' post in their parent department - Appeal partly allowed.
