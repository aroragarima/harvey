Superintendent (Tech.I), Central Excise, I.D.D v Pratap Rai

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Jaswant Singh, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 487; (1978) 3 SCC 113; (1978) SCC (Cr) 371; AIR 1978 SC 1244; 1978 CRLJ 1266; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 613; [1988] 114 ITR 231; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 729

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Central Excise

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 122 and 128 - Appeal - Vacation of Order of Assistant Collector - On ground of non-observance of principles of natural justice - Determination of bar on jurisdiction of Assistant Collector to start proceedings afresh - Held, not barred - Appeal Allowed.
