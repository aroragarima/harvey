Smt.Shashi Agarwal v State Of Up And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 74; (1988) SCC (Cr) 178; (1988) 1 SCC 436; AIR 1988 SC 596; 1988 (1) AWC 571; 1988 (3) Crimes 184; 1988 (1) Crimes 542; 1988 CRLJ 839; 1988 CRLR 184; JT 1988 (1) SC 83; 1988(1) SCALE 40; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 593

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: communal harmony, communal violence, communalism

Summary: National Security Act, 1980, s. 3 - Detention on ground that detenu was in jail and was trying to come out on bail and there was enough possibility of him being bailed out - No materials on record to show that detenu, if released on bail, was likely to commit activities prejudicial to maintenance of public order - Held, detention order was illegal.
