Firm Sardarilal Vlshwanath And Ors. v Pritam Singh

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 296; (1978) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1978 SC 1518; 1978 (2) RCJ 591; 1978 (2) RCR(Rent) 589; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 111; 1978 UJ 617

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 106, 111(a) and 116 - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 13 - Tenancy - Need for notice to statutory tenant on expiry of lease by efflux of time - When tenant holding over and suit filed for ejectment - Held, tenant is not entitled to notice u/s. 106 of 1882 Act - Petitions dismissed.
