Harbanslal Jagmohandas & Anr. v Prabhudas Shivlal

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 201; (1977) 1 SCC 575; AIR 1976 SC 2005; 1977 (18) GLR 157; 1976 RCJ 756; 1976 RCR(Rent) 570; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 628; 1976 UJ 337

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 12(3)(a) and 12(3)(b) and 11(3) and 11(4) - Tenancy - Notice of termination - Ground, arrears of rent - Dispute as to standard rent - When to be raised - Dispute as to standard rent held, has to be raised latest before expiry of one month after service of notice - Appeal No.C. A. No. 282 of 1971 Dismissed; Appeal No. C. A. No. 2068 of 1971 Allowed.
