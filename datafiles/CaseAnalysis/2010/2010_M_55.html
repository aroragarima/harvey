Commissioner Of Central Excise, Visakhapatnam-Ii v M/S Ncc Blue Water Products Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

24 September 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

D.K. Jain, H.L. Dattu

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 786; (2010) 12 SCC 761; 2010 (258) E.L.T. 161; JT 2010 (10) SC 234; 2011 (5) RCR(Civil) 120; 2010(10) SCALE 170; [2010] 11 S.C.R. 741

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 3(1) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 9(2) and 173Q(1) - Duty Liability - Assessee (100% Export Oriented Unit), produced and sold 11,15,29,540 shrimp seeds and 48,365 Kgs. of shrimps in Domestic Tariff Area (DTA) without obtaining the permission of the Development Commissioner and without payment of Excise Duty - Show cause notice issued to assessee demanding a duty of Rs.1,83,46,493/- on the shrimp seeds, shrimps and fish and interest at 20% as being the duty evaded on shrimp seeds, shrimps and fish cleared - Penalty of Rs. 1,13,05,410/- was also imposed on assessee u/s. 11AC of the Act - Commissioner confirmed said demand with interest and penalty - On appeal, Tribunal reversed the order of the Commissioner and held that since goods were cleared to other exporters by the assessee, there was no duty liability as per s. 3(1) of Act - Hence, present appeal - Whether the sales of shrimps and shrimp seeds by the assessee in DTA, without requisite permission from the Development Commissioner, are to be assessed to Excise Duty u/s. 3(1) of the Act or under proviso to the said Section? - Held, SC in SIV Industries Ltd. vs. Commissioner of Central Excise & Customs 2000 INDLAW SC 491, held that goods having been sold without permission of the Central Government to debond the unit, the duty on the goods sold by the assessee was leviable under main s. 3(1) of the Act - Hence, duty of Central Excise on goods produced and removed by the assessee in the DTA without the approval of the Development Commissioner, would be payable u/s. 3(1) of the Act and not under the proviso appended thereto - Moreover, even if the stand of the revenue is accepted and shrimp seeds are classified under sub-heading 0301.00 of the Tariff Act, the rate of Excise Duty chargeable would be nil - Impugned order of Tribunal upheld - Revenue's appeals dismissed.
