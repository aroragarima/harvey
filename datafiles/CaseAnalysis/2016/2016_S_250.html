Shashi Gupta and another v State of Haryana and others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 520; AIR 2016 SC 4817; 2016 (117) ALR 455; JT 2016 (5) SC 588; 2016 (132) RD 621; 2016(5) SCALE 636

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013

Summary: Land & Property - Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, s. 24(2) - Lapse of Land Acquisition Proceedings - Appellants challenged land acquisition proceedings initiated against them before HC but were unsuccessful - Hence, instant appeals - Whether land acquisition proceedings initiated against appellants were lapsed.

Held, there was no stay on either possession or acquisition of lands of appellants after 12-8-2008 and there was no payment of compensation in accordance with law as declared by SC. Hence, appellants were not dispossessed five years prior to coming into force of the Act. Hence, it is declared that land acquisition proceedings initiated against appellants were lapsed in view of operation of s.24(2) of the Act. Appeals disposed of.
