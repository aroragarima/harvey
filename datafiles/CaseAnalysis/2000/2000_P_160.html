Chandrika Prasad v Pullo (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S. Saghir Ahmad, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 495; (2000) 4 SCC 227; AIR 2000 SC 1785; 2000 ALJ 1084; 2000 (40) ALR 268; 2000 (2) AWC 1559; JT 2000 (4) SC 354; 2000 (91) RD 340; 2000(3) SCALE 155; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1145; 2000 (4) SLT 390; 2000 (4) Supreme 459

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1951, ss. 20 and 21 - United Provinces Tenancy Act, 1939, ss. 3(22) and 3(23) - Adhivasi rights - Claiming of - Sub-tenant from mortgagee - Entitlement - Held, tenant does not include mortgagee - No entitlement.
