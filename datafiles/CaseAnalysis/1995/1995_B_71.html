The Municipal Corporation For City Of Pune And Anr. v Bharat Forge Co. Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1762; (1995) 3 SCC 434; AIR 1996 SC 2856; 1995 (3) Bom.C.R. 468; JT 1995 (3) SC 312; 1995(2) SCALE 245; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 716; 1995 (2) UPLBEC 740

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, Cantonments (Extension Of Rent Control Laws) Act, 1957, Cantonments Act, 1880, Cantonments Act, 1910

Summary: Cantonments Act, 1880 (Since repealed), s. 22; Cantonments Act, 1924, s. 60 - Cantonment area - Octroi duty - Imposition by municipality - Notification issued in 1881 providing for levy 'at rates then prevalent' under municipal Law - Held, reading as prevalent from time to time - Cannot be assailed on ground of impermissible delegation - Absence of agreement between municipality and cantonment Board inconsequential - Change in rate of tax already in operation - Objections not required to be invited.
