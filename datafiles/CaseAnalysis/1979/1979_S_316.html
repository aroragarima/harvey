Bishnu Deo Shaw v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 586; (1979) 3 SCC 714; (1979) SCC (Cr) 817; 1979 (16) ACC 175; AIR 1979 SC 964; 1979 CRLJ 841; 1979 CrLR(SC) 402; 1979 MLJ 565; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 355

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, ss. 354(3) and 361 - Murder - Capital punishment or life imprisonment - Homicide suspecting that deceased was not his son - High Court - Confirms death sentence - Ground, 'cruel and brutal' because accused showed no mercy - Held, mere use of such adjectives does not supply 'special reasons' - Death sentence liable to be set aside - Petition disposed off.
