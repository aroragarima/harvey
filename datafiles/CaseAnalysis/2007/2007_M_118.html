Cce Lucknow v M/S. Wimco Ltd

Supreme Court of India

05 October 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1178; (2007) 8 SCC 412; 2007 (217) E.L.T. 3; JT 2007 (11) SC 628; 2007(11) SCALE 619; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 560; [2009] 20 VST 240

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Appeal allowed, Tariff Item, Manufacturing Process, Levy Of Duty, Mis-Statement, Paper Boards

Summary: Indirect Tax - Rule 173-B of the Central Excise Rules, 1944 - Excise - Dutiability - Waste/scrap/parings of paper board which are generated during the process of manufacture of paper and paper board - CEGAT held that no duty was chargeable - Held, CEGAT has not dealt with the factual scenario in detail and has abruptly come to an abrupt conclusion that no manufacture is involved - Matter remitted for fresh consideration.
