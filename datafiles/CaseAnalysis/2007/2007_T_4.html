Madishetti Bala Ramul (D) By Legal Representatives v Land Acquisition Officer

Supreme Court of India

17 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 543; (2007) 9 SCC 650; 2007(5) ALL MR 904; 2007 (68) ALR 632; 2007 (3) AWC 2957; JT 2007 (8) SC 180; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 881; 2007 (3) RCR(Civil) 455; 2007(8) SCALE 184; 2007 (5) SCJ 367; [2007] 7 S.C.R. 222

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Oil Mills

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 54 - High Court held that Appellants are not entitled to additional market value and interest from the date of taking of the possession till the date of the award - Whether s. 25 of the Act will have any application in the fact of the present case? - Held, s. 25 of the Act merely prohibits that total amount of the award granted by the Collector cannot be reduced - S. 25 which has undergone an amendment in the year 1984, thus, merely lays down that the amount of compensation awarded by the reference court shall not be less than the amount awarded by the Collector, and in no circumstances the amount awarded by the Collector can be reduced - Direction that additional interest @ 15% per annum on the amount awarded in terms of award dated 02.01.1999 for the period 16.03.1979 till 22.12.1991, should be granted - Appeal disposed of.
