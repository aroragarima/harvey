Controller Of Estate Duty, Gujarat I,Ahmedabad. v M.A. Merchant Accountable Person Oflate Shri A.G Merchant

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 438; (1989) Supp1 SCC 499; AIR 1989 SC 1710; 1989 (77) CTR 177; [1989] 177 ITR 490; JT 1989 (3) SC 177; 1989(1) SCALE 1479; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 987; [1989] 44 TAXMAN 274

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Estate Duty Act, 1953, s. 59 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Estate duty - Reassessment - Provision of s. 59 providing for reassessment introduced by Amendment Act of 1958 - Retrospectivity - Not retrospective - Appeal dismissed.
