Chaman Lal And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of Haryana Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28500; (1987) 3 SCC 113; (1987) SCC (L&S) 175; AIR 1987 SC 1621; 1987 (3) ATC 779; 1987 (54) FLR 612; JT 1987 (2) SC 135; 1987 LabIC 1299; 1987 (2) LLN 408; 1987(1) SCALE 761; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 923; 1988 (1) SLJ 1; 1987 (4) SLR 4; 1987 (2) UJ 246

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Central Bank of India (Officer) Service regulation 1979

Summary: Service - Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22 - Service Rules - Teachers acquiring higher qualification after joining in service - Whether entitled to the higher pay scales - Government act upon the principle in some cases and depart from it in other cases - Held, entitled to grant of higher grade admissible to Masters to all the teachers who have acquired the B.T./B.Ed. qualification with effect from the respective dates of their acquiring the qualification - Appeal allowed.
