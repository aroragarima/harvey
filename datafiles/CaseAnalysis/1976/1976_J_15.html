Timblo Irmaos Limited, Margo v Jorge Anibal Matos Sequeira and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 340; (1977) 3 SCC 474; AIR 1977 SC 734; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 451

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s. 73 - (A) Construction of Power of attorney - (B) Claim for demurrage or damages on the basis of agreement - Appellant breach obligations, that he would afford proper facilities for loading - Held, true meaning was the one which parties themselves understood i.e. one which included the power to sell iron ore - Held, respondent could not be made liable for delay in loading - Appeal allowed.
