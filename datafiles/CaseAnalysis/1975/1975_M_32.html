Mahmood v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 643; (1976) SCC (Cr) 72; (1976) 1 SCC 542; AIR 1976 SC 69; 1975 CRLJ 10; 1975 CRLR 614; 1975 UJ 875

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Fingerprints

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3 and 45 - Murder - Appreciation of cicumstantial evidence - Value of expert opinion - Held, on review the circumstantial evidence was found too shaky, suspicious and fragile to furnish a sound foundation for conviction - Appeal Allowed.
