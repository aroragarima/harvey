Parsuram Pandey and others v The State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

14 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

P.P. Naolekar, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 869; (2004) 13 SCC 189; (2005) SCC (Cr) 113; AIR 2004 SC 5068; 2004 CRLJ 4978; JT 2004 (9) SC 149; 2004(8) SCALE 783; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 475

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Defence and Security Forces - Practice and Procedure - IPC - All accused convicted and sentenced - Appeal against conviction - Held, appellant could not point out any prejudice being caused to accused / appellants on account of irregular, imperfect statement recorded under s 313 of the Cr P C - Accused are not entitled for any benefit for lacuna in recording statement of accused under s 313 of Cr P C - Some appeals allowed and some dismissed - Appeal dismissed.
