M/S. Pankaj Jain Agencies v Union Of India And Other

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 811; (1994) 5 SCC 198; AIR 1995 SC 360; AIR 1994 SCW 4552; 1994 (55) ECR 28; 1994 (72) E.L.T. 805; JT 1994 (5) SC 64; 1994 (3) RLT 903; 1994(3) SCALE 316; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 602

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s.25(1); Notification No. 142/ 86-Cus. D/13-2-1986 - Constitution of India, art.19(1)(g) - Compliance of - Notification published in official gazette - Prescribed mode adopted - Held, failure to make law known cannot be attributed - Notification acquires elements of operativeness and enforceability - There is no fundamental right to import.
