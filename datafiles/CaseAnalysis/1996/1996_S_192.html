Laxmikant And Ors. v Satyawan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3623; (1996) 4 SCC 208; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 132; AIR 1996 SC 2052; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 386; 1996 (2) CCC 15; 1996 (2) CLT 51; JT 1996 (3) SC 746; 1996(3) SCALE 30; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 532; 1996 (3) Supreme 114; 1996 (2) UJ 463

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land and Property - Contract & Commercial - Nagpur Improvement Trust Act, 1936, s. 89 - Nagpur Improvement Trust Land Disposal Rules, 1983, r. 4(3) - Condition that public auction will be completed only after confirmation letter is issued - Whether highest bidder get right to property when order staying auction was received before third round of bidding - Held, no right had accrued to the highest bidder either on the basis of the statutory provision u/r. 4(3) or under the conditions of the sale which had been notified before the Public auction - Appeal allowed.
