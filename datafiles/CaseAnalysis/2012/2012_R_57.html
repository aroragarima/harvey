H. P. Housing and Urban Devt. Auth. and another v Ranjit Singh Rana

Supreme Court of India

12 March 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, H.L. Gokhale

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 593; (2012) 4 SCC 505; 2012 (91) ALR 869; 2012 (3) AWC 2217; 2012 (2) CTC 445; JT 2012 (3) SC 626; 2012 (4) MahLJ 553; 2012 (3) MPLJ 336; 2012 (2) RCR(Civil) 488; [2012] 2 S.C.R. 427

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitrator and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 34, 37 - Arbitral award - Payment of interest - Extent - Parties entered into agreement for the construction of residential complex - Disputes were arose between parties, as per said agreement Arbitrator was appointed - Arbitrator passed award on 12-8-1998 - Aggrieved by said award, appellants filed objections u/s. 34 (3) of the Act - HC remitted matter back to Arbitrator for giving reasons in support of award - They deposited entire amount due under award before HC - Arbitrator again passed award on 14-2-2001, respondent started execution of said award - Ultimately HC rejected objections filed by appellant - Aggrieved by said, appellant filed intra Court appeal - HC held that respondent was entitled to post-award interest @ 18% p.a. from the date of award till the date of actual payment - Hence instant appeal - Whether deposit of entire award amount by appellants into HC amounts to payment to respondent and appellants were liability to pay interest @ 18% p.a. from the date of award ceased from that date - Held, there was no dispute that entire amount due under Award dt.14-2-2001 was deposited by appellants before HC on 24-5-2001 - In context of s. 37(1)(b) of the Act, payment means extinguishment of liability arising under the award - Deposit of award amount into the Court was nothing but a payment to the credit of decree- holder - In this view, once award amount was deposited by appellants before HC on 24-5-2001, the liability of post-award interest from 24-5-2001 ceased - HC, thus, was not right in directing appellants to pay interest @ 18% p.a. beyond 24-5-2001 - Impugned order of HC was modified and directed appellants shall be liable to pay interest @ 18% p.a. for post-award period from the date of award until 24-5-2001 - After 24-5-2001, appellants were not liable to pay any interest on the award amount u/s. 37(1) (b) of the Act - Appeal partly allowed.
