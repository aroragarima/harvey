Punjab Beverages Pvt. Ltd., Chandigarh v Suresh Chand And Anr

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, M. Hameedullah Beg, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 153; (1978) 2 SCC 144; (1978) SCC (L&S) 165; AIR 1978 SC 995; 1978 (36) FLR 383; 1978 LabIC 693; 1978 (2) LLJ 1; 1978 (1) LLN 665; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 370

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Labour Appellate Tribunal, Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, Demotion

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 33(2)(b) and 33 C(2) - Order of dismissal from service - Effect of contravention of s. 33(2)(b) of Act - Held,Order not rendered void ab initio and workman not entitled to maintain application u/s. 33-C(2) of Act - Appeal allowed.
