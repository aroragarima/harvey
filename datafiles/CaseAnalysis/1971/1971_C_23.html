Assistant Collector of Customs v Charan Das Malhotra

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 523; (1971) SCC (Cr) 321; (1971) 1 SCC 697; AIR 1972 SC 689; 1990 (28) ECR 439; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1477; 1972 (1) SCJ 579; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 802

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Keywords: Defence of India Rules, 1962, Order Of Extension

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 110(2), Proviso, 124(a) - Contraband Goods - Collector extended period for giving notice of confiscation u/s 124(a) - Without giving opportunity of hearing - Permissibility - Held, Collector cannot extend period for giving notice of confiscation u/s. 124(a) without giving opportunity of hearing to person whose articles are seized as contraband - Appeal dismissed.
