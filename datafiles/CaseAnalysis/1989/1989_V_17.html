Prathama Bank Head Office, Moradabad, Through Its v Vijay Kumar Goel And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 200; (1989) 4 SCC 441; (1989) SCC (L&S) 664; AIR 1989 SC 1977; 1989 (11) ATC 862; 1990 (1) BankCLR 69; [1990] 67 Comp Cas 71; 1989 (3) CompLJ 199; 1989 (59) FLR 601; JT 1989 (3) SC 432; 1989 LabIC 2016; 1989 (2) LLN 726; 1989(2) SCALE 350; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 935; 1989 (5) SLR 381; 1990 (1) UJ 169; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 68

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, Uttar Pradesh Public Service Tribunal Act, 1976

Summary: Service - Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunals) Act, 1976, ss. 2(b) and 6 - CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, ss. 3 - Constitution, art. 12 - Regional Rural Bank - Being an instrumentality of Central Govt., it comes in the perview of 'State' u/art. 12 - But its employees are not public servant - Disciplinary proceedings - Termination of the service - Employee filed civil suit challenging disciplinary proceedings and order terminating his service - (A) Whether he is barred from filing suit u/s. 6 of U.P. Public Services (Tribunals) Act, - (B) Whether the employee is entitled to Relief of reinstatement u/s. 3 of Regional Rural Banks Act - Suit held, is not barred by s. 6 - Relief of reinstatement is available to the employee of Regional Rural Banks - Appeal dismissed.
