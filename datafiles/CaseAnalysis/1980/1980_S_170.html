Mota Singh and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 391; (1980) Supp SCC 600; AIR 1981 SC 484; 1980 UJ 913

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Court Fees Act - Deficit of court fees - Number of truck owners joined as petitioners in a single petition to evade court fees - Held, each one of truck owners has his own cause of action arising out of liability to pay fees individually, petition of each one would be a separate and independent petition and each such person would be liable to pay legally payable court fee on his petition - Office to scrutinize afresh each one of cases referred to in office report and ascertain whether requisite court fee has been paid in each of them - Order accordingly.
