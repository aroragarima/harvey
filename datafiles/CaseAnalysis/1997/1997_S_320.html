M\S. R.S. Rekhchand Mohotaspinning & Ors Weaving Mills Ltd. v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1521; (1997) 6 SCC 12; AIR 1997 SC 2591; 1998 (2) Bom.C.R. 128; 1997(4) SCALE 339; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 238; 1997 (6) Supreme 29

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Parliament and Legislature - Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966, ss. 20 and 70 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Constitution, art. 246, sch. 7, list 2, entry 45 - (A) Interpretation of statutes - Legislative entry - (B) Levy of cess on flowing water by State u/s. 70 of 1966 Code - Legislative competence of State - Held, to be interpreted broadly and widely - Power of State is traceable to entry 45 of list 2 of Constitution - Appeal disposed of.
