V.M. Salgoacar And Brother Limited v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1768; (1995) 2 SCC 302; 1995 (1) CCC 111; 1995(1) SCALE 220; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 188

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4(1) - Determination of compensation - Whether appellant entitled to market value at Rs. 50 per square metre for land acquired on basis of small scale transactions? - It is true that in an appropriate case, potential value of land as on date of notification, realised or realisable price would form base and would be taken into consideration for determining compensation - That is to be considered in light of facts and circumstances of each case - Held, appellant having purchased property for construction of staff quarters and not for selling to third parties, sale instances and prices indicated therein are absolutely irrelevant for determination of compensation to acquired lands - Appeal dismissed.
