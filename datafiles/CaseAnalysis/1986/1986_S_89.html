Regional Director, Employees State Insurance Corporation,Mad v South India Flour Mills (P) Ltd. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 306; (1986) 3 SCC 238; (1986) SCC (L&S) 511; AIR 1986 SC 1686; 1986 (2) CCC 522; 1986 (52) FLR 682; 1986 LabIC 1193; 1986 (2) LLJ 304; 1986 (2) LLN 358; 1986(1) SCALE 1315; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 863; 1986 (3) SLJ 52; 1986 (2) SLR 597; 1986 (2) UJ 625

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Esi Corporation, Sickness Benefit

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, Pre., s. 2 (9) - ESI - Construction work of additional buildings for expansion of factories - Casual employees and Workers employed for construction , are employees in perview of the Act - Held, casual employees come under perview of Act and workers are employees u/s.2(9) of the Act - Appeal allowed.
