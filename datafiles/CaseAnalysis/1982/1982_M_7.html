Controller of Estate-Duty, Bihar v Mahant Umesh Narain Puri

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

Amarendra Nath Sen, R.S. Pathak, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 159; (1982) SCC (Tax) 91; (1982) 2 SCC 303; AIR 1982 SC 1153; [1982] 135 ITR 139; 1982(1) SCALE 395; 1982 UJ 231

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Family Property, Coparcenary Property

Summary: Estate Duty Act, 1953, ss. 2(16), 6, 7 and 64 - Properties belonging to Math - Actually granted by Moghul Emperor - Mahant in charge of properties as trustee - Death - Properties do not pass - Not liable to estate duty - Reference - Whether High Court can interfere with - Held, HC can interfere with finding on mixed question of law and fact - Appeals dismissed.
