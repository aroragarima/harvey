Surya Pal Singh v Siddha Vinayak Motors and another

Supreme Court of India

21 February 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Balbir Singh Chauhan, Jagdish Singh Khehar

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 634; (2012) 12 SCC 355; 2012 (4) AWC 3635

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, 1986-Section 2 (1) (g)-Deficiency in service-Under Hire Purchase Agreement, financier is real owner of vehicle and person who takes loan retain vehicle only as bailee/trustee-Therefore, taking possession of vehicle on ground of non-payment of instalment has always been upheld to be legal right of financier-Courts below committed error in granting compensation to petitioner-Impugned orders of courts below non-sustainable in law.
