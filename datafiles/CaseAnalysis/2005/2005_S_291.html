Popat and Kotecha Property v State Bank of India Staff Association

Supreme Court of India

29 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 512; (2005) 7 SCC 510; 2005 (6) ALD(SC) 27; 2005 (4) AWC 3869H; JT 2005 (12) SC 302; 2005 (99) RD 730; 2005(7) SCALE 3; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1030

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 6, r. 2(1), o. 7, r. 11(d) - Limitation Act, 1963 - Execution of lease deed - Suit dismissed as barred by limitation - Several other grounds not considered - Held, diverse claims were made and Division Bench was wrong in proceeding with assumption that only non-execution of lease deed was basic issue - Appeal allowed.
