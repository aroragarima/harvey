Â Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, R.S. Bachawat, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 421; AIR 1966 SC 740; 1966 CRLJ 608; 1966 (2) SCJ 549; [1966] 1 S.C.R. 709

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Land & Property

Keywords: Emergency Provisions, Defence Regulations

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32 and 352 - Defence of India Rules, 1962, r. 30(1)(b) - Whether order of detention u/art. 30(1)(b) is justified by the Act or Rules? - Held, detention order is illegal and cannot be justified - Whether petitioner can move Court u/arts. 21 and 22 in view of President's order u/art. 359(1) of Constitution, 1950? - Held, right to move Court granted u/art. 32 - Petition allowed and petitioner be set at liberty.
