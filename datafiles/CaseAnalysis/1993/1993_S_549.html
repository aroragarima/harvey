Atia Mohammadi Begum Smt v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 950; (1993) 2 SCC 546; AIR 1993 SC 2465; AIR 1993 SCW 2740; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 544; 1993 (1) RentLR 517; 1993(2) SCALE 167; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 295; 1993 (2) UJ 142

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, s. 2(o), Expln. (c) - Applicability - Vacant land - Use to be deemed as per master plan - For that, whether master plan should be in existence at time of commencement of Act? - Held, should be in existence - Appeal allowed.
