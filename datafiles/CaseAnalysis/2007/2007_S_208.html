New India Assurance Company Ltd v Smt. Shanti Pathak And Ors

Supreme Court of India

10 July 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P.K. Balasubramanyan, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 724; (2007) 10 SCC 1; 2007 (3) ACC 505; 2008 (1) ACC 45; AIR 2007 SC 2649; 2007 (4) AWC 3369; JT 2007 (9) SC 318; 2007 (6) MLJ(SC) 707; 2008 (149) PLR 784; 2007 (3) RCR(Civil) 593; 2007(9) SCALE 216; 2007 (5) SCJ 892; [2007] 8 S.C.R. 237

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Tribunal awarded a sum of Rs.4, 10, 000/- in favor of the respondents 1 and 2 applying multiplier of 17 - Appeal against - Held, it would be appropriate to take the multiplier of 5 considering the fact that the mother of the deceased is about 65 years at the time of the accident and age of the father is more than 65 years - Taking into account the monthly contribution at Rs.3, 500/- as held by the Tribunal and the High Court, the entitlement of the claim would be Rs.2, 10, 000/- - Appeal disposed of.
