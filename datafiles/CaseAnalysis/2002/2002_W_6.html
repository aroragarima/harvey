West Bengal Electricity Regulatory Commission v Central Excise S.C. Limited Etc

Supreme Court of India

03 October 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.N. Agrawal, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1151; (2002) 8 SCC 715; AIR 2002 SC 3588; 2003 (1) JhrCR(SC) 194; JT 2002 (7) SC 578; 2002(7) SCALE 217; 2002 (6) SLT 53; 2002 (7) Supreme 206

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy

Keywords: Finance Corporation Act, 1951, West Bengal Electricity Regulatory Commission (Appointment of Chairperson and Members Functions, Budget and Annual Report) Rules, 1999, Sick Industries Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1985, West Bengal Electricity Regulatory Commission (Conduct of Business) Regulations 2000

Summary: Electricity Regulatory Commissions Act, 1998 -- S 27-Powers of High Court while hearing appeal against fixation of tariff by the West Bengal Electricity Regulatory Commission -Right of Consumers to intervene in proceedings - Whether the High Court can re-determine the tariff and enhance the same? Nature of Powers of Regulatory Commission-Power to pass interim orders - Bias- Authority to determine the tariff- Comprehensive directions.
