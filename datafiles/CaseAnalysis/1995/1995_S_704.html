Sumati Dayal v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Bangalore

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, B.L. Hansaria, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 400; (1995) Supp2 SCC 453; AIR 1995 SC 2109; 1995 (125) CTR 124; [1995] 214 ITR 801; JT 1995 (3) SC 393; 1995(2) SCALE 490; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 1173; [1995] 80 TAXMAN 89

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(24), 68 and 69A - Whether assessee claiming receipts for unexplained money to be won from horse races is all five events said to be winning from Jackpot ? - Held, claim of assessee was false and based probability basis and 'receipt' challenged termed as income from undisclosed sources - Appeal dismissed.
