Institute of Chartered Accountants of India v Price Waterhouse and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2233; (1997) 6 SCC 312; AIR 1998 SC 74; [1997] 90 Comp Cas 113; JT 1997 (6) SC 607; 1997(5) SCALE 16; [1997] Supp2 S.C.R. 267; 1997 (7) Supreme 647; [1997] 93 TAXMAN 588

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Accounts & Audits - Chartered Accountants Act, 1949, ss. 21(4) and 30, chap. 1, Part 1, cls. (6) and (7) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Interpretation of regn. 16(3) of ICAI Regulations under Act - Harmonious construction of - Enquiry into professional misconduct - Report of disciplinary committee recording 'no guilt' - If council can still call further report from disciplinary committee - Held, can call further report - Appeal allowed.
