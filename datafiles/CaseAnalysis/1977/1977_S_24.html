V. Tulasamma and Others v Sesha Reddi (Dead) By L. Rs.

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 281; (1977) 3 SCC 99; AIR 1977 SC 1944; 1977 HLR 287; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 261

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Keywords: Rights Of Inheritance, Coparcener, Succession & Inheritance, Marriage & Divorce, Ancestral Immovable Property

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, ss. 14(1) and 14(2) - Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956 - Applicability of provisions of s.14 - Hindu female - Acquired property under compromise - For maintenance - Limited interest prescribed - Held, still s. 14(1) applies and not s.14(2) of the Act - Appeal allowed.
