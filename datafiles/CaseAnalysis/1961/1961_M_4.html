T. V. R. Subbu Chetty's Family Charities v M. Raghava Mudaliar and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 54; AIR 1961 SC 797; 1961 (2) MLJ(SC) 1; 1961 (2) SCJ 9; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 624

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Law - Land & Property - Whether ratification serves as a bar to reversioners right to challenge alienation, when reversioner is receiving a benefit under a family arrangement ? - Held, mere receipt of benefit does not preclude a presumptive reversioner from disputing validity of said alienation when he becomes actual reversioner - It must be always a question of fact as to whether conduct of said reversion, on which plea of ratification is based, does in law amount to ratification properly so called - Appeal dismissed.
