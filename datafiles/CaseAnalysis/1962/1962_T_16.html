Â Petlad Turkey Red Dye Works Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay, Ahmedabad

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Raghubar Dayal, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 417; AIR 1963 SC 1484; [1963] 48 ITR 92; [1963] Supp1 S.C.R. 871

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Civil Procedure - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 - Income-tax Act, 1922, s. 66 - Validity of order of High Court asking for supplemental statement and allowing evidence from appellate tribunal - Whether order of HC is judgement within meaning of s. 66(5) or s. 66 A(2) of 1922 act and so not appellable? - Held, HC was exercising its advisory jurisdiction and that it passed an interlocutory order calling upon tribunal to make a supplemental statement of case it cannot be said that it was a judgment against which an appeal was competent under provisions of s. 66 A(2) - HC has power to direct a supplemental statement to be made it has no power to direct additional evidence - HC had no jurisdiction to direct tribunal to submit a supplemental statement of case after taking additional evidence - Matter remitted back to HC.
