State of Madhya Pradesh and Another v State Bank of Indore and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20833; (2002) 10 SCC 441; 2001 (2) BC 527; [2002] 108 Comp Cas 622; 2001 (4) SLT 467; 2002 (126) STC 1; 2001 (7) Supreme 256

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Sales Tax - Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1958, s. 33C - Whether sales tax has priority over bank loans? - S. 33C creates a statutory first charge over any charge that may be in existence - Held, therefore, charge created in favour of state in respect of sales tax dues of second respondent prevailed over charge created in favour of bank in respect of loan taken by second respondent - Appeal allowed.
