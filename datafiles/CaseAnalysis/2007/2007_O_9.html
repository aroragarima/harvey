Ongc Ltd v Garware Shipping Corpn. Ltd

Supreme Court of India

14 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1156; (2007) 13 SCC 434; AIR 2008 SC 456; 2007 (7) AWC 7079; 2008 (1) Bom.C.R. 410; 2008 (1) CompLJ 471; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 113; 2007(13) SCALE 148; [2007] 11 S.C.R. 1154

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - Dispute pertains to cost of repairs and maintenance of the respondent's OSVs for the eleventh to the sixteenth year of their operation - Held, though there was some controversy as to whether the year referred to is the financial year as reimbursement was on year to year basis after receipt of the auditor's statement from SCI the norms obviously relate to financial year - No proposition that the courts could be slow to interfere with the arbitrator's Award, even if the conclusions are perverse, and even when the very basis of the Arbitrator's award is wrong - Appeal partly allowed.
