Unissi (India) Private Limited v Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research

Supreme Court of India

01 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1622; (2009) 1 SCC 107; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 407; 2009 (1) AWC 329; 2008 (4) JhrCR(SC) 100; JT 2008 (11) SC 89; 2008 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 3145; 2009 (1) MLJ(SC) 936; 2009 (2) MPLJ 498; 2008 (4) RCR(Civil) 678; 2008(13) SCALE 11; [2008] 14 S.C.R. 108; 2008 (3) UPLBEC 2774

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - Appeal against order dismissing the application filed by the appellant for appointment of an Arbitrator on the ground that no Arbitration Clause was in existence between the parties - Held, although no formal agreement was executed, the tender documents indicating certain conditions of contract contained an arbitration clause - Appeal allowed.
