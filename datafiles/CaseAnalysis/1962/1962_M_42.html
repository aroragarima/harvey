Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City I v Messrs. Shoorji Vallabhdas and Company

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 341; [1962] 46 ITR 144

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922 - Whether the sums involved are income of the previous year relevant to the assessment year? - Income tax is a levy on income - A mere book keeping entry cannot be income, unless income has actually resulted - Held, assessee firm to secure a long term managing agency arrangement changed the terms of the agreement and thereby reducing the income - Appeal dismissed.
