Union Of India v C.Rama Swamy And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 951; (1997) 4 SCC 647; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1158; AIR 1997 SC 2055; JT 1997 (4) SC 605; 1997 (2) LLN 619; 1997(3) SCALE 528; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 760; 1997 (2) SLR 584; 1997 (4) Supreme 158

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: All India Services (Death-cum-Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958, r.16A; Constitution of India, art.309; Evidence Act, 1872, s.115 - Change of date of birth of officer - Direct recruit, belonging to All India Service - Appointment prior to amendment - Change claimed after coming into force of rule - Held, he is entitled to seek change only in bona fide mistake in recording date of birth at time of employment - Estoppel applies.
