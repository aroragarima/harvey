Secretary To The Government, Transport Deptt. Madras v Munuswamy Mudaliar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 748; (1988) Supp SCC 651; AIR 1988 SC 2232; 1989 (11) ARBLR 50; 1988 (2) CCC 601; JT 1988 (4) SC 730; 1988(2) SCALE 1070; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 673; 1989 (1) UJ 217

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s.5 - Removal of the named arbitrator -Certain superintending engineer named as arbitrator in the contract with Govt. - Parties submitting to jurisdiction - Removal of his successor - Ground, he being subordinate to Chief Engineer will have bias against contractor - Held, not justified - Appeal allowed.
