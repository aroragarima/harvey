Auditor General Of India v G.Ananta Rajeswara Rao

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1354; (1994) 1 SCC 192; (1994) SCC (L&S) 500; AIR 1994 SC 1521; 1994 (26) ATC 580; 1994 LabIC 754; 1994 (2) LLJ 812; 1994 (1) LLN 566; 1993 (8) SLR 420

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 16(2) and 309 - Government of India (Office of the Auditor General), Office Memorandum No. 14014/1/77-Estt.(D), D/- 25-11-1978 - Restrictions on appointment on compassionate ground - Validity of provisions enabling appointment of 'near relatives' on compassionate grounds - Held, violative of art. 16(2) - However, provisions vis-a-vis appointment of son, daughter or widow entitled to be appointed , not violative of art. 16(2) - Appeal allowed.
