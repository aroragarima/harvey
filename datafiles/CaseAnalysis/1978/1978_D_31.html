Collector, Raigarh v Dr. Harisingh Thakur and Another and Vice Versa

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, R.S. Pathak, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 274; (1979) 1 SCC 236; AIR 1979 SC 472; 1978 (27) BLJR 48; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 183; 1979 UJ 41

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 133 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - (A) Land acquisition - Building sites - Test for determining potential value of land - For enhancement of compensation - (B) Documentary evidence - Land acquistion cases - Non examination of vendees or persons attesting sales - Mere statement of sale - Held, interference by Supreme Court - Held not admissible in evidence and could not be relied upon - Appeals dismissed.
