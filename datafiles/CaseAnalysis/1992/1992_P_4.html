Commissioner of Income Tax v P. L. Karuppan Chettiar

Supreme Court of India

11 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 705; (1993) Supp1 SCC 580; 1993 (109) CTR 317; [1992] 197 ITR 646

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Hindu Undivided Family - Income -Assessment - Exemption - Whether income from properties was assessable in hands of assessee Hindu Undivided Family - Held, income from properties was not assessable in hands of assessee Hindu Undivided Family - Order accordingly.
