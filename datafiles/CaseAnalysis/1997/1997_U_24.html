Union Of India & Another v G. Ganayutham

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 587; (1997) 7 SCC 463; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1806; AIR 1997 SC 3387; JT 1997 (7) SC 572; 2000 (2) LLJ 648; 1998 (2) SLJ 102; 1997 (5) SLR 298; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 549; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1649

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution Of India, Arts.309 and 16; Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, rr.9 and 3 - Power to withhold/ withdraw pension - Judicial review on disciplinary action - Held, pension includes gratuity - Gratuity can be withheld/withdrawn under r.9 - Judicial review of administrative or executive action - Permissible only if Court finds in violation of Wednesbury principle or CCSU norms - Court may remit back the matter to disciplinary authority.
