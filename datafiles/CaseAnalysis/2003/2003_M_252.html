Krishna Mohan Private Limited v Municipal Corporation of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 552; (2003) 7 SCC 151; AIR 2003 SC 2935; 2003 (5) ALD(SC) 83; 2003 (105) DLT 645; JT 2003 (6) SC 535; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 138; 2003(5) SCALE 515; 2003 (4) SLT 624; [2003] Supp1 S.C.R. 848

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Media & Entertainment; Municipalities & Local Governments; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, ss.113, 114, 115, 116, 169, 171, 247, 481, 483 - Delhi And Ajmer Rent Control Act, 1952 - Whether cost of plant and machinery installed in or upon a building is includible for purpose of arriving at reteable value of building - Whether ss.116(3), DMC Act, 1957 vests arbitrary and uncanalised discretion in Commissioner and is, therefore, invalid for excessive delegation of legislative powers - Held, cost of plant and machinery situate in or upon any land or building cannot be included in computation of rateable value of land and building unless a valid notification contemplated by s. 116(3) has been issued - s. 116(3) is declared invalid as it delegates unguided and uncanalised legislative powers to Commissioner to declare any plant or machinery as part of land or building for purpose of determination of rateable value thereof.
