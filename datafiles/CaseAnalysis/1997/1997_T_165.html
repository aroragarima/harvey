Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras v Thirumalaiswamy Naidu and Sons

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1479; (1998) 1 SCC 186; 1998 (146) CTR 529; [1998] 230 ITR 534; JT 1998 (4) SC 353; [1998] 98 TAXMAN 57

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, s. 41(1) - Deletion from taxable trading receipt of assessee - Sales tax from purchasers - Assessee had actually made payment of sales tax under provisions of Central Sales Tax Act - Held, any payment of sales tax made by assessee was liable to be deducted from profits made by assessee - Entire amount of sales turnover of assessee inclusive of amount of tax collected was clearly includible in assessee's taxable income - Tribunal was in error in deleting amount from trading receipt of assessee - Appeal allowed.
