Ramesh Mehta v Sanwal Chand Singhvi & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 281; (2004) 5 SCC 409; AIR 2004 SC 2258; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 274; 2004(5) SCALE 27; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 418

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Council Of States, Electoral College

Summary: [A.] Election - Municipalities & Local Governments - Rajasthan Municipalities Act, 1959 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 243R, 243R(2)(a)(i), 243R(2)(b), 243S(5), 54, 61 - Constitution (Seventy-Fourth Amendment) Act, 1992 - Rajasthan Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 2000 - Rajasthan Municipalities (Motion of No-Confidence against Chairman/Vice-Chairman) Rules 1974, rr. 3, 3(5), 3(8), 3(9) - Whether nominated members in a municipal board are to be counted for calculating majority required for carrying a no confidence motion against a chairman/vice-chairman of board - Held, chairman/vice chairman shall be removed on passing of no-confidence motion by requisite majority of members having right to vote - Appeal dismissed.
