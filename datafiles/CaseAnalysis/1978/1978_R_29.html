Ram Singh and Sons Engineering Works v Commissioner of Sales Tax, U.P.

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 265; (1979) SCC (Tax) 71; (1979) 1 SCC 487; AIR 1979 SC 545; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 621; 1979 (43) STC 195; 1979 TAXLR 1716

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 7 - Tests for determining whether contract is a contract of sale or contract for work and labour - Held, Contract for fabrication and erection of cranes on facts are contract for work and labour - Appeal allowed.
