Baradakanta Mishra v Mr. Justice Gatikrushna Misra

Supreme Court of India

21 June 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, D.G. Palekar, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 109; (1975) SCC (Cr) 99; (1975) 3 SCC 535; AIR 1974 SC 2255; 1975 CRLJ 1; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 524; 1974 (2) SLR 549

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Contempt Of Courts Act, 1971, ss. 19(1), 15, 17 and 20 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Appeal - Order of High Court rejecting motion made by a Judicial Officer - And refusing to initiate a proceeding for contempt against Chief Justice and other Judges - Order is not appealable u/s. 19(1) of 1971 Act - Supreme Court can however, in suitable cases interfere u/art. 136 - Appeal dismissed.
