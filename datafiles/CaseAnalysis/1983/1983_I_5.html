B.S. Minhas v Indian Statistical Institute & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 42; (1983) 4 SCC 582; (1984) SCC (L&S) 26; AIR 1984 SC 363; 1984 LabIC 15; 1984 (1) LLJ 67; 1984 (1) LLN 239; 1983(2) SCALE 574; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 395; 1984 (1) SLJ 217; 1983 (3) SLR 150; 1984 UJ 77

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 12, 32, 14 and 16 - Statistical Institute Act, 1959, ss. 2(c), 8, 11 and 12 - (A) Maintainability of writ petition u/art. 32 against Statistical Institute, an 'authority' in art. 12 of Constitution - (B) Statistical Institute Bye-laws, Bye-law 2 - Appointment of Director in breach of bye-law which requires that vacancy should suitably publicise before appointment - Held, is maintainable - Appointment is illegal - Petition allowed.
