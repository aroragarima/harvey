Ram Kishan v U. P. State Roadways Transport Corporation

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1096; (1994) Supp2 SCC 507

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Tort - Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Appellants' son died as a result of accident - Application for condonation of delay in filing for compensation rejected by Motor Accident Claims Tribunal - Held, keeping in view extreme shock under which appellants were labouring, MACT should have condoned delay and decided claim on merits - Appeal allowed.
