Â Jagannath Misra v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, P. Satyanarayana Raju

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 424; AIR 1966 SC 1140; 1966 CRLJ 817; 1966 (32) CutLT 897; 1966 (1) MLJ(SC) 168; 1967 SCJ 641; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 134

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Defence of India Rules, r. 30(1)(b) - Defence of India Act, ss. 3(2)(15) and 44 - Whether detention order issued u/r. 30(1)(b) of Defence of India Rules by govt. of Orissa had been passed on satisfaction of State govt.? - Held, detention order passed without application of mind - Petition dismissed.

ICPL HEADNOTE

The petitioner was detained by an order issued under r. 30 (1)(b) of the Defence of India Rules by the Government of Orissa on - He challenged his detention order - Whether the respective detention order was made after due application of mind?
