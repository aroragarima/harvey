State of Haryana and Others v Karnal Distillery Company Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 434; (1977) 2 SCC 431; AIR 1977 SC 781; 1977 UJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Renewal of licence - State filed instant appeal against order of HC granting stay of dispossession in favour of Distillery - Whether impugned order could is sustainable.

Held, Distillery in filing writ petition in HC for renewal of licence at Karnal Distillery misled the Court and started proceedings for oblique and ulterior purposes. Hence, appeal is accepted and HC order of stay of dispossession is set aside but no orders on application for initiating proceedings for contempt are being made. Appeal allowed.
