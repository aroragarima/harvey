Supreme Court Legal Aid Committee Through Its Hony. Secretary v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 591; (1991) 3 SCC 482; (1991) SCC (Cr) 639; 1991 (2) ACJ 1034; 1991 CRLR 657

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Human Rights - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 32 - Inhumane behaviour meted out to a person in police custody - Negligence of Havaldar has led to death of victim - Held, obligation of police particularly after taking a person in custody to ensure appropriate protection of person taken into custody including medical care if such person needs it - Compensation directed.
