Indian Performing Right Society Ltd v Eastern India Motion Pictures Association

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 185; (1977) 2 SCC 820; AIR 1977 SC 1443; 22 PTC 877; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 206

Case Digest

Subject: Intellectual Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Assignment Of Copyright

Summary: Intellectual Property - Copyright Act, 1957, ss. 14, 17 - Copy Right - Whether right of Music composer is capable of assignment and whether right can be defeated by producer of film by engaging him - Held, capable of assignment subject to the conditions u/ss. 18 and 19 of 1957 Act - Can be defeated as in s. 17 (b) and (c) of the Act - Order accordingly.
