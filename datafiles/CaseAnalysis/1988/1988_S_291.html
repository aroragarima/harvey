Ikramuddin Ahmed Borah v Superintendent Of Police, Darrang And Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 236; (1988) Supp SCC 663; (1989) SCC (L&S) 55; AIR 1988 SC 2245; 1988 (8) ATC 796; 1988 (57) FLR 622; JT 1988 (3) SC 814; 1989 LabIC 1224; 1989 (1) LLN 231; 1988(2) SCALE 1502; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 323; 1988 (6) SLR 104; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 323

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311(1)(2) - Dismissal of Sub-Inspector appointed by Principal, Police Training College, from Police Service by Superintendent of Police - Whether dismissal was in contravention of arts. 311(1) and (2) of Constitution? - Held, dismissal could not be held illegal having been passed by an authority subordinate to that by which he was appointed - Appeal dismissed.
