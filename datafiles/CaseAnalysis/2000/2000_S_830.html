Shivdev Singh and Another v Sucha Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 245; (2000) 4 SCC 326; AIR 2000 SC 1935; 2000 (40) ALR 218; JT 2000 (4) SC 177; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 8; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 765; 2000(3) SCALE 10; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 878; 2000 (3) SLT 441; 2000 (3) Supreme 94

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 60 - Right of redemption - Deed preventing or hampering redemption - Validity - Substantial extent of land mortgaged for a paltry sum - Redemption stipulated after 99 years - Clog on redemption, whether - Held, is question of fact - Such term has to avoid - Redemption is a statutory right - Deed hampering or preventing it anyway - Is void.
