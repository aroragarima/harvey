S. Kandaswamy Chettiar v State of Tamil Nadu and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 354; (1985) 1 SCC 290; AIR 1985 SC 257; 1985 (1) MLJ(SC) 11; 1984(2) SCALE 933; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 398; 1985 UJ 491

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution of India, 1950, art.14 - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 29 - Grant of exemption from provisions of 1960 Act - Constitutionality of total exemption granted to buildings owned by religious public trusts and public charitable trusts - Held, not violative of art. 14 - Appeal dismissed.
