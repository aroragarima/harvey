Jawahar Lal Singh v Naresh Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28717; (1987) 2 SCC 222; (1987) SCC (Cr) 347; AIR 1987 SC 724; 1987 BBCJ 75; 1987 (1) Crimes 472; 1987 CRLJ 698; 1987 CRLR 287; 1987 CrLR(SC) 287; JT 1987 (1) SC 388; 1987(1) SCALE 284; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 220

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Charge of Offence u/s. 396, IPC,1860 - Sessions Court on considering evidence discarded evidence acquitting all accused persons - High Court without examining reasons on basis of which Sessions Court discarded evidence, dismissed leave petition and appeal in limine and without passing reasoned order - Held, case remanded to HC for consideration - Direction to HC to dispose off matter after hearing parties and giving reasons for conclusions in accordance with law.
