Â Khem Chand v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, J.C. Shah, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 78; AIR 1963 SC 687; 1963 (1) LLJ 665; [1963] Supp1 S.C.R. 229

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Question of validity of r. 12(4) of Central Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1957? - Held, r. 12(4) does not contravene any of constitutional provisions - Public servant is deemed to be placed under suspension from date of original order of dismissal - Appeal dismissed.
