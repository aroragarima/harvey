Calcutta Municipal Corporation and Others v Messrs Shrey Mercantile Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 178; (2005) 4 SCC 245; AIR 2005 SC 1879; JT 2005 (3) SC 143; 2005(3) SCALE 20; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 584; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1941

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Calcutta Municipal Corporation Act, 1980, Calcutta Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1988, Calcutta Corporation (Taxation) Regulations, 1989

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Indirect taxes - Calcutta Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1988 - Mutation - Whether imposition for process of change in name of owner in assessment books of corporation is in nature of a fee or tax? - Held, quantum of levy indicates that it is a tax and not a fee -Mutation enquiry is instituted in interest of corporation for tax purposes and not for benefit of tax payer - Levy is irrational, arbitrary, discriminatory - Appeals dismissed .
