Supreme Court of India

03 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.2, items 3 and 6 - Termination of service - Plea of loss of confidence in employee - Stigma, if - Held, amounts to stigma -Termination thereon is irrespective of nature of employment, government servant or a workman, but then procedural safeguards are different - Appeal dismissed.
