Jaipur Development Authority v Radhey Shyam

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1351; (1994) 4 SCC 370; JT 1998 (4) SC 416; 1994 (1) RentLR 574; 1994(2) SCALE 243; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 1; 1994 (1) UJ 746

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Rajasthan Land Acquisition Act, 1953, ss. 4(1), 11, 18, 23, 26(2), 31(3) and (4) - Land Acquisition - An awarded made - Possession of acquired land was taken and reference made u/s. 18, Civil Court confirmed award - Writ Petitions for grant of land in lieu of compensation and respondents filed an execution to enforce award passed by term Civil Court - Executing Court partly upheld objection but on revision by respondents Division Bench allowed revision saying that it is not permissible for appellants to raise objection in execution of award - Whether Collector has power under any provision of Act to award land in lieu of compensation from acquired lands or any lands? - Held, s. 31(4) of Act indicates that Land Acquisition Officer has no power or jurisdiction to give any land under acquisition or any other land in lieu of compensation sub-s. (4) though gives power to him in matter of payment of compensation, it does not empower him to give any land in lieu of compensation - Appeal allowed.
