Gyarsi Rai and Others v Dhansukh Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 219; AIR 1965 SC 1055; 1966 (2) ALT(SC) 9; 1976 (2) ALT(SC) 9; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 154

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Transfer of Property Act, s. 76 (h) - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 34, r.5 (1) - Whether preliminary decree passed in suit debars mortgagors from claiming that mortgagee has to account for profits realized by him from mortgaged properties in his possession; if he is not so debarred, what is period for which mortgagee could be compelled to render accounts in respect of said profits? - Held, though a mortgage suit would be pending till a final decree was made, the, matters decided or ought to have been decided by preliminary decree were final -Order modified.
