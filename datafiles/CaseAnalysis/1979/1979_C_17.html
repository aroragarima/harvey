Chote Lal and Another v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 533; (1979) 4 SCC 336; (1979) SCC (Cr) 972; AIR 1979 SC 1494; 1979 CRLJ 1126; 1979 CRLR 102; 1979 UJ 302

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 366, 325 and 323 - (A) Whether a mere finding that accused abducted woman is sufficient for sustaining conviction u/s. 366 of Code - (B) Legality of convictions u/ss. 325 and 323 simpliciter - When evidence of defence witness and other relevant circumstances not considered - Held, not sufficient and further finding that accused abducted woman for any of purposes mentioned in s. 366 necessary - Further held, miscarriage of justice, conviction liable to be set aside - Appeal allowed.
