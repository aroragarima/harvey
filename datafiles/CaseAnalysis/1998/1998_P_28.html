Patel Valmik Himatlal and Others v Patel Mohanlal Muljibhai (Dead) Through L.Rs

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 843; (1998) 7 SCC 383; AIR 1998 SC 3325; 1998 (4) CLT 2; JT 1998 (6) SC 59; 1998 (2) RCJ 371; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 256; 1998(4) SCALE 714; 1998 (7) SLT 165; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 126; 1998 (7) Supreme 28

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Framing Of Issues

Summary: Rent Control - Land & Property - Civil Procedure - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s.29(2) ; Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Tenancy - Eviction decree - Revision - Reappraisal of entire evidence by HC - There was no error of law or material irregularity - Different view is possible is a sufficient ground for exercise of revisional jurisdiction - Sustainability of Order - Held, Order of HC cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
