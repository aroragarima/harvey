The Special Land Acquisition Officer, Davangere v P. Veerabhadarappa Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 263; (1984) 2 SCC 120; AIR 1984 SC 774; 1984 (42) CTR 357; [1985] 154 ITR 190; 1984(1) SCALE 37; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 386; 1984 UJ 231

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Land and Property -Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Proper multiplier - Whether there has been any error in principle or in law in the method of valuation arrived at by the courts below in adopting 'fifteen' to be the multiple for computation of capitalized value of certain agricultural lands acquired in the years 1971 and 1972 - Method of capitalising return - Determination of multiplier - Held that the multiple of 12 1/2 should be applied in computation of the capitalized value of the lands - Appeal allowed.
