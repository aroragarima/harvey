Northern India Caterers (India) Ltd. v Lt. Governor Of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 110; (1980) 2 SCC 167; (1980) SCC (Tax) 222; AIR 1980 SC 674; 1980 (17) DLT 191; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 650; 1980 (45) STC 212; 1980 TAXLR 1657; 1980 UJ 494

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, art. 137 - CPC, 1908, O. 47, r. 1 - Supreme Court Rules, 1966, O. 40, r. 1 - Extent of power of Supreme Court to review - What constitute error apparent on face of record - Impuned decision based on undisputed facts - Held, no review on ground that certain alternative situations were not placed before Court - Petition dismissed.
