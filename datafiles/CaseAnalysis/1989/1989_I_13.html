India Cements Ltd v Collector Of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 849; (1989) SCC (Tax) 348; (1989) 2 SCC 676; AIR 1989 SC 1496; 1989 (22) ECC 284; 1989 (25) ECR 477; 1989 (41) E.L.T. 358; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 137; 1989(1) SCALE 1058; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 715; [1989] 45 TAXMAN 55; 1989 (2) UJ 142

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Civil Procedure - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 37 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 11 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 35 - Excise duty - Claim for refund - Levy of duty on price of packing material - Letter against, given by manufacturer to authorities - Refusal to refund - Costs - Held, was in nature of protest - Issue of limitation would not arise for refund of duty - Manufacturer entitled to refund of amount with interest @ 6% from date of refusal of refund with costs - Appeal allowed.
