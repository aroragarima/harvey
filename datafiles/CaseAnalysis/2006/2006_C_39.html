Chairman, A.P. State Electricity Board and Others v M. Kurmi Naidu

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 483; (2006) 8 SCC 62; 2006 (6) ALD(SC) 83; 2006(9) SCALE 177; 2006 (7) SCJ 327; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 34

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - APSE Board Employees Discipline Appeal Regulations 1990 - Respondent was working as Assistant Engineer - Charge of release of unauthorized agricultural pumpsets by violating set norms - Chairman of Board, inflicted punishment of compulsory retirement from service - Competancy of chairman to impose punishment - Whether respondent was deprived of his right of appeal? - Held, all grievances laid by respondent in detail in his appeal memo dated 1/10/1995 was considered by highest hierarchy, namely Board, and was rejected - No prejudice whatsoever has been caused to respondent - Appeal allowed.
