Â Mathra Parshad and Sons v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, J.L. Kapur, J.C. Shah, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 39; AIR 1962 SC 745; 1962 (13) STC 180; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 913

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Constitution - East Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, ss. 4, 5, 6, 7, 10 and 27 - Punjab Tobacco Vend Fees Act, 1954 - Whether exemption in Notification issued on 27-09-1954, have effect from that date, or from beginning of financial year? - Notification did not fix date from which exemption was to operate, probably because Act omitted to make such provision, enabling State to do so, and exemption must, therefore, operate for whole year, during which it was granted - Held, Notification had effect from beginning of financial year - Appeal allowed.
