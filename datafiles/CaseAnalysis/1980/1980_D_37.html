Oil and Natural Gas Commission and Others v Dr. Mohd. S. Iskender Ali

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 338; (1980) 3 SCC 428; (1980) SCC (L&S) 446; AIR 1980 SC 1242; 1980 (40) FLR 479; 1980 LabIC 698; 1980 (2) LLJ 155; 1980 (2) LLN 165; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 603; 1980 SLJ 591; 1980 (2) SLR 792

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311(2) - Termination simpliciter - Probationer appointed in temporary post after dropping enquiry against him - Work not satisfactory - Termination if attracted art.311? - Held, it cannot be said that order of termination attracted provisions of art. 311 - It did not involve any stigma - Appeal allowed.
