Stridewell Leathers (P) v Bhankerpur Simbhaoli Beverages Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 482; (1994) 1 SCC 34; AIR 1994 SC 158; [1994] 79 Comp Cas 139; JT 1993 (5) SC 684; 1993(4) SCALE 7; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 645; 1994 (1) UJ 140

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Keywords: Companies (Amendment) Act, 1988, Companies Act, 1913

Summary: Civil Procedure - Corporate - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 20 - Companies Act, 1956, s. 10 F - Scope - Expression 'the High Court' in s. 10-F - Meaning of - Held, means HC having jurisdiction in relation to place at which registered office of company concerned is situate - Appeal allowed.
