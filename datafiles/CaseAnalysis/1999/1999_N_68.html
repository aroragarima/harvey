Union of India and Others v No. 664950 Im Havildar/CLERK Sc Bagari

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1084; (1999) 3 SCC 709; (1999) SCC (L&S) 828; AIR 1999 SC 1412; 1999 (1) CLR 1089; JT 1999 (3) SC 124; 1999 LabIC 1561; 1999 (2) LLN 1007; 1999(2) SCALE 614; 1999 (2) SCJ 372; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 690; 1999 (3) SLJ 207; 1999 (2) SLR 775; 1999 (4) SLT 65; 1999 (4) Supreme 138

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Army Act, 1950, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - 'Officer' - Study leave - Grant of, only to Regular Commissioned Officers to exclusion of Clerical staff of Army - Held, not arbitrary - Cannot be challenged as violative of arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution.
