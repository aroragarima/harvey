Â Dattatraya Govind Mahajan & Ors. Etc v State Of Maharashtra & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 157; (1977) 2 SCC 548; AIR 1977 SC 915; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 790

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Constitution (Seventeenth Amendment) Act, 1964, Constitution (Thirty-Fourth Amendment) Act, 1974, Constitution (Thirty-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1975, Constitution (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1955, Maharashtra Agricultural Land (Celling on holdings) Act, 1961

Summary: Constitution - Land & Property - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 31A, 31B and 39 - Maharashtra Agricultural Land (Ceiling on holdings) Act, 1961, ss. 2, 3 and 4 - Whether impugned Act, in so far as it creates an artificial concept of family unit for fixing ceiling on holding of land by such family unit, is in conflict with second proviso to cl. (1) of art. 31A? - Held, land deemed to be held by family unit being in excess of ceiling limit applicable to family unit - Acquisition is of any land 'within ceiling limit applicable to him' - Hence no question of any violation of provision enacted in second proviso to cl. (1) of art. 31A - Appeals dismissed.
