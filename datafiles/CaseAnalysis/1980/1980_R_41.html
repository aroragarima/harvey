Manik Chand And Anr. v Ramachandra Son Of Chawriraj

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 306; (1980) 4 SCC 22; AIR 1981 SC 519; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1104; 1980 UJ 739

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Summary: Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956, s. 8 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 55(5)(b) - Family Law - Women and Child - Contract for purchase of immovable property - Power of Hindu guardian to bind minor in it - Held, it cannot be said that guardian would be binding the minor by his personal covenant - Contract entered into by the guardian on behalf of the minor is enforceable - Appeal Allowed.
