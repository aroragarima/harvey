Delhi Cloth and General Mills Limited and another v S. Paramajit Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, P.B. Sawant, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 429; (1990) 4 SCC 723; AIR 1990 SC 2286; 1991 (1) CCC 285; JT 1990 (4) SC 110; 1991 (1) RCJ 90; 1990 (2) RCR(Rent) 629; 1991 (1) RentLR 56; 1990(2) SCALE 774; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 218; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 218

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Jammu and Kashmir Houses and Shops Rent Control Act, 1966, s. 3(iii) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Constitution - Tenancy - Whether classification of tenants on basis of income u/s. 3(iii) of Act is discriminatory in nature? - Not discriminatory, it has reasonable relation to twin object of protecting economically weaker tenants and encouraging construction of new buildings - Appeal dismissed.
