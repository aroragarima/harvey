Life Insurance Corporation of India and Others v Jagmohan Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1264; (1998) 9 SCC 219; (1998) SCC (L&S) 528; 1999 (1) AWC 700B; 1998 (78) FLR 820; JT 1998 (7) SC 387

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Power of judicial review - Promotion - Whether single judge was justified in directing promotion of R1? - Held, appropriate direction of High Court would have been to direct appellants to consider case of R-1 for his promotion to post of Asst. Divisional Manager/Senior Branch Manager from date his juniors amongst respondents 5 to 67 in writ petition were promoted on same criteria on which those respondents were promoted - Direction of single judge modified - Appeal allo
