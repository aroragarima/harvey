Margaret Lalita Samuel v Indo Commercial Bank Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 283; (1979) 2 SCC 396; AIR 1979 SC 102; [1979] 49 Comp Cas 86; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 914

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Limitation Act, 1908, art. 57 and 115 - Constitution, art. 136 - CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 23 - Bank - Continuing guarantee bond - Suit to enforce - (A) Starting point of limitation u/art.115 - (B) Interference of SC u/art. 136, sought against wrong order of remand by HC - Limitation run from the date of breach u/art.115 - SC decline to exercise its discretionary power to interfere - Appeal partly allowed.
