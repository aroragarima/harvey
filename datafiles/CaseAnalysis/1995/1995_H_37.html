Mathunni Mathai v Hindustan Organic Chemicals Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1060; (1995) 4 SCC 26; AIR 1995 SC 1572; 1996 (1) BC 134; JT 1995 (4) SC 233; 1995 (1) KerLJ 695; 1995 (1) KLT 784; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 59; 1995 RD 334; 1995 (2) RentLR 172; 1995(3) SCALE 159; 1995 (1) SCJ 551; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 765

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, rr. 1(2) and 1(4) - Limitation Act, 1908 - Notice of deposit or manner of appropriation of decretal amount in court not given to decree holder - Intimation about manner of appropriation also not given - If deposit can be deemed to be towards principal - Held, deposit cannot be deemed to towards principal, only because deposit was made in pursuance of court order - Appeal allowed.
