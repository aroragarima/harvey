Union of India and Others v K. P. Tiwari

Supreme Court of India

06 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21176; (2003) 9 SCC 129; (2003) SCC (L&S) 1233; 2002 (1) CLR 215; JT 2001 (10) SC 264; 2002 (1) LLJ 672

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Dying in harness - Held, respondent has been appointed now and has been in service for more than five years - Not appropriate to disturb that state of affairs by making any other order resulting in uprooting the respondent from his livelihood - No interference in High Court order - Appeal dismissed.
