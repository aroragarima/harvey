Tamboli Ramanlal Motilal (Dead) By Lrs v Ghanchi Chimanlal Keshavlal (Dead) By Lrs. and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1008; (1993) Supp1 SCC 295; AIR 1992 SC 1236; AIR 1992 SCW 1170; 1992 (2) CCC 89; JT 1992 (2) SC 525; 1992(1) SCALE 548; 1992 (1) UJ 509

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 41 and 58 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Mortgage deed - Interpretation - Clauses of deed consistent with express intention of making transaction a conditional sale with option to repurchase - Whether document was not a mortgage by conditional sale - Held, document was not a mortgage - Appeal dismissed.
