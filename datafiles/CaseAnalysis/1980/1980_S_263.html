Nepal Singh v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 337; (1980) 3 SCC 288; (1980) SCC (L&S) 376; AIR 1980 SC 1459; 1980 (6) ALR 350; 1980 LabIC 747; 1980 (2) LLJ 161; 1980 (2) LLN 448; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 613; 1980 SLJ 711; 1980 (2) SLR 108

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 311(2) - Service matter - Order of temporary of Government Servant - Test to determine nature of Order - Whether penal in nature so as to attract art. 311 (2) - Held, if it is discovered on the basis of material adduced that although innocent in its terms the order was passed in fact with a view to punishing the Government servant, it is a punitive order which can be passed only after complying with art. 311 (2) - Appeal Dismissed.
