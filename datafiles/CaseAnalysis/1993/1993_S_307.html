Ravi Raman Prasad and Another v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 875; (1993) 2 SCC 3; (1993) SCC (Cr) 489; AIR 1994 SC 109; 1993 (1) CCR 74; 1993 (1) Crimes 709; 1993 CRLR 224; 1993 CrLR(SC) 224; JT 1993 (4) SC 526; 1993(1) SCALE 442; [1993] 1 S.C.R. 393

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 144 and 482 - Dispute as to possession and ownership of property - Abuse of process of Court - Proceedings u/s. 144 initiated - Sub-divisional Magistrate delivering possession of property to party obtaining actual physical possession thereof earlier in execution of eviction decree - Whether order passed by High Court directing possession of property to other party, is proper? - Held, HC order was passed on wrong appreciation of facts - Not proper - Appeal allowed.
