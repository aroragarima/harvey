Vijay Kumar Nigam (Dead) Through Lrs v State of Madhya Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1657; (1996) 11 SCC 599; AIR 1997 SC 1358; 1997 (1) ESC 618; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 223; 1996(8) SCALE 591; 1997 (1) SLR 17; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 544; 1997 (1) Supreme 183

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 311 - Whether non-supply of report of preliminary enquiry by Department conducted against delinquent police officer violates principle of natural justice? - Whether order of removal from service on ground of misconduct is proper? - Held, non-supply of preliminary enquiry report conducted against delinquent officer does not violate principles of natural justice - Dismissal of delinquent from service is proper on ground of misconduct which is proved by sufficient evidence of illegal gratification from organizer in gambling and also by that of co-accused.
