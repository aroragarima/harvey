A.V.R. And Co. And Ors. v Fairfield Cooperative Housing Societyltd. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 630; (1988) 4 SCC 408; AIR 1989 SC 81; 1989 (1) Bom.C.R. 325; JT 1988 (3) SC 780; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 427; 1988 (2) RentLR 650; 1988(2) SCALE 813; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 84; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 84

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Contract & Commercial - Land & Property - Rent Control - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 19 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 15 A, 5(4-A) and 28 - Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, ss. 91(1) and 91(b) - Eviction of member and his licencees from society's flat - Whether a violation of arts. 14 and 19? - Held, not tenable as leave and licence agreement produced by one licencee had expired - Other licencee did not produce any agreement - Dispute between Co-operative Society and non - Member claiming through member to be decided by Co-operative Court, does not offend arts. 14 and 19 - Appeal dismissed.
