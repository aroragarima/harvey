Commissioner Of Income Tax, Bombay v Rasiklal Maneklal

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 740; (1989) 2 SCC 454; AIR 1989 SC 1333; 1989 (2) CompLJ 341; 1989 (77) CTR 31; [1989] 177 ITR 198; JT 1989 (2) SC 16; 1989(1) SCALE 719; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 179; [1989] 43 TAXMAN 259; 1990 (2) TLR 219

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes -Income Tax Act ,1922 , s. 12 E - Capital gains - Amalgamation of companies - Issue of its shares by Transferee Company to shareholder of amalgamated company, in place of his existing shares - Whether constitutes exchange or relinquishment of capital asset ? - Held, neither exchange nor relinquishment - Appeals dismissed.
