State of Tamil Nadu and Another v M. Rayappa Gounder and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 412; (1971) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1971 SC 231; 1970 UJ 923

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Constitution - T.N. Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966 - Validity of s. 7 of 1966 Act - Section does not change the law retrospectively - However, it attempts to validate invalid assessments - And it also attempts to overrule decision of High Court - Held, section is invalid - Appeal Dismissed.
