K. K. Jerath v Union Territory, Chandigarh and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1586; (1998) 4 SCC 80; (1998) SCC (Cr) 809; AIR 1998 SC 1934; 1998 (2) CCR 95; 1998 (4) Crimes 118; 1998 CRLJ 2555; JT 1998 (4) SC 478; 1998 (2) RCR(Criminal) 418; 1998(2) SCALE 471; 1998 (4) SLT 46; 1998 (3) Supreme 318

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Socio-Economic; Tort

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 438 - Anticipatory bail - Refusal - Validity - Investigations by CBI and administrative authorities - Accusation under provisions of Prevention of Corruption Act - Finding of High Court, acts of accused may obstruct investigation and may tamper prosecution evidence - And custodial interrogation would be more appropriate in such a case - Held, refusal to grant anticipatory bail on basis - Valid - Petition Dismissed.
