Hindu Singh and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 450; (1982) 3 SCC 368B; 1983 CRLR 527

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 324, 325 - Grievous hurt -

Held, in view of the fact that the parties are closely related SC grant them permission to compound the offences u/s. 324 and 325 of the Penal Code. Order of conviction and sentence is accordingly set aside and the accused are acquitted. Appeal disposed of.
