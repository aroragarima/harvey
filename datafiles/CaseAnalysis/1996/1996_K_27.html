K. Narasimha Rao v T.M. Nasimuddin Ahmed

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, N.P. Singh, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 594; (1996) 3 SCC 45; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 492; AIR 1996 SC 1214; 1996 (2) CCC 72; JT 1996 (3) SC 181; 1996 (2) RCJ 517; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 395; 1996 (1) RentLR 742; 1996(2) SCALE 570; 1996 (3) Supreme 381; 1996 (1) UJ 501

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - TN Buildings (Lease and Rent Control), Act, 1960, s. 7(2) - Eviction of tenant for wilful default in payment of rent - But advance paid, far in excess of arrears of rent - Landlord was bound to refund or adjust the advance, but not doing so - Whether tenant can be said to be wilful defaulter - Held, cannot be said so - Appeal dismissed.
