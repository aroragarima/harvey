Union of India and Others v Kaumudini Narayan Dalal and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 December 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 756; (2001) 10 SCC 231; 2001 (168) CTR 3; [2001] 249 ITR 219; 2001(4) SCALE 227; 2001 (6) SLT 52A; [2001] 117 TAXMAN 375

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 261 and 269 UC - High Court followed earlier judgment of same HC in Pradip Ramanlal Sheth [1992 Indlaw GUJ 90] - No appeal against that Pradip Ramanlal Sheth judgment - Not open to Revenue to accept Sheth judgment in case of assessee in that case and challenge its correctness in case of other assessees without just cause - Appeal dismissed.
