B. Desraj v C.I.T. Salem

Supreme Court of India

01 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. Sudershan Reddy

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1022; [2008] 301 ITR 439

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Trade

Keywords: Appeal allowed, Income Tax Act, 1961, Chargeable To Tax, Cash Assistance, Business Profits, Exports, Finance Act, 1990, Cash System, Gross Total Income

Summary: Whether Tribunal was right in holding that the deduction under s. 80HHC in respect of Duty Drawback and Cash Compensatory Support is allowable even though no export was done by the assessee during the assessment year 1991- 92 - Held, CBDT clarified that export incentives, namely, Cash Compensatory Support and Duty Drawback have to be included in the profits of the business for computing the deduction under s. 80HHC - Appeal allowed.
