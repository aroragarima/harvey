Uma Charan v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, V. Balakrishna Eradi, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 254; (1981) 4 SCC 102; (1981) SCC (L&S) 582; AIR 1981 SC 1915; 1982 (44) FLR 152; 1981 LabIC 1521; 1981 (2) LLJ 303; 1982 (1) LLN 5; 1981(3) SCALE 1246; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 353; 1981 (2) SLJ 252; 1982 (1) SLR 262; 1981 UJ 736

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Indian Police Service (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations, 1955, regn. 5(5) -

Constitution, arts. 14, 16, 226 and 311 - Service Rules - Member of State Police Service -Review of selection list - Supersession without recording reason by Selection Committee - Held, contravention of regn. 5 (5) of 1955 Regulations - Appeal Allowed.
