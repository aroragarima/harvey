The Haryana Urban Developmentauthority & Anr. v Roochira Ceramics & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 207; (1996) 6 SCC 584; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 240; 1996 (4) CCC 150; 1996 (67) ECR 693; JT 1996 (11) SC 54; 1996(8) SCALE 95; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 638; 1996 (8) Supreme 38; 1997 (1) UJ 368

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Sustainability, Allotment of plot, Non - payment of installments

Summary: Land & Property - Huda Act, ss. 17(3), 17 (4) - Allotment of plot - Non - payment of installments - Sustainability - Respondent was allotted an industrial plot and he paid 25% of price and balance in 6 installments - Respondent paid only first installment - Show cause notice was issued u/s. 17 (3) of the Act - On non appearance of respondent, plot was resumed u/s. 17 (4) of the Act and forfeited deposit amount - Aggrieved respondent preferred appeal and same was dismissed - On appeal before HC, HC allowed petition - Hence instant appeal.

Held, the respondent who had not responded to repeated notices could not be allowed to plead financial stringency for the first time before HC. No finding is recorded by the HC with regard to procedure adopted by the Estate Officer was either not in accordance with the statutory provisions or was in violation of the principles of natural justice. As an appellate authority the HC can only examine the procedural correctness but cannot go into the merits of the controversy. Appeal allowed.
