State If West Bengal And Others v Nuruddin Mallik And Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1126; (1998) 8 SCC 143; AIR 1999 SC 1466; AIR 1998 SCW 3989; 1998 (4) CLT 94; JT 1998 (6) SC 451; 1998(5) SCALE 327; 1999 (2) SLJ 258; 1998 (8) SLT 9; 1998 (7) Supreme 430

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Consititution - Constitution, art. 133 - Separation of power - Discretion of statutory authorities to make decision on staff pattern in a Madrasah - Request by Madrasah to Apex Court to make decision over the matter - Held, Court may either direct to statutory authorities to use their discretion - Or check its validity - But cannot substitute itself for that authority - Application disposed of and appeal dismissed.
