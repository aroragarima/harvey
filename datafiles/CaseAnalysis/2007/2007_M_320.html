Canteen Mazdoor Sabha v Metallurgical Engineering Consultants (I) Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 831; (2007) 7 SCC 710; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 758; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 784; 2007 (7) AWC 7534; 2007 (4) JhrCR(SC) 170; JT 2007 (10) SC 292; 2007 (3) LLJ 771; 2007 (6) SCJ 429; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 210; 2007 (5) SLR 424

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10(2) - Whether the canteen employees are part of Mecon or not? - Held, Division Bench correctly approached the matter and found that since there is no master and servant relationship between the employees of the canteen and Mecon, the workers of the canteen are not entitled to claim the salary which is given to the employees serving in the V.I.P.Guest House or Tea Club - Appeal dismissed.
