Jupudi Kesava Rao v Pulavarthi Venkata Subbarao And Others

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 526; (1971) 1 SCC 545; AIR 1971 SC 1070; 1972 (2) MLJ(SC) 5; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 590

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Stamp Act, 1899, ss. 2(14), 35, 36 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 91 - (A) Definition of 'instrument ' s. 2(14) - If covers copy of instrument as well - (B) Secondary evidence by way of oral evidence or copy of document - Insufficiently stamped - Admissibility - Held, does not cover copy of document for purposes of Stamp Act - In view of ss. 35 and 36 secondary evidence by way of oral evidence or copy of document insufficiently stamped is not admissible in a suit even though objection to its admissibility cannot be taken under Evidence Act - Appeal dismissed.
