M/s Natwar Parikh and Company Limited v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 524; (2005) 7 SCC 364; AIR 2005 SC 3428; 2006 (1) G.L.R. 6; JT 2005 (8) SC 39; 2005 (141) PLR 775; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 61; 2005(7) SCALE 91; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1100

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Indirect Tax - Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957, ss. 16, 3, 3(1), 4, 6, 7, 8 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 2(44), 2(46), 2(47), 41(4), 46, 66, 88(12) - Tractor-trailer - Denial of exemption u/s. 16 - Whether tractor-trailer was a distinct category of 'goods carriage' requiring permit u/s. 66? - Held, it is use of motor vehicle on given occasion which determines category of motor vehicle, whether it is adapted for that purpose or not -trailer falls u/s. 2(14) as a 'goods carriage' and consequently, it falls under definition of 'transport vehicle' u/s. 2(47) - Appeal dismissed.
