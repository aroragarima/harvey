Derasari K. G. and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1624; (2001) 10 SCC 496; 2001 (90) FLR 498; JT 1999 (10) SC 486; 2000 (2) LLJ 1572

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether Tribunal was justified in an application for contempt by issuing direction which tantamounts to review of its earlier decision? - Tribunal was not entitled in a contempt proceeding, to consider legality of its earlier order which has reached finality not being assailed or annulled by a competent forum - Not permissible for a Tribunal or Court to examine correctness of earlier decision which has not been assailed, and reverse its earlier decision - Held, impugned order of Tribunal, set aside - Appeal allowed.
