Dehri Rohtas Light Railway Company Limted v District Board Bhojpur And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1079; (1992) 2 SCC 598; AIR 1993 SC 802; AIR 1992 SCW 3181; JT 1992 (3) SC 573; 1992(1) SCALE 637; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 155; 1992 (2) UJ 26

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax

Summary: Constitution of India, art. 226 - West Bengal Cess Act, 1880, s. 6 - Condonation - Delay and laches - Dismissal of belated claim - Rule of law or Rule of practice - Held, is a rule of practice - Illegality in order - Demanding cess - Reason for not challenge in earlier proceedings explained - Delay condoned.
