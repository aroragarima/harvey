Amalgamated Coffee Estates, Limited, and Others v Their Workmen and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 42; 1965 (10) FLR 328; 1965 (2) LLJ 110

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Whether settlement was binding on respondents? - Held, notice was served to unions - No one appeared to object - Tribunal considered issue carefully - Settlement a fair one and should be binding on all estates - Appeals to be decided in accordance with settlement - Order accordingly.
