Utkal Contractors and Joinery Private Limited and Others v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28779; (1987) Supp SCC 751; AIR 1987 SC 2310; JT 1987 (4) SC 1; 1987(2) SCALE 639; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 314

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Orissa Forest Produce (Control Of Trade) (Amendment And Validation) Ordinance,

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Orissa Forest Produce (Control Of Trade) Act, 1981, s. 1(3) - Orissa Forest Produce (Control of Trade) (Amendment and Validation) Ordinance - Validity of - Held, Govt. refused to accept royalty from petitioners in respect of certain forest divisions on ground that notification had effect of rescinding existing contracts between Govt. and petitioners - Petitioners thereupon moved Orissa High Court with WPs for declaration that said notification was void and did not have effect of rescinding their contracts in relation to sal seeds - The Orissa HC dismissed WPs - Writ Appeals allowed by Supreme Court - Act was not concerned with sal seeds grown in Govt. lands or Govt. Forests - Purporting to Act, a notification D/- 9-12-1982 was issued by the State Govt. directing that the Act shall come into force at once in the whole of the State of Orissa in relation to sal seeds - Notification has been validated u/s. 5 of ordinance not withstanding any judgment, decree or order of any Court to contrary - It shall be deemed to have been issued in respect of sal seeds also grown or found in Govt. forests - It shall be valid and effectual as if it were issued u/s. 1(3) of Act as amended by ordinance - Appeal dismissed.
