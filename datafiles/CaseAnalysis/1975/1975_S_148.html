State of Gujarat and Another v Patel Chaturbhai Narsibhai and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 423; (1975) 1 SCC 583; AIR 1975 SC 629; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 284; 1975 UJ 156

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6, 39 and 40 - Land Acquisition (Companies) Rules, 1963, r. 4 - Land - Acquisition - For company - Enquiry u/r. 4 of 1963 Rules - Opportunity of being heard - Entitlement - Held, land-owner is entitled to be given opportunity of hearing at enquiry - Appeal dismissed .
