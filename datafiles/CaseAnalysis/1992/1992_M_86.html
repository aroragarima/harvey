Mir Fazeelath Hussain and Others v Special Deputy Collector, Land Acquisition, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 478; (1992) 3 SCC 239; 1992 (2) CCC 499; JT 1992 (4) SC 339; 1992 (2) MLJ(SC) 59; 1992(1) SCALE 1241; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 394

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23(2), 15, 18 and 28 - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, s. 30(2) - Government acquired land by notification - Compensation paid belt wise - Collector gave the award on 10-06-1968 - City Civil Court gave award on 30-08-1972 and the High Court decided the appeal on 24-06-1974 - Entitled to solatium at the rate of 15 per cent only on the enhanced amount of compensation - First belt at the rate of Rs. 5,000 per acre - Second belt at Rs. 4,000 per acre - Third belt at Rs. 3,000 per acre - Fourth, Rs. 2,000 - Fifth belt at Rs. 1,000 per acre and sixth belt at the rate of Rs. 500 per acre as it was a hillock - Issues - (A) Whether there is no distinction with regard to the land in dispute and compensation must be at least at the rate of Rs. 1.75 per sq. yard as earlier fixed by the High Court? - Court held, so far as the land comprised in four belts i.e. 1, 2, 3, and 4 the compensation ought to have been awarded at the rate of Rs. 1.75 per sq. yard uniformly -They are similarly circumstanced and in the fifth belt Rs. 2000 per acre - Sixth belt is proved on record that the area consists of hillocks, at the rate of Rs. 500 per acre - (B) Whether under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 as amended by the Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984 the claimants are entitled to solatium at 30 per cent of the market value irrespective of the dates on which the acquisition proceedings were initiated or the dates on which the award had been passed? - No - Benefit of the enhanced solatium to cases where the award by the Collector or by the Court is made between 30-04-1982 and 24-09-1984 or to appeals against such awards decided by the High Court and the Supreme Court whether the decisions of the High Court or the Supreme Court are rendered before 24-09-1984 or after that date - (C) Whether enhanced rate of interest should be paid on enhanced amount of compensation? - Court held, interest on the enhanced amount of compensation at the rate of 6 per centum per annum from 24-06-1968 the date of taking possession up to September 23, 1984 and at 9 per centum per annum from 24-09-1984 till the payment of a such amount in the court - Appeal partly allowed.
