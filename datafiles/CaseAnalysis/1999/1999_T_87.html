F.C.I., Bombay and Others v Transport and Dock Workers Union and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 504; (1999) 7 SCC 59; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1220; 2000 (33) LabIC 2114; 1999 (2) LLJ 1389; 2000 (3) LLN 527

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970, ss. 2(1)(a), 2(a) and 10 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - 'Appropriate Govt. ' - Definition in s. 2(1)(a) - Whether 'appropriate Govt. ' is a state Govt. or Central Govt.? - Held, although cases referred did not agree with view already expressed earlier that appropriate Govt. would be state Govt. and thus there is an obvious conflict of opinion on this question which needs to be resolved - Matter referred to larger bench.
