State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v Dr. Rahimuddin Kamal

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 923; (1997) 3 SCC 505; (1997) SCC (L&S) 827; AIR 1997 SC 947; 1997 (76) FLR 16; JT 1997 (2) SC 385; 1997 LabIC 852; 1997 (1) LLJ 889; 1997 (2) LLN 522; 1997(2) SCALE 1; 1997 (1) SCJ 282; 1997 (1) SLR 513; 1997 (2) Supreme 85

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Civil Services Conduct Rules, 1964

Summary: Prevention Of Corruption Act, 1947 - Whether the civil servant has committed misconduct and on that basis can be removed from his service, without consulting vigilance commission - Held, remaining absent from his duty for more than 5 days by a civil servant amounts to misconduct and order for his removal withot consulting vigilance commission is not illegal.
