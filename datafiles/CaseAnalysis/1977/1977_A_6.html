Additional Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat v Messrs Gurjargravures Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 95; (1977) 4 SCC 571; AIR 1978 SC 40; 1978 CTR 1; [1978] 111 ITR 1; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 169; 1977 UJ 765

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 84 and 251 - Profit exempt from income tax u/s. 84 - Assessee did not made any claim for, nor any material on record supporting such claim before ITO - Admissibility of claim raised for first time before Appellate Assistant Commissioner - Held, not entertainable - Appeal allowed.
