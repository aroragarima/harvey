Regional Director, Employees State Insurance Corporation, Trichur v Ramanuja Match Industries

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 347; (1985) SCC (L&S) 213; (1985) 1 SCC 218; AIR 1985 SC 278; 1985 LabIC 544; 1985 (1) LLJ 69; 1984(2) SCALE 815; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 119; 1985 (2) SLR 405; 1985 UJ 221

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, s. 2(9) - Interpretation of Statutes - Beneficial interpretation - Determination of whether partner of firm is an employee - Held, not an employee merely because he works for wages for partnership - Appeal dismissed .
