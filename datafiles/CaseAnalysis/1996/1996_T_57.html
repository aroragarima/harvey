G. Viswanathan v Hon'BLE Speaker Tamil Nadulegislative Assembly

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1136; (1996) 2 SCC 353; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 920; AIR 1996 SC 1060; JT 1996 (1) SC 607; 1996(1) SCALE 531; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 895; 1996 (1) Supreme 609; 1996 (1) UJ 325

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution of India, art.191(2), Sch.10, Paras 1(a), 2(1) - Disqualification - Member of House of Legislature - Expelled from his political party - Joining another political party thereafter - Declared as 'unattached member' - Held, such labelling is not recognised in Sch.10 - Disqualification.
