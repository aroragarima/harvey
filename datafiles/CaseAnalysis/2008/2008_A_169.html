Prem Nath Motors Limited v Anurag Mittal

Supreme Court of India

14 November 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1748; (2009) 16 SCC 274; AIR 2009 SC 567; 2009(2) ALL MR 951; 2008 (4) CPJ(Sc) 37; 2009 (1) CTC 190; JT 2008 (12) SC 307; 2008(14) SCALE 226; [2008] 16 S.C.R. 45

Case Digest

Subject: MRTP; Trade; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Trade Practice

Summary: Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1969 - Claim for the refund of the booking amount of a car - Held, s. 230 of the Contract Act categorically makes it clear that an agent is not liable for the acts of a disclosed principal subject to a contract of the contrary - No such contract to the contrary has been pleaded - Appeal allowed.
