Maghar Singh v Jashwant Singh

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2474; (1998) 9 SCC 134; 1999 (81) FLR 31; JT 1998 (7) SC 544; 1999 (3) LLJ 71

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923 - Whether appellant was injuried in course of employment or not? - Held, appellant was employed by respondent when he suffered injury while operating 'toka' machine of respondent and thus he is entitled to compensation - Appeal allowed.
