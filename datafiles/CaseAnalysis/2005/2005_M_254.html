State of Rajasthan v Messrs Nav Bharat Construction Company

Supreme Court of India

04 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 613; (2006) 1 SCC 86; AIR 2005 SC 4430; JT 2005 (9) SC 173; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 473; 2005(8) SCALE 372; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 988

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Reasoned Award, Lump Sum Award, Workmens Compensation Act, 1923

Summary: [A.] Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 11, 20, 30, 33 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Minimum Wages Act, 1948 - Award by arbitrator - Objections to award and claim for compound interest dismissed by HC - Appealed herein - Held, majority of claims are against terms of Contract - There are also other claims which are not against terms of Contract - Award can not be set aside merely on the ground of misconduct - Award set aside and matter refered back to an independent Umpire appointed by the Court - Appeal disposed of.
