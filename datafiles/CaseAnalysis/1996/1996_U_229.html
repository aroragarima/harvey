Namdi Francis Nwazor v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, B.N. Kirpal, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2644; (1998) 8 SCC 534; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1516; 1997 (1) CCR 27

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Narcotics - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 50 - Non-compliance of s. 50 - Appellants bag which was not in his possession at relevant time was found containing heroin - Appellant further alleged that he was not informed about his right to be searched as provided u/s. 50 - Whether non-compliance of provisions of search is fatal to prosecution case? - Held, no - When an article from which incriminating articles are found is not in possession of accused, it cannot attract requirements of s. 50 for reason that it was not found from person of accused - Question of compliance with s. 50 does not arise - Appeal dismissed.
