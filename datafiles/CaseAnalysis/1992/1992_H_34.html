Hindustan Construction Company Limited v State of Jammu and Kashmir

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 585; (1992) 4 SCC 217; AIR 1992 SC 2192; 1992 (2) ARBLR 412; JT 1992 (5) SC 325; 1992(2) SCALE 427; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 297; 1992 (2) UJ 750

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether setting aside of award on basis of error on face of it is proper - Whether grant of interest from date of award to date of decree or realisation of award is within jurisdiction of arbitrator - Held, setting aside award on ground of error apparent on face of award not proper - Grant of interest is permitted.
