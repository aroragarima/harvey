Viluben Jhalejar Contractor (D) By Lrs v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 278; (2005) 4 SCC 789; AIR 2005 SC 2214; 2005 (59) ALR 618; 2005 (2) AWC 1355B; 2005 (5) Bom.C.R. 331; 2005 (3) G.L.R. 2074; JT 2005 (4) SC 282; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 492; 2005(4) SCALE 97; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 542; 2005 (Supp) RD 824; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 2092

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 11, 18, 23(2), 28, 34, 4(1), 9 - Disputes relates to compensation - Held, having regard to entire facts and circumstances of case that interest of justice would be subserved if compensation is determined at rate of Rs. 160/- Per square meter for large plots and Rs. 175/- Per square meter for small plots - Claimants would be entitled to interest on solatium as said question is no longer res integra - Appeals disposed of.
