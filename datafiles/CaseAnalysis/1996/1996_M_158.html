State Of Maharashtra v Mahboob S. Allibhoy & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 819; (1996) 4 SCC 411; (1996) SCC (Cr) 675; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 194; AIR 1996 SC 2131; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 531; 1996 (2) CCR 207; 1996 (2) Crimes 130; 1996 CRLJ 2879; 1997 (69) ECR 249; 1996 (85) E.L.T. 22; JT 1996 (6) SC 151; 1996(4) SCALE 158; 1996 (3) SCJ 144; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 166; 1996 (4) Supreme 55

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, art.136 ; Contempt of Courts Act 1971, s.19 - Appeal for contempt of proceedings - Order dropping proceeding for contempt or refusing to initiate proceeding for contempt - Held, appeal against this order is not maintainable although person at whose instance proceeding has been initiated can move to Supreme Court under art.136 of Constitution.
