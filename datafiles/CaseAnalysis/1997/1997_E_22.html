Eugenio Misquita and Others v State of Goa and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 631; (1997) 8 SCC 47; AIR 1997 SC 3939; 1998 (2) Bom.C.R. 12; JT 1997 (8) SC 317; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 38; 1997(6) SCALE 328; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 362; 1997 (9) Supreme 125; 1997 (2) UJ 761

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1908 - Declaration u/s. 6 of Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Computation of limitation period - Which of the, date of publication of declaration in official gazette or date of last publication in series prescribed u/s. 6 (2), is relevant? - Held, only date of publication of declaration in official gazette is relevant date - Appeal dismissed.
