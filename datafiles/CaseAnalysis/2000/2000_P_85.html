State Of Andhra Pradesh v Polamala Raju & Rajarao

Supreme Court of India

09 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 453; (2000) 7 SCC 75; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1284; AIR 2000 SC 2854; AIR 2000 SCW 3009; 2000 (3) CCR 132; 2000 (3) Crimes 167; 2000 CRLJ 4014; 2000 (2) JCC 730; JT 2000 (9) SC 104; 2000 (3) RCR(Criminal) 732; 2000(5) SCALE 588; 2000 (6) SLT 305; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 329; 2000 (5) Supreme 558

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Women and Children - Criminal - IPC, 1860, s. 376(2) - Rape committed on child below 12 years - Sentence - Lesser than statutory minimum as exceptional cases - Appeallate Court reduced minimum prescribed sentence awarded by Trial Court without disclosing any special or adequate reasons - Held, not proper - Long pendency of proceedings, not an extenuating factor - Appeal allowed.
