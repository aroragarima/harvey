United Commercial Bank v Bank of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 353; (1981) 2 SCC 766; AIR 1981 SC 1426; [1982] 52 Comp Cas 186; 1981(1) SCALE 548; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 300; 1981 UJ 653

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 126 - Irrevocable letter of credit - Grant of injunction - Plaintiffs failed to establish that they would be put to an irreparable loss unless an interim injunction was granted - Interference by Supreme Court with such an interlocutory order - Held, normal banking transactions allowed to go forward - Appeal allowed.
