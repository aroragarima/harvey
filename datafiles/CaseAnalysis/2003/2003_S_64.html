Sanjiv Kumar v State of Haryana and others

Supreme Court of India

25 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1605; (2005) 5 SCC 517

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of Indian, 1950, art. 32 - Petitioner was an IAS officer of the year 1985 - In November 1999 about 4000 vacancies of JBT teachers were advertised by the Department of Primary Education, State of Haryana - Applications were invited and short-listed candidates were called for interview - At the time when the process of selections was initiated and the selections were held, by 'R' who was the Director, Primary Education - 'R' kept the original selection lists in a almirah and sealed the same - Key of the almirah was placed in an envelope which too was closed, signed and sealed - However, in spite of the process of selection having been completed, the selection lists were not released and made public - Later, petitioner was asked to take additional charge of the post of Director, Primary Education - It was alleged that the petitioner was pressurised for substituting another list of selected candidates in place of the lists originally prepared and preserved by 'R' - Petitioner refused to succumb to the pressure which was brought to build upon him by threats of dire consequences being administered to him and also to his family members - After some time, all the members of 19 District Selection Committees were called, fresh and false lists of selected candidates were got prepared and the members of the Committees were forced to sign such lists - Lists were handed over to the petitioner with a direction that such lists should be substituted immediately in place of the original lists - Petitioner opened the almirah which was sealed by 'R' and released the original select lists of candidates - Appointments have been made pursuant to this list - Thereafter, petitioner was transferred from his post - Petitioner filed instant petition u/art. 32 of the Constitution seeking directions for investigation and also protection for the life and liberty of himself and his family members - Held, in the peculiar facts and circumstances of the case, looking at the nature of the allegations made and the mighty people who were alleged to be involved, matter should be investigated by CBI - Directions issued - Petition disposed of.
