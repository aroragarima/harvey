S. Jamaldeen v High Court of Madras

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, A.M. Ahmadi, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1865; (1997) 4 SCC 30; (1997) SCC (L&S) 918; AIR 1997 SC 3780; 1997 (3) CLT 48; JT 1997 (4) SC 413; 1997 LabIC 1924; 1997 (2) LLN 658; 1997(3) SCALE 236; 1997 (2) SLJ 177; 1997 (5) SLR 391; 1997 (3) Supreme 468

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India,1950, arts. 226 and 309, Proviso - Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service Rules, 1995, r. 20, Proviso 3 - Seniority - Fixation - Direct recruited through Public Service Commission - Held, must be appointed to service only on date when they actually started discharging duties in view of rr. 2(b) and 2(10) - Cannot be placed in seniority list above officers in question who were already in service on that date on integration of their services - Though that date was later than date of selection of direct recruits by Public Service Commission.
