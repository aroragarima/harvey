Sprint Rpg India Limited v Commissioner of Customs-I, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

20 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 592; (2000) 2 SCC 486; AIR 2000 SC 749; 2000 (88) ECR 737; 2000 (116) E.L.T. 6; JT 2000 (1) SC 371; 2000(1) SCALE 300; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 332; 2000 (1) SLT 538; 2000 (1) Supreme 395

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975 - Notifications No 59/95 D/- 16-3-1995 - Whether customs duty on imported computer software loaded on a 'hard disk drive' is to be levied on basis of 'hard disk' simpliciter or 'computer software'? - What is imported is software on a container which is a hard disk drive - Therefore, it can be said that what is imported by appellant is essentially a computer software - Held, computer software imported by appellant on a hard disk drive is assessable at rate of 10% as per Heading 85.24 r/w exemption notification - Appeal allowed.
