Â D. S. Reddy v Chancellor, Osmania University and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah, K. Subba Rao

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 122; AIR 1967 SC 1305; 1967 MLJ 44; [1967] 2 S.C.R. 214

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education

Keywords: Constitutionality, Osmania University Act, 1959

Summary: Education - Constitution - Osmania University Act, 1959 ss. 5, 7 A, 12(1), (2), 13, 33A and 51 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Appointment of vice chancellor - Term of office - (A) Whether Act 1959 is ultra vires of art.14 of Constitution? - Held, classification of appellant, as a class by himself, is not founded on any intelligible differentia, which distinguishes him from other Vice-Chancellors and it has no rational relation to object of statute, s.5 of Second Amendment Act (Act XI of 1966), introducing s. 13 A in Act, is discriminatory and violative of art. 14 of Constitution and, as such, has to be struck down as unconstitutional - Appeal allowed.
