Governor General In Council v Musaddi Lal

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 505; AIR 1961 SC 725; 1961 (1) MLJ(SC) 169; 1961 (1) SCJ 597; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 647

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Practice & Procedure - Railways Act, 1890, ss. 77, 72 - Limitation Act, 1908, arts. 30 and 31 - Whether provisions of art.30 and 31 of Limitation Act, 1908 can be projected upon ss. 72 and 77 of Railways Act, 1890? - Held, no, arts. 30 and 31 of Limitation Act provide different points of time from which period of limitation is to run - It is not possible to infer that claim covered by either article is not for compensation for loss, destruction or deterioration of goods - Provisions of arts. 30 and 31 of Limitation Act, 1908 cannot be projected upon ss. 72 and 77 of Railways Act, 1890 - Appeal allowed.
