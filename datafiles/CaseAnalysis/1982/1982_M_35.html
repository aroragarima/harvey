Mrs. Saraswathi Seshagiri v State of Kerala and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 18; (1982) 2 SCC 310; (1982) SCC (Cr) 423; AIR 1982 SC 1165; [1983] 54 Comp Cas 394; 1982 CRLJ 1251; 1982 CRLR 252; 1982(1) SCALE 274; 1982 UJ 389

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3(1) - (A) Appeal against detention on ground that detenu might repeat his illegal act of export of indian currency in future and that his detention was necessary to prevent him from repeating same - Validity of detention - (B) Subjective satisfaction of detaining authority - Scope of interference by court - Held, detention was proper - Further held, Court cannot question whether grounds given in detention order are sufficient or not for subjective satisfaction - Petition dismissed.
