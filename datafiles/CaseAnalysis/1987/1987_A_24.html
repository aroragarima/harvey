Additional Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat, Ahmedabad v Mohanbhai Pamabhai and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28732; (1987) Supp SCC 704; (1988) SCC (Tax) 99; [1987] 165 ITR 166

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income tax & Direct taxes - Held, SC came to conclusion that in view of decisions taken in Sunil Siddharthbhai v. CIT and Kartikeys V. Sarabhai v. CIT, appeals must be dismissed - Appeals dismissed.
