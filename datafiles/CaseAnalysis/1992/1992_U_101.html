Umesh Chandra Misra v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 232; (1993) Supp2 SCC 210; (1993) SCC (L&S) 441; 1993 (24) ATC 243; 1993 (1) CCC 707; 1993 (2) CLR 17; 1993 (66) FLR 1015; 1993 (1) LLJ 187; 1992(2) SCALE 916; 1992 (5) SLR 608; 1992 (2) UJ 770

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Payment of Wages Act, 1936, s. 15 - Whether appellant is entitled to subsistence allowance during period between November, 1975 to February, 1977? - Held, appellant could not have been removed from service prior to 17-02-1977 which was date on which his appeal against his conviction by Sessions Court was dismissed by High Court, he is entitled to his subsistence allowance upto 17-02-1977 - Appeals allowed.
