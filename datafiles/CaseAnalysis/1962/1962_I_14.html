Tata Oil Mills Company, Limited v Its Workmen and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 73; 1963 (6) FLR 257; 1963 (2) LLJ 78

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Service Rule, r. 40(1) - Dismissal - Legality - Whether tribunal erred in concluding that enquiry held in respect of charges was unfair, and its conclusion that dismissal of respondent is wrongful and illegal? - Tribunal did not bear in mind limitations of its jurisdiction - Respondent was given enough time for explanation and consultation - Simply stating that evidence as unreliable would not make enquiry unfair - No averment nor any grounds in statement alleging that enquiry was unfair - Appellant entitled to terminate service by way of dismissal - Order of tribunal set aside - Appeal allowed.
