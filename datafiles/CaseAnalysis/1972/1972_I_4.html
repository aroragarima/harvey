Narain Das and Others v Improvement Trust, Amritsar and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 639; (1973) 2 SCC 265; AIR 1972 SC 865; 1972 UJ 696

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Orchard

Summary: Land & Property - Punjab Town Improvement Act, 1922, ss. 56, 24, 28(2) and 36 - Land under orchards - Application for exemption u/s. 56 - Held, competence of application for exemption u/s. 56 has never been questioned by respondent - Cannot be entertained by the court - Trust was bound to give reasons for holding as to why entire land of appellants was necessary for executing scheme - No provision of Act which imposes such an obligation on trust when coming to a decision u/s. 56 - Appellants conceded this point in High Court - Appeal dismissed.
