Supreme Court of India

18 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 617; (2004) 8 SCC 667; AIR 2004 SC 4360; 2004 ALJ 3433; 2004 (56) ALR 700; 2004 (4) AWC 2901; [2004] 122 Comp Cas 141; JT 2004 (6) SC 501; 2005 (141) PLR 540; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 239; 2004(7) SCALE 30; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 613; 2004 (2) UC 1273B

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - s. 166 - Goods vehicle - Accident - Liability to pay compensation - Whether insurer liable to make payment of compensation? - Tribunal held that insurer not liable to make payment of compensation and it was owner who has to make payment of awarded amount with interest @ 9% p a - Tribunal and HC right in holding that insurer not liable to pay compensation - Appeal disposed of.
