Raipur Manufacturing Company Limited, Ahmedabad v Nagrashna M. N. and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 47; AIR 1960 SC 407; 1959 (2) LLJ 837

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938, s. 42(2), item. (5) Sch. III - Payment of Wages Act,1936 - Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1947, s. 42(2) - (A) Whether authority under Payment of Wages Act has jurisdiction to decide application under Payment of Wages Act ? - Held, it had jurisdiction - View of authority on merits was correct - There was no failure of justice - Appeal dismissed.
