Ramesh Chand Daga v Rameshwari Bai

Supreme Court of India

16 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 192; (2005) 4 SCC 772; 2005 (59) ALR 640; 2005 (2) AWC 1115; 2005 (5) Bom.C.R. 354; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 122; JT 2005 (3) SC 476; 2005 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 146; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 749; 2005(3) SCALE 229; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 927; 2005 (2) UC 1098

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family and Personal - Practice and Procedure - Family - Judicial separation - Return of ornaments - HC directed payment of interest - Held, HC failed to notice that direction upon Appellant to pay interest was subject to condition that if he did not return ornaments, direction to pay interest on aforementioned amount of Rs. 3, 25, 655/ would operate - No interest could have been directed to be paid but what could be directed was payment of compensation - HC judgment not sustainable - Appeal allowed.
