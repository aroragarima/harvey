Masjid Kacha Tank, Nahan v Tuffail Mohammed

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 901; (1991) Supp2 SCC 270; AIR 1991 SC 455

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil procedure - Code of Civil Procedure,1908, s. 115 - Concurrent finding of Lower Courts - Re-appreciation of evidence and interference with findings by High Court on ground when findings of facts are perverse or there has been non-application of mind - Courts coming to conclusion that disputed building was old and in dilapidated condition and needed reconstruction - Order of eviction passed after inspection of building - Held, HC was wrong in interfering with findings of facts in its revision jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
