Shri Hanuman Cotton Mills and Others v Tata Air Craft Limited

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, I.D. Dua, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 449; (1969) 3 SCC 522; AIR 1970 SC 1986; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 127

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 74 - Contract for sale of scrap goods - Advance payment made - Buyer communicating regarding cancellation of contract for difference in quantity of goods on site - Forfeiture of earnest money - Whether the forfeiture of earnest money amounts to breach of terms of contract - Held, forfeiture as per the terms of the contract - No breach of terms of contract - Appeal dismissed.
