State of Haryana v Des Raj Sangar and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 300; (1976) SCC (L&S) 336; (1976) 2 SCC 844; AIR 1976 SC 1199; 1976 LabIC 849; 1976 (1) LLJ 301; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 1034; 1976 SLJ 222; 1976 (1) SLR 191

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Employment Under The State

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 - Abolition of Civil Service Post - On economical grounds - Held, decision whether a post should be retained or abolished is essentially a matter for Govt. to decide and if taken in good faith cannot be altered by High Court - Appeal allowed.
