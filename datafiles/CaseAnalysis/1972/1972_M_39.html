Workmen of Delhi Electric Supply Undertaking v Management of Delhi Electric Supply Undertaking

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 32; (1974) 3 SCC 108; AIR 1973 SC 365; 1972 (25) FLR 118; 1972 LabIC 1438; 1972 (2) LLJ 130

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 79 (c) - Factories Act, 1948, s. 2 (m) - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 3, item 2 - (A) Service Regn. No. 17 (a) under 1948 Act - Sub-stations and zonal stations not being 'factories', Technical Superintendents and Inspectors working therein - Entitlement to overtime payment - (B) Conveyance allowance - Rejection of claim of workmen for revision of allowance - Held, are not entitled for overtime pay - When a fairly high rise in motor-car allowance to higher officers has been given mere fact that any disturbance of allowance fixed for persons having scooters and motor-cycles will have repercussions on other categories of workmen is not a ground for rejecting claim of workmen for revision of allowance - Appeal allowed.
