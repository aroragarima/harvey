Â Sita Ram Goel v Municipal Board, Kanpur And others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Sudhi Ranjan Das, K. Subba Rao, S.K. Das, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 65; AIR 1958 SC 1036; 1959 ALJ 106; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 1148

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Municipalities & Local Govts - Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, ss. 326 and 58 - Limitation Act, 1908, s. 9 - Suit for wrongful dismissal - (A) whether suit was barred by limitation?- Held, suit was barred by limitation, as appellant failed to institute suit within prescribed time - Cause of action accrued to appellant moment resolution of board was communicated to him - (1)Whether time for such an application ought to be calculated from date of decision of last appeal?- Further held, time for an application in this particular case would be calculated from date, resolution of board was communicated and not from decision of last appeal - Appeal dismissed.
