Food Corporation of India v Messrs Thakur Shipping Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, P.K. Goswami, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 220; (1975) 4 SCC 815; AIR 1975 SC 469; 1975 (2) MLJ(SC) 31; [1975] S.C.R. 146; 1975 UJ 137

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Carriers & Transportation; Criminal; Water

Keywords: Legal Proceedings

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 34 - Stay of legal proceeding - Necessary conditions - 'Ready and willing' - Silence in face of repeated requests - Stay - Held, is improper - Court must satisfy itself that he is ready and willing to do everything necessary for proper conduct of arbitration also at commencement of proceedings - Appeals allowed.
