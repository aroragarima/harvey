Canara Bank v Canara Sales Corporation And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28893; (1987) 2 SCC 666; AIR 1987 SC 1603; 1987 (2) BankCLR 564; [1987] 62 Comp Cas 280; JT 1987 (2) SC 491; 1987(1) SCALE 924; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1138

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Trade

Keywords: Accountant, Privy Council

Summary: Banking and Finance - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 85 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Banker's liability is to be determined in regards to payment made by the banker to forged cheques - Whether plea of estoppel based on negligence of customer is available to the banker u/s. 85 of the 1881 Act and in the light of 115 of 1872 Act - The plea is not available - Appeal Dismissed.
