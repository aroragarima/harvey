Smt. Parayankandiyal Eravathkanapravan Kalliani Amma And Ors . v K. Devi And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2858; (1996) 4 SCC 76; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 333; AIR 1996 SC 1963; AIR 1996 SCW 2337; 1996 (3) CLT 27; 1996 (2) DMC 82; JT 1996 (4) SC 656; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 82; 1996(4) SCALE 131; 1996 (2) SCJ 645; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Tarwad, Polygamy, Kerala Joint Hindu Family System Abolition Act, 1975, Kerala Interpretation And General Clauses Act, 1925

Summary: Kerala Joint Hindu Family System (Abolition) Act, 1976, s. 7 - Madras Marumakkathayam Act, 1933, s. 5 - Kerala Interpretation and General Clauses Act, s. 4(b) - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Prohibition as to second marriage under Marumakkattayam Act - Repealed - Held, previous operation of said Act would not be affected - Conferment of status of legitimacy under Hindu Marriage Act s. 16 - Not ultra vires Constitution.
