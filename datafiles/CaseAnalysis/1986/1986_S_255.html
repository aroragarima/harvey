Prabhu Dayal Sesma v State of Rajasthan and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 622; (1986) 4 SCC 59; (1986) SCC (Cr) 411; (1986) SCC (L&S) 731; AIR 1986 SC 1948; 1986 (1) ATC 341; 1986 CRLR 512; 1986 (53) FLR 496; JT 1986 SC 256; 1986 LabIC 1928; 1986 (2) LLN 745; 1986(2) SCALE 362; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 665; 1987 (1) SLJ 207; 1986 (3) SLR 48; 1986 (2) UJ 713

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Minority, Adoption & Guardianship

Summary: Majority Act, 1875 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Rajasthan State and Subordinate Services Direct Recruitment by Combined Competitive Examinations Rules (1962), r. 11 B - Direct recruitment - Age limit for candidates specified - Mode for determination of age of candidate - Candidate attains specified age not on anniversary of his birthday but on day preceeding anniversary of his birth day - Person qualified for Rajasthan Administrative Service - Whether candidate exceeded age limit? - Held, candidate disqualified for recruitment - Legal day commences at 12 O' clock midnight - Exceeded one day - Appeal dismissed.
