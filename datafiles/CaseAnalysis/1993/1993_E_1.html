Food Corporation Of India And Ors. v E.Kuttappan

Supreme Court of India

21 June 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1187; (1993) 3 SCC 445; AIR 1993 SC 2629; 1993 (22) ALR 470; 1993 (2) ARBLR 266; 1993 (2) CCC 645; 1993 (51) DLT 175; JT 1993 (4) SC 90; 1993(3) SCALE 49; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 1028; 1993 (2) UJ 367

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act , 1940, s. 14(2) - (A) When award filed in Court can it be said to be filed on behalf of arbitrator? - (B) Objection to award - Limitation - Starting point - Party's counsel taking award before Court and Court accepting it - (C) Effect of notice issued to parties about filing of award before Court subsequently - Held, (A) When arbitrator has send award to party's counsel in response to specific request by party to give it for filing in Court - (B) Limitation qua such party runs from that date - (C) Fact that Court subsequently issued notice would be inconsequential - Appeal allowed.
