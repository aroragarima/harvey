Raiasthan Stat Eelectricity Board v Associated Stone Industries & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

08 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 400; (2000) 6 SCC 141; AIR 2000 SC 2382; JT 2000 (6) SC 522; 2000(4) SCALE 663; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 1179; 2000 (5) SLT 72; 2000 (4) Supreme 529

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Electricity and Energy - Rajasthan Electricity (Duty) Act, 1962, s. 3(3) - Whether pumping out water from a mine comes within the meaning of manufacture, production, processing or repair of goods as to claim exemption from duty under notifications issued under sub-s. (3) of s. 3 of the 1962 Act - Held, should be understood in its commercial sense and definitions in other legislatures cannot be applied - Not covered so not exempted - Appeal allowed.
