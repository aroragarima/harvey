State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Paras Nath

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1382; (1998) 2 SCC 412; (1998) SCC (L&S) 570; AIR 1998 SC 2612; 1998 LabIC 3103; 1999 (2) LLJ 454; 1999 (4) LLN 80; 1999 (1) MPLJ 172; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 31; 1998 (1) UPLBEC 742

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Govt. Servants Dying in Harness Rules, 1973 - Compassionate appointment on death of Govt. employee for immediate financial assistance to family as per Service Rules - Maintainability of application which is made seventeen years after employee's death - Held, cannot be allowed - Considerations of financial need cannot operate after a long period of time - Appeal allowed.
