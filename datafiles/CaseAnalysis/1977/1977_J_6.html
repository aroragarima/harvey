Cosmosteels Private Limited v Jairam Das Gupta and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 137; (1978) 1 SCC 215; AIR 1978 SC 375; [1978] 48 Comp Cas 312; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 422; 1978 TAXLR 1745; 1978 UJ 43

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 77, 101-104, 397, 398, 400, 402 and 406 - Notice regarding reduction of capital - In Petition u/s. 397 and 398 on ground of oppression of minority shareholders - (A) Appeal to Supreme Court - Court ordered reduction of share capital by buy back of shares valued on th basis of debts - Legality of order for not giving any notice to creditors before passing such order - (B) Notice regarding issued to Central Govt. - Appeal - Need of fresh notice - Held, order was not vitiated for not following procedure u/s. 101- 104 or for denying natural justice - Fresh notice is not necessary - Petition dismissed.
