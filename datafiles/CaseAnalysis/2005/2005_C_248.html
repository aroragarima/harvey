M/s. Birla Corporation Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 444; (2005) 6 SCC 95; 2005 (186) E.L.T. 266; JT 2005 (6) SC 550; 2005(6) SCALE 1; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 821

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Indirect Tax - MODVAT - Modvat credit - Whether duty paid on spares of ropeway used for purpose of transporting crushed limestone from mines located 4.2 kms away to factory, is entitled to Modvat credit? - Held, following J K Udaipur Udyog Ltd (2001 (130) ELT 996) and Pepsico India Holdings Ltd (2000 Indlaw CEGAT 2065), assessee was entitled to the Modvat credit - Respondent having taken a conscious decision to accept principles laid down in Pepsico India Holdings Ltd (a) cannot be permitted to take opposite stand in this case - Appeal allowed.
