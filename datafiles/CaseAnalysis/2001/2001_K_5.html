A. P. S. R. T. C. Cuddapah v K. Bajjanna

Supreme Court of India

06 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20592; (2002) 9 SCC 739; (2002) SCC (L&S) 1136; 2002 (1) AWC 432; 2002 (1) CLR 214; 2002 (92) FLR 206; JT 2001 (10) SC 254; 2002 LabIC 2471; 2002 (1) LLJ 460; 2002 (1) LLN 652; 2002 (1) SLR 521; 2002 (4) SLT 95

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Validity of order of termination - Reinstatement - High Court remanded matter to Labour Court to examine question whether another workman in similar circumstances had been reinstated with back wages - Labour Court directed reinstatement of respondent in service with full back wages - HC dismissed appeals - Held, fair and reasonable to order payment of full back wages as similarly placed workman was given relief of reinstatement with back wages - No reason why respondent should not have granted similar relief - Appeals disposed of.
