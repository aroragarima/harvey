The State Of West Bengal & Ors. v Sri Kartick Chandra Das & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2875; (1996) 5 SCC 342; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 395; AIR 1996 SC 2437; 1996 (3) CCC 114; JT 1996 (6) SC 626; 1996(5) SCALE 140; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 373; 1996 (5) Supreme 443

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Contempt Of Courts Act, 1971, s. 19 - Whether delay in filing Letters Patent Appeal against contempt notice by single Judge is condonable? - As s. 5 of Limitation Act does apply to appeals filed against order of single Judge for enforcement by way of a contempt - Held, condonable u/s. 5 of Limitation Act - Appeal allowed.
