Himalaya House Co. Ltd. Bombay v Chief Controlling Revenue Authority

Supreme Court of India

01 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 390; (1972) 1 SCC 726; AIR 1972 SC 899; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 332

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Stamp Act, 1899, s. 27 and art. 23 - Bombay Stamp Act, 1958 - Deed - Construction - Incorporation in a document terms and conditions of other documents - Failure to set forth consideration truly and fully u/s. 27 - Relevant matters for computation of value of consideration u/art. 23 of the Stamp Act - Held, mere reference to some earlier transactions in a document does not amount to an incorporation - Remdy - Value of consideration under conveyance deed or as disclosed upon an examination of the terms of the instrument as a whole left open are relevant - Appeal Allowed.
