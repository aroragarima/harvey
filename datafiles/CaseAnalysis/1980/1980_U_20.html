R.R. Verma And Ors. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 211; (1980) 3 SCC 402; (1980) SCC (L&S) 423; AIR 1980 SC 1461; 1980 LabIC 749; 1980 (2) LLJ 152; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 478; 1980 (2) SLR 335; 1980 UJ 627

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 16, 133 and 226 - All India Services (Conditions of Service-Residuary Matters) Rules, 1960, r. 3 - Service Rules - Power to relax rules u/r. 3 of 1960, Rules - If it renders r. 3 , unconstitutional - Review of order by Central Govt. - Writ petition dismissed as infructuous - Held, the r. 3 is not unconstitutional on ground that it vests unfettered discretion in Govt. - It can review though such power is not conferred by Rules - Certificate u/art. 133 cannot be granted - Appeal dismissed.
