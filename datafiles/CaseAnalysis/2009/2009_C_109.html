State of Madhya Pradesh v Chunnilal @ Chunni Singh

Supreme Court of India

15 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 509; (2009) 12 SCC 649; 2009 (3) JCC 2317; 2009 (2) KLT 335; 2009 (2) RCR(Criminal) 758; 2009(5) SCALE 710; [2009] 6 S.C.R. 145

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - IPC, 1860, ss. 376, 506 - Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989, ss. 3, 9 - Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules, 1995, r. 7Quashing the order framing charge - Held, when the offence complained are both under the IPC and any of the offence enumerated in s. 3 of the Act the investigation which is being made by a competent police officer in accordance with the provisions of the Code cannot be quashed for non investigation of the offence under s. 3 of the Act by a competent police officer - Order shall be restricted to the offence under s. 3 of the Act and not in respect of offences punishable under the IPC - Appeal partly allowed.
