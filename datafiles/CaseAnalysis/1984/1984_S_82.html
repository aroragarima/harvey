J. K. Bharati v State Of Maharashtra And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 170; (1984) 3 SCC 704; AIR 1984 SC 1542; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 201; 1984 UJ 1126

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Bombay Lotteries (Control and Tax) and Prize Competitions (Tax) Act, 1958

Summary: Constitution - Bombay Lotteries (Control And Tax) And Prize Competitions (Tax) Act, 1958, s.32 (c) - Constitution of India, 1950, art.14, sch. 7, list I, entry 40 - Exemption from applicability of the Act u/s. 32(c) granted to lotteries specially authorised by the State Govt. but not other States - What is presently in question in the writ petitions is the ban on the sale of tickets of lotteries authorised but not organized by the Govts. of other States - Held, not discriminatory - Petition dismissed.
