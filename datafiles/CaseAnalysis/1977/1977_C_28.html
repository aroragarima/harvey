Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat Iii v Kurji Jinabhai Kotecha

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 226; (1977) 2 SCC 719; AIR 1977 SC 1142; 1977 CTR 137; [1977] 107 ITR 101; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 26

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Forward Contracts

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 24(1) and 24(2)(i) - (A) Setting off and carry forward of losses - Loss incurred in hedging transactions - Of forward business in banned items - (B) Legal effect of s. 24(2)(i) of the Act on loss incurred in speculative transactions, in banned transactions, apart from hedging transactions - Held, cannot be set off against profits of other business of previous year - Assessee not entitiled to carry forward such loss to next year - Appeal Allowed.
