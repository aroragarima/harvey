Raj Narain Sarin (Dead) Thru Lrs. and Others v Laxmi Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 July 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 2597; (2002) 10 SCC 501

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151, O .7, r. 11 - Application under O. 7, r. 11, r/w s. 151, CPC for rejection of plaint allowed by ADJ and plaint rejected - High Court set aside order of rejection - Correctness of HC order challenged - Held, HC misread and misapplied provision in contextual facts, no explanation whatsoever is available on plaint as to situation under which suit was filed after about a long period of 40 years therefore HC fell in error - Litigation cannot but be termed to be utterly vexatious and abuse of process of court, more so by reason of fact that deed of sale being executed as early as 1941 stands unassailed for a period of over 50 years - Appeal allowed.
