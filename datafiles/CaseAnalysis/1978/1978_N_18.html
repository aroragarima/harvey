Badri Narain Choudhary & Ors v Nilratan Sarkar

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 255; (1978) 3 SCC 30; AIR 1978 SC 845; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 467

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Partition Act, 1893, ss. 2 and 3 - (A) Applicability of ss. 3(1) and 2 - Condition precedent - Request by shareholder for sale of property is sine qua non for directing sale - (B) Case not covered by ss. 2 and 3 - Power of Court to partition property by equitable method - Property incapable of division in specie - Held, Request lacking in instant case - Power not affected - Resort to process of owelty - Appeal allowed.
