Union Of India & Ors v M/S Upper Ganges Sugar & Industries Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

05 January 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 10; (2005) 1 SCC 750; AIR 2005 SC 778; 2005 (179) E.L.T. 277; JT 2005 (1) SC 137; 2005 (66) RLT 327; 2005(1) SCALE 106; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 140

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise and Salt Act - Rebates on sugar - Claim for Rs 27, 84, 474/- Whether respondents are entitled to interest on rebate? - Held, having already made a prayer and same not having been granted, it was not open to respondents to again make a claim for interest - In the absence of any provision in the contract or any statutory provision, interest could not have been awarded - Impugned Judgment cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
