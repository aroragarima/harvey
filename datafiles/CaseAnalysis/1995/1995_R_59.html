Bhoop Singh v Ram Singh Major And Ors

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1362; (1995) 5 SCC 709; AIR 1996 SC 196; AIR 1995 SCW 3927; 1996 (2) BC 365; 1996 (2) CLT 225; 1995 (2) HLR 432; JT 1995 (6) SC 534; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 38; 1995(5) SCALE 228; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 466

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17(2)(vi) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 23, r. 3, O. 12, r. 6 - (A) When instruments are required to be compulsory registrated - (B) Compulsory registration of decree or order of court - (C) Compromise decree - Need for registration - Held, compulsorily registrable if it creates new rights - Legal position qua cl. (vi) of s. 17(2) prescribed by court in this regard - Would require registration - Petition dismissed.
