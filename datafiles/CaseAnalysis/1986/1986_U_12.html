Andhra Re-Rolling Works, Hyderabad v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 310; (1986) Supp SCC 263; AIR 1986 SC 1964; 1986 (9) ECC 282; 1986 (2) ECR 340; 1986 (25) E.L.T. 3; JT 1986 SC 322; 1986(2) SCALE 1208; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 1001; 1987 (64) STC 139; 1986 TAXLR 2057; 1986 (9) UJ 279

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Central Excise Rules 1944 (contd)

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, item 26 AA(1) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 10 and 10 A - Excise Duty - (A) Exemption - Notification M.S. Rounds manufactured by re-rolling - Excisable under item 26 AA - Re-rolling of untested rails, an exempted article - Whether manufacturer of rounds is entitled to benefits of Notification No. 89/62 D/- 10-05-1962? - (B) Whether demand for duty was bad since it was made beyond period of three months which is time limit specified in r. 10 of Central Excise Rules, 1944? - Held, cannot get benefit of exemption notification - Further held, demand is covered by r. 10 A and not r. 10 and it is within limitation - Appeal dismissed.
