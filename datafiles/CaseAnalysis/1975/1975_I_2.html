Govind das and Others v Income Tax Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 341; (1976) SCC (Tax) 133; (1976) 1 SCC 906; AIR 1977 SC 552; 1976 CTR 192; [1976] 103 ITR 123; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 44; 1977 TAXLR 149

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 171(6) - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 25A - Provision of s. 171(6) of 1961 Act - Scope and retrospectivity - Assessment of HUF for A.Y. 1950-51 to 1956-57 - Completed u/s. 25A of 1922, Act - Determination of tax in reassessment proceedings - Permissibility of its recovery u/s.171(6) of the Act - (B) Whether words 'all the provisions of this Act shall apply accordingly' in s. 297(2)(a)(ii) can be construed as incorporating s. 171 (6) - Held, ITO not entitled to avail of the provision enacted in s. 171 (6) r/w s-s.(7) of the new Act for the purpose of recovering the tax - Held, cannot be construed as incorporating by reference 17(6) to make it applicable for recovery of the tax reassessed on the HUF in cases falling u/s. 297(2)(d)cl. (ii) - Appeals Allowed.
