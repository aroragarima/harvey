M. C. Chacko v State Bank of Travancore, Trivandrum

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 514; (1969) 2 SCC 343; AIR 1970 SC 504; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 658

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 100 - Specific Relief Act, 1877, s. 23 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 2(d) - (A) Banking and Finance - Creation of charge on immovable property - No particular form of words needed - But document must disclose intention to create charge - Family arrangement providing for discharge of personal debt of donor out of share of another - (B) Contract - Suit by strangers to contract - When maintainable - Family arrangement providing for discharge of personal debt of donor out of share of another did not create a charge on property in favour of the creditor - Held, the beneficiaries under the terms of the contract or where the contract is a part of the family arrangement may enforce the covenant - Order Accordingly.
