Santokh Singh Arora v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 441; (1992) 1 SCC 492; AIR 1992 SC 1809; 1992 (1) ARBLR 168; JT 1991 (6) SC 507; 1991(2) SCALE 1436

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether inclusion of fresh items is permitted after reference of matter for arbitration? - Whether grant of interest pendente lite from date of appointment of arbitrator to date of award valid? - Held, inclusion of fresh item is not permitted and interest pendente lite could be awarded.
