Guntur Municipal Council v Guntur Town Rate Payers’ Association

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 162; (1970) 2 SCC 803; AIR 1971 SC 353; 1971 (2) ALT(SC) 257; [1972] 83 ITR 473; 1971 (2) MLJ(SC) 7; 1970 RCJ 989; 1970 RCR(Rent) 967; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 423

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: T.N. District Municipalities Act, 1920, s. 82(2) - Land and Property - Annual value - Method of assessment of - Consideration of measure of fair rent - Held, measure of fair rent determinable under Rent Control Act must be taken into account - Order Accordingly.
