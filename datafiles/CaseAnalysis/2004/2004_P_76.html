Lalit Mohan Pandey v Pooran Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 381; (2004) 6 SCC 626; AIR 2004 SC 2303; 2004 ALJ 1770; 2004 (56) ALR 443; 2004 (3) AWC 1995; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 228; 2004(5) SCALE 267; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 737; 2004 (2) UC 729; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1385

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Election Dispute, Equality Of Votes, Council Of States, Electoral College

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Election - Uttar Pradesh Kshettra Samitis And Zila Parishads Adhiniyam, 1961, s. 237 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 243C, 243C(3) - Constitution (Seventy-Third Amendment) Act, 1992 - Local Government (Miscellaneous and Previous) Act, 1953, ss. 14(1), 14(2) - Uttar Pradesh Zila Panchayats (Election of Adhyaksha and Up-Adhyaksha and Settlement of Election Disputes) rules, 1994 - Application of 'Hare System' in Municipal Election - Election by Proportional Representation by means of single transferable vote by ballot - Appellant received highest number of first preference votes and in that view of matter he should have been declared elected.
