Everest Industrial Corporation And Others. v Gujarat State Financial Corporation.

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28328; (1987) 3 SCC 597; AIR 1987 SC 1950; 1987 (2) BankCLR 449; 1987 (2) CCC 567; [1987] 62 Comp Cas 513; 1987 (3) CompLJ 188; JT 1987 (3) SC 113; 1987(2) SCALE 75; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 607; 1988 (1) ShimLC 56; 1987 (2) UJ 348

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Bombay Court Fees Act, 1959

Summary: Civil Procedure - State Financial Corporations Act, 1951 - Proceedings u/s. 31 - Order u/s. 32 for sale of hypothecated property and appropriation of amount realised towards dues of corporation - Payment of interest - Applicability of s. 34 of Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Held, does not apply - Appeal disposed of.
