T.L. Muddukrishana and Another v Lalitha Ramchandra Rao Smt

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2546; (1997) 2 SCC 611; AIR 1997 SC 772; 1997 (30) ALR 183; 1997 (2) CalLT(SC) 56; 1997 (1) CLT 342; JT 1997 (1) SC 540; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 5; 1997 RD 205; 1997(1) SCALE 321; 1997 (1) SCJ 190; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 11; 1997 (1) Supreme 483

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Land & Property - Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 10 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 54 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6, r. 17 - Suit for specific performance with regard to agreement for sale of property - Suit for mandatory injunction directing respondent to comply with requirements in agreement - Whether rejection of application under O. 6, r.17 filed pendelite suit after three years limitation fixed under contract on ground that cause of action which was required to be stated initially in plaint was not pleaded be proper? - Held, proper - Appeal dismissed.
