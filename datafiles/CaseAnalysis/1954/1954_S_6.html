Sakhawat Ali v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1954

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1954 Indlaw SC 13; AIR 1955 SC 166; 1955 SCJ 262; [1955] 1 S.C.R. 1004

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Orissa General Clauses Act, 1937, Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act, 1922

Summary: Election - Oriss Municipal Act, 1950 - Bihar and Oriss Municipalities Act, 1922, ss. 16(1), 23 and 198 - Constitution of India ,1950, art. 14, 19(1)(g), 132(1) and 226 - Whether disqualificataion of candidate due to his employment as a legal practitioner on behalf of municipality justified? - Held, yes - It is a reasonable restriction - The object is to achieve purity of public life and to avoid a conflict between interest and duty - Such restriction is not violative of fundamental rights - Appeal dismissed.
