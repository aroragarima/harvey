Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996-Sections 11 (5) and (6)-Appointment of Arbitrator-Designated Judge fallen into error by opining that disputes raised are not "billing disputes", for same should have been left to be adjudicated by Arbitrator-Impugned order of High Court partly reversed.
