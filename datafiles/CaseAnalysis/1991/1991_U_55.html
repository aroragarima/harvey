Union of India and Another v Bal Ram Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 October 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Ranganath Misra, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 297; (1992) Supp2 SCC 136

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6 - Declaration u/s. 6 quashed - Whether acquisition of residential house on a part of property to be acquired is justified when purpose of acquisition is for providing housing? - Held, purpose for which acquisition had been initially notified not changed merely because by passage of time Delhi Development Authority came into existence - Further held, no difficulty for Delhi Development Authority if residence and vacant side appurtenant thereto are released from acquisition - Declaration is not open to challenge - Appeal allowed.
