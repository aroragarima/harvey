Calcutta State Transport Corporation v Md. Noor Alam

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 117; (1973) SCC (L&S) 414; (1974) 3 SCC 70; AIR 1973 SC 1404; 1973 (27) FLR 84; 1973 LabIC 1120; 1973 (2) LLJ 248; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 113

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33(2)(b), Proviso - Industrial Dispute - Whether dismissal, payment of wages and application for approval form part of same transaction - Test - Held, proviso to s. 33 (2) (b) contemplates three things to be simultaneous and to be part of same transaction - It is conduct of employer that has to be considered from point of view of finding it out - Appeal allowed.
