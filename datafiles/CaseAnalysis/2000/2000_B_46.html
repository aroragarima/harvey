Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Keywords: limitations of Actions Act, 1958.

Summary: Advocates and Judges - Contract Act, 1872, s. 171 - Bar Council of India Rules, 1961, rr. 28, 29 - Advocates Act, 1961, ss. 15, 35 - Advocate - 'Professional misconduct' - (A) Determination of attorney's lien over the litigation files -(B) Whether refusal of Advocate to return files to his client, amounts to misconduct - Held, no lien for his unpaid fees on files entrusted to by client as they are not 'goods bailed' or 'saleable goods' - Amounts to misconduct - Appeal disposed of.
