State of Maharashtra v Balram Bama Patil and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 February 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 414; (1983) 2 SCC 28; (1983) SCC (Cr) 320; AIR 1983 SC 305; 1983 (1) Crimes 481; 1983 CRLJ 331; 1983 CRLR 151; 1983 MLJ 474; 1983(1) SCALE 93

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 307 - Not necessary that bodily injury capable of causing death should have been inflicted - Injuries inflicted were in nature of simple hurt cannot be sustained - Held, not necessary, injury caused to be sufficient under ordinary circumstances to cause death of person assaulted - Sufficient if there is present an intent coupled with some overt act in execution - Appeal dismissed.
