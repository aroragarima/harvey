Municipal Corporation of Delhi and another v Sushila Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1168; (1999) 4 SCC 317; 1999 ACJ 801; AIR 1999 SC 1929; 1999 (36) ALR 587; 1999 (3) AWC 2294; JT 1999 (3) SC 567; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 1198

Case Digest

Subject: Tort

Keywords: Patent, Danger

Summary: Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 478 - Suit against Corporation - Tort - Negligence - Death of person passing by road due to fall of branch of tree on his head - Held, Municipal Corporation negligent to perform its duty under common law - Liable to pay damages.
