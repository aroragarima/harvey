Messrs. P.M. Patel And Sons And Others, Etc. v Union Of India And Others, Etc

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, V.D. Tulzapurkar, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 266; (1986) 1 SCC 32; (1986) SCC (L&S) 155; AIR 1987 SC 447; 1985 (51) FLR 534; 1986 LabIC 1410; 1986 (1) LLJ 88; 1986 (1) LLN 54; 1985(2) SCALE 860; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 55; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 55; 1986 (1) UJ 706

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Beedi and Cigar Workers (Conditions of Employment) Act, 1966

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - (A) Employees Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, ss. 2(f) and 5 - Interpretation - Whether home worker rolling beedies at home is an 'employee ' within meaning of s. 2(f) of Act? - Held, he is employee - Employees' Provident Funds Scheme, 1952, Para 69(1)(a) - (B) Application of scheme to workers rolling beedies at home - Feasible, non-fixation of retirement age considered as no ground to say that scheme cannot be implemented - Appeal dismissed.
