Balai Chandra Hazra v Shewdhari Jadav

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, P.N. Bhagwati, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 324; (1978) 2 SCC 559; AIR 1978 SC 1062; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 147; 1978 UJ 224

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, art.19(1)(f) - Constitution (Thirtieth Amendment) Act, 1972 - Letters Patent (Cal), cls.15 - Appeal u/cls. 15 of the Letters Patent - Amended the petition - Asking High Court to look into facts and additional evidence - Held, that the function of appreciation of evidence is to be undertaken by the trial court or at the most the first appellate court but not the High Court - Appeal allowed.
