Gernal Manager, Indian Bank v R. Rani and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 December 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1266; (2007) 12 SCC 796; (2008) 2 SCC (L&S) 956; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 381; 2008 (1) ESC 72; JT 2008 (1) SC 88; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 125; 2007(13) SCALE 804; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 1024; 2008 (1) SLR 492; 2008 (1) UPLBEC 906

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Service

Summary: Private respondents were appointed in the banks against the vacancies of Scheduled Tribe as they claimed themselves to be members of Konda Reddy Community which is a Scheduled Tribe - Cancellation of certificate and termination - Orders passed by the Committee cancelling the certificates and consequential orders of termination have been quashed and it has been directed that it would be open to the properly constituted Committee to hold fresh enquiry in accordance with law by High Court - Appeal against - Whether the directions contained in the decision of this court in the case of Kumari Madhuri Patil and Anr. vs. Addl. Commissioner, Tribal Development and Ors., [1994] 6 SCC 241 were merely guidelines or law laid down by this Court? - Held, as the constitution of the District Level Committee was in infraction of law laid down by this Court the defect could not have been cured by taking the matter in appeal to State Level Committee - High Court was quite justified in quashing the orders passed by District Level Committee, State Level Committee and the orders of termination - Appeals dismissed.
