Ganpat Ram Sharma And Ors. v Smt. Gayatri Devi

Supreme Court of India

17 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28316; (1987) 3 SCC 576; AIR 1987 SC 2016; JT 1987 (3) SC 99; 1987 (2) RCJ 253; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 162; 1987 (2) RentLR 575; 1987(2) SCALE 46; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 539; 1987 (2) UJ 370

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(h) - Tenancy - Eviction - On whom burden of proof lies as to availability of alternative accommodation? - Held, onus lies on landlord, but once so proved, it is for tenant to prove that accommodation is not suitable - Appeal dismissed.
