Messrs Prestige Lights Limited v State Bank Of India

Supreme Court of India

20 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 828; (2007) 8 SCC 449; 2007 (5) ALJ 578; 2007 (4) AWC 3761; [2007] 139 Comp Cas 169; 2007 (3) CompLJ 547; 2007 (2) DRTC 401; JT 2007 (10) SC 218; 2007 (5) MLJ 1100; 2007 (4) RCR(Civil) 46; 2007(10) SCALE 131; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 112

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Securitization, Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of Security Interest Act, 2002 - Company mortgaged its land and building with the respondent-Bank - Notice to take over possession of the residential house of the Director - Writ petition against dismissed in limine - Appeal against - Held, collusive action has been taken with a view to deprive the respondent- Bank from realizing legal and legitimate dues to which it was otherwise entitled - Company had never disclosed that it had created third party's interests in the property mortgaged with the Bank - No case has been made out for interference with the action taken by the respondent-Bank or the order passed by the High Court - Appeal dismissed.
