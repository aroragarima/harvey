H. L. Mehra v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 392; (1974) SCC (L&S) 351; (1974) 4 SCC 396; AIR 1974 SC 1281; 1974 LabIC 984; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 138; 1974 SLJ 379; 1974 (2) SLR 187

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Subsistence, Post And Telegraph

Summary: Central Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1965, rr. 10(5)(b), 34 proviso (b), 10(3), 10(4) and 10(5) - (A) Applicability of rr. 10(5)(b), 34 proviso (b) - To pending proceedings under repealed rules of 1957 - Conditions for applicability - Order of suspension - On dismissal suspension comes to end - On setting aside dismissal, order of suspension directed to continue u/r.10 (5) (b) - Held, proviso (b) to r. 34 of 1965 rules makes these rules applicable to pending proceedings under the 1957 rules notwithstanding their repeal - Not valid - Appeal Partly Allowed.
