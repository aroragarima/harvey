Government Of Orissa v M/S Ashok Transport Agency & Others

Supreme Court of India

30 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 307; (2002) 9 SCC 28; 2002 (3) JhrCR(SC) 29; JT 2002 (4) SC 548; 2002 (3) MLJ(SC) 98; 2002(4) SCALE 340; [2002] 3 S.C.R. 632; 2002 (4) SLT 96; 2002 (4) Supreme 631

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Order XXII Rule 10, CPC - An ex parte decree cannot be executed against the transferee in whose favour an assignment, creation or devolution of any interest during the pendency of the suit has taken place but who was not brought on record, as, such decree would not be binding on such transferee when it was not impleaded in the suit and the plaintiff had not taken steps for continuing the suit against him.
