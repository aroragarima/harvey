Atlas Cycle Industries Ltd. And Ors v State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 279; (1979) 2 SCC 196; (1979) SCC (Cr) 422; AIR 1979 SC 1149; 1979 CRLJ 927; 1978 CrLR(SC) 591; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1070

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Control Order, Houses Of Parliament, Iron and Steel (Control) Order, 1956, Kerala Education Bill, 1957

Summary: Parliament and Legislature - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3(6) - Iron and Steel Control Order 1956, cl. 15(i) - Essential Commodity - Fixation of maximum selling prices for iron and steel including black plain iron sheets - Central Govt. notification fixing rates for , not placed before the Houses of Parliament - Legality - Held, not void - Laying before House of Parliament u/s. 3(6) is directory - Apeal dismissed.
