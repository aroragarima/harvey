Gyan Chand v K.B. Lal And Others

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 224; (1977) 3 SCC 317; AIR 1977 SC 858; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 212; 1977 (1) RentLR 685; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 324

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 13A - Constitution, art. 136 - (A) Scope of s.13A of the Act - Application for special leave to appeal to Supreme Court or appeal by special leave to SC - If 'appeal' u/s.13-A - Interpreting the word 'Proceedings' - (B) Constitution - Concurrent findings of fact - On the question of failure to participate in eviction proceedings by tenant appellant despite service of summons - Held, an application for special leave to appeal to the SC or an appeal by special leave to the SC is not an 'appeal' within the meaning of s.13-A of the Act - SC refused to interfere in appeal by special leave - Appeal Dismissed.
