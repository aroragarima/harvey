Perfect Circle Victor Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 503; (1989) 3 SCC 478; AIR 1989 SC 2021; 1989 CRLR 515; 1989 (22) ECC 305; 1989 (23) ECR 497; 1992 (60) E.L.T. 676; JT 1989 (2) SC 396; 1989(1) SCALE 1414

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Constitution - Limitation Act, 1908 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Issue regarding deduction of post manufacturing expenses from assessable value - Held, formal order giving certain directions issued by Court - Liberty to mention for having matter listed for directions.
