Bombay Municipal Corporation v Dhondu Narayan Chowdhary

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.M. Sikri, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 163; AIR 1965 SC 1486; 1965 (67) BomLR 427; 1965 MahLJ 510; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 929

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Municipalities & Local Governments - Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888, ss. 68(1) and 105 B(1)(a)(ii) - Administrative - (A) Whether delegation by Commr., Municipal Corporation of his function u/ss. 105 B to 110 E are valid or proper? - Held, administrative side of delegate's duties of Commr. were to be subject of control and revision but not essential power to decide whether to take action or not in a particular case - Appeal No. 865 of 1964 allowed.
