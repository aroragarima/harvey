V. B. Dharmyat (Deceased) Through Lrs v Shree Jagadguru Tontadrya and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1079; (1999) 6 SCC 15; 2000 (38) ALR 186; JT 1999 (9) SC 469; 2000 (91) RD 124; 1999 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 25

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Registration Act, 1908, s. 2(7) - Agreement to execute lease for 99 years - Date from which lease was to commence not stipulated - Lease to commence only after municipality vacates premises - Possession of land to be given simultaneously with execution and registration of a lease deed in future at a non specified time - Whether agreement entered into by parties is within meaning of s. 2(7) of Act? - Held, no - An agreement between two parties, which entitles one of them merely to claim execution of a lease from other without creating a present and immediate demise in his favour is not an agreement to lease within meaning of s. 2(7) of Act - Appeals allowed.
