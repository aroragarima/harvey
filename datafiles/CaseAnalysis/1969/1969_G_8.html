Â S. L. Agarwal v General Manager, Hindustan Steel Limited

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 36; (1970) 1 SCC 177; AIR 1970 SC 1150; 1970 (2) ALT(SC) 335; 1970 (20) FLR 227; 1970 LabIC 1029; 1970 (2) LLJ 499; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 363; 1970 SLR 351; 1970 UJ 97

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 133, 311 and 311(2) - (A) Protection of cl. (2) - Scope of - (B) Holder of a civil post under Union or State - Employee of Hindustan Steel Ltd. - Protection of art. 311(2), if available - (C) New grounds - Points which were never raised before High Court nor touched upon in HC's Judgment or grounds of appeal - If can be raised before Supreme Court - Held, extends to persons referred to in cl. (1) - Employee held, is not a holder of such post so protection of art. 311 (2) cannot be claimed - Not allowed to be raised - Appeal Dismissed.
