Bal Chand Choraria v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 132; (1978) SCC (Cr) 77; (1978) 1 SCC 161; AIR 1978 SC 297; 1978 CRLJ 159; 1978 CRLR 11A; 1978 MLJ 254; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 401; 1978 (2) ShimLC 213; 1978 UJ 118

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 22 and 21 - Detention - Construction of representation made by detenu - To be liberal and not technically so as to frustrate or defeat concept of liberty - Validity of detention order when representation not considered by detenu - Detention order, held, vitiated - Appeal allowed.
