Narne Rama Murthy v Ravula Somasundaram and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 470; (2005) 6 SCC 614; 2005 (61) ALR 158; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 123; JT 2005 (7) SC 422; 2005 (3) RCR(Civil) 824; 2005(6) SCALE 605

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Limitation - Suit whether barred by limitation - Held, in cases where question of limitation is a mixed question of fact and law and suit does not appear to be barred by limitation on face of it, then facts necessary to prove limitation must be pleaded, an issue raised and then proved - SLPs dismissed.
