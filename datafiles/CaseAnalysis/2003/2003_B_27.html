Babu S/O Raveendran v Babu S/O Bahuleyan & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

11 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 604; (2003) 7 SCC 37; (2003) SCC (Cr) 1569; 2003 (3) CCR 119; 2003 (3) Crimes 285; 2004 (1) CRJ 568; 2003 (3) JCC 1290; JT 2003 (6) SC 615; 2003 (3) KHCACJ 711; 2003(6) SCALE 276; 2003 (4) SLT 838; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 389; 2003 (5) Supreme 466

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860 - s. 302 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - ss. 161, 164, 313 - Murdered by husband on the second day of marriage - Held, though the murder is gruesome, but taking the facts and circumstances into consideration, the crime committed by the accused does not fall within the ambit of the 'rarest of rare' cases. Therefore, the sentence of life imprisonment for an offence under s. 302 would be adequate.
