H. S. Bains Director Small Saving-Cum-Deputy v The State (Union Territory Of Chandigarh)

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 204; (1980) 4 SCC 631; (1981) SCC (Cr) 93; 1981 (18) ACC 146; AIR 1980 SC 1883; 1980 CRLJ 1308; 1980 CrLR(SC) 671; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 935

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: CrPC, 1973, ss. 156(3), 173(1), 190(1)(b), 200, 203 and 204 - Magistrate directing investigation u/s. 156 (3) in a complaint case - Police Report - Accoding to it no case was made out - Whether Magistrate can take cognisance and issue process - Held, still Magistrate can take cognisance and issue process - Appeal Dismissed.
