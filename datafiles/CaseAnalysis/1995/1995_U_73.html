Union Of India v S.B. Mishra

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 496; (1995) 5 SCC 657; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1314; AIR 1996 SC 613; 1995 (31) ATC 532; 1996 LabIC 619; 1995(5) SCALE 84; 1996 (1) SLJ 94; 1995 (5) SLR 201; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 704; 1996 (1) UJ 10

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 309, 310 and 311(2) - Rules framed u/art. 309 of the Constitution are subject to doctrine of pleasure and not applicable to defence personnel - Whether defence personnel is entitled to supply of copy of Inquiry report - Held, not entitled - Appeal allowed. .
