Kanwal Ram and Others v Himachal Pradesh Admn

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Raghubar Dayal, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 446; AIR 1966 SC 614; 1966 CRLJ 472; [1966] 1 S.C.R. 539

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, s. 494, r/w 109 - Family Law - Bigamy - Issue, whether second marriage of the appellant legally proved? - Held, a marriage is not proved unless the essential ceremonies required for its solemnisation are proved to have been performed - Admission by the accused not admissible 'evidence' in a case of bigamy, proof of marriage necessary - Appeal allowed.
