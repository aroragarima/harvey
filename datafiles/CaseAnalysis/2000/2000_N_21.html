Parmanand v Nagar Palika Dehradun and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 1093; (2003) 9 SCC 290; 2004 (1) LLJ 235; 2003(10) SCALE 481

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Whether municipality be treated as industry? - Held, termination of services of appellant without any prior notice or information - Activity of municipal corporation carried on in any of departments except those dealing with assessment and levy of house tax, assessment and levy of octroi, removal of encroachment and removal and pulling down of dilapidated house, prevention and control of food adulteration and maintenance of cattle pounds, to fall within definition of 'industry' as arising under Industrial Disputes Act - None of reasons given by municipality have any basis and labour court perfectly justified in setting aside order of termination and directing appellants reinstatement with full back wages - Appeal allowed.
