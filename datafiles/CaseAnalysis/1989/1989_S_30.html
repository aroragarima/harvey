Gopal Saran v Satyanarayana

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 815; (1989) 3 SCC 56; AIR 1989 SC 1141; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 21; 1989 (1) RCJ 481; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 50; 1991 (1) RentLR 1; 1989(1) SCALE 497; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 767; 1989 (2) UJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Electricity & Energy; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Rent control - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 137 and 138 - Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 13(1)(e) - (A) Party not subjecting himself to cross-examination in spite of order of court - Examination- chief, if can be relied upon - (B) Assignment or sub-letting or parting with possession - Tenant additionally carrying on business of putting up advertisement of customers on shop's terrace - For charges - Held, (A) It is not safe to rely on examination-chief - (B) Nobody given right to put up boards so no assignment, subletting or parting with possession - Appeal allowed.
