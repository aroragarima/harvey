Case Analysis

Bench

N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, K. Subba Rao, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 230; AIR 1964 SC 1099; [1964] 6 S.C.R. 129

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Representation of People Act, 1951 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908, s. 29 - Whether the terms of s. 29 are apt to take in appeals under the Representation of the People Act? - The requirement of a prescription by the special law "of a period different" from that prescribed by the schedule is satisfied - Appeal dismissed
