Gian Mahtani v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 2; (1971) SCC (Cr) 601; (1971) 2 SCC 611; AIR 1971 SC 1898; 1971 CRLJ 1417; 1999 (110) E.L.T. 400; 1971 UJ 890

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Appreciation of evidence - Suspicion cannot take the place of proof - Contrabands smuggled in luxury items - Held, prosecution must prove its case beyond all reasonable doubt - Victims of the machinations of seasoned smugglers held, not as innocent as has been sought to be made out - Appeal Dismissed.
