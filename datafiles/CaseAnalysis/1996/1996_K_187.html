Mancheri Puthusseri Ahmed and Others v Kuthiravattam Estate Receiver

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2544; (1996) 6 SCC 185; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 57; AIR 1997 SC 208; 1996 (4) CLT 126; JT 1996 (8) SC 107; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 138; 1996(6) SCALE 452; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 813; 1996 (6) Supreme 762

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land & Property - Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964 (as amended by Act 35 of 1969), s. 4 A - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 60 and 76 - 'Deemed tenant' - Decree in redemption suit against erstwhile mortgagees in possession of suit land, becoming final - Execution of decree by means of deposit of mortgage amount by decree holder-mortgagor on 14-03-1969 - Order of delivery of possession to mortgagor by executing Court - Whether mortgagees would be 'deemed tenant' as he was not mortgagee in possession on 01-01-1970 as provided by s. 4A of 1964 Act nor he was in possession as mortgagee for period of fifty years before 1970? - Mortgagees would not be deemed tenant - Appeal dismissed.
