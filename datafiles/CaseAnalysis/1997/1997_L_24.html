Laxmi Narain Mehar v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1038; (1997) 3 SCC 87; (1997) SCC (L&S) 643; AIR 1997 SC 1347; 1997 (3) AWC 1794; 1997 (1) CLR 828; JT 1997 (3) SC 444; 1997(2) SCALE 531; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 463; 1997 (2) SLR 383; 1997 (3) Supreme 107; 1997 (1) UJ 583

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art.16 - Validity of transfer challenged - Candidate posted near home town on compassionate grounds - Later on transferred on ground that services of experienced officer were necessary - Order was based on administrative exigency - Held, order of transfer proper and no interference required - Appeal dismissed.
