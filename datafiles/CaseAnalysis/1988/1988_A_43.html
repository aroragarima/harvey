Anil Kumar Neotia and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 460; (1988) 2 SCC 587; AIR 1988 SC 1353; 1988 (2) CompLJ 91; JT 1988 (2) SC 227; 1988(1) SCALE 817; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 738; 1988 (2) UJ 77

Case Digest

Subject: Textiles

Keywords: Swadeshi Cotton Mills Company Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1986, Swadeshi Cotton Mills Ltd Act 1981, Ganesh Flour Mills Company Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1984, Aluminium Corporation of India Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Aluminium Undertaking) Act, 1984, Britannia Engineering Company Limited (Mokameh Unit) and Arthur Butler and Company (Muzaffarpore) Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertaking) Act,1978

Summary: Textiles - Swadeshi Cotton Mills Co. Ltd. (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1986, ss. 3, 4 - Takeover of Mill for public purpose and thereby acquisition of shares of other companies held by Mill - Shares so acquired charged with liability of Mill - Effect on nature of acquisition - Held, preamble to the Act which states and proclaims that the Act was passed to carry out the object of art. 39(b) and (c) of the Constitution and in view of the scheme of the Act, it is clearly manifest that the Act in question was passed for a public purpose and for the acquisition of shares there was a public purpose - Petition dismissed.
