Commissioner of Income Tax v Sarabhai Management Corporation Limited

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 935; 1992 (102) CTR 164; [1991] 192 ITR 151

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Date of commencement of respondent-assessee's business - High Court had contrary view on Tribunal's opinion - Appellant argues it being finding of fact and therefore could not be interfered with by HC - Held, Tribunal view was based on misapprehension of nature of business of assessee - HC view upheld - Appeal dismissed.
