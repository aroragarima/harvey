Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation Limited v S. Rajapriya and Two Others

Supreme Court of India

20 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 320; (2005) 6 SCC 236; (2005) SCC (Cr) 1436; 2005 (2) ACC 476; AIR 2005 SC 2985; 2005 (60) ALR 154; 2006 (2) Bom.C.R. 376; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 138; 2005 (2) KLT 848; 2005 (140) PLR 651; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 628; 2006 (1) RCR(Criminal) 598; 2005(4) SCALE 336; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 737

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Future Pecuniary Benefit, Computation Of Compensation, Assessment Of Damage

Summary: [A.] Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Fatal Accidents Act, 1846 - Fatal Accidents Act, 1976 - Law Reforms (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 1934 - Compensation of Rs. 6, 09, 552/- Allowed - Multiplier of 16 was applied - Appeal against compensation - Held, considering age of deceased and principles appropriate multiplier would be 12 and not 16 as adopted by Tribunal and affirmed by HC - Interest rate reduced from 9% per annum to 7 .5% per annum - Appeal partly allowed.
