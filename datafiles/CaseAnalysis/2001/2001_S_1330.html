Stock Exchange, Ahmedabad v Assistant Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

02 March 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 260; (2001) 3 SCC 559; AIR 2001 SC 1071; 2001 (2) CLC 425; [2001] 105 Comp Cas 76; 2001 (166) CTR 285; [2001] 248 ITR 209; JT 2001 (3) SC 378; 2001(2) SCALE 352; 2001 (2) SCJ 320; [2001] 2 S.C.R. 255; 2001 (2) SLT 607; 2001 (3) Supreme 119; [2001] 115 TAXMAN 471

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Securities Exchange Board of India

Summary: Securities Exchange Board of India - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 159 and 281B - Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act, 1956 - Writ petition by appellant-Stock Exchange - High Court challenging orders of provisional attachment and the garnishee notice, dismissed - Validity of HC judgment challenged - Court held, membership right was not the property of assessee and, therefore, it could not be attached u/s. 281B of the IT Act, 1961 and no amount was due from or held by the Stock Exchange and, therefore, s. 226(3) could not be invoked and quashed order of provisional attachment and garnishee notice - Appeal allowed.
