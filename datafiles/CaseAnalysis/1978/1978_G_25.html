Commissioner of Income Tax, Patiala v Groz-beckert Saboo Limited

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 226; (1979) 1 SCC 340; AIR 1979 SC 376; 1979 (8) CTR 155; [1979] 116 ITR 125; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 371; 1979 TAXLR 126; 1979 UJ 88

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 28 - Determination of profits of business - When assessee converting capital assets into stock-in-trade - Held, market value of assets as on date of conversion to be deducted from sale proceeds - Appeal dismissed.
