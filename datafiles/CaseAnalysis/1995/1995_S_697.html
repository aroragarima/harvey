State of Jammu and Kashmir v Sudershan Chakkar and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1303; (1995) 4 SCC 181; (1995) SCC (Cr) 664; AIR 1995 SC 1954; 1997 (35) AllCriC 414; 1995 (3) CCR 28; 1995 CRLJ 3606; 1995(3) SCALE 407; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 294; 1995 (2) UJ 292

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Service - Jammu and Kashmir Prevention of Corruption Act, 2006, ss. 5(2), 173 and 220 - Question regarding charge of continuous neglect of duty designedly - Held, conclusion regarding charge of continuous neglect of duty designedly when can be reached be reached at only after collection of all materials during investigation and not in isolation - Letter written by accused to his superiors cannot be relied on and court has to confine its attention to documents referred to in s. 173 - Appeal allowed.
