Rattan Singh v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3370; (1997) 11 SCC 396; 1998 (3) LLJ 714

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25F - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 25 - Termination - Validity of - Whether termination of appellant was in violation of s. 25-F of Industrial Disputes Act, 1947? - Appellant had continuously worked for more than 240 days in a year - Since he was a workman, he was entitled to protection of s. 25-F of Act and said protection could not be denied to him on ground that he was a daily-rated worker - It is not case of respondents that provisions of s. 25-F of Act were complied with while terminating services of appellant - Appeal disposed of.
