Grindlays Bank Limited v Central Government Industrial Tribunal and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 88; (1980) Supp SCC 420; (1981) SCC (L&S) 309; AIR 1981 SC 606; 1981 (29) BLJR 104; 1981 (42) FLR 88; 1981 LabIC 155; 1981 (1) LLJ 327; 1981 (1) LLN 196; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 341; 1981 (1) SLJ 510; 1981 UJ 42

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10, 11 and 17A - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1957, rr. 22, 24 - CPC, 1908, s. 113, O. 9, r. 13 - Competancy of Tribunal to set aside ex parte award - In the light of fact that it is based on evidence - Setting aside does not amount to review - Held, fact is immaterial - Tribunal does not become functus officio provided application is filed within 30 days of publication of award - Appeal Dismissed.
