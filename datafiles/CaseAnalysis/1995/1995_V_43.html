Union of India and Others v Virpal Singh Chauhan

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1803; (1995) 6 SCC 684; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1; AIR 1996 SC 448; AIR 1995 SCW 4309; 1995 (31) ATC 813; JT 1995 (7) SC 231; 1995(5) SCALE 648; 1996 (1) SLJ 65; 1995 (5) SLR 400; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 158; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1983

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Indian Railway Establishment Manual

Summary: Indian Railway Establishment Manual - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16(4) - Railway services - Reservation for Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes candidates - Letters providing for reservation, in favour of Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes candidates, rosters and their operation and on subject of seniority as between general candidates and reserved category candidates - Held, are in nature of special rules - Prevail over general instructions - Percentage of reservation has to be worked out with reference to total number of posts in a cadre and not with reference to vacancies.
