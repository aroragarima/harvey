Kanpur Suraksha Karamchari Union (Regd.) v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 742; (1988) 4 SCC 478; (1989) SCC (L&S) 13; AIR 1988 SC 1965; 1988 (57) FLR 844; JT 1988 (3) SC 461; 1989 LabIC 184; 1989 (1) LLJ 26; 1988 (2) LLN 919; 1988(2) SCALE 431; 1989 (1) SLJ 40; 1988 (4) SLR 284; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 590; 1988 (2) UJ 667

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Practice and Procedure - Factories Act, 1948, ss.46 - whether the workmen who were working in canteens established under section 46 of the Factories Act in Defence Installations are entitled to claim the period of service in such canteens prior to 22. 10. 1980 as part of the qualifying service for claiming pension - Employer, is 'occupier' of factory and not Canteen Managing Committee - Held, service rendered by them prior to that date would also qualify for pension - Petitions allowed.
