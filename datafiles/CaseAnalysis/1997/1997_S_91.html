State of Punjab and Others v Sardara Singh

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1695; (1998) 9 SCC 709

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Daily-wage labourer - High Court directed that respondent be regularised since he has put in more than three years of service - State Govt. framed a scheme for regularisation vide circular D/-7-5-1993 - Held, HC could not direct for regularisation of respondent but could only direct appellants to frame a scheme for said regularisation and since scheme has already been framed, regularisation can only be made in accordance with said scheme - Appeal allowed.
