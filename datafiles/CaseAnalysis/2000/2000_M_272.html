Dilip v Mohd. Azizul Haq and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 209; (2000) 3 SCC 607; AIR 2000 SC 1976; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 324; JT 2000 (3) SC 114; 2000 (1) RCJ 643; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 448; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 350; 2000(2) SCALE 347; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 280; 2000 (2) SLT 720; 2000 (2) Supreme 337

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Central Provinces and Berar Regulation of Letting of Accommodation Act, 1946, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, Berar Regulation of Accommodation Act, 1947

Summary: Rent Control - C.P. and Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949, cls.13A, 2(4A) - CPC, 1908,s.2 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882,s.106 ,s.O. 7 r. 11 - Suit against the appellant regarding the plot on the ground that the premises is open land and the provisions of 1949 Order was not applicable to the said premises and that the tenancy of the appellant stood terminated by efflux of time - No eviction decree in suit without permission of Controller - Determination of retrospective applicability of cl. 13 A ( introduced while pendency of appeal) to tenant - Held, tenant would be entitled to protection of cl. 13-A - It does not have retrospective effect - Appeal Allowed. .
