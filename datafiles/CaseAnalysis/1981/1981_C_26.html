Choudhary Sahu (Dead) By Lrs. v State Of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 325; (1982) 1 SCC 232; AIR 1982 SC 98; 1982(1) SCALE 161; [1982] 2 S.C.R. 178; 1982 UJ 86

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC,1908 - Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1962, ss. 10 and 30 - Scope of power of Court of appeal under O. 41 rr. 21 and 33 of the CPC, 1908 - Collector's Order - Allowing certain units to landholder - No appeal filed by State - Appeal filed by Landholder - Commissioner, held, had no power to interfere with finding in favour of landholder - Appeal Allowed.
