Â Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board, Etc v A. Rajappa and Others, Etc., Etc

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar, P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 42; AIR 1978 SC 548; 1978 (2) LLJ 73

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s.2 - Whether callings, services and undertaking should be excluded from term 'industry'? - S 2(j) uses words of very wide denotation, a line would have to be drawn in a fair and just manner so as to exclude some callings, services or under takings - Held, in view of difficulty experienced in defining true denotation of term 'industry' and divergence of opinion in regard thereto as has been case with this Bench also, it is appropriate that legislature to do the same - Order accordingly.
