Bhura Ram and Others v State of Rajasthan and Anther

Supreme Court of India

02 April 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

P.P. Naolekar, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 585; (2008) 11 SCC 103; AIR 2008 SC 2666; 2008 ALL MR (Cri) 1707; 2008 CRLJ 3494; JT 2008 (4) SC 611; 2008 (150) PLR 531; 2008 (2) RCR(Criminal) 761; 2008 (3) RLW 1924; 2008(4) SCALE 666

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Time Barred

Summary: Indian Penal Code, ss. 498A, 406 - Held, on the basis of factual scenario disclosed by complainant in the complaint, inevitable conclusion is that no part of cause of action arose in Rajasthan and, therefore, the Magistrate concerned has no jurisdiction to deal with the matter - As a consequence thereof, proceedings before the Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Sri Ganganagar are quashed - Appeal allowed.
