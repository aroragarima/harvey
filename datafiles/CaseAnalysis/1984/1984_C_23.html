Azhar Ali Khan and Others v Commissioner, Municipal Corporation of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 407; (1984) SCC (Cr) 438; (1984) 3 SCC 549; (1984) SCC (L&S) 588; AIR 1984 SC 1240; 1984 LabIC 898; 1984 (2) LLN 42; 1984(1) SCALE 666; 1984 (2) SLJ 463; 1984 UJ 711

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service -(A) Whether respondent 1 should be directed to fill up eight posts of Assistant Engineers from amongst those in the select panel approved on May 2, 1979? - There is nothing wrong in the respondent 1 - Corporation proceeding to appoint Assistant Engineers (Civil) by direct recruitment as per the Recruitment Regulations or in fixing the 50 : 50 quota and working it out or in the selection of the petitioners in Writ Petition No. 1194 of 1979 as Assistant Engineers pursuant to the decision to appoint eight Assistant Engineers (Civil) by direct recruitment in the interviews held for that purpose on March 6 and 7, 1979 - Held, respondents 1 and 2 should issue orders of appointment to those posts to eight of the petitioners in Writ Petition No. 1194 of 1979 who are in the select list within six weeks from this date (July 13, 1983), if not already issued as undertaken by Mr. U. R. Lalit (senior counsel of respondents 1 and 2) on July 29, 1981 within five months from that date - (B) Whether the office order dated April 11, 1978 entrusting Junior Engineers with current duty charge of the posts of Assistant Engineers and the office order dated June 21, 1979 promoting 2 Junior Engineers as Assistant Engineers on current duty charge on their own pay scale should be quashed ? - Continuing current duty charge and ad hoc appointments for such a long period exceeding the period of one year mentioned in the said memorandum dated December 30, 1976 of the Government of India is irregular though that memorandum could not be stated to be automatically binding on respondent 1 - What is totally wrong is that appointment of Junior Engineers on current duty charge as Assistant Engineers has been made by the impugned order dated June 21, 1979 even after the approval of the select list prepared for the appointment of eight Assistant Engineers those persons who are in the select list - Held, the appointment of six diploma-holder Junior Engineers by the order dated April 10, 1978 (Annexure I) and of two such Junior Engineers by the order dated June 21, 1979 (Annexure M) as Junior Engineers on current duty charge for periods which are proved to be too long is irregular - (C) Whether respondent 1 should be directed to fill up the remaining posts of Assistant Engineers in the direct recruitment quota from amongst the empanelled petitioners who were graduate Junior Engineers? - Respondent 1 is admittedly bound by the Recruitment Regulations made with the approval of the Central Government as required by s. 480(2) of the Act and it shall fill the remaining posts of Assistant Engineers (after filling up the eight posts of Assistant Engineers as aforesaid in regard to prayer No. 1) in the direct recruitment quota which are kept vacant in the seniority list dated September 2, 1978 (Annexure G in Writ Petition No. 1194 of 1979) and any further posts which might have become available - Held, in accordance with those Recruitment Regulations within six months from today (July 13, 1983) or from the date on which further vacancy to the posts of Assistant Engineers in the direct recruitment quota arises, as the case may be, if permissible from out of the select list approved on May 2, 1979 - (D) Whether respondent 1 should be restrained from giving ad hoc promotions to current duty charge holders amongst Junior Engineers? - Respondent 1 shall not make current duty charge/ad hoc appointments and promotions except strictly and truly in accordance with the instructions and Regulations referred to above land other instructions, if any, issued in that regard - Held, respondent 1 be restrained from giving ad hoc promotions to current duty charge holders amongst Junior Engineers - Petition disposed of.
