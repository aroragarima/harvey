Shrikant v Vasantrao and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 15; (2006) 2 SCC 682; AIR 2006 SC 918; JT 2006 (1) SC 394; 2006 (1) RCR(Civil) 591; 2006(1) SCALE 344; 2006 (1) SCJ 789; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 496

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election

Keywords: Election Expenses, Election Dispute

Summary: [A.] Election - Representation Of People Act, 1951, ss. 10-A, 100(1)(a), 100(1)(d), 101(1)(a), 116-A, 6, 7, 8, 8-A, 9-A, 98 - Maharashtra Godawari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation Act 1998 - Maharashtra Jeevan Authority Act, 1976 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 102 (1)(a), 12, 171, 173, 191, 191(1)(b), 191(1)(c), 191(1)(d), 226, 32, 58(2), 66(4) - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 2(60) - Whether a Govt. Contractor in business for execution of works undertaken by Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran is contracting with State Govt and if appellant was disqualified from being chosen as a Member of Legislative Council, u/s. 9A of Act on account of works contracts? - Held, term 'State Govt' in s. 9A r/w s. 7 of Act should be understood in its ordinary and normal sense, and not with reference to extended meaning u/art.12 of Constitution - Held, Appellant had no subsisting contract with Maharashtra Govt. and did not incur any disqualification u/s. 9 A of Act - Appeal allowed.
