Jagathigowda, C. N. and Others v Chairman, Cauvery Gramina Bank, and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2247; (1996) 9 SCC 677; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1310; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 325; AIR 1996 SC 2733; 1996 (2) CLR 1058; 1997 (75) FLR 82; JT 1996 (10) SC 175; 1996 LabIC 2433; 1996 (2) LLN 1238; 1996(5) SCALE 678; 1996 (3) SLJ 119; 1996 (4) SLR 700; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 190; 1996 (6) Supreme 318

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, Sch. 2, item 6 - Promotion - Cadre of Senior Managers in Rural Bank - Guidelines by National Bank providing procedure for such Promotion - Seniority-cum-merit basis - Subsequent circular - Providing that promotions of all employees of Rural Bank would be on basis of comparative assessment of performance appraisal of officers concerned - Promotions in question made on basis of performance appraisal - Held, cannot be challenged on ground of violation of guidelines providing seniority - merit basis.
