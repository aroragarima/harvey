K K. Krishnan v M.K. Vijaya Raghavan

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 167; (1980) 4 SCC 88; AIR 1980 SC 1756; 1981 (1) RCJ 707; 1980 (2) RCR(Rent) 683; 1980 (2) RentLR 495; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 139; 1980 UJ 795

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 11(4)(i) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 108(j) - Tenancy - Subletting - In the absence of landlord's consent or right under lease deed - Eviction in the light of rights u/s.108 (j) of 1882 Act - Held, tenant can be evicted u/s.11(4)(i) - Such rights are no defence - Appeal Dismissed.
