Babulal v Raj Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3258; (1996) 3 SCC 154; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 330; AIR 1996 SC 2050; 1996 (1) CCC 305; 1996 (1) CLT 528; JT 1996 (2) SC 716; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 29; 1996(2) SCALE 438; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 763; 1996 (2) Supreme 601; 1996 (1) UJ 700

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, rr. 97 and 98 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Execution of decree for specific performance of sale deed objected by non party to decree - Order dismissed as not maintainable - Legality - Held, illegal - Adjudication was required to be conducted under O. 21, r. 98 before such dismissal - Appeal allowed.
