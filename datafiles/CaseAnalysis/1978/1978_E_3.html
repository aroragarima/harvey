Royal Talkies, Hyderabad & Ors v Employees State Insurance Corp

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 196; (1978) 4 SCC 204; (1978) SCC (L&S) 497; AIR 1978 SC 1478; 1978 (37) FLR 128; 1978 LabIC 1245; 1978 (2) LLJ 390; 1978 (2) LLN 268; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 80; 1978 UJ 546

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, ss. 2(9), 2(17) and 40 - ESI - Definition of ' Employee' u/s. 2(9) of Act - Employees of cycle stand and canteen run in a cinema theatre by contractors - Whether cinema owner liable as principal employer for their contribution - Held, employees and so cinema owner liable - Appeal dismissed.
