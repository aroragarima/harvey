Divisional Forest Officer v Bishwanath Tea Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 511; (1981) 3 SCC 238; AIR 1981 SC 1368; 1981(1) SCALE 771; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 662; 1981 UJ 470

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Constitution, 1950, arts. 19 and 226 - Assam Land and Revenue Regulation Rules, Regn. 37 - (A) Locus standi of incorporated body to file writ petition - Company not a citizen and not entitled to complain breach or violation of fundamental right u/art. 19 - Denial of equality before law - (B) Writ jurisdiction - Contractual right from a lease - When breach of statutory provision not proved - Writ can be directed and company can also file petition for enforcement of right under statute - Right cannot be enforced in writ petition - Appeal Allowed.
