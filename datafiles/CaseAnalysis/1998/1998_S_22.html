Ministry of Finance and Another v S. B. Ramesh

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 367; (1998) 3 SCC 227; (1998) SCC (L&S) 865; AIR 1998 SC 853; 1998 (1) CLR 659; 1998 (78) FLR 700; JT 1998 (1) SC 319; 1998 LabIC 623; 1998 (1) LLN 968; 1998(1) SCALE 285; 1998 (2) SLJ 67; 1998 (1) SLR 618; 1998 (3) SLT 5; 1998 (1) Supreme 387; 1998 (2) UPLBEC 1087

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 311 and 309 - Central Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1964, r. 3(1)(iii) - Misconduct - Govt. servant with another women - Live-in relation - Having living wife - Departmental enquiry findings - Guilty of misconduct deserving departmental action and punishment - Held, is not sustainable - Order retiring delinquent compulsorily - Not proper - What transpires between them is not a concern of their employer - Law not interferes unless relationship is adulterous or promiscuous.
