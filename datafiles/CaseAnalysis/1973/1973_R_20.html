Krishan Chander v Ram Lal

Supreme Court of India

10 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.N. Dwivedi

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 317; (1973) 2 SCC 759; AIR 1973 SC 2513; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 349

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 82(b)and 86 - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 14 - Conduct of Election Rules, 1961, r. 94 A, Form 25 - Parties to election petition - (A) Joining of parties to election petition - Nature and constitutionality of s. 82(b) - (B) issue that necessary parties are not impleaded - If preliminary issue - Held, section is mandatory but does not violate art. 14 of Constitution - Issue has to be tried as preliminary issue - Appeal dismissed.
