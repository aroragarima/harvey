Manganese Ore (India) Ltd. A v The Regional Assistant Commissioner Of Sales Tax,Jabalpur

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 310; (1976) 4 SCC 124; AIR 1976 SC 410; 1976 (5) CTR 303; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 99; 1976 (37) STC 489; 1976 TAXLR 1407

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Keywords: Sales Tax Authorities

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art.286 - Whether sales were outside sales and not in course of inter-State trade or commerce and therefore provisions of Central Sales Tax Act did not apply? - Held, No - It was manganese ore and manganese ore alone which was sought to be sold by appellant to various buyers in India, therefore, all incidents of an inter-State sale are present and HC was correct that sales were covered by s.3(a) of Central Sales Tax Act - Appeal dismissed.
