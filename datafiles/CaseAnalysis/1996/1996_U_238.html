Pratibha Processors and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1849; (1996) 11 SCC 101; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 261; AIR 1997 SC 138; 1996 (67) ECR 453; 1996 (88) E.L.T. 12; JT 1996 (9) SC 478; 1996 (17) RLT 333; 1996(7) SCALE 737; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 596

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Keywords: Warehoused Goods, Warehouse

Summary: Customs Act 1962, s. 61(2) - Whether interest should be paid on duty payable on clearance of goods when such duty is exempted? - Interest payable u/s. 61(2) is mere accessory of principal amount - Held, since amount of duty payable is nil because of exemption so there is no reason to pay interest.
