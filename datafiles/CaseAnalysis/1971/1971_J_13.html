Jage Ram, Inspector of Police and Another v Hans Raj Midha

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 285; (1972) SCC (Cr) 118; (1972) 1 SCC 181; AIR 1972 SC 1140; 1972 CRLJ 768; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 409

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Tort

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 367 & 561A - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 21 - Deetermination of nature of expunging remarks made in a judgment - Held, remarks must be such as can be described as unwarranted, unnecessary or irrelevant or can be characterised as generalisation or of a sweeping nature - Appeal dismissed.
