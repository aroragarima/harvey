Â Gangadhar Narsingdas Agarwal v P. S. Thrivikraman And Another

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, S.M. Sikri, A.N. Grover, D.G. Palekar, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 128; (1972) 3 SCC 475; AIR 1973 SC 350; 1972 (1) CTR 371; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1491; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 874; 1972 TAXLR 2288

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Trade

Keywords: Tariff Act, 1934, Entry Outwards Of, Entry Outwards To

Summary: Customs- Customs Act, 1962, s. 39, Proviso to s. 16 - Tariff Act, 1934, s. 4A - Notification D/- 02-08-1966 of Act - Relevant date for determination of rate of duty and tariff valuation - Held, since order was made prior to notification, authorities acted without jurisdiction by holding that, date of entry outwards of vessel was the date ' when vessel arrived ' - Appeal allowed.
