Jaswant Singh v State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

04 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 254; (2000) 4 SCC 484; (2000) SCC (Cr) 991; AIR 2000 SC 1833; 2000 (2) CCR 23; 2000 (2) Crimes 150; 2000 CRLJ 2212; 2000 (1) JCC 410; JT 2000 (4) SC 114; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 545; 2000(3) SCALE 42; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 903; 2000 (3) SLT 406; 2000 (3) Supreme 320

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Murder trial - Evidence - Appreciation of - Unimpeachable medical evidence and consistent, corroborative and credible prosecution witnesses suggest involvement of accused - Omissions in testimony of eye - witness were not contradictions, concurrent findings - Validity of conviction - Held, conviction valid.
