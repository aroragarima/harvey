Ramendra Kishore Biswas v State of Tripura and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M. Srinivasan, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1813; (1999) SCC (L&S) 295; (1999) 1 SCC 472; AIR 1999 SC 294; 1999 (1) CLR 846; 1999 (81) FLR 398; JT 1998 (8) SC 402; 1999 LabIC 178; 1999 (2) LLJ 192; 1999 (1) LLN 300; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 138; 1998(6) SCALE 454; 1999 (1) SLR 257; 1998 (10) SLT 129; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 400; 1998 (9) Supreme 260; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 198

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Jurisdiction of civil court - Service matter - Whether High Court has jurisdiction regarding service matters? - Held, order holding that civil court had no jurisdiction at time of hearing of second appeal not proper.
