State of Kerala and Others v M. Padmanabhan Nair

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 250; (1985) SCC (L&S) 278; (1985) 1 SCC 429; AIR 1985 SC 356; 1985 (1) ALT(SC) 303; 1985 (50) FLR 145; 1985 LabIC 664; 1985 (1) LLJ 530; 1985 (2) LLN 18; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 476; 1985 (1) SLJ 106; 1985 (1) SLR 750; 1985 UJ 267

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 311 and 300 A - Treasury Code (Ker), r. 186 - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, s. 4 - Delay in payment of retirement dues - Govt.'s liability to pay interest at market rate - Commences on expiry of two months from date of retirement - Delayed payment due to non-production of last pay certificate by retiree - Suit for interest - Held, non production of certificate was due to lapse of issuing authority - Govt. liable to pay interest - Order accordingly.
