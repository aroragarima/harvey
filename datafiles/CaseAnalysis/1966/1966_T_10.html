T. D. Kumar and Brothers Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Vishishtha Bhargava, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 192; [1967] 63 ITR 67

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 28(1)(c) - Appellant was charged of fabricating the accounts and penalty u/s. 28(1)(c) was imposed - Held, reference of questions to High Court as sought by the appellant did not arise out of the order of the tribunal and were new questions which were never raised before tribunal - Appeal dismissed.
