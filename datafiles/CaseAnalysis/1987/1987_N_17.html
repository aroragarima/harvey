Narain and Others v Om Prakash Gupta

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28238; (1987) 3 SCC 61; AIR 1987 SC 1602; 1987 (1) CCC 1044; JT 1987 (2) SC 258; 1987 (2) RCJ 1; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 579; 1987 (2) RentLR 479; 1987(1) SCALE 1253; 1987 (1) UJ 669

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, ss.2(h), 13 - Whether commercial tenancies are heritable - Eviction order against heirs of original tenant - When original tenancy started about 50 years ago by the predecessors-in-interest of the present appellant - Ground, being let out for commercial purpose disputed premises was not heritable - Held, Order liable to be set aside and rent enhanced - Point in respect of commercial purposes is not open - Appeal disposed of.
