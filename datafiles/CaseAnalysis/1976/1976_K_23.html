Controller of Estate Duty, Gujarat v Kantilal Trikamlal

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Hans Raj Khanna, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 230; (1976) 4 SCC 643; (1977) SCC (Tax) 90; AIR 1976 SC 1935; 1976 CTR 391; 1978 HLR 782; [1976] 105 ITR 92; 1977 (1) MLJ(SC) 26; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 9; 1976 TAXLR 831

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Keywords: Estate Duty Act, 1953, Hindu Law, Gift Tax Act, 1958, Hypothetical

Summary: Estate Duty Act, 1953, s. 2(15) expln. 2, ss. 9(1) and 27 - Property - Disposition - HUF property - Partition by deceased within 2 years before his death - Taking lesser share than his entitlement - Whether amounts to disposition in favour of relative - Difference between value of deceased's share and what he actually received - Whether a 'gift' - Held, difference in value whether chargeable to estate duty - Appeal No. CA 1677 of 1973 Dismissed; Appeal No. 1095 of 1970 Allowed.
