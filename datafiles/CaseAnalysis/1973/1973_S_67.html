Â State Of Kerala And Anr. v The Gwalior Rayon Silk Manufacturing (Wvg.) Co. Ltd. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, A.N. Ray, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 190; (1973) 2 SCC 713; AIR 1973 SC 2734; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 671

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Kerala Private Forests (Vesting and Assignment) Act, 1971, Constitution (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1955, Kerala Forest Act, 1961, Tenancy and Land Laws Kerala Private Forests Act 26 of 1970, Kannan Devan Hills (Resumption of Lands) Act, 1971

Summary: Land & Property - Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Constitutionality of Kerala Private Forests (Vesting and Assignment) Act, 1971 - Held, act is protected by art. 31 A (1) - Act purports to vest jenmam rights to forest lands in Govt. Forest lands are 'estate' within art. 31 A (2) of Constitution - Petition dismissed.
