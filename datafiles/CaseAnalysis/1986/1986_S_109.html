Bakul Oil Industries And Anr v State Of Gujarat And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 375; (1987) 1 SCC 31; (1987) SCC (Tax) 74; AIR 1987 SC 142; 1987 (1) CCC 187; 1987 (10) ECR 1; 1987 (27) E.L.T. 572; [1987] 165 ITR 6; JT 1986 SC 801; 1986(2) SCALE 750; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 185; 1987 (64) STC 304; 1987 TAXLR 2059; 1987 (1) UJ 38

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Gujarat Sales Tax Act, 1970, ss. 15 and 49(2) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Establishment of new industry - Industry engaged in decorticating and crushing cotton seeds and groundnuts and manufacturing oil - Availability of exemption from purchase and sales tax in respect of raw materials - Held, that exemption was in form of concession and could be withdrawn without violating rule of promissory estoppel - Appeal dismissed.
