Madan Gopal Kanodia v Mamraj Maniram and Others and Vice Versa

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 568; (1977) 1 SCC 669; AIR 1976 SC 461

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6 r. 4 - Plaintiff filed suit in Court of Civil Judge for recovery of sum - Court passed decree - Defendants appealed to High Court which reversed judgment - Whether defendants are liable to pay cost of 21 bales of cloth to plaintiff - Held, on careful consideration of oral and documentary evidence produced by plaintiff on this point, plaintiffs claim with this regard to price of 21 bales of cloth which had been sold by defendants' firm and whose sale proceeds were not paid to plaintiff has been proved satisfaction, therefore HC was wrong in disallowing claim - Appeal partly allowed.
