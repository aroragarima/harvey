Controller Of Estate Duty, Madras v Parvathi Ammal

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 170; (1975) 4 SCC 176; AIR 1975 SC 435; [1974] 97 ITR 621; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 685

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Estate Duty Act, 1953 - Scope of s.10 - Extent of non-exclusion - Gift of entire house by businessman - Donor taking back property, on lease and carrying on his business in it - Entire property held must be deemed to pass on death of donor, for estate duty - Appeal Allowed.
