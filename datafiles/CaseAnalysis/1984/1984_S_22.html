Sebastian M. Hongray v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 142; (1984) 3 SCC 82; (1984) SCC (Cr) 407; AIR 1984 SC 1026; 1984 (2) Crimes 22; 1984 CRLJ 830; 1984(1) SCALE 629; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 544; 1984 UJ 1021

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2(b) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Writ of Habeas Corpus issued to Govt. of India to produce certain persons - Govt. finally failing to do so - Submission of Govt. as to persons in question leaving certain camp, alive, found incorrect - Held, Govt. to pay Rs. 1 lac to wife of each person - Guilty of civil contempt because of their wilful disobedience to writ.
