Shri Ritesh R. Sah v Dr. Y.L. Yamul And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 560; (1996) 3 SCC 253; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 224; AIR 1996 SC 1378; JT 1996 (2) SC 495; 1996(2) SCALE 341; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 695; 1996 (2) SLR 46; 1996 (2) Supreme 585; 1996 (1) UJ 735

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226 - Reservation - Admission to M.B.B.S. and B.D.S. course - Mode for filling reserved seats - Candidate belonging to reserved category entitled to be admitted on basis of merit - Held, cannot be considered to be admitted against seats reserved for reserved category.
