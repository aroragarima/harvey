Indo International Industries v Commissioner of Sales Tax, Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, E.S. Venkataramiah, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 186; (1981) SCC (Tax) 130; (1981) 2 SCC 528; AIR 1981 SC 1079; 1986 (7) ECR 284; 1981 (8) E.L.T. 325; 1981(1) SCALE 582; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 294; 1981 (47) STC 359; 1981 TAXLR 2963; 1981 UJ 372

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: U.P. Sales Tax Act, 1948, sch. I, entries 39, 44 - Sales tax on clinical syringes - Cannot be considered as 'glassware' under entry 39 - And its turnover (before deletion of entry 44 ) would be covereede under entry dealing with 'hospital equipment' - Held, turnover from deletion of entry 44 would be taxable as unclassified item - Appeal allowed.
