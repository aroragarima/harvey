Dipak Bose Alias Naripada v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 445; (1973) 4 SCC 43; (1973) SCC (Cr) 684; AIR 1972 SC 2686; 1973 CRLR 54

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 3(1) - Constitution, art. 32 - Detention u/s. 3(1) of the Act - Grounds of - Validity - Acts of assaurt in public place resulting in death of victim may cause horror and panic - But do not necessarily cause disturbance of public order - Held, grounds related to and fell within the area of law and order and were not relevant to the object of the Act - Detention illegal - Petition Allowed.
