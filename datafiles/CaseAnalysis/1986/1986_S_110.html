Indravadan H. Shah v State Of Gujarat And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 283; (1986) Supp SCC 254; (1986) SCC (L&S) 637; AIR 1986 SC 1035; 1986 (1) CCC 985; 1986 LabIC 786; 1986 (2) LLJ 166; 1986 (2) LLN 33; 1986(1) SCALE 456; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 926; 1986 (3) SLJ 221; 1986 (2) SLR 12; 1986 (2) UJ 10

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Service

Keywords: Gujarat Judicial Service Recruitment rules 1961 (as amended up to 1979 by Gujarat Judicial Service Recruitment (Amendment) Rules 1979)

Summary: Service - Gujarat Judicial Service Recruitment Rules, 1961, rr. 6(4)(i), 6(4)(iii)(a) - Validity of Rules rr. 6(4)(i), 6(4)(iii)(a) - Appointment to post of an Assistant Judge - On promotional basis - Restriction on age - Held, above rules are unreasonable as there is no nexus to the object sought to be achieved by introducing the age restriction in regard to appointment of Assistant Judge by promotion from amongst members holding posts of Civil Judges, Junior and Senior Division whose names have been entered in the select list - Appeal allowed.
