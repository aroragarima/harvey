State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Messrs Indore Iron and Steel Mills Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1788; (1998) 6 SCC 416; AIR 1998 SC 3050; JT 1998 (5) SC 501; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 1140; 1998 (111) STC 261

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1958 ; M.P. General Sales Tax Act 1959, s.12, Sch., Item 2(b) - Notification issued by State Government dated 8-10-1978 - Exemption notification - Exemption granted from payment of sales tax on goods that had 'suffered' entry tax under Entry Tax Act - Held, use of word 'suffered' means there has to be actual payment of entry tax to claim exemption.
