Lt. C. Reethama Joseph (Mrs v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1748; (1997) 10 SCC 721

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Vires of rules which enable respondent to release petitioner from service after marriage in accordance with order of President of India - Held, though constitutional validity of this rule/order is challenged, argument cannot be accepted in view of earlier order of Supreme Court in Lt Indira Kumari Kartiayoni (1990 Indlaw SC 520) - Reserves right of petitioner to challenge order of release before appropriate authority - Petition dismissed.
