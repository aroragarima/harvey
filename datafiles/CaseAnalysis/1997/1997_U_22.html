Union of India and Others v Umosh Dhaimode

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2358; (1997) 10 SCC 223; 2002 (176) CTR 97; 1998 (77) ECR 32; 1998 (98) E.L.T. 584

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Customs - Practice & Procedure - Customs Act, 1962, s. 128(2) - Whether appellate authority vested with power to remand? - Held, an order of remand necessarily annuls decision which is under appeal before appellate authority - Appellate authority is also invested with power to pass such order as it deems fit - Appellate authority has power to set aside decision which is under appeal before it and to remand matter to authority below for fresh decision - Appeal allowed.
