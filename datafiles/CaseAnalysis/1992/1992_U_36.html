Harbans Singh Tuli And Sons Builders Pvt. Limited v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1062; (1992) 2 SCC 225; AIR 1992 SC 1124; 1992 (2) ARBLR 93; 1993 (1) BC 158; 1992 (1) CCC 494; JT 1992 (1) SC 517; 1992(1) SCALE 307; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 602; 1992 (2) UJ 538

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure

Summary: Arbitrator Act, 1940 -Appointment of arbitrator - Clause in contract providing for appointment of arbitrator - Arbitrator actually appointed by authority like court or person, by name or person holding any office - Held, appointment of arbitrator by court is illegal as conditions under s. 8(1)(b) were not satisfied and award made by him is liable to be set aside.
