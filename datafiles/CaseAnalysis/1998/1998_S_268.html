Sachida Nand Singh and Another v State of Bihar and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1947; (1998) 2 SCC 493; (1998) SCC (Cr) 660; 1998 (3) ACC 466; AIR 1998 SC 1121; 1998 (36) AllCriC 466; 1998 (1) CCR 297; 1998 (1) Crimes 142; 1998 CRLJ 1565; JT 1998 (1) SC 370; 1998 (1) RCR(Criminal) 823; 1998(1) SCALE 307; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 492; 1998 (3) SLT 90; 1998 (1) Supreme 419

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 145, 156 and 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 468, 469 and 471 - Whether prosecution can be maintained in respect of a forged document produced in court unless complaint had been filed by concerned court as u/s. 195(1)(ii) CrPC,1973? - Held, bar contained in s. 195(1)(b)(ii) is not applicable to a case where forgery of document was committed before document was produced in a court - Appeal dismissed.
