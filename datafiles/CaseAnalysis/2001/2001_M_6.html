Ouseph Mathai and Others v M. Abdul Khadir

Supreme Court of India

05 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20391; (2002) 1 SCC 319; AIR 2002 SC 110; 2002 (46) ALR 269; JT 2001 (9) SC 517; 2002 (1) KLT 3; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 182; 2001(8) SCALE 110B; 2001 (8) SLT 70; [2001] Supp5 S.C.R. 118; 2001 (8) Supreme 262

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 227 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, ss. 11(2)(c) and 20 - Lease and rent - Property - (A) Whether High Court u/art. 227 in setting aside the judgment of Appellate Authority in dismissing respondent tenant application u/s. 11(2)(c) of Act confirmed is valid? - Held, u/s. 11(2)(c) even after passing of eviction order further rights are granted upon tenant and not entitled to discretion u/art. 227 - HC order set aside by upholding order passed by appellate and revisional authority against respondent tenant - Appeal allowed without any costs.
