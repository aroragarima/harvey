Messrs Nahar Industrial Enterprises Limited and v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 608; (2004) 7 SCC 618; 2004 (170) E.L.T. 518; JT 2004 (6) SC 374; 2004 (63) RLT 805; 2004(6) SCALE 535; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 432

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Textiles; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excise and Salt Act, 1944 - Additional Excise Act - s. 3 - Additional Excise Duty - Circular and notifications issued issued by Central Excise authorities for payment of AED on yarn cleared in DTA - Correctness of clarification issued vide one of circular - HC held that introduction of words 'or any other law for time being in force' took away exemptions granted to manufacturers like appellants under Notification No 55/91-CE - Whether by addition of words 'or under any other law for time being in force' producers or manufacturers other than 100% EOUs incur a liability to pay AED? - Held, 100% EOU is exempt from paying duty in excess of amount of BED plus amount of AED plus any other duties of excise under any other law for time being in force, paid by producer or manufacturer who is not 100% EOU - Circular quashed - Appeals allowed partly.
