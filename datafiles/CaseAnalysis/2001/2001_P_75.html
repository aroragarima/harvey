Commissioner of Customs v Parasrampuria Synthetics Limited

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20641; (2001) 9 SCC 74; AIR 2001 SC 3501; 2001 (98) ECR 185; 2001 (133) E.L.T. 9; [2002] 253 ITR 274; JT 2001 (7) SC 243; 2001 (46) RLT 499; 2001(6) SCALE 53; 2001 (6) SLT 378; 2002 (125) STC 255; [2001] Supp2 S.C.R. 333; 2001 (6) Supreme 810

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Functional Test

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 28(1) and 112 - Income Tax Act - Notification No. 25/95, Cus, D/- 16-03-1995 - Whether benefit of exemption under Notification No. 25/95, D/- 16-03-1995, as amended, is available to goods, i.e., printed drawings, designs and plans under Foreign Transfer of Technology Agreement imported by respondent? - Legislature has used words 'printed books' and clarified it by inclusion of covers - Intent thus seems to be rather obvious to mean books in ordinary sense and not any other meaning - Held, benefit of said notification in not available to respondent - Appeal allowed.
