S. P. S. Balasubramanyam v Suruttayan Alias Andali Padayachi and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 408; (1992) Supp2 SCC 304; AIR 1992 SC 756; AIR 1992 SCW 441

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Family & Personal - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 112 and 114 - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 5 - Cohabitation of spouse - Validity of marriage - Presumption - Man and woman living under same roof and cohabiting for a number of years - Presumption that they lived as husband and wife arises - Presumption, whether destroyed by circumstance and evidence proved in instant case? - Legitimacy of children born to them, if illegitimate - Held, presumption, not destroyed - Not illegitimate - Appeal allowed.
