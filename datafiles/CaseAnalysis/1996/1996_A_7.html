State Of Tamil Nadu v A. Jaganathan

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2887; (1996) 5 SCC 329; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1026; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 593; AIR 1996 SC 2449; AIR 1996 SCW 3009; 1996 (3) CCR 85; 1996 (3) Crimes 257; 1996 CRLJ 3495; JT 1996 (6) SC 621; 1997 (1) LLJ 565; 1996(5) SCALE 382; 1996 (3) SLJ 9; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 572; 1996 (5) Supreme 616

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 389(1) and 482 - Suspension of conviction and sentence during pendency of appeal/revision - Discretion - Charge of serious criminal offences to Govt. Servants - Held, without considering above - Exercise of discretion not proper.
