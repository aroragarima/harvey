K. Raheja Constructions Limited and Another v Alliance Ministers and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 325; (1995) Supp3 SCC 17; AIR 1995 SC 1768; 1996 (2) BC 152; 1995 (2) CCC 363; 1996 (3) CLT 213; 1995(3) SCALE 692B; 1995 (3) SCJ 54; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 960

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 17 - Limitation Act , 1908 - Amendment of plaint - Suit for permanent injunction - To restrain alienating, encumbering of property etc. - Amendment of plaint sought for relief of specific performance of contract - Plea, it is necessary in view of subsequent knowledge about permission being granted by Charity Commissioner to alienation - Whether relief can be granted when defendannts refused to abide contract and after seven years - Held, plea not tenable - Relief for specific performance ought to have been asked in original suit itself and cannot be added , as barred by limitation - Petition dismissed.
