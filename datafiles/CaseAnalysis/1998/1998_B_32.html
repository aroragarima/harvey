Union of India and Another v Banwari Lal Agarwal

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2044; (1998) 7 SCC 652; AIR 1999 SC 196; 1999 (156) CTR 300; [1999] 238 ITR 461; JT 1998 (7) SC 384; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 356; [1998] 101 TAXMAN 508

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income - Tax Act, 1961, s. 279(2) - Tax evasion - Prosecution for - Quashing of - On plea that a compromise assessment with an understanding not to take penal action was made - Court accepting plea - No reliance on any material on record or any provision in respect thereof - Held, is improper - Giving of prior notice or opportunity of hearing for compounding of offence - Not necessary.
