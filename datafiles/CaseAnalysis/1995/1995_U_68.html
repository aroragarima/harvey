National Federation Of S.B.I. And Others Etc. v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, R.M. Sahai, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1763; (1995) 3 SCC 532; (1995) SCC (L&S) 730; AIR 1995 SC 1457; 1995 (29) ATC 622; 1995 (1) CLR 790; JT 1995 (3) SC 336; 1995 LabIC 1795; 1996 (1) LLJ 435; 1995 (1) LLN 1135; 1995(2) SCALE 282; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 748; 1995 (3) SLJ 107; 1995 (2) SLR 397; 1995 (2) UJ 157

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16(1)(4) - Appointment - Public Sector Banks - Promotion by selection - Class I - Employees of Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes - Office Memorandum providing that senior Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes officers in zone of consideration for promotion so as to be within number of vacancies for which select list has to be drawn up, would be included in select list provided not unfit - Held, speaks of concession and not reservation as contemplated by art. 16(4) - In absence of such reservation forty-point roster cannot be applied - No retrospective effect.
