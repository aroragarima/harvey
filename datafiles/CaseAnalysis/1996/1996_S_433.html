Sri Narayan Bal and Others v Sridhar Sutar and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3166; (1996) 8 SCC 54; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 932; AIR 1996 SC 2371; AIR 1996 SCW 899; 1996 (1) CCC 161; 1996 (1) CLT 324; 1996 (1) HLR 174; JT 1996 (1) SC 711; 1996 (4) KarLJ 302; 1996(1) SCALE 570; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 999; 1996 (1) Supreme 638; 1996 (1) UJ 315

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Minority, Adoption & Guardianship

Summary: Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956 - Joint Hindu family property in which minor had an undivided share - Whether sale/disposed of by Karta is valid for minor's property? - Without prior permission of court - Held, previous permission of Court before disposing said property not required - Joint Hindu family by itself is a legal entity capable of acting through its Karta.
