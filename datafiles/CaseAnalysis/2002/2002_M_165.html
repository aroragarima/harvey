Messrs Konkan Railway Corporation Limited and v Messrs Rani Construction Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

30 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.S.M. Quadri, U.C. Banerjee, S.N. Variava, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 60; (2002) 2 SCC 388; AIR 2002 SC 778; 2002 (2) ALD(SC) 14; 2002 (46) ARBLR 326; 2002 (6) Bom.C.R. 313; 2002 (3) CLC 88; 2002 (1) CompLJ 393; JT 2002 (1) SC 587; 2002 (2) MLJ(SC) 28; 2002 (1) RAJ 165; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 845; 2002(1) SCALE 465; 2002 (1) SCJ 556; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 728; 2002 (1) SLT 533; 2002 (1) Supreme 419; 2002 (1) UC 582

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: The order of the Chief Justice or his designate under Section 11 of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 nominating an arbitrator is not an adjudicatory order and the Chief Justice or his designate is not a tribunal. Furhter, such an order cannot properly be made the subject of a petition for special leave to appeal under Article 136.
