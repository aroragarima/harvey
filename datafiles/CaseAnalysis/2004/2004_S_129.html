State of Rajasthan and another v Senior Higher Secondary School, Lachhmangarh and others

Supreme Court of India

15 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, D.M. Dharmadhikari, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1756; (2005) 10 SCC 346; 2005 (4) RLW 3040

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Leave Encashment, Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions Act, 1989

Summary: Service - Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions Act, 1989, ss.2(r), 16, 29 - Leave encashment benefits - Entitlement - Claim for allowances - Teachers of non-government educational institutions NGEIs claimed that they were entitled to leave encashment benefits after retirement under the Act and the Rules framed - Full Bench of HC held that by virtue of s.29 of the Act, teachers of NGEIs were entitled to similar benefit as was allowed to teachers of govt. educational institutions and leave encashment was 'allowance' and teachers in NGEIs would have right to claim allowance - Hence, instant Appeal - Whether leave encashment was 'allowance' and teachers in NGEIs would have right to claim this allowance on par with employees of govt. institutions - Held, s. 29 of the Act directed maintenance of parity in respect of 'scales of pay and allowances' between same categories of employees of private aided institutions and Govt. institutions - However, wider definition of word 'salary' was to be read into s. 29 of the Act which directed maintenance of parity in pay and allowances of employees of aided institutions and Govt. institutions and expression included benefit of leave encashment which was nothing but salary for unveiled leave to credit of employee - Therefore, s. 16 of the Act conferred rule-making power on State Govt. to frame rules regarding entitlement of leave, but it was beyond purview of s.29 of the Act, was fallacious - Hence, HC had not committed any error in holding that employee of aided private educational institutions in question were entitled to benefit of leave encashment on par with employees of Govt. institutions - Appeal dismissed.
