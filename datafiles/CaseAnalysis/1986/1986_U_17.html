R.P. Bhatt v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 240; (1986) SCC (L&S) 330; (1986) 2 SCC 651; AIR 1986 SC 1040; 1986 ATC 37; 1986 (52) FLR 354; 1986 LabIC 790; 1986 (2) LLN 1; 1986(1) SCALE 322; 1986 (1) SLJ 383; 1986 (1) SLR 470

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Central Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1965 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts.309, Proviso and 311(2) - Non compliance of requirement u/r.27(2) of Service Rules - Validity of order dismissing appeal against order passed by the Director-General, Border Roads Organization as to removal of employee from service - Held, was liable to be set aside - Appeal allowed.
