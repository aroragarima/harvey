Kunj Behari Lal Butail and Others v State of Himachal Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, S. Saghir Ahmad, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 160; (2000) 3 SCC 40; AIR 2000 SC 1069; JT 2000 (2) SC 307; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 10; 2000(1) SCALE 691; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1054; 2000 (2) SLT 620; 2000 (2) Supreme 254

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Bihar Money Lenders Act, 1938, Land Ceiling Act, 1972, Himachal Pradesh Ceiling on Land Holdings Rules, 1973

Summary: Himachal Pradesh Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1972, s. 26 - H P Ceiling on Land Holdings Rules 1973, r. 3(1), Proviso - Validity - Land subservient to tea plantation - Prohibition on alienation - Powers delegated by Act - Held, tea estates were already excluded by Act itself - Beyond powers of state Govt.
