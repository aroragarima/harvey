B. R. Limited v V. P. Gupta, Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 419; (1978) 3 SCC 70; (1978) SCC (Tax) 141; AIR 1978 SC 1320; 1978 CTR 82; [1978] 113 ITR 647; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 877; 1978 TAXLR 881; 1978 UJ 497

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 24(2) - Carry forward and set off of business losses - Mixed question of law and fact - Test to determine the question 'Whether the business was the same business ' - Held, common management, a common business organisation, a common administration, a common fund and a common place of business showed in the instant case the interlacing and inter dependence of the businesses carried on - Appeal Allowed.
