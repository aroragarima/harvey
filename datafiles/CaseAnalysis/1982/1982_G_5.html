Gambhir v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 27; (1982) 2 SCC 351; (1982) SCC (Cr) 431; AIR 1982 SC 1157; 1982 (2) Bom.C.R. 471; 1982 CRLJ 1243; 1982 CRLR 275; 1982 MLJ 396; 1982(1) SCALE 388; 1982 UJ 475

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, ss. 299, 300 and 302 - Evidence Act,1872, ss. 3, 45 - Appreciation of evidence in murder case based on circumstantial evidence - Tests to be satisfied - High Court's approach in appreciating and convicting unsatisfactory - Held, conviction and sentence set aside - Appeal allowed.
