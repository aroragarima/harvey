State of Madhya Pradesh v Srikant Chaphekar

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 406; (1992) 4 SCC 689; (1993) SCC (L&S) 48; AIR 1993 SC 1221; 1993 (23) ATC 377; 1992 (2) CLR 915; 1992 (65) FLR 926; JT 1992 (5) SC 638; 1993 LabIC 96; 1993 (2) LLJ 662; 1992 (2) LLN 743; 1992(2) SCALE 370; 1992 (3) SLJ 73; 1992 (5) SLR 635; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 345; 1992 (2) UJ 593; 1992 (2) UPLBEC 1380

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Administrative - Service - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Jurisdiction of Tribunal - Promotion - Denial of - Annual confidential report - Adverse remarks - Conclusions by Tribunals that adverse remarks were vague and not sufficient to deny employee his promotion - Order directing promotion of employee on that basis - Whether competent - Held, not competent to pass such order - Appeal allowed.
