Ghafoor Ahmad Khan v Bashir Ahmad Khan (Dead) By L. Rs

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 227; (1982) 3 SCC 486; AIR 1983 SC 123; 1982(2) SCALE 1372; 1982 UJ 829

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 22, r. 10 - Abatement - High Court's order directing that appeal has abated and is, therefore, dismissed cannot be sustained - This is not a case where on death of sole respondent his heirs are sought to be brought on record - It is a case of devolution of interest and case falls under O. 22, r. 10 and there will be no question of abatement - Matter remitted to High Court.
