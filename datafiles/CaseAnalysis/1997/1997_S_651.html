Mohd. Aman and Another v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2152; (1997) 10 SCC 44; (1997) SCC (Cr) 777; AIR 1997 SC 2960; 1997 (3) AWC 1480; 1997 (4) CCR 188; 1997 CRLJ 3567; JT 1997 (5) SC 451; 1997(4) SCALE 134; 1997 (4) Supreme 635

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 302 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 4, 5 and 45 - Appeal against conviction - Prosecution failed to prove that seized article was not tampered before sending to Bureau - Specimen finger - prints not taken before Magistrate - Seized articles not produced and exhibited - Conviction on basis of finger - print report not proper - Possibility of tampering and fabrication of evidence - Appellants acquitted - Appeal allowed.
