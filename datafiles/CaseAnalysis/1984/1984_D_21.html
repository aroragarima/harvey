Ghanshyam Dass And Others v Dominion Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 128; (1984) 3 SCC 46; AIR 1984 SC 1004; 1984 (10) ALR 318; 1984(1) SCALE 528; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 229; 1984 UJ 604

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 80 - Notice u/s.80 - Death of contractor after issuing notice for recovery of dues on govt. under the contract - Subsequently his son instituted suit before amendment in 1976 - Whether fresh notice is required - Held, no fresh notice was necessary and the notice already served enured to their benefit - Appeal allowed.
