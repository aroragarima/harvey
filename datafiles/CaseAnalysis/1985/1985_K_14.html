The Commissioner Of Wealth Tax Gujarat, Ahmedabad v Kantilal Manilal Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 308; (1985) 2 SCC 343; AIR 1985 SC 924; 1985 (45) CTR 220; [1985] 152 ITR 447; 1985(1) SCALE 446; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 297; 1985 TAXLR 850; 1985 UJ 737

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s.2(m)(iii)(a) - Computation of wealth tax - Wealth tax liability on debt owed by assessee - Notice of demand served on assessee subsequent to valuation date - (A) Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was right in holding that the provisions of s. 2(m)(iii)(a) were not applicable in respect of liabilities arising under the wealth-tax assessments of the assessee for the assessment years 1960-61 and 1961-62 ? (B) Whether claim of assessee that tax is not payable must be subsisting on valuation date - Held, s. 2(m) is not attracted - Challenge need not be subsisting on valuation date - Appeals dismissed.
