Sri Srinivasa Theatre and Others v Government Of Tamil Nadu And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 312; (1992) 2 SCC 643; AIR 1992 SC 999; AIR 1992 SCW 899; JT 1992 (2) SC 312; 1992(1) SCALE 643; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 164; 1993 (89) STC 201

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Media & Entertainment - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Act, 1939, ss. 5A and 5B - Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporation Act - Tamil Nadu Gram Panchayats Act - Validity of s. 5-A (1) of Act challenged on several grounds but negatived by High Court - Legality and correctness of HC judgment challenged - Court held, classification or the distinction made between theatres situated within the belt and the theatres outside the belt is not an unreasonable one and also establishes that the theatres within the belt are akin to and comparable to the theatres situated within the areas of corporations and specials grade municipalities - Appeals dismissed.
