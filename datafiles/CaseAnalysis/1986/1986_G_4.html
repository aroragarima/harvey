Basavantappa v Gangadhar Narayan Dharwadkar and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 619; (1986) 4 SCC 273; AIR 1987 SC 53; 1986 (12) ALR 686; 1986 (12) AWC 1097; [1986] 162 ITR 373; JT 1986 SC 443; 1986(2) SCALE 431; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 734; 1987 (1) UJ 3

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O.21, rr.89, 92(2) - Limitation Act, 1908, art.127 - Execution of decree - Application for setting aside of auction sale along with deposit of decretal amount u/r.89, O.21- Whether by reason of O.21 r. 92(2) the deposit required by r. 89 not having been made within 30 from the date of sale, the application made by the judgment-debtor was not maintainable? - Held, sale was liable to be set aside - Construing the provisions of O.21 rr. 89 and 92(2) of the Code and that of art. 127 of the Limitation Act harmoniously - Petition dismissed.
