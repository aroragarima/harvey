Afjal Imam v State Of Bihar & Ors

Supreme Court of India

01 April 2011

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Panchal, H.L. Gokhale

Where Reported

2011 Indlaw SC 218; (2011) 4 SCC 725; JT 2011 (4) SC 155; 2011 (5) RCR(Civil) 231; 2011(4) SCALE 151; [2011] 4 S.C.R. 174

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Entitlement of, Municipal Councillors

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Practice & Procedure - Bihar Municipal Act, 2007, s. 24 - Municipal Councillors - Entitlement of -

Respondent no.3 District Magistrate was directed to administer oath of secrecy u/s. 24 of the Act to seven Municipal Councillors nominated by appellant to Empowered Standing Committee - Appellant as well as members of Empowered Standing Committee should be entitled to exercise all powers as Mayor and members of Empowered Standing Committee as provided in the Act in accordance with law - Appeal allowed.
