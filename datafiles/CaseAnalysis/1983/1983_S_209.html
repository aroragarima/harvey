T. V. Vatheeswaran v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 441; (1983) 2 SCC 68; (1983) SCC (Cr) 342; AIR 1983 SC 361B; 1983 BBCJ 60; 1983 CRLJ 481; 1983 (2) MLJ 39; 1983 (2) MLJ(SC) 39; 1983(1) SCALE 115; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 348

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19 and 21 - Death sentence - Prolonged delay in execution of a sentence of death - Whether lawful to execute death sentence after a long gap of eight years? - Solitary confinement for eight years - Held, delay of more than two years in executing death sentence entitles person to invoke art. 21 and demand quashing death sentence - Death sentence modified to life imprisonment - Appeal allowed.
