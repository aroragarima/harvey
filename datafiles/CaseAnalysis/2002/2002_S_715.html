Md. Israils and others v State of West Bengal and others

Supreme Court of India

07 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 6; (2002) SCC (L&S) 294; (2002) 2 SCC 306; AIR 2002 SC 468; 2002 (18) CLR 988; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 483; JT 2002 (5) SC 26; 2002(1) SCALE 8; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 13; 2002 (2) SLR 299; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1850

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: West Bengal Services (Determination of Seniority) Rules, 1981, West Bengal Services (Revision of Pay & Allowances) Rules, 1970, West Bengal Public Service Commission (Consultation by Governor) Regulation of 1995

Summary: If the promotion of employees is purely on ad hoc basis and subject to the approval of the Public Service Commission, then the services rendered on such ad hoc basis till the approval of the Public Service Commission is obtained, will not count for reckoning the seniority in the cadre.
