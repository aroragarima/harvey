State of Orissa and Others v Commissioner of Land Records and Settlement,Cuttack and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1176; (1998) 7 SCC 162; AIR 1998 SC 3067; JT 1998 (5) SC 662; 1998(4) SCALE 682; 1998 (7) SLT 128; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 130; 1998 (7) Supreme 31

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950, Orissa Forest Act, 1972

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 47, r. 1 - Orissa Board of Revenue Act, 1951, s. 7 - Orissa Survey and Settlement Act, 1959, s. 15 - Review - Powers of - Order of delegate of Commr. in revisional jurisdiction - Held, Commr. can review and not Board - Revisional power under s. 7 is not wider than O. 47, r. 1 of CPC - Review intends correction of 'mistake or errors apparent on face of record'.
