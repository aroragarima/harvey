West Bengal Housing Board Etc. v Brijendra Prasad Gupta And Others Etc.

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2445; (1997) 6 SCC 207; AIR 1997 SC 2745; JT 1997 (6) SC 169; 1997(4) SCALE 590; [1997] Supp2 S.C.R. 22; 1997 (6) Supreme 474; 1997 (2) UJ 378

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Bombay Land Requisition Act, 1948

Summary: Land and property - WB Land (Requisition and Acquisition) Act, 1948, ss. 4, 50 - Registration Act, 1908 - Requisition of land - Public purpose - (A) Notice served on persons recorded as owners in Record of Rights - If proper - (B) Housing for poor - State Govt. and Housing Board not able to construct - So Housing Boards Act amended and joint sector company incorporated - Approval by govt. - Housing Board to oversee the Project - Legality and mode adopted, challenged - Held, is proper and satisfies requirements of law - Mode adopted by Govt. cannot be said to be not in public interest or arbitrary and irrational - Appeal allowed.
