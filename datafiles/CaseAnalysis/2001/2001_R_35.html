Ashok Nagar Welfare Association and another v R.K. Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 189; (2002) 1 SCC 749; AIR 2002 SC 335; JT 2001 (Supp2) SC 24; 2002 (131) PLR 757; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 512; 2001(8) SCALE 503; 2001 (8) SLT 415; [2001] Supp5 S.C.R. 662; 2001 (8) Supreme 647; 2002 (1) UC 270

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Special leave to appeal under Article 136 of the Constitution cannot be granted on the question whether the Division Bench or the High Court could entertain the appeal under Section 10 of the Delhi High Court Act despite the bar under Section 6(3) of the Specific Relief Act.
