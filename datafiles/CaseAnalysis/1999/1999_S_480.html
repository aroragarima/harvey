Veena Hasmukh Jain and Another v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 944; (1999) 5 SCC 725; AIR 1999 SC 807; AIR 1999 SCW 429; 1999 (2) Bom.C.R. 124; JT 1999 (1) SC 186; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 302

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Bombay Stamp Act, 1958, explaination I to art. 25 of sch. I, 32-A - Maharashtra Apartment Ownership Act, 1971 - Maharashtra Ownership Flats (Regulation of Promotion of Construction, Sale, Management and Transfer) Act (MOF Act), 1963, s. 4 - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17(1) - Agreement for sale was executed in terms of s. 4 of MOF Act - It was lodged for registration as required u/s. 4 of MOF Act r/w s. 17(1) of Registration Act - Whether document in question is an agreement to sell, is a document conveying right, title and interest of flat in favour of appellants? - Held, provisions of s. 32-A of 1958 Act shall apply mutatis mutandis to such agreement which is deemed to be a conveyance under provisions of explaination I to art. 25 of sch. I of 1958 Act - Agreement entered into clearly provides for sale of an immovable property and there is also a specific time within which possession has to be delivered - Document in question clearly falls within scope of explaination I of 1958 Act - Appeals dismissed.
