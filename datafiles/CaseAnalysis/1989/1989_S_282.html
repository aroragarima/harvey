Commissioner Of Income Tax, Amritsar v Straw Board Manufacturing Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 744; (1989) Supp2 SCC 523; AIR 1989 SC 1490; 1989 (2) CompLJ 176; 1989 (77) CTR 75; [1989] 177 ITR 431; JT 1989 (2) SC 264; 1989(1) SCALE 1151; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 772; [1989] 44 TAXMAN 189

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 33, 80E, sch. 5, item 16 - Development rebate - Priority industry - Claim for concessions - Straw board industry - Whether can be described as part of paper and pulp industry? - Held, can be described as forming part of paper and pulp industry - Appeals dismissed.
