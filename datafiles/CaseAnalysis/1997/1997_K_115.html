State of Punjab and Others v Krishan Niwas

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1822; (1997) 9 SCC 31; (1997) SCC (L&S) 998; AIR 1997 SC 2349; 1997 (1) CLR 855; JT 1997 (4) SC 213; 1997 LabIC 2296; 1997(3) SCALE 338; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 1135; 1997 (2) SLR 514; 1997 (3) Supreme 551

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 325 - Employee dismissed from service on conviction in a murder case - On appeal, his conviction was altered from s. 302 to s. 325 - Department Appellate authority imposed reduced punishment in view of altered conviction - Employee accepted said punishment and joined post - Whether subsequent application by employee challenging reduced punishment barred? - Held, it would be barred as he accepted punishment - Appeal allowed.
