K. L. Rathee v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1738; (1997) 6 SCC 7; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1253; AIR 1997 SC 2763; 1997 (2) CLR 423; 1997 (77) FLR 197; JT 1997 (5) SC 698; 1997 LabIC 2853; 1998 (1) LLJ 159; 1998 (1) LLN 357; 1997(4) SCALE 384; 1997 (4) SLR 545; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 426; 1997 (7) Supreme 248; 1997 (2) UJ 103

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, arts.16 and 309 ; Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules 1972, r.5(1) - Computation of pensionary benefits was in queastion - Average of last ten month's emoluments form basis for calculation of pension - Held, quantum of emoluments during last ten months of service of each Government employee must not be assumed to be the same.
