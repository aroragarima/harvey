A.I. Railway Parcel & Goods Porters Union v Union Of India & Ors

Supreme Court of India

22 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 655; (2003) 11 SCC 590; (2004) SCC (L&S) 100; AIR 2003 SC 3673; 2003 (106) DLT 604; 2003 (99) FLR 203; JT 2003 (9) SC 269; 2003 LabIC 3137; 2003 (3) LLJ 827; 2003 (4) LLN 22; 2003(6) SCALE 774; 2003 (1) SCW 4165; 2004 (1) SLJ 319; 2003 (6) SLR 16; 2003 (5) SLT 498; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 842; 2003 (6) Supreme 222; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1187

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Writ petitions disposed of

Summary: Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 - s. 10 - Regularisation - Abolition of contract system of labour - Railway parcel and goods porters - Whether to be treated as permanent employees of the Railways as they are discharging duties which are of permanent and perennial nature working continuously for long years without any break in service - Held, the facts disclosed in the report and the findings recorded in regard to the perennial nature of work cannot be overruled - Directions issued.
