Indian Overseas Bank v Indian Overseas Bank Officer Association and

Supreme Court of India

04 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 70; (2001) 9 SCC 540; AIR 2001 SC 4007; 2001 (3) CLR 772; 2001 (91) FLR 623; 2001 (3) JhrCR(SC) 327; JT 2001 (8) SC 306; 2001 (2) LLJ 1417; 2001(6) SCALE 626; 2001 (4) SCJ 577; 2002 (1) SLJ 97; 2002 (4) SLR 153; 2001 (7) SLT 109; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 554; 2001 (7) Supreme 758

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service Regulations of Banks containing a provision prohibiting an officer/employee in any disciplinary proceedings to take the assistance of any other officer-employee having two or more pending on hand in which he is giving assistance to defend him held valid and not discriminatory.
