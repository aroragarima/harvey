G. Claridge And Company Limited v Collector Of Central Excise, Pune

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 876; (1991) 2 SCC 229; 1991 CRLR 253; 1993 CrLR(SC) 253; 1991 (33) ECR 433; 1991 (52) E.L.T. 341; JT 1991 (1) SC 394; 1991(1) SCALE 188; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 317

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excise Rule, 1944, r. 8 - Whether egg trays and other similar products manufactured by appellant can be regarded as 'containers' under relevant entries in Central Excise Tariff? - Expression 'containers' has to be constructed to mean 'packing containers' which are analogous to boxes and cartons, that is an enclosed receptacle which can be used for storage and transportation of articles - Held, Appellate Tribunal was right in taking view that egg trays and other similar products manufactured by appellant cannot be regarded as 'containers' under relevant items of Excise Tariff - Appeals dismissed.
