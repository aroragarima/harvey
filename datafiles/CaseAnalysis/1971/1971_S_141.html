Â Trilochan Mishra Etc v State Of Orissa & Ors

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 753; (1971) 3 SCC 153; AIR 1971 SC 733

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Summary: Orissa Kendu Leaves (Control of Trade) Act, 1961 - Constitutional validity of ss. 3(2)(a), 5B(7), 5B(8), 5B(9) and 5B(16), rr. 5B(6), 6(3), 7(1) and 8(1) framed under the Act - Held, are not violative of arts.14, l 5, 19 (1) of the Constitution - Petition Dismissed.
