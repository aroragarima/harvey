JAGANNATH AGARWALA v STATE of ORISSA

Supreme Court of India

08 March 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.L. Kapur, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 352; AIR 1961 SC 1361; 1962 (1) SCJ 179; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 205

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Miscellaneous - Board of Revenue Act, 1913 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Administration of Mayurbhanj State Order, 1949 - Whether appellant should have been served with a notice by the Board before recommendations of the Claims Officer were rejected? - Court held, Member, Board of Revenue, asked appellant to submit all documents and arguments in support of his contention that the claim was within limitation, and to that extent, therefore opportunity might have been afforded to appellant - Appeal dismissed.
