Â Lachhman Dass and Others v Municipal Committee, Jalalabad and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, Mohammad Hidayatullah, R.S. Bachawat, G.K. Mitter, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 240; (1969) 1 SCC 653; AIR 1969 SC 1126; [1969] 3 S.C.R. 645

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Land & Property - Administration of Evacuee Property (Central) Rules, 1950 - Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19 and 31 - Displaced Persons (Compensation and Rehabilitation) Act, 1954, ss. 20A and 20B - Evacuee Interest Separation Act, 1951 - Whether s. 20B of Displaced Persons (Compensation and Rehabilitation) Act, 1954 be declared ultra vires Constitution? - Held, u/s. 20B Central Govt. is entitled not to restore property to serve a purpose other than a public purpose also section does not fix any compensation or lay down any principles for compensation consequently section is ultra vires art. 31(2) of Constitution of India, 1950 - Appeals dismissed.
