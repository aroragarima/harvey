Printers House Pvt. Limited v Saiyadan

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1518; (1994) 2 SCC 133; AIR 1994 SC 1160; 1993 (3) CCC 668; JT 1993 (6) SC 123; 1993 (2) RentLR 612; 1993(4) SCALE 173

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of market value - Method of valuation of separate plots of acquired lands with differing features - Should not be valued at uniform rate per unity measure - Not correct , court is to consider price reflected in sale/award for closest or nearest land acquired - Appeal No. 369-371 of 1976 dismissed; Appeal No.946-48 of 1977 partly allowed.
