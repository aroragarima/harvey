Karam Pal And Ors. Etc v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.B. Misra, P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 295; (1985) 2 SCC 457; (1985) SCC (L&S) 471; AIR 1985 SC 774; 1985 (50) FLR 414; 1985 LabIC 592; 1985(1) SCALE 488; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 271; 1985 (1) SLR 639; 1985 UJ 624

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 16, 32, 309 and 311 - Central Secretariat Service Rules, 1962, rr.13, 18 - Assistants - Determination of seniority - Select list for grade of Section Officers - Interference sought, despite substantial compliance in implementing scheme under Rules - Held, court cannot interfere - Petition fails.
