Amalgamated Electricity Company v Municipal Committee, Ajmer

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, R.S. Bachawat

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 432; AIR 1969 SC 227; [1969] 1 S.C.R. 430

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 80 - Ajmer Merwar Municipalities Regulation, 1925, regn. 233 - Bombay Electricity Surcharge Act, 1946, ss. 3, 4 and 6 - Indian Electricity Act, 1910, s. 35, cl. 12 of Schedule - Ajmer Merwar (Extension of Laws) Act, 1947, s. 2 - Suit for surcharge on bills decreed but in appeal High Court dismissed suit - Whether decision of HC is in accordance with law? - Held, cl. 12 of Schedule in Electricity Act imposes no fetters on powers of provincial legislatures in matter of hanging price of electricity supplied by license for street lighting, therefore HC is wrong that suit is bad because of want of a valid notice u/s. 233 of Ajmer Merwar Municipalities Regulation or notification imposing surcharge is invalid for any reason - Appeal allowed.
