Major Pakhar Singh Atwal & Ors. v The State Of Punjab & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 571; (1995) Supp2 SCC 401; AIR 1995 SC 2185; 1995 (1) CCC 358; JT 1995 (2) SC 379; 1995 (1) RentLR 576; 1995(1) SCALE 826; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 535

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s.35; Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.23; Constitution of India, art.136 - Compensation - Claim for enhancement - Determination by Tribunal - Proper process by Tribunal for determination - Held, not interfered - Claimants however entitled to solatium at 30% on enhanced compensation - Mutation entries - Not admissible in evidence.
