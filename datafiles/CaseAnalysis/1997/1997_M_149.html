Mansukhlal Vithaldas Chauhan v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2751; (1997) 7 SCC 662; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 705

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Service; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Companies Act, 1956 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - Anti- Corruption Laws (Amendment) Act, 1964, s. 6 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 161 - Appellant convicted by Trial Court and upheld by High Court - Whether there was valid sanction to prosecute appellant who was a public servant? - Meaning of 'public servant' and 'sanction' discussed - Held, no valid sanction given to prosecute appellant - HC incorrect in exceeding its powers u/art. 226 - Appellant acquitted - Appeal allowed.
