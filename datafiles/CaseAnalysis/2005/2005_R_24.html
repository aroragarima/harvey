State of Maharashtra and another v R.S. Bhonde and others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 479; (2005) 6 SCC 751; (2005) SCC (L&S) 907; 2005 (6) Bom.C.R. 826; 2005 (106) FLR 1212; JT 2005 (7) SC 400; 2005(6) SCALE 558; 2005 (5) SLR 388; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 763

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Maharashtra Recognition Of Trade Unions And Prevention Of Unfair Labour Practices Act, 1971, s. 28 - Punjabrao Krishi University (Krishi Vidyapeeth) Act, 1968, s. 50(B) - Regularisation of service - HC held that Industrial Court's order of reinstatement was to be modified by excluding words 'subject to approval of State Govt' - Held, prayer for enforcement of Industrial Court's order in its original form could not have been made, when same had been modified by HC's order which had attained finality - Appeal allowed.
