Ex. Major N. C. Singhal v Director-General, Armed Forces Medical Services, New Delhi and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 59; (1972) 4 SCC 765; AIR 1972 SC 628; 1972 LabIC 342; 1972 (1) LLJ 249; 1972 SLR 178; 1972 UJ 388

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Conditions of service of persons serving, Union or a State - Power to alter or modify conditions - With retrospective effect and to prejudice of Govt. servant - Held, Govt. has no power to alter or modify conditions of service of a Govt. servant with retrospective effect to prejudice of Govt. servant - Appeal partly allowed.
