M. P. Davis v Commissioner of Agricultural Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 164; AIR 1959 SC 719; [1959] 35 ITR 803

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Coorg Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1951

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Trusts & Associations - Contract & Commercial - Partnership Act, 1932, s. 6 - Coorg Agricultural Income-Tax Act, 1951, ss. 26 and 54(2) - What are elements to be taken into account in order to find real relationship between parties under s. 6 of Partnership Act, 1932 - Held, in determining whether a partnership exists or not, regard shall be had of all circumstances of case, especially conduct of parties, together with important terms of document - In circumstances, it cannot be said that it was intended to bring about relation of partnership - Appeal dismissed.
