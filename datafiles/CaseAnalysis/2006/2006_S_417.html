State of Karnataka and Others v C. Lalitha

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 31; (2006) 2 SCC 747; JT 2006 (2) SC 322; 2006(2) SCALE 73; 2006 (2) SCJ 561; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 971; 2006 (2) SLR 599

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Validity of an amendment of reservation policy of State - This Court declared that revised reservation policy was not applicable to selection initiated prior thereto - SLP by respondent allowed holding that she would be placed below last candidate for purposes of seniority - Appellant directed to consider respondent's seniority in terms of order passed in SLP - Appeal partly allowed.
