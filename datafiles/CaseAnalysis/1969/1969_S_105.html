Â Mohd. Faruk v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 355; (1969) 1 SCC 853; AIR 1970 SC 93; 1969 MahLJ 950; 1969 MPLJ 874; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 156

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution, art. 19(1)(g) - M.P. Municipal Corporation Act, 1956, s. 430 - Right to carry on trade in slaughtering bulls and bullocks - Restriction on right - Byelaws of Municipality regulating such trade - Notification issued by Governor cancelling bye-laws having effect of prohibiting trade - Constitutionality - Notification held, is violative of art. 19(1)(g) - Petition Allowed.
