Mhadagonda Ramgonda Patil And Ors. v Shripal Balwant Rainade And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, K.N. Singh, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 456; (1988) 3 SCC 298; AIR 1988 SC 1200; 1988 BBCJ 68; JT 1988 (2) SC 759; 1988(1) SCALE 958; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 689; 1988 (2) UJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Final Decree For Sale, Conditional Sale, Final Decree For Foreclosure

Summary: Banking and Finance - CPC, 1908 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 58 and 60 - (A) Suit for redemption of mortgage - Not a mortgage by conditional sale or an anomalous mortgage - Final decree - Non redemption, as fail to deposit decretal amount - Not a merger of mortgage debt in decretal debt - Second suit for redemption by mortgagor - Maintainability - (B) Mortgage - Applicability of rule of Damdupat - Held, maintainable - Rule of Damdupat is applicable to mortgage as the inference drawn in Madhwa Sidhanta case from the provision of s. 2 about the non-applicability of the rules of Hindu law including the rule of Damdupat to mortgages cannot now be drawn from the amended provision with regard to any rule of Hindu law - Appeal dismissed.
