Superintendence Company Of India (P) Ltd. v Krishan Murgai

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, N.L. Untwalia, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 302; (1981) 2 SCC 246; AIR 1980 SC 1717; 1980 (41) FLR 137; 1981 (1) LLJ 121; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1278

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Restraint Of Trade

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 57 - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 10 and 27 - Interpretation of Statutes - Law relating to contracts - English doctrine of restraint of trade - Service contracts - Question arise as to - Enforceability of post service negative covenent , considering the meaning of 'leave' u/s. 57 of 1963 Act - Applicability of importing of principles of English Law - The construction of the agreement in the light of provisions of ss. 10 and 27 of 1872 Act - Held, not enforceable, 'leave' was intended to refer only to a case where the employee had voluntarily left the services of his own - Import not permissible - Construction, narrower rather than wider - Appeal dismissed.
