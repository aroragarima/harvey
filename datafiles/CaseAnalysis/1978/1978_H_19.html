Purshotam Das Goyal v Hon’Ble Mr. Justice B. S. Dhillon And Ors

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 163; (1978) SCC (Cr) 195; (1978) 2 SCC 370; AIR 1978 SC 1014; 1978 CRLJ 772; 1978 CrLR(SC) 233; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 510; 1978 UJ 596

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 19(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Maintainability of appeal against order initiating contempt proceeding by issuing notice u/s. 17 - Held, not appealable u/s. 19(1) - Appeal dismissed.
