Justiniano Augusto De Piedade Barreto v Antonio Vicente Da Fonseca and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 165; (1979) 3 SCC 47; AIR 1979 SC 984; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 494

Case Digest

Subject: International; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Arbitration Act, Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962, Bombay Land Requisition Act, 1948, Goa, Daman and Diu (Administration) Act, 1962, Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, s. 29(2) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 254(1) - Whether provisions of Portuguese Civil Code were repugnant to provisions of Limitation Act? - Held, provisions of Portuguese Civil Code were saved by s. 29(2) - Body of provisions in Portuguese Civil Code dealing with subject of limitation of suits etc. and in force in Union Territory of Goa, Daman and Diu only is local law within meaning of s. 29(2) of Limitation Act - No question of repugnance arises.
