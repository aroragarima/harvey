Commissioner of Sales-Tax, Madhya Pradesh v H. M. Esufali H. M. Abdulali

Supreme Court of India

18 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 224; (1973) 2 SCC 137; (1973) SCC (Tax) 484; AIR 1973 SC 2266; 1973 (2) CTR 317; [1973] 90 ITR 271; 1973 MPLJ 858; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 1005; 1973 (32) STC 77; 1973 TAXLR 2258

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Sales Tax - Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s.19(1) and 18(4) - MP General Sales Tax Rules, 1959, r.33 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s.9(3) - (A) Turnover escaping assessment - Reassessment - Sales escaping (B) Re-'Best judgment assessment' - Assessing authority is not bound to adduce proof in support of its estimate - Validity of levy of penalty - Held, sales escaping assessment can be assessed by best judgment assessment - Penalty imposed on assessee was justified - Appeals allowed.
