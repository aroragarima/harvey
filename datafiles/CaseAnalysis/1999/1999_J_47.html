Joseph Fernandez v State of Goa

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 533; (2000) SCC (Cr) 300; (2000) 1 SCC 707; AIR 2000 SC 3502; AIR 2000 SCW 2431; 2000 (2) CCR 223; 2000 CRLJ 3485; 2003(10) SCALE 419; 2000 (4) SLT 791

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 42 and 50 - Search and seizure - Search in presence of Gazetted Officer or Magistrate - Right of accused - Search done before witness in question who had obliged investigation in other instances - Held, independent character in doubt - Substantial compliance of s. 50 if Intelligence Officer informing accused that if he wished he could be searched in presence of Gazetted Officer or Magistrate.
