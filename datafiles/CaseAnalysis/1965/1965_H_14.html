Â State of Punjab and Another v Hari Krishan Sharma

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, P. Satyanarayana Raju

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 434; AIR 1966 SC 1081; 1966 (68) PLR 510; [1966] 2 S.C.R. 982

Case Digest

Subject: Media & Entertainment

Keywords: Licences, Cinematograph Act, 1918

Summary: Media & Entertainment - Punjab Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1952, ss. 3, 5, 11 and 12 - Whether High Court was right in holding that appellant No. 1 had no jurisdiction to deal with the respondent's application in the manner it has done u/s. 5(3) of Act? - Held, High Court was right that appellant No. 1 had no authority or power to require all applications for licences made under the provisions of the Act to be forwarded to it, and to deal with them itself in the first instance - Appeal dismissed.
