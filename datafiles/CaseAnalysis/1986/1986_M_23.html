Tejinder Singh and Another v M/S. Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited And Another

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 216; (1986) 4 SCC 237; (1986) SCC (L&S) 765; AIR 1987 SC 51; 1986 (2) CCC 862; JT 1986 SC 405; 1987 LabIC 19; 1987 (2) LLJ 225; 1986 (2) LLN 1037; 1986(2) SCALE 391; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 739; 1987 (1) SLJ 188; 1986 (3) SLR 58; 1987 (1) UJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts.14 and 32 - Burmah Shell (Acquisition Of Undertakings In India) Act, 1976 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s.10 - Superannuation - Constitutional validity of the age of superannuation - Age of superannuation of the Management Staff fixed at 58 years while that of clerks at 60 years - Held, not discriminatory - Moreover discussion of jurisdictions u/art. 32 of Constitution and s. 10, Industrial Dispute Act - Petitions dismissed.
