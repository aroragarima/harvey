Â Hiralal Thakorlal Dalal v Broach Municipality & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 143; (1976) SCC (Tax) 314; (1976) 3 SCC 398; AIR 1976 SC 1446; 1976 (17) GLR 938; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 82; 1976 UJ 514

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Indirect Tax

Summary: Bombay Municipal Boroughs Act, 1923, s. 73(1)(iv) - Government Of India Act, 1935 -

Octroi tax - Interpretation of Statutes - Meaning of expression 'For consumption use or sale' - Held, law laid down in Burmah Shell's case governs the present case and does not require reconsideration - Appeal Allowed.
