Surender v State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 845; (2006) 12 SCC 375; (2007) 2 SCC (Cr) 210; 2007 CRLJ 779; 2007 (1) RCR(Criminal) 288; 2006(12) SCALE 254; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 296

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 306, 34, 498A - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 313 - Appeal against conviction - Agreed with HC that a young pregnant women having a child in womb would not ordinarily commit suicide unless she was compelled to do so - Both courts below have held against accused - Appeal dismissed.
