Gujarat University v Rajiv Gopinath Bhatt And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, A.M. Ahmadi, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2583; (1996) 4 SCC 60; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 541; AIR 1996 SC 2066; JT 1996 (5) SC 333; 1996(4) SCALE 305; 1996 (4) SLR 563; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 184; 1996 (4) Supreme 497; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 2119

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 14 - Education - Admission to super specialty courses in medicine - Provision for first preference to candidates of universities in Gujarat state - Any vacancy remaining after this shall remain unfilled - Held, not a rational provision.
