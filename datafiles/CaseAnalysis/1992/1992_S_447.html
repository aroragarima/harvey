Kumari Smt v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 814; (1992) 2 SCC 223; (1992) SCC (Cr) 386; 1992 (1) ACJ 283; AIR 1992 SC 2069; 1992 (19) ALR 427; JT 1992 (2) SC 16; 1992(1) SCALE 427; 1992 (1) UJ 527

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Torts - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Compensation - Death of six year child - Due to falling in 'uncovered sewerage tank - Issue of Writ of mandamus - Petition dismissed by High Court as it was not able to infer appropriate authority responsible - Held, state directed to pay Rs. 50,000/ - As compensation with liberty to initiate appropriate proceedings against concerned 'authority' - Appeal allowed.
