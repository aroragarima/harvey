Kunwar Pal Singh (Dead) By L.Rs; and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Lokeshwar Singh Panta, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 249; (2007) 5 SCC 85; AIR 2007 SC 1675; 2007 (3) ALJ 770; 2007 (3) AWC 2529; JT 2007 (5) SC 1; 2007 (5) MahLJ 389; 2007 (3) MPLJ 439; 2007 (147) PLR 511; 2007 (3) RCR(Civil) 121; 2007(5) SCALE 122; 2007 (5) SCJ 23; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 409; 2007 (2) UC 1135

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Petition challenging the correctness and validity of the Award passed by the Collector on the grounds that Award was made by the Land Acquisition Officer after the statutory period as contemplated under s. 11A dismissed by High Court - Appeal against - Held, High Court was not correct and justified in holding that the last date of publication of the declaration under s. 6 was 13.8.1985 and not 25.07.1985 - Appeal allowed.
