Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Food Corporations Act, 1964, Warehousing Corporations Act, 1962, Engagement, Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, Solicitor, National Housing Bank Act, 1987, Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946, Bombay Industrial Relation Act, 1946, Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970, Contract Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Amendment Act, 1986, Coal Mines Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1948, Banking Service Commission Act, 1975, Jammu and Kashmir Societies Registration Act, 1898

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Bombay Industrial Relation Act, 1946 - Banking Service Commission Act, 1975, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 12, 39, 41, 42, 49 and 226 - Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970, s. 10(1)z - Dock Workers (Regulation of Employment) Act, 1948, s. 5A - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Indian Contract Act, 1872 - The Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946 - Minimum Wages Act, 1948 - National Housing Bank Act, 1987, s. 3 - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 - Held, the employment of contract labour in the concerned establishment of the appellant was prohibited by the notification issued by the Central Government u/s. 10(1) of the Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970 on 16-11-1999 - Following the judgment of this court in Air India's case (supra), the High Court directed the appellant to absorb the contract labour - Judgment of this court in Air India's case (supra) is overruled, the direction given by the HC cannot be sustained - It is left open to the respondent-union to seek appropriate relief in terms of the main judgment - The order, under challenge, is set aside - Appeal allowed.
