Gurucharan Singh and Another v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, T.L. Venkatarama Aiyyar

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 114; AIR 1956 SC 460; 1956 CRLJ 827

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, s. 342 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Murder - Appellants convicted to life imprisonment and conviction upheld by High Court - Appeal - Four eye-witnesses - Not shaken in cross-examination - No question of law involved and no ground for interference - Appeal dismissed.
