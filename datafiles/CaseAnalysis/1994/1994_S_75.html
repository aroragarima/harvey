Union Of India v Sayed Muzaffar Mir

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1759; (1995) Supp1 SCC 76; (1995) SCC (L&S) 256; AIR 1995 SC 176; 1995 (1) CLR 118; JT 1994 (6) SC 288; 1995 (1) LLJ 157; 1995 (1) LLN 1; 1994(4) SCALE 258; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 729

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution - Railway Establishment Code, arts. 1802(b) and 1801(d) - Constitution, art. 311 - Voluntary retirement or retirement - Employee under suspension - Power of authority to withhold permission or retain incumbent in service - Has to be exercised by passing appropriate order - Employee applying for voluntary retirement - No order withholding permission passed - Retirement comes into effect on completion of notice period - Acceptance by authority - If necessary - Effect of order of removing employee from service passed after expiry of notice period - Held, not necessary - Order of removal is non est - Appeal dismissed.
