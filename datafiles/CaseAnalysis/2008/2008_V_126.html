Vishnu Dev Sharma v State Of U.P. & Ors

Supreme Court of India

23 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 100; (2008) 3 SCC 172; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1416; 2008(2) ALL MR 755; 2008 (2) AWC 1473; 2008 (1) ESC 126; 2008 (116) FLR 835; 2008 (2) MLJ(SC) 1097; 2008(1) SCALE 655; [2008] 1 S.C.R. 1111; 2008 (1) UPLBEC 52

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Remitted the matter to High Court

Summary: Service - Dispute related to fixation of seniority - Writ petition dismissed - Held, portion of the order goes to show that practically no reason was indicated - Dismissal of the writ petition in such summary manner without indicating any reason is clearly indefensible - Absence of reasons has rendered the High Court's judgment not sustainable - Remitted the matter to High Court.
