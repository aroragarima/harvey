L. Radhakrishnan v Parakulangara Devaswom and another

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2017

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R. Banumathi

Where Reported

2017 Indlaw SC 894

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Kerala Forest (Vesting and Management of Ecologically Frangible lands) Act, 2003

Summary: Land & Property - Kerala Forest (Vesting and Management of Ecologically Frangible lands) Act, 2003, ss. 10,9 - Ecologically fragile land - Restoration of land - Instant matters pertaining to claim of respondents that they are entitled to restoration of land which was declined by State on ground that it was ecologically fragile land.

Held, the jurisdiction over the crucial question of whether disputed land is an ecologically fragile land, is wholly vested in the Tribunal constituted u/s. 9 of the Act. Hence, impugned order dt. 17-10-2007 passed in contempt case is set aside and appeals are disposed with liberty to respondents to take recourse to statutory remedy u/s.10 of the Act before Tribunal. Order accordingly.
