State Bank of Travancore, Trivandrum, Kerala v E. J. Joseph and Another

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 411; (1993) Supp2 SCC 530; AIR 1993 SC 1265; [1993] 77 Comp Cas 83; JT 1992 (6) SC 736; 1993 (1) KLT 100; 1992(3) SCALE 274; 1993 (1) UJ 123

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Kerala Agriculturists Debt Relief Act, 1970

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, art. 14 - Commission of Inquiry Act , 1952, s. 3 - A full time member to the Enquiry Committee formed to enquire cases of riots ensuing Mrs. Indira Gandhi's assassination - Honorarium fixed in lumpsum and partly paid to him - Members of the other committees were paid in accordance with the guidelines O.M. D/- 8-10-87 - But he was't paid accordingly - No explanation given - SC directed payments in accordance with the guidelines and as to adjusting the payment already made - Appeal allowed.
