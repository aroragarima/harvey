Superintending Engineer, Public Health, U. T Chandigarh and Others v Kuldeep Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2284; (1997) 9 SCC 199; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1044; AIR 1997 SC 2133; JT 1997 (2) SC 508; 1997 LabIC 2097; 1997(2) SCALE 138; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 454; 1997 (1) SLR 699; 1997 (2) Supreme 738; 1997 (1) UJ 742

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16(1)(4 A), 15(4) and 335 - Promotion - Post reserved for Scheduled Tribes candidates according to roster - Population of Scheduled Tribes not available - Held, candidate belonging to Scheduled Caste, may be considered.
