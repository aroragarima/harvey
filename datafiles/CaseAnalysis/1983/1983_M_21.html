Baidyanath Ayurveda Bhawan Mazdoor Union, Patna v Management Of Shri Baidyanath Ayurveda Bhawan Pvt. Ltd. &Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 178; (1984) SCC (L&S) 127; (1984) 1 SCC 279; AIR 1984 SC 457; 1984 (48) FLR 122; 1984 LabIC 148; 1984 (1) LLJ 116; 1984 (1) LLN 236; 1983(2) SCALE 814; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 927; 1984 (1) SLJ 139; 1984 UJ 155

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965, Pre., ss.17, 34 - Bonus - Attendance bonus paid apart from bonus under the Act - Claim that no separate attendance bonus was payable - Held, not proper as attendance bonus is outside the purview of the Act - Appeal allowed.
