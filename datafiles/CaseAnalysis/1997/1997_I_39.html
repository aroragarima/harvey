Indore Municipal Corporation v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3278; (2001) 10 SCC 498; 2001 (166) CTR 511; [2001] 247 ITR 803

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 32 - Nature of expenditure on construction of metal roads on trenching grounds and if of capital nature - Whether depreciation allowable? - Relied on Travancore Cochin Chemicals Ltd v. CIT, 1977 106 ITR 9001977 (106) ITR 9001977 Indlaw SC 223 - Roads neither adjuncts of buildings nor being used for purposes of business could not be treated as 'building' - Held, expenditure was capital in nature but depreciation not allowable as in case of buildings - Appeal dismissed.
