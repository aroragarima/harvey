Shri M.L. Jaggi v Mahanagar Telephones Nigam Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 4171; (1996) 3 SCC 119; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 454; AIR 1996 SC 2476; 1997 ARBLR 621; 1996 (1) CCC 199; 1996 (1) CLT 439; JT 1996 (1) SC 215; 1996(1) SCALE 247; 1996 (1) SCJ 407; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 25; 1996 (2) Supreme 93; 1996 (1) UJ 531

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Telecom

Summary: Telecom - Telegraph Act, 1885, s. 7B - Army Act, 1950 - Nature of award to be passed by arbitrator in disputes regarding telephone bill - Held, arbitrator is required to give reasoned award - And any decision made prior to this day by any arbitrator u/s. 7B of Act is not liable to be reopened - Appeal allowed.
