Babu Sukhram Singh v Ram Dular Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 853; AIR 1973 SC 204

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 22, r. 4 - Whether appeal has abated against LRs. who were not brought on record within time prescribed? - Held, no separate claim was made against any of defendants - Appeal has abated as a whole under O. 22, r. 4 - Appeal dismissed.
