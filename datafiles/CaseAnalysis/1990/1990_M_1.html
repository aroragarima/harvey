(Mrs.) Roshan Sam v B.R. Cotton Mills Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Kuldip Singh, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 1005; (1990) 2 SCC 636; AIR 1990 SC 1881; 1990 CRLJ 1770; JT 1990 (3) SC 522; 1990 (1) RCJ 611; 1990 (2) RCR(Rent) 41; 1990(1) SCALE 718; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Breach Of Injunction

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act 1971, s. 2 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 142 - Contempt of court was in question - Giving undertaking to court based on assumption which undertaker knows are false - Held, party is guilty of misconduct amounting to contempt.
