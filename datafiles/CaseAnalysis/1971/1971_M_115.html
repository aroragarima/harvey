Municipal Corporation Of Delhi v Shiv Shankar

Supreme Court of India

01 February 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, S.M. Sikri, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 850; (1971) SCC (Cr) 195; (1971) 1 SCC 442; AIR 1971 SC 815; 1971 CRLJ 680; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 607

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, s. 1 - General Clauses Act, 1897, ss. 6 and 26 - Fruit Products Order, 1955, cl. 15 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, ss. 1 and 3 - Interpretation of Statutes - Whether Prevention of Food Adulteration Act impliedly repealed by Essential Commodities Act or Fruit Products Order made thereunder - Cannot be held to be impliedly repealed - Appeals allowed.
