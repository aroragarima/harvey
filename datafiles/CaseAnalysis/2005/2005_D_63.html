P.C. Varghese v Devaki Amma Balambika Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 608; (2005) 8 SCC 486; AIR 2006 SC 145; 2005 (61) ALR 628; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 38; JT 2005 (10) SC 278; 2005 (4) KLT 530; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 469; 2006 (100) RD 83; 2005(8) SCALE 320; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 267

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 12, 12(3), 20, 22 - Suit for specific performance of undivided property - Minor's share - Conditions mentioned to obtain necessary permission from Civil Court relating to minor's share - Trial Court not only granted a decree for specific performance of contract but also a preliminary decree for partition - HC held that it is an essential term for execution of contract and since such permission had not been granted, entire contract failed - Appealed herein - s. 22 enacts a rule of pleading that in order to avoid multiplicity of proceedings, plaintiff may claim a decree for possession and/ or partition in a suit for specific performance - Impugned judgment set aside - Appeal allowed.
