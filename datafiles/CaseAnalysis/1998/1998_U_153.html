Siel Limited and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1288; (1998) 7 SCC 26; AIR 1998 SC 3076; 1998 (4) CLT 36; JT 1998 (6) SC 323; 1998(5) SCALE 246; 1998 (7) SLT 502; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 560; 1998 (7) Supreme 367

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Keywords: Bihar Molasses (Control) Act, 1947, Uttar Pradesh Sheera Niyantran Adhiniyam, 1964

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Sheera Niyantran Adhiniyam, 1964, s. 10 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(g) and 254, Sch. 7, List 1, Entry 52, List 3, Entry 33- Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951, ss. 18G, Entry 25 - Sugar Industry - Molasses - Legislative power - Held, entire field is not occupied by Parliament - State legislature is competent to legislate under Entry 33 of List 3 subject to art. 254 - Uttar Pradesh Sheera Niyantran Adhiniyam, 1964, enacted by state, constitutionally valid - Principle of harmonious construction.
