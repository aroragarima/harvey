Bimal Chandra Ranerjee v State Of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 151; (1970) 2 SCC 467; AIR 1971 SC 517; [1971] 81 ITR 105; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 844

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution, art. 265 - M.P. Excise Act, 1915, ss. 25 and 62 - (A) Tax by rule or regulation - Imposition of - Authority - (B) Excise Duty - On excisable articles u/s. 25 -Liquor which contractors failed to lift is not an excisable article - Notification issued u/s. 62 imposing duty on liquor which contractors failed to lift - Validity - Held, cannot be imposed unless specially authorised by statute - Held invalid - Appeal Allowed.
