State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Arjunlal Rajak

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 260; (2006) SCC (L&S) 429; (2006) 2 SCC 711; 2006 (5) AWC 5143; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 135; JT 2006 (3) SC 56; 2006(2) SCALE 610; 2006 (3) SCJ 583; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 625; 2006 (7) SLR 257; 2007 (2) SLR 145; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 991

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 25B, 25F - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136, 14, 16, 311 - Services of respondent were terminated on ground that production unit in which he was working itself had been closed - Direction to reinstate respondent with full back wages - Appeal against - Held, since services of respondent were terminated on ground that production unit in which he was working itself had been closed, interest of justice would be sub-served if a monetary compensation of Rs. 10, 000/- Is granted to him - Appeal partly allowed.
