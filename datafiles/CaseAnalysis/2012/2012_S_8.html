S.M. Sharmila v National Insurance Co.Ltd. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

A.R. Dave, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 113; (2012) 2 SCC 770; 2012 (1) ACC 520; 2012 ACJ 693; AIR 2012 SC (Supp) 76; 2012(2) ALL MR 63; 2012 (2) AWC 1268; 2012 (5) Bom.C.R. 813; 2012 (132) FLR 1028; JT 2012 (2) SC 64; 2012 (3) LLJ 1; 2012 (4) MahLJ 11; 2012 (3) MPLJ 34; 2012 (166) PLR 539; 2012 (1) RCR(Civil) 975; 2012(2) SCALE 46

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Carriers & Transportation - Workmen's Compensation - Insured - Award - Sustainability - Appellant owner of vehicle and her workmen had been injured in an accident while they were travelling, workmen had suffered injuries in accident arising out of and in course of their employment, they had filed cases claiming compensation before Commissioner for Workmen's Compensation - Commissioner awarded compensation holding that vehicle involved in accident, was insured with respondent - Aggrieved respondent filed appeal in HC, HC reversed award passed by Commissioner holding that on date of accident vehicle was not insured with respondent - Whether vehicle in question was insured with respondent at time of accident - Held, HC came to conclusion that vehicle in question was not insured on 3rd April, 1998 and vehicle in question had been insured for a period commencing from 3rd March, 1997 to 2nd March, 1998 - HC had recorded sound reasons for coming to conclusion after carefully appreciating evidence adduced before Commissioner - Postage book of respondent shows that insurance policy was dispatched on 25th March, 1997, which denotes that policy was taken prior to 25th March, 1997 - HC rightly believed version of respondent - This fact rules out possibility of vehicle being insured on 3rd April, 1998 - Cover note relied upon by respondent workmen was not found to be genuine by HC - no legal issue was involved in these appeals and there was no reason to interfere with findings arrived at by HC - Appeals dismissed.
