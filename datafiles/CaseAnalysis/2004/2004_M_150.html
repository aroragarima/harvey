Dattatreya And Ors. v Mahaveer And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

31 May 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 458; (2004) 10 SCC 665; AIR 2004 SC 3362; JT 2004 (Supp2) SC 580; 2004(6) SCALE 57; [2004] Supp2 S.C.R. 811

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961, ss. 45, 48a - Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977, ss. 12(2), 5, 5(2)(i) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Land Tribunal declared respondents as tenants and granted occupancy tenancy rights in 1979 - Occupancy tenancy certificate was also issued - No appeal or revision filed - After 11 years order challenged in a writ - Refusal of HC to interfere with order on ground of laches - Another writ with out impleading respondents - Application for registration of occupancy by appellants not possible since land was not available for purpose - Appeal dismissed.
