State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Sirajuddin Khan

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 68; AIR 1965 SC 198; [1964] 53 ITR 158; 1964 MPLJ 483; 1964 (2) SCJ 349; [1964] 7 S.C.R. 838

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Annuity, Madhya Pradesh Abolition of proprietary Rights (Estates, Mahals, Alienated Lands) Act, 1950

Summary: Land & Property - Madhya Pradesh Abolition of Proprietary Rights (Estates, Mahals, Alienated Lands) Act, 1951, ss. 8(1), chap I, r. 2(2)(c) - Assessing Income Tax - Super tax excluded in assessing Income Tax - Whether expression 'Income Tax' in cl. (c) of sub-r (2) of r. 2 of Sch. I to Madhya Pradesh Abolition of Proprietary Rights includes super-tax? - Legislature as used expression 'Income Tax' If intention was to refer to both taxes, it would have stated 'Income Tax and super-tax' mention of one and omission of other is a sure indication of its intention - Held, having regard to terms of rule expression 'Income Tax' of r. 2 (2)(c)of Sch. I to Madhya Pradesh Abolition of Proprietary Rights (Estates, Mahals, Alienated Lands) Act 1950, does not include super-tax - Appeal dismissed.
