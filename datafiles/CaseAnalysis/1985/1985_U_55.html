Radhu K. Kakde of Bombay v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, R.B. Misra, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 123; (1986) SCC (L&S) 177; (1986) 1 SCC 400; AIR 1986 SC 291; 1986 (1) CCC 897; 1986 (52) FLR 149; 1986 LabIC 196; 1986 (1) LLJ 300; 1986 (1) LLN 290; 1985(2) SCALE 1261; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 822; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 822; 1986 (1) UJ 383

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965, s. 32(iv) - Bonus - Exemption from Act - Establishment of Central or State Govt. - Canteen Stores Department is an establishment engaged in industry carried by or under authority of Central Govt. - Provision of Act not applicable to its employees - Appeal dismissed.
