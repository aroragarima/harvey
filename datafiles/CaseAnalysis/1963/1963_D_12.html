Mrs. Chandnee Widya Vati Madden v Dr. C. L. Katiai, and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 122; AIR 1964 SC 978; 1964 (66) PLR 138; 1964 (2) SCJ 824; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 495

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific performance of a contract of sale of a house property - Whether the contract is enforceable being of a contingent nature? - Held, the agreement was a completed contract for sale of the house in question, subject to the sanction of the Chief Commr. before the sale transaction could be concluded, but that the Trial Court was in error in holding that the agreement was inchoate, and that, therefore, no decree for specific performance of the contract could be granted - The High Court was entirely correct in decreeing the suit for specific performance of the contract - Appeal dismissed.
