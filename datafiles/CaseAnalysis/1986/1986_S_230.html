Hussain Sasan Saheb Kaladgi v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 104; (1988) 4 SCC 168; (1988) SCC (L&S) 932; AIR 1987 SC 1627; 1987 (55) FLR 304; 1987 LabIC 1304; 1987 (2) LLJ 506; 1987 (1) LLN 422; 1988 (1) SLR 72

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133(1)(b) - Only a promote can be reverted - Whether order of State Govt. reverting direct recruit to lower post of primary teacher is sustainable - Held, the view taken by the High Court is thoroughly unsustainable - Appeal allowed.
