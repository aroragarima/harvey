B. Banerjee v Anita Pan

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 195; (1975) 1 SCC 166; AIR 1975 SC 1146; 1975 RCR(Rent) 293; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 774

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: West Bengal Premises tenancy (Second Amendment) Act 1969

Summary: W.B. Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, s. 13(3A) - W.B. Premises Tenancy (Second Amendment) Act, 1969, ss. 4 and 13 - Constitution, arts. 14 and 19(1)(f) - Validity of s. 13(3A) - Held, is valid - Not violative of art. 19(1)(f) due to its retrospectivity - Matter Remitted To High Court.
