Esha Bhattacharjee v Managing Committee of Raghunathpur Nafar Academy and others

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 601; 2014 (1) ALD(SC) 21; 2014 (2) AWC 1655; 2013 (4) CLT(SC) 62; 2013 (5) CTC 547; JT 2013 (12) SC 450; 2013 (4) RCR(Civil) 785; 2013(11) SCALE 418; [2013] 9 S.C.R. 782; 2014 (5) SLR 288

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963-Section 5-Condonation of delay-Enormous delay of 2449 days in filing appeal-Grounds urged did not justify condonation of such enormous delay-Impugned order of Division Bench of High Court condoning delay-Set aside-Obligation of court while dealing with application for condonation of delay and approach to be adopted while considering grounds for condonation of such colossal delay-Principles stated.
