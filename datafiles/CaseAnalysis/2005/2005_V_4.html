Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Indian Succession Act, 1959

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, ss. 2(7), 21(1)(a), 21(1)(h), 29(4) - Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 116 - Indian Succession Act, 1959, s. 152 - Eviction granted on ground of bona fide personal need of landlord - Appealed here - Challenge on ground that no landlord-tenant relationship between parties - Held, appellants cannot deny fact that they were inducted as tenants by father of respondent and her predecessor-interest - Question as to existence of bona fide personal need is a pure question of fact and, find no reason to interfere with finding - Appeal dismissed.
