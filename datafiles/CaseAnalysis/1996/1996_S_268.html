Collector of Customs, Bombay v Shibani Engineering Systems, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2153; (1996) 10 SCC 42; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 58; AIR 1996 SC 2558; 1996 (3) CCR 107; 1996 (66) ECR 217; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 453; JT 1996 (7) SC 222; 1996 (15) RLT 671; 1996(5) SCALE 593; 1996 (6) Supreme 383

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Tariff Act 1975, s. 11 - Exemption notification No. 70 of 1989, serial No. 6(a) - Exemption to 'cups and cones' of roller bearing is available even when cups or cones are separately imported - Held, interpretation of statutes as well as exemption notification should be read as ordinary man would read.
