Jogi Jha v State of Bihar and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 442; (1994) SCC (L&S) 837; (1995) Supp4 SCC 400; 1994 (27) ATC 442

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Bihar, OriPolice Manual, 1938, r. 853-A - Revision - Limitation of - Whether period of limitation for filing suit will be counted from date of final order passed by State Govt. in revision filed under rules? - Suit filed on April 26, 1967 shall be within period of limitation as revision in present case filed u/r. 853-A of Bihar and Oriss Police Manual, 1938 was dismissed by Govt. on October 28, 1965 and communicated on October 29, 1965 to appellant - Held, revision was made within period of limitation - Matter remitted to High Court.
