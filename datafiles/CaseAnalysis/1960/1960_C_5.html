MAHARAJADHIRAJA SIR KAMESHWAR SINGH v COMMISSIONER of Income Tax, BIHAR and ORISSA

Supreme Court of India

25 October 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 407; AIR 1961 SC 362; [1961] 41 ITR 169; 1961 (1) SCJ 250; [1961] 2 S.C.R. 74; 1960 (11) STC 169

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Agriculture & Agri. Products - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 2(1), cl. (a) of s. 4(3)(viii) - Whether remuneration for management of trust of agricultural lands be exempt as income u/s. 4(3)(viii) of Income Tax Act, 1922? - Held, both source and character of income is altered when part of income of trust is appropriated as remuneration - Remuneration, not being received as rent or revenue of agricultural lands under a title, legal or beneficial in property from which income was received - Income not exempt u/s. 4(3)(viii) of Income Tax Act, 1922 - Appeal dismissed.
