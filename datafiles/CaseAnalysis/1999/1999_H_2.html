India Photographic Company Limited v H. D. Shourie

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 502; (1999) 6 SCC 428; AIR 1999 SC 2453; 1999 (37) ALR 105; 1999 (4) Bom.C.R. 867; 1999 (2) CPJ 36A; JT 1999 (5) SC 333; 2000 (1) RCR(Civil) 308; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 9

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Milk, Sale Price, Weights And Measures, Standards Of Weights And Measures Act, 1976

Summary: Standards of Weights and Measures Act, 1976, s. 83 - Standards of Weights and Measures (Packaged Commodities) Rules, 1977, r. 6 - Sale of pre-packed commodity - Declaration of sale price on every package - Held, is an obligation - Not only on manufacturer - But on dealer/distributor also.
