Municipal Corporation Of Delhi v Jagan Nath Ashok Kumar And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28409; (1987) 4 SCC 497; AIR 1987 SC 2316; 1987 (2) ARBLR 344; JT 1987 (4) SC 25; 1987(2) SCALE 695; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 180; 1988 (1) UJ 226

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Constitution of India, 1950 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether reasonableness of reasons given by an arbitrator in making his award cannot be challenged in Special Leave Petition - Held, reasonableness of reasons given by an arbitrator in making his award cannot be challenged in Special Leave Petition.
