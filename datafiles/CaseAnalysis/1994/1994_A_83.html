Union Of India v Anand Kumar Pandey

Supreme Court of India

18 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 872; (1994) 5 SCC 663; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1235; AIR 1995 SC 388; 1994 (28) ATC 165; 1994 (2) CLR 437; JT 1994 (4) SC 419; 1995 (2) LLJ 25; 1994 (2) LLN 413; 1994(3) SCALE 386; 1995 (1) SCJ 52; 1995 (1) SLJ 118; 1994 (4) SLR 486; 1994 (3) UPLBEC 1659

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Appeal - Eastern Railway - Written examination held for selection - Post of non-technical popular category - Candidates found adopting unfair means - Cancellation of selection without affording opportunity to candidates - Whether cancellation of Selection - Against Principles of Natural justice - Held, principles of natural justice not violated.
