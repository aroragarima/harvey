T.G. Venkataraman Etc v State Of Madras & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 July 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 145; (1969) 2 SCC 299; AIR 1970 SC 508; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 38; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 615; 1970 (25) STC 196

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: T.N. General Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 59(1), 17(1) - Constitution, arts. 14, 246 and 301 -Notification - Imposing tax on sale of 'Cane jaggery' while 'palm jaggery' exempted from liability to tax - Constitutionality of - On ground of discrimination , violation of art. 301and colourable legislation - Held, no unlawful discrimination - Imposition of tax did not affect freedom of trade within art. 301 - Act held not open to challenge on plea of colourable exercise of power - Appeal Dismissed.
