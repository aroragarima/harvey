High Court of Judicature At Allahabad Through Registrar v Sarnam Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1297; (2000) SCC (L&S) 258; (2000) 2 SCC 339; AIR 2000 SC 2150; 2000 ALJ 1791; 2000 (1) AWC 617; JT 1999 (10) SC 99; 2000 (33) LabIC 2098; 2000 (2) LLN 34; 2000 (2) SLJ 375; [1999] Supp5 S.C.R. 344

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 311, 309 and 235 - Uttar Pradesh Judicial Officers (Retirement on Superannuation) Rules, rr. 2 and 4 - Compulsory Retirement - Applying Rule of scrutiny - Subordinate judicial officer - Retirement age enhanced from 58 to 60 years - Held, officer gets right to work till age of 60 years - Application of rule of scrutiny not necessary as was a transitional direction by Supreme Court - Is improper.
