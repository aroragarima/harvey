Ramji Bhagala v Krishnarao Karirao Bagra and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 423; (1982) 1 SCC 433; AIR 1982 SC 1223

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Admission of appeal in part

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41 cl. 12A - Admission of appeal in part - Legality - Held, SC by judgment dt.5-10-1979, Court admitted the appeal in part and rejected the appeal so far as prayer (b) and the enquiry for mesne profits are concerned. Learned Judge proceeded on the assumption that there has been a recent amendment in CPC under which he could admit the appeal as to a part and reject it as to the other. Court does not find any such amendment in CPC nor has any such amendment been brought to notice by parties. Appeal is remanded to HC so that HC may consider whether the appeal should be admitted wholly. Order accordingly
