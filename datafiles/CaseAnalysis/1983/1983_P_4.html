Panchdeo Narain Srivastava v Km. Jyoti Sahay and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 395; (1984) Supp SCC 594; AIR 1983 SC 462; 1983(1) SCALE 719; 1983 UJ 406

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 115, O.6, r. 17 - Amendment of plaint - Trial Court granting an application for amendment of the plaint inter alia seeking deletion of the word 'Uterine' from the plaint for effective adjudication of dispute - Interference by High Court in revision - Legality - Held, the High Court in its revisional jurisdiction for a reason which is untenable ought not to have interfered with the order made by the trial court - Appeal allowed.
