Parmananda Pegu v State Of Assam

Supreme Court of India

02 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 692; (2004) 7 SCC 779; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2081; AIR 2004 SC 4197; 2005 (1) CGLJ 43; 2004 CRLJ 4197; JT 2004 (7) SC 192; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 955; 2004(7) SCALE 330; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 1; 2004 (2) UC 1295

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 365 and 302 - Evidence Act, 1872. s. 26 - Confession - Retraction therefrom - Confessional statement of the accused - Appellant far from receiving corroboration of any sort from other circumstances, contradicts the medical evidence relating to cause of death - Not safe to convict the appellant solely on the basis of the alleged confession which has been retracted - Appeal allowed.
