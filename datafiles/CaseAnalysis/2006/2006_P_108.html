Purshottam Lal Das and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 678; (2006) 11 SCC 492; 2006 (6) AWC 6072; 2007 (1) JhrCR(SC) 22; 2006(10) SCALE 89; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 51; 2007 (1) UPLBEC 196

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Appellants challenged their reversion as well as recovery of amounts paid on account of promotion - HC held that this was in order - Appeal against - Held, promotions were granted without placing their cases before Establishment Committee and Committee which accorded approval was not properly constituted, and reservation policy was not followed and promotions were given without adopting procedure relating to advertisement - Order of reversion in each case cannot be faulted - No recovery shall be made from amounts already paid in respect of promotional posts - Appeals disposed of.
