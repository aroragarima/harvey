Workmen of Subong Tea Estate v Outgoing Management of Subongtea Estate and Another

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 106; AIR 1967 SC 420; 1964 (8) FLR 91; 1964 (1) LLJ 333; [1964] 5 S.C.R. 602

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10(1)(d), 25F, 25FF, 25G - Retrenchment - Compensation - Reinstatement - Back wages - Respondent no. 1 managing Tea Estate transferred Estate to respondent no. 2 - On occasion, respondent no. 1 retrenched eight workmen/appellants and paid them adequate retrenchment compensation - Appellants contended that at relevant date when 8 workmen were retrenched, respondent no. 2 was their employer and respondent no. 1 had no authority to pass orders of retrenchment - Issue of retrenchment was referred to Tribunal by Govt - Tribunal decided issue against appellants - Hence, instant appeal- Whether workmen were entitled to reinstatement - Held, it was conceded that if retrenchment was held to be effected by respondent no. 2, it did not comply with s. 25F or s. 25G of the 1947 Act - There could be little doubt that failure to comply with s. 25F would make retrenchment invalid, and so would failure to comply with s. 25G of the 1947 Act, because no reasons were recorded by respondent no. 2 for departing from rule prescribed by s. 25G of 1947 Act - In fact, no case was made out for effecting any retrenchment at all - Employer's right to retrench his employees could be validly exercised only where it was shown that any employee became surplus in undertaking - Hence, it should be held that retrenchment of 8 workmen being invalid in law, it should not be said to have terminated relationship of employer and employee between respondent no. 2 and 8 workmen concerned - Appellants were entitled to reinstatement with continuity of service and they would also be entitled to recover their full wages for the period between date retrenchment and date of reinstatement - Impugned award of Tribunal was set aside - Appeal allowed.
