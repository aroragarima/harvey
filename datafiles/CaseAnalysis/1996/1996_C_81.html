Naihati Municipality and Others v Chinmoyee Mukherjee and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2152; (1996) 10 SCC 632; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 502; AIR 1997 SC 378; 1997 (1) CalLT(SC) 101; 1996 (3) CCC 354; 1996 (3) CLT 313; JT 1996 (7) SC 359; 1996 (2) RentLR 380; 1996(6) SCALE 1; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 316; 1996 (6) Supreme 564

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6(1) - Land acquisition - Declaration - Quashing of declaration of land acquisition for public purpose - Resolution passed by municipality to acquire land for rehabilitation of hawkers - Funds collected from hawkers to meet cost of acquisition and deposited with municipality - Govt. had put restriction to use amount for rehabilitation of hawker as said amount would become part of municipal funds - Rehabilitation of hawkers is indisputably a public purpose - Whether quashing of declaration u/s. 6 on ground that final resolution not passed by Municipality directing commr. to spend money from its funds and there is no public purpose for acquiring land legal? - Held, quashing of declaration u/s. 6 is illegal - Appeal allowed.
