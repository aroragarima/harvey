Tarapore and Company, Madras and another v V. O. Tractors Export, Moscow and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 1418; (1969) 1 SCC 233; AIR 1970 SC 891; [1970] 40 Comp Cas 447; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 105; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 920

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Chamber Of Commerce, Negotiation

Summary: Banking Regulation Act, 1949, s. 6 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 38 - Constitution, sch.7, list 1, item 41, art.133 - Banking - International trade - Nature of irrevocable letters of credit - Machanism of such letter is of great importance in international trade - Court interfering with that automony - Interim order u/art. 133 of Constitution sought -

Courts held, should refrain from interfering - Interference with, by SC - Appeal No 2305 and 2306 Allowed.
