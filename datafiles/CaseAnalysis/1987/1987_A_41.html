All Bihar Christian Schools Association And Another. v State Of Bihar And Others.

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28490; (1988) 1 SCC 206; AIR 1988 SC 305; JT 1987 (4) SC 491; 1987(2) SCALE 1200; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 49; 1988 (2) SLR 1

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Gujarat University Act, 1949, Kerala University Act, 1969, Bihar Secondary Education Board Act, 1976, Societies Registration Act, 1862

Summary: Education - Delhi School Education Act, 1973 - Gujarat University Act, 1949 - Kerala University Act, 1969 - Whether management is free to administer its school in accordance with rules framed by it - And State's entitlement to take over management of school - Held, management is free to administer its school in accordance with rules framed by it - State is entitled.
