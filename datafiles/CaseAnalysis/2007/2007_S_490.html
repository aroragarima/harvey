Atma Singh (Died) Through Lrs. and Others v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 December 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1278; (2008) 2 SCC 568; AIR 2008 SC 709; 2008 (70) ALR 790; 2008 (1) AWC 81; JT 2007 (13) SC 473; 2008 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 1; 2008 (3) MLJ(SC) 806; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 555; 2007(14) SCALE 109; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 1120

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Hypothetical, Tenure- Holder

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, s. 4 - Claimant-appellants had sought enhancement of the amount of compensation for acquisition of their land - Held, reasons given for the principle that price fetched for small plots cannot form safe basis for valuation of large tracks of land, according to cases referred to above, are that substantial area is used for development of sites like laying out roads, drains, sewers, water and electricity lines and other civic amenities - A deduction of 10% from the market value of the land, which has been arrived at by the High Court would meet the ends of justice - Appeals partly allowed.
