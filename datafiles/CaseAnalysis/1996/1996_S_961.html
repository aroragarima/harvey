Delhi Cloth and General Mills Company Limited and Another v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K. Venkataswami, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3079; (1996) 2 SCC 449; AIR 1996 SC 2930; JT 1996 (1) SC 390; 1996(1) SCALE 332; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 518; 1996 (1) Supreme 469

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Kota Municipal Limits (Continued Existence) Validating Act, 1975, s. 3 - Validating Act not curing defects of not following mandatory provisions of Rajasthan Municipalities Act in case of certain villages - Validity of Act questioned - Act held, bad in law and liable to be stuck down - Appeal allowed.
