Supreme Court of India

28 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 610; (1989) 3 SCC 271; (1989) SCC (L&S) 465; AIR 1989 SC 1565; 1989 (59) FLR 734; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 155; 1989 LabIC 1546; 1989 (2) LLJ 558; 1989 (2) LLN 718; 1989(1) SCALE 1544; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 802; 1991 (3) SLR 102; 1989 (2) UJ 159

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10(1) - Reference of an industrial dispute is an administrative function of appropriate Govt. - Question that whether reference should be made or not - Whether Govt. while considering question can delve into merits of dispute and determine lis itself - Govt. held, cannot do so - Appeal allowed.
