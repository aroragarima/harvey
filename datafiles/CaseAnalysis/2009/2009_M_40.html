General Manager, Telecom v M. Krishnan and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 September 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1082; (2009) 8 SCC 481; AIR 2010 SC 90; 2009 (77) ALR 302; 2009 (4) AWC 3633; 2009 (5) Bom.C.R. 501; 2009 (3) CPJ(Sc) 71; 2009 (5) CTC 62; JT 2009 (12) SC 267; 2009 (3) KLT 991; 2009 (8) MLJ(SC) 1418; 2010 (1) MPLJ 336; 2009 (4) RCR(Civil) 8; 2009(12) SCALE 123; [2009] 14 S.C.R. 86; 2010 (1) UC 257; 2010 (1) WLN(SC) 24

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection

Summary: Consumer Protection - Indian Telegraph Act, 1885, s.7-B - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Jurisdiction of Consumer forums - Matter relating to telephone bills - Due non-payment of telephone bill telephone connection provided to respondent No. 1 was disconnected - Respondent No. 1 filed a complaint before Consumer Forum - Consumer Forum allowed the complaint - Writ petition filed before HC challenging jurisdiction of Consumer Forum was dismissed - Writ appeal was also dismissed - Hence, present appeal - Whether Consumer Forum has jurisdiction in matter relating to telephone bills? - It is well settled that special law overrides general law - Held, when there is a special remedy provided in s.7-B of Telegraph Act regarding disputes in respect of telephone bills, then remedy under Consumer Protection Act is by implication barred - Relied on Chairman, Thiruvalluvar Transport Corporation Vs. Consumer Protection Council 1995 INDLAW SC 1728 - Impugned judgment of HC as well as order of District Consumer Forum set aside - Appeal allowed.
