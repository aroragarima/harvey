Rajendra Narain Singh and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 202; (1980) 3 SCC 217; (1980) SCC (L&S) 363; AIR 1980 SC 1246; 1980 LabIC 703; 1980 (2) LLJ 138; 1980 (2) LLN 451; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 450; 1980 SLJ 632; 1980 (2) SLR 43

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Bihar Police Service (Recruitment) rules 1953

Summary: Service - Bihar Police Service (Recruitment) Rules, 1953, rr. 2, 3, 22, 23 and 24 - Bihar Police Service (Recruitment) Rules, 1953, r. 648-B - Promotion in rank of Deputy Superintendent of Police - Seniority between direct recruits and promotees - Held, r. 3 does not prescribe a fixed proportion of promotees and direct recruits for vacancies to be filled in any year but only ensures not less than half of vacancies for promotees filling more than half of vacancies by promotees - Promotion in the present case cannot be an infringement of r. 3 - Appeal allowed.
