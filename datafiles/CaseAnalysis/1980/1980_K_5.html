Fuzlunbi v K. Khader Vali and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.P. Sen, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 587; (1980) 4 SCC 125; (1980) SCC (Cr) 916; AIR 1980 SC 1730; 1980 CRLJ 1249; 1980 CrLR(SC) 524; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1127

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1974, s. 127(3)(b) - Payment of Mehr under Mahomedan Law - Whether and when releases husband from his liability u/s. 125 - Held, liquidated sum paid at the time of divorce must be a reasonable and will release the quondam husband from the continuing liability, only if the sum paid is realistically sufficient to maintain the ex-wife and salvage her from destitution which is the anathema of the law - Appeal Allowed.
