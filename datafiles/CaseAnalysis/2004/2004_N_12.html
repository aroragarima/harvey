Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Limitation Act, 1963, arts. 14, 64 and 65 - Whether suit by appellant filed after 13 years from dispossession was barred by limitation? - In suit No. 357/60 instituted by respondent herein, appellant in his evidence, had admitted that he was in possession of suit property up to 1971, which indicates his ouster from possession - Held, art. 64 of 1963 Act is applicable to present suit, hence suit has been rightly dismissed by both courts below - Appeal dismissed.
