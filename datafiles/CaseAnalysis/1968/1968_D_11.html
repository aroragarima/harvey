Security & Finance (P) Ltd. & Anr. v Dattaraya Raghav Agge & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 165; (1969) 1 SCC 181; AIR 1970 SC 720; 1970 CRLJ 756; 1969 MahLJ 522; 1969 MPLJ 454; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 668

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1952, s. 3 - Customs Act, 1962 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 35 -

Interference with due course of justice - Authority (having jurisdiction) holding enquiry in good faith - Parallel enquiry imminent or pending does not amount to contempt - Ciivil suit filed while pendency of arbitration proceeding - Proceeding continued even after receipt of notice - Held, no contempt - Appeal Allowed.
