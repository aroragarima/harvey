Amolak Ram Khosla v Commissioner of Income Tax, Delhi Ii

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Baharul Islam, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 346; (1981) 4 SCC 576; AIR 1982 SC 16; 1981 (21) CTR 31; [1981] 131 ITR 589; 1981(3) SCALE 1232; [1981] 7 TAXMAN 51; 1981 UJ 717

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 22 and 256(2) - Income Tax - (A) Question of law - Application for making reference - High Court holding no question of law arose in view of its Full Bench decision and refusing to require reference - (B) Proper mode of determination of Annual value of building - Held, in view of Supreme Court decision in 1979 INDLAW SC 154 overturning said Full Bench decision, HC was wrong in declining to do so - Annual value of the building must be taken to be the standard rent of the building determinable under the provisions of the Rent Act and not the actual rent received - Appeals Allowed.
