Lt. Commander Pascal Fernandes v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 September 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vishishtha Bhargava, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 495; AIR 1968 SC 594; 1968 CRLJ 550; [1968] 1 S.C.R. 695

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Approver

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Law Amendment Act,1952, s. 8(2) - Code Of Criminal Procedure, ss. 337-339 and 540 - Power to grant pardon u/s. 8(2) - Held, s. 540 does not governs 337 or 338 or s. 8(2) of Act - Further held, section is wide enough to enable Special Judge to tender a pardon to any person directly or indirectly privy to offence and arraigned before him - Appeal dismissed.
