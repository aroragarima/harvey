Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Calcutta, Nowst Bengal Iii v Imperial Chemical Industries (India) Private Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 244; (1969) 1 SCC 629; AIR 1969 SC 1160; [1969] 74 ITR 17; [1969] 3 S.C.R. 804

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 66(1) - Whether compensation payable by selling agent to ex-agents was justified in disallowed? - Absence of any agreement with regard to quantum of commission to be paid to assessee - High Court is not entitled to interfere with finding on question of fact of Appellate Tribunal u/s. 66(1) - Held, exemption u/s. 10(2)(xv) not allowed as payment made was in character of agent of its principal - Appeal allowed.
