Smt. Ram Rati v Saroj Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3022; (1997) 6 SCC 66; AIR 1997 SC 3072; 1997 (2) CTC 753; JT 1997 (5) SC 601; 1997(4) SCALE 307; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 1050; 1997 (6) Supreme 6

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Election - Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Adhiniyam, 1994, s. 95(1) - Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Election Rules, 1994, r. 76 - Sarpanch of Gram Panchayat - Order directing recounting without there being application u/r. 76, which is essential condition-precedent - Validity of recounting of votes - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
