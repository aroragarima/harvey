Tata Engineering And Locomotivecompany Ltd. And Anr. v Municipal Corporation Of The City Of Thane And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 170; (1993) Supp1 SCC 361; AIR 1992 SC 645; AIR 1992 SCW 293; JT 1991 (6) SC 322; 1992 (86) STC 363; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 445

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Sales Tax; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, Food Corporations Act, 1964, Companies Act, 1913, Collected, Maharashtra Municipalities Act, 1965

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Maharashtra Municipalities (Octroi) Rules, 1968, r. 25(3)(d), 14, 24, Form 4, Form 11, Form 12 - Maharashtra Municipalities Act, 1965, s. 2(28) - Contract Act, 1872, s. 72 - Refund of Octroi - Company permitted to keep dutiable goods in bonded warehouse with current account facility - Goods exported out of Corporation area after repacking - Rejection of refund application on ground of non-compliance of r. 25(3)(d) - If legal - Octroi not shown to be separately charged and collected by Petitioner Company from dealers to whom goods were sold - Plea of unjust enrichment - Whether maintainable? - Held, on facts that rejection on ground that r. 25(3)(d) had not been complied with was illegal - Question of unjust enrichment does not arise - Appeal allowed.
