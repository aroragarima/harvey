Danda Rajeshwar v Bodavula Hanumayamma and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2920; (1996) 6 SCC 199; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 300; AIR 1997 SC 1541; 1996 (3) CLT 240; JT 1996 (7) SC 212; 1996(5) SCALE 871; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 169; 1996 (6) Supreme 148

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Act, 1994, s. 14 - Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj (Election Tribunal) in respect of Gram Panchayat and Mandal Parishads and Zilla Parishad Rules, 1995, r. 3(1) - Appeal against dismissal of petition challenging election of Sarpanch of Gram Panchayat on ground of alternative remedy - High Court directed filing of election petition before Tribunal and further direction to Tribunal not to go into question of limitation - Jurisdiction challenged on ground that it nullified limitation - Cannot be held to be without jurisdiction - Petition dismissed.
