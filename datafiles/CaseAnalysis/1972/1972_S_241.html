Kapurchand Kesrimal Jain v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 441; (1973) SCC (Cr) 253; (1973) 3 SCC 299; AIR 1973 SC 243; 1973 CRLJ 284; 1973 CRLR 56

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Summary Dismissal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 418 & 421 - Constitution - Appeal in High Court raises substantial and arguable questions of fact and law - HC dismiss it in limine without indicating its reasons for dismissal - Held, in such case Supreme Court would have no opinion but to send case back to HC for fresh decision in accordance with law - Appeal allowed.
