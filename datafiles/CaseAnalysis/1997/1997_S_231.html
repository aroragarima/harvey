Smt. Shanti Rani Das Dewanjee v Dinesh Chandra (Dead) By Lrs

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 621; (1997) 8 SCC 174; AIR 1997 SC 3985; JT 1997 (8) SC 228; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 34; 1998 (89) RD 344; 1997(6) SCALE 260; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 177; 1997 (8) Supreme 374; 1997 (2) UJ 810

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Constitution, art.133 - CPC, 1908, O. 8, r. 6A - Limitation Act, 1908 - (A) Civil Procedure - If application u/r. 6 of O. 8 of CPC, 1908 is ex facie barred - (B) Constitution - Question whether suit and counter-claim were barred under Limitation Act - Raised for first time in appeal before SC - Held, is not ex facie barred after filing of written statement - Cannot be raised - Appeal dismissed.
