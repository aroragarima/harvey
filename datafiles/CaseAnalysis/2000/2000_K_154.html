Divisional Manager, A.P. Srtc v Kondi K. Rambabu and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3991; (2000) 9 SCC 270; (2000) SCC (L&S) 905

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial - Loss of job due to nationalisation - Respondent working as a cleaner-cum-driver with a private operator - Whether appellant Corporation required to appoint displaced persons as cleaners though they had worked as drivers and had been found ineligible by the appellant Corporation for the post of cleaner? - Held, no jurisdiction in the High Court to pass such orders - Relief is to be granted on merits - Where there is no merit in the writ petitioner's case in law, the Court cannot, to meet what it says are the ends of justice, direct the respondent to a writ petition to do that which is impermissible for it to do in law - HC order quashed - Appeals allowed.
