Indian Aluminium Company Limited v Thane Municipal Corporation

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 983; (1992) Supp1 SCC 480; AIR 1992 SC 53; 1991 (55) E.L.T. 454; JT 1991 (4) SC 31; 1991(2) SCALE 656; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 208; 1992 (1) UJ 76

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Maharashtra Municipalities (Octroi) rules 1968

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Indirect Tax - Maharashtra Municipalities (Octroi) Rules, 1968, r. 4(2), sch 2, part I A, entry 77, form 14 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 72 - (A) Octroi - Concession u/r. 4(2) in form of payment of lower rate of octroi - Requirement to file form 14 - Failure to fulfill - Whether disentitles Company to claim concession ? - (B) Octroi - Concession claim for, by Company after lapse of long time - Unjust enrichment - Whether claim is justified - Held, (A) disentitles Company - Has to be availed when it was available and in manner prescribed - No justification exists to claim refund - Duty must have been passed on to consumers - (B) Claim rejected - Appeal dismissed.
