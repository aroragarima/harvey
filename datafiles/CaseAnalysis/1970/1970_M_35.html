Mathura Prasad Bajoo Jaiswal and Others v Dossibai N. B. Jeejeebhoy

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 562; (1970) 1 SCC 613; AIR 1971 SC 2355; 1970 RCJ 1091; 1970 RCR(Rent) 396; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 830

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 11 - Res Judicata - Question relating to jurisdiction of Court - Question cannot be deemed to have been finally determined by erroneous decision of Court - Held, such decision cannot operate as res judicata in subsequent proceedings - Appeal Allowed.
