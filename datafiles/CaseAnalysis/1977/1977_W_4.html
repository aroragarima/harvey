Shukla Manseta Industries Private Limited v Workmen Employed Under It

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 268; (1977) 4 SCC 31; (1977) SCC (L&S) 428; AIR 1977 SC 2246; 1977 (35) FLR 246; 1977 LabIC 1541; 1977 (2) LLJ 339; 1977 (2) LLN 498; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 249; 1978 SLJ 208; 1977 UJ 536

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Period Of Operation

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 19(2) and 19(6) - Notice if termination of settlement - Law does not require that notice should be given only after date of expiry of settlement - Notice in advance - Terminating settlemtnt after 2 months (coincides with date of expiry of settlement) - Validity - Notice held, is valid - Appeal dismissed.
