M/S Virtual Soft Systems Ltd v Commissioner Of Income Tax, Delhi-I

Supreme Court of India

06 February 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 100; (2007) 9 SCC 665; [2007] 289 ITR 83; JT 2007 (3) SC 125; 2007(2) SCALE 612; 2007 (5) SCJ 37; [2007] 2 S.C.R. 289; [2007] 159 TAXMAN 155

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961 - High Court held that the Tribunal was not right in deleting the penalty imposed under s. 271(1)(c) merely on the ground that the total income of the assessee was assessed at a minus figure/loss - Appeal against - Held, it is held that prior to its amendment by Finance Act, 2002 in the absence of any positive income and no tax being levied, penalty for concealment of income could not be levied - Appeal allowed.
