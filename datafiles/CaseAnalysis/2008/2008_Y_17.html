Rajesh Kumar @ Raju v Yudhvir Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 834; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 91; (2008) 7 SCC 305; 2008 (4) ACC 354; AIR 2008 SC 2396; 2009 (1) ALD(SC) 21; 2008 (72) ALR 488; 2008 (3) AWC 2474; 2008 (1) CCC 384; 2008 (6) MahLJ 21; 2009 (1) MLJ(SC) 995; 2008 (4) MPLJ 242; 2008 (152) PLR 372; 2008(8) SCALE 497; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 532

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 166 - Held, claim petition was filed under s. 166 and not under s. 163A thereof - Tribunal can determine the amount on the basis of the basic datas provided - In absence of any contention having been raised in regard to the applicability of the Workmen's Compensation Act which, ex facie has no application, the same cannot be permitted to be raised for the first time - Appeal dismissed.
