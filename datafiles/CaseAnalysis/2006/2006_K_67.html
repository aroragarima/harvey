State of Karnataka and Others v Kgsd Canteen Employees Welfare Association and

Supreme Court of India

03 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1; (2006) 1 SCC 567; AIR 2006 SC 845; 2006 (5) AWC 4461; 2006 (1) CLR 407; 2006 (1) CTC 414; JT 2006 (1) SC 84; 2006 (2) KarLJ 1; 2006 (2) RLW 1156; 2006(1) SCALE 35; 2006 (1) SCJ 712; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 93; 2006 (2) SLR 324; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 1061

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Factories Rules, 1950

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 226, 309, 311 - Factories Act, 1948, s. 46 - Canteen Employees - Canteen for benefit of secretariat employees - Request for grant of scale of pay and other service benefits applicable to Govt. servants rejected - HC held that employees of canteen are employees of State Govt. and directed to regularize their services - Appeal against - Whether HC was justified in directing regularization of services of Respondents? - Held, they did not hold any post - No post for canteen was sanctioned by State - HC not correct in holding that members of First Respondent could be treated at par with Hospitality Organization of State of Karnataka - Only because food is prepared and served, same would not mean that a canteen run by a Committee can be equated thereto - Writ filed by canteen employees not maintainable - Appeals disposed of.
