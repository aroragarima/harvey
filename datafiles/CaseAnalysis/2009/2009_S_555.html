State Of Nct Of Delhi v Ashif Khan @ Kalu

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 285; (2009) 4 SCC 42; (2009) 2 SCC (Cr) 54; AIR 2009 SC 1977; 2009 (2) JhrCR(SC) 217; 2009 (1) KLT 957; 2009 (2) RCR(Criminal) 267; 2009(3) SCALE 429; [2009] 3 S.C.R. 940

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Personal Consumption, Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Amendment) Act, 2001

Summary: Criminal - Health & Drug - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 21(a) and (b) - High Court held that the quantity seized was a small quantity and, therefore, the conviction would be under s. 21(a) - Appeal against - Held, percentage of heroin content translated into weight is relevant - Appeal dismissed.
