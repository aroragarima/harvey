Ramanlal Mohanlal Pandya v State of Bombay

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 277; AIR 1960 SC 961; 1960 CRLJ 1380

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 157 - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Constitution - Illegal Gratification - Appellant, a public prosecutor, convicted by Trial Court and High Court for accepting bribe of Rs. 250 - Held, complainant's case corraborated by two independent witnesses - If public servant is guilty of corruption, courts should not look upon it with leniency - Appeal dismissed.
