State of Madhya Pradesh v Mangi Lal Sharma

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 394; (1998) 2 SCC 510; (1998) SCC (L&S) 599; AIR 1998 SC 743; 1998 (1) CLR 395; 1998 (78) FLR 541; JT 1997 (10) SC 345; 1998 (1) LLJ 995; 1998 (2) LLN 63; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 27; 1997(7) SCALE 781; 1998 (1) SLR 241; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 662; 1998 (1) Supreme 69

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 47 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 34 - Decree - Execution - Powers of executing court - Alteration in decree - Court not granting relief as to arrears of salary and interest thereon as not claimed - Executing court grants - Held, not proper - Executing Court bound by terms of decree - Cannot alter decree.
