Narendrakumar J. Modi v Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat Ii, Ahmedabad

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, Hans Raj Khanna, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 211; (1976) 4 SCC 456; AIR 1976 SC 1953; 1976 CTR 286; [1976] 105 ITR 109; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 112; 1976 UJ 733

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family & Personal - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 25A(3) - Joint Hindu Family - Karta, a younger member of family - Right given up by senior member with consent of all other members - Legality - Held, permisible - Appeal dismissed.
