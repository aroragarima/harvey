State Of Madhya Pradesh v Rameshwar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 398; (2009) 11 SCC 424; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1820; 2009 CRLJ 2415; 2009 (2) RCR(Criminal) 709; 2009(5) SCALE 214; [2009] 5 S.C.R. 510

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Appeals Disposed Of, Co- Accused

Summary: Criminal - Socio-Economic - IPC, 1860, ss. 409, 420, 120-B - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, ss.13(1)(d), 13(2) - Question of status of the respondents as 'public servants' - Held, respondent Nos.1 and 3, in their capacity as the Chairman and Executive Officer of the Bank, come within the definition of 'public servant' - High Court in revision erroneously quashed the charges framed against the respondents - Appeals disposed of.
