State of Maharashtra and Another v Basantibai Mohanlal Khetan and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 652; (1986) 2 SCC 516; AIR 1986 SC 1466; 1986 (3) Bom.C.R. 243; 1986(1) SCALE 404; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 707; 1986 (1) UJ 715

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Constitution, arts.31C , 39(b), 14, 19, 31, 300A, 21 - Bombay Housing Boards Act, 1948 - Determination of compensation for acquisition of land situated in Municipal limits - Constitutional validity of provision of s. 44(3) and (4) of Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1977 - Considering that (1) Valuation of lands in municipal limits governed by these provisions but rural lands are governed by Land Acquisition Act - (2) Provisions are protected from being challenged u/arts. 14, 19 and 31 in view of art. 31C which applies to case - Held, provisions are not violative of art. 14 but are protected by art. 31C - Provisions cannot be struck down on ground that they are neither just nor fair or reasonable or by invoking art. 21 - Appeals allowed.
