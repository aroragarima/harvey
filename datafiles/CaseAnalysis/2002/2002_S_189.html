Sri Shibu Chandra Dhar v Sri Pasupati Nath Addya

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, G.B. Pattanaik, K.G. Balakrishnan, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 149; (2002) 3 SCC 617; AIR 2002 SC 1252; AIR 2002 SCW 1048; 2002 (2) AllCJ 957; 2002 (47) ALR 423; JT 2002 (3) SC 94; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 363; 2002(2) SCALE 468; 2002 (2) SCJ 308; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 249; 2002 (2) SLT 403; 2002 (2) Supreme 214

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Under Section 17 of the West Bengal Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, court can condone delay and/or extend time in cases of small defaults or where default is for reasons beyond the control of the tenant if it has power to extend time, but in case of willful, gross or deliberate defaults, Court may refuse to extend time even if it has power to extend time.
