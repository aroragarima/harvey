Kishori v State of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 876; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1; (1999) 1 SCC 148; AIR 1999 SC 382; 1999 (38) AllCriC 154; 1998 (4) CCR 121; 1999 (1) Crimes 18; 1999 CRLJ 584; JT 1998 (8) SC 248; 1999 (1) RCR(Criminal) 240; 1998(6) SCALE 327; 1999 (1) SCJ 247; 1998 (9) SLT 558; 1998 (9) Supreme 268

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Notions

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 149 - Murder - Riots - Accused persons members of mob alleged to have attacked and killed three deceased persons of a particular community - No discrepancy in testimony of eye - witnesses about death of deceased and in identifying presence of accused persons in mob - Held, guilt of accused persons as members of mob established - Their conviction was proper.
