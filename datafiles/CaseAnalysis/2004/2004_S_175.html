Shri Kailash Chand and another v Shri Dharam Dass

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 878; (2004) 8 SCC 482; 2004 (57) ALR 708; JT 2004 (9) SC 170; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 505; 2004(8) SCALE 811; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 295

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Himachal Pradesh Urban Rent Control Act, 1987, s. 14(3) - Interpretation - Eviction - Rent Controller and Appellate Authority ordered ejectment - HC reversed order - Held, third proviso to s. 14(3) may apply to facts which were before court when suit/application was decided by court/authority and landlord obtained possession of a building or a part thereof - But if circumstances changed and necessity increases, it may be possible for landlord to apply u/sub-s. (3) of s. 14 on ground of bona fide requirement - To such a situation, third proviso to s. 14(3) may not prohibit him from approaching a competent court/authority - Fresh look necessary - Matter placed for consideration of question by a Bench of three Judges - Order accordingly.
