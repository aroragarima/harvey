Chandrama Tewari v Union Of India, Through General Manager, Eastern Railways

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28797; (1987) Supp SCC 518; (1988) SCC (L&S) 226; AIR 1988 SC 117; 1987 (5) ATC 369; 1988 (1) CCC 218; 1988 (56) FLR 323; JT 1987 (4) SC 398; 1988 LabIC 339; 1988 (1) LLN 669; 1987(2) SCALE 1058; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1102; 1988 (1) SLJ 180; 1988 (7) SLR 699; 1988 (1) UJ 340

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 26 and 311 - Non supply of copy of document having no bearing on charges or which is not relied on by Inquiry Officer to support charges in disciplinary inquiry - Whether amounted to violation of principles of natural justice? - Held, no - Appeal dismissed.
