Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - s. 100 - Adverse possession - Proposition of law relating to ouster to a co-sharer vis-Ã -vis adverse possession - Held, the High Court while determining the question should have formulated substantial questions of law in terms of s. 100, CPC, 1908 - Further held, the matter should be considered afresh by the High Court which may proceed to decide the matter on framing proper substantial questions of law arising in the second appeal.
