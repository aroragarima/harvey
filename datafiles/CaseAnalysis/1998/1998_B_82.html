Board of Trustees, Port of Mumbai v Indian Oil Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1643; (1998) 4 SCC 302; AIR 1998 SC 1878; [1998] 93 Comp Cas 228; 1998 (2) CompLJ 377; JT 1998 (3) SC 178; 1998(2) SCALE 714; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 774; 1998 (4) SLT 345; 1998 (4) Supreme 134

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Major Port Trust Act, 1963, s. 64 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Lien of port trust - Sale of vessel for recovery of charges - Statutory lien of harbour authority - Determination of priority over secured creditors in winding up - Held, has paramountcy even over claims of secured creditors in winding up - Appeal allowed.
