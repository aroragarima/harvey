State of Maharashtra and Another v Champalal Kishanlal Mohta

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 84; (1970) 1 SCC 611; AIR 1971 SC 908; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 46; 1971 (27) STC 116

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(13) - Govt. of India Act, 1935 - Constitution Of India, sch. 7, list 2, entry 54 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, s. 2(7) - Contract of sale - Standing timber agreed to be severed under - Whether sale of timber can be regarded as sale of goods u/s 2(13) of 1959 Act - Held, sale of timber can be regarded as sale of goods - Appeal allowed.
