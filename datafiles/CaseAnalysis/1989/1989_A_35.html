State Of Tamil Nadu v Anandam Viswanathan

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 103; (1989) SCC (Tax) 109; (1989) 1 SCC 613; AIR 1989 SC 962; JT 1989 (1) SC 181; 1989(1) SCALE 176; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 301; 1989 (73) STC 1; 1989 (1) UJ 404

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 3 - Works contract or contract for sale - Test to determine - Contract for printing question papers of universities and educational institutions - Eligibility to tax - Held, not eligible to sales tax - Appeals dismissed.
