Romesh Chander v Smt. Savitri

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 329; (1995) 2 SCC 7; AIR 1995 SC 851; AIR 1995 SCW 647; 1995 (25) ALR 536; 1995 (1) CCC 97; 1995 (1) DMC 231; 1995 (1) HLR 325; JT 1995 (1) SC 362; 1995(1) SCALE 177; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 212; 1995 (1) UJ 434

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s.13; Constitution of India, art.142 - Divorce - Marriage lost its essence - Husband complete irresponsible either towards his wife or son - Child born out of wedlock having now grown and being in service - Held, continuance of marital alliance would be cruelty specially when - Husband expressing remorse - Dissolution of marriage subject to transfer of house of husband in name of his wife.
