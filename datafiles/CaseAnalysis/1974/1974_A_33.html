Murthy Match Works and Others v Assistant Collector of Central Excise and another

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 509; (1974) SCC (Tax) 278; (1974) 4 SCC 428; AIR 1974 SC 497; 1974 (3) CTR 91; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 429; 1974 (2) MLJ(SC) 16; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 121; 1974 TAXLR 1851

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 3 and 37 - Central Excise Rules 1955 - Constitutional validity of Notification No. 162 of 1967 issued u/r. 6(1) of 1955 Rules - Held, not invalid as violating art.14 of constittution - Appeal Dismissed.
