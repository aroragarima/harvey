Jagdish Ch. Patnaik and Others v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 759; (1998) 4 SCC 456; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1156; AIR 1998 SC 1926; JT 1998 (3) SC 105; 1998 LabIC 1879; 1998 (2) LLN 430; 1998(2) SCALE 662; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 676; 1998 (3) SLJ 91; 1998 (2) SLR 799; 1998 (4) SLT 305; 1998 (3) Supreme 572

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Haryana Service Of Engineers Class I Pwd (Roads And Buildings Branch) Rules

Summary: Constitution, art. 16 - Orissa Service of Engineers Rules, 1941, r. 26 - Limitation Act, 1908 - (A ) Service Rules - Determination of inter se seniority among direct recruits vis-a-vis promotees - Final power of appointment vests in State Govt. - Relevancy of year of appointment and year in which vacancy arose - (B) Review of petition filed by persons who were not party to the main petition by dismissal of application against tribunal's decision - SLP before SC - Whether review petition before Tribunal is not maintainable - Held, actual year of appointment governs seniority and year in which vacancy arose and against which recruitment is made, is irrelevant - Question does not arise - Appeal dismissed.
