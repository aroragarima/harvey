Sacs Eagles Chicory v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

21 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2276; 2002 (175) CTR 201; [2002] 255 ITR 178; [2002] 123 TAXMAN 221

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s.80HH - AY:- 1981-82 - Deductions from total income (hotels in backward areas) - Civil appeal - s.80HH r/w ss.80-I,80J of Act - &apos;Process&apos; carried on by assessee involved making powder from chicory roots including (i) roasting of roots and (ii) powdering them - Nothing else described process - Whether assessee-firm industrial undertaking eligible for deduction u/ss.80HH,80-I,80J of Act? - Held, no - Process does not satisfy test laid down in Aspinwall and Co. Ltd. v. CIT [ 2001 Indlaw SC 57] - Appeal dismissed.
