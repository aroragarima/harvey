P.K. Ramachandran v State Of Kerala & Anr

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 622; (1997) 7 SCC 556; AIR 1998 SC 2276; AIR 1998 SCW 2177; 1997 (31) ALR 412; 1998 (32) ALR 284; 1997 (4) CLT 95; 1997 (72) ECR 785; JT 1997 (8) SC 189; 1998 (89) RD 18; 1997(6) SCALE 209; 1998 (1) SLJ 626; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 204; 1997 (8) Supreme 332

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 ; Civil Procedure Code 1908, s.96 - Appeal against delay condoned - Delay condoned without recording satisfactory explanation for inordinate delay - No explanation offered by State - Held, condonation of delay was not proper and judicious and so the order cannot be sustained.
