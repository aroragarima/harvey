Hmm Limited And Anr. v Administrator Bangalore City Corporation,Bangalore And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 147; (1989) 4 SCC 640; (1990) SCC (Tax) 1; AIR 1990 SC 47; 1997 (91) E.L.T. 27; JT 1989 (4) SC 147; 1989(2) SCALE 791; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 353; 1990 (77) STC 17; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 353

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Indirect Taxes - City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, s. 98(2) - Refund of Octroi - Milkfood powder imported in bulk - Octroi paid - Repacking/rebottling and then export out of Banglore - Octroi is refundable, r. 24 not attracted since opening powder drums and rebottling amounted to 'breaking bulk' - Rebottling did not amount to use or consumption - No question of undue enrichment - Appeal allowed.
