Kavita w/o Sunder Shankardas Devidasani and another v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 242; (1981) 3 SCC 558; (1981) SCC (Cr) 743; AIR 1981 SC 1641; [1983] 53 Comp Cas 384; 1981 CRLJ 1262; 1981 CrLR(SC) 637; 1981(3) SCALE 1117; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 138

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Specially Empowered

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3 and 8 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(4) - Petitioner's husband detained u/s. 3 to prevent him from smuggling goods and abetting smuggling of goods - Detention challenged on ground that he was detained for period longer then prescribed - Whether detention was justified? - Held, yes - Advisory Board to decide final or tentative period of detention - Period for which a person is to be detained under COFEPOSA, 1974 is not to be determined and specified at time of making original order of detention - No obligation can be imposed upon Govt. to consider question of length of detention - Detention Order valid - Petition dismissed.
