Shaikh Mohd. Ali v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 500; (1972) 2 SCC 784; (1973) SCC (Cr) 111; AIR 1973 SC 43; 1973 CRLJ 166; 1973 UJ 299

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 418 and 421 - Appeal against conviction raising arguable questions of law and fact - High Court is not justified in dismissing appeal summarily without a speaking order - Held, HC is is not justified in dismissing appeal - Appeal allowed.
