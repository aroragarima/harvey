Dewan Daulat Rai Kapoor v New Delhi Municipal Committee and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 154; (1980) 1 SCC 685; (1982) SCC (Tax) 116; AIR 1980 SC 541; 1980 (17) DLT 88; [1980] 122 ITR 700; 1980 (1) RCJ 468; 1980 (1) RCR(Rent) 618; 1980 (1) RentLR 87; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 607

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Rent Control

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Punjab Municipal Act, 1911, s. 3(1)(b) - Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 116 - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 2(k), 9 and 12 - Determination of annual value - Building governed by 1958 Act - Held, annual value cannot exceed standard rent - Further held, landlord actually receiving higher rent is immaterial - Appeal dismissed.
