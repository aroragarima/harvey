Patel Narshi Thakershi and Others v Shri Pradyumansinghji Arjunsinghji

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 553; (1971) 3 SCC 844; AIR 1970 SC 1273; 1970 UJ 418

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 47, r. 1 - Saurashtra Land Reforms Act, 1951, s. 63 - (A) practice and Procedure - Review - Power of , if inherent in nature - (B) State Govt. - If empowered to review its own order u/s. 63 - Held, power to review is not an inherent power, it must be conferred by law either specifically or by necessary implication - State Govt. has no power to review its own order u/s. 63 - Appeal Dismissed.
