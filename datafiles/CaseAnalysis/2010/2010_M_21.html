VA Tech Escher Wyass Flovel Limited v M. P. S. E. Board and another

Supreme Court of India

14 January 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, R.M. Lodha

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 402; (2011) 13 SCC 261; 2010 ILR(MP) 769; 2010(2) M.P.H.T. 13

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Madhyastham Adhikaran Adhiniyam, 1983

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 9 - Madhya Pradesh Madhyastham Adhikaran Adhiniyam, 1983, s. 7(1) - Appellant was awarded a work contract by the respondents - Dispute arise between the parties - Appellant filed an application u/s. 9 of 1996 Act which was rejected by the Additional District Judge - Said order has been upheld by the HC - Hence, present appeal - Whether order of HC could be upheld? - Held, Parliament enacted the 1996 Act which covers all kinds of disputes including the dispute relating to work contracts - 1983 Act and the 1996 Act can be harmonised by holding that the 1983 Act only applies where there is no arbitration clause but it stands impliedly repealed by the 1996 Act where there is an arbitration clause - Hence, the impugned judgment cannot be sustained and application u/s. 9 of the 1996 Act was maintainable - Appeal allowed.
