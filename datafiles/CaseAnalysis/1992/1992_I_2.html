Ramesh Chandra v Iiird Additional District Judge And Others

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, L.M. Sharma, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 326; (1992) 1 SCC 751; AIR 1992 SC 1106; JT 1992 (1) SC 356; 1992 (1) RCJ 253; 1992 (1) RCR(Rent) 304; 1992(1) SCALE 122; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 349

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 2(2), 20(2) and 43 - Applicability of Act - Exemption available even to building constructed prior to Act - Construction of building in question completed prior to commencement of Act - Suit for eviction filed prior to completion of 10 years period from date of its construction - Fact that statutory period of 10 years expired during pendency of proceeding under Act - Held, Act does not apply to building in question - Fact immaterial - Appeal allowed.
