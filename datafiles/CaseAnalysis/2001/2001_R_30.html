Union of India v R.C. Fabrics Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

06 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, S.P. Bharucha, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 408; (2002) 1 SCC 718; AIR 2002 SC 191; 2002 (139) E.L.T. 12; JT 2001 (10) SC 584; 2002 (48) RLT 125; 2001(8) SCALE 98; 2001 (8) SLT 36; 2001 (8) Supreme 279

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 108 - Whether show cause notice could be discharged on ground that test report D/-26-11-1990/04-12-1990 could not be taken into consideration as same is deemed to have been taken into consideration while making order D/-10/15-01-1991? - Merits of case not gone into because Collector accepted first submission regarding applicability of principles of constructive res judicata - High Court in impugned judgment and order has observed that order of Collector D/-22-10-1993 was not subject matter of challenge before it - Held, in light of observations of HC that order of Collector D/-22-10-1993 was not subject matter of challenge, Collector was right in holding that he had no jurisdiction to pass afresh order of adjudication in view of earlier order D/-10/15-01-1991 covering consignment in question - Matter remanded to Collector for fresh decision on show cause notice - Appeal allowed.
