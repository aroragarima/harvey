Pallavi Refractories and Others v M/s. Singareni Colleries Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 January 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 44; (2005) 2 SCC 227; AIR 2005 SC 744; 2005 (1) AWC 673; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 289; JT 2005 (1) SC 107; 2005(1) SCALE 21; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 30

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 16 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 Dual pricing - Whether resort to dual price fixation classifying its customers into core/linked sector and non-core/unlinked sector by respondent and charging different prices for coal from such customers is discriminatory treating equals as unequals? - Held HC rightly came to conclusion that cl. 10 of Price Notification did not violate art.14 - By evolving dual price policy and charging lesser price from core-sector industries respondent has not treated equals as unequals or that classification made was not rational - Appeals dismissed.
