Mandir Shree Sitaramji Alias Shree Sitaram Bhandar v Land Acquisition Collector and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 492; (2005) 6 SCC 745; AIR 2005 SC 3581; 2005 (60) ALR 862; 2005 (4) AWC 3657; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 56; JT 2005 (7) SC 624; 2005 (4) MLJ(SC) 126; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 25; 2005(6) SCALE 730; 2006 (Supp) RD 163; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 969

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Society

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Municipalities & Local Governments - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 3(e), 39, 4, 40, 41, 48, 5A, 6 - Delhi Development Act, 1957 - Challenge to proposed acquisition - Held, possession of land had already been taken - Scheme would not take away rights of Delhi Development Authority to acquire for development of Delhi - Scheme was not applicable to lands of Appellants - Once possession is taken Govt. cannot withdraw from acquisition - Appeal dismissed.
