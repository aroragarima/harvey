State of Karnataka v B.M. Ashraf and Company

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 661; (1997) 8 SCC 468; AIR 1998 SC 63; JT 1997 (8) SC 458; 1997(6) SCALE 425; 1997 (107) STC 571; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 520; 1997 (9) Supreme 13

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Sales Tax - Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 6 - Constitution of India, 1950 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 5(2) - Purchase tax on registered dealer who were purchasing oil from unregistered dealers within State - And selling to exporter - Sale by registered dealer comes u/s. 5(2) of Central Sales Tax Act and not intra-State sale - Exigibility to purchase tax to be determined - Held, would not exclude applicability of purchase tax u/s. 6 of State Act - Appeal allowed.
