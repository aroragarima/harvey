Regional Engineering College Hamirpur and Another v Ashutosh Pandey

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3710; (2002) 9 SCC 720; JT 2000 (10) SC 216; 2000 (5) SLR 317; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 2702

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Semester Examinations

Summary: Education - Academic Regulations, Regn. 4.1 - Regional Engineering College - Rejection of condonation of excess absence - Whether Principal had power to condone absence in excess of 10%, in addition to 25%? - Held, respondent did not make any application in prescribed pro forma - Therefore, it is not possible to say whether his case comes within contingencies mentioned in Regulation - Principal was right in saying that he had no power to condone absence in excess of 10%, in addition to 25% - Appeal allowed.
