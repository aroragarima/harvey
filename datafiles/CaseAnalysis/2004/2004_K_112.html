Kumar Dhirendra Mullick & Others v Tivoli Park Apartments (P) Ltd

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 886; (2005) 9 SCC 262; 2005 (1) ALD(SC) 74; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 187; JT 2004 (9) SC 446; 2005 (2) MLJ(SC) 65; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 763; 2004(9) SCALE 184; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 800

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963 - Rescission of agreement -Whether oral lease agreement dated 16-08-1980 was liable to be rescinded for alleged breaches? - Held, decree in question is not a self operative final decree - It does not prescribe any consequence of non deposit of premium - Decree holder was repeatedly assured of settlement - Decree holder was put in possession under deed of assignment dated 16-08-1980 - Respondent was not put in possession under agreement dated 16-08-1980 - Trial Court erred in directing rescission of agreement - Appeal dismissed.
