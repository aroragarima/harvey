Life Insurance Corporation Of India And Another v Shri Raghavendra Seshagiri Rao Kulkarni

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1530; (1997) 8 SCC 461; AIR 1998 SC 327; 1997 (77) FLR 782; JT 1997 (8) SC 373; 1998 LabIC 411; 1998 (2) LLJ 1161; 1998 (1) LLN 56; 1997(6) SCALE 357; 1997 (5) SLR 774; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 207; 1997 (9) Supreme 6; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 2141

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Oil and Natural Gas Commission, Life Insurance Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1981

Summary: Service - Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, s. 48(2A) - LIC of India (Staff) Regulations, 1960, Regn. 14(4) - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(oo) - Service Rules - Termination - Letter of appointment on probation stipulating discharge from service without notice or reasons - Termination, if bad for lack of opportunity of hearing or does it amounts to retrenchment u/s. 2(oo) of the Industrial Dispute Act - Held, neither bad, nor amount to retrenchment - Appeal allowed.
