Satya Narain v Dhuja Ram and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, S.N. Dwivedi, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 277; (1974) 4 SCC 237; AIR 1974 SC 1185; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 20; 1974 UJ 126

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: High Court Rules

Summary: Election - Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 80, 81, 86- Constitution, art. 329 - Election - Presentation of election petition - Effect of non-compliance with requirement of spare copies - Held, requirenent u/s. 81(3) is mandatory and non-compliance with it results in dismissal of the election petition - Appeal dismissed .
