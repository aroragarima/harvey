Shah Mathuradas Maganlal and Company v Nagappa Shankarappa Malage and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 400; (1976) 3 SCC 660; AIR 1976 SC 1565; 1976 RCR(Rent) 866; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 789; 1976 UJ 389

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 62 and 111 - Tenancy - Premises in possession of tenant mortgaged to him - Therby possession to remain with mortgagee - No interest to be paid - For redemption after 10 years - Inference - Held, tenancy right was extinguished and on redemption owner-mortgagor was entitled to possession - Appeal dismissed.
