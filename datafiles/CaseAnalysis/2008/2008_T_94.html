The Commissioner Of Income Tax, Ii,Tiruchirapalli v City Union Bank Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

13 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2142; (2009) 16 SCC 403; 2008 (220) CTR 8; 2008(14) SCALE 191; [2008] 14 S.C.R. 385; [2009] 176 TAXMAN 25

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Government securities, Appeal disposed of, Income Tax Act, 1961, Interest On Securities, Chargeable To Tax, Interest Tax Act, 1974, Loans And Advances

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961- Interest Tax Act, 1974, s. 2(7) - Interest - Whether interest earned by assessee's-banks on dated Government securities was liable to be assessed u/s. 2(7) r/w s. 4 of Interest-tax Act, 1974? - Held, difference between loans and advances on one hand and investments/securities on other discussed in Bombay HC judgments in Discount & Finance House of India Ltd. v. S.K. Bhardwaj, 2002 INDLAW MUM 168 and in CIT v. United Western Bank Ltd, 2002 INDLAW MUM 169 - Revenue accepted said two judgments of Bombay HC - Views taken in said two judgments upheld - Appeal disposed of.
