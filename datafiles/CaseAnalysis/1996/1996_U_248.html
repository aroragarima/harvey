S. Jagannath v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1079; (1997) 2 SCC 87; AIR 1997 SC 811; AIR 1997 SCW 635; JT 1997 (1) SC 160; 1996(9) SCALE 167; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 848

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research, Cattle, Fisheries Act, 1897, Effluent, Polluting

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 48 A - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25 F - Environment Protection - Notification D/- 19-02-1991, Para 2(i) - Adverse effect of setting up of shrimp culture industry within prohibited area and in ecology fragile coastal area, on environment and coastal ecology and economics - Cannot be permitted to operate - Employees of industries directed to be closed - Justice for - Held, entitled to retrenchment compensation and 6 years wages as additional compensation High power authority directed to be constituted for granting permission for installation of shrimp industry - Petition allowed.
