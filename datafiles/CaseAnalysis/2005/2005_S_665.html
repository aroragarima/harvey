Ashutosh v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 511; (2005) 7 SCC 308; AIR 2005 SC 3434; 2005 (61) ALR 210; 2005 (4) AWC 3869N; 2005 (4) BC 13; 2006 (1) Bom.C.R. 760; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 155; JT 2005 (8) SC 58; 2005 (4) KLT 264; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 56; 2005(7) SCALE 27; 2005 (Supp) RD 376; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1057

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: [A.] Corporate - Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 21, rr. 49, 50, 58, o. 30, rr. 6, 7, 10, ss. 96, 144, 145 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, s. 53 - Indian Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 24, 25, 30 - Decree against a partnership firm - Interest on decretal amount unpaid - Attachment of property of one partner - Whether a decree can be executed against a partner when decree was against partnership firm? - Held, partner is always liable for partnership debt unless there is implied or express restriction - Interest at 18% excessive - Directed to pay Rs. 1, 00, 000/- in full satisfaction of claim - Appeal disposed of.
