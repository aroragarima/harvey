Mithilesh Garg Etc. Etc. v Union Of India And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, M.H. Kania, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 378; (1992) 1 SCC 168; 2006 (4) ACC 650; AIR 1992 SC 443; AIR 1992 SCW 41; 1991 ALJ 1167; JT 1993 (4) SC 447; 1992 (1) KerLJ 247; 1991(2) SCALE 1088; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 428

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Healthy Competition, Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Act, 1958

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Constitution - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 80 and 88 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) - Motor Vehicles - Stage carriage permits - Grant of, to private operators - Liberalization policy - Constitutionality - Held, not violative of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) - Object is to encourage healthy competition and eliminate corruption - Petition dismissed.
