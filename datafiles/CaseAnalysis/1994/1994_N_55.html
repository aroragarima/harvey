Babu Lal v New Delhi Municipal Committee

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 699; (1994) Supp2 SCC 633; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1173; AIR 1994 SC 2214; 1994 (28) ATC 92; 1994 (69) FLR 514; JT 1994 (4) SC 298; 1995 (1) LLJ 1119; 1994(3) SCALE 163; 1994 (4) SLR 432; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 576

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.14, 16 and 39(d) - Pay scale - Equal pay for equal work - Class IV employees in service of New Delhi Municipal Committee - Similar work to that of other Class IV employees - Same status and similar responsibilities - Denial of benefit of pay scales - Held, is arbitrary and discriminatory.
