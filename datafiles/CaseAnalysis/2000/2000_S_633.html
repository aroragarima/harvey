State of Punjab and Others v Bhagwan Singh

Supreme Court of India

16 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3769; (2002) 9 SCC 636; (2002) SCC (L&S) 1092; JT 2001 (Supp1) SC 7; 2001 (4) SLR 613

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Practice & Procedure - Order of discharge of respondent by competent authority on ground that he is unlikely to prove a good police officer - Whether assessment of competent auhtority amounts to stigma? - Held, because respondent was working under said officers and it was their assessment that was referred to and that was source for opinion expressed by competent authority to discharge respondent - Learned District Judge and High Court were, therefore, in error in treating that removal order caused stigma - Appeal allowed.
