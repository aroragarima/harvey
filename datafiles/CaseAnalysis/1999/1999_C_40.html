Chandra Kishore Jha v Mahavir Prasad and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 737; (1999) 8 SCC 266; AIR 1999 SC 3558; JT 1999 (7) SC 256; 1999(6) SCALE 40; 1999 (8) SLT 245; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 754; 1999 (8) Supreme 389

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 86 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 10 - Presentation and mode of election petition - Last date of limitation and working of open court suspended, but petition presented before Bench clerk - Refiled in open court on very next date - Presentation and Refiling if proper and in limitation - Held, presentation incorrect improper - Refiling is within limitation, s. 10 of 1897 Act applies to save limitation period - Appeal allowed.
