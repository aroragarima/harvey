Vijay Pratap and Others v Sambhu Saran Sinha and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2245; (1996) 10 SCC 53; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 539; AIR 1996 SC 2755; 1996 (3) CCC 334; 1996 (3) CLT 238; JT 1996 (7) SC 226; 1996(5) SCALE 805; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 173; 1996 (6) Supreme 529

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 1, r. 10 - Necessary parties to suit for specific performance of contract - Validity of dismissal of application for bringing son of respondent u/r. 10 of O. 1 on record as respondent on entering into compromise got his name deleted from arrangement - Plea that genuineness of deed of relinquishment signed by father cannot be decided - Court held, dismissal as proper - Tenable as suit is one for specific performance and not for title - Petition dismissed.
