Â Maharao Sahib Sri Bhim Singhji Etc v Union of India and Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

01 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A.P. Sen, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 404; (1986) 4 SCC 615; AIR 1985 SC 1650; 1985(2) SCALE 289; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 862

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Kerala Private Forests (Vesting and Assignment) Act, 1971, Constitution (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1955, Kerala Agrarian Relations Act, 1961, Defence And Internal Security Of India Act, 1971, Constitution (Fifth Amendment) Act,1955

Summary: Land and Property - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1950 - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, ss. 2(f), 11(6), 23, 27(1) - Constitution, arts. 14, 31, 31A, 31B, 39 - Validity of Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976 - Held, sub-s. (1) of s. 27 of the Act is invalid as it imposes a restriction on transfer of urban property for a period of ten years from the commencement of the Act, in relation to vacant land or building thereon, within the ceiling limits - Petition dismissed.
