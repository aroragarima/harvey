C. S. I. R. and Others v Dr. Ajay Kumar Jain

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 246; (2000) 4 SCC 186; (2000) SCC (L&S) 456; AIR 2000 SC 2710; AIR 2000 SCW 2888; 2000 (85) FLR 547; JT 2000 (4) SC 162; 2000 LabIC 2900; 2000 (2) LLN 421; 2000(2) SCALE 713; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 839; 2000 (3) SLJ 339; 2000 (4) SLR 688; 2000 (3) SLT 676; 2000 (3) Supreme 27; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1436

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Scheme Of Quick Recruitment Of Scientists (Fellows) For Major Projects

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Scientists Pool Scheme - Scheme of Quick Recruitment of Scientists (Fellows) for major projects (Quick Hire Scheme) - Scientist absorbed in pool scheme for 3 years - Later appointed under Quick Hire Scheme for one year - Extension of six months given - Less emoluments than what he was drawing under Pool Scheme fixed without giving any reasons - Held, his termination after 11/2 years and fixing lower emoluments were arbitrary.
