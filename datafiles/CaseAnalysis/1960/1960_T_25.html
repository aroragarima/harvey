Travancore Rubber and Tea Company, Limited v Commissioner of AgriculturalIncome Tax, Kerala

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 297; AIR 1961 SC 604; [1961] 41 ITR 751; 1961 KerLJ 402; 1961 KLT 31; 1961 (1) MLJ(SC) 55; 1961 (1) SCJ 306; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 279

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Travancore-Cochin Agricultural Income-Tax Act, 1950

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Agriculture & Agri Products - Kerala Agricultural Income Tax Act, 1950, s. 5(j) - Whether expenses incurred for maintenance and upkeep of immature plants are permissible deduction u/s 5(j) of Kerala Agricultural Income Tax Act, 1950? - Held, amount expended on superintendence, weeding etc, of whole estate should have been allowed against profits earned - Expenses incurred for maintenance are permissible deductions - Appeal allowed.
