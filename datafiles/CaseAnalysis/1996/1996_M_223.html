Inder Mani and Others v Mathehwari Prasad and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1733; (1996) 6 SCC 587; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 299; 1996 (4) CLT 218; JT 1996 (9) SC 135; 1996(7) SCALE 577; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 400; 1996 (7) Supreme 470; 1997 (1) UJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Judicial Discipline - Administrative instructions issued by Chief Justice of High Court and compliance of such instructions - Prerogative of Chief Justice to constitute benches - Constitution of Division Bench - Work assigned - Single Judge instead disposed of work and passed ex parte order allowing writ petition - Whether proper? - Held, no - Disregard of administrative directions given by Chief Justice of HC, deprecated in strong words - Impugned order of single judge set aside - Matter remitted - Appeal disposed of.
