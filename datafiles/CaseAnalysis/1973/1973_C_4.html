Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal v Calcutta Discount Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 240; (1974) 3 SCC 260; AIR 1974 SC 1358; 1973 (2) CTR 425; [1973] 91 ITR 8; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 952; 1974 TAXLR 477

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(1) and 33 - (A) Transfer of shares - At less than market price - By assessee company to subsidiary company - Whether difference between market price and actual price is deemed profits - (B) Procedural defect in memo of appeal - Influence upon tribunals or courts in granting relief - Held, cannot be deemed to be profits of assessee - Relief cannot be refused - Appeal Dismissed.
