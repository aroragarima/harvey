Abdul Razak Abdul Wahab Sheikh v S. N. Sinha, Commissioner of Police, Ahmedabad and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 494; (1989) 2 SCC 222; (1989) SCC (Cr) 326; AIR 1989 SC 2265; 1989 (3) Crimes 122; 1989 CRLJ 475; 1989 CRLR 421; 1989 CrLR(SC) 421; JT 1989 (1) SC 478; 1989(1) SCALE 542; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 890; 1989 (2) UJ 36

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Past Conduct, Terrorist And Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Amendment Act, 1985, Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Criminal - Gujarat Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act, 1985, s. 3(2) - Bombay Police Act, 1951 - Detention - No application for bail made on behalf of detenu who was in Jail - Possibility of his coming out non-existent - No fresh grounds disclosed that if released on bail, detenu would indulge in activities prejudicial to maintenance of public order - Held, there was no subjective satisfaction by detaining authority - Detention order liable to be quashed - Order accordingly.
