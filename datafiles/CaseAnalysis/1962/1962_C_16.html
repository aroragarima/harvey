Â British India Corporation Limited v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.K. Sarkar, Raghubar Dayal, S.K. Das, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 253; AIR 1963 SC 104; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 307; [1963] 3 S.C.R. 642

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Provisional Collection Taxes Act, 1931, Finance Act, 1954

Summary: Excise - Factories Act, 1948 - Central Excises And Salt Act, 1944 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Whether the imposition of Excise Duty on the petitioner by virtue of item No. 17 "Footwear" of the First Schedule to the Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 and calculation of the duty advaloram by including in the price, charges for freight, packing and distribution was valid?
