All India Trade Union of Food Corporation v J. M. Lyngdoh and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 861; (1993) 2 SCC 31; (1993) SCC (L&S) 315; 1993 (23) ATC 942; 1993 (1) CLR 579; 1993 CRLR 210; JT 1993 (1) SC 606; 1993 (2) LLJ 1148; 1993 (2) LLN 249; 1993(1) SCALE 206; 1993 (1) SLJ 115; 1993 (1) SLR 711; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 874

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - (A) Revision of pay - Recommendations made in report of High Power Pay Committee - Were to be implemented w.e.f. 01-01-1986 - (B) Switch over to Industrial Dearness Allowance (IDA) pattern of scales of pay - Voluntary basis - Option - Exercise of - Direction of the Court - Non-compliance with - Contempt proceedings - Held, Food Corporation of India (FCI) has granted revised scales to Group 'A' and 'C' of Class III as 975-1540 and Rs. 1500-2300 respectively, only Group 'B' employees have been correctly given revised personal scales as Rs. 1350-2200 - As far as Group 'A', 'B' and 'C' of Class IV employees are concerned, FCI has given Rs. 750-940, Rs. 750-940 and Rs. 800-950 respectively - There is thus, obviously a non-compliance of direction given by this Court as far as grant of revised scales is concerned - There is non-compliance of direction to give revised personal scales of pay as recommended by HPP Committee to said employees of FCI as well as of direction to give option to employees to switch over to IDA pattern of scales of pay - It is to be directed that FCI to give to its employees concerned, revised personal scales of pay as recommended by High Powered Committee - Further, FCI to keep open option of employees to switch over to IDA pattern of scales of pay for a period of six months from the date of the judgment - Such employees who desire to exercise the option may do so, within the period - Limitation of time modified - Contempt petition disposed of.
