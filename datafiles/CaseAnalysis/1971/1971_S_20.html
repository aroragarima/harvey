Shree Agency v S.K. Bhattacharjee

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 851; AIR 1972 SC 780; 1977 (1) E.L.T. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Appeal by Special leave - Concurrent findings of fact - Inference - Evidence on record to support concurrent findings of fact by tribunals - Held, Supreme Court will not interfere with those findings unless it is shown that findings are vitiated by error of law or some grave injustice is caused to party by such findings - Appeal dismissed.
