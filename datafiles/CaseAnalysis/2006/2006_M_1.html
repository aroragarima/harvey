(1) Union of India; (2) Promotee Telecom v (1) Madras Telephone Sc and St Social Welfare

Supreme Court of India

28 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 528; (2006) 8 SCC 662; 2006 (6) AWC 6042; 2006(9) SCALE 626; 2007 (2) SLR 641; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 694

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 309 - Application for clarification by applicants pursuant to liberty granted to them by order of SC Court D/- 18-07-2003 in SLP(Civil) No 9189/2003 in Promotee Telecom Engineers Forum and Ors Vs Secretary, Department of Telecommunications and others - Whether applicants can claim protection of their seniority and consequent promotion on basis of observations and clarification contained in judgment of this Court reported in (2000 Indlaw SC 2861)? - Directed that such of applicants whose seniority had been determined by competent authority, and who had been given benefit of seniority and promotion pursuant to orders passed by Courts or Tribunals following principles laid down by Allahabad HC and approved by this Court, which orders have since attained finality, cannot be reverted with retrospective effect - Order accordingly.
