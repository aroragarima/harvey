Vasanta v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 442; (1983) SCC (Cr) 535; (1984) Supp SCC 648; AIR 1983 SC 361A; 1983 (1) Crimes 728; 1983 CRLJ 693; 1983 CRLR 174; 1983(1) SCALE 871; [1983] S.C.R. 348

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 304, Part II - Appeal challenging conviction awarded by High Court - Appellant entered in a verbal altercation and assaulted decease with very great force even though altercation was not that serious - Injury on chest and heart as per medical evidence - Held, case fell u/s. 302 - Appeal dismissed.
