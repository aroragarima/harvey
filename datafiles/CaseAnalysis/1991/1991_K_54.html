Dattatraya @ Prakash And Ors v Krishna Rao @ Lala Saheb Baxi Through L.Rs.Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 760; (1993) Supp1 SCC 32; AIR 1991 SC 1972; 1991 (2) HLR 503; JT 1991 (6) SC 160; 1991(2) SCALE 368; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 644; 1991 (2) UJ 524

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family & Personnel - Madhya Bharat Abolition of Jagir Act, 1951, s. 9 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Hindu Law - Jagir grant - Impartible estate - Partition of joint family property - Considering - (A) Succeeded by lineal eldest descendant of family by rules of primogeniture - (B) Compensation received towards such estate - Held, concept of coparcenary is not applicable to it - Retains character as impartible - Appeal partly allowed.
