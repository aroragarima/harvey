M/s. Vadilal Chemicals Limited v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 473; (2005) 6 SCC 292; AIR 2005 SC 3073; JT 2005 (6) SC 560; 2005(6) SCALE 68; 2005 (142) STC 76; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Processing, Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1974

Summary: [A.] Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 20(2) - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 9(2) - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Production and bottling of liquid gases - Whether appellant is entitled to exemption from payment of sales tax as per the notification D/- 17-03-1993? - Held, matter was considered in depth and sanctioned by District Level Committee of which, DCCT was a part - Appellant made a full disclosure of process undertaken in respect of which sales tax exemption was granted - No malafides has been alleged against appellant - Eligibility certificates were issued by Department of Industries and Commerce and could not be cancelled by Sales Tax Authorities - Appeal allowed.
