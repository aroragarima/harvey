Eicher Motors Limited and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, K. Venkataswami, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 943; (1999) 2 SCC 361; AIR 1999 SC 892; 1999 (152) CTR 273; 1999 (81) ECR 7; 1999 (106) E.L.T. 3; JT 1999 (1) SC 205; 1999 (30) RLT 829; 1999(1) SCALE 230; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 295; 1999 (1) SLT 452

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 37 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 57 F(4 A) - Applicability of - Goods manufactured prior to 16-3- 1995 on which duty was paid and credit facility thereto has been availed of for purpose of manufacture of further goods - Held, not applicable.
