Suresh Kumar v Town Improvement Trust, Bhopal

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 499; (1989) 2 SCC 329; AIR 1989 SC 1222; JT 1989 (1) SC 439; 1989(1) SCALE 534; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 908; 1989 (1) UJ 650

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Constitution - Madhya Pradesh Town Improvement Trust Act, 1960 - Reasonability of awarded for land acquisition is questioned - Held, compensation should be enhanced by 1/6th to Rs. 14,000/- Per acre - By ascertaining market value - Appeal allowed.
