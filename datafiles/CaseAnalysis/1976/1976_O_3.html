Union of India v Om Parkash

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 95; (1976) 4 SCC 32; AIR 1976 SC 1745; 1976 (2) ALR 362; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 998; 1976 UJ 450

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30(c), 8(2) and 20(4) - Meaning of 'Or is otherwise invalid' - Appointment of arbitrator u/s. 8(2) - Jurisdiction of Court - Held, no further jurisdiction to proceed further to make an order referring dispute to arbitrator - Such reference made and awards passed - Awards can be set aside u/s. 30(c) - Phrase covers every form of invalidity including invalidity of reference.
