Supreme Court of India

09 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Solatium and interest - Validity of decision of Supreme Court in Khoday Distilleries Ltd case 1994 Indlaw SC 1123 - Held, judgment and order of this court passed u/art. 136 is not amenable to judicial review u/art. 32 of Constitution - Petition dismissed.
