Sitaram Kashiram Konda v Pigment Cakes and Chemicals Manufacturing Company

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 25; (1979) 4 SCC 12; (1979) SCC (L&S) 351; AIR 1980 SC 16; 1979 (39) FLR 347; 1979 LabIC 1378; 1979 (2) LLJ 444; 1979 (2) LLN 337; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 125; 1979 SLJ 552

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Service matter - Wrongful dismissal - Maintainability of suit for reinstatement and in the alternative for compensation - Held, main reliefs could not be granted as grant would amount to specific performance of the contract of service - But in alternative prayer, this relief could be granted by the Civil Court - Appeal Allowed.
