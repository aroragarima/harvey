Director Of School Education Madras And Others v O. Karuppa Thevan

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1930; (1994) Supp2 SCC 666; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1180; 1994 (28) ATC 99; 1996 (1) SLR 225; 1996 (1) UPLBEC 347A

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether employee ought to have been heard before transfer? - Held, no law requires an employee to be heard before his transfer when authorities make transfer for exigencies of administration - View of Court that in effecting transfer, fact that children of an employee are studying should be given due weight, if exigencies of service are not urgent - Transfer not to be effected in current academic year - Appeal allowed.
