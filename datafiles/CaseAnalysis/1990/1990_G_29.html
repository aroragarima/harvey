Suresh Chand v Gulam Chisti

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jagannatha Shetty, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 684; (1990) 1 SCC 593; AIR 1990 SC 897; 1990 ALJ 145; 1990 (16) ALR 228; 1990 (1) AWC 274; JT 1990 (1) SC 118; 1990 (1) RCJ 337; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 302; 1990 (1) RentLR 380; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 186

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 39 and 40 - Eviction - Benefit u/s. 39 - Entitlement - Eviction suit filed prior to commencement of Act - Failure of tenant to deposit arrears of rent within a month from date of commencement of Act - Tenant depositing arrears within a month after moratorium period expired in 1977 - Held, he would not be entitled to benefit of s. 39 - Appeal allowed.
