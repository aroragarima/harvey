Commissioner Of Income-Tax Andhra Pradesh, Hyderabad. v Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation, Hyderabad.

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 344; (1986) 2 SCC 391; AIR 1986 SC 1054; 1986 (52) CTR 75; [1986] 159 ITR 1; 1986(1) SCALE 353; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 570; 1986 TAXLR 604; [1986] 25 TAXMAN 63

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950, s. 3 and 23 - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.11, 22 etc. - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s.4(3)(i) - Appeal u/s. 261 of 1961 Act - Whether exemption u/s. 4(3)(i) of 1922 Act and s. 11 of 1961 are available in regard to income derived by SRTC from activities carried on by it under 1950 Act - Corporation held, was entitled to the exemption claimed by it both under the 1922 Act and under the 1961 Act - Appeal dismissed.
