Kerala State Road Transport Corporation v K.O. Varghese and others

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 380; (2003) 12 SCC 293; AIR 2003 SC 3966; 2003 (97) FLR 1010; 2004 (1) JhrCR(SC) 210; JT 2003 (Supp2) SC 145; 2003 (2) KHCACJ 539; 2003 (2) KLT 706; 2003 LabIC 2042; 2003(4) SCALE 229; [2003] 3 S.C.R. 779; 2003 (1) SCW 2847; 2003 (2) SLJ 278; 2003 (3) SLT 770; 2003 (3) Supreme 715; 2003 (4) UPLBEC 3301

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Ex Gratia Payment

Summary: [A.] Service - Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950, s.34 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 366(17) - Insurance Companies Act, 1982 - Industrial and Provident Societies Act, 1965 - Kerala Service Rules, 1959 - Concept of pension - Held, pension payable to an employee is earned by rendering long and sufficient service and therefore can be said to be a deferred portion of compensation for service rendered discernible - Purpose thus underlying pension scheme or a statute introducing pension scheme must inform interpretative process and accordingly it should receive a liberal construction and Courts may not so interpret such statute as to render them obscure - No dispute about entitlement and what was in controversy related to date for which payment was to be made - State Govt's letter dated 17/3/1984 authorized Corporation to pay pension to its employees as per Kerala Service Rules - Whether letter in question constituted a direction u/s. 34 of Act is an issue which is linked with several other issues like power of Corporation to fix a different date dehors any special direction of Govt. u/s. 34 of Act - Even if it is held that letter was not in nature of a special direction, other issues were required to be considered - That has apparently not been done - One of issues which needed consideration was indication of KSR Part III on question of paying pension in Corporation's order dated 5/5/1984 - No scope for interference with that part of HC's directions which related to recovery of amounts allegedly paid extra to employees - Other issues to be examined by HC afresh - Appeal disposed of.
