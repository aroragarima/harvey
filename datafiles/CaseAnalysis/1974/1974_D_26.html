Daya Singh (dead) Through L.Rs. and Another v Dhan Kaur

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 279; (1974) 1 SCC 700; AIR 1974 SC 665; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 528

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Female Heir, Succession & Inheritance, Marriage & Divorce

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 8 - CPC, 1908, s. 11 - Hindu Law Of Inheritance (Amendment) Act, 1929 - (A) Applicability of s. 8 of 1956 Act - Widow succeeding to husband's estate dying after Act came into force - Succession to estate - (B) Practice and Procedure - Suit by reversioners challenging gift by widow in favour of daughter - Res judicata - Applicability - Held, s. 8 applies - Decree obtained is binding between parties and operates as res judicata - Appeal dismissed.
