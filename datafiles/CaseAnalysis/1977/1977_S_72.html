D. Nagaraja Etc v State Of Karnataka & Ors

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 146; (1977) 2 SCC 148; (1977) SCC (L&S) 220; AIR 1977 SC 876; 1977 LabIC 533; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 626; 1977 SLJ 217; 1977 (1) SLR 441; 1977 UJ 162

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts.14, 16 and 226 - Karnataka Village Offices Abolition Act, 1961 - Karnataka General Services (Revenue Subordinate Branch) Village Accountants (Recruitment) Rules, 1970, rr. 4, 5 - Who can apply for writ - Maintainability of petition challenging constitutionality of rr. 4 and 5 of Rules, 1970 - Held, petitioners who did not apply for appointment could not complain of the unconstitutionality - Appeal dismissed.
