State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Visan Kumar Shiv Charan Lal

Supreme Court of India

05 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1911; (2008) 15 SCC 233; AIR 2009 SC 1999; 2009 (120) FLR 313; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 156; JT 2008 (13) SC 76; 2009 (2) LLJ 695; 2009(1) SCALE 187; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 156; 2009 (3) SLR 407; 2009 (1) UPLBEC 629

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - High Court dismissed Letters Patent Appeal on the ground that it was not maintainable as the order was in terms of art. 227 of the Constitution of India, 1950 - Held, High Court was not justified in holding that the Letters Patent Appeal was not maintainable - Writ appeal shall be heard by the Division Bench on merits - Appeal allowed.
