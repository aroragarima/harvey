Ahmedabad Manufacturing and Calico Printing Company Limited and another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Yogeshwar Dayal, N. Venkatachala, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1402; (1993) Supp2 SCC 7; 1993 (46) ECR 538; 1993 (63) E.L.T. 601; JT 1993 (1) SC 85; 1993(1) SCALE 63; [1993] 1 S.C.R. 142

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Finance Act, 1982, s. 11A, 11B and 51, rr. 9 and 49 - Whether duty of excise could be levied and collected in respect of yam which was obtained at an intermediate stage and, thereafter subjected to an integrated process for the manufacture of different fabrics under tarif items 18.1, 18.111 and 18E? - Held, that the measure for imposition of excise duty is by weight "per kilogram" in all the three items, namely 18.1, 18.111 and 18E - Hence, the decision in J.K . Couon Mills will be applicable to all types of cases under items 18.1, 18.111 and 18E - Further, held, in view of this peculiar fact it would not be in the interest of justice if the parties are directed to contest the individual show cause notices issued by the respondents in view of the aforesaid directives - Impugned notices quashed.
