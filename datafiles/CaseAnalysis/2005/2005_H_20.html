Ajai Agarwal and Others v Har Govind Prasad Singhal and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 November 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 719; (2005) 13 SCC 145; 2006 (1) ADJ 294; AIR 2006 SC 282; 2006 (62) ALR 1; 2006 (1) AWC 1; JT 2005 (10) SC 161; 2006 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2891; 2005(9) SCALE 133; 2006(9) SCALE 83; 2006 (1) SCJ 23; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 21; 2006 (1) UC 325

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 16(10) - Eviction - Agreement between parties for periodical enhancement of rent - Finding that agreement between parties for periodical enhancement of rent and concluded that tenant was in arrears in payment of rent - Appealed herein - Held, in absence of any proper evidence regarding purported agreement for periodical enhancement of rents, it becomes difficult to accept story of such agreed enhancement as made out on behalf of landlord - Petition by tenant allowed - Appeal allowed.
