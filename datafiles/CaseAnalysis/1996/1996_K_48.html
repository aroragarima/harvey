Special Tehsildar, Land Acquisition, Kerala v K.V. Ayisumma

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3736; (1996) 10 SCC 634; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 739; AIR 1996 SC 2750; AIR 1996 SCW 3445; 1996 (31) CCC 200; 1996 (3) CLT 215; JT 1996 (7) SC 204; 1996(5) SCALE 548; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 848; 1996 (5) Supreme 774

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Condonation of delay in filing application for review - Whether explanation of each day's delay can be asked from State Govt.? - Supreme Court held that such insistence of explaining every day's delay would be improper as such strict standard of proof will leads to grave miscarriage of public justice - Appeal allowed.
