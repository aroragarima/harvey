Shri Abhey Ram & Ors v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1149; (1997) 5 SCC 421; AIR 1997 SC 2564; 1997 (2) CLT 450; JT 1997 (5) SC 354; 1997(4) SCALE 193; 1997 (2) SCJ 205; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 931; 1997 (5) Supreme 130; 1997 (2) UJ 230

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Constitution, art. 226 - Composite notification u/s. 4 and declaration u/s. 6 regarding land acquisition - Some landowners filed Petition against - Stay Order - Subsequently declaration u/s. 6, quashed in respect of writ petitioners - Ground, objections filed u/s. 5A were not properly considered - The said decision operates only in regards to the writ petitioners and not the persons who were not eo nomine parties to that decision - Effect - Declaration u/s. 6 qua persons other than writ petitioners was not barred by proviso to s. 6 nor it would be vitiated by any error of law warranting interference - Appeal dismissed.
