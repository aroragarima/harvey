Union of India v Messrs R. B. Ch. Raghunath Singh and Company

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 163; (1979) 4 SCC 21; AIR 1980 SC 103; 1979 ALJ 1317; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 128

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 8(1)(b) - Power of court to appoint arbitrator - Whether there was a named Arbitrator? - Held, even though arbitrator was named by office, it was open to court to supply vacancy in his place u/s. 8(1)(b) - Appeal dismissed.
