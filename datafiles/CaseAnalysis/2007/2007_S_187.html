Zenith Steel Tubes and Industries Limited and Another v Sicom Limited

Supreme Court of India

21 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1161; (2008) 1 SCC 533; AIR 2008 SC 451; 2008(2) ALL MR 350; 2008 (5) AWC 4254; 2008 (1) Bom.C.R. 30; [2008] 141 Comp Cas 428; 2007 (6) CompLJ 409; 2008 (1) DRTC 421; JT 2007 (13) SC 102; 2007(13) SCALE 384; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 413

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Matter referred to Larger Bench

Summary: Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1985; State Financial Corporations Act, 1951 - Appellant company took loan from respondent and second appellant executed a personal guarantee for repayment of the loan amount in case of default by the appellant-company - Division Bench held that provisions of s. 22 of SICA, as amended in 1994, did not prohibit any proceeding, other than a suit for enforcement of any security against the guarantor - Appeal against - Held, matter should be referred to a larger Bench to resolve the existing anomaly resulting from different views expressed in the two cases - Matter referred to larger Bench.
