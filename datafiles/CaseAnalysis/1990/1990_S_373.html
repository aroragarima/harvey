East India Hotels and Another v State of West Bengal and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 608; (1990) Supp SCC 755; AIR 1990 SC 2029; JT 1991 (5) SC 138

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: West Bengal Entertainments and Luxuries (Hotels and Restaurants) Tax Act, 1972

Summary: Indirect Tax - Media & Entetainment - West Bengal Entertainments And Luxuries (Hotels and Restaurants) Tax Act, 1972, ss. 3 and 4 - Entertainment tax and luxury tax respectively u/s. 3 and 4 - Validity - Taxable event mere existence of luxury and entertainment - It provides nexus for valid enactment - Taxable event in respect of 'entertainment' service rendered and not entertainment - Whether proper - Held, valid - Proper - Ensuring certainty - Appeal dismissed.
