Union of India and Others v National Hydroelectric Power Corporation Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, N. Santosh Hegde, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 19851; (2001) 6 SCC 307; AIR 2001 SC 2512; 2001 (4) CompLJ 7; JT 2001 (6) SC 70; 2001(4) SCALE 531; 2001 (4) SCJ 320; 2001 (5) SLT 454; [2001] Supp1 S.C.R. 14; 2001 (5) Supreme 352

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Water

Keywords: Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977, ss. 2(c)3 and 16 - Whether there can be a valid levy under provisions of Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, on hydropower generating industry? - When Act was promulgated, hydel power generating industry was not included in Sch. I - In order to be able to impose cess on this industry, notification No GSR 377(E) D/- 16-04-1993 was purported to be issued u/s. 16 of Act - No resolution relating to notification D/- 16-04-1993 had been moved in Parliament - If no resolution had been moved, question of Parliament giving approval does not arise - Held, levy and purported realization of cess was not in accordance with law - Appeal dismissed.
