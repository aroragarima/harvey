Sterlite Industries (India) Limited v Commissioner Of Customs, Chennai

Supreme Court of India

19 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 485; (2007) 10 SCC 474; 2007 (210) E.L.T. 180; 2007(4) SCALE 627; 2007 (8) SCJ 956; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 84

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Tariff Item

Summary: Excise - Classification - S.H. 9022.19, 9027.30, Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Fully Automated Sequential X-ray Spectrometer - Whether classifiable under S.H. 9022.19 based on the use of X-ray as claimed by the Department or whether classifiable as Spectrometer under S.H. 9027.30 being an apparatus for physical or chemical analysis - Held, catalogue shows that the spectrometer is so designed that it functions in an integrated manner along with the computer and can be operated and controlled only with computer - Matter remitted to the Tribunal as none of these aspects had been considered by the authorities below - Impugned order set aside.
