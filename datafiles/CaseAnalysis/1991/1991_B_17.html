Nandganj Sihori Sugar Co. Limited, Rae Bareli And Anr. v Badri Nath Dixit And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, K. Jagannatha Shetty, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 630; (1991) 3 SCC 54; (1991) SCC (L&S) 981; AIR 1991 SC 1525; 1991 ALJ 213; 1991 (2) CLR 135; 1991 (2) CompLJ 241; 1991 (62) FLR 828; JT 1991 (2) SC 338; 1991 (1) LLN 1102; 1991(1) SCALE 794; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 468; 1991 (2) SLR 768

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Service - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 14 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 10 - Whether contract of employment can be specifically enforced - Chairman of holding company, advising subsidiary company to appoint a plaintiff - Held, such contract cannot specifically enforced - Suit to enforce contract liable to be dismissed - Even if there was contract in terms of which plaintiff was entitled to seek relief, only relief which was available in law was damages and not specific performance - Appeal allowed.
