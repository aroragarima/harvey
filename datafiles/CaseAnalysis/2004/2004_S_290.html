State Of Bihar And Ors. v Rajendra Singh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

24 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 649; (2007) 15 SCC 688; AIR 2004 SC 4419; 2004 (57) ALR 300; 2004 (4) AWC 2969; 2004 CRLJ 4187; JT 2004 (8) SC 168; 2004(7) SCALE 114; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 735

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Contempt proceedings - HC directed re-consideration of order purported to have been passed in compliance with direction of HC - Whether direction for re-consideration sustainable in law? - Held, rightness or wrongness of order cannot be urged in contempt proceedings - Right or wrong order has to be obeyed - While dealing with an application for contempt Court cannot traverse beyond order, non-compliance of which is alleged - Matter remitted for fresh consideration - Appeal allowed.
