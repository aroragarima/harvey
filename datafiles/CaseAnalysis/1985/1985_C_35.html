Badri Prasad and Another v Commissioner of Income Tax, Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 333; (1985) 4 SCC 664; (1986) SCC (Tax) 130; AIR 1986 SC 358; 1985 (49) CTR 223; [1985] 156 ITR 430; 1985(2) SCALE 1246; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 879; 1985 TAXLR 1382; [1985] 23 TAXMAN 14; 1986 (1) UJ 691

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922 - Expression 'previous year' - Joint family business - Partition of family - Business discontinued - Succeeded by partnership firm - Benefit of s. 25 (4) - Relevant year of assessment - Held, that assessee was entitled to relief u/s 25 (4) in assesment year 1949-50 - Appeal allowed.
