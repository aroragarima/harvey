Â Associated Cement Companies, Limited (Dwarka Cement Works, Dwarka and Bombay), and Others v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Sudhi Ranjan Das, S.K. Das, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 21; AIR 1959 SC 967; 1959 (1) LLJ 644; 1961 (2) SCJ 490; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 925

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Fair Wage

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Whether demand for bonus by workmen should be considered? - Calculations show that there is no available surplus from which respondents can claim any bonus for relevant year - Appellant has already paid respondents bonus for relevant year and it is likely that it may continue to do so in future - Award made by tribunal is set aside - Appeal allowed.
