Shri Kishan & Krishan Kumar v Manoj Kumar Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 600; (1998) 2 SCC 710; AIR 1998 SC 999; AIR 1998 SCW 769; JT 1998 (1) SC 633; 1998 (1) RCR(Rent) 283; 1998(1) SCALE 582; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 830; 1998 (2) SLT 667; 1998 (2) Supreme 478

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, s. 13 - Eviction suit - Bar as to jurisdiction of Civil Court - Suit for eviction instituted during period, the building was exempted from operation of Act - Pendency - Expiry of exemption period - Whether Civil Court can continue the suit and pass decree - Whether execution of said decree is prohibited by s. 13 - Held, a suit instituted during the period of exemption of building from operation of Act could be continued and a decree passed therein could be executed even though the period of exemption came to an end during the pendency of the suit - Execution of decree not prohibited - Appeal dismissed.
