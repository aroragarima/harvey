M. Ramachandran v Govind Ballabh and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 738; (1999) 8 SCC 592; AIR 1999 SC 3601; 1999 (83) FLR 559; JT 1999 (7) SC 271; 1999(6) SCALE 82; 1999 (5) SLR 318; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 38; 1999 (3) UPLBEC 2441

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Central Administrative Tribunal (Group B and C Miscellaneous Posts) Recruitment Rules, 1989, r. 5 - Seniority - Fixation of - (A) Method of determining seniority - Held, the r. 5(2) hold the field, the Official Memorandum D/- 3-07-1986 has to give way in the matter of determination of inter se seniority of the persons recruited to the service on the same date - The seniority to be reckoned from the date of the officer's original date of regular appointment - (B) Whether employees recruited in service u/r. 5(1) are entitled to benefit of service on equivalent post in their parent departments? - Held, yes - Respondent officials are directed to finalize seniority list of all employees recruited in service u/r. 5(1) of Rules strictly applying provisions of sub-r. (2) - Fresh seniority list be finalized at earliest and if possible within a period of three months from today - Till finalization of seniority list, parties shall be permitted to hold posts presently held by them - Appeal allowed.
