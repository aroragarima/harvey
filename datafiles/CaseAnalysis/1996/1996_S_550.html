M.C. Mehta v State Of Tamil Nadu And Others

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1746; (1996) 6 SCC 756; (1997) SCC (L&S) 49; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 582; AIR 1997 SC 699; JT 1996 (11) SC 685; 1997 (1) LLN 12; 1996(9) SCALE 42; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 726; 1996 (8) Supreme 450; 1997 (1) UJ 243

Case Digest

Subject: Women & Children

Keywords: Compulsory Education, Beedi and Cigar Workers (Conditions of Employment) Act, 1966, Employment Of Children Act, 1938, Children (Pledging of Labour) Act, 1933

Summary: Women and Child - Child Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986, s. 3 - Employment Of Children Act, 1938 - Constitution, arts. 39(e), 39(f), 41and 47 - Tackling the problem of child labour - Matter under consideration of Supreme Court - Held, SC issued direction to State Govts.regarding fulfilment of legislative intendment behind enactment - Offending employer to pay compensation of Rs. 20,000 for every child employed in contravention of the provisions - Petition disposed of.
