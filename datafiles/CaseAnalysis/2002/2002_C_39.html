Gulabrao Balwantrao Shinde and others v Chhabubai Balwantrao Shinde and others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1389; (2003) 1 SCC 212; AIR 2003 SC 160; 2003 (3) AWC 2192; 2003 (3) Bom.C.R. 84; 2003 (1) JhrCR(SC) 170; JT 2002 (9) SC 314; 2002(8) SCALE 356; 2002 (6) SLT 488; 2003 (Supp) RD 563; 2003 (1) UC 116

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Keywords: In Lieu Of Maintenance

Summary: Hindu Succession Act - Partition - suit for - Held, in the absence of any pleadings or evidence to the effect that Balwantrao Shinde had given the properties to respondent by way of maintenance, the finding that the properties were given in lieu of maintenance to respondent which right could be enlarged into full ownership right could not be recorded. Also, the properties were ancestral lands of Balwantrao Shinde in which plaintiff No. 1 had a right by birth. - Further, since the appellants had claimed only half share of the property by way of partition they could not be declared owners of the entire extent of the property.
