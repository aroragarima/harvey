Raja Srivalgoti Sarvagna Kumara Krishna Yachandra Bachadurvaru v Special Land Acquisition Officer, City Improvement Trust Board, Bangalore and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 526; (1979) 4 SCC 356; AIR 1979 SC 869; 1979 UJ 282

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of market value for purpose of land acquisition - Held, estimation of market value in many cases must depend largely on evaluation of many imponderables and hence, it must necessarily be to some extent a matter of conjecture or guess - Appeal allowed.
