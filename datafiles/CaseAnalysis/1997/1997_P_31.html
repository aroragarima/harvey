Suresh Kumar Bhikamchand Jain v Pandey Ajay Bhushan and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1722; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1; (1998) 1 SCC 205; AIR 1998 SC 1524; 1998 (5) Bom.C.R. 250; 1998 CRLJ 1242; JT 1997 (9) SC 365; 1998 (1) RCR(Criminal) 165; 1997(7) SCALE 282; 1998 (2) SLT 98; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 524; 1997 (10) Supreme 101

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Government servant

Summary: Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947, s. 23(4)(a) - Orissa sale, Tax Rule, 1947, r. 80 - Whether Commr. can exercise revisional powers over appellate order passed by Asst. Commr.? - Whether revisional powers of Commr. are limited under proviso to s. 23(4)? - Whether Commr. can exercise revisional power over appellate order passed by Asst. Commr.? - Whether revisional powers of Commr. are limited under proviso to s. 23(4)? - Held order of Asst. Commr. gets revised suo motu by Commissioner in exercise of revisional powers u/s. 23(4)(a) - Applicable only where a person filing revision have remedy by way of appeal u/s. 23(3).
