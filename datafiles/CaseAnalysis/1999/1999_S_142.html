Kiran v Sharad Dutt

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 2077; (2000) 10 SCC 243; 2000 (2) DMC 503; JT 2000 (1) SC 532

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Family & Personal

Summary: Family & Personal - Constitution - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 13B - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 142 - Divorce by mutual consent - Consented decree by SC - Held, SC in exercise of power u/art. 142 of the Constitution accepted the consent terms filed by the parties - Direction issued for decree for divorce by mutual consent u/s. 13B of the Act - Petition allowed.
