State of Punjab and Others v Gurdeep Kumar Uppal and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 362; (2003) 11 SCC 732; (2004) SCC (L&S) 444; AIR 2001 SC 2691; 2001 (2) CLR 403; 2001 (34) LabIC 2804; 2001(2) SCALE 584; 2001 (3) SLR 256

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Punjab Civil Medical Services Class II (Recruitment, and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1943, r. 7(5) - Ad Hoc Service - Seniority - Whether period of ad hoc services be counted towards seniority for giving higher scale of pay? - R. 7(5) provided that seniority of members, in each branch shall be determined by dates of their confirmation in service - Held, only regular service to be counted towards seniority - Relied on State of Haryana v. Haryana Veterinary and AHTS Association and Another - For calculating higher scale of pay and for determination of seniority only regular service rendered by employee to be counted and not ad hoc service - Appeal allowed.
