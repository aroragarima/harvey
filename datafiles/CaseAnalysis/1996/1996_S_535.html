Bijaya Kumar Agarwala Etc v State Of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 872; (1996) 5 SCC 1; (1996) SCC (Cr) 864; AIR 1996 SC 2531; 1996 (3) Crimes 99; 1996 CRLJ 3577; JT 1996 (7) SC 56; 1996(5) SCALE 530; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 249

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Socio-Economic

Summary: Socio- Economic - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3(1) - Orissa Rice and Paddy Control Order, 1965, cl. 3 - Carrying of goods in vehicle is not storing perse - But when persons moving in trucks with Paddy in excess than permitted quantity , whether it amounts to 'storing' of goods - Held, by itself would not amount to 'storing' of goods - Appeal allowed.
