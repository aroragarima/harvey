P.T.R. Exports (Madras) Pvt Ltd. & Ors. v The Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2973; (1996) 5 SCC 268; AIR 1996 SC 3461; 1996 (4) CCC 163; 1996 (66) ECR 7; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 3; JT 1996 (6) SC 435; 1996 (3) SLJ 41; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 662; 1997 (1) UJ 126

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: International - Trade - Constitution - Foreign Trade (Development and Regulation) Act, 1992, s.5 - Constitution of India, 1950, art.14 - Evidence Act, 1872, s.115 -Export and Import Policy of 1992-93 regarding ready-made garments classifying allotment under different heads - Final approval of International Agricultural Policy (GATT) incorporating separate agreements to Textile Sector - Change in export import policy accordingly - New policy of 1996 envisaging new methods for allotment - If can be challenged on ground of promissory estoppel or legitimate expectation - Held, cannot be challenged - Petition dismissed.
