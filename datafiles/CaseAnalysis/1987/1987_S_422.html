Khargram Panchayat Samiti and Another v State of West Bengal and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28250; (1987) 3 SCC 82; 1987 (1) BankCLR 204; 1987 (1) CalLT(SC) 5; JT 1987 (2) SC 266; 1987(1) SCALE 1142; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1207; 1987 (2) UJ 406

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Trade

Keywords: West Bengal Panchayat Act 1973, West Bengal Panchayat (Samiti Administration) Rules 1984

Summary: Miscellaneous - West Bengal Panchayat Act, 1973, s. 117 - West Bengal Panchayat (Samiti Administration) Rules, 1984 - Whether Panchayat Samiti being vested with the authority to grant a licence for the holding of a hat or fair u/s. 117 of Act within the limits of its territorial jurisdiction, must necessarily be held to have the consequential or incidental power to specify a day for the holding of such hat or fair? - Court held, power to specify a day must be held to be a power incidental to or consequential upon the principal power of issuing a licence u/s. 117 of Act for holding of a hat or fair - Appeal allowed.
