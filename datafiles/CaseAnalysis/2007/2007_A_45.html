Union Of India & Anr v Adani Exports Ltd. & Anr

Supreme Court of India

12 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1144; (2007) 13 SCC 207; 2007 (218) E.L.T. 164; JT 2007 (13) SC 143; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 268; 2008 (1) RCR(Criminal) 275; 2007(13) SCALE 4; 2007 (8) SCJ 485; [2007] 11 S.C.R. 974; 2009 (13) S.T.R. 81

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Customs Act 1962 - Application for dispensation of pre-deposit was rejected by the Appellate Tribunal for Foreign Exchange - Not only the High Court held that the order directing deposit was unsustainable but also held that the order of adjudication was unsustainable, overlooking the fact that the appeals were pending before the Tribunal - Appeal against - Held, while dealing with that the High Court was not justified in going into the merits and expressing its views and thereafter remitting the matter to the Tribunal. Such a course was not available to be adopted - Appeal disposed of.
