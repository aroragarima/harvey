Sital Prasad Saxena (Dead) By Lrs v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 196; (1985) 1 SCC 163; AIR 1985 SC 1; 1984 (10) ALR 702; 1985 (1) CCC 125; 1985 (1) LLN 6; 1984(2) SCALE 536; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 659; 1984 (2) SLJ 544; 1985 (1) SLR 112; 1985 UJ 206

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 100 and O. 22, rr. 3 and 9 - Dismissal of suit for declaration of status and arrears of salary - Appellant died during pendency of second appeal - Validity of substituting legal heirs and condonation of delay in applying - Approach of High Court as revisional Court is wrong - Order accordingly.
