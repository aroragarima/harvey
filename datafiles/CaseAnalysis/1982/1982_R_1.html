Baldev Sahai Bangia v R. C. Bhasin

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 243; (1982) 2 SCC 210; AIR 1982 SC 1091; 1982 (2) RCJ 98; 1982 (2) RCR(Rent) 133; 1982 (1) RentLR 741; 1982(1) SCALE 366; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 670; 1982 UJ 405

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Finding of fact

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14 (1)(d) - Tenancy - Interpretation of word 'Family' - Tenant shifted to foreign country - Left behind mother, sister and brother in tenanted house - Plea, they ceased to be members of family - Eviction sought - Held, cannot be evicted on such ground - Appeal allowed.
