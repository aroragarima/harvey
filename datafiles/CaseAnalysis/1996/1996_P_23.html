Karnataka Public Service Commission v P. S. Ramakrishna

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3085; (1996) 2 SCC 519; (1996) SCC (L&S) 532; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 942; 1996 (33) ATC 230; 1996 (72) FLR 764; JT 1996 (2) SC 393A; 1996 (4) KarLJ 340; 1996(1) SCALE 39; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 692; 1996 (2) SLR 469; 1996 (1) Supreme 730; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 850

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Held, since respondents are avoiding service of notice, notice must, therefore, be deemed to have been served on respondent - Controversy raised in this case is covered by decision of this Court in Karnataka Public Service Commission case - Following said decision, appeal is allowed and order of Tribunal is set aside - Appeal allowed.
