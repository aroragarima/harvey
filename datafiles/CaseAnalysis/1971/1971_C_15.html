Century Spinning and Manufacturing Company Limited and Others v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Subimal Chandra Roy, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 580; (1972) 3 SCC 282; (1972) SCC (Cr) 495; AIR 1972 SC 545; 1972 CRLJ 329

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898 - Prohibition Act - Scope of s. 251(A)(2) - Held, sub-s (2) has to be read along with sub-section (3) - Reading them together it clearly means that if there is no ground for presuming that accused has committed an offence, charges must be considered to be groundless, which is same thing as saying that there is no ground for framing charges - Appeal allowed.
