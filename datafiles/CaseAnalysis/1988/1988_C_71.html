R.K.Palshikar (Huf) v Commissioner of Income Tax, M. P., Nagpur and

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 815; (1988) 3 SCC 594; AIR 1988 SC 1305; 1988 (70) CTR 31; [1988] 172 ITR 311; JT 1988 (2) SC 266; 1988(1) SCALE 1128; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 989; [1988] 38 TAXMAN 166

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 66, 12B, 66A, 2(4A) - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 2(4A), 12B, 66 and 66A - Conversion of agricultural land into non agricultural one - Premium charged on leasing it after developing as housing sites - (A) Reference - Question of law neither raised nor considered by the Tribunal - Questions as to 'transfer' and ' Consideration', since it was received as gift - (B) Capital asset - Transfer of, if - Held, question whether it amounted to 'transfer' raised and decided but question whether acquisition of asset was without consideration would not arise out of it - Held, s.12B of 1922 Act applicable and grant of leases amounts to transfer of capital assets - Appeal dismissed.
