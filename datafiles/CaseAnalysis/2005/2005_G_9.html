G. Srinivas Goud v State of A.P.

Supreme Court of India

03 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arun Kumar, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 859; (2005) 8 SCC 183; (2005) SCC (Cr) 1999; 2005 CRLJ 4367; JT 2005 (12) SC 215; 2005(8) SCALE 34; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 840

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Excise Official

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 22, 41(1), 41(2), 41(3), 42(2) - Conviction and sentence to rigorous imprisonment for ten years and a fine of Rupees one lakh each - Appeal against conviction - Contention that there is non-association of independent witnesses and non-compliance of s. 42 - Whether it is necessary for officers of gazetted rank to comply with s 42(2)? - Held, no means suggesting that provisions of Section 42 are not mandatory - Wherever they are attracted, compliance is mandatory - Appeal dismissed.
