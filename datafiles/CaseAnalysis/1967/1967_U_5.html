Â D.R. Nim, I. P. S. v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

05 January 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, K. Subba Rao, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 157; AIR 1967 SC 1301; 1967 (3) DLT 627; 1968 (1) LLJ 264; [1967] 2 S.C.R. 325; 1967 SLR 221

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Year Of Allotment, Ips (Regulation Of Seniority) Rules, 1954, Indian Police Service (Regulation of seniority) Rules 1954

Summary: Service - Indian Police Service (Regulation of seniority) Rules, 1954 - All India Services Act, 1951, s.3 - Whether a wrong year of allotment had been given to appellant? - Held, Central Govt. must consider question of approval of officiation period and come to an ad hoc decision after considering all relevant circumstances in consultation with State Govt. concerned and fix year of allotment - Further, Central Govt. is directed to fix year of allotment and seniority of appellant in accordance with this judgment and law - Appeal allowed.
