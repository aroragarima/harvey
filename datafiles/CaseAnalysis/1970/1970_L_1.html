>L. Debi Prasad (Dead) By LRs v Smt. Tribeni Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 526; (1970) 1 SCC 677; AIR 1970 SC 1286; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 101

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3, 60, 101 and 114 - Hindu Law - Adoption - Onus of proof - Ancient adoption - Allegation that person was adopted soon on his birth - Hearsay evidence of - But absence of evidence of giving and taking - Effect - When alleged adopted son treated by adoptive father and relatives (also who challenges adoption), as son of adoptive father - Adoption held proved
