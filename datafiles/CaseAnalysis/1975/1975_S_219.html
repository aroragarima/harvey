Raghbir Singh v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 486; (1975) SCC (Cr) 776; (1976) 1 SCC 145; AIR 1976 SC 91; 1976 CRLJ 172; 1975 CRLR 646

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Respectable

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s.161 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3 and 133 - CrPC, 1973, s. 51 - Bribery case - (A) Appreciation of evidence - Duty of officers in anti-corruption department to secure independent and respectable witnesses of raid - (B) Duty of Police Officer while laying of trap for public servant - Held, they should insist on observing this safeguard for the protection of public servants against whom a trap may have been laid and in the present case the search witnesses were interested witnesses - Duty of Police Officer pointed out - Appeal Allowed.
