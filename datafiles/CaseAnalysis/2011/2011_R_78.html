Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 October 2011

Case Analysis

Bench

D.K. Jain, A.R. Dave

Where Reported

2011 Indlaw SC 752; (2011) 10 SCC 292; AIR 2012 SC (Supp) 238; 2011 (273) E.L.T. 3; [2011] 13 S.C.R. 1; 2012 (27) S.T.R. 193

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Excise - Practice & Procedure - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11BB - Rebate of duty - Delayed refunds - Claim for interest - Sustainability - Appellant filed certain claims for rebate of duty before Assistant Commissioner of Central Excise which was rejected - Aggrieved, appellant filed appeal before Commissioner, which was allowed and sanctioned rebate claim - Aggrieved by said order Revenue filed appeal before Joint Secretary, Govt. which was dismissed - Ultimately rebate was sanctioned and appellant filed a claim for interest u/s. 11BB of the Act on account of delay in payment of rebate - Show cause notice was issued to appellant, proposing to reject their claim for interest on ground that rebate had been sanctioned to them within 3 months of receipt of order of Commissioner - Assistant Commissioner rejected claim relying on explanation to s. 11BB of the Act, - On appeal, Commissioner allowed appeal and directed Assistant Commissioner to compute and pay interest to appellant - Aggrieved by said order, Assistant Commissioner filed revision application before Joint Secretary, Govt. who set aside order passed by Commissioner and held appellant was not entitled to interest u/s. 11BB of the Act - Appellant filed writ petition before HC, relied on decision of Union of India & Anr. Vs. Shreeji Colour Chem Industries 2008 Indlaw SC 1479 HC upholded claim of assessee for interest u/s. 11BB of the Act - Hence, instant appeals - Revenue contended that no refunds were sanctioned u/s. 11BB of the Act were not attracted and refund orders was sanctioned within 3 months of passing of orders by Appellate authority, interest under said section was not payable - Whether liability of revenue to pay interest u/s. 11BB of the Act commences from date of expiry of 3 months from date of receipt of application for refund or on expiry of said period from date on which order of refund was made - Held, liability of revenue to pay interest u/s. 11BB of the Act commences from date of expiry of 3 months from date of receipt of application for refund u/s. 11B(1) of the Act and not on expiry of said period from date on which order of refund was made - Hence, in Shreeji Colour Chem Industries 2008 Indlaw SC 1479 relied on - However, in so far as reckoning of period for purpose of payment of interest u/s. 11BB of the Act was concerned, emphasis had been laid on date of receipt of application for refund - Appeals disposed of.
