Adri Dharan Das v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

21 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 127; (2005) 4 SCC 303; (2005) SCC (Cr) 933; AIR 2005 SC 1057; 2006 (2) CalLT(SC) 29; 2005 CRLJ 1706; 2005 (117) DLT 686; JT 2005 (2) SC 548; 2005 (2) MahLJ 259; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 32; 2005(2) SCALE 212; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 188

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 438 - Prayer made by appellant to extend protection available under s. 438 refused by Division Bench - Held, an interim order restraining arrest, if passed while dealing with an application under s. 438 will amount to interference in the investigation, which cannot, at any rate, be done under s. 438 of the Code - Uphold rejection of the prayer - Appeal disposed of.
