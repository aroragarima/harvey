Smt. Paniben v State Of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 919; (1992) SCC (Cr) 403; (1992) 2 SCC 474; AIR 1992 SC 1817; 1992 (1) CCR 1100; 1992 (3) Crimes 1180; 1992 CRLJ 2939; 1992 CrLR(SC) 334; JT 1992 (4) SC 397; 1992(1) SCALE 655; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 197

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 32 - Murder - Bride burning - Dying declaration - Satisfies credibility - Chance of suicide - Held, dying declaration acceptable - Conviction of mother-in-law justified - Question of leniency for sentencing does not sustain as deterrent theory applies.
