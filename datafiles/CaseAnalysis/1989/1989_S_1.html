Subhash Chand Jain v 1St Additional District And Sessions Judgesaharanpur And Other

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.H. Kania, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 488; (1989) 2 SCC 110; AIR 1989 SC 1070; 1989 ALJ 516; 1989 (15) ALR 300; 1989 (1) AWC 423; JT 1989 (1) SC 408; 1989 (2) RCJ 650; 1989 (1) RCR(Rent) 459; 1989(1) SCALE 483; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 837; 1989 (1) UJ 665

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, Uttar Pradesh Rent Act 1972

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 20(4) - Arrears of rent - Deposit - 'Date of first hearing' - Eviction suit decreed ex-parte - Decree set aside and fresh date for hearing fixed - Application for amendment of pleadings, by tenant - Date on which amendment was allowed would be date of first hearing within s. 20(4) - Being a small cause suit, Small Causes Courts Act does not contemplate fixation of date for settlement of issues - Entire arrears not deposited on that date - Effect - Held, tenant not entitled to benefit of s. 20(4) - Appeal dismissed.
