Sonik Industries, Rajkot v Municipal Corporation Of The City Of Rajkot

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 762; (1986) 2 SCC 608; AIR 1986 SC 1518; 1986 (1) CCC 870; 1986(1) SCALE 500; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 59; 1986 (2) UJ 33

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Bombay Municipal Boroughs Act, 1925, ss. 77 and 102 - (A) Publication of notice u/s. 77 reciting sanction and stating that inspection of rules can be had in office - Need of publication of rules in newspaper - (B) Challenge as to publication of rules u/s. 77 of Act - Held, as there was compliance with s. 77, such publication not necessary - It cannot be challenge u/s. 102 - Appeal dismissed.
