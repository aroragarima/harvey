Â Bangalore Woollen, Cotton and Silk Mills Company Limited, Bangalore v Corporation of The City of Bangalore, By Its Commissioner, Bangalore City.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah, S.K. Das, T.L. Venkatarama Aiyyar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 479; AIR 1962 SC 562; 1962 (1) SCJ 214; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 707

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Summary: Constitution - Indirect Tax - Municipalities & Local Governments - Terminal tax - City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, ss. 38(1)(b), 97, 130 and 98(2) - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 276, 301 and 305, schs. 3 and 7, List 2 Entry 52 and Entry 60 - (A) City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act - Whether non-compliance with s. 98(2) is fatal to legality of imposition of tax under Act? - Held, no, it does not - s. 38(1)(b) validates any defect in any act done or proceedings taken under Act - Makes immune from any defect or irregularity in such act or proceedings not affecting merits of case - Because it is in a chapter dealing with Municipal Authorities or other parts of section dealing with another subject is no reason for confining its operation to such defects only - Resolution published in newspapers and communicated to those affected by it - Mere failure to notify final resolution - Not fatal - (B) Constitution - Whether provisions of art. 301 of Constitution affect provisions of an existing law? - Further held, according to art. 305 - No, it does not except when President may otherwise direct - Combined effect of ss. 97 and 130 and Part V of Sch. III including class VIII is that words are of very general nature and would have same effect as if all articles were intended to be and were included - Even if certain octroi duty was levied under Class VIII set out in Part V of sch. III of Act after Constitution there was no making or passing of new regulation but imposition would still be covered by art. 305 - Imposition of octroi duty does not contravene art. 301 - Petition dismissed - Appeal dismissed.
