Central Board Of Direct Taxes And Others A v Aditya V. Birla

Supreme Court of India

27 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28494; (1988) Supp SCC 120; AIR 1988 SC 420; 1988 (67) CTR 165; [1988] 170 ITR 137; JT 1987 (4) SC 653; 1987(2) SCALE 1296; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 115; 1988 TAXLR 421; [1988] 36 TAXMAN 9; 1988 (1) UJ 304

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Technician, Finance Bill, 1975

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80 RRA - Income Tax - Deduction - Meaning of remuneration from foreign employer - Cannot be restricted to salary received by employee abroad - Whether fee received by consultant or technician is covered under s. 80 RRA of Act ? - Held, would also come within purview - Appeal dismissed.
