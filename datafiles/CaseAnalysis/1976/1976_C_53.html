Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal-1 v Simon Carves Limited

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 168; (1976) 4 SCC 435; AIR 1976 SC 2368; 1976 CTR 220; [1976] 105 ITR 212; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 207; 1976 UJ 771

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 147(b) - Income Tax Rules, 1962, r. 33 - Income escaping assessment - ITO adopting method of computation u/r. 33 - Resulted in lower tax liability as compared to other permissible method - Whether a case of income escaping assessment - Held, not such a case u/s.147(b) - Appeal Dismissed.
