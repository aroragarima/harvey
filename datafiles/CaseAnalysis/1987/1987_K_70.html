State of Karnataka v Krishna Alias Raju

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28543; (1987) SCC (Cr) 198; (1987) 1 SCC 538; 1987 (1) ACJ 443; AIR 1987 SC 861; 1987 (1) Crimes 397; 1987 CRLJ 776; 1987 CRLR 206; 1987 CrLR(SC) 206; JT 1987 (1) SC 217; 1987(1) SCALE 135; 1987 (1) SCJ 474; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 1103; 1987 (1) UJ 354

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Criminal; Tort

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939 - Enhancement of sentence - Driver convicted for killing one person and injuring another for offences under s. 304A and under other provisions - Sentence of fine of Rs. 250 - Respondent pleaded guilty to all charges - Accordingly convicted - Magistrate imposed trivial amounts of fines in awarding sentences to respondent for several convictions - High Court declined to exercise powers under s. 377, CrPC - Refusal of HC to enhance sentence of fine of Rs. 250/- for all offences - Not proper.
