Orient Paper Mills Limited v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 3; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 345

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Administrative - Whether direction given by Board in discharge of its quasi judicial functions valid? - When administrative officers are entrusted with quasi-judicial functions of ten times, they are unable to keep aside administrative considerations while discharging quasi-judicial functions - Held, in this case, both Collector as well as Central Govt. have ignored line that demarcates their administrative duties and their judicial functions - Direction invalid - Appeals allowed.
