Munshi Singh(Dead) By Lrs. And Ors. v Smt. Sohan Bai (Dead) By Lrs.

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 509; (1989) 2 SCC 265; AIR 1989 SC 1179; 1989 (15) ALR 672; 1989 (2) HLR 118; JT 1989 (1) SC 621; 1989(1) SCALE 682; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 1012; 1989 (2) UJ 88

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Keywords: Female Heirs, Marriage & Divorce

Summary: Family & Personal - Land & Property - Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 14 - Scope of s. 14 of Act - Gift of property by Hindu widow before Act came into force - Widow had no right in property on date on which Act came into force - Held, she could not get any advantage from coming into force of Act - Appeal dismissed.
