Supreme Court of India

14 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Orissa Education Act, 1969, Berhampur University Act, 1966

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, art. 226 - Pleading - Acceptance of - Cannot be in part - Lecturer in private college - Termination of service - Setting aside, on basis of admission by management as part of petitioner's plea - Validity - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
