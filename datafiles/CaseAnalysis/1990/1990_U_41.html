India Cement Limited Etc. v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K. Jayachandra Reddy, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 385; (1990) 4 SCC 356; AIR 1991 SC 724; JT 1990 (3) SC 572; 1990(2) SCALE 291; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 850; 1990 (2) UJ 584

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Trade

Keywords: Cement Control Order 1967

Summary: Land & Industrial law - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 133 - Industries (Development And Regulation) Act, 1951 - Cement Control Order, 1967, cl. 12 - Fixation of uniform retention price for all producers in Cement Industry on basis of expert opinion - Which were founded on relevant factors - Differential treatment given to only uneconomic unit - Held, no interference - Further held, not discriminatory - Appeal dismissed.
