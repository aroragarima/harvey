F.S. Gandhi (Dead) By Lrs. v Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, Allahabad

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 883; (1990) 3 SCC 624; AIR 1991 SC 1866; 1990 (84) CTR 35; [1990] 184 ITR 34; JT 1990 (3) SC 476; 1990 SCALE 89; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 886; [1990] 51 TAXMAN 15; 1991 (2) TLR 688

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Net Wealth, Interest In Property

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 2(e)(2)(iii) and 2(e)(v) - Exclusion of interest in property u/s.2(e)(2) (iii) of Wealth Tax Act from definition of 'Asset' - Word 'is' therein refers to present and future and does not mean 'has been' - Tenant holding over and new tenancy from month to month for unstated period - Tenancy precarious as could be terminated - Held, interest created by such tenancy Court not be treated as asset - Appeals allowed.
