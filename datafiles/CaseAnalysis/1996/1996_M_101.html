Mr. A. Trehan v M/S. Associated Electricalagencies And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3002; (1996) 4 SCC 255; (1996) SCC (L&S) 928; 1996 (2) ACC 53; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 6; AIR 1996 SC 1990; 1996 (2) CCC 307; 1996 (2) CLR 348; 1996 (74) FLR 1886; JT 1996 (5) SC 648; 1996 LabIC 1723; 1996 (2) LLJ 721; 1996 (2) LLN 97; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 27; 1996(4) SCALE 469; 1996 (2) SCJ 513; 1996 (4) SLR 17; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 728; 1996 (4) Supreme 289; 1996 (2) UJ 362

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Employees State Insurance (Central) Rules, 1950

Summary: Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923, s. 1 - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, s. 53 - Nature of bar under provisions - Held, bar takes away right of employee insured under Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 to claim compensation under Workmen's Compensation Act.
