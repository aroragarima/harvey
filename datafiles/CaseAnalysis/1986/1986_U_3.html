Dr. Keshva Ram Pal v U.P. Higher Education Services Commission, Allahabad and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 113; (1986) SCC (L&S) 195; (1986) 1 SCC 671; AIR 1986 SC 597; 1986 (12) ALR 174; 1986 (12) AWC 188; 1986 (1) CCC 705; 1986 (52) FLR 332; 1986 LabIC 553; 1986 (1) LLJ 311; 1986 (1) LLN 473; 1986(1) SCALE 107; 1986 (1) SLR 681; 1986 (1) UJ 331

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 315 - Service Matter - Selection - Omission of interview board to sub-divide total viva voce marks into sub-heads - Held, not obligatory in absence of specific instructions to sub-divide - Petition dismissed.
