Sheikhar Hotels Gulmohar Enclave and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 845; (2008) 14 SCC 716; 2008 (6) ADJ 307; AIR 2008 SC 2284; 2008 (4) ALD(SC) 141; 2008 (72) ALR 490; 2008 (4) AWC 3410; JT 2008 (7) SC 120; 2008 (3) RCR(Civil) 813; 2009 (107) RD 465; 2008(8) SCALE 322; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 273

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Corporate Body, National Capital Region Planning Board Act, 1985, Urban Planning and Development Act, 1973

Summary: Land Acquisition Act - High Court affirmed the notification dated 15.6.2006 issued under s. 4 (1) r/w s. 17(1) and 17(4) and the notification dated 19.10.2006 issued under s. 6 - Appeal against - Held, proposal was approved by the Board and it got the sanction from the National Capital Region Planning Board and ultimately the Government invoked the power under s. 17(4) r/w s. 5-A of the Act dispensing with the objections - Invocation of s. 17(4) r/w s. 5-A of the Act was well warranted and we see no reason to interfere with the order passed by the Division Bench of the High Court - Appeal dismissed.
