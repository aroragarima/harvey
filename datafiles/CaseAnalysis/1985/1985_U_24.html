P. Savita S/O Shri P.L. Savita v Union Of India, Ministry Of Defence (Department Of Defence P

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 79; (1985) Supp SCC 94; (1985) SCC (L&S) 826; AIR 1985 SC 1124; 1985 (2) CCC 516; 1985 LabIC 1221; 1986 (1) LLJ 79; 1986 (1) LLN 67; 1985 (2) SLJ 331; 1985 (3) SLR 29; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 101

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Equal pay for equal work - Classification between two groups of Senior Draughtsmens (differentiation is not based on any intelligible ground) - Recommendation of higher salary for one of the two groups of Senior Draughtsmans in Ministry of Defence Production - Only on the basis of seniority-cum-fitness - They were doing same work and discharging same functions - Whether Constitutional - Held, grouping is violative of art. 14 - Appeal allowed.
