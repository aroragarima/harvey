State of Orissa and Others v Rajasaheb Chandanmull Indrakumar Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 588; (1973) 3 SCC 739; AIR 1972 SC 2112; 1972 CutLT 649

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Keywords: Colliery

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Determination of locus standi - Assignee of licence - Renewal of mining lease in his favour is alleged to be illegally cancelled - Held, unless legal right is established High Court exercising writ jurisdiction cannot grant him any relief - Matter remitted to HC.
