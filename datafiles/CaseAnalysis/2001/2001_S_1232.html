Grosons Pharmaceuticals Private Limited and v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20830; (2001) 8 SCC 604; AIR 2001 SC 3707; 2001 (44) ARBLR 298; 2001 (4) AWC 3051; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 15; JT 2001 (7) SC 500; 2001 (4) RCR(Civil) 645; 2001(6) SCALE 209; 2001 (3) SCJ 470; 2001 (6) SLT 449; [2001] Supp2 S.C.R. 567; 2001 (6) Supreme 840

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Contract - Blacklisting of contractor - Appellant being approved contractor for supply of drugs to Government departments in U. P.-Irregularities in purchase of medicines-Vigilance enquiry set up-Criminal prosecution launched against appellant under Prevention of Corruption Act read with Section 120, I.P.C.-Appellant asked to show cause why it should not be blacklisted-And sending reply- Appellant blacklisted after considering its reply-Non-supply of materials on which charges in S.C.N. based-Not to render order of blacklisting illegal-Opportunity to show cause given-And that is sufficient-Order of blacklisting giving elaborate reasons.
