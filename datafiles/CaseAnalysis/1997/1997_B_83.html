Bhakta Ramegowda and Others v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2316; (1997) SCC (L&S) 544; (1997) 2 SCC 661; AIR 1997 SC 1038; JT 1997 (2) SC 325; 1997 (1) LLJ 886; 1997(1) SCALE 624; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 535; 1998 (1) SLJ 208; 1997 (2) SLR 381; 1997 (3) Supreme 108

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Karnataka Civil Services (General Recruitment) Rules, 1977, r. 8, Proviso - Reservation of appointment or posts - Validity of guidelines which make proviso to r. 8 unworkable - Held, not illegal on ground that it cannot be retrospective - Proviso to rule is also not non est want of guidelines for filling up backlog vacancies - Appeal partly allowed.
