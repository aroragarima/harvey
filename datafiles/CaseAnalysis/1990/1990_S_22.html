State Bank Of India And Ors. Etc. v S. Vijaya Kumar And Ors. Etc

Supreme Court of India

18 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 327; (1990) 4 SCC 481; (1991) SCC (L&S) 852; AIR 1991 SC 79; 1990 (14) ATC 787; 1990 (2) BankCLR 437; 1990 (2) CLR 551; 1990 (61) FLR 605; JT 1990 (3) SC 308; 1991 LabIC 31; 1991 (2) LLJ 122; 1991 (1) LLN 22; 1990(2) SCALE 110; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 398; 1990 (4) SLR 753; 1990 (2) UJ 467; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 961

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Shall Mean And Include, State Bank Of India (Officers And Assistants) Service Rules, State Bank of India General Regulations 1955 (as amended), State Bank Laws (Amendment) Act, 1973

Summary: State Bank of India Act, 1955, ss. 43 and 50 - State Bank of India General Regulations, 1955, Regn. 55(2)(a) - Constitution Of India, 1950 art. 311 - Bank Officer - Competent authority for dismissal from service - Appointment by Executive Committee - Dismissal by Chief General Manager, an authority lower than Executive Committee - Held, Chief General Manager at relevant time has become under amended Regulations appointing authority in respect of delinquent officers - Date on which order is passed is relevant for determining competency - Is valid.
