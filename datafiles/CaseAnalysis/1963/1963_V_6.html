Valia Peedikakkandi Kutheessa Umma and Others v Pathakkalan Naravanath Kumhamu and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.K. Sarkar, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 370; AIR 1964 SC 275; [1964] 4 S.C.R. 549

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Possession Of Land

Summary: Family & Personal - Muslim law - Hanafi School - Whether gift from husband to minor wife through registered deed is valid when no guardian alive to accept?- Held, there was a complete intention to divest ownership, on part of husband donor to transfer property to donor - If husband had handed over deed to his wife, gift would have been complete under Muhammadan law - It is impossible to hold that by handing over deed to his mother-in-law, in whose charge his wife was, husband did not complete gift - Appeal allowed.
