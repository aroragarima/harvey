Punjab National Bank v Ghulam Dastagir

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 45; (1978) SCC (L&S) 353; (1978) 2 SCC 358; AIR 1978 SC 481; 1978 (36) FLR 198; 1978 LabIC 519; 1978 (1) LLJ 312; 1978 SLJ 214; 1978 UJ 90

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Workman - Driver of the Area manger of the Bank - Held, personal allowance of the Area Manager to enable him to employ a personal driver of his own - Crucial test in most cases is as to who exercises control and supervision over the workman - Direction and control are the telling factors to decide but it does not exclude other factors - Oral and documentary evidence adduced before the Tribunal only shows the Bank facilitates the Area Manager to employ a driver - No nexus between Bank and driver - No record to indicate that the control and direction of the driver vested with the Bank - Therefore, driver is not a workman of the bank.
