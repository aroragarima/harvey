Hindustan Lever Limited v S. M. Jadhav and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 March 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21131; (2001) 4 SCC 52; (2001) SCC (L&S) 639; AIR 2001 SC 1666; 2001 (2) CLR 182; JT 2001 (4) SC 129; 2001(2) SCALE 565; 2001 (2) SLR 9; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1174

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956 - Date of birth - Determination - 1st respondent date of birth showed as on 12-06-1927 in Company Annual reports - Settlement between management and Employees' Federation that age of employees decided on basis of birth certificate - 1st respondent raised no dispute - Retire, as per Company rules, on 01-04-1987 - Filed suit with contention that date of birth 12-6-1927 - Whether date of birth of 1st respondent may be changed? - At fag end of career, a party cannot be allowed to raise a dispute regarding his date of birth - Held, in application filed by 1st respondent date of birth is 12-06-1927 - If he informed, company must have enquired whether record is correct or not - 1st respondent cannot be allowed to raise such a dispute at end of his career - Appeal allowed.
