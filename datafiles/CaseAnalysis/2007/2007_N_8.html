Messrs L and T Komatsu Limited v N. Udayakumar

Supreme Court of India

03 December 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1263; (2008) 1 SCC 224; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1752; 2008(1) ALL MR 491; 2008 (1) LLJ 849; 2007(13) SCALE 648; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 820; 2008 (1) SLR 747

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Charge of unauthorized absence - Termination - Management was directed to reinstate the workman with continuity of service but without back wages - Labour Court awarded punishment of stoppage of four increments with cumulative effect - Appeal against - Held, Labour Court and High Court were not justified in directing the reinstatement by interference with the order of termination - Appeal allowed.
