Kirti Bhusan Singh v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 171; (1986) 3 SCC 675; (1986) SCC (L&S) 703; AIR 1986 SC 2116; 1986 (53) FLR 585; 1986 LabIC 1936; 1987 (1) LLJ 1; 1987 (1) LLN 28; 1986(2) SCALE 26; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 230; 1986 (3) SLR 29; 1986 (2) UJ 687

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Bihar Service Code, 1952

Summary: Service - Bihar Service Code, 1952 - Constitution, art. 309, Proviso and art. 311(2) - Bihar Pension Rules, 1950, r. 116 - Bihar Service Code, 1979, r. 73(f) - Service Rules - Dismissal - Retirement of servant while pendency of disciplinary proceedings, on invalid pension before age of retirement - Order of Govt. dismissing servant after revoking order of retirement - If valid - Whether r. 73(f) of 1979 Code attracted - Not valid - Rule not attracted - Appeal allowed.
