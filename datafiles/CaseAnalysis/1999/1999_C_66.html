Samishta Dube v City Board, Etawah and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 982; (1999) 3 SCC 14; (1999) SCC (L&S) 592; AIR 1999 SC 1056; AIR 1999 SCW 694; 1999 ALJ 688; 1999 (1) AllCriR 707; 1999 (2) AWC 1277; JT 1999 (2) SC 67; 1999 (1) LLJ 1012; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 930; 2000 (1) SLJ 254; 1999 (2) Supreme 389; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 951

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(k), 2(z), 6 P and 6 N - Whether general administration department of Municipal Board is 'industry' and person doing clerical work in such department is 'workman' and whether he can seek reference to Labour Court and whether rule of last come and first go applies to a daily wager - Held, general administration of Municipal Board is 'industry' and person doing clerical work in such dept. is 'workman' and so can seek reference to Labour Court - Order of Labour Court directing Municipal Board to reappoint a daily wager clerk in case any person junior to him has been retained is proper - Rule of last come, first go applies even to a daily - wager.
