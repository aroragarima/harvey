Deputy Commissioner of Agricultural Income-Tax and Sales Tax, Quilon v Dhanalakshmi Vilas Cashew Company

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 61; (1970) 3 SCC 273; 1969 KLT 238; 1969 (24) STC 491; 1969 UJ 186

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1125, ss. 12, 14, 15 and 24(2) - Kerala General Sales Tax Rules, 1950, rr. 33 and 35 - Whether the Deputy Commr. had the jurisdiction to proceed in the matter u/s. 15(1)(i)? - Ratio of decision in State of Kerala v. M. Appukutty followed - Held, Deputy Commr. did not have the jurisdiction - Appeal allowed - Decision of the High Court is set aside.
