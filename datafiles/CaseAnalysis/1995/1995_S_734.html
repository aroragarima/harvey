H.M.T. House Building Co-Op. Society v Syed Khader & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S.P. Bharucha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1559; (1995) 2 SCC 677; AIR 1995 SC 2244; AIR 1995 SCW 1766; JT 1995 (2) SC 543; 1995(2) SCALE 58; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 200

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 3(f)(vi) 4 and 6 - Public purpose - Co-operative Housing Society - Acquisition of land - For public purpose - Condition, prior approval of housing scheme by Govt. - Held, housing scheme means scheme serving for maximum members.
