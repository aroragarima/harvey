Union of India and Others v Priyankan Sharan and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1425; (2008) 9 SCC 15; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 972; JT 2008 (10) SC 41; 2009 (2) MLJ(SC) 86; 2008(12) SCALE 346; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 237

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Education

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - Armed Force Medical College - Bond to serve nation on completion of Training - (A) Whether the cadet's prayer for discharge of bond executed to serve the nation for a period of five years on the ground of medical disability has been rightly accepted by the High Court - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Cadet submitted that proviso of cl. 4(a) has been lost sight of - Relevant clauses 4, 5 and 6 - Construction of - Held, HC's view is a rational one on the facts of each case; these are not the cases where any interference is called for - Held, cl. 4(a) is subject to cl. 6 and the proviso appended to cl. 4 is in the nature of exception; taken note of a maxim 'Ad ea quae frequentius accidunt jura adaptantur' - Appeal Dismissed.
