Dayal Singh v State Of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 251; (2004) 5 SCC 721; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1715; AIR 2004 SC 2608; 2004 CRLJ 2100; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 37; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 609; 2004(4) SCALE 580; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 103

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: [A.] Health & Drug - Criminal - Prevention Of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 23(1), 7/16 - Probation Of Offenders Act, 1958, ss. 11, 11 (1), 11(3), 3, 4, 6 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 20 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, s. 433 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules, 1955 - Notification substituting new standards in place of old under Prevention of Food Adulteration Act - No retrospective operation - Ratio of Rattan Lal v State Of Punjab [1964 Indlaw SC 196] clarified - Also held, strict adherence to Prevention of Food Adulteration Act and Rules framed there under is essential for safeguarding interest of consumers of articles of food - Appeal dismissed.
