Central Bank Of India v S.Satyam & Ors

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 963; (1996) 5 SCC 419; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1273; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 732; AIR 1996 SC 2526; 1996 (3) AWC 1685; 1996 (2) CLR 1095; 1996 (74) FLR 2063; JT 1996 (7) SC 181; 1996 LabIC 2248; 1996 (2) LLJ 820; 1996(5) SCALE 567B; 1996 (3) SLJ 1; 1996 (4) SLR 695; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 214; 1996 (6) Supreme 61; 1996 (2) UJ 506; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 329

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 25H, 25F - Constitution, art. 226 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1957, rr. 77 and 78 - Retrenched workman seeking re-employment - Delay and laches - (A) If application of s. 25H of 1947 Act, can be restricted to one category of retrenched workmen - (B) Writ petition filed after several years - Effect of lapse - Held, chap. VA of Act enacted for all cases of retrenchment thus application of s. 25H cannot be restricted only to one category - Sufficient to decline relief - Appeal allowed.
