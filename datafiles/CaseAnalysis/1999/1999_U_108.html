R. P. Kapur v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 688; (1999) 8 SCC 110; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1440; AIR 1999 SC 3589; 1999 (2) CLR 627; 1999 (3) ESC 2158; 1999 (83) FLR 142; JT 1999 (5) SC 400; 1999 (3) LLN 844; 1999(4) SCALE 507; 2000 (1) SLJ 307; 1999 (4) SLR 501; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 121

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Railway Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 1986

Summary: Railway Service (Pension) Rules, 1993, rr. 49 and 50, Note 1 Proviso - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Pension - Average emoluments drawn - Determination of - Employee is reinstated without forfeiture of service - Held, emoluments that he would have drawn except any increase which is only notionally fixed without right for payment, shall be considered - Suspension allowance is an emolument - Suspension period cannot therefore be disregarded for calculating average emoluments u/r. 50 on ground that suspended employee does not draw any emoluments.
