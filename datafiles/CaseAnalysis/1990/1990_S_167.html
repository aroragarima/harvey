Vijay Kumar Sharma & Ors. Etc. v State Of Karnataka & Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, K. Ramaswamy, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 732; (1990) 2 SCC 562; AIR 1990 SC 2072; JT 1990 (2) SC 448; 1990(1) SCALE 342; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 614

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, Bihar Finance Act, 1981, Sugarcane Control Order, Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976, Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1953, Presidency Small Causes Courts Act, 1882, Railway Act 1890, Goa, Daman and Diu (Administration) Act, 1962, Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950, Travancore-Cochin Motor Vehicles Act 1950, Railways (Amendment) Act, 1961, Essential Supplies (Temporary Power) Act, 1949, Prevention of Adulteration of Food, Drugs and Cosmetics (West Bengal) (Amendment) Act, 1973

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 254 - Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976, ss. 14 and 20 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 74 and 80 - (A) Nationalisation of Contract Carriages - Scheme for, under Karnataka Act - Not repugnant to provisions for grant of permits under Motor Vehicles Act - Both Acts occupy different fields - Whether ss.14 and 20 of Karnataka Act are repealed - (B) State and Central legislations - Repugnancy - Determination - Legislations under different entries of Concurrent List - Held, (A) not impliedly repealed by Motor Vehicles Act - (B) Doctrine of pith and substance can be applied - Order accordingly.
