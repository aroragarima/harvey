Â Khazan Singh and another v State Of U.P. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A. Alagiriswami, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 155; (1974) 1 SCC 295; AIR 1974 SC 669; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 562; 1974 UJ 85

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Nationalisation, Delhi Road Transport Authority Act, 1950

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 68D, 68D(3) Proviso - Constitution, arts. 245(1) and 298 - Carriers and Transportation - (A) State Govt.'s power to approve schemes for inter-State route - (B) State Govt. approving scheme for inter-State route - (1) Applicability of restrictions u/art. 245(1) - (2) Power to approve inter-State schemes u/s. 68-D of 1939 Act - Held, State Govt. can approve schemes but it should before publishing the scheme obtain the prior approval of the Central Govt. - Held, restrictions u/art. 245(1) not applicable - Such a scheme does not entail encroachment by one State Govt. upon the executive sphere of another State Govt. - Appeal Dismissed.
