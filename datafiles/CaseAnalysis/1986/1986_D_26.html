Dr. Kishore Chand Kapoor and Others v Dharam Pal Kapoor and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 October 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 468; (1986) 4 SCC 505; AIR 1987 SC 66; 1986 (30) DLT 514; JT 1986 SC 788; 1986(2) SCALE 684; 1987 (1) UJ 284

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Land and Property - Partition Act, 1893, s. 2 and 3 - Partition - Mode of determination of market value of building - (A) Validity of valuation of land and building separately by considering size, situation, and utility - When method of capitalization of rent actually received or which might reasonably be received - Is not the only method - (B) Whether putting the impuned property to auction sale to determine its market value by highest bid is proper mode - Held, not improper - Not proper mode - Appeal disposed of.
