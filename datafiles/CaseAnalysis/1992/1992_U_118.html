Shakambari and Company v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Yogeshwar Dayal, S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1277; (1993) Supp1 SCC 487; AIR 1992 SC 2090; AIR 1992 SCW 2462; 1992 (2) ARBLR 501; 1992(1) SCALE 883

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act - Delay in filing Special Leave Petition by 214 days - (A) Whether delay is to be condoned ? - Subsequent delay is because he had to gather necessary particulars regarding calculation of interest get back certified copy which had gone for execution purposes and intervening vacation of this court - (B) Award of interest - Held, thus, delay in filing of Special Leave Petition should be condoned - Question regarding awardability of interest by Arbitrator had been referred to a larger Bench by an order of court - Appeal allowed.
