Commissioner of Income Tax, Guwahati v Ashok Paper Mills

Supreme Court of India

09 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1505; [2008] 167 TAXMAN 220

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 143 and 143(1 A) - Effect of the amendment of s. 143(1A) of the Income Tax Act, 1961 with retrospective effect - Impact of J.K. Synthetics Ltd. case [2001 Indlaw SC 250] requires to be considered - Matter remitted to High Court for fresh consideration - Appeal disposed of
