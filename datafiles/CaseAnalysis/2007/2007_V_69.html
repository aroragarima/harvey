M. Naga Venkata Lakshmi v Viskhapatnam Municipal Corp. And Anr

Supreme Court of India

18 September 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, H.S. Bedi

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 925; (2007) 8 SCC 748; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1633; 2007 (69) ALR 312; 2007 (6) ALT 1; 2008 (1) AWC 255; JT 2007 (11) SC 542; 2007(11) SCALE 273; 2007 (7) SCJ 290; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 12; 2008 (1) UPLBEC 525

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) Act, 1975

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Urban Areas (Development) Act, 1975 - Application for sanction of a building plan before the Visakkhapatnam Municipal Corporation - Application dismissed on the premise that the proposed constructions fell on the reserved open space in the Radha Krishna Nagar Layout - Appeal against - Held, on what basis the layout plan had been drawn resulting in deprivation of a valuable right of the appellant, therefore, was required to be determined - If VUDA wanted to deprive the appellant from a valuable right of property, the question which should have been posed was as to whether therefore the authorities should have acquired the property or not - There is no provision in terms whereof the appellant could be deprived of her right of property without payment of any compensation - Matter remitted to Single Judge - Appeal disposed of.
