S. S. Karmalkar And Others v Ibrahim Husseni Tamboli And Others

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 268; (1989) SCC (L&S) 157; (1989) 1 SCC 219; AIR 1989 SC 430; 1989 (9) ATC 270; 1989 (1) LLN 829; 1988(2) SCALE 1516; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 712; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 712

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309, Proviso 311 - Promotion of employees - Challenged - Ground - Food and Civil Supplies Department, Sholapur was abolished on abolition of statutory rationing and it was part and parcel of Revenue Department - Held, cannot be challenged - Food and Civil Supplies Department and Revenue Department, Sholapur are two separate Govt. Departments - Even after abolition of statutory rationing - Employees will be governed by rules of Food and Civil Supplies Department - Appeal allowed.
