Industrial Supplies Pvt. Ltd. & Anr v Union Of India & Ors.(And Vice Versa)

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 295; (1980) 4 SCC 341; AIR 1980 SC 1858; 1980 (41) FLR 248; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 375; 1980 UJ 868

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Safety

Summary: Coking Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Act, 1972, ss. 3(n), 4(1), 22(3) and 22 - Mines Act , 1952, s. 2(1) - (A) Meaning of word 'Owner' -Whether 'Raising contractor' is covered under it , for s.4(1) of 1972 Act - (B) ' Amount due to coking coal mine'- Safety operations carried out by raising contractor prior to nationalisation -Utilization of subsidy u/s. 22 (3) - - Held it includes 'Raising contractor' and he being an occupier, cannot escape consequences of vesting u/s.4 (1) - Could be utilized by Central Government to meet liabilities of coking coal mines - Appeal Dismissed.
