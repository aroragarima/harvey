Shahazada Bi & Ors. v Halimabi (Since Dead) By Her Lrs.

Supreme Court of India

30 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 515; (2004) 7 SCC 354; AIR 2004 SC 3942; 2004 (3) AWC 2598; 2004 (2) CGLJ 319; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 233; JT 2004 (6) SC 172; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 673; 2004(6) SCALE 373; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 222

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 22 r. 11, O. 22 r. 4, O. 22 r. 4 (3), s. 100 - Declaration of title - Omission to implead LR of a defendant - Whether HC right in coming to conclusion that suit against defendant no 4 alone abated and that entire suit did not abate? - Held, rule does not provide that by omission to implead LR of a defendant, suit will abate as a whole - Trial Court and HC right in coming to conclusion that suit against defendant no 4 alone abated.
