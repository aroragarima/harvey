State of Gujarat v Chinubhai Gopaldas

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Grover, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 339; AIR 1968 SC 1275; 1968 CRLJ 1478; 1968 MLJ 733; [1968] 3 S.C.R. 447

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Examination - Confiscated - Sustainability - Appeal was filed against judgment and order of HC by which order confiscating 1500 and odd bottles said to contain intoxicating liquor by City Magistrate had been set aside - Hence, instant appeal - Whether order passed by HC was justified - Held, all bottles were of same kind as ones which were sent for chemical examination - However examination of random samples could be made and if they satisfied court that bottles contained contraband articles bottles could be confiscated - Order of HC was set aside - Appeal allowed.
