Thermax Private Limited v Collector Of Customs (Bombay) New Coustoms House

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 765; (1992) 4 SCC 440; AIR 1993 SC 1339; AIR 1993 SCW 509; 1992 (42) ECR 422; 1992 (61) E.L.T. 352; JT 1992 (5) SC 281; 1992(2) SCALE 212; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 943

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 8 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3(1) - Whether imported goods are exempted from levy of countervailing duty ? - Held, in view of Notifications 63/85,D/-17-3-1985 and 93/76 issued u/r. 8 of Central Excise Rules importer was entitled to concession in respect of additional duty - Appeal dismissed.
