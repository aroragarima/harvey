Munna Tuin v District Magistrate, Lucknow and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 9; (1982) 3 SCC 320; (1983) SCC (Cr) 29; AIR 1982 SC 878; 1982 (2) Bom.C.R. 316; 1982 CRLJ 630

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Detenu was supplied grounds of detention at time of his arrest - Inquiry report was not supplied to him along with grounds - Counter affidavit was filed by an incompetent person - Held, District Magistrate was directed to file counter affidavit which alone could be treated as a proper return - Despite this direction, no counter-affidavit has been filed by District Magistrate - No proper explanation or return to rebut allegations made by detenu - Petitioner directed to be released - Petition allowed.
