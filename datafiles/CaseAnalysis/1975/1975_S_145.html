Sushil Kumar Sen v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 158; (1975) 1 SCC 774; AIR 1975 SC 1185; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 942

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 47, rr. 1 and 4 - Award in land acquisition reference - Review by State - Effect of allowing review application - When compensation was reduced - Appeal filed by State - And cross appeal by claimant against maintainability of review application - But no appeal by State against original order on reference - High Court holding review not maintainable - Effect - Held, proper order to be passed - Appeal Allowed.
