Nrisingha Murari Chakraborty and Others v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 280; (1977) 3 SCC 7; (1977) SCC (Cr) 417; 1977 (14) ACC 309; AIR 1977 SC 1174; 1977 CRLJ 961; 1977 CrLR(SC) 349; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 521

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Foreigners

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, ss. 420 and 415 - Indian Passport Act, 1920 - Passports Act, 1967, s. 2(b) - Whether 'Passports' is property within the meaning of ss. 415 and 420 of the Code - Held, looking to the importance and characteristics of a passport it can be said that it is a 'property' - Appeal dismissed.
