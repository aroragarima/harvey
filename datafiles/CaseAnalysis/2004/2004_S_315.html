M/S Anand Buttons Ltd. v State Of Haryana & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1046; (2005) 9 SCC 164; AIR 2005 SC 565; 2005 (2) ALD(SC) 106; 2005 (58) ALR 333; 2005 (1) AWC 146; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 276; JT 2004 (10) SC 397; 2005 (1) RCR(Civil) 224; 2005 (98) RD 351; 2004(10) SCALE 354; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 446

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 5A, 6 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - National Capital Region Planning Board Act, 1985 - Punjab Scheduled Roads and Controlled Areas (Restriction of Unregulated Development) Act, 1963 - Acquisition proceedings - Whether decision of State Govt, not to grant exemption from acquisition to their lands, was arbitrary, discriminatory and violative of art.14 of Constitution? - Held, if after considering all circumstances, State Govt. has taken view that exemption of lands of appellants would render askew development scheme of industrial estate, it is not possible for HC or this Court to interfere with satisfaction of concerned authorities - No fault found with judgment rendered by DB.
