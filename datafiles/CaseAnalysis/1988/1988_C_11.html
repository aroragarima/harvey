Jagan Nath (Deceased) Through L.Rs. v Chander Bhan And Others

Supreme Court of India

11 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 676; (1988) 3 SCC 57; AIR 1988 SC 1362; JT 1988 (2) SC 441; 1988 (2) RCJ 123; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 629; 1988 (2) RentLR 169; 1988(1) SCALE 1079; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 325; 1988 (2) UJ 267

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(b) - Whether the mischief contemplated u/s. 14(1)(b) of the Act has been committed as the tenant had sublet, assigned, or otherwise parted with the possession of the whole or part of the premises without obtaining the consent in writing of the landlord - Tenant retaining right of possession - Held, provisions of s. 14(1)(b), not attracted - Appeal allowed.
