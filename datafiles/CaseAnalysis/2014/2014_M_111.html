M. Faseela v Munnerul Islam Madrasa Committee and another

Supreme Court of India

31 March 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, Kurian Joseph

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 268; AIR 2014 SC 2064; 2014 (4) ALD(SC) 81; 2014(5) ALL MR 971; 2014 (104) ALR 758; 2014 (3) AWC 2920; JT 2014 (7) SC 247; 2014 (2) RCR(Civil) 890; 2014 (1) RCR(Rent) 404; 2014 (124) RD 256; 2014 (4) RLW 3167; 2014(5) SCALE 531; [2014] 4 S.C.R. 613

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Waqf Act, 1995-Sections 6 and 7 (as amended by Act 27 of 2013)-Suit for eviction against tenant(s) of waqf property-Question for determination-Whether suit for eviction by landlord against tenant relating to waqf property is triable by civil court or suit lies within exclusive jurisdiction of Waqf Tribunal?-Held, suit for eviction against tenant relating to waqf property is exclusively triable by civil court as such suit not covered by disputes specified in Sections 6 and 7-Impugned order of High Court not sustainable and set aside-Civil court to proceed with suit accordingly.
