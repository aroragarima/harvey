Jacob M. Puthuparambil And Ors. Etc. Etc. v Kerala Water Authority And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 458; (1991) SCC (L&S) 25; (1991) 1 SCC 28; AIR 1990 SC 2228; 1991 (15) ATC 697; 1990 (61) FLR 696; JT 1990 (4) SC 27; 1990 (2) KLT 673; 1991 (2) LLJ 65; 1990 (2) LLN 1032; 1990(2) SCALE 588; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 28; 1990 (6) SLR 54; [1990] supp1 S.C.R. 562

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, Kerala Water Supply And Sewerage Act, 1986, Kerala Water and Waste Water Ordinance 1984, Kerala State Subordinate Service Rules 1958, Kerala Public Service Commission (Additional Functions as respects certain Corporations and Companies) Act, 1970

Summary: Service - Kerala Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1986, ss. 8, 8(1), 19(1), 68 and 69 - Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, rr. 9(9)(i), 9(a)(i) and 9(e) - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 41 - Kerala Water Authority - Provisions of r. 9 of 1958, Rules - (A) Regularisation in service of its employees in various categories - (B) Interpretation of statutes - Service Rules framed u/art. 309 of Constitution - Statutory force - Corporations instrumentalities etc u/art. 12 adopting rules by passing resolution - (C) Temporary employee - Continued in service for many years - Actually appointment under stop-gap arrangement - Regularisation - Held, certain directions issued - Rules do not continue to have statutory force - Employee entitled that his service should be regularised - Appeal allowed.
