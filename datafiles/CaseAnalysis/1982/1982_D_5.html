Jyoti Basu & Others. v Debi Ghosal & Others.

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 110; (1982) 1 SCC 691; AIR 1982 SC 983; 1982(1) SCALE 115; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 318

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: Election Dispute, House Of The People, Any Other Candidate

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 82, 86(4), 87(1) and 99 - Who may join an election petition as a respondent - Person mentioned in ss. 82 and 86 (4) is the only person who can be joined - And who is not a candidate may not be joined - Appeal Allowed.
