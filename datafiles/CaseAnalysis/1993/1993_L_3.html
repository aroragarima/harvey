State Of Uttar Pradesh And Another v Labh Chand

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1278; (1993) 2 SCC 495; (1993) SCC (L&S) 616; AIR 1994 SC 754; 1993 (24) ATC 267; 1993 (2) AWC 1139; 1993 (1) CLR 723; [1993] 76 Comp Cas 846; 1993 (115) CTR 333; 1993 (66) FLR 887; [1993] 200 ITR 647; JT 1993 (2) SC 298; 1994 LabIC 746; 1993 (2) LLJ 724; 1993 (2) MLJ(SC) 51; 1993(1) SCALE 470; [1993] 1 S.C.R. 878; 1993 (4) SLR 391; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 1219

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunals) Act, 1976, s. 4, 6 and 7 - Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunals) Rules, 1976, r. 1 - Writ petition dismissed by Division Bench of High Court - Ground, not availing of alternative remedy - Maintainability of second writ petition on same grounds before single Judge of same HC - Held, not maintainable - Appeal allowed.
