New Kenilworth Hotel Private Limited v Orissa State Finance Corporation And Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2280; (1997) 3 SCC 462; AIR 1997 SC 978; 1997 (1) BC 529; 1997 (2) CLT 250; JT 1997 (1) SC 712; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 52; 1997(1) SCALE 472; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 395; 1997 (4) Supreme 355

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 104(2) - Letters Patent (Ori), cl. 10 - Letters Patent Appeal filed against appellate order of single Judge vacating order of status quo in suit for injunction - Maintainability - Held, is expressly barred in view of s. 104(2) of Code - Appeal dismissed.
