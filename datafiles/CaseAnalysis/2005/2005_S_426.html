State of Haryana and others v Raj Rani

Supreme Court of India

29 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, G.P. Mathur, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 507; (2005) 7 SCC 22; 2005 (3) ACC 811; AIR 2005 SC 3279; 2005 (61) ALR 463; 2005 (6) Bom.C.R. 927; 2005 (4) CPJ(Sc) 28; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 227; JT 2005 (8) SC 56; 2005 (4) MLJ(SC) 131; 2005 (141) PLR 795; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 169A; 2005 (4) RLW 2511; 2005(7) SCALE 1; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1027

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Health & Drug; Tort

Summary: [A.] Consumer Protection - Tort - Compensation - Medical negligence - After sterilization operation woman became pregnant and delivered a child - Held, in absence of proof of negligence, surgeon cannot be held liable to pay compensation - Appeal allowed.
