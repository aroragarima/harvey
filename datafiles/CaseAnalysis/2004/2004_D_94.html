Executive Engineer ZP Engineering Division and Another v Digambara Rao etc. etc.

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 823; (2004) 8 SCC 262; (2004) SCC (L&S) 1097; AIR 2004 SC 4839; 2004 (4) AWC 3218; 2004 (3) CLR 972; 2004 (103) FLR 460; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 118; JT 2004 (8) SC 436; 2004(8) SCALE 313B; 2004 (6) SLR 168; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 716

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Service - Regularisation of daily wagers - Respondents originally employed on daily wages in relation to a Scheme - Termination - Directed to be reinstated with 50% of back-wages by Labour Court - Whether respondents entitled to regularization? - Held, completion of 240 days of continuous service in a year may not by itself be a ground for directing an order or regularization - It is also not case of Respondents that they were appointed in accordance with extant rules - No direction for regularization of services could be issued - Labour Court and HC committed a manifest error - Appeals allowed.
