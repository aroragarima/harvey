Sh. Bileshwar Khand Udyog Khedutsahakari Mandali Ltd v State Of Gujarat And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 872; (1992) 2 SCC 42; AIR 1992 SC 872; JT 1992 (1) SC 597; 1992(1) SCALE 194; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 391

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Indirect Tax - Constitution - Socio-Economic - Bombay Prohibition Act, 1949, s. 58 A - Constitution, sch. 7, List 3, Entry 33 - Cost of maintenance of Excise/Police Staff for supervision of manufacture, storage etc. of industrial alcohol - Power of State Govt. to demand cost from manufacturer etc. - Whether s. 58 A is invalid ? - Held, power of State Govt., traceable to entry 33 of concurrent list - S. 58 A is, therefore, not invalid - Appeal dismissed.
