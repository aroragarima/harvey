Supreme Court of India

24 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 417; (1988) 2 SCC 338; AIR 1988 SC 1007; 1988 (2) ARBLR 270; 1988 BBCJ 54; 1988 (1) CCC 1032; 1988 (34) DLT 362; JT 1988 (2) SC 6; 1988(1) SCALE 612; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 351; 1988 (1) UJ 692

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Limitation Act, 1908 - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss.20 and 8 - Determination of period of limitation for application for reference of dispute arising out of contract for building houses - Held, art. 137 applies - Period of limitation has to be computed from date claim is asserted and payment is denied - The High Court is directed to make an order u/s. 20 of the Act and give consequential directions in respect of the same - Appeal allowed.
