State of Orissa and Others v Klockner and Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2808; (1996) 8 SCC 377; AIR 1996 SC 2140; 1996 (1) ARBLR 591; 1996 (2) CCC 108; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 644; JT 1996 (4) SC 254; 1996(3) SCALE 527; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 368

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Foreign Awards (Recognition and Enforcement) Act, 1961, s. 3 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 7, rr. 11(a) and (d) - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 32 - (A) Foreign Awards - Party invoking arbitration agreement, except filing application for rejection of plaint in suit filed by opposite party not taking any step, in legal proceedings - Invoking of s. 3 - Validity - (B) Validity of rejection of plaint when applicant specifically not pleading that there is no cause of action - Held, application cannot be construed as any step in legal proceedings - It is not bar in invoking of s. 3 of 1961 Act - Rejection is not proper - Appeal dismissed.
