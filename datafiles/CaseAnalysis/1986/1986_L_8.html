Bansi Lal And Ors. v Laxman Singh

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 168; (1986) SCC (Cr) 342; (1986) 3 SCC 444; AIR 1986 SC 1721; 1986 (3) Crimes 159; 1986 CRLJ 1603; 1986 CrLR(SC) 354; 1986 (30) DLT 340; JT 1986 SC 49; 1986(2) SCALE 2; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 191

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 397, 401- IPC, 1860, s. 34 - Revision - Exercise of High Court' s power to set aside order of acquittal - Contention that High Court has transgressed the bounds of revisional jurisdiction in reappreciating the evidence and setting aside acquittal - Held, power should be sparingly exercised - Appeal allowed.
