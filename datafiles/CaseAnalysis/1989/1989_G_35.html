S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 13 and 30 - Arbitration award - (A) Setting aside of - Reasons for making award not given by arbitrator - Interference sought - (B) Powers of arbitrator - Interpretation of contract is a matter for arbitrator - Amounts awarded by Arbitrator - By taking particular view of contract - Whether court can substitute its decision - Held, (A) Court cannot interfere with award - (B) Cannot - Appeal allowed.
