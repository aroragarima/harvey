Styabrata Biswas v Kalyan Kumar Kisku

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1923; (1994) 2 SCC 266; AIR 1994 SC 1837; 1994 (2) CalLT(SC) 1; JT 1994 (1) SC 325; 1994(1) SCALE 286; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 413

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s.151, o.39, r.1; Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, ss.2 and 10 - Property - Status quo ordered - Creation of sub-lease - Contempt jurisdiction - Scope - Held, illegal, even against the person not party to the interlocutory application for status quo - Outside the scope of contempt jurisdiction.
