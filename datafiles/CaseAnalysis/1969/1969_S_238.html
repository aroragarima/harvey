Supreme Court of India

05 November 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, C.A. Vaidyialingam, A.N. Grover, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 445; (1970) SCC (Cr) 92; (1970) 1 SCC 219; AIR 1970 SC 675; 1970 CRLJ 743; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 225

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Preventive Detention Act, 1950, ss. 7, 10 - Constitution, art. 22(5) - Detention - Representation of detenu - Procedure as to - Held, appropriate Govt. must itself expeditiously exercise judgment thereon before forwarding it to Advisory Board - Petition Allowed.
