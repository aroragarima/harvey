Union Of India And Anr. v Cynamide India Ltd. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28421; (1987) 2 SCC 720; AIR 1987 SC 1802; 1987 (2) CompLJ 10; 1987 (12) ECR 199; JT 1987 (2) SC 107; 1987(1) SCALE 728; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 841; 1987 (2) UJ 198

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Sale Price, Bulk Drug, Quantity, Code of Civil Procedure 1908, Formulations, Mustard Oil (Price Control) Order, 1977, Sugarcane Control Order, 1966, Drugs (Price Control) Order, 1979

Summary: Health and Drug - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s.3 - Drugs (Price Control) Order, 1979, paras.3, 12, 13 and 27 - - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32, 226, 246 - Price fixation - Alleged exploitation by big manufacturers of bulk drug; high prices - (A) Scope of interference by Court and applicability of rules of natural justice to subordinate legislation - Held, (A) Price-fixation is neither the function nor the forte of the Court however court has jurisdiction to enquire into the question, whether relevant considerations have gone in and irrelevant considerations kept out of the determination of the price - Legislative action, plenary or subordinate, is not subject to rules of natural justice - (B)Whether while fixation of price of formulation, it is required to await result to review application in regard to price of bulk drug - Held, need not await - Appeal allowed.
