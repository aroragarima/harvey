Amrit Bhikaji Kale and Others v Kashinath Janardhan Trade and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 May 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 163; (1983) 3 SCC 437; AIR 1983 SC 643; 1983 MahLJ 711; 1983(1) SCALE 632; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 237; 1983 UJ 473

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, ss. 32F, 32G, 14 and 29 - Land and Property - Tillers' day - Deemed purchase by tenant - Subsequently landlord died but after bequeathing impuned land on certain minor - Sale is not postponed - Validity of subsequent proceedings - Held, void - Supreme Court directed the amount deposited in Court by appellants to be paid to respondent who came in rags and urged that he was too poor to engage a counsel - Appeal dismissed.
