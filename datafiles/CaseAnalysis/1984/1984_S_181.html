State of West Bengal and Others Etc v Sampat Lal and Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 300; (1985) SCC (Cr) 62; (1985) 1 SCC 317; AIR 1985 SC 195; 1995 AllCriC 206; 1985 CRLJ 516; 1984(2) SCALE 942; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 256

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Intervene

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 173 - Constitution, arts. 226, 134 - Delhi Special Police Establishment Act, 1946, s. 5 and 6 - (A) Investigation - Mysterious deaths - High Court directing State Govt. to appoint Special Officer to inquire matter when police investigation was already pending - Without hearing of State Govt. or its officers - Validity of Order - (B) Scope of ss. 5 and 6 of 1946 Act - Court directing CBI to investigate - Need of santion u/s. 6 - Order held, liable to be set aside - Sanction u/s. 6 not necessary - Appeal allowed.
