Messrs P. K. Ramaiah and Company v Chairman and Managing Director, National Thermal Power Corporation

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1710; (1994) Supp3 SCC 126; 1994(1) SCALE 1

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Criminal

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Can there be an arbitrable dispute for reference to arbitration once full and final settlement has been reached and acknowledged ? - Held, that appellant having acknowledged settlement and also accepted measurements and having received amount in full and final settlement of claim, there is accord and satisfaction - There is no existing arbitrable dispute for reference to arbitration - Appeal dismissed.
