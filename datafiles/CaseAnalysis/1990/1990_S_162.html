Som Raj And Ors. Etc. v State Of Haryana And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 974; (1990) SCC (L&S) 380; (1990) 2 SCC 653; AIR 1990 SC 1176; 1990 (13) ATC 702; 1990 (60) FLR 494; JT 1990 (1) SC 286; 1990 LabIC 942; 1990 (2) LLJ 1; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 535; 1990 (2) SLR 32; 1990 (2) UJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Punjab Subordinate Agricultural Service rules 1933

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 and 309 - Punjab Subordinate Agricultural Service Rules, 1933, rr. 7 and 10 - Service rules - Combined provisions - Common seniority - Upgradation of offices by State Govt. - Offices of directorates upgraded as A class and subordinate offices as B class - Validity of classification - Held, employees of subordinate offices cannot be treated at par with directorate employees or as one unit - Classification not arbitrary violating arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution - Appeal dismissed.
