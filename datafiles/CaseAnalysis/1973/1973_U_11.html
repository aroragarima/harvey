Supreme Court of India

11 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray, Hans Raj Khanna, A. Alagiriswami, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 516; (1974) SCC (Tax) 183; (1974) 1 SCC 459; AIR 1974 SC 1510; 1974 (3) CTR 18; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 619; 1974 (33) STC 254; 1974 TAXLR 1999

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Keywords: Sales Tax Authorities

Summary: Constitution, art. 286(1)(b) - CST Act, 1956, s. 5(2) - What constitute sale in the course of import - Held, even if the contracts envisaged the import of goods and their supply to the DGS & D from out of the goods imported, it did not follow that the movement of the goods in the course of import was occasioned by the contracts of sale by the petitioner with DGS & D - Petition Allowed.
