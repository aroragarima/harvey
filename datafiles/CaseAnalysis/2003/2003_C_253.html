Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: HC

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 256(2) - HC - Held, no reason to interfere with conclusion arrived by HC on question as framed u/s. 256(2) of the Act. Appeals dismissed.
