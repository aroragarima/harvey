Tulsiram Sanganaria & Anr. v Shrimati Anni Rai & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 199; (1971) 1 SCC 284; AIR 1971 SC 671; [1971] 79 ITR 502; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 310; 1971 UJ 159

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 54(1) - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 65(e) and 74 - Assessment order - Confidential document u/s. 54 - Whether admissible in evidence - Held, would be admissible in evidence if produced by an assesses or his representative-in-interest - Appeal Dismissed.
