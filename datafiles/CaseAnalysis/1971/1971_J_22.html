Commissioner of Income Tax, Andhra Pradesh v Jayalakshmi Rice And Oil Mills Contractor Co

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 109; (1971) 1 SCC 280; AIR 1971 SC 1015; [1971] 79 ITR 549; 1971 (2) MLJ(SC) 86; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 365; 1971 UJ 185

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Application For Registration, Indian Partnership Act, 1932

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26A - Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 58 and 59 - Firm when registered - For purposes of r. 2(b), a firm will not be treated as registered before the end of the previous year - An application for its registration u/s. 58 of 1932 Act was made before end of previous year - Held, it was actually registered in accordance with s.59 of that Act only after the end of such year - Appeal Allowed.
