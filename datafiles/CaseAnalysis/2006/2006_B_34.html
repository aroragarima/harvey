State of Punjab and Another v Balkaran Singh

Supreme Court of India

18 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Balasubramanyan, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 695; (2006) 12 SCC 709; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 645; AIR 2007 SC 641; JT 2006 (9) SC 178; 2007 (1) LLN 51; 2006(10) SCALE 288; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 298; 2007 (1) UC 268

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 1, r. 8, s. 80 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 58 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 32 - Punjab Agricultural Service (Class-I) Rules, 1974, r. 11 - Punjab Horticultural Service (Class-I) Rules, 1990 - Claiming pay at enhanced scale - Held, HC made no attempt to see for itself whether on basis of rules and arguments put forward on behalf of State, respective plaintiffs could be fitted in scale of pay of Rs. 1200 -1850/- And in third suit where earlier decree became final, whether reliefs claimed could be granted merely on ground that there was an earlier decree in favour of plaintiff therein granting him a higher scale of pay - Jdgments and decrees granted by courts below are set aside - Appeals allowed.
