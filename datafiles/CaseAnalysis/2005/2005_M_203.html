N.V. Srinivasa Murthy and Others v Mariyamma (Dead) By Proposed Lrs and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 402; (2005) 5 SCC 548; AIR 2005 SC 2897; 2005 (5) ALD(SC) 113; 2005 (60) ALR 748; 2005 (3) AWC 2541; 2005 (2) CGLJ 198; JT 2005 (6) SC 1; 2005 (3) RCR(Civil) 414; 2005 (99) RD 395; 2005(5) SCALE 343; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 411

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - CPC,1908, o. 2, r. 2, o. 7, r. 11, o. 7, r. 11(d) - Specific Relief Act - Whether trial court and HC were right in holding that plaint u/o. 7, r. 11 was liable to be rejected? - Held, cause of action for seeking a declaration and for obtaining re-conveyance deed arose on 25-03-1987 when plaintiffs claimed to have paid back entire loan amount and obtained a promise from defendants to reconvey property - Reckoning cause of action from 25-03-1987, suit filed on 26-08-1996, was barred by time - HC does not seem to be right in rejecting plaint on ground that it does not disclose any 'cause of action' - Trial court was right in coming to conclusion that accepting all averments in plaint, suit seems to be barred by limitation - Appeal dismissed.
