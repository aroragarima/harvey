Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Indirect Tax

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2-A and 10 - Dispute - Between employer and employee - Jurisdiction of civil Court - Held, forums under Act - Have jurisdiction in matter of recognition, observance or enforcement of any of rights or obligations created by Act - Dispute arises from general law of contract, Civil court.
