Commissioner of Income Tax, T.N. -V, Madras v Kotagiri Industrial Cooperative Tea Factory

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2542; (1997) 9 SCC 537; AIR 1997 SC 1865; 1997 (4) ALD(SC) 2A; 1997 (139) CTR 359; [1997] 224 ITR 604; JT 1997 (4) SC 94; 1997(2) SCALE 728; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 738; 1997 (4) Supreme 44; [1997] 91 TAXMAN 214; 1997 (2) TLR 559

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 72, 80 P(2) and 80 B(5) - Can deduction u/s. 80 P be allowed before set - off of unabsorbed losses of earlier year? - Held, officer had rightly set off carried forward losses of earlier years in accordance with s. 72 of 1961 Act - On finding that losses exceeded income he rightly did not allow any deduction u/s. 80 P(2) - Thus, in terms of expression 'gross total income' in s. 80 B(5) for purpose of Chap. VIA, there is no scope for construing said expression differently in s. 80 P - Appeal allowed.
