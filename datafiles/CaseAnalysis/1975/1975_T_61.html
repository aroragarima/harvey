State Of Madhya Pradesh & Ors. v Tikam Das

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 174; (1975) 2 SCC 100; AIR 1975 SC 1429; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 234; 1975 TAXLR 1751; 1975 UJ 410

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: M.P. Excise Act, 1915, s. 62 - M.P. Excise Rules 1960, rr. XXV and XXVa and XXVI - M.P. Foreign Liquor Rules 1960, r. IV - Constitution, art. 245 - (A) Amendment of r. IV - Effect is ante-dated - Determination of vires - (B) Enhancement of scale of fees - Balance of stock of foreign liquor on 31-03-1964 out of stock obtained under licence which was valid upto 31-3-1964 - Revision of scales from 01-04-1964 - Liability as to enhanced fee - Amendment held, is not obnoxious to the scheme nor ultra vires s. 62 - Licensee is liable to pay enhanced fee - Appeal Dismissed.
