France B. Martins and Another v Mafalda Maria Teresa Rodrigues

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 668; (1999) 6 SCC 627; AIR 1999 SC 3243; 1999 (5) ALD(SC) 105; 1999 (37) ALR 324; 1999 (4) AWC 3354; [2001] 104 Comp Cas 14; 1999 (4) CompLJ 32; 1999 CPJ 41; JT 1999 (6) SC 238; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 55; 2000 (124) PLR 398; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 685

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 24A - Applicability of provisions of Limitation Act 1963 to proceedings - In absence of any limitation period being prescribed before insertion of s. 24A in 1993 - Held, provisions of Limitation Act 1963, cannot be applied - Appeal dismissed.
