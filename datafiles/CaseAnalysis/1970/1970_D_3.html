Girdhari Lal Gupta and Another v D. N. Mehta, Assistant Collector of Customs and

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 589; (1970) SCC (Cr) 496; (1970) 2 SCC 530; AIR 1971 SC 28; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 748

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1947, s. 23 C(1) - Appreciation of evidence - Offence of attempting to export Indian Currency by firm of partners - Held, slips, being part of things discovered during search by investigation officer - Slips as evidence - No general rule can be laid down that his evidence cannot be relied upon unless it is corroborated - (B) Liability of acting partner for offence - Unless proves, absence of knowledge of contravention or exercised due diligence to prevent such contravention - Appeals dismissed.
