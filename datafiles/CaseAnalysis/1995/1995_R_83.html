Rao Mahmood Ahmed Khan Through Their L.R. v Shri Ranbir Singh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 595; (1995) Supp4 SCC 275; AIR 1995 SC 2195; JT 1995 (2) SC 383; 1995 RD 240; 1995(1) SCALE 842; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 230; 1995 (2) UJ 205

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: U.P. Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1951, s.128

U.P. Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Rules, 1952, r.285D - Auction sale - Immediate deposit of 25% of bid amount - Rule contemplate deposit by cash and not by cheque - Held, mandatory - Deposit of cheque is not valid tender under the rule.
