Andhra Steel Corporation Ltd. Etc. v Andhra Pradesh State Electricityboard And Ors

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, M.N. Venkatachaliah, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 499; (1991) 3 SCC 263; AIR 1991 SC 1456; AIR 1991 SCW 1358; JT 1991 (2) SC 581; 1991(1) SCALE 864; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 624; 1991 (2) UJ 244

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 78 A - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Tariff - Grant of concession - Direction by State Govt. to State Electricity Board - (A) Whether grant immunity to consumers from payment of minimum charges stipulated under agreements? - (B) Whether subsequent withdrawal of concession has affected principles of natural justice? - Held, (A) Does not grant - (B) Principles not attracted - Appeal dismissed.
