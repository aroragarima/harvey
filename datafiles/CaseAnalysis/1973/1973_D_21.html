Delhi Development Authority v Durga Chand Kaushish

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 417; (1973) 2 SCC 825; AIR 1973 SC 2609; 1973 RCR(Rent) 708; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 535

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Land & Property - Interpretation of Statutes - Principles for construction of deed - Held, in construing document one must have regard, not to presume intention of parties, but the meaning of words they have used - And if two interpretations are possible, one which would give effect and meaning to all its parts should be adopted - Appeal dismissed.
