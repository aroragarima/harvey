Union Of India v J. K. Gas Plant

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 310; (1980) 3 SCC 469; AIR 1980 SC 1330; 1980 ALJ 581; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 893

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Defence of India Act, 1962, Railway Act 1890, Iron and Steel Control order, 1941

Summary: Constitution - Government of India Act, 1935 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 70 - Liablity to pay - Govt. of India supplied steel to a company for manufacturing Gas Plants - Instruction to company to transfer the stock to third party - Govt. derived benefit from transaction - Held, that Govt. of India is liable to pay pay price of steel supplied to third party - Appeal dismissed.
