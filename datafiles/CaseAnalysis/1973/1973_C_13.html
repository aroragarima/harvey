Silver Jubilee Tailoring House and Others v Chief Inspector of Shops and Establishments and

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.K. Mukherjea, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 54; (1974) SCC (L&S) 31; (1974) 3 SCC 498; AIR 1974 SC 37; 1973 (27) FLR 350; 1974 LabIC 133; 1973 (2) LLJ 495; 1974 (1) MLJ(SC) 95; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 747

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: A.P. (Telangana Area) Shops and Establishments Act, 1951, s. 2(24) - Payment Of Wages Act, 1936 - 'Person employed' in tailoring establishment - Determination -Test -Question whether employer and employee relationship exists - Held, the right to control manner of work is not the exclusive test for determining the relationship of employer and employee - Rather it is also to be considered as to who provides the equipment - Appeal Dismissed.
