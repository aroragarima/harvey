Supreme Court of India

25 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 180; (2000) 3 SCC 5; AIR 2000 SC 961; 2000 ALJ 618; 2000 (2) AWC 1288; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 500; JT 2000 (2) SC 443; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 15; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 504; 2000(2) SCALE 110; 2000 (2) SCJ 104; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1205; 2000 (2) Supreme 7; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1815

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Indian Tolls Act, 1851

Summary: Indian Tolls Act, 1851, s. 2 - Levy of toll tax on bridges - Cost of stationery and maintenance and interest on interest - On expenditure on construction - Held, State Govt. must justify levy with reference to benefit conferred on public - Must be a corresponding benefit to public - Imposition of toll not reasonable.
