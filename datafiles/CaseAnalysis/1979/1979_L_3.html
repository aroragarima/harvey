State Of Punjab v Labour Court, Jullundur & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 75; (1980) 1 SCC 4; (1980) SCC (L&S) 123; AIR 1979 SC 1981; 1979 (39) FLR 353; 1980 LabIC 1084; 1981 (1) LLJ 354; 1980 (1) LLN 39; 1980 MahLJ 166; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 953; 1979 UJ 878

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, ss.1(3), 4(1)(b), 7 -Payment of Wages Act, 1936, s. 2(ii)(g) - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33C(2) - Industrial Dispute - Gratuity - Hydel Upper Bari Doab Construction Project - Retirement -Proceedings for payment of gratuity due - (A) Applicability of Payment of Gratuity Act to the Project - (B) Re trenchment, if comes with in the perview of s. 4(1)(b) of the Gratuity Act - (C) Applications u/s. 33C (2) of Industrial Disputes Act, if lies - Held, being an establishment, Act applies to it - Retrenchment is a termination of service and falls within the scope of s. 4 (1) - Do not lie - Appeal Allowed.
