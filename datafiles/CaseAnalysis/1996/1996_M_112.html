Collector Of Central Excise,Baroda v M/S. M.M. Khambhatwala

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2969; (1996) 5 SCC 100; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 144; AIR 1996 SC 3319; 1996 (64) ECR 686; 1996 (84) E.L.T. 161; JT 1996 (5) SC 515; 1996 (14) RLT 624; 1996(4) SCALE 466; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 608; 1996 (5) Supreme 188

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 2(f) - 'Manufacturer'- 'Agarbatti, dhoop' etc. falling u/item 68 - Manufactured on assesee's behalf without aid of power in various premises of household ladies outside factory premises and also sold through such premises - (A) Whether assessee is manufacturer of 'agarbatti' etc - (B) If assessee got the goods in question manufactured by 'hired labourers' - Held, (A) Cannot be said to be manufacturer - It cannot be said - (B) House-hold ladies were the manufacturers of the goods in question - Appeal allowed.
