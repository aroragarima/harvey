K.K. Khosla And Anr. v State Of Haryana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, N.M. Kasliwal, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 973; (1990) 2 SCC 199; (1990) SCC (L&S) 227; AIR 1990 SC 1069; 1990 (12) ATC 754; 1990 (60) FLR 524; JT 1990 (1) SC 290; 1990 LabIC 836; 1990 (2) LLJ 144; 1990 (1) LLN 748; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 464; 1990 (3) SLR 868

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Haryana Service of Engineers Class I PWD (Public Health Branch) Rules 1961

Summary: Service - Haryana Service of Engineers Class I PWD (Public Health Branch) Rules 1961, rr. 5, 8, 9, 15 and 22 - Whether respondent 3's promotion as Executive Engineer in Class I service without completion of conditions as prescribed in PWD (Public Health Branch) Rules is valid or not ? - Held, respondent had not cleared departmental examination as required because he had already cleared same during his tenure as a Sub-Divisional Engineer and it was not considered necessary by Govt. for him to give exam again - Appellants' contention that unless respondent had satisfactorily completed probation period, he could not be promoted to post of Executive Engineer, is misconceived as there is no specific provision in rules requiring completion of probationary period for promotion in services - Appeal dismissed.
