Prakash Cotton Mills Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax (Central) Bombay

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1338; (1993) 3 SCC 452; AIR 1993 SC 2174; 1993 (111) CTR 389; 1993 (67) FLR 189; [1993] 201 ITR 684; JT 1993 (2) SC 619; 1993 (2) MLJ(SC) 115; 1993(2) SCALE 425; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 983; [1993] 67 TAXMAN 546; 1993 (2) TLR 826

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - U PSugarcane Cess Act, 1956, Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 37(1)(2) and 256 - What protion of miscellaneous expenditure can be allowed as deductible - Statutory impost paid by assessee by way of damages or penalty or interest - Held, deduction allowed if impost is compensatory in nature - A matter to be decided by fact finding authorities - Appeal allowed Partly.
