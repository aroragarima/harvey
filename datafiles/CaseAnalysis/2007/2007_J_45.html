Jayanti Food Processing Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

22 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 833; (2007) 8 SCC 34; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 789; 2007 (215) E.L.T. 327; JT 2007 (10) SC 327; 2007(10) SCALE 223; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 221

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Excise; Indirect Tax; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Standards Of Weights And Measures Act, 1976

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, ss. 4 and 4A - Standards of Weights and Measures Act, 1976, s. 2(v) - Standards of Weights and Measures (Packaged Commodities) Rules, 1977 - Whether the package of KITKAT sold by the appellant to M/s. Pepsico India Holdings Ltd., under a contract of Rs 4.80 per KITKAT are required to be assessed at that price in terms of s. 4 of the Act or the assessable value of the same is required to be arrived at in terms of s. 4A of the Act? - If the contract of the chocolates was for the purpose of advertising of a particular product of the particular industry, it would be covered within the expression 'servicing any industry' - Held, order of the Tribunal set aside - Appeal allowed.
