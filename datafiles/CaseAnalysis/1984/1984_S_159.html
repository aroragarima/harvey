Tholan v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 429; (1984) 2 SCC 133; (1984) SCC (Cr) 164; AIR 1984 SC 759; 1984 CRLJ 478

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 300, 304, Part II - Murder - Whether Part I or Part III of s.300, IPC would be attracted in the facts of this case - One blow with knife - No dispute etc. - Incident occurring on spur of moment - Conviction u/s 302 - Held, offence u/s. 304, Part II committed - So Conviction altered from s. 302 to s.304, Part II - Appeal allowed.
