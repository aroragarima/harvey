Shree Chamundi Mopeds Ltd. v Church Or South India Trust Assn. Csi Cinodsecretariat, Madr

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, N.M. Kasliwal, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1284; (1992) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1992 SC 1439; AIR 1992 SCW 1517; [1992] 75 Comp Cas 440; 1992 (2) CompLJ 121; JT 1992 (3) SC 98; 1992 (1) RCJ 562; 1992 (2) RCR(Rent) 237; 1992 (1) RentLR 532; 1992(1) SCALE 947; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 999; 1992 (2) UJ 257

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Civil Procedure - Rent Control - Corporate - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151 - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 23(1) - Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, s. 22 - (A) Interim order - Order staying operation of order under challenge - Effect - (B) Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986 - Suspension of legal proceedings against sick companies - Whether eviction proceedings are covered by s. 22 - (C) Statutory tenant - Nature of his leasehold interest - Held, (A) Existence of order under challenge is not wiped out, proceedings that have been terminated by passing of order under challenge do not get restored - (B) Not covered - (B) Has a limited interest which cannot be assigned or transferred - Appeal dismissed.
