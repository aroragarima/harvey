Deputy Commissioner of Agricultural Income Tax and Sales Tax, Quilon v Travancore Rubber and Tea Company

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 89; 1967 (20) STC 520

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1953 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 2(b) - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1944 - C.P. and Berar Sales Tax Act - Whether on admitted facts of case, namely, appellants only selling rubber sheets produced by conversion of latex obtained from trees grown on lands belonging to them, they could be held to be dealer within definition in Act? - Department has failed to discharge onus of proving assessee to be a 'dealer' - Held, assessee is not a dealer - Appeal dismissed.
