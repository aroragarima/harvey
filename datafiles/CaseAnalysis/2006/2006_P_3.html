Lekha v P. Anil Kumar

Supreme Court of India

21 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 781; (2006) 13 SCC 555; 2007 (66) ALR 150; 2007 (1) ALT 35; 2007 (5) AWC 4954; 2007 (2) JhrCR(SC) 221; JT 2006 (10) SC 516; 2007 (2) MLJ 298; 2007 (2) MLJ(SC) 298; 2007 (148) PLR 71; 2007 (1) RCR(Civil) 308; 2006(12) SCALE 163; 2006 (3) ShimLC 422; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 234

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: [A.] Family & Personal - Guardians and Wards Act, 1890, ss. 17, 25 - Custody of a minor child - Held, remarriage of mother cannot be taken as a ground for not granting custody of child to mother - Paramount consideration should be given to welfare of child - HC committed a grave error in not ascertaining wishes of minor, which has consistently been held by Courts to be of relevance in deciding grant of custody of minor children - Appeal allowed.
