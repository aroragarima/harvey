Gujarat Agricultural University v Rathod Labhu Bechar and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20530; (2001) 3 SCC 574; (2001) SCC (L&S) 613; AIR 2001 SC 706; 2001 (1) CLR 995; 2001 (89) FLR 18; JT 2001 (2) SC 16; 2001 LabIC 649; 2001 (1) LLJ 710; 2001(1) SCALE 270; 2001 (2) SCJ 46; [2001] 1 S.C.R. 413; 2001 (1) SLT 641; 2001 (1) Supreme 239; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 834

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 (1)(c) - Appeals arise out of industrial disputes through references by Govt. of Gujarat u/s. 10(1)(c) of Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Appellant an educational institution engaged daily-rated labourers contends that as no posts are sanctioned for them thus their appointments are on irregular basis and not in accordance with Recruitment Rules - Respondents raised an industrial dispute - Tribunal directed appellant to regularize services of all such daily-rated labourers - High Court directed appellant to submit a scheme for extending permanent status to workmen - Held, workmen are eligible for absorption subject to any eligible qualification under rule, if any - Appeals disposed of.
