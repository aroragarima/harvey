Pandhyan Insurance Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, K. Subba Rao, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 193; AIR 1965 SC 1004; [1965] 35 Comp Cas 268; [1965] 55 ITR 716; [1965] 1 S.C.R. 367

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(7), rr. 3(b) and 6 - Insurance Act, 1938 - Income Tax - General insurance business - Writing off amount, as depreciation in respect of depreciation of building - Whether ITO can revalue or correct discrepencies in valuation under provisions of Insurance Act, 1938 - Held, he has no power to revalue or to correct discrepancy in valuation - Appeal allowed.
