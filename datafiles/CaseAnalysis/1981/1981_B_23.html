Beniram and Others v Gaind and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 496; (1981) 4 SCC 209; AIR 1982 SC 789

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Withdrawing the suit - Liberty of - Held, non-pleading may prove a technical impediment and may result in dismissal of appeal which may impede a fresh adjudication - If a point is to be made though belated, in interests of justice appellant (plaintiff) is permitted to withdraw suit with liberty to file a fresh suit - Suit dismissed as withdrawn - Appeal disposed of.
