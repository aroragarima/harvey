Radhey Shyam Rastogi v Ashish Kumar And Another

Supreme Court of India

02 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2217; (2008) 10 SCC 225; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 642; 2008 (73) ALR 285; 2008 (4) AWC 3624; 2008 (4) RCR(Civil) 642; 2008 (2) RCR(Rent) 419; 2008(12) SCALE 131; 2008 (3) UC 1630

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: U.P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of letting Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Constitution of India-Article 226 - U. P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972-Section 21 (1) (b)-Writ petition-Order of release under Section 21 (1) (b) challenged-Petition dismissed by High Court showing non-application of mind-High Court under wrong impression that order rejecting prayer for appointment of Engineer/Advocate Commissioner not challenged in writ petition-Which was admittedly not the position-High Court to appoint Engineer Commissioner-And decide petition after report of Commissioner by reasoned order.
