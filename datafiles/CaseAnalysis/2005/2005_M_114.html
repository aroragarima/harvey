Abdul Rais and Others v Madhya Pradesh Wakf Board and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 January 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 3; (2005) 1 SCC 741; 2005 (2) ALD(SC) 3; 2005 (60) ALR 517; 2005 (1) AWC 449; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 148; JT 2005 (1) SC 67; 2005 (2) MPLJ 273; 2005(1) SCALE 13; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 1; 2005 (Supp) RD 422

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Wakf Act, 1954

Summary: [A.] Wakf Act, 1954, ss. 5, 6, 6(1) - Wakf Act, 1995, s. 83(a) - Declaration as to whether a particular property was Wakf's property or not - Whether petition filed by appellants was barred by time? - Held, civil suit shall be filed within one year from date of publication of notification issued u/s.5(2) - Effect of relief sought for in suit and effect of decisions of this Court referred were required to be kept in view - Remitted matter to HC for a fresh consideration keeping in view decisions in Punjab Wakf Board's case, Radha Kishan's case [1978 Indlaw SC 99] and Siraj-ul-Khan's [1958 Indlaw SC 213] case - Appeal disposed of.
