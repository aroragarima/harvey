Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, o. 7, r. 11 - Rejection Of Plaint - Application therefore - Whether made out? - Entire plaint has to be rejected - Parts of plaint cannot be rejected - Court can strike off part of pleadings only u/O. 6 - Plaintiff can file a fresh plaint u/r. 13 - On facts - Matter remanded - Appeal allowed partly.
