Nagar Palika, Nataur v U. P. Public Services Tribunal, Lucknow and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1352; (1998) 2 SCC 400; (1998) SCC (L&S) 567; 1998 (79) FLR 746; JT 1998 (4) SC 241; 1998 (2) LLJ 287; 1998 (3) LLN 900; 1998 (3) UPLBEC 2044

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Dismissal - Whether order of dismissal of second respondent ultra vires of principles of natural justice? - Held, failure on part of second respondent to reply to charge sheet and to appear before Enquiry Officer despite repeated reminders, that charges were held proved by Enquiry Officer on basis of material which was available before him - This conclusion cannot be considered as ultra vires of principles of natural justice - Fact that copy of Enquiry Report was not given to second respondent will not vitiate proceedings - Order of dismissal upheld - Appeal allowed.
