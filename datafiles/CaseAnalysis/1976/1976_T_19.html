Adoni Cotton Mills Etc. Etc. v The Andhra Pradesh State Electricity Board &Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 223; (1976) 4 SCC 68; AIR 1976 SC 2414; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 133

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Land & Property

Summary: Electricity Supply Act, 1948, s. 49 - Electricity Act, 1910, s. 22B - Board has power u/s. 49 of 1948 Act - To regulate supply and fix higher rates for consumption in excess of quota - Whether conflicting with s. 22-B of 1910 Act - Held, no conflict between ss. 49 and 22-B -

Appeal Dismissed.
