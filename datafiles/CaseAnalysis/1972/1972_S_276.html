Ram Prakash Arora v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 594; (1972) 3 SCC 652; (1972) SCC (Cr) 696; AIR 1973 SC 498; 1972 CRLJ 1293; 1972 UJ 857

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Illegal Gratification

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 367 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 5(1)(d) and 5(2) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 161 and 165 - Appreciation of evidence - Evidence of trap witness - Corroboration - Held, no independent search witness and evidence for corroboration of evidence given by raiding party and prosecution had not proved charges beyond all reasonable doubt - Conviction set aside - Appeal allowed.
