Ram Niwas (Dead) Through Lrs v Bano Smt. and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 571; (2000) 6 SCC 685; AIR 2000 SC 2921; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 58; 2001 (43) ALR 389; JT 2000 (8) SC 340; 2001 (1) MLJ(SC) 33; 2001 (127) PLR 428; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 83; 2001 (92) RD 498; 2000(5) SCALE 363; 2000 (5) SLT 742; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 39; 2000 (5) Supreme 337

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 19(b) - Bona fide purchaser for value without notice - Shop in possession of tenant - Agreement between tenant and vendor - Landlord for sale of suit shop - Subsequent sale of suit shop by vendor - Landlord - Plea by subsequent purchaser that earlier agreement for sale between tenant and vendor was forged - Court holding that subsequent purchasers were bone fide purchasers for value without notice of earlier agreement - No finding however recorded as to whether document was forged or not - Held, case remanded back to High Court for decision in accordance with law - Appeal allowed.
