Commissioner of Customs, Patna v Precise Laboratories Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

09 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1827; 2008 (231) E.L.T. 209

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Raw Materials, Legality, Classifiable under Heading 3306.10

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975 - Raw materials - Classifiable under Heading 3306.10 - Legality - Question for determination was whether the product 'Dant Mukta' imported by the respondent is classifiable under sub-heading 3306.90 or 3306.10 of the Schedule to the 1985 Act.

Held, The Tribunal did not find any merit in the Revenue's contention that the product is not classifiable under Heading 3306.10 after observing that 'Dant Mukta' and 'Dant Shakti' are identical products which are used as raw materials for manufacture of 'Lal Dant Manjan'. The Tribunal held in favour of the assessee. Against the aforesaid order of the Tribunal, Revenue filed appeal before this Court in the assessee's own and the same was dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits. The point in issue in the instant appeals is covered by the aforesaid decision of instant Court which affirmed the decision of the Tribunal on merits. Appeals dismissed.
