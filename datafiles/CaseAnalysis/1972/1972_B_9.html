Haridwar Singh v Bagun Sumbrui and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 620; (1973) 3 SCC 889; AIR 1972 SC 1242; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 629

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Conditional Acceptance

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 4 and 7 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 166(3) - Bihar Rules of Executive Business, r. 10(1) - (A) Concluded contract - Auction sale - Conditional acceptance of bid - Effect - (B) Rules u/art. 166(3) - Interpretation of r. 10(1) of Business Rules which requires prior consultation with Finance Department is mandatory - Coup - Settlement of, private treaty without prior consultation - Held, no concluded contract - Settlement of coup by private treaty without prior consultation with Finance Department held invalid - Appeal allowed.
