Medical Council of India v Madhu Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 September 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1995; (2002) 7 SCC 258; AIR 2002 SC 3230; AIR 2002 SCW 3742; 2002 (6) ALD(SC) 40; JT 2002 (7) SC 1; 2002(6) SCALE 332; 2002 (6) SLR 180; 2002 (5) SLT 209; [2002] Supp2 S.C.R. 228; 2002 (6) Supreme 256

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug

Summary: Education - Indian Medical Council Act, 1956, ss. 10-A, 10-B, 10-C and 19 - Belated admissions to medical colleges - Statutes governing medical education against mid session admissions - Whether mid session admissions are to be allowed in medical course? - Held, there is no scope for admitting students mid-term - Unfilled seats not ground for mid session admissions - Appeal allowed.
