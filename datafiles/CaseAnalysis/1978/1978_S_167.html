Hindustan Sugar Mills and another v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 292; (1978) SCC (Tax) 225; (1978) 4 SCC 271; AIR 1978 SC 1496; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 276; 1979 (43) STC 13; 1978 TAXLR 2258; 1978 UJ 624

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Railway Freight, Packing Charges, Packing Charges And Excise Duty, Cement Control Order 1967

Summary: Sales Tax - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, s. 2(p) - CST Act, 1956, s. 2(h) - Industries (Development And Regulation) Act, 1951 - Ingredients of 'Sale price' - Whether covers railway freight paid by producer under the terms of Control order governing the sale of the commodity - Held, freight forms part of 'sale price' - Appeal dismissed.
