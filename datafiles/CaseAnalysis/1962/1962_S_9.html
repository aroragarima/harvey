Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Bombay Police Manual, 1950

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - Bombay Police Manual, 1950, s. 545 (8) - Whether cl. 8 of s. 545 of Bombay Police Manual is contravening rules of natural justice on bases of procedure followed at inquiry by Deputy Superintendent of police was in violation of rules of natural justice? - Procedure followed by Deputy Superintendent of Police in this case is one followed by many tribunals exercising quasi-judicial powers - Tribunals exercising quasi-judicial functions are not courts and that therefore they are not bound to follow procedure prescribed for trial of actions in Courts nor are they bound by strict rules of evidence - Held, provision is not liable to be set aside on ground that procedure followed at inquiry was in violation of rules of natural justice - Appeal partly allowed.
