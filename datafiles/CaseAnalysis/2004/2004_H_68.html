Haryana Urban Development Authority v Neelam Sharma

Supreme Court of India

18 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1847; (2005) 9 SCC 486

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Consumer Protection - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Interest Act, 1978 - Non-delivery of plot - Claim of refund - Computation of interest - Respondent was allotted plot but possession was not delivered - Respondent surrendered plot and asked for refund - As amount was not paid, respondent filed complaint with District Forum which directed appellant to refund of balance amount along with interest at 18% p.a. - Both appellants and respondent went in appeal to State Forum which dismissed appeal of appellants but allowed appeal of respondent - Appellants filed appeal to National Commission which disposed of - Hence instant appeal.

Held, allotment was made on 29-8-1991 but possession was not delivered. Respondent vide letter dt.7-10-1996 surrendered plot and asked for refund. As amount was not paid, respondent filed complaint with District Forum. Pending that complaint, sum of Rs 84,047 was refunded on 11-12-1997. District Forum held, in Court view correctly, that where fault was of appellants in not delivering possession they were bound to refund entire amount without making any deduction. District Forum directed refund of balance amount of Rs 16,105 along with interest thereon at 18% p.a. from date of deduction till repayment. Both appellants and respondent went in appeal to State Forum. State Forum dismissed appeal of appellants. In appeal of respondent State Forum directed payment of interest at 18% p.a. on all amounts deposited till date of refund of these amounts. Appellants then filed appeal to National Commission which has been disposed of. In case amounts have to be refunded, Interest Act, 1978 would apply. In Court view, in these matters as it is not possible for Court to ascertain what prevailing rate of interest was, Court has been directing payment of interest at 12% p.a. from date amounts were deposited till repayment. Court is told that interest at 12% p.a. has been paid. Court accepts that statement. Court directs that no further amount will be payable by appellants to respondent. Appeal disposed of.

Ratio - Claimant shall not be entitled to any further amount in excess of principal amount and interest already paid.
