Rameshwar Manjhi v Managment Of Sangaramgarh Colliery

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 296; (1994) 1 SCC 292; (1994) SCC (L&S) 521; AIR 1994 SC 1176; 1994 (26) ATC 594; 1994 (1) CLR 9; 1994 (68) FLR 60; JT 1993 (6) SC 337; 1994 (1) KLT 1; 1994 (1) LLJ 376; 1994 (1) LLN 515; 1994 (1) MLJ 71; 1994 (1) MLJ(SC) 71; 1993(4) SCALE 407; 1994 (1) SLJ 173; 1994 (1) SLR 22; 1994 (1) UJ 13

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(k), 2 A, 18 and 33 C(2) - Whether death of workman pending reference or application u/s. 33 C(2) abate proceedings or heirs of deceased can continue it - Held, do not abate - Heirs can continue as s. 18 cannot be invoked for denying right of heirs to be substituted - Appeal allowed.
