State of Kerala v Alex George and another etc.

Supreme Court of India

18 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1006; (2005) 1 SCC 299; AIR 2005 SC 1224; [2004] 271 ITR 290; JT 2004 (10) SC 104; 2005 (1) KLT 164; 2004(9) SCALE 545; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 163

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Indirect Tax - Kerala Finance Act, 1987, s. 1(2) - Kerala Plantations Tax Act, 1960, ss. 2(9), 27, 3, 3(1), 3(2), 3(3), 5, 9A - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Kerala Plantations (Additional Tax) Rules, 1960, r. 16 - Question as to true scope and operation of s 1(2) of Kerala Finance Act substituting schedule-I to Kerala Plantations Tax Act, 1960 w e f 1/7/1987 - Whether in present case, revised schedule introduced in 1960, Act, by Finance Act, 18 of 1987, results in two assessments? - Held, function of Finance Act primarily is to prescribe rate of tax and manner of calculation of tax; and it is not intended to incorporate entire procedural and substantive law relating to tax - State can always revise rates in middle of financial year provided assessable extent of lands comprised in plantation as on Ist April of each year is not altered.
