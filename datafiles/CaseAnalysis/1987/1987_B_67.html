Bhupinder Singh v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28661; (1987) 2 SCC 234; (1987) SCC (Cr) 328; 1987 CRLR 546

Case Digest

Subject: Exchange Control & FDI

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22 - Exchange Control - Detention - Foreign Exchange seized from petitioner's premises - Representation - Delay in supply of legible copies of documents - Detention confirmed - Held, detenu was denied opportunity of making a representation and there was, therefore, a clear contravention of right guaranteed by art. 22 of Constitution - Detenu is entitled to be set at liberty - Appeal allowed.
