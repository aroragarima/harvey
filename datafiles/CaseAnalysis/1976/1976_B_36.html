Â Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, West Bengal v Bishwanath Chatterjee And Others

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 164; (1976) 3 SCC 385; AIR 1976 SC 1492; 1976 CTR 266; 1978 HLR 397; [1976] 103 ITR 536; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 1096; 1976 UJ 499

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Women & Children

Keywords: Succession & Inheritance, Debt Laws

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 2(m) and 3 - Liability to wealth-tax - Meaning of 'Net wealth' - Property of Hindu male governed by Dayabhaga School - Whether assessable jointly in the status of HUF after his death - Held, not assessable jointly in the status of Hindu undivided family after his death - Appeal Allowed.
