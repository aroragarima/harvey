Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, New Delhi v P.N. Sikand

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 45; (1977) SCC (Tax) 368; (1977) 2 SCC 798; AIR 1977 SC 1657; 1977 CTR 253; [1977] 107 ITR 922; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 418

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Covenant Running With The Land, Debt Laws

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s. 7(1) - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Valuation of asset ( lease hold property ) - Assessee covenanting to pay 50% of unearned increase in value of land to lessor at time of assignment - Treatment while valuation - Held, such proportion of unearned increase must be deducted from valuation - Appeal Dismissed.
