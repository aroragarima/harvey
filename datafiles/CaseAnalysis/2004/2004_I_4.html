Commissioner of Central Excise, Hyderabad v I.T.C Limited

Supreme Court of India

02 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1426; (2005) 13 SCC 689; 2005 (179) E.L.T. 15; 2005 (66) RLT 234

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Central Excise Act, 1985

Summary: Excise - Whether pre-deposit made as a pre-condition for hearing of appeal, on assessee being ultimately successful, refundable to assessee with interest? - Learned Solicitor General has taken instructions and has stated before this court that Central Board of Excise and Customs proposes to issue a circular in connection with payment of interest on all such pre deposits - Held, interest to be paid in accordance draft circular - Appeal disposed of.
