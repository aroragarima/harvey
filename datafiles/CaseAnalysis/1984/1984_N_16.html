State Of Punjab v Nohar Chand

Supreme Court of India

17 May 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 154; (1984) SCC (Cr) 432; (1984) 3 SCC 512; AIR 1984 SC 1492; 1984 CRLJ 1153; 1984 CrLR(SC) 273; 1984 (76) PLR 694; 1984(1) SCALE 869; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 839; 1984 (8) ShimLC 362; 1984 UJ 938

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Criminal - CrPC, 1974, ss.179 and 180 - Jurisdiction of court regarding trial of a case - Place of manufacturing or place of marketing of sub-standard fertilizer - Held, Court where the sub-standard fertilizer is marketed will equally have jurisdiction to try the manufacturer of the sub-standard fertilizer whose manufacturing activity is at a different place - Appeal allowed.
