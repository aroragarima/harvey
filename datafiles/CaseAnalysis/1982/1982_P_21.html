Municipal Corporation Of Delhi v Purshotam Dass Jhunjunwala And Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 172; (1983) 1 SCC 9; (1983) SCC (Cr) 123; AIR 1983 SC 158; 1983 CRLJ 172; 1983 CrLR(SC) 30; 1982(2) SCALE 1118; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 895

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 20, 16 and 17, Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - Prosecution of Directors of Sugar Mill for manufacturing adultered milk toffee - Complete details - Of role played by Directors - The extent of their liability - Complaint against accused could not be said to be vague and not implicating directors - Held, High Court not justified in exercising its discretion under s.482, CrPC to quash the proceedings against accused.
