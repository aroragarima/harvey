Bir Bajrang Kumar v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 510; AIR 1987 SC 1345

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Two cases involving an identical point in High Court - One case admitted by the HC but another identical petition was dismissed by the same HC - Held, there is a clear possibility of two contradictory judgments being rendered in the same case by the HC - Appeal allowed.
