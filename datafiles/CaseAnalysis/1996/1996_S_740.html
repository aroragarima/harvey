(1) Vasavadatta Cements; (2) Rajashree Cement v State of Karnataka and another

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1107; (1996) 2 SCC 88; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 759; AIR 1996 SC 1035; 1996 (64) ECR 217; JT 1996 (1) SC 508; 1996(1) SCALE 483; 1996 (1) SCJ 663; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 749; 1996 (101) STC 168; 1996 (1) Supreme 594

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Packing Material, Packing Materials

Summary: Sales Tax - Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 5(3D) - Industries (Development And Regulation) Act, 1951 - Levy of sales tax on packing material i.e. gunny /plastic bags used for packing cement - Determination of tax liability - Held, liability is to be determined after investigation into facts and determining what were the ingredients of contract and intention of parties - Appeal allowed.
