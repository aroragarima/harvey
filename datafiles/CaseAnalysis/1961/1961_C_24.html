Commissioner of Income Tax, Andhra Pradesh v Mis. Bhikaji Dadabhai and Company

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 110; AIR 1961 SC 1265; [1961] 42 ITR 123; 1962 (1) SCJ 554; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 923

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Finance Act, 1950 ss. 11,13, 13(1) - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922 - Assessment year - Imposition of penalty - Repealing of act - Jurisdiction - For year of assessment Fasli 1357 assessees returned income of Rs. 50,384/- - Income Tax Officer found that books of account maintained by assessees were unreliable and assessed their total income at Rs.1, 63,131/- and issued notice to assessees u/s.40 of Hyderabad Income Tax Act and directed the assessees to pay penalty Rs. 42,000/-in addition to tax - Appellate Assistant Commissioner confirmed the order in appeal - On appeal, Income Tax Appellate Tribunal stated that by virtue of provisions of s.13 (1) of 1950 Act, Hyderabad Income Tax Act ceased to have effect and power to impose penalty u/s. 40 of Hyderabad Income Tax Act was not saved, order imposing penalty was without jurisdiction - HC concluded that appeal to Appellate Assistant Commissioner was competent and word assessment in s.13 (1) included whole procedure for imposing liability upon taxpayer but not to procedure for imposing penalty - Hence instant appeal - Whether repeal of Hyderabad Income Tax Act by Finance Act, 1950 power to impose penalty in respect of years preceding date of repeal was lost - Held, s. 13, Hyderabad Income Tax Act ceased to have effect from dt. 1-4-1950 but operation of that Act in respect of levy, assessment and collection of income tax and super tax in respect of periods prior thereto for which liability to Income-tax could not be imposed under Indian Income-tax Act, 1922, was saved - S.13(1) of Finance Act, 1950, Hyderabad Income Tax Act cease to operate dt. 1-4-1950, except for purposes of levy, assessment and collection of income tax and super tax - Proceedings for penalty may be commenced and continued under Acts specified in s.11 whereas no such proceedings may be commenced or continued under Hyderabad Income Tax Act not indicated - HC erred in holding that proceedings for imposing penalty could not be continued after enactment of s.13 (1) of 1950 Act - Appeal allowed.
