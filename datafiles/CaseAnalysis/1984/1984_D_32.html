Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Practice and Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 141, 144, 226 - Writ petition - Majority of writ petitions filed merely for obtaining interim orders - Supreme Court considering the tendency to grant interim orders - Including those practically giving principal relief sought in petition for mere reason that prima facie case has been made out - Whether remedy can be granted under writ jurisdiction when alternate remedy is available - Held, tendency of granting interim order, deprecated - Supreme Court can take judicial notice when thereof - Appeal allowed.
