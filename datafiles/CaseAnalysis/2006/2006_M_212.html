M. Meenakshi and Others v Metadin Agarwal (D) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 450; (2006) 7 SCC 470; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1848; 2006 (6) ALD(SC) 20; 2006 (65) ALR 621; 2007 (5) AWC 5039; 2006(8) SCALE 566; [2006] Supp5 S.C.R. 505

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 20 - Suit for specific performance of contract - Trial Court while rejecting prayer for grant of specific performance of contract, directed Defendant to refund amount of advance as also damages of Rs. 15, 000/- together with interest @ 6% p a - Appeal by plaintiff allowed by Division Bench - Appeal against - Plea - HC acted illegally and without jurisdiction in ignoring orders passed by competent authority under 1976 Act; decree for specific performance granted by DB is contrary to statutory provisions contained in 1976 Act - Whether having regard to proceedings pending before competent authority under 1976 Act, Defendant could perform their part of contract? - Held, HC committed a manifest error in opining that Appellants should have questioned orders passed by competent authority - If they have not done so, same would not mean that DB could go thereinto suo motu - Findings of DB cannot be sustained - Appeals allowed.
