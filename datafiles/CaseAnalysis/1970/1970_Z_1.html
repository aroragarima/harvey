Zora Singh v Shri J. M. Tandon and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 454; (1971) 3 SCC 834; AIR 1971 SC 1537; 1970 UJ 829

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Writ petition u/art. 226 - Certiorari - Conclusions of Tribunal - Some of reasons relied on by Tribunal, extraneous - Interference, if - Held, Superior Court would not interfere if there was legal evidence before Tribunal upon which it was entitled to rest its findings - Appeal Dismissed.
