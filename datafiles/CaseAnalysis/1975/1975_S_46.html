Shiv Prasad v Durga Prasad & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 130; (1975) 1 SCC 405; AIR 1975 SC 957; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 526; 1975 UJ 227

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 21, r. 89(2) and 90 - Application u/r. 90 - Subsequently u/r. 89 of the Code - Requirement of withdrawal of application u/r. 90 - Held, mere conveyance to court that he is withdrawing his application u/r. 90 is enough - Appeal Dismissed.
