Sophia Gulam Mohd. Bham v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 630; (1999) 6 SCC 593; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1160; AIR 1999 SC 3051; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 170; 1999 CRLJ 4064; JT 1999 (5) SC 577; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 421

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Detention order - Subjective satisfaction - Possession of diamonds at airport - Alleged, smuggling out of India - Seizure of documents from premises - Detaining authority opines on basis of those documents - That detenu was a 'carrier' for some smugglers - Non supply of such documents to detenue - Held, detention order illegal.
