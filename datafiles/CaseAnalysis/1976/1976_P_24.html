State Of Kerala And Ors. v Philomina, Etc. Etc. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 319; (1976) 4 SCC 314; AIR 1976 SC 2363; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 273

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964, ss. 81(a), cl.(i), ss.84 and 85 - (A) Amendment - Retrospectivity - Amendment by s. 65 of Amending Act - Volunatary transfers in respect of Kayal padasakharams between 15-09-1963 and 01-01-1970 - (B) Applicability of s.84 - Transfers between 01-01-1970 to 02-11-1972 - Held, ammendment not retrospective and transfer valid - Appeal Dismissed.
