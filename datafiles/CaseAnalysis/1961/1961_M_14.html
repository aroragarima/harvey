Mahabir Prashad Rungta v Durga Datt

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 56; AIR 1961 SC 990; 1961 (1) MLJ(SC) 142; 1961 (1) SCJ 569; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 639

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Indian Contract Act, Interest Act

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure(5 of 1908), s. 34 - Contract Act, ss. 55 and 73 - Whether time was essence of contract in order to entitle person to damages for breach of contract? - Held, in this particular contract, payment of bills by a particular date was expressly mentioned, case was therefore covered by s. 55 of Contract Act and respondent was entitled to rescind contract and to sue for damages as time was of essence - Person is entitled to rescind contract and to sue for damages - Appeal dismissed.
