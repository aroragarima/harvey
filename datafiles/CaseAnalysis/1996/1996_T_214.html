Inspector General Of Police And Anr v Thavasiappan

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3425; (1996) 2 SCC 145; (1996) SCC (L&S) 433; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 869; AIR 1996 SC 1318; 1996 (32) ATC 663; 1996 (2) CLR 521; 1996 (74) FLR 2510; JT 1996 (6) SC 450; 1997 (2) LLJ 191; 1996 (2) LLN 515; 1996(1) SCALE 522; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 977; 1996 (2) SLR 470; 1996 (1) Supreme 565; 1996 (1) UJ 424

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 311(1) - Nature of penalty in disciplinary proceedings - Legality of imposition of penalty of compulsory retirement after charge memo being served by DSP to Inspector - On the ground of competency of DSP to award penalty - Held, not illegal - Appeal allowed.
