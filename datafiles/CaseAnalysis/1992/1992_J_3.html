Union of India and Another v J. K. Synthetics Limited

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 398; (1993) Supp3 SCC 566; AIR 1994 SC 1541; [1993] 199 ITR 14; 1994 (2) TLR 321

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 146 - Reopening of assessment for more than ten years made on basis of calendar year being previous year - Subsequent amendment in Act making financial year as previous year - Held, cancellation of order permitting change of previous year in assessment year 1972-73, not proper in view of upsetting of assessments for over ten years - Supreme Court declined to express any opinion on revocation of change of previous year - Appeal disposed of.
