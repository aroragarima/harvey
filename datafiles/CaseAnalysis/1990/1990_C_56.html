Messrs Bharat Forge and Press Industries Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Baroda Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, N.D. Ojha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 655; (1990) SCC (Tax) 166; (1990) 1 SCC 532; AIR 1990 SC 616; 1990 CRLR 493; 1990 CrLR(SC) 493; 1990 (27) ECR 1; 1990 (45) E.L.T. 525; JT 1990 (1) SC 35; 1990(1) SCALE 30; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 60; 1992 (84) STC 414; 1990 (1) UJ 511

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, Item 26 AA(iv), Item 68 - Whether Pipes and tubes fitting are covered under item 26 AA (iv) or item 68? - Held, meaning of Pipe fitting such as elbows, bends and reducers are covered under item 26 AA (iv) and not under item 68
