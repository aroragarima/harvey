Babulal Nagar And Ors. v Shree Synthetics Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 397; (1984) Supp SCC 128; (1984) SCC (L&S) 594; AIR 1984 SC 1164; 1984 (49) FLR 140; 1984 (2) LLN 470; 1984(1) SCALE 884; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 772; 1984 (2) SLJ 67; 1984 UJ 755

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - M.P. Industrial Relations Act, 1960, ss. 61, 66 - Jurisdiction - Whether the High Court in exercise of its extraordinary jurisdiction under Articles 226 and 227 should have by giving undue importance to a technical objection of jurisdiction which on proper fathoming, it itself lacked should have set aside a well-considered reasoned judgment of the President of Industrial Court which again had merely remanded the matter thus prolonging to some extent the agony of the unemployed workers commencing from 1975 - Held improper - Appeal allowed.
