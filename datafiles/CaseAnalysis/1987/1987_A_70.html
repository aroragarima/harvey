Suraj Mal Saraf and Another v Assistant Collector of Customs, Amritsar

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28064; (1987) 2 SCC 283; (1987) SCC (Cr) 348; AIR 1987 SC 1158; 1987 CRLJ 1057; 1987 CRLR 193; 1987 (11) ECC 367; 1987 (28) E.L.T. 60; JT 1987 (1) SC 485; 1987(1) SCALE 36; 1987 (1) UJ 468

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal - Sea Customs Act, 1878, s.167, sch. item 81 - Determination of validity of restoration of conviction of accused persons aged about 80 and 65 years in appeal - When they were acquitted from charge u/s.161(81) - Supreme court held, it was proper - However considering the age and indifferent health, there sentence modified to period already undergone and fine imposed - Appeal dismissed.
