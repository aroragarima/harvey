Â Messrs Motipur Zamindary Company Private Limited v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 222; AIR 1962 SC 660; 1962 (2) SCJ 288; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 498; 1962 (13) STC 1; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 498

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947, Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959, Bihar Agricultural Income Tax Act, Bihar Agricultural Income Tax Act, 1948

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Constitution - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947, ss. 2(c), 6 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 246, sch 7, list 2, entry 54 (A)Does 'Sugarcane' fall within entry 6, u/s. 6 of Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947? - Held, 'Vegetables' denoting class of vegetables grown in kitchen garden or in farm - Sugarcane cannot fall within entry 6, u/s. 6 of Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947 - (B) Whether absence of President's assent to amending Act affects validity of s. 2(c) of Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947?- Further held, no - As tax is on sale of goods - A matter entirely within entry 54 of State List - Amendment in word 'Dealer' u/s. 2(c) of Bihar Sales Tax Act, does not require President's assent - Appeal dismissed.
