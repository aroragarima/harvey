Union of India v Parle Products Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1324; (1994) Supp3 SCC 662; AIR 1994 SC 106; 1994 (74) E.L.T. 492

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises And Salt Act, 1944, s. 2(f) - 'Manufacture' -(A) Whether process involved in converting 'aluminium-foil' into 'paper-backed aluminium foil' amounts to 'manufacture' - (B) Whether article that results by applying process must be new and commercially distinct article - Held, (A) Amounts to 'Manufacture' - (B) New and commercially distinct article must emerge at end of process - Appeal allowed.
