Raghunath G. Panhale (Dead) By Lrs v Chaganlal Sundarji and Company

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1504; (1999) 8 SCC 1; AIR 1999 SC 3864; 2000 (2) Bom.C.R. 9; JT 1999 (8) SC 219; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 629

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 13(1)(g) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6, r. 17 - Eviction suit - Word 'reasonable' and bona fide requirement - Proof of - Landlord in company service - Seeking eviction for opening grocery shop - Held, word means something more than mere desire but not dire necessity - Proof of becoming jobless or financial capacity need not to proof - Interference on finding of fact is permissible - In rare situations to prevent grave and substantial injustice.
