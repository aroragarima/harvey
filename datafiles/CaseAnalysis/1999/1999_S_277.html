Gobind Sugar Mills Limited v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1371; (1999) 7 SCC 76; AIR 1999 SC 3097; JT 1999 (6) SC 120; 1999 (115) STC 358

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Collected, Levy Of Purchase Tax

Summary: Bihar Finance Act, 1981, s. 4 - Bihar Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1982, s. 49(1)(b) - Is purchase tax levy on sugarcane under both acts is proper? - Held, not proper - Sugarcane Act being a special Act would override Finance Act - Purchase tax levied under Sugarcane Act - Not entirely appropriated towards requirement of board and council constituted under Act - Cannot, therefore, be said that taxation under Sugarcane Act was incidental and two Acts operate in different fields - Purchase tax levied under Sugarcane Act also cannot be said to be fee.
