R.N. Gosain v Yashpal Dhir

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1252; (1992) 4 SCC 683; AIR 1993 SC 352; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 770; 1992 (2) RCJ 582; 1993 (1) RCR(Rent) 156; 1992 (2) RentLR 666; 1992(2) SCALE 913; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 257

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: EP Urban Rent Restriction Act 1949

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6, r. 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Pleadings - Tenant giving undertaking to effect of vacating premises subject to his right to move to Supreme Court to avail protection from eviction - He cannot assail order of High Court u/art. 126 - Held, SC vacated stay of eviction granted earlier - Petition dismissed.
