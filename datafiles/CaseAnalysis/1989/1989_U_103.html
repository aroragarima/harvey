Puri Construction Private Limited v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 410; (1989) 1 SCC 411; AIR 1989 SC 777; 1989 (37) DLT 353; JT 1989 (1) SC 132; 1989(1) SCALE 126; 1989 (1) UJ 416

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act,1940 - Whether court can examine the correctness of award on merit - No separate reply to certain claim made by opposite party - Profit on amount of claim should be limited to certain percentage - Held, Court has no jurisdiction to sit in appeal and examine the correctness of the award on merits-Objection cannot be allowed - Decision is incorrect - No occasion for percentage of profit arose for decision.
