State of Punjab and Others v Mohan Lal Jindal

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2250; (2001) 9 SCC 217; 2001 (7) SLR 77

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug; Service

Summary: Service - Medical reimbursement on basis of new policy - Held, questions involved in present proceedings are squarely covered by a judgment of in case of State of Punjab v Ram Lubhaya Bagga 1998 Indlaw SC 1505 - Further held, additional amount which he has claimed was granted by High Court on ground that new policy was not sufficiently published - Said view of HC cannot be sustained - Respondent will be entitled to medical reimbursement in light of new policy i.e. at AIIMS rates - Appeal allowed.
