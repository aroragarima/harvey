K. Nandakumar v Managing Director, Thanthal Periyar Transport Corporation

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3230; (1996) SCC (Cr) 451; (1996) 2 SCC 736; 1996 ACJ 555; AIR 1996 SC 1217; AIR 1996 SCW 1247; 1996 (1) CCC 282; [1996] 86 Comp Cas 435; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 505; 1996(2) SCALE 308; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 633; 1996 (2) UJ 45

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act 1939, s. 92 A - No fault liability - Injured suffered permanent disability - If benefit of s. 92 A of Act can be refused on ground negligence of injured alleged - Held, no ground to refuse - Appeal allowed.
