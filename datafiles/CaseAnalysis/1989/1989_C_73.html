Commissioner Of Income Tax, Culcutta v Karam Chand Thapar And Bros. (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 735; (1989) 2 SCC 31; AIR 1989 SC 1045; [1989] 65 Comp Cas 728; 1989 (1) CompLJ 284; 1989 (76) CTR 36; [1989] 176 ITR 535; JT 1989 (1) SC 304; 1989(1) SCALE 421; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 638; [1989] 43 TAXMAN 45

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 256 - Reference - Finding of Tribunal that loss from sale of shares is revenue loss and not capital loss - Consideration of all relevant material - Finding is finding of fact - Interference sought - Held, no interference is warranted - No reference - Appeal dismissed.
