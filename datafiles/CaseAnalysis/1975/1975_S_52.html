Cooper Engineering Limited v Shri P. P. Mundhe

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, A. Alagiriswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 221; (1975) SCC (L&S) 443; (1975) 2 SCC 661; AIR 1975 SC 1900; 1975 (31) FLR 188; 1975 LabIC 1441; 1975 (2) LLJ 379; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 361

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s.15, sch. 2, r. 3 - Industrial Disputes - (A) Industrial adjudication - Absence of domestic or defective enquiry - Controversy - (B) Workmen - Dismissal - Tribunal awarded relief of reinstatement - Challenged - Held, opportunity to management to adduce evidence - Relief converted to one of compensation by Supreme Court - Appeal Dismissed.
