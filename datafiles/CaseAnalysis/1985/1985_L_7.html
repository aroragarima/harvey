Union Of India v Lakshmi Sugar And Oil Mills Ltd., Hardoi

Supreme Court of India

21 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 290; (1986) 1 SCC 26; AIR 1986 SC 388; 1986 ALJ 254; 1986 (1) CCC 250; 1986 (1) CompLJ 198; 1985(2) SCALE 1088; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 758; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 758; 1986 (1) UJ 152

Case Digest

Subject: Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1953, Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1978, Sugar Undertaking (Taking Over of Management) Ordinance 1978

Summary: Agriculture and Agri. Products - Sugar Undertakings (Taking over of Management) Act, 1978, s.3(1)(b) - Sugar undertaking - Arrears of cane dues exceeding permissible limit - Whether arrears cannot be confined to certain period u/s. 3(1)(b) of Act - Held, total arrears upto a particular date have to be calculated; language of the provision is wide enough to include all the arrears of cane dues accumulated upto 'that date', including the arrears pertaining to sugar cane purchased in earlier years - Appeal allowed.
