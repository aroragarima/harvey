A.P. State Road Transport Corporation v P.V. Ramamohan Chowdhary And Ors

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 888; (1992) 2 SCC 235; 1993 (3) ACC 642; AIR 1992 SC 888; JT 1992 (4) SC 377; 1992(1) SCALE 410; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 830; 1992 (2) UJ 245

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Constitution - Motor Vehicles Act 1939, ss. 68 C, 68 D and 68 E - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Scheme for nationalisation - Exemption of some routes from scheme - Partial exemption by State Transport Undertaking, if not discriminatory - Held, such power is specifically given by s. 68 C to State Transport Undertaking - Partial exemption cannot, therefore, be questioned as discriminatory - Appeal allowed.
