State Of T.N. & Anr v Kethiyan Perumal

Supreme Court of India

11 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 847; (2004) 8 SCC 780; 2005 CRLJ 105; JT 2004 (9) SC 331; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 571; 2004(8) SCALE 651; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 322

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot leggers, Drug Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Slum Grabbers Act, 1982, s. 3(1) and 5A - Detention - Habeous corpus petition filed by wife of detenu - HC quashed order of detention - Held, effect of confessional statement and background facts have not been taken note of by HC - Effect of s 5A of Act cannot be lost sight of - On both aspects factual as well as legal, in both appeals, HC's judgments are not sustainable and are, therefore, set aside - Appeals allowed.
