Mepco Industries Limited, Madurai v Commissioner of Income Tax and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 November 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, H.L. Dattu, Deepak Verma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1576; (2010) 1 SCC 434; 2009 (248) E.L.T. 3; [2009] 319 ITR 208; JT 2009 (14) SC 339; 2009(14) SCALE 87; [2009] 15 S.C.R. 1026

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Interest On Advance, Interest On Advance Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 154 - Rectification of mistake - Petitioner received power subsidy from Pondicherry Government, filed returns on basis that subsidy was revenue receipt - Later petitioner claimed that it was capital receipt and sought revision of assessment - Revision petitions filed by petitioner allowed by Commissioner but subsequently order rectified on basis of Sahney Steel and Press Works Limited & Ors. v. CIT, 1997 INDLAW SC 1527- On appeal, HC held that in view of subsequent decision of this Court, Department was entitled to invoke s. 154 of Act - Decision of Single Judge affirmed by Division Bench - Hence, present appeal - Whether it was open to Commissioner to rectify its own order u/s. 154 of Act on basis of subsequent judgement of SC? - Held, subsidy in Sahney Steel and Press Works Limited was an incentive subsidy linked to production - Production incentive scheme is different from Scheme giving subsidy for setting up industries in backward areas - Therefore, Department has erred in invoking s. 154 of Act - Impugned judgement of Division Bench set aside - Appeals allowed.
