Goutam Kundu v State Of West Bengal And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

14 May 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1165; (1993) 3 SCC 418; (1993) SCC (Cr) 928; AIR 1993 SC 2295; 1993 (2) ALT 356; 1993 (2) Crimes 481; 1993 CRLJ 3233; 1993 CRLR 487; 1993 CrLR(SC) 487; JT 1993 (3) SC 443; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 917

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code , 1973, s. 125 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 112 - Grant of maintenance to child when paternity was in dispute and blood test was to be conducted - (A) Validity of rejection of application for test - (B) Permissibility - Held, was proper, as pourpose was to avoid payment of maintenance - Permisible - Appeal allowed.
