Konkan Railway Corporation Limited and Others v Messrs Mehul Construction Company

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Doraiswamy Raju, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 678; (2000) 7 SCC 201; AIR 2000 SC 2821; 2001 (44) ALR 377; 2000 (3) ARBLR 162; 2001 (1) Bom.C.R. 771; 2000 (1) CLC 1609; 2000 (4) CompLJ 273; JT 2000 (9) SC 362; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 112; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 153; 2000(6) SCALE 71; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 563

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Corporate; Trade

Keywords: Undue Delay

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11(6) - Appointment of arbitrator - Chief Justice or his nominee discharging functions under s. 11(6) - Would be acting in administrative capacity to appoint arbitrator - Refusal to appoint arbitrator - Held, would not be amenable to jurisdiction of Supreme Court under art. 136 - Being an act of non - performance of duty, mandamus would lie.
