Reena Kumari and others v Praveen Kumar and others

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, Rohinton Fali Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 851

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Disposal of contempt appeal - Directions to HC - Held, as DB of HC in its order dt.17-12-2013 referred to entire background of selection and clarified about relief the Court intended to give, HC is requested to dispose of pending contempt appeal - Further, appellants are at liberty to bring to notice of HC that they were appointed during pendency of matter with Single Judge and that in pursuance of impugned orders, their services were terminated - Appeals and SLP disposed of with said directions - Order accordingly.
