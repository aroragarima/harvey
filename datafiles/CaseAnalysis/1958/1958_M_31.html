Shri Kishori Lal v Mst. Chaltibai

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, S.K. Das, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 137; AIR 1959 SC 504; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 698; [1959] Supp1 S.C.R. 698

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Family & Personal - Practice & Procedure - Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, s. 11(vi) - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 31, 115 and 114 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 6 r. 2 and O. 8 r. 2 - (A) Whether conduct of parties can establish a right which facts themselves disapprove? - Held, presumptions arising from conduct cannot establish a right which facts themselves disapprove - (B) Whether an admission can be retracted by a person making it? - Further held, unless an admission constitutes estoppel - Maker can prove that it was mistaken or was untrue - Appeal dismissed.
