Collector of Central Excise, New Delhi v Bhiwani Textile Mills

Supreme Court of India

03 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 170; (1997) 10 SCC 522; 1997 (68) ECR 772; 1996 (88) E.L.T. 639; 1996 (17) RLT 1126

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - (A) Classification - Followed, Rajasthan Spg. Wvg. Mills Ltd. case [1990 Indlaw SC 525] - Order of the tribunal upheld - (B) Demand for duty - Decision of Madhumilan Syntex Pvt. Ltd. [1988 Indlaw SC 395] covers the case against the appellant - Held, though appellant succeeds on the aspect of classification, he shall not be able to enforce the demand - Appeal partly allowed.
