Ramkishan And Others v State Of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2748; (1997) 7 SCC 518; (1997) SCC (Cr) 1106; AIR 1997 SC 3997; AIR 1997 SCW 3935; 1997 (4) CCR 153; 1997 (3) Crimes 268; 1998 CRLJ 54; JT 1997 (7) SC 722; 1997(6) SCALE 6; 1997 (3) SCJ 146; 1998 (5) SLT 505; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 700; 1997 (8) Supreme 44

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 300, 304, Part II, 149 - Proof of culpable homicide or murder - Armed accused persons attacked complainant party with intention to cause injuries - Inflicted injuries on body of deceased - No evidence showing any pre-meditation - Offence of culpable homicide not amounting to murder made out in absence of specific charge being framed as to applicability of s. 149 of the Code - Validity of conviction u/s. 304, Part II r/w s.149 - Held, proper - Appeal allowed.
