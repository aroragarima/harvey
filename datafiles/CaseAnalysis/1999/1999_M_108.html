Mary Angel and Others v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1659; (1999) 5 SCC 209; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1296; AIR 1999 SC 2245; 1999 (39) AllCriC 184; 1999 CRLJ 3513; JT 1999 (3) SC 638; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 594; 1999 (5) Supreme 370

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Non Cognizable

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 148(3), 342, 359 and 482 - Inherent powers of Court - Scope in context of exemplary costs - Can be exercised for imposing exemplary costs while rejecting petition as being both frivolous and vexatious - Appeal dismissed.
