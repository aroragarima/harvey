Ishwar Chand Jain v High Court Of Punjab And Haryana And Another

Supreme Court of India

26 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 881; (1988) 3 SCC 370; (1988) SCC (L&S) 797; AIR 1988 SC 1395; 1988 (57) FLR 433; JT 1988 (2) SC 473; 1988 LabIC 1651; 1988 (2) LLN 557; 1988(1) SCALE 1188; 1988 (2) SLR 564; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 396; 1988 (2) UJ 381

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 235 and 311 - Dismissal of judicial officer on probation - Inquiry report of Vigilance Judge in respect of four complaints forming basis for dismissal not showing work and conduct as unsatisfactory - However, investigation suggested but no further inquiry - Held, dismissal is unwarranted - Appeal allowed.
