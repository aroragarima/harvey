Jasraj Inder Singh v Hem Raj Multan Chand

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 165; (1977) 2 SCC 155; AIR 1977 SC 1011; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 973

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Tort

Summary: CPC, 1908, ss. 11 and 109 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Res judicata - Efect of Remand Order passed by High Court - Appeal to Supreme Court from decree of High Court in same matter - Adjudication of entire subject-matter, if - Held, appeal being from suit as a whole entire subject-matter can be adjudicated - Order Accordingly.
