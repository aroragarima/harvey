Jainul Haque v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 398; (1974) SCC (Cr) 1; (1974) 3 SCC 543; AIR 1974 SC 45; 1974 CRLJ 143; 1973 CRLR 750; 1974 UJ 26

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: IPC, 1860, s. 323 - Abetment by exhortation to assault - Discrepancy between FIR and deposition of reporter - Regarding part played by accused - Evidence of eye-witnesses contradictory and unconvincing - Still conviction for abetment - Credibility of evidence of exhortation - Held, conviction for abetment cannot be recorded without clear, cogent and reliable evidence in this respect - Evidence of exhortation is by nature weak - Appeal Allowed.
