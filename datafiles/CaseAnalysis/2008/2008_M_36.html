P. Swaroopa Rani v M. Hari Narayana @ Hari Babu

Supreme Court of India

04 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 333; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 79; (2008) 5 SCC 765; AIR 2008 SC 1884; 2008 (72) ALR 171; 2008 (5) CTC 358; 2008 (106) CutLT 539; 2008 (4) MPLJ 512; 2008 (2) RCR(Criminal) 492; 2008 (3) RLW 2293; 2008(3) SCALE 501; [2008] 3 S.C.R. 900; 2008 (2) UC 1064

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Suit for specific performance of the contract - Allegation that receipt (Ex. A.15) was a fabricated document - High Court stayed the investigation - Appeal against - Held, in a given case, civil proceedings and criminal proceedings can proceed simultaneously - Whether civil proceedings or criminal proceedings shall be stayed depends upon the fact and circumstances of each case - Filing of an independent criminal proceeding, although initiated in terms of some observations made by the civil court, is not barred under any statute - High Court was not correct in staying the investigation - Appeal dismissed.
