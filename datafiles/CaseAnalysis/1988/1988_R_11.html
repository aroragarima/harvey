Subhash Kumar Lata v R.C. Chhiba And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 644; (1988) 4 SCC 709; AIR 1989 SC 458; 1988 (36) DLT 203; JT 1988 (4) SC 65; 1988 (2) RCJ 513; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 462; 1988 (2) RentLR 772; 1988(2) SCALE 790; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 241; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 241

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 21 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(f) - Limited tenancy rights - Conferment of - Sanction of Rent Controller - Obtained by landlord - Withholding information that disputed premises was already let - Validity of sanction - Held, sanction granted is void - Appeal dismissed.
