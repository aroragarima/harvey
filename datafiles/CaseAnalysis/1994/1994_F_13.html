T. V. Usman v Food Inspector, Tellicherry Municipality, Tellicherry

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1284; (1994) SCC (Cr) 187; (1994) 1 SCC 754; AIR 1994 SC 1818; 1994 AllCriC 226; 1994 (1) BLJR 728; 1994 (1) CCR 78; 1994 (1) FAC 1; 1994 (1) GLH 420; JT 1994 (1) SC 260; 1994(1) SCALE 179; 1994 (1) UJ 365

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Health & Drugs - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 13 and 23 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules, 1955, r. 7(3) - Rule requiring copy of analysis report to be provided to Local Health Authority within a period of 45 days - Whether mandatory? - Held, it is directory - Appeal dismissed.
