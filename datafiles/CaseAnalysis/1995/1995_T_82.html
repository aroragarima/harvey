The Chairman And Managing Director,Sipcot, Madras - 8 And Or v Contromix Pvt.Ltd. By Its Director(Finance) Seetharaman, Mad

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1375; (1995) 4 SCC 595; AIR 1995 SC 1632; [1995] 84 Comp Cas 110; JT 1995 (6) SC 283; 1995(3) SCALE 717; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 415; 1995 (2) UJ 602

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Land & Property - State Financial Corporations Act, 1951, s. 29 - Companies Act, 1956 - Disposal of property for recovery of loan - Whether sale by inviting tenders is invalid? - Held, not ipso facto invalid, validity of such a sale have to be considered in light of facts and circumstances of case - Appeal allowed.
