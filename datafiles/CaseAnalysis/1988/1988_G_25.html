R. Prabha Devi And Ors. v Government Of India, Through Secretary,Ministry Of Personnel

Supreme Court of India

08 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 546; (1988) 2 SCC 233; (1988) SCC (L&S) 475; AIR 1988 SC 902; 1988 (7) ATC 63; JT 1988 (1) SC 488; 1988 LabIC 999; 1988 (2) LLJ 56; 1988 (1) LLN 955; 1988(1) SCALE 453; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 147; 1988 (3) SLJ 140; 1988 (2) SLR 110; 1988 (1) UJ 602

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Central Secretariat Service Rules

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 - Central Secretariat Service Rules, 1962 - Promotion - Grade I Post of C.S. S. - Proviso 3 to r. 12(2) prescribing eligibity condition of 8 years of approved service in grade of Section Officer - Constitutionality of - Held, rule being equally applicable to direct recruit and promotee Section Officers is not ultra vires of arts. 14 and 16 - Appeal dismissed.
