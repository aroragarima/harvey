Iswar Bhai C. Patel Alias Bachu Bhai Patel v Harihar Behera and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1000; (1999) 3 SCC 457; AIR 1999 SC 1341; 1999 (35) ALR 755B; 1999 (2) CalLT(SC) 99; 1999 (2) CLT 103; JT 1999 (2) SC 250; 1999 (3) MLJ(SC) 22; 1999(2) SCALE 108; 1999 (2) SCJ 158; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 1097; 1999 (2) SLT 597; 1999 (3) Supreme 121

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114(g) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 1, r. 3, O. 2, r. 3, O. 41, r. 1 - Appeal - Locus to file - Suit for recovery of money - Claim for decree against both defendants - Prayer for decree against both defendants so that decretal amount may be realised from either of defendant - Decree passed against only one of defendants - Held, Plaintiff can file appeal against decree - Adverse inference can be drawn - Against defendant who did not enter witness box to deny plea.
