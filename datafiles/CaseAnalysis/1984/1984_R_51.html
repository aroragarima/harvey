Mohammad Idris And Anr. v Rustam Jahangir Babuji And Others.

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 195; (1984) 4 SCC 216; (1984) SCC (Cr) 587; AIR 1984 SC 1826; 1984 (10) ALR 755; 1984 (2) Crimes 800; 1985 CRLJ 353; 1984(2) SCALE 213; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 598; 1984 UJ 1105

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1971 - Appeal filed against order of single Judge of High Court to Division Bench - Statutory right of appeal gets exhausted - There is no further right of appeal to Supreme Court - Contempt of court - Breach of undertaking given by party - Held, Breach of undertaking given by petitioners - Justified in giving appropriate directions to close breach in addition to punishing party for contempt of court.
