K. Sukhendar Reddy v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1311; (1999) 6 SCC 257; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1088; AIR 1999 SCW 4906; 2000 (1) AWC 660; JT 1999 (10) SC 237; 2000 (1) LLJ 474; 1999 (4) LLN 647; 1999(2) SCALE 612; 2000 (1) SLR 23; 1999 (4) SLT 188

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - All India Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1969, s. 3 (1) - Suspension - Validity - Criminal case pending against appellant - Held, Govt. cannot be permitted to resort to selective suspension - It cannot be permitted to place an officer under suspension just to exhibit and feign that action against officers, irrespective of their high status in service hierarchy, would be taken - Appeal allowed.
