Parmeshwar Prasad v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20400; (2002) SCC (L&S) 112; (2002) 1 SCC 145; AIR 2001 SC 2982; 2002 (1) AWC 63; 2001 (3) CLR 1052; 2002 (92) FLR 19; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 41; JT 2001 (9) SC 275; 2001 LabIC 3993; 2001(7) SCALE 507; 2002 (1) SLJ 333; 2002 (1) SLR 86; 2001 (7) SLT 541; [2001] Supp4 S.C.R. 506; 2001 (7) Supreme 875; 2002 (1) UPLBEC 390

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: IAS (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations, 1955, IAS (Appointment by Selection) Regulations, 1956

Summary: Services - IAS (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations, 1955 - IAS (Appointment by Selection) Regulations, 1956 - Non consideration for promotion to IAS - Held, IAS (Appointment by Selection) Regulations, 1956 envisages that state Govt. may, from time to time, consider by way of selection cases of persons not belonging to State civil service but serving in connection with affairs of state - Provisions contained therein also interdict State Govt. from considering cases of persons, who have attained age of 54 years on first day of April of year in which cases of persons are considered and consequently, no claims can be asserted in derogation of such stipulation - Appeal dismissed.
