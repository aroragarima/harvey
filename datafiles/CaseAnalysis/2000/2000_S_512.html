K. Karunakaran v State of Kerala and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 235; (2000) 3 SCC 761; (2001) SCC (Cr) 183; AIR 2000 SC 3381; 2000 (2) CCR 2; 2000 (2) Crimes 57; 2000 CRLJ 2278; 2000 (2) JCC 442; JT 2000 (3) SC 532; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 454; 2000(2) SCALE 604; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 735; 2000 (3) SLT 366; 2000 (2) Supreme 618

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, s. 154 - FIR - Registration of - Against then Chief Minister and others - Writ petition - For issuance of direction to State Govt. to register case of corruption against Chief Minister and others - Dismissed - Ground, no case made out - Change in Govt. - Preliminary enquiry on request of Commr. - Secy. to Director of Vigilance - Reveals commission of offences punishable under various provisions of law - Held, registration of FIR against then Chief Minister and others not barred - Not mala fide.
