Union Of India And Others v M/S. Rai Singh Deb Singh Bist & Anr

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 325; (1973) 3 SCC 581; AIR 1974 SC 478; 1973 (2) CTR 129; [1973] 88 ITR 200; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 102; 1974 TAXLR 211

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 34(1)(a) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114, Illus. (g) - Income Tax Assessment - Reopening of - Conditions for exercise of Jurisdiction - Notice - Validity - Held, in the circumstances of the case, not possible to conclude that facts necessary to confer jurisdiction on the ITO to proceed u/s.34(1)(a) of the Act had been established - Appeals dismissed.
