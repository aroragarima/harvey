General Engineering Works v Commissioner of Central Excise, Jaipur

Supreme Court of India

10 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1055; (2010) 15 SCC 696; 2007 (212) E.L.T. 295

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Whether in computing value of points and crossings manufactured by appellants on behalf of Railways, value of scrap has got to be added? - Value of scrap would be included in value of points and crossings only in case where it is shown that conversion charges (as charged by appellants) get depressed by fact that processor is allowed to keep and sell scrap - Contract entered into between parties clearly indicates that price (conversion charges) have been worked out on basis that 5% wastage would be available to appellants - This indicates that price has been affected by sale of scrap - Held, in computing value of points and crossings value of scrap sold has to be taken into account - View of Tribunal upheld - Appeal dismissed.
