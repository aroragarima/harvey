M\S. Bombay Chemical Private Limited v The Collector Of Central Excise, Bombay I, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

18 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S.B. Majmudar, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1017; (1995) Supp2 SCC 646; AIR 1995 SC 1469; 1995 (58) ECR 177; 1995 (77) E.L.T. 3; JT 1995 (3) SC 552; 1995 (8) RLT 121; 1995(5) SCALE 716; 1995 (2) SCJ 675; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 369; 1995 (99) STC 339

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, sch. I, Tariff item 68 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - Exemption notification under 55/75-CE D/- 01-03-1975 item 18 - Disinfectant and fluids - Having capability of killing bacteria - If entitled to exemption under notification - Held, entitled - Appeal allowed.
