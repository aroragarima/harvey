Anter Singh v State Of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

05 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 79; (2004) 10 SCC 657; AIR 2004 SC 2865; 2004 (2) Crimes 38; 2004 CRLJ 1380; JT 2004 (2) SC 332; 2004 (1) KarLJ 758; 2004(2) SCALE 271; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 123

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Blood-Stained Earth, Non-Explanation, Footprints, Indian Evidence Act, 1972

Summary: Evidence - s.27, Evidence Act - information leading to the discovery of a fact - cannot form the foundation of the prosecution case - merely one one link in the chain of proof - other links must be forged in manner allowed by law - held on facts, several discrepancies in evidence - prosecution has not discharged burden - accused acquitted - appeal allowed.
