Omar Salay Mohamed Sait v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 156; AIR 1959 SC 1238; [1959] 37 ITR 151

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Constitution - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 66(1) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - (A) Whether question that a decision of a court of fact is based on material irrelevant to enquiry is a question of law? - Held, yes it is - (B) Whether ITAT is a fact finding tribunal or not? - Further held, yes it is a fact finding tribunal - Supreme Court cannot interfere in conclusions of fact arrived at by tribunal after due consideration of evidence before it - Appeal allowed.
