Usha Agarwal v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 741; (2007) 1 SCC 295; 2007 CRLJ 39; JT 2006 (9) SC 507; 2007 (1) RCR(Criminal) 190; 2006(11) SCALE 173; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 342

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: COFEPOSA, Foreign Trade (Regulation) Rules, 1993

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3(1), 8 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 22(5), 32 - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 82 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 2(39) - Foreign Trade (Regulation) Rules, 1993, r. 7 - Preventive detention on ground that detenu had diverted goods, imported duty free for manufacture of goods for export, into domestic market - Held, it cannot be said that document whereby EXIM benefits to one of companies controlled by detenu was stopped, was a 'relevant' document, non-consideration of which would vitiate detention order - Appeal dismissed.
