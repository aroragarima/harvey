Commissioner Of Central Excise, Shillong v Woodcraft Products Ltd

Supreme Court of India

09 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1354; (2002) 10 SCC 734; 2002 (143) E.L.T. 247; 2002 (51) RLT 388

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, ss. 11 and 11 A - Whether Tribunal erred in holding that demand for return of refunds was not justified in view of provisions of s. 11 A? - Revenue honoured Tribunal's order and made refund, upon reversal by Supreme Court of Tribunal's order, assessee was bound in law to restitute amounts of such refund to revenue - Held, assessee shall pay to revenue amounts refunded to it pursuant to orders of Tribunal within twelve weeks, with interest - Appeals allowed.
