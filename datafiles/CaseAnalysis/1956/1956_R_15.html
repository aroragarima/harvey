Ram Chandra and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1956

Case Analysis

Bench

Syed Jaffer Imam, P. Govinda Menon

Where Reported

1956 Indlaw SC 127; AIR 1957 SC 381; 1957 CRLJ 559

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 364 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 30 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 201, 364, 120-B and 368 - Murder - Appellants kidnapped a 14 yrs old boy for extortion and murdered him - Nature of confession - Held, no tangible evidence to support conviction for murder but kidnapping and extortion proved, sentences modified accordingly - Appeal dismissed.
