S.P.S. Rathore v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 May 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 353; (2005) 10 SCC 1; 2005 (4) ALD(SC) 67; 2005 (5) AWC 4769; JT 2005 (5) SC 257; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 876; 2005(4) SCALE 734

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Custodial Death

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 354, 509 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 21, 226, 32 - Whether the order of the High Court directing the District Judge to conduct enquiry to the effect that respondent was implicated in false criminal cases and harassed by police at instance of appellant, a police officer, sustainable? - Held, news report as well as judgment of discharge neither mentioned anything about appellant's involvement in registration of FIRs against r. 5 and harassment by police at his instance - HC went beyond material on record while taking suo motu cognizance of matter - Impugned judgment directing District Judge to conduct enquiry cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
