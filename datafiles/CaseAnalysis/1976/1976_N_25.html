New Delhi Municipal Committee v Kalu Ram and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 147; (1976) 3 SCC 407; AIR 1976 SC 1637; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 87; 1976 UJ 525

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Companies Act, 1913, Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorized Occupation) Act, 1958

Summary: Rent Control - Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1958, s. 7 - Power to recover arrears of rent - Applicability of provisions of s. 7 of 1958 Act, on claims barred by limitation also - Held, section cannot be resorted to if claim is barred by limitation - Appeal dismissed.
