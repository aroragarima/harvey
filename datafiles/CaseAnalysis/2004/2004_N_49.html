Commissioner of Central Excise and Customs (Appeals), Ahmedabad v Narayan Polyplast

Supreme Court of India

24 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1428; (2005) 10 SCC 121; 2005 (179) E.L.T. 20; 2005 (66) RLT 237

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Modvat credit rules - Whether an assessee is bound to avail of exemption or can forgo same in order to avail Modvat credit? - Tribunal that assessee had option not to avail of exemption but to pay duty and avail of Modvat credit - Held, no necessity for court to interfere with decision of tribunal issue is merely technical with no revenue implication - Appeals dismissed.
