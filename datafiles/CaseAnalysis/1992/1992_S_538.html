Union of India and Others v Surya Phosphate Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1270; (1992) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1993 SC 1620; JT 1992 (4) SC 481; 1992(2) SCALE 159; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 817; 1992 (2) UJ 620

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative

Keywords: Kerala Private Forests (Vesting and Assignment) Act, 1971, Uttar Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act 1975, Vienna Convention on Consular relations Act 36, Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Societies Employee service Regulation 1975, Fertilizer (Control) Order 1989, Gujarat Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules 1971, Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Dairy Federation and Milk Union Centralised Service Rules 1984

Summary: Socio Economic - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3 - Interpretation of circular providing for subsidy on manufacture of single super phosphate at differential rate - Held, subsidy would be based on ex-factory price of each unit as worked out by committee and not on ex-factory price - Appeal allowed.
