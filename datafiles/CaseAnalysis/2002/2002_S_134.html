Shreejee Traco (I) Private Limited v Paperline International Inc

Supreme Court of India

17 December 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2117; (2003) 9 SCC 79

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11 - Constitution of India, 1950 - Ex parte - Notice - Contracting parties was a foreigner - International commercial arbitration agreement - In absence of clear indication that parties intended proper law of India to govern arbitration in spite of place of arbitration having been agreed to be at New York? - Held, so far as language employed by Parliament in drafting sub-s (2) of s. 2 of Act is concerned, suffice it to say that language is clear and unambiguous - Saying that this Part would apply where place of arbitration is in India tantamounts to saying that it will not apply where place of arbitration is not in India - Petition u/s. 11(4) of Act is not maintainable before Chief Justice of India or his designate - Petition dismissed.
