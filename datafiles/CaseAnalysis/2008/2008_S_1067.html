Green Earth Asphalt and Power Private Limited v State of Maharashtra. Tr. P.S.O. and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 August 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1352; (2008) 8 SCC 278; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 481; 2009 ALL MR (Cri) 1266; 2008 (106) CutLT 696; 2009 (2) MPLJ 10; 2008(11) SCALE 389; [2008] 12 S.C.R. 110

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Negotiable Instruments, Legal Fiction

Summary: Negotiable Instruments Act, s. 141; Cr.P.C, s. 482 - Appeal against order quashing the Summary Criminal Case - Held, s. 141 raises a legal fiction in terms whereof the Directors of a Company which would include the partners of a Firm would be deemed to have committed an offence along with the Company if they are in-charge of the affairs of the Company and responsible to it - Averments which were necessary to be made to rope in respondent Nos. 4 and 5 herein having not been made, the criminal proceeding could have been quashed against them but not against the respondent Nos. 2 and 3 - Appeal partly allowed.
