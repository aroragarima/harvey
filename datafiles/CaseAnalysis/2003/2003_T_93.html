Tolin Rubbers Private Limited v Commissioner of Customs, Cochin

Supreme Court of India

11 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1217; 2004 (163) E.L.T. 289

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs valuation - second hand machinery - assessing authority determined the assessable value of second hand machinery in question in terms of Rule 8 of Customs Valuation (Determination of Price of Imported Goods) Rules, 1988 on the basis of chartered engineer's certificate showing residual life of at least 10 years and giving maximum depreciation of 70% - None of the authorities below advert to Rule 4(2) ibid or say for what reasons as provided under said Rule, the transaction value will have to be rejected - Impugned order set aside and Bill of Entry made by importer accepted - appeal allowed
