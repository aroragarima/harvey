Supreme Court of India

27 January 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A. Varadarajan, Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 283; (1983) 2 SCC 33; (1983) SCC (L&S) 231; AIR 1984 SC 161; 1983 (1) GLR 708; 1983 LabIC 391; 1983 (1) LLJ 284; 1983 (1) LLN 534; 1983(1) SCALE 66; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 287; 1983 (1) SLJ 268; 1983 (1) SLR 581; 1984 (56) STC 193

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship

Summary: Municipalities and Local Governments - Gujarat Panchayats (Third Amendment) Act, 1978 - Gujarat Panchayats Act, 1962, s. 203 - Constitution, arts. 14 and 311 - Local Govt. - Constitution of Panchayat Service - (A) Members of Gujarat Panchayat service - Position - (B) Constitutionality of 1978 Act - Held, members are govt. servants - Act is unconstitutional, as it offends arts. 311 and 14 and is arbitrary and unreasonable - Appeal dismissed.
