Patel Motibhai Naranbhai And Anr. v Dinubhai Motibhai Patel And Ors

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1171; (1996) 2 SCC 585; AIR 1996 SC 997; 1996 (27) ALR 232; 1996 (1) ARBLR 389; 1996 (1) CCC 120; JT 1996 (1) SC 265; 1996 RD 80; 1996(1) SCALE 294; 1996 (2) SCJ 205; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 239

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and Adr - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 14(2) - Limitation Act, 1963, arts.119 and 137 - Limitation- Suit base upon arbitral award - When party's application become time barred, it induced arbitrator to make application after six years of award - Application by arbitrator for filing of award - Held, cannot be allowed - Further held that if art. 119 does not apply to arbitrator's application, art. 137 would apply - Appeal allowed.
