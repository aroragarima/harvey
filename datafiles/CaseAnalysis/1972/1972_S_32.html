Santiranjan Das Gupta v Dasuram Murzamull

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 717; (1973) 3 SCC 463; AIR 1973 SC 48; 1973 (2) CTR 198

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Partnership Act, 1932, s. 6 - Partnership, existence of - Absence of any written contract - No record of terms and conditions of oral partnership either - No account of partnership maintained for use of partners - No written instruction to Govt. Officials who were concerned in business of alleged partnership - Held, no inference can be drawn as to existence of partnership - Appeal dismissed.
