Commissioner Of Income Tax, Madras v G.R. Karthikeyan, Coimbatore

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1092; (1993) Supp3 SCC 222; AIR 1993 SC 1671; 1993 (112) CTR 302; [1993] 201 ITR 866; JT 1993 (3) SC 174; 1993 (2) MLJ 82; 1993(2) SCALE 588; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 328; [1993] 68 TAXMAN 145; 1993 (2) TLR 637

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Defence & Security Forces; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Finance Act, 1972 - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 2(24) - Constitution of India, 1950, sch. 7, List I, Entry 82 - Whether prize money obtained in motor race included under 'income'? - Held, motor rally is a contest - Participant entered contest to win it and to win first prize - Use of skill and endurance and constitutes income - Appeal allowed.
