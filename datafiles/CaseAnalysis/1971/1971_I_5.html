Shekhawati General Traders Limited v Income Tax Officer, Company Circle I, Jaipur

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 163; (1971) 2 SCC 754; AIR 1971 SC 2389; 1972 (1) CTR 120; [1971] 82 ITR 788; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 927

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income tax Act, 1961, ss. 48 & 55(2)(i) - Income Tax - Calculation of capital loss or gain - Whether for ascertaining fair market value of shares any event on date prior to or subsequent to January 1, 1954 will be relevant?- Held, any event prior or subsequent to said date is wholly extraneous and irrelevant and cannot be taken into consideration - Appeal allowed.
