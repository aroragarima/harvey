C. Masilamani Mudaliar and Others v Idol of Sri Swaminathaswami Swaminathaswami Thirukoil and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3169; (1996) 8 SCC 525; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 250; AIR 1996 SC 1697; 1996 (1) CCC 338; 1996 (2) CLT 33; 1996 (1) HLR 359; JT 1996 (3) SC 98; 1997 (6) KarLJ 292; 1996(2) SCALE 664; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 1068; 1996 (2) Supreme 720

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Election; Women & Children

Keywords: Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956.

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, ss. 14(1) and 14(2) - Applicability - Property conferred on wife and cousin wife under will before come into force of Act - Recognition of their right to maintenance under Sastric law - Held, it cannot be construed as right acquired for first time under will - Same blossoms into absolute ownership under s. 14(1) after coming into force of Act - As s. 14(1) applies and not s. 14(2).
