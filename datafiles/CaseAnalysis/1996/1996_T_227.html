Sukhdeo v The Commissioner Amravati Division, Amravati And Another

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 400; (1996) 5 SCC 103; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1141; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 198; 1996 (73) FLR 1644; JT 1996 (5) SC 477; 1996(4) SCALE 605; 1996 (2) SLJ 3; 1996 (4) SLR 8; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 287; 1996 (2) UJ 153

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, r. 65 (1)(b) - Whether the exercise of power of Govt. in compulsory retiring the appellant was in public interest? - Held, entire record of service, specially the last period of service is to be closely scrutinised and report prepared by the controlling officer with impartiality and fairness - Controlling officer not used due diligence in making remarks - Power exercised by the officer is illegal - Appeal allowed.
