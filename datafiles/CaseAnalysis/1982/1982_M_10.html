Municipal Board, Pratabgarh and Another v Mahendra Singh Chawla and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 278; (1982) 3 SCC 331; (1983) SCC (L&S) 19; AIR 1982 SC 1493; 1982 LabIC 1783

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Rajasthan Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1958 - Industrial Disputes Act, s. 33 (2) - Directions for fixing salary and wages - Whether High Court in ignoring order of termination and not entitling salary or wages to respondent for termination period is justifiable? - Municipal Board which itself must have taken umbrage at such deviation from path of rectitude by its employee was influenced by sincerity, capacity and capability of respondent when it noted while giving a fresh appointment that respondent worked even during period of suspension and that too at behest of Municipal Board, and therefore, it was persuaded to overlook lapse on part of respondent in giving a fresh appointment and that too after seven years service at entry scale for Overseer - Held, salary to be fixed in relevant scale after reinstatement as if in termination period - Respondent to report for duty within two weeks from judgment date - Appeal allowed.
