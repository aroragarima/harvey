State of Himachal Pradesh v Mast Ram

Supreme Court of India

10 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 889; (2004) 8 SCC 660; AIR 2004 SC 5056; JT 2004 (7) SC 617; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 401; 2004(7) SCALE 637; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 269

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ballistics

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, s 302 - Culpable homicide amounting to murder - Punishment therefor - Conviction by Trial Court - HC reversing conviction - Reversal whether proper? Important evidence like the Forensic Science Laboratory Report submitted by a Govt. scientific expert not properly appreciated - HC order set aside, order of conviction passed by trial court restored - Appeal allowed.
