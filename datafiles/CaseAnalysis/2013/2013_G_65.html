Ganapath Singh Gangaram Singh Rajput and another v Gulbarga University and another

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Chandramauli Kumar Prasad, Kurian Joseph

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 724; (2014) 3 SCC 767; AIR 2014 SC 77; 2014(1) ALL MR 445; 2013 (6) AWC 6044; 2014 (140) FLR 69; 2013(13) SCALE 398; 2014 (1) SLR 174

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Summary: (1) Employment-Appointment-Eligibility-Appointment as Lecturer in M.C.A. in University-Appellant admittedly did not have post-graduate degree in M.C.A.-But had Master Degree in Mathematics-"Board of appointment" for selecting suitable candidate interviewed candidates and ultimately made recommendation for appointment of appellant-Plea of appellant is that Mathematics is one of subject in M.C.A. and, therefore, appellant possessing Masters Degree in Mathematics is eligible-Not plea of University that Masters' Degree in Mathematics is interchangeable with M.C.A.-Advertisement requires post-graduate degree in 'relevant subject'-Relevant subject would, therefore, in context of appointment to post of Lecturer, mean post-graduate degree in M.C.A.-For appointment to post of Lecturer, Masters' degree in Mathematics-Not relevant subject-Language of advertisement-Clear and explicit-Does not admit any ambiguity-Hence, it has to be given effect to-Board of appointment chosen unqualified and ineligible person for appointment-Its recommendations, therefore, illegal and invalid-University acting on such recommendations and appointing appellant as Lecturer cannot be allowed-Since appellant did not have Masters' degree in computer application-He was not entitled to be considered for appointment as Lecturer in M.C.A.-Division Bench of High Court right in setting aside his appointment.

(2) Employment-Appointment-Unqualified person cannot be appointed, whoever may be the recommendee.

(3) Employment-Appointment-Writ of mandamus-Issuance-Only in exceptional cases court can issue mandamus for appointment-Instant case not one of those case where High Court ought to have issued mandamus for appointment-High Court erred in issuing mandamus for appointment.
