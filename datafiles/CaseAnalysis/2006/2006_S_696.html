Sunila Jain v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 65; (2006) 3 SCC 321; 2006 CRLJ 1636; 2006 (1) JCC 462; JT 2006 (2) SC 612; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 146; 2006(2) SCALE 520; 2006 (4) SCJ 278; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 475

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Fema

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3(3) - Foreign Trade (Development and Regulation) Act, 1992 - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 108, 135 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 22, 22(5) - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985 - Whether a copy of bail application is required to be taken into consideration for purpose of passing an order of preventive detention in terms of the Act? - Held, If a person had been released on bail on ground that offence is bailable, it would not be necessary to bring said fact before detaining authority - Order of detention has been taken note of fact that detenu had already been released on bail - Application for bail was not a vital document copy whereof was required to be supplied to detenu - Order of detention is not vitiated - Appeal dismissed.
