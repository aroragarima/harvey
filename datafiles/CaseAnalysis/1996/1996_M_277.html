Miheer H. Mafatlal v Mafatlal Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2537; (1997) 1 SCC 579; AIR 1997 SC 506; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 792; 1996 (4) CompLJ 124; JT 1996 (8) SC 205; 1996(6) SCALE 595; [1996] Supp6 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, ss. 391 and 393 - Scheme of compromise and arrangement - Sanction by Court - Of majority - Requisite of - Scheme of amalgamation - Meeting of shareholders - Interest of Directors - Family dispute between Director of transferee-company and son of his cousin brother regarding right to hold share of transferee-company - Communication to voters - Extent of - Held, not need to communicate - Only special interest should be constituted - Sanction by Court not to be granted on mere ipse dixit of majority members - Scheme should not be unconscionable, illegal or unfair - Court, however, cannot scrutinise scheme as appellate Court.
