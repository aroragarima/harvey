Union of India and Others v Rajendra Singh and another

Supreme Court of India

12 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Yogeshwar Dayal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 781; (1993) Supp2 SCC 176; (1993) SCC (L&S) 349; AIR 1993 SC 205; 1993 (24) ATC 83; 1993 (66) FLR 102; JT 1992 (6) SC 118; 1992 LabIC 2435; 1992 (2) LLN 1079; 1992(2) SCALE 803; 1993 (2) SLJ 81; 1992 (5) SLR 857; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 937; 1993 (1) UPLBEC 60

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Railway Protection Force Act, 1957, ss. 6, 9 and 21 - Railway Protection Force Rules, 1959, r. 20 - Constitution of India, art. 311 - Dismissal - Rakshak - Order of appointing authority - Held, power of appointment not only with Chief Security Officer - Also rests with Asst. Security Officer under r. 20.
