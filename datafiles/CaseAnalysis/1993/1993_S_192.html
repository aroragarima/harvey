Khem Chand v State Of H.P.

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 109; (1994) Supp1 SCC 7; (1994) SCC (Cr) 212; AIR 1994 SC 226; 1993 (4) CCR 481; 1994 CRLJ 253; 1993 CrLR(SC) 568; JT 1993 (5) SC 310; 1993(3) SCALE 738

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act 1954, s. 16 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules 1955, r. 9(j) - Sending of analyst report to accused - Non-compliance of r. 9(j) alleged - Food Inspector deposing sending of analyst report by registered post was not cross-examined - Inference of receipt of report drawn - Held, question of mode of sending report does not assume importance in this case - Conviction of accused reduced from imprisonment to fine due to minor nature of offence.
