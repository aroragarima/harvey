Malwa Bus Service (P) Limited v State Of Punjab And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 446; (1983) SCC (Tax) 162; (1983) 3 SCC 237; AIR 1983 SC 634; 1983 (2) CompLJ 319; 1983 (37) CTR 123; 1983(1) SCALE 534; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 1009; 1983 UJ 538

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution, arts. 14, 19(1)(g), 301, 304(b) and sch.7, List 2, entry 57 - Punjab Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1924, ss. 3(1), 3A - Constitutionality of notification u/s. 3(1) D/- 19-03-198, in view of - (A) Enhancement of tax on stage carriages - (B) Different rates of tax on stage carriages and public carriers - Held, compensatory in character and arts. 19 (1) (g), 301 and 304 (b) of Constitution not violated - Not discriminatory - Petition Dismissed.
