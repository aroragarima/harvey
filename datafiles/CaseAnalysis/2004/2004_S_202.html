Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Constitution of India-Article 226-Employment-Transfer-Order showing to be on administrative grounds-Unless transfer order shown to be mala fide or in violation of statutory provisions-Normally, no interference called for-Whether High Court justified in interfering with transfer order on proceeding on basis that it was connected with departmental proceedings and was punitive passed without awaiting decision in said proceedings?-Held, ânoâ-No iota of material to arrive at that conclusion-No mala fides attributable as order purely on administrative grounds and in public interest-Judgment of High Court indefeasible and set aside.
