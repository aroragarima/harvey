H. Sadashiv Patil v D. Vithal Teke And Others/ Y. Ashok Patil And Others

Supreme Court of India

31 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2832; (2000) 8 SCC 82; AIR 2000 SC 3044; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 46; JT 2000 (9) SC 604; 2000(6) SCALE 209; 2000 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 829

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Maharashtra Local Authority Members' Disqualification Act, 1986, Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samities Act, 1961

Summary: Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1987, s. 3(1)(b) - Whip/Direction - Issuance of - Person or authority authorised to issue whip - Absence of resolution - Held, need not be a Councillor or member of Municipal Party, voting contrary to direction by party - Violation thereof would not attract disqualification.
