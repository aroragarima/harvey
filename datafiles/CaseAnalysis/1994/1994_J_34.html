Janki Devi Smt. and Another v G. C. Jain

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1746; (1994) 5 SCC 337B

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Registration of Societies Act - Whether appellant is in juridical possession of the premises? - To constitute subletting there must be parting of the legal possession i. e. possession with the right to include and also right to exclude others - Held, she is merely the Secy., and cannot either include or exclude others in relation to possession - In a partnership nobody can predict as to what exactly is the share of a partner and each partner owns the entire partnership until dissolution - Caselaw Helper Girdharbhai distinguishable - Appeal dismissed.
