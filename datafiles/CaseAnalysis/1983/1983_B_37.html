Workmen Of The Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (Refiningdi v Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. And Another

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 28; (1983) 4 SCC 470; (1983) SCC (L&S) 536; AIR 1984 SC 356; 1983 CRLJ 1457; 1983 (47) FLR 499; 1984 LabIC 8; 1984 (1) LLJ 35; 1984 (1) LLN 76; 1983(2) SCALE 736; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 251; 1984 (1) SLJ 198

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 6 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Consideration for fixation of age of superannuation - Trend in particular area is most important - Clerical staff of BPCL, Refinery Division - Tribunal raising retirement age to 58, at par with clerks in Marketing Division without considering their having benefit of pension scheme - Held, Supreme Court raised retirement age of clerks in refinery division to 60 years - Appeal dismissed.
