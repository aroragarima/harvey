Commissioner of Sales Tax, U. P., Lucknow v Super Cotton Bowl Refilling Works and another

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 431; (1989) 1 SCC 643; AIR 1989 SC 922; 1989 (40) E.L.T. 247; JT 1989 (1) SC 458; 1989(1) SCALE 257; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 421; 1989 (73) STC 61; [1989] 43 TAXMAN 91

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Trade Tax Act, 1948

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, ss. 10, 11 and 35 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Indirect Tax Decision by Commr. u/s. 35 Not administrative but is quasi-judicial final and subject only to appeal before tribunal - Further revision to High Court, not maintainable revision u/s. 11 of Act ( In special cases involving question of law) - Held, Sales Tax - Appeal dismissed.
