State Of U.P.And Another v Malik Zarid Khalid

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28468; (1988) 1 SCC 145; AIR 1988 SC 132; 1989 (15) ALR 219; 1988 (1) AWC 244; JT 1987 (4) SC 578; 1988 (2) RCJ 275; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 665; 1987(2) SCALE 1262; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 948; 1988 (1) UJ 32

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 2(1)(a), 3(o) and 21(8) - Expression 'Public buildings' include buildings in which Govt. is only tenant - Landlord's remedy against Govt. to recover possession - Whether available under 1972 Act? - Held, would lie under general law and not under Act of 1972 - Appeal dismissed.
