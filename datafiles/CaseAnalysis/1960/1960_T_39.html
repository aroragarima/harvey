Kantilal Manilal and Others v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 396; AIR 1961 SC 1038; [1961] 41 ITR 275; 1961 (1) MLJ(SC) 139; 1961 (1) SCJ 506; [1961] 2 S.C.R. 584

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 2(6 A) - Whether on facts and circumstances of case, distribution of right to apply for shares of Bank of India by Navjivan Mills Ltd, amounted to a distribution of 'dividend'? - Held, definition of dividend u/s. 2(6 A) of Income Tax Act, includes its ordinary meaning - Dividend need not be in money - Distribution of right to purchase certain Bank shares at half market rate amongst shareholders, amounts to distribution of dividend - Appeal Dismissed.
