Â Makhan Singh Tarsikka v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K. Subba Rao, Raghubar Dayal, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 312; AIR 1964 SC 1120; 1964 CRLJ 217; [1964] 4 S.C.R. 932

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Defence & Security Forces; Land & Property

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Defence of India Rules, 1962, r. 30 (1) (b) - Whether order of detention can be made against a person who is in Jail custody? - Service of a detention order on a person who is already in jail virtually seeks to effectuate what may be called a double detention' and such double detention is not intended either by s. 3 (1) (a) or by r. 30 (1) (b) - Plea of malafides must always be made by proper pleadings at trial stage - Held, that service of order is invalid and is outside scope of r. (30) (1) (b) of Rules - Appeal allowed.
