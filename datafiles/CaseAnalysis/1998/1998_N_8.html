State Of Andhra Pradesh v N. Radhakishan

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 757; (1998) 4 SCC 154; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1044; AIR 1998 SC 1833; JT 1998 (3) SC 123; 1998 (2) LLN 452; 1998(2) SCALE 672; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 693; 1998 (3) SLJ 162; 1998 (2) SLR 786; 1998 (4) SLT 273; 1998 (3) Supreme 584

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Civil Services (CCA) Rules, 1991

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts.16, 309 and 311 - AP Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1963, r. 19 - AP Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1991, rr. 20 and 45 - Disciplinary proceedings - Consideration of facts is required - Delay - Not due to delinquent - Charge memo issued 10 years ago , under 1963 Rules - Inquiry Officer was appointed - No progress - Departmental records not examined even when case depend on them - If proceedings vitiated in the grounds of - Fresh memo issued under 1991 Rules on same charges without cancelling earlier memo - Held, factors to be considered, stated - Charge memo liable to be quashed - Disciplinary proceedings not vitiated - Appeal dismissed.
