Thapar Institute Of Engineering Andtechnology, Patiala (Deem v Abhinav Taneja And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, K. Jagannatha Shetty, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 833; (1990) 3 SCC 468; AIR 1990 SC 1222; JT 1990 (3) SC 72; 1990 SCALE 37; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 394; 1990 (1) UJ 699; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 1056

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Education - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Admission - Extraordinary jurisdiction - Exercise of - Admission to course in certain institutes on basis of merit list prepared on basis of combined entrance test by University - High Court directing admission of students in question even after pointing out that students placed higher in merit list were there - Exercise leads to injustice - More meritorious students, however, not in position to avail of seats due to lapse of time - Whether interference required - Held, no interference - Appeal dismissed.
