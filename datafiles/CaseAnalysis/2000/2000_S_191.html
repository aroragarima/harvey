Deo Narain Choudhury v Shree Narain Choudhary

Supreme Court of India

31 October 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2562; (2000) 8 SCC 626; 2000 (41) ALR 716; 2001 (1) ARBLR 296; JT 2000 (Supp2) SC 260; 2001 (1) MLJ(SC) 130; 2001 (1) RCR(Civil) 65; [2000] Supp4 S.C.R. 307

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Limitation Act, 1963 - Whether, after award had been made and filed, filling of a caveat in court of Sub-Judge, praying that before any order is passed notice be issued to Caveator and he be heard, running of 30 day limitations period commenced u/art. 119 of Limitation Act? - Held, mere filing of a caveat did not start period of limitation - By caveat respondent was merely asking court to give it notice - Caveat did not absolve court from its statutory duty to give notice u/s. 14(2) of Arbitration Act, 1940 - Appeal dismissed.
