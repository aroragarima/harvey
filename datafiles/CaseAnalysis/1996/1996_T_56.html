Annamalai Club v Government of T.N. and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1766; (1997) 3 SCC 169; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 227; AIR 1997 SC 3650; AIR 1997 SCW 1569; 1996(8) SCALE 428; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 652; 1997 (1) Supreme 145

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Summary: Government Grants Act, 1895, s. 3 - Tamil Nadu Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1976, ss. 4 and 5 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Govt. grants - Termination in accordance to grant itself - Resumption of possession - Unilaterally without taking recourse to law - Held, not legal - Notice under Eviction Act and appointment of Estate Officer not necessary as possession was already resumed - Recourse to art. 226 also not proper remedy to establish title.
