Priyanka Estates International Private Limited v State of Assam and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Deepak Verma, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1845; (2010) 2 SCC 27; AIR 2010 SC 1030; 2010 (1) MLJ(SC) 1005; 2009(14) SCALE 353; [2009] 16 S.C.R. 80

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, Guwahati Municipal Corporation Act, 1971, Guwahati Metropolitan Development Authority Act

Summary: Land & Property - Illegal construction - Appellant sought permission from Guwahati Metropolitan Development Authority (GMDA) to purchase land - GDMA granted permission - Appellant sought sanction from Guwahati Metropolitan Corporation (GMC) for constructing a building with 5 floors - GMC granted sanction - Appellant applied for further construction of floors up to 8th floor - GMC refused permission - Appellants filed appeal before Standing Appellate Committee (SAC) of GMC - SAC allowed appeal, however, Municipal Commissioner of GMC had not given formal accord - Appellant continued the constructions work - GDMA issued notice of demolition - Appellant challenged the notice before HC - HC dismissed the appeal - Held, even after passing of the order by SAC in appeal, there was no further sanction by the Municipal Commissioner or by Chief Executive Officer of GDMA granting permission to raise the height of the building up to 8th floor - Construction of the building beyond 5th floors was not only illegal, unauthorized and without any sanction or approval of plans but was also against the spirit of Appellate Order of SAC - Therefore, direction issued to GDMA to demolish the floors constructed by appellants beyond 5th floor, however, time granted to the occupiers of the flats to vacate the same - Appeals dismissed.
