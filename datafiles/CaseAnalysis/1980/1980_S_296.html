Suraj Prakash Bhasin v Smt. Raj Rani Bhasin and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 475; (1981) 3 SCC 652; AIR 1981 SC 485; 1980 UJ 593

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 17 - (A) Amendment of pleadings - Suit for partition and share in cinema theatre - Amendment to plaint sought , in order to include relief of dissolution of partnership and rendition of accounts - Whether totally alters nature of action and legality of permission of court to amend - (B) Gross delay in seeking amendment - Effect - Amendment held did not totally alter nature of action and discretion exercised by Court in allowing amendment was not illegal - Court, while allowing amendments, should order heavy costs - Appeal Dismissed.
