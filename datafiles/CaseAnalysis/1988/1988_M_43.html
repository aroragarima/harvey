Government of Andhra Pradesh v Messrs Anabeshahi Wine and Distilleries Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

N. D. Ojha, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 371; (1988) 2 SCC 25; AIR 1988 SC 771; 1988 CRLR 213; 1988 (16) ECC 176; JT 1988 (1) SC 358; 1988(1) SCALE 337

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution- Constitution of India, 1950 - A.P. Excise Act, 1968, ss.28, 17 - A.P. Distillery Rules, 1970, r.15 - Distillery - Licensee manufacturing wine - Payment of salaries etc. of Excise staff stationed at his factory - Licensee is liable for - Demand for establishment charges - Nature of - Held, it is neither tax nor fee - It is consideration for parting with privilege of government to manufacture wine - Appeal allowed.
