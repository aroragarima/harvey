Collector of Central Excise v Ram Body Builders

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1284; (1997) 8 SCC 482; 1998 (77) ECR 437; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 442; JT 1998 (4) SC 425; 1997 (22) RLT 441

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff, headings nos. 87.02, 87.04 and 87.07 - Fabricate of bodies for buses or trucks on Chassis supplied by customers - Whether bodies so manufactured are classifiable under heading no. 87.07 or under headings nos. 87.02 and 87.04 of Central Excise Tariff? - Held, bodies which are built on chassis supplied by customers would fall under heading no. 87.07 of Central Excise Tariff and would be entitled to exemption from excise duty under notification no. 175 of 1986 D/- 01-03-1986 - Appeals dismissed.
