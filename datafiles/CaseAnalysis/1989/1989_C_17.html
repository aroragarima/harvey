Changki Village Through Tinnunokcha Ao and Others v Tibungba Ao and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 146; (1989) Supp2 SCC 125; AIR 1990 SC 73; JT 1989 (4) SC 62; 1990 (1) ShimLC 238; 1990 (1) UJ 4

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Indian Independence Act, 1947, Nagaland Hills District Rules

Summary: Civil Procedure - Rules for Administration of Justice and Police in Nagaland Hills District,1937, r. 30 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 1, O. 22, rr. 3 and 4 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Whether civil disputes in Nagaland that are governed by 1937, rules, provisions of CPC, 1908 would applicable? - Held, CPC, 1908 not applicable in all force and vigour - Appeal dismissed.
