Communist Party of India (M) v Bharat Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3153; (1998) 1 SCC 201; AIR 1998 SC 184; 1998 (1) MLJ 99; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 99; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 131

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 19 - Issue relating to call or enforce 'Bandh' which interfere with exercise of fundamental rights is under judicial consideration - Held, there is no right to call or enforce 'bandh' 'which interfere with exercise of fundamental freedoms of other citizens, in addition to causing national loss in many ways - Appeal dismissed.
