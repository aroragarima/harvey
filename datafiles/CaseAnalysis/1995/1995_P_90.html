Vidya Devi Alias Vidya Vati (Dead) By Lrs v Prem Prakash and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1300; (1995) 4 SCC 496; AIR 1995 SC 1789; JT 1995 (4) SC 607; 1995 (2) RentLR 9; 1995(3) SCALE 580; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 316

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Delhi Land Reforms Act, 1954, s. 186 - Limitation Act, 1908 - No Limitation prescribed for partition suit for holding by co-bhumidhar - Other co-bhumidhar defended that he acquired title by adverse possession - Reference to Civil Court, if needed - Held, question of title requiring reference to Civil Court does not arise - Appeal allowed.
