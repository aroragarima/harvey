Mahesh Balmiki Alias Munna v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 673; (2000) SCC (Cr) 178; (2000) 1 SCC 319; AIR 1999 SC 3338; AIR 1999 SCW 3334; 1999 (3) CCR 229; 1999 (4) Crimes 109; 1999 CRLJ 4301; 1999 (2) JCC 444; JT 1999 (6) SC 279; 1999 (4) RCR(Criminal) 91; 1999(5) SCALE 237; 1999 (7) SLT 391; 1999 (7) Supreme 468

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 302, Exception 4 - Single Blow on deceased - Accused calling deceased for receiving watch at particular place - Gave single fatal blow with knife while other three co-accused holding him - Held, cannot be said it was without premeditation - Exception 4 to s. 300 not applies in facts and circumstances.
