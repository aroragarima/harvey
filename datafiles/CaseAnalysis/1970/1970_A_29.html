Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation v K. Venkataramireddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 557; (1971) 3 SCC 803; 1970 UJ 408

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 57 and 62 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Grant of temporary permits for notified routes - Held, in application, which was filed by appellant for grant of temporary permits no purpose has been indicated against item No. 4 which requires purposes to be indicted for which stage carriage permit is required -In absence of any purpose or reason for which temporary permits were asked for Regional Transport Authority should have dismissed application in limine because a temporary permit can be granted only if permit is required for purposes or reasons mentioned from (a) to (d) in s. 62 of Act - Appeal dismissed.
