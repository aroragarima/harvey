Smt. Nirmala R. Bafna and another v Khandesh Spinning and Weaving Mills Company Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 343; (1992) 2 SCC 322; AIR 1993 SC 1380; 1994 (1) Bom.C.R. 627; [1992] 74 Comp Cas 1; 1992 (2) CompLJ 9; JT 1992 (4) SC 245; 1992 (1) RCJ 428; 1992 (1) RCR(Rent) 479; 1992 (2) RentLR 579; 1992(1) SCALE 485; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 985

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947

Summary: Rent Control - Corporate - Companies Act,1956, ss. 468 and 446(3) - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 13 - Liquidation of tenant company do not affect the sub tenant's right to protection where sub-tenancy is with landlord's consent - High Court issued unwarranted directions to the liquidator regarding possession and rent - Right of sub-tenant was not adjudicated upon in rent control proceedings - (A) Whether the sub-tenant's claim of protection unde 1947 Act, can be rejected - (B) Whether 'Tenancy rights' of liquidated Company are assets for liquidation proceedings - (C) Whether liquidator's plea that he requires the premises for storing books and taking steps for revival of liquidated Company - is relevant for liquidator's continued possession - Held, sub-tenant's claim can not be rejected - 'Tenancy rights' of liquidated Company may not be assets for liquidation proceeding - Liquidator's plea considered to be relevant - Appeal dismissed.
