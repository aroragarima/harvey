Kharkan and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, K.C. Das Gupta, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 317; AIR 1965 SC 83; 1965 CRLJ 116; [1964] 4 S.C.R. 673

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal -Practice & Procedure - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 40, 41, 42 and 43 A -Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 147 and 149 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 236, 237 and 403 - Autrefois acquit - Whether acquittal in prior case operates as a bar to conviction in present case? - A plea of autrefois acquit u/s. 403 of CrPC, 1973 arises when a person is tried again for same offence or on same facts for any other offence for which a different charge from one made against him might have been made - Provision is not applicable to present facts because two offences were distinct and spaced slightly by time and place - Held, acquittal in second case does not operate as a bar to conviction in present case - Appeal dismissed.
