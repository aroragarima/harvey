Bhagat Singh and Others v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K. Venkataswami, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2698; (1996) 8 SCC 649; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1089; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 285; AIR 1996 SC 1705; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 414; 1996(2) SCALE 851; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 433; 1996 (2) SLR 335; 1996 (3) Supreme 68; 1996 (1) UJ 848

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, arts. 14 and 309 - Punjab Government National Emergency (Concession) Rules, 1965, r. 2 - Military service - Amended definition - Curtailed definition - Exclusion of those who had been enrolled or commissioned before proclamation of Emergency and had served during its operation - Held, not discriminatory - Persons joining armed forces as career prior to Emergency and those joining during Emergency - Form different intelligible classes.
