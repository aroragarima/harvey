State Of Orissa & Ors v Md. Illiyas

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 832; (2006) 1 SCC 275; AIR 2006 SC 258; 2006 (1) JhrCR(SC) 314; JT 2005 (10) SC 64; 2005(9) SCALE 466; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 395

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Orissa Gram Panchayat Act, 1964

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Orissa Gram Panchayat Act, 1964, ss. 115(1), 115(2) - Suspension of Sarpanch of Neulapur Gram Panchayat - HC held that pre-requisites for taking such action u/s. 115(1) not satisfied and directed suspension of respondent was illegal - Appeal against - Held, Collector, on basis of materials contained in report of Sub-Collector has opined that these are wilful acts - HC has completely lost sight of these relevant facts - Unless there is total absence of material and/ or non-application of mind courts should not interfere - Appeal allowed.
