Commissioner of Income Tax v Hero Cycles Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2849; (1997) 8 SCC 502; AIR 1998 SC 1555; 1997 (142) CTR 122; [1997] 228 ITR 463; JT 1997 (7) SC 726; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 612; [1997] 94 TAXMAN 271; 1998 (2) TLR 283

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 154, 44 and 35 B - Allowability of weighted deduction u/s. 35 B of Act - Allowed by tribunal without considering facts and provisions provided under sub-cl. of s. 35 B - Expenditure with regard to salary, Director's remuneration, rent, printing and stationery, postage and telegrams etc. - Not proved to be wholly or exclusively incurred for any of purposes mentioned in s. 35 B - Not allowable for weighted deduction u/s. 35-B - Matter remanded back to tribunal.
