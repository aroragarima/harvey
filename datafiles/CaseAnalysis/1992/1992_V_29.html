State Of Maharashtra v Vikas Sahebrao Roundale And Ors

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.N. Ray, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 709; (1992) 4 SCC 435; AIR 1992 SC 1926; AIR 1992 SCW 2182; 1993 (1) Bom.C.R. 437; JT 1992 (5) SC 175; 1992(2) SCALE 163; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 792; 1992 (5) SLR 74; 1992 (2) UJ 680

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950 arts. 32, 51 A and 226 - Students of unauthorised D. Ed Colleges - Seeking direction from Court to appear for examination - Not entitled to direction from Court to permit them - Held, not permissible, to sit in examination or to accommodate them in recognised institution to pursue further study - Appeal allowed.
