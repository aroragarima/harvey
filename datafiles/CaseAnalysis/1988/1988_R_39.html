State Of Haryana v Ram Kishan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 784; (1988) 3 SCC 416; AIR 1988 SC 1301; 1988 (36) DLT 36; JT 1988 (2) SC 444; 1988(1) SCALE 889; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 1015; 1988 (2) UJ 252

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Mines and Minerals - Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, s. 4A - Constitution, art. 226 - Mining lease - Premature termination - Ground, Govt. company or Corporation has equipped itself to undertake the work - (A) Who is the Final Authority on matter of major minerals Lease - (B) Whether suficient ground for termination - Held, Central Govt. and not State Govt. is final deciding authority - Not ground for premature termination of existing leases, opportunity of hearing must be given to affected persons - Appeal dismissed.
