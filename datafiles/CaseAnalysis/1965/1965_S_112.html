Baijnath Gupta and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 455; AIR 1966 SC 220; 1966 CRLJ 179; [1966] 1 S.C.R. 210

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, s. 197 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 197 and 477A r/w s. 109 - Sanction for prosecution when not necessary - Held, sanction for prosecution held not necessary when act of criminal misappropriation was not committed while being in discharge of duties - Conviction u/s. 477A r/w s. 109 cannot be sustained as sanction to start proceedings had not been duly obtained u/s. 197 of CrPC, 1898 - Appeals partly allowed.
