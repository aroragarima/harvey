Canara Exports v State of Karnataka. (And Others Appeals)

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 125; (1986) 3 SCC 527; AIR 1987 SC 747; 1987 (66) STC 153

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Taxability - Legality - Purchase of shrimps, prawns and lobsters made by appellants for the purpose of fulfilling the existing contracts for export shall not be liable to be included in the taxable turnover of the appellants. Sterling Foods v. State of Karnataka, 1986 Indlaw SC 177, followed. Appeals allowed.
