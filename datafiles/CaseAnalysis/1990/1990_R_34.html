Om Prakash and Others v Ram Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 600; (1991) 1 SCC 441; AIR 1991 SC 409; JT 1991 (5) SC 51; 1991 (1) RCJ 1; 1991 (1) RCR(Rent) 354; 1991 (1) RentLR 186; 1990(2) SCALE 1161B; 1991 (1) UJ 89

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Rent Control - Civil Procedure - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, s. 13(2) - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 7, r. 7 - Eviction proceedings instituted by landlord against another person who is not real tenant, suppressing real facts - Impleadment of real tenant - Not under obligation to tender arrears of rent in absence of such allegation against him - Whether rejection of application of eviction is justified? - Held, justified - Appeal dismissed.
