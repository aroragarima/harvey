Jagbir Singh And Ors. v General Manager, Punjab Roadways And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 October 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 210; (1986) 4 SCC 431; (1986) SCC (Cr) 495; 1987 (2) ACC 1; 1987 (1) ACJ 15; AIR 1987 SC 70; 1986 CRLR 566; 1986 CrLR(SC) 566; JT 1986 SC 699; 1986(2) SCALE 681; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 1095; 1987 (1) UJ 11

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss.110B, 110CC - High Court reducing the compensation determined by Tribunal on basis of material on record - Validity - Held, not proper , so order of Tribunal restored and 12% interest granted - Order accordingly.
