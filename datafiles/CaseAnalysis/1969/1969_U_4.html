Union Of India & Ors. v M/S. Bhim Sen Walaiti Ram

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 90; (1969) 3 SCC 146; AIR 1971 SC 2295; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 594

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Summary: Delhi Liquor License Rules, 1976, r. 5.34, cl. 21 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 7 - Auction sale of liquor shop - Conditions of auction - Final bid subject to confirmation of Chief Commissioner - Resale ordered without the bid having be confirmation - Held, auction purchaser whose bid is provisionally accepted not liable for any shortfall on reauction - Appeal Allowed.
