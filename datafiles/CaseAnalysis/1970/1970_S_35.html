State of Bihar and Others v Shiva Bhikshuk Mishra

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 32; (1970) 2 SCC 871; AIR 1971 SC 1011; 1971 LabIC 724; 1970 (2) LLJ 440; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 191; 1970 SLR 863

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Test for applicability of art. 226 - Impugned order, if required to be necessarily refer to stigma attributable to conduct of Govermnent servant - Held, need not necessarily refer to stigma, circumstances attendant on impugned order are relevant - Appeal Dismissed.
