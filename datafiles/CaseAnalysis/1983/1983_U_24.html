A. Janardhana v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 277; (1983) SCC (L&S) 467; (1983) 3 SCC 601; AIR 1983 SC 769; 1983 LabIC 849; 1983 (2) LLJ 175; 1983 (2) LLN 310; 1983(1) SCALE 443; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 936; 1983 (1) SLJ 564; 1983 (2) SLR 113

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 16, 14, 226 and 308 Proviso - Military Engineering Services, Class I (Recruitment, Promotion and Seniority) Rules, 1951, rr. 3 and 4 - Service Rules - (A) Promotee, AEE of 1962 finding place in seniority list of 1963 and 1967 - List as per relaxation garanted in quota rules - Exxclusion from 1974 list - Validity of 1974 List - (B) Necessary parties - Maintainability of writ petition challenging seniority - Relief sought only against UOI and the concerned Ministry - Held, list invalid as being violative of arts. 14 and 16 - Petition could not be dismissed merely on the ground of absence of direct recruits before the Courts - Appeal allowed.
