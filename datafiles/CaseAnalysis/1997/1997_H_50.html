Haryana State Electricity Board v K.C. Gambhir

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1152; (1997) 7 SCC 85; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1617; AIR 1997 SC 2403; JT 1997 (4) SC 725; 1997 LabIC 2341; 1997 (2) LLJ 622; 1997 (3) LLN 287; 1997(3) SCALE 654; 1997 (2) SCJ 191; 1997 (4) Supreme 225

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Compulsory retirement - Validity of - Delinquent was involved in shortage of material and committed financial irregularity - Action was initiated against him - Order of compulsorily retiring him from service passed after taking into consideration his representation against adverse remark and after completion of departmental inquiry - Held, mere fact that only nine months had remained for his superannuation cannot be a ground to infer malice - Compulsory retirement valid - Appeal allowed.
