Supreme Court of India

20 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research, Northern India Canal and Drainage Act, 1873

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(j) - Northern India Canal And Drainage Act, 1873 - Whether irrigation department of Govt. is 'Industry' u/s. 2(j) of Act? - Held, its functions, when subjected to dominant nature test, clearly come within ambit of 'Industry' - Appeal allowed.
