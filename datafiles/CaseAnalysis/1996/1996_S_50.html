Suvaran Rajaram Bandekar and Others v Narayan R. Bandekar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2095; (1996) 10 SCC 255; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 517; 1997 (1) AWC 107; JT 1996 (8) SC 290; 1996(7) SCALE 20; 1998 (Supp) RD 365; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 439; 1996 (7) Supreme 232

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Power of court to reschedule payment of amounts under consent decree - Held, in a consent decree on compromise, court would be loath to interfere with terms thereof by way of modification unless both parties give consent thereto - Appeal dismissed.
