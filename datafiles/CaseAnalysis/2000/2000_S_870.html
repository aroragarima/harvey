State of Himachal Pradesh v Surinder Mohan and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 345; (2000) 2 SCC 396; (2000) SCC (Cr) 400; AIR 2000 SC 1862; 2000 (1) CCR 187; 2000 (1) Crimes 218; 2000 CRLJ 1429; 2000 (1) JCC 320; JT 2000 (1) SC 593; 2000 (1) RCR(Criminal) 618; 2000(1) SCALE 450; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 659; 2000 (2) SLT 61; 2000 (1) Supreme 466

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Approver, Committal Proceedings

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 306(4)(a) and 465(2) - Granting pardon to approver - Not cross examined by other accused - Validity - Objection raised first time only in final argument - Held, when no failure of justice or prejudice appears because of said omission - Objection not permissible.
