Zakarius Lakra and others v Union of India and another

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 106; (2005) 3 SCC 161; (2005) SCC (Cr) 667; AIR 2005 SC 1560; 2005 CRLJ 1716; 2005 (117) DLT 675; JT 2005 (5) SC 536; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 18; 2005(2) SCALE 166

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Constitution - Criminal - Curative petition - Death sentence - Death sentence confirmed by SC - Petitioners prayed for quashing death sentence on ground that appellant was a juvenile on the date of commission of offence - Held, in view of decision in Rupa Ashok Hurra vs Ashok Hurra (2002) 4 SCC 388) this petition under art 32 is not maintainable - Appropriate remedy is only to file a curative petition as per procedure indicated by this Court in the decision - Petition dismissed - Without prejudice to the remedy left open to the petitioners to file curative petition.
