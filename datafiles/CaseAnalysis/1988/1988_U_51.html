P And T Scheduled Caste/Tribe Employees’ Welfareassociation (R v Union Of Indla And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 579; (1988) 4 SCC 147; (1988) SCC (L&S) 919; AIR 1989 SC 139; 1988 (8) ATC 154; 1988 (2) CCC 679; JT 1988 (3) SC 486; 1989 LabIC 1045; 1989 (1) LLJ 76; 1988 (2) LLN 914; 1988(2) SCALE 662; 1989 (1) SLJ 122A; 1988 (4) SLR 361; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 623; 1988 (2) UJ 727

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 15, 16, 29, 330, 332 and 335 - Promotion - New policy - Reservation of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes - Cancellation of - Constitutionality - Held, amounts to violation of equality clause - Petition allowed.
