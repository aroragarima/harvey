Managing Committee, Khalsa Middle School and Another v Mohinder Kaur Smt. and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 549; (1993) Supp4 SCC 26; (1994) SCC (L&S) 24; JT 1993 (4) SC 604

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Education; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Consolidated Fund Of India, Eligibility - Merit

Summary: Service - Delhi School Education Act, 1973, s. 8 - Delhi School Education Rules, 1973, r. 120 - Whether appellant institution is a minority institution and whether appellant was required to comply with provisions of Delhi School Education Act and Delhi School Education Rules before passing order of termination of respondent? - Held, school which was a minority institution till amendment of Rules and Regulations in 1963 and had ceased to be a minority institution as a result of amendment in 1963 regained its status as a minority institution after July 1, 1979, when rules and regulations were amended and original position was restored - In view of restoration of minority character of institution provisions of Education Act and Education Rules ceased to be applicable to institution after July 1, 1979 - Appeal allowed.
