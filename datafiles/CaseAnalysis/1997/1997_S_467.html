State of Haryana v Surinder Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 378; (1997) 3 SCC 633; (1997) SCC (L&S) 844; AIR 1997 SC 2129; 1997 (3) AWC 1492; 1997 (2) ESC 1089; 1997 (76) FLR 305; JT 1997 (4) SC 82; 1997 LabIC 2096; 1998 (2) LLJ 516; 1997 (1) LLN 1027; 1997(3) SCALE 108; 1997 (1) SCJ 601; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 917; 1997 (2) SLJ 203; 1997 (2) SLR 40; 1997 (3) Supreme 406

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 39(d) - Whether workers appointed as daily wagers on contract basis entitled to same pay scale as regular employee? - Held, appointment of these workers were not made in accordance with rules applicable and therefore they are not entitled to same pay scale - Petition dismissed.
