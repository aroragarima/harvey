Vasu Dev Singh & Ors. v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 743; (2006) 12 SCC 753; 2006 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 3453; 2006 (144) PLR 802; 2006 (4) RCR(Civil) 849; 2006 (2) RCR(Rent) 561; 2006(11) SCALE 108; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 535

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent & Eviction) Control Act, 1960, East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act (Extension To Chandigarh) Act, 1974, Punjab Reorganization Act, 1966, Punjab Rent Act, 1995, Madras (Lease & Rent Control) Act, 1946, Jammu and Kashmir Rent Control Act

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 3 - Punjab Reorganization Act, 1966 - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act (Extension To Chandigarh) Act, 1974, s. 1 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 12, 14, 19(1)(a), 239, 245, 246 - Rent Control Act - Punjab Rent Act, 1995, s. 3 - Notification issued - Petition questioning vires of s.3 as also validity of said notification dismissed - Appeal against - Whether exemption could be granted by an executive order issued u/s.3 or only by way of an amendment? - Held, legislative objective and policy indisputably must be considered having regard to preamble and other core provisions of Act - s.3 although is a part of Act, but same cannot be said to contain an in- Built policy so as to empower Administrator to do all such things which can be done by legislature itself - Appeals allowed.
