S. Thangappan v P. Padmavathy

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 667; (1999) 7 SCC 474; AIR 1999 SC 3584; JT 1999 (6) SC 464; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 12; 1999(5) SCALE 283; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 715

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Tamil Nadu Buildings Lease and Rent Control Act, 1960, s. 14(1)(b) - Eviction - Willful default - Title denied - Tenant inducted into premises by landlord - Payment of rent by tenant to landlord - Subsequently, on knowing that there was paramount title holder, a devasthanam, tenant paying rent to it and not to his landlord without even being informed by such devasthanam that it was owner - Held, default was willful - Denial of title against landlord from very inception - Forbidden by s. 116 of Evidence Act.
