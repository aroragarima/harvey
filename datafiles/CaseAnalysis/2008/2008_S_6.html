Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Appeals Disposed Of

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Quantum of compensation payable for the lands acquired from Villages Ratgal, Sunderpur and Palwal - Held, lands are abutting the Kurukshetra town and can easily be said to be a part and parcel of the Kurukshetra town, having a great potential - Division Bench should have taken into consideration both the Annexures A-1 and A-2 and then should have decided the question of market price - Directed remand of the matters to the Division Bench to reconsider the matter - Appeals disposed of.
