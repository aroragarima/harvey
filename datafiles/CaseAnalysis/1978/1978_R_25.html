Ram Parkash Sharma v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

18 April 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 315; (1978) SCC (Cr) 309; (1978) 2 SCC 491; AIR 1978 SC 1282; 1978 CRLJ 1120; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 691; 1978 (2) ShimLC 405

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s.457 - Release of property from whom property has been recovered - Case in suspicion - Investigation not over - Charge-sheet not laid - Held, question of release decided on its own merits in each case - Discretion of court exercised after due consideration of interests of justice - Including prospective necessity of production of seized articles at time of trial - If release of property seized will, in any manner, affect or prejudice course of justice at time of trial, it will be a wise discretion to reject claim for return - Appeal allowed.
