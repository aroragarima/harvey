Â Workmen Of Meenakshi Mills Limited Etc. Etc. v Meenakshi Mills Ltd. And Anr. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, K. Jayachandra Reddy, R.C. Patnaik, G.N. Ray, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 486; (1992) 3 SCC 336; (1992) SCC (L&S) 679; AIR 1994 SC 2696; AIR 1992 SCW 1378; 1992 (2) CompLJ 273; 1992 (65) FLR 1; JT 1992 (3) SC 446; 1995 LabIC 1; 1992 (2) LLJ 294; 1992 (1) LLN 1055; 1992(1) SCALE 1248; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 409; 1992 (4) SLR 315

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1984, Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1982, Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1976, Industrial Disputes (Central) ) Rules 1957, Punjab Special Powers (Press) Act, 1956

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25 N - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 19(1)(g), 226 and 14 - Right of employer - To close down a business as an integral part of right to carry on any business - Held, not amounts to unreasonable restriction - Not unconstitutional.
