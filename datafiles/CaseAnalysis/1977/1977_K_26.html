Kunwar Nripendra Bahadur Singh v Jai Ram Verma And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 91; (1977) 4 SCC 153; AIR 1977 SC 1992; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 208; 1977 UJ 528

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1950, s. 27 - Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 2(1)(e), 62 and 100(1)(d)(iii) - Election - To State Legislative Council in local authorities constituency - Some of the members ceasing to be members of Kshetra Samitis - Still continued in electoral rolls and participated in election - Effect - Held, participation of such members in election did not render election illegal - Appeal Allowed.
