Avinder Singh Etc v State Of Punjab & Anr. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 87; (1979) 1 SCC 137; AIR 1979 SC 321; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 845

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Punjab Municipal Corporation Act, 1976

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Punjab Municipal Corporation Act, 1976, ss. 90, 90(4) and 90(5) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14, 19, 265 and 245 - Double taxation - Levy of tax on Indian made Foreign Liquor at flat rate, if discriminatory and amounts to excesive delegation of legislative powers, etc - Held, not prohibited - Not discriminatory - Power u/s. 90 (4) is permissible only if tax is new and not already imposed - None of canons governing delegation of legislative power have been breached - Petition dismissed.
