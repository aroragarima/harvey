Union of India and Others v Kannadapara S.O. and Kannadigara and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 2406; (2002) 10 SCC 226; JT 2001 (10) SC 570

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Administrative - Railways Act, 1989 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Whether High Court erred in judgment on ground that decision of Central Govt. to locate HQ of SW Railways at Hubli instead of Bangalore was vitiated on account of legal mala fides? - Held, decision of Central Govt. is an administrative decision and subsequently altered by same authority and cannot lead to conclusion that there has been legal mala fides - Further held, it is not function of court to decide location of HQ and it is an administrative function and HC decision set aside as incorrect - Writ petition dismissed.
