St. John’S Teacher Training Institute(For Women). Madurai Et v State Of Tamil Nadu And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 June 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1183; (1993) 3 SCC 595; AIR 1994 SC 43; AIR 1993 SCW 3089; JT 1993 (4) SC 78; 1993(3) SCALE 10; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 985; 1993 (4) SLR 353; 1993 (2) UJ 490

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Tamil Nadu Minority Schools (Recognition and Payment of Grants) Rules, 1977, r. 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Teachers training institute - Recognition - Grant of - Compliance of rules as condition precedent - Plea in matter of compliance - Distinction between institutes functioning earlier and after rules coming into force - Held, not tenable.
