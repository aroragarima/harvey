Metalware and Company and Others v Bansilal Sharma and Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 225; (1979) 3 SCC 398; AIR 1979 SC 1559; 1980 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1979 (2) RCJ 452; 1979 (2) RCR(Rent) 239; 1979 (2) RLR 161; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 1107

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Demolition And Reconstruction

Summary: Rent Control - TN Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 14(1)(b) - Tenancy - 'Bona fide required by landlord for immediate purpose of demolition and reconstruction' u/s. 14(1)(b) - Existing condition of building far from being a totally irrelevant - Vital factor - Held, age and existing condition of building whether it is a recent construction or old and whether it is in a good and sound condition or has become decrepit or dilapidated are relevant factors forming part of all the circumstances that have to be considered while determining bona fide requirement - Appeals allowed.
