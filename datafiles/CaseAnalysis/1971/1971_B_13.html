State of Punjab and Others v Bakhtawar Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 563; (1972) 4 SCC 730; AIR 1972 SC 2083; 1972 SLR 85; 1972 UJ 244

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Politics

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 10(1)(e)(iv) - Administrative law - Removal of member of Electricity Board - (A) Validity - (B) Order should be a speaking order - Authority had not applied his mind - Not even indicating what charges were established - Held, removal contemplated by section being by way of punishment, rules of natural justice require that he should be informed of charges against him and given a reasonable opportunity to rebut them - Order cannot be upheld as being arbitrary - Appeal dismissed.
