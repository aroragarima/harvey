M/s. W.P.I.L. Limited, Ghaziabad v Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 148; (2005) 3 SCC 73; AIR 2005 SC 1321; 2005 (181) E.L.T. 359; JT 2005 (2) SC 499; 2005 (67) RLT 149; 2005(2) SCALE 296; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 246

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Wood, Pumps, Centrifugal, Composite, Table

Summary: Excise - Demand of duty - Power driven pumps - Held, both power driven pumps as well as parts of power driven pumps used for manufacturing of pumps within factory were exempted from payment of excise duty - Authorities were in error in upholding demand and in directing appellant to pay excise duty - Appeals allowed.
