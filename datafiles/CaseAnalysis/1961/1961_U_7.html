Â Kishori Mohanlal Bakshi v Union Saw Mills and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, A.K. Sarkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 292; AIR 1962 SC 1139; [1962] 44 ITR 532

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16(1) - (A) Whether art. 16 of Constitution is violated, if citizens holding posts in different grades in Govt. service are denied equality of opportunity in matters of promotion? - Held, as between citizens holding posts in different grades in Govt. service there can be no question of equality of opportunity - Art. 16 is not violated - (B) Whether different pay scales for doing same kind of work is violative of art. 14 of Constitution?- Further held, pay scales of class I and class II ITO are different though they do same kind of work - Not violative of art. 14 of Constitution - Petition dismissed.
