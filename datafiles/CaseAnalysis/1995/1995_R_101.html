Bank Of Maharashtra v Rice Shipping and Transport Company Private

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 368; (1995) 3 SCC 257; AIR 1995 SC 1368; 1995 (2) BC 140; [1995] 83 Comp Cas 478; JT 1995 (3) SC 175; 1995(1) SCALE 785; 1995 (1) UJ 459

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226 - Writ jurisdiction - Legality of interim order granting principal releif Challenged - Bank alleged to have made payment on forged cheque - Petition for reimbursement by account holder - Bank disputing that cheque was forged - Disputed facts - Reimbursement of amount ordered - No primafacie case - Balance of convenience, etc. deprecated - Held, when facts were seriously disputed it could not be said that prima facie case was made out by account holder for reimbursement - Interim order improper.
