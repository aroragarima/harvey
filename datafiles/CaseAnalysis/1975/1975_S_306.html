Commissioner of Income Tax, Nagpur v Sutlej Cotton Mills Supply Agency Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, V.R. Krishna Iyer, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 329; (1975) 2 SCC 538; AIR 1975 SC 2106; 1975 CTR 228; 1975 (2) ITJ 285; [1975] 100 ITR 706; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 126; 1975 TAXLR 967

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Adventure In The Nature Of Trade, Devaluation

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 10 and 66 - Income Tax - Adventure in the nature of trade - Tribunal finding that certain profit was trading profit - Interference with finding by High Court in reference - Held, Tribunal had considered the evidence on record and applied the correct test - No scope for interference as jurisdiction was advisory and conclusion of facts are conclusion on which the HC is to exercise the advisory jurisdiction - Appeal Allowed.
