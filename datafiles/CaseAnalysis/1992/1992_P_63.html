Malhotra Steel Syndicate v Punjab Chemi-Plants Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1194; (1993) Supp3 SCC 565

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Issue with regard to form and verification of affidavit filed in support of application for winding-up - Held, verification, on a proper and liberal construction, does contain an averment to effect that statements made in affidavit are true and correct to knowledge of appellant - Even if there is some slight defect or irregularity in filing of affidavit, appellant should have been given an opportunity to rectify same - Division Bench was in error in dismissing appeal on short ground that affidavit filed in support of petition was not in proper form - Company appeal to stand revived - Appeal allowed.
