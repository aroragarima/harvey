Sher Singh and Others v Gamdoor Singh

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2032; (1997) 2 SCC 485; AIR 1997 SC 1333; 1997 (29) ALR 409; 1997 (1) CLT 399; JT 1997 (1) SC 396; 1997(1) SCALE 214; 1998 (Supp) RD 586; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 105; 1997 (1) Supreme 287

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Family & Personal - Land & Property - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Coparcenary property - Existence of joint family - Property held by family assumes character of coparcenary property - (A) Whether finding of Court that every member of family was entitled to 1/6 th share in property proper? - (B) Whether previous decree in respect of same property wherein plaintiff member was given 5/6 th share in property would bind other member being collusive decree? - Held, (A) finding of Court is proper - (B) previous decree in respect of same property would not bind other member - Appeal dismissed.
