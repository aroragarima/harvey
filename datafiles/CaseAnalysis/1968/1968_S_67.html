State of Andhra Pradesh v Kokkiliagada Meerayya and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 349; (1969) 1 SCC 161; AIR 1970 SC 771; 1970 CRLJ 759; 1970 LLJ 759; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 98; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 1004

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 107 and 403 - Constitution, art. 20(2) - Principle of issue estopped - Applicability of - To proceeding u/s.107 on basis of specific incident - Rejection of evidence - Trial thereafter, if barred - Held, sbsequent trial in respect of that incident is not barred - Appeal Allowed.
