Â Lilly Kurian v Sr. Lewina And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Sarkaria, N.L. Untwalia, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 85; (1979) 2 SCC 124; (1979) SCC (L&S) 134; AIR 1979 SC 52; 1978 LabIC 1644; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 820; 1979 (1) SLR 26

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Reduction In Rank, Gujarat University Act, 1949, Kerala University Act, 1957, Kerala University Act 1969

Summary: Service - Kerala University Act, 1957, ss. 19(j) and 19 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 30(1) - Ordinance 33 framed under 1957 Act - (A) Whether an educational institution established and managed by a religious or linguistic minority is bound by provisions of Ordinance 33(4), chap LVII of Ordinances framed by Syndicate of University of Kerala, u/s 19(j) of Kerala University Act, 1957? - (B) Powers of Vice-Chancellor on appeal as to dismissed teacher - (C) Vires of / constitutionality of Ordinance 33 (4) - Held, would not be applicable to an educational institution established and managed by a religious or linguistic minority - He can reinstate dismissed teacher - Ordinance 33 (4) is violative of art. 30 (1) of Constitution - Appeal dismissed.
