Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, C.A. Vaidyialingam, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 586; (1970) 1 SCC 189; AIR 1970 SC 1133; 1970 KLT 181; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 383

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Kerala Plantation (Additional Tax) Act, 1960 - Kerala Plantation (Additional Tax) Amendment Act, 1967 - Property Tax - Constitutional validity of 1960 Act as amended by 1967 Act - Validity - Held, Act does not contravene art. 14 - Petition Dismissed.
