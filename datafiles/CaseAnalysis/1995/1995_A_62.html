Amrit Banaspati Co. Limited v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1754; (1995) 3 SCC 335; AIR 1995 SC 1340; JT 1995 (2) SC 359; 1995(1) SCALE 809; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 25; 1995 (1) UJ 570

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 301 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 101, 102, 103 and 104 - Constitutionality of Delhi Municipal Corporation Act - Burden to prove - Held, violation of constitutional provisions should have specific, clear and unambiguous allegations - Burden is on person challenging constitutionality.
