Mukund Lal Bhandari and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 May 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1431; (1993) Supp3 SCC 2; AIR 1993 SC 2127; JT 1993 (3) SC 342; 1993(2) SCALE 933; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 891; 1993 (2) UJ 202; 1993 (3) UPLBEC 1561

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, art. 72 - Freedom Fighters Pension Scheme, 1972 - Pension for freedom fighter - (A) Whether application for pension has to be accepted when not made before prescribed date nor accompanied by requisite eligibility data - (B) Benefit commence from - Held, immaterial - Only from date of application - Petition dismissed.
