Messrs Jagdish Chand Radhey Shyam v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 493; (1973) 3 SCC 428; AIR 1972 SC 2587; 1972 RCR(Rent) 862; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 97; 1973 UJ 382

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: Capital of Punjab (Development and Regulation) Act, 1952, s. 3 - Constitution, arts.14, 19(1)(g) - Once auction sale is complete title in land or building does not remain with the Govt. even though a portion of consideration remains unpaid - Hence, once auction sale is complete title in land or building does not remain with the Govt. even though a portion of the consideration remains unpaid - Appeal Allowed.
