Arun Paswan, S.I. v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1136; (2004) 5 SCC 53; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1503; AIR 2004 SC 721; 2004 (1) CCR 109; 2004 (2) CGLJ 90; 2004 (1) Crimes 184; JT 2003 (10) SC 459; 2004 (1) RCR(Criminal) 643; 2003(10) SCALE 658; 2003 (1) SCW 6863; 2004 (1) SLT 324; [2003] Supp6 S.C.R. 724; 2003 (8) Supreme 866

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Contempt of Courts Act, 1926

Summary: Contempt of Courts Act, 1971 - s. 10 - Slogan shouting and leveling abusive language against the judge - Held, bar of the jurisdiction of the High Court imposed by proviso to s. 10, Contempt of Courts Act, is not attracted in the cases where the offences under ss. 178, 179, 180 and 228, IPC, 1860, are not committed in the view or presence of the Court.
