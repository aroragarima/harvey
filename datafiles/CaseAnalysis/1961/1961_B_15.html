Bangalore Woollen, Cotton and Silk Mills Company Limited v Corporation of The City of Bangalore By Its

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 59; AIR 1962 SC 1263; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 698

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949

Summary: Trade - Indirect Tax - Municipalities & Local Govts - City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, ss. 130 and 98(1) - (A) Whether resolution choosing goods for levy of octroi and fixing rates is a contravention of s. 130 of City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949? - Held, u/s. 130, it's not necessary that first a resolution is to be passed specifying goods then another resolution to be passed showing intention of Municipality to tax those goods - If procedure laid down in s. 98(1) of Act is followed then resolution choosing goods for levy of octroi and fixing rates is not a contravention of s. 130 of City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949 - Appeal dismissed.
