R. Kolandaivelu And Others. v The Govt. Of Tamil Nadu And Another

Supreme Court of India

11 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, Surinder Singh Nijjar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2034; (2010) 2 SCC 97; AIR 2010 SC 413; 2010 (2) ALD(SC) 44; 2010 (78) ALR 463; 2010 (1) AWC 806; 2010 (1) CLT(SC) 161; JT 2009 (15) SC 406; 2010 (2) MLJ(SC) 401; 2010 (2) MPLJ 259; 2010 (2) RCR(Civil) 104; 2010 (109) RD 261; 2009(14) SCALE 524; [2009] 16 S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 11A - Notification dt. 24-12-1986 - Said notification was issued for acquisition of impugned land - Writ petitions filed questioning validity and legality of aforesaid notification and declaration of acquisition proceedings after expiry of 2 years - Interim order was passed but same order was made final therefore, it cannot be said that acquisition proceedings had lapsed due to expiry of 2 years - Held, two years from date of declaration must be computed after excluding period when parties had approached court and obtained interim stay of such acquisition notices - Hence, State/respondent was entitled to exclude period mentioned earlier and that it must also be held that if such period is excluded period of two years from date of declaration u/s. 6 of Act before award is passed would not expire and accordingly, question of holding that entire acquisition proceeding shall lapse, cannot arise at all - Appeal dismissed.
