Rattan Lal And Ors. Etc.Etc. v State Of Haryana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 247; (1985) 4 SCC 43; (1985) SCC (L&S) 938; AIR 1987 SC 478; 1985 (51) FLR 352; 1986 LabIC 1599; 1986 (1) LLJ 23; 1985 (2) LLN 828; 1985(2) SCALE 354; 1985 (2) SLJ 437; 1985 (3) SLR 548; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 569; 1985 UJ 1093

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 32 - Appointment of teachers on ad hoc basis at the commencement of year - Termination before summer vacation - Constitutionality of such Policy of State Govt.- Held, is violative of arts. 14 and 16 - State Govt. directed to make appointments as per rules and fill up vacancies in which teachers who are working on ad hoc basis are now working - Petitions disposed of.
