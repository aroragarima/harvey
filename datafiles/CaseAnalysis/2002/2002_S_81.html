Champa Lal v Shaik Najmuddin Alias Gulsheer Pasha and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 May 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1252; (2002) 5 SCC 20; AIR 2002 SC 2076; 2002 (1) AllCJ 706; 2002 (47) ALR 690; JT 2002 (4) SC 478; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 620; 2002(4) SCALE 277; 2002 (4) SLT 1; 2002 (4) Supreme 66

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent control - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, ss. 14, 15, 16, 17, 21(1)(h) and 50 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Petition filed by landlord u/s. 21(1)(h) of Karnataka Rent Control Act - Small causes court dismissed petition for eviction - Revision allowed by High Court and ordered eviction of tenant - Whether HC in exercise of its revisional jurisdiction could not upset finding of fact recorded by Small Cause Court since such power was not vested u/s. 50 of Act? - Held, HC has not exceeded limit u/s. 50(1) of Act in setting aside order passed by Small Cause Court, order of HC is not based on re appreciation of evidence but on proper reading of evidence of landlord in entirety which Trial Court had omitted to do - Appeal dismissed.
