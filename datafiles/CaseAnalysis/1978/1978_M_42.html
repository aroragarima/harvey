Gujarat State Financial Corporation v Messrs Natson Manufacturing Company Private

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 73; (1979) 1 SCC 193; AIR 1978 SC 1765; [1979] 49 Comp Cas 187; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 372; 1978 UJ 687

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: State Financial Corporations Act, 1951, ss. 31 and 32 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882 - Bombay Court fees Act, 1959, sch.1, arts. 1 and 7 and sch.2, art.1(c) - Proceedings u/ss. 31 and 32 of 1951, Act - Nature of - Application u/s. 31 (1) - Whether the court-fee payable is a fixed fee u/sch. II, art. 1 (c) or ad valorem either u/sch. I arts. 1 or 7 of 1959 Act - Held, court-fee payable is a fixed fee - Appeal Allowed.
