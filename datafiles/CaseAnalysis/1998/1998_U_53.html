Kishan Lal v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1414; (1998) 2 SCC 392; AIR 1998 SC 862; 1998 (145) CTR 450; 1998 (75) ECR 11; [1998] 230 ITR 85; JT 1998 (1) SC 317; 1998(1) SCALE 254; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 320; 1998 (2) SLT 521; 1998 (1) Supreme 338; [1998] 97 TAXMAN 556

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 220(2A) - Interest - Levied for non payment of income-tax within due date - Application for reduction/waiver of interests before Central Board of Direct Taxes - Held, should be decided by a speaking order - Application rejected without recording reasons - Order vitiated.
