Messrs I.D.L. Chemicals Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2904; (1996) 5 SCC 373; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 265; AIR 1996 SC 2540; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 182; JT 1996 (8) SC 261; 1996(5) SCALE 505; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 881; 1996 (5) Supreme 730

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Excise - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch, Tariff, Entry 14 HH, Entry 68 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, chap. 10 - Exemption of excisable goods used in specified industrial process from excise duty - Licensee suffers adverse civil consequence because of reclassification - Whether he has locus to challenge? - Held, has locus standi - Appeal allowed.
