Pratibha Co-Operative Housing Society Limitedand Anr. v State Of Maharashtra And Ors

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 794; (1991) 3 SCC 341; AIR 1991 SC 1453; AIR 1991 SCW 1354; 1992 (1) Bom.C.R. 453; JT 1991 (2) SC 543; 1991(1) SCALE 920; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 745; 1991 (2) UJ 196; 1991 (2) UPLBEC 972

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Interference with Administrative decisions under writ jurisdiction - Housing Society raising unlawful constructions - Violating floor space index - Corporation decided to demolish eight upper floors - Society submitting alternative proposals - Same rejected by Municipal Commissioner after getting them examined by architects and engineer - Held, High Court is not appellate Court on administrative decisions so such decisions, not liable to interference - Petition dismissed.
