Ram Lal and Another v State of Jammu and Kashmir

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1679; (1999) SCC (Cr) 123; (1999) 2 SCC 213; AIR 1999 SC 895; 1999 CRLJ 1342; JT 1999 (1) SC 147; 2000 (1) RCR(Criminal) 92; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 230

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 320, Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 326 - Compounding of offences - Non compoundable u/s. 320 - Held, cannot be made compoundable with permission of Court - Court considering fact that parties have come to settlement and victims were having no grievance against accused and latter having undergone imprisonment of about six months, sentence reduced to period already undergone.
