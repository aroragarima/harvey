Shilpi Bose (Mrs.) and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 576; (1991) Supp2 SCC 659; (1992) SCC (L&S) 127; AIR 1991 SC 532; 1991 (17) ATC 935; 1991 LabIC 360; 1991 (2) LLJ 591

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 309 and 311 - Transfer of Govt. servants - Made by competent authority on request of such servants - Aggrieved filed petition before High Court - No mandatory rule violated by transfer - Interference with order by HC - Legality - Held, illegal - It cannot and should not be interfered with by court merely because transfer orders were passed on request of employees concerned, unless transfer orders are made in violation of any mandatory statutory rule or on ground of mala fide - Appeal allowed.
