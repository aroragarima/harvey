Shambhu Murari Sinha v Project and Development India and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 269; (2000) 5 SCC 621; (2000) SCC (L&S) 741; AIR 2000 SC 2473; AIR 2000 SCW 2577; 2000 (3) AWC 2330; JT 2000 (6) SC 358; 2000 LabIC 2610; 2000 (2) LLJ 935; 2000 (3) LLN 513; 2000(4) SCALE 262; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 9; 2000 (3) SLJ 367; 2000 (4) SLR 577; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1752

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Employment - Voluntary retirement-Withdrawal before effective date but after acceptance-Legally permissible-Application seeking voluntary retirement made on 18.10.1995-Accepted on 30.7.1997-But appellant allowed to continue in service till 26.9.1997-He had already withdrawn his offer of voluntary retirement on 7.8.1997-Withdrawal legally permissible-Direction that appellant shall be allowed to continue in service with all consequential benefits.
