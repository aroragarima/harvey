Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Indian Arbitration Act, 1859

Summary: Constitution - Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (the Act), ss. 11, 11(6), 13, 17, 36, 37, 8 - Indian Arbitration Act, 1859 - CPC, 1908 - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 20, 34, 7, 8, 9 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 11, 136, 226, 227, 32 - What is nature of function of Chief Justice or his designate u/s. 11 of the Act? - Correctness of view in Konkan Rly Corpn Ltd Vs Mehul Construction Co (2000 Indlaw SC 678) as approved by Constitution Bench in Konkan Railway Corpn Ltd and anr Vs Rani Construction Pvt Ltd (2002 Indlaw SC 60) questioned - Held, power exercised by Chief Justice of HC or Chief Justice of India u/s. 11(6) of Act is not an administrative power - It is a judicial power - Adjudication is involved in constitution of an arbitration tribunal - Chief Justice of High Court cannot designate district Judge to perform functions u/s. 11(6) - Konkan Railway Corpn Ltd and anr Vs Rani Construction Pvt Ltd overruled.
