State of W. B. and Others v Shivananda Pathak and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1747; (1998) 5 SCC 513; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1402; AIR 1998 SC 2050; 1998 (2) CLR 544; 1998 (2) CLT 105; 1998 (77) ECR 17; 1998 (79) FLR 684; JT 1998 (3) SC 701; 1998 (2) LLJ 887; 1998(3) SCALE 411; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 811; 1998 (3) SLJ 250; 1998 (4) SLR 340; 1998 (5) SLT 57; 1998 (4) Supreme 467

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 226 - Natural justice - Bias, on account of judicial obstinacy - Direction given by judge in writ petition was overruled in appeal - Same judge sitting in subsequent proceedings between same parties reiterates overruled direction - Held, has a biased mind.
