Chinnammal And 4 Ors. v P. Arumugham And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 659; (1990) 1 SCC 513; AIR 1990 SC 1828; 1990 (1) AWC 202; 1990 (1) CCC 408; 1990 (1) GLH 114; JT 1990 (1) SC 51; 1990 MahLJ 285; 1990 (1) MLJ 31; 1990 (1) MLJ(SC) 31; 1990(1) SCALE 43; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 78

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 144, O. 21, r. 29 - Restitution - Money decree - Execution - Auction sale of property - Appeal pending against decree - Purchaser aware of at time of purchase - Sale confirmed by Court - Decree set aside in appeal - Whether judgment debtor is entitled to restitution of property? - Held, judgment - Appeal dismissed.
