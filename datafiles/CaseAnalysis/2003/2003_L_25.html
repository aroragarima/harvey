Panna Lal Ghosh and Others v Land Acquisition Collector and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1137; (2004) 1 SCC 467; AIR 2004 SC 1179; 2004 (1) ALD(SC) 122; 2004 (54) ALR 169; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 517; 2003(10) SCALE 725; 2004 (1) SLT 296; [2003] Supp6 S.C.R. 740; 2003 (8) Supreme 821

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - ss. 4(1), 6, 23(2) - Acquisition - Compensation - Enhancement - Solatium - Held, due to different nature of the land, the High Court was right in disallowing the claim for enhancement for compensation - Further held, the appellants are thus entitled to enhanced solatium @ 30% and interest under s. 23(2) of the Act.
