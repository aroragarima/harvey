State of Bihar and Others Etc v Samsuz Zoha Etc

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 666; (1996) 4 SCC 546; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1048; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 448; AIR 1996 SC 1961; 1996 (74) FLR 1919; JT 1996 (6) SC 7; 1996 LabIC 1722; 1996 (2) LLJ 647; 1996 (2) LLN 496; 1996(4) SCALE 100; 1996 (3) SCJ 123; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 807; 1996 (4) SLR 235; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 1974

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution, art. 16 - Compassionate appointment - Candidates do not have right to claim particular appointment - Policy decision of State to appoint such candidates to class IV post - Order of HC directing to consider such candidates for appointment to class III post as fresh appointees or by way of promotion - Whether permissible - Held, order is impermissible - Appeal allowed.
