Yeshwantrao Laxmanrao Ghatge and Another v Baburao Bala Yadav (Dead) By L. Rs

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 446; (1978) 1 SCC 669; AIR 1978 SC 941; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 814; 1978 UJ 153

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950, s. 52 A - (A) Extinguishments of right of trust to property before coming into force of s. 52 A - What is impact of s. 52 A on such right? - (B) Sale of property once belonging to trust in 1947 - Vendor acquiring right to property long before by adverse possession - Property not even shown as trust property, when trust was declared a Public Trust - Suit by Trust to recover it - Held, s. 52 A had, by no stretch of imagination, effect of reviving an extinguished, lost claim, giving life to a dead horse - Suit, is not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
