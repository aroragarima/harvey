Bharat Drilling and Foundation Treatment Private Limited v State of Jharkhand and others

Supreme Court of India

20 August 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2197; (2009) 16 SCC 705

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act,1996 - Arbitral award - There was a dispute between the parties to the contract and the matter was referred to an arbitrator, who gave three different awards since three contracts were involved in the dispute - Awards were made rule of the court - Appeal filed thereagainst were partly allowed - Hence, instant appeals - Whether HC rightly based its judgment on the reasoning that the claims which were allowed by the arbitrator were barred by the contract Cl. 1.21 - Held, scope of interference by the court with a reasoned award was very limited - Hence, impugned judgment and order of the HC was set aside and awards passed by the Arbitrator were restored - Appeals allowed.
