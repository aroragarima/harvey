Messrs Krishna Bus Service Private Limited Etc v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 399; (1985) 3 SCC 711; AIR 1985 SC 1651; 1985 (2) CCC 682; 1985 CRLR 478; 1985 CrLR(SC) 478; 1985(2) SCALE 72; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 330

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act 1939, s. 133A - Punjab Motor Vehicles Rules, 1946, r. 10.2 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 19(1)(g) - Whether the appointment of the General Manager of Haryana Roadways as an officer who can exercise the powers exercisable by a DSP under the 1939 Act by the State govt. under the Notification D/-16-03-1973 issued u/s.133-A of the Act is valid or not - Held, not valid being violative of art. 19(1)(g) of Constitution - Appeal allowed.
