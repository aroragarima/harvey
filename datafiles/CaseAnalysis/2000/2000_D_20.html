D.L.F. Universal Ltd v Appropriate Authority And Anr, Etc

Supreme Court of India

04 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 736; (2000) 5 SCC 552; AIR 2000 SC 1985; 2000 (160) CTR 401; [2000] 243 ITR 730; JT 2000 (5) SC 469; 2000(4) SCALE 351; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 970; 2000 (4) SLT 267; 2000 (4) Supreme 273; [2000] 110 TAXMAN 315

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Pre-Emptive Purchase, Appeals disposed of accordingly

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961,ss. 269UC , 269UD and 269UL - Income Tax Rules, 1962,r. 48l ,ss. 269UA(a) , 269UA(d) and 269UA(f) - Compliance of procedure for NOC for transfer of immovable property - (A) What does 'agreement for transfer' u/r. 48-L(2) (c) of the Rules, refers to - (B) Statement to be furnished u/s. 269-UC(3) of the Act - Whether r. 48-L therein, providing for period, is mandatory - (C) Determination of effect of failure of appropriate authority to make any order within three months of receipt of the said statement for purchase by Central Govt. of immovable property - Held, has reference to statement in Form 37-I - Is only directory and not mandatory - Authority was duty bound to issue no objection certificate to transfer of property - Appeals disposed of .
