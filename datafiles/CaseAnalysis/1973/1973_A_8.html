Agricultural & Industrial Syndicate Ltd. v State Of U.P. And Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Dwivedi, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 162; (1974) 2 SCC 27; AIR 1974 SC 1920; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 253

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holding Act, 1954, Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holding Act (1 of 1981)

Summary: U.P. Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1961, Pre. - U.P. Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 5(2) - Proceedings under 1961 Act - Effect of consolidation operations upon it - Held, cannot be continued during consolidation operations - Appeal Allowed.
