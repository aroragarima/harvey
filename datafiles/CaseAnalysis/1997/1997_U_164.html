Hukam Raj Khinvsara v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 459; (1997) 4 SCC 284; (1997) SCC (L&S) 943; AIR 1997 SC 2100; 1997 (3) CLT 356; 1997 (76) FLR 167; JT 1997 (4) SC 193; 1997 (2) LLJ 152; 1997 (2) LLN 1059; 1997(3) SCALE 334; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 1157; 1998 (1) SLJ 226; 1997 (2) SLR 599; 1997 (3) Supreme 555

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Civil Procedure - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, ss. 27 and 21(1)(a) - Limitation period of execution of order passed by Tribunal - (A) Whether application for execution of final order of Tribunal filed after two years and nine months barred by limitation? - (B) Whether condonation of delay possible? - Held, (A) application barred by limitation - (B) question of condonation of delay would not arise in absence of application - Appeal dismissed.
