Delhi Development Authority, New Delhi v Smt. Lila D. Bhagat and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 324; (1975) SCC (Cr) 151; (1975) 1 SCC 410; AIR 1975 SC 495; 1975 CRLJ 435

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - CrPC, 1898, ss. 439 and 561A - Delhi Development Act, 1957, ss. 14 and 29(2) - Question of fact - Whether Master Plan had specified a particular use of a particular building and whether the person prosecuted had incurred penalty u/s. 29(2) of 1957 Act for violation of s.14 of same Act - Held, are questions of fact - Supreme Court declined to interfere - Appeal Allowed.
