All India Judges’ Association v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, A.M. Ahmadi, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 1072; (1992) SCC (L&S) 9; (1992) 1 SCC 119; AIR 1992 SC 165; AIR 1992 SCW 2869; 1992 ALJ 185; 1992 (19) ATC 42; JT 1991 (4) SC 285; 1993 (1) LLJ 723; 1991 (2) LLN 1187; 1991(2) SCALE 969; 1992 (1) SLJ 53; 1992 (1) SLR 60; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 206; 1992 (1) UJ 155

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 32, 234 and 235 - Judicial service - Subordinate judiciary - Conditions of service - Setting up All India Judicial Service - Directions sought - Held, directions issued by Supreme Court for setting up All India Judicial Service and for bringing uniform conditions of service for members of subordinate judiciary throughout country - Order accordingly.
