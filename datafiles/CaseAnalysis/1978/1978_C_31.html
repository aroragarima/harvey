Dharmaposhanam Company v Commissioner of Income Tax, Kerala

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Y.V. Chandrachud, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 410; (1978) 3 SCC 414; AIR 1978 SC 1443; 1978 (7) CTR 114; [1978] 114 ITR 463; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 1030; [1979] 1 TAXMAN 61

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961 - Indian Companies Act, 1913 - Charitable trust - Memorandum of Association - Tax exemption - Objectives - Appellant assessee was a company constituted under a license issued by the Govt. of Cochin and registered with limited liability u/s. 26(1) of the 1913 as applied to Cochin - Appellant was governed by a Memorandum of Association - During the A/Ys 1962-63 to 1965-66, the appellant assessee derived income from property, money lending and business in kuries or chit funds - Appellant assessee claimed exemption from tax of the income from kuries and money lending u/s. 11 of the 1961 Act - Tribunal held that the appellant assessee was not entitled to exemption even after appellant assessee made alterations in the Memorandum of Association - Reference made to the HC - HC upheld Tribunal's decision - Hence, the instant appeal - Whether the income of the appellant assessee for the A/Ys 1962-63 to 1965-66 was exempt under the provisions of s. 11 of the 1961 Act - Held, where one or more of the objects mentioned in the Memorandum of Association although included therein were never intended to be undertaken no exemption could be claimed on mere inclusion of those objects - If the evidence pointed to a conclusion clearly the Court would ignore the objects and proceed to consider the case as the objects had not existed in the Memorandum - Also, appellant assessee had not engaged itself in any industry or in any other activity of public interest - Appellant was not entitled to exemption u/s. 11 of the 1961 Act - Thus reference was answered in favor of the Tribunal-respondent and against the appellant assessee - Appeal dismissed.
