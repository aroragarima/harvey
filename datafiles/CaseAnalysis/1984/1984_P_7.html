V. Sasidharan v Peter And Karunakar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 190; (1984) 4 SCC 230; (1984) SCC (L&S) 697; AIR 1984 SC 1700; 1985 (1) CCC 210; 1984 (49) FLR 342; 1984 LabIC 1587; 1984 (2) LLJ 385; 1984 (2) LLN 531; 1985 (1) RentLR 196; 1984(2) SCALE 201; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 601; 1985 (1) SLR 222; 1985 UJ 54

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Advocates and Judges - Labour and Industrial Law - Kerala Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1960, ss. 2(4), 2(15) - Office of lawyer or of firm of lawyers - Whether a firm of lawyers is a 'commercial establishment' or a shop under the provisions of the Act - Held, neither it is a commercial establishment nor it is a shop - Appeal dismissed .
