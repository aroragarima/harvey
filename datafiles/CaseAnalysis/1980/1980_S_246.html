Tara Chand v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 247; (1980) 2 SCC 321; (1980) SCC (Cr) 441; AIR 1980 SC 1361; [1982] 52 Comp Cas 53; 1980 CRLJ 1015; 1980 MLJ 795; 1980 UJ 336

Case Digest

Subject: Fema

Keywords: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities (Amendment) Act, 1996

Summary: COFEPOSA Act, 1974, s.11(1) - Constitution, art. 22(5) - Detention - By state govt. u/s. 11 of 1974, Act - Representation made against, to central govt. - Which is duty bound to consider it u/art. 22 of the Constitution - Inordinate delay in consideration - Validity of the detention challenged - Inordinate delay in consideration renders detention invalid - Petition Allowed.
