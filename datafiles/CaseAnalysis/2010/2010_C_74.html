Ramala Sahkari Chini Mills Ltd., U.P. v Commissioner Central Excise, Meerut-I

Supreme Court of India

29 November 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

D.K. Jain, H.L. Dattu

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 1010; 2010 (260) E.L.T. 321; JT 2010 (13) SC 778; 2010(12) SCALE 549

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11AB - CENVAT Credit Rules, 2002, rr. 2 and 13 - CENVAT Credit - Inputs - Appellant engaged in the manufacture of V. P. Sugar and molasses, availed CENVAT credit on welding electrodes u/r. 2 of 2002 Rules - 3 show cause notices dt. 4.10.2002, 3.04.2003 and 7.7.2003 were issued to the assessee for the periods February 2002, March, 2002 to October, 2002 and November, 2002 to April, 2003, respectively proposing to recover the wrongly availed credit amount for those periods amounting to a total of Rs. 1,33,871/- together with interest and penalty - Asst. Commissioner confirmed the demand of Rs. 1,33,871/- and also imposed a penalty of equal amount u/r. 13 of 2002 Rules and charged interest u/s. 11AB of the 1944 Act - Commissioner dismissed appeal filed against said order - Tribunal also dismissed appeal filed against said order and held that appellant was not eligible for credit either as capital goods or as inputs - Hence, present appeal - Whether appellant is eligible for CENVAT credit as input in respect of welding electrodes? - Held, SC in Maruti Suzuki Limited vs. Commissioner of Central Excise 2009 INDLAW SC 976, held that welding electrodes would come within the ambit of 'inputs' as defined in r. 2(g) of the 2002 Rules, in as much as they are used in relation to the manufacture of the final product - Having regard to the language of r. 2(g) of the 2002 Rules, and the analysis of the aforenoted decision, it appears that by employing the phrase 'and includes', legislature did not intend to impart a restricted meaning to the definition of 'inputs' and therefore, the interpretation of the said term in Maruti Suzuki Limited, may require reconsideration by a larger bench - Referred to larger bench
