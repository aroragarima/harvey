Indian Oil Corporation Limited v A. P. Industrial Infrastructure Corporation Limited and others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sikri, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 815; 2016 (1) ALD(SC) 123; JT 2015 (12) SC 618; 2015(13) SCALE 394

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Act, 1994

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Act, 1994, ss. 2(19), 2(3), 20 - Storage of Tanks in depots - Demand for Property Tax - Challenged - Appellant/corporation has storage depots and bottling plant situated in industrial development area of respondent no.1 - Respondent no.1/corporation by virtue of transfer of power by local govt. issued notices and demanded property tax from appellant - HC dismissed writ petitions of appellant and held appellant liable to pay property tax under provisions of the Act - Hence, instant appeal and writ petition - Whether storage tanks which store LPG could be considered as 'house' within meaning of the Act as taxes were leviable, inter alia, on houses.

Held, as per s. 2(19) of the Act, 'House' means a building or a hut fit for human occupation, whether as a residence or otherwise and includes any shop, factory, workshop or warehouse or any building used for garraging or parking buses or as a bus- stand, cattle shed (other than a cattle shed in any agricultural land, poultry shed or dairy shed. Hence, a place where goods are stored would be 'warehouse' which is specifically mentioned in the definition of house contained in s. 2(19) of the Act. It would, thus, follow that it may not be necessary that such a place is capable of frequent visits by the human beings or fit for human occupations. The requirement that a house should be fit for human occupations is only for huts which is defined u/s. 20 of the Act. It is this fictional definition of 'house' which is to be kept in mind for the purpose of levy of tax. Hence, impugned appeal and writ petition is devoid of any merit and the same are dismissed. Appeal dismissed.
