International Airport Authority Of India v K.D. Bali And Another

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 422; (1988) 2 SCC 360; AIR 1988 SC 1099; 1988 (1) ARBLR 408; 1988 BBCJ 44; JT 1988 (2) SC 1; 1988(1) SCALE 631; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 370; 1988 (1) UJ 729

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Question relating to the constitutional validity of s. 6(4) of the Act - Power of State Govt. u/s. 6(4)of the Act as to remitting award for reconsideration - Held, power is unguided and uncanalised and provision is violative of art. 14 - Cannot be upheld by reading into it certain requirements - Appeal allowed.
