Noor Mohammad Mohd. Yusuf Momin v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 87; (1970) SCC (Cr) 274; (1970) 1 SCC 696; AIR 1971 SC 885; 1971 CRLJ 793; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 119

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution, art. 136 - IPC, 1860 (A) Constitution, art. 136 - Supreme Court normally does not review and appraise evidence for itself - (B) IPC, 1860 - Distinction among provisions of s. 34, 109, 120B of IPC, 1860 - Held, conspiracy need not be proved by direct evidence - Held, u/s. 34 participation in the commission of offence necessary while u/s. 109 accused need not be present , but u/s. 120B mere agreement is sufficient - Appeal Dismissed.
