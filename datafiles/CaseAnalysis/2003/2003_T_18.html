Messrs Sathyanarayana Brothers Private Limited v Tamil Nadu Water Supply & Drainage Board

Supreme Court of India

18 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1011; (2004) 5 SCC 314; AIR 2004 SC 651; 2004 (1) ARBLR 1; 2003(9) SCALE 769; 2003 (1) SCW 6117; [2003] Supp5 S.C.R. 733

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Indian Arbitration Act, 1940 - Award - Claim partly allowed by the Umpire - Held, the order of the Division Bench, reversing the decision of the Single Judge, is not sustainable and the matter may be required to be remitted to be considered in the light of the 'handing over note' of the Chief Engineer, in respect whereof an application was moved by the appellant before the arbitrator as well as before the Umpire, which remained unattended to by the forum and later did not accede to the request.
