Iron and Metal Traders Private Limited, Bombay v M. S. Haskiel and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 35; (1984) SCC (L&S) 140; (1984) 1 SCC 304; AIR 1984 SC 629; 1983 (47) FLR 434; 1984 LabIC 182; 1983 (2) LLJ 504; 1984 (1) LLN 75; 1983(2) SCALE 391B; 1983 UJ 805

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Strike - Finding of the Industrial Tribunal that this is a case of discrimination inasmuch many strikers were reinstated but respondents were singled out for drastic treatment - Tribunal ordered reinstatement of three workers and awarded compensation to seven in lieu of relief of reinstatement - Legality of order challenged - Held, approach of the Tribunal is fair, just and reasonable - No justification for interfering with the award - Appeals dismissed.
