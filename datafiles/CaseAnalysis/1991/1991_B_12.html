Bangalore Medical Trust v B.S. Muddappa And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 1038; (1991) 4 SCC 54; AIR 1991 SC 1902; AIR 1991 SCW 2082; JT 1991 (3) SC 172; 1991(2) SCALE 131; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 102; 1991 (2) UJ 415

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Maharashtra Regional Town Planning Act, 1966, City of Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govt. - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Bangalore Region Development Authority Act, 1976, ss. 2(b), 15(3),19, 19(4) and 65 - Public interest litigation against conversion of public park into private nursing home by state Govt. - Petition against - (A) Determination of locus standi - (B) Whether private nursing home is amenity - (C) Scope of s. 15(3) as regard to alteration of scheme - (D) Scope of powers of Govt. u/s. 65 - In regards to directions for conversion of site - Held, maintainable - Private nursing home is neither amenity nor can be considered improvement over necessity like public park, moreover conversion is illegal - Further held, s. 15(3) cannot be stretched to entitle Govt. to alter any scheme or convert any site - Powers are restricted, conversion not permissible - Appeal dismissed.
