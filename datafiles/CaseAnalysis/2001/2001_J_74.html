Stanny Felix Pinto v Jangid Builders Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20350; (2001) SCC (Cr) 345; (2001) 2 SCC 416; AIR 2001 SC 659; 2001 (2) ALD(SC) 96; 2001 (1) BC 712; 2001 (1) CCR 228; 2001 (1) CompLJ 405; 2001 (1) Crimes 190; 2001 CRLJ 1039; JT 2001 (1) SC 620; 2001 (1) RCR(Criminal) 537A; 2001(1) SCALE 228; 2001 (2) SCJ 17; [2001] 1 S.C.R. 390; 2001 (1) SLT 528; 2001 (1) Supreme 194; 2001 (1) UC 350A

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Criminal - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - Suspension of sentence - Deposit of fine to be remitted in court within a specified time - Held, advisable that court imposes a condition that fine part is remitted within a certain period - No ground for interference - Appeal dismissed.
