Atma Ram Mittal v Ishwar Singh Punla

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 563; (1988) 4 SCC 284; AIR 1988 SC 2031; JT 1988 (3) SC 745; 1988 (2) RCJ 368; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 423; 1988 (2) RentLR 641; 1988(2) SCALE 658; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 528; 1988 (2) UJ 633

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973 - Determination of availability of exemption from the Act - When suit for eviction filed within ten years from completion of construction - But expiry of ten years during pendency of suit - Held, benefit of exemption not lost - The costs of the appeal will be the costs of the suit - Appeal allowed.
