Â Mithu, Etc., Etc., v State Of Punjab Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 375; (1983) 2 SCC 277; (1983) SCC (Cr) 405; AIR 1983 SC 473; 1983 (1) Crimes 957; 1983 CRLJ 811; 1983 CRLR 298; 1983 MLJ 485; 1983(1) SCALE 331; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 690

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Land & Property

Keywords: Life Convict, Disposal on Merits

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 and 21 - IPC, 1860, s. 303 - Infringement of fundamental right - Whether s.303 of the IPC, 1860 infringes the guarantee contained in art. 21 of the Constitution which provides that 'no person shall be deprived of his life or personal liberty except according to procedure established by law' - Held, liable to be struck down as unconstitutional being violative of arts. 14 and 21- Order accordingly.
