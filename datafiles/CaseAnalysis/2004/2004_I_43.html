Itc Limited v Cc and Ce and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1476; 2004 (62) RLT 358

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Payment of duty by assessee - Held, upon perusing materials on records and keeping in view decision of this court in Polar Industries Ltd v Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut, reported in 7831999 Indlaw SC 87 (S C ) and decision of this court in ITC Ltd v Collector of Central Excise, Bombay, reported in 2002 Indlaw SC 1426, interest of justice would be met if without delving deep into question and without expressing any opinion on merits of rival contentions of parties, a direction is issued that appellant herein may not be called upon to deposit impugned duty and appeal is directed to be disposed of by appellate authority expeditiously preferably within a period of twelve weeks from date of receipt of copy of this order - Appeal allowed.
