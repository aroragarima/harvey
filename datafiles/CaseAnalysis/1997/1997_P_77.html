Supreme Court of India

02 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 823; (1997) 4 SCC 413; AIR 1997 SC 2399; 1997 ALJ 1369; 1997 (30) ALR 307; JT 1997 (4) SC 425; 1997 (1) RCR(Rent) 591; 1997 (1) RentLR 522; 1997(3) SCALE 400; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 508; 1997 (3) Supreme 597

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - U.P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 21(1)(a) - Eviction decree passed on the ground of bona fide requirement - Death of landlord subsequently - Order of Appellate Authority became final - Whether same can be interfered with by HC under writ jurisdiction - Held, bona fide need for starting business does not lapse on the death of landlord as the business can be carried on by his widow - Order cannot be interfered with by HC by taking into account subsequent event - Appeal dismissed.
