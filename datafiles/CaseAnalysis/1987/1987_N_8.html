Gujarat University v N.U. Rajguru And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28892; (1987) Supp SCC 512; AIR 1988 SC 66; JT 1987 (4) SC 307; 1987(2) SCALE 1093; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 899; 1988 (1) UJ 81

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Election - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Gujarat University Act, 1949, s. 58 - Alternative remedy - Maintainability of challenge to election of certain members to 'Court' of Gujarat University by writ petition - Held, not maintainable in view of mandatory provision of s. 58 for reference of dispute relating to election, to any 'Authority' of university to State Govt. - Appeal allowed.
