Ram Sukrya Mhatre v R. D. Tyagi and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 862; (1992) Supp3 SCC 65; (1992) SCC (Cr) 960; AIR 1994 SC 1134; 1994 CRLJ 63

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3 - Time frame for disposal of representation against Preventive detention cannot be laid down - Delay in not properly explained but order file disclosing expeditious disposal - Counter affidavit indicating issue of detention order after considering likely future indulgance - Plea of non application of mind - Held, plea not tenable - No infirmity in detention order - Appeal dismissed.
