Bajaj Auto Limited v N. K. Firodia and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 264; (1970) 2 SCC 550; AIR 1971 SC 321; [1971] 41 Comp Cas 1; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 40

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 111(2) - Company - Transfer of shares - Absolute discretion vested in directors by articles of association of company to refuse registration - Test for court to consider how far reasons for refusal were justified - Refusal, held arbitrary and unjustified - Appeals dismissed.
