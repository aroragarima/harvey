State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others v Sajad Ahmed Mir

Supreme Court of India

17 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 326; (2006) 5 SCC 766; AIR 2006 SC 2743; 2006 (6) AWC 6209; JT 2006 (6) SC 387; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 605; 2007 (1) RLW 73; 2006(7) SCALE 136; 2006 (5) SLR 646; [2006] Supp3 S.C.R. 576

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 - Jammu & Kashmir (Compassionate Appointment) Rules, 1994 - Appointment on compassionate ground - Held, single Judge was right in dismissing petition on ground of delay and laches by holding that applicant had not done anything for a considerable period after March, 1996 when his claim was rejected even though he was informed about decision and was very much aware of it - Appeals allowed.
