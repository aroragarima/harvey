Fitrat Raza Khan v tate of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 6; (1982) 2 SCC 449; (1982) SCC (Cr) 472; AIR 1982 SC 146; 1982 CRLJ 338; 1982 CRLR 1; 1981(3) SCALE 1813; 1982 UJ 26

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: communal harmony, communal violence, communalism

Summary: Constitution - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art.22(5) - Past conduct - Relevancy - Maintenance of public order - Detention - Propensities to instigate communal violence - Ground - Based on antecedent history of a petitioner - Validity - Held, Valid.
