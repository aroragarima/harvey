Avadh Kishore Das v Ram Gopal and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 341; (1979) 4 SCC 790; AIR 1979 SC 861; 1979 UJ 251

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 92 - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1950, ss. 6, 209 and 210 - Religious endowment - Suit contested by defendant, alleged that trust not a religious or charitable trust for public - Decree against defendant - Appeal to High Court dismissed - Whether entire estate of village Bahawalpura is property of a public religious Trust or endowment dedicated to temple idol of Shri Thakurji Maharaj or secular and absolute property of appellant? - Held, on evidence reinforced by oral evidence on record, HC concluded that Math and temple in this case are a public religious Trust and not personal property of defendant therefore affirmed order of HC - Appeal dismissed.
