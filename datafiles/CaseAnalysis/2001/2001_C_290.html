Commissioner of Income Tax v Punjab Bone Mills

Supreme Court of India

19 July 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20822; 2001 (170) CTR 558; [2001] 251 ITR 780

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Cash incentive - Exports - Exemption - Whether cash incentive for exports had been placed before Tribunal - Held, Relevant material regarding cash incentive for exports did not appear to had been placed before Tribunal and without material it was not possible to decide contention of Revenue was correct - Appeals disposed of.
