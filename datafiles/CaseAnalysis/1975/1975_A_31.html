District Controller of Stores, Northern Railway v Assistant Commercial Taxation Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 83; (1976) SCC (Tax) 98; (1976) 1 SCC 660; AIR 1976 SC 489; 1976 (5) CTR 206; 1976 (37) STC 423; 1976 TAXLR 1453; 1976 UJ 163

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954 - Rajasthan Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1965 - Unserviceable materials and scrap - HC held that sales of unserviceable materials were eligible to tax and hence, instant appeal - Whether appellant is liable to pay sales tax on sales of unserviceable materials and scrap, etc.

Held, even it be assumed that the activity involved in selling unserviceable material and scrap-iron, etc., would not amount to carrying on business in the normal connotation of that terms, still, it would be 'business' within cl.(i) as introduced by amending Act. Further, since Railway is concerned in activity of transportation is engaged is commerce within the meaning of cl. (i) of definition and that the sale of unserviceable materials and scrap-iron, etc., is transaction in connection with or ancillary to such commerce within the cl. (ii) of that definition. Hence, order of HC is upheld and appeal dismissed with costs. Appeal dismissed.
