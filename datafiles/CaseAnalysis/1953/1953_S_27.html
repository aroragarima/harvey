Â Ram Prasad Narayan Sahi and Another v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1953

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Patanjali Sastri, Bijan Kumar Mukherjea, Vivian Bose, Ghulam Hasan, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 105; AIR 1953 SC 215; 1953 (32) ILR(Pat) 375; 1953 SCJ 267; [1953] 4 S.C.R. 1129

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 13(1) and 226 - Board Of Revenue Act, 1913 - Sathi Lands (Restoration) Act, 1950, s. 2 - Bengal Act, 1879 - Whether statute contains discriminatory provisions so far as appellants are concerned and if so, whether these discriminations could be reasonably justified ? - Held, present case comes directly within principle enunciated by Apex Court in Ameerunniss Begum v Mahboob Begum Indlaw[1953] S C R 404 - Thus, set aside judgment of High Court - Thus, a writ in nature of mandamus shall issue directing respondents not to take any steps in pursuance of Sathi Lands (Restoration) Act of 1950 or to interfere with possession of appellants in respect to lands comprised in lease referred to in that Act - Appeal allowed.
