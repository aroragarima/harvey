E.I.D. Parry (India) Ltd. v Assistant Commissioner of Commercial Taxes

Supreme Court of India

03 May 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, AR. Lakshmanan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 366; (2005) 4 SCC 779; AIR 2005 SC 2645; JT 2005 (5) SC 129; 2005(4) SCALE 615; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 1144; 2005 (141) STC 12

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Collection Of Tax

Summary: [A.] Sales Tax - Agriculture & Agri. Products - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966 - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 13(2), 24(3), 3D(2), 3E, 7E - Whether advance paid, pursuant to advice of Govt, can be considered to be price and thus includible in monthly turnover of Appellants? and whether interest u/s. 24(3) can be charged on price fixed under cl 5-A and/ or on advance paid? - Held, if amounts are paid towards cl 5-A price, they would be payment towards price and therefore part of turnover even though they may be paid in advance - In absence of any assessment, even provisional, and a notice of demand no interest would be payable u/s. 24(3) - Appeals allowed.
