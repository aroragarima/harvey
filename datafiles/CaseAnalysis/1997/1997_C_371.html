Harbans Kaur Smt v Commissioner of Wealth Tax, Jullundur

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2245; (1997) 2 SCC 567; AIR 1997 SC 973; 1997 (2) CalLT(SC) 82; 1997 (2) CLT 248; 1997 (138) CTR 211; [1997] 224 ITR 418; JT 1997 (1) SC 471; 1997(1) SCALE 284; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 184; 1997 (2) Supreme 15; [1997] 90 TAXMAN 398; 1997 (2) TLR 275

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Wealth-Tax Act, 1957, s. 18B - Penalty for failure to furnish returns - Power of Commr. to waive penalty - Waiver need not necessarily be of entire amount - Held, order of Wealth-Tax Commr. reducing penalty by giving sufficient reasons - Is proper - No interference in appeal - Appeal dismissed.
