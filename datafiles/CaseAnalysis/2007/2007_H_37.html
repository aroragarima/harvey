Boddam Narsimha v Hasan Ali Khan (Dead) By L.R. and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 999; (2007) 11 SCC 410; AIR 2007 SC 1145; 2007 (1) ALD(SC) 101; 2007 (3) ALT 50; JT 2007 (2) SC 629; 2007(2) SCALE 274; [2007] 1 S.C.R. 1223

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Hyderabad Tenancy and Agricultural Land Act, 1950, Land Grabbing (Prohibition) Act, 1982, A. P. (Telangana Area) Tenancy & Agricultural Lands Act, 1950, Andhra Pradesh Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Book Act, 1971

Summary: Land & Property - Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1950, ss. 5, 37-A and 38E - Andhra Pradesh Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Book Act, 1971, s. 4 - Whether statute confers upon a tenant, who is in possession on 12-03-1956, status of a protected tenant and such a right continues even if person entitled thereto fails to move authorities for grant of a declaration? - It is evident from s. 38-E that said section has been enacted for those protected tenants who are declared to be protected tenants and included in Register prepared for that purpose - A person becomes a protected tenant when he is a holder on dates or for periods mentioned in ss. 35, 37 and 37-A - Once a person becomes a protected tenant, he is entitled to an Ownership Certificate u/s. 38 - Benefit of s. 38-E is given to persons who hold lands as protected tenants and who continue to hold lands as protected tenants on 1-1-1973 - Protected tenancy has to be enforced on 1973 - Held, having failed in civil court and before special court under Land Grabbing Act present appellant falsely claimed to be a protected tenant as on 1-1-1973 - Appeal dismissed.
