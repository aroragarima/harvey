Sheikh Zakir v State Of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

02 June 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 66; (1983) 4 SCC 10; (1983) SCC (Cr) 761; AIR 1983 SC 911; 1983 (9) ALR 452; 1983 CRLJ 1285; 1983 CrLR(SC) 413; 1983(1) SCALE 644; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 312

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - IPC, 1860, s. 375 - Appeal against conviction - Appellant accused of rape - No medical report - Whether conviction proper ? - Held, yes - Prosecutrix and her husband belonged to backward community living in a remote area - No idea that they had to run to a a doctor after the occurrence of the incident - Other evidences were reliable - The accused was guilty of rape and was convicted accordingly - Appeal dismissed.
