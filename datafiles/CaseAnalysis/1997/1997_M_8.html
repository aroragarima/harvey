State Of Kerala & Ors. v M. Bhaskaran Pillai & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1273; (1997) 5 SCC 432; AIR 1997 SC 2703; AIR 1997 SCW 2673; 1997 (3) CLT 32; JT 1997 (6) SC 22; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 79; 1997(4) SCALE 295; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 87; 1997 (6) Supreme 54

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.16 - Assignment of land acquired for public purpose - Remaining unutilised after achieving public purpose - If can be disposed of by way of sale to erstwhile owner - Held, should be put to public auction - Appeal dismissed.
