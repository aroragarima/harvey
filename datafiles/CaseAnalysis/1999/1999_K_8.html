Omparkash Shivprakash v K. I. Kuriakose and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 469; (1999) 8 SCC 633; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1; AIR 1999 SC 3870; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 727; 2000 CRLJ 26; JT 1999 (8) SC 569; [1999] Supp4 S.C.R. 269

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 20A and 20 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, ss. 254 and 319 - Impleadment of manufacturer, distributor or dealer - Held, is permissible only after evidence adduced in trial - Impleadment at stage of appearance of accused firm from where accused purchased - Premature.
