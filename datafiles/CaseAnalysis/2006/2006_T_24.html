Chairman Railway Board and Another v T. Vittal Rao and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 41; (2006) 2 SCC 467; AIR 2006 SC 3515; 2006 (5) AWC 4871; 2006 (1) CLR 753; JT 2006 (2) SC 161; 2006(2) SCALE 153; 2006 (2) SCJ 282; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 1097; 2006 (2) SLR 410

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Respondents were placed in supervisory category, and they became disentitled to draw overtime allowance in accordance with a circular 2.8.84 - Circular withdrawn on or about 11/4/2001 - Tribunal directed payment of overtime allowance in favour of respondents for period from 2/8/1984 to 11/4/2001 - WP dismissed - Appeal against - Held, Circular dated 11/4/2001 does not state that it is prospective in nature - It does not further more state that overtime allowance would be payable to respondents only after issuance thereof - Respondents entitled to overtime allowance for period from 2- 8- 84 to 11- 4- 01 - Appeal dismissed.
