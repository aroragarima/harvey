His Holiness Acharya Swami Ganesh Dassji v Sita Ram Thapar

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3930; (1996) 4 SCC 526; AIR 1996 SC 2095; JT 1996 (5) SC 460; 1996(4) SCALE 476; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 111

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 16(C) - An illustrious case of dilatory tactics - Agreement that draft sale deed should be finalised within seven days and sale deed registered thereby time being essence of contract - Inferrence of 'ready and willing to perform' from conduct and circumstances - Defendant/ vendor was in dire need of money to celebrate his daughter's marriage, where as purchaser not having enough funds - But draft sale deed not returned - Validity of High Court order refusing to enforce contract - Held, rightly refused to grant relief of specific performance to - Petition dismissed.
