Dena Nath And Ors v National Fertilizers Ltd. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Yogeshwar Dayal, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 384; (1992) SCC (L&S) 349; (1992) 1 SCC 695; AIR 1992 SC 457; 1992 (64) FLR 39; JT 1991 (4) SC 413; 1992 LabIC 75; 1992 (1) LLJ 289; 1992 (1) LLN 53; 1991(2) SCALE 1081; 1992 (1) SLR 229; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 401

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, ss. 7, 12 and 24 - Contract Labour - Principal employer or Contractor - Non-compliance with provisions of registration or license - Consequences - Held, only consequence is their exposure to prosecution - Contract labour employed does not become direct employees of principal employer - Appeal dismissed.
