Punjab Traders And Ors. v State Of Punjab And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, K.N. Singh, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 453; (1991) 1 SCC 86; AIR 1990 SC 2300; 1990(2) SCALE 603; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 499; [1990] supp1 S.C.R. 499

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: East Punjab Molasses (Control) Amendment Act 1973

Summary: Agriculture & Agri Products - East Punjab Molasses (Control) Amendment Act, 1973, - East Punjab Molasses (control) Act, 1948, arts. 2(c) and 2(f) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 304(b), Proviso - Validity and need of previous sanction of President - Held, Amendment Act of 1973 is merely clarificatory - Its object is to clarify that Principal Act i.e., E.P. Act 1948 applies to Khandsari units - Previous sanction of President not required - Appeal dismissed.
