Ashwani Kumar And Others Etc. Etc v State Of Bihar And Others Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 830; (1996) 7 SCC 577; (1996) SCC (L&S) 834; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 391; AIR 1996 SC 2833; JT 1995 (8) SC 563; 1996 (1) LLN 38; 1995(6) SCALE 779; 1995 (7) SLR 562; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 367

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Central Secretariat Service Rules, 1962, Central Secretariat Service Rules

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Appointment - Large number of appointments made by Govt. authority beyond sanctioned strength - Prescribed Procedure not followed - No advertisement inviting applications nor were names called from employment exchange - Appointments pursuant to notification of vacancies put up on notice Board - Held, breach of arts. 14 and 16(1).
