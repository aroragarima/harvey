Surya Narain Upadhyaya v Ram Roop Pandey and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 871; (1995) Supp4 SCC 542; AIR 1994 SC 105

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 20 and 16(c) - Sale-agreement - Specific performance - Purchaser pleading his readiness and willingness to perform and depositing balance amount of consideration in court - Not sufficient court-fee by purchaser - Drawing inference of incapacity for paying consideration and refusing relief of specific performance on such ground - Held, not proper as is irrelevant.
