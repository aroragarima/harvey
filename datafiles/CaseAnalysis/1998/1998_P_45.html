Sheel Chand v Prakash Chand

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 868; (1998) 6 SCC 683; AIR 1998 SC 3063; AIR 1998 SCW 2923; 1998 (3) AWC 2347; 1998 (4) CLT 61; JT 1998 (6) SC 192; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 410; 1998 (89) RD 623; 1998(5) SCALE 142; 1998 (7) SLT 331; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 297; 1998 (7) Supreme 166

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 100 - Tenancy - Suit for eviction - Bona fide requirement - Second appeal - Existence of substantial question of law is necessary for exercising jurisdiction by High Court u/s. 100 - Concurrent findings of lower courts - Proper appreciation of evidence - Validity of interference by HC in second appeal - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
