Supreme Court of India

31 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, Kuldip Singh, M.K. Mukherjee, M.M. Punchhi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1836; (1997) 1 SCC 326; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 473; AIR 1997 SC 1815; JT 1996 (10) SC 116; 1996(8) SCALE 71; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 193

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961, s. 18 (Amendment Act, 1978) - Acquisition of surplus land - Compensation - Computation - Notification D/- 4-4-1973 - Vesting of land - Compensation entitlement of landowner - Reduction of - Payable from 9 times to 2 times of net annual value - Validity - Held, it has effaced amendments which had been introduced by Act - Constitutional - No need to go into applicability of arts. 31 - A, 31 - B and 31 - C.
