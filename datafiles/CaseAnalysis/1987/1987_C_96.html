Vinod Coal Syndicate v Commissioner of Sales Tax, U. P., Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

30 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28112; 1989 (73) STC 317

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 2(i) Expln II - Appellant commission agent in coal business - Assessment completed including freight paid by principals in turnover of appellant - Whether freight which was separately charged can be included in taxable turnover of appellant ? - The legislature intended that where cost of freight was charged separately, that amount could not be included in turnover of a dealer - In present case, freight was separately charged and was paid accordingly by principals - Held, High Court erred in including cost of freight in taxable turnover of appellant - Appeal allowed.
