Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation v State of Uttar Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1026; (2005) 1 SCC 444; 2005 (1) ACC 51; AIR 2005 SC 446; 2005 (1) AWC 220; JT 2004 (10) SC 173; 2005 (1) RCR(Civil) 235; 2004(10) SCALE 28; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 442; 2005 (1) UC 617

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Grant Of Permit, CPC

Summary: [A.] Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 68C - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 100(2), 100(4), 103(1-A, 80 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 32 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Appeal against quashing of scheme dated 3/2/1986 published u/s.68-C and approved scheme published on 29/5/1993 - Held, a draft scheme made u/s.68-C would lapse after 30/6/1990 - But competent authority long before 30/6/1990 held that scheme had lapsed - Scheme had not lapsed, appellants are not entitled for renewal of their permits - Appeals disposed of.
