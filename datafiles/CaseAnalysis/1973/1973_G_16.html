Gujarat Electricity Board v Ahmedabad Electricity Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, R.S. Sarkaria, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 271; (1974) 4 SCC 623; AIR 1974 SC 314; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 492

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Electricity & Energy

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act,s. 46 -Electricity(supply) Act,1948,ss. 57 and 57 A, ch 6, Para XVI- Reference by respondent electricity Co. of an alleged dispute between itself, Appellant Board to arbitration of Central Electricity Authority -Dispute between licensee, Electricity Board - (A)Whether a dispute relating to non-compliance of provisions of Sixth Schedule between Board and licensee is referable to an arbitration? - In absence of any express provision substituting Board in place of State Govt. for purpose of arbitration in a dispute or difference between Board and licencee, second proviso to Section 57 A (1) cannot be construed as contemplating an arbitration before Authority in a dispute to which only licensee and Board are parties - Held, it is not that a dispute between Board and licensee is not referable to arbitration of C E Authority at all,ss. 44(2), 45(3) and 55(2) of Act of 1948 alone provide for such arbitration - A dispute relating to non-compliance of provisions of Sixth Schedule between Board and licensee is not referable to arbitration - (B) Whether arbitration clause in Sixth Schedule be described as 'an obligation' of State Govt. within s.60 of Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948 Act? - Under arbitration clause both State Govt. and licensee were equally entitled to refer their dispute or difference to arbitration of Authority and, similarly, equally, obliged thereunder to submit to its arbitration - Further held, such a clause cannot be described as an obligation incurred by State Govt. in favour of licensee for any of purposes of Act of 1948 - s. 60 of Act of 1948 cannot be invoked with a view to substitute Board in place of State Govt. for purposes of arbitration under para XVI - Matter remitted to High Court.
