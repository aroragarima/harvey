Bharat v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 528; (1971) 3 SCC 950; (1972) SCC (Cr) 198; 1970 UJ 484

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Evidence Act - Murder - Law of confession - Retracted confession - Unsafe to rely upon a retracted confession unless it is voluntary and has been corroborated in all material particulars - Held, circumstances which have been proved furnish corroboration for retracted confession - Confession was voluntary and true -Conviction upheld - Appeal dismissed.
