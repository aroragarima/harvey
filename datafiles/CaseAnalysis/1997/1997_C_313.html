Sahney Steel and Press Works Ltd. v Commissioner of Income Tax, A.P. - I, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1527; (1997) 7 SCC 764; AIR 1997 SC 3968; 1997 (142) CTR 261; [1997] 228 ITR 253; JT 1997 (8) SC 173; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 189; [1997] 94 TAXMAN 368; 1997 (2) TLR 1064

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Capital Receipt

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 28 - Character of subsidy in hands of recipient - Determination - Grant of subsidies/incentives to new industries by Govt. - In order to run profitably for period of five years from date of commencement of production - They are operational subsidies and not capital subsidies - Held, its character would be revenue and not capital - Appeal dismissed.
