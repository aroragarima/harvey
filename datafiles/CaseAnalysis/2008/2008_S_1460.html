Syed Abdul Qadir and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, H.S. Bedi, G.S. Singhvi

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1984; (2009) 3 SCC 475; (2009) 1 SCC (L&S) 744; 2010 (1) JhrCR(SC) 97; JT 2009 (1) SC 385; 2009(1) SCALE 36; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 917; 2008 (7) SLR 642

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Eligibility Conditions, Fixation, Tenure Post, Prospective Or Retrospective, Bihar Non-Government Secondary School [Take Over of Management and Control] Act, 1981

Summary: Service - Fixation of pay on promotion in respect of different classes of teachers - Whether provisions of FR.22-C or FR.22(I)(a)(1) and FR.22(I)(a)(2) would apply to the appellants-teachers? - Held, benefit of an additional increment would be extended to a government servant in the event of his being promoted or appointed to a substantive, temporary or officiating capacity to another post carrying duties and responsibilities of greater importance than those attaching to the post held by him - FR.22-C was substituted even prior to the issuance of Resolution dated 18.12.1989 and was replaced by FR.22(I)(a)(1) and FR.22(I)(a)(2) - Appellants - teachers on their promotion to the higher post/grade would be governed by the amended provisions of FR.22-C, i.e., FR.22(I)(a)(1) and FR.22(I)(a)(2) - No recovery of the amount that has been paid in excess to the appellants-teachers should be made - Appeals partly allowed.
