Smt. Poonam Lata v M.L. Wadhawan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28831; (1987) 3 SCC 347; (1987) SCC (Cr) 506; AIR 1987 SC 1383; 1987 (1) BankCLR 108; [1988] 63 Comp Cas 50; 1987 CRLJ 1130; 1987 CRLR 410; 1987 CrLR(SC) 410; 1987 (12) ECC 346; 1987 (30) E.L.T. 3; JT 1987 (2) SC 204; 1987(1) SCALE 849; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1123

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs

Summary: Criminal - COFEPOSA Act, 1974, ss.9, 10, 12 - Customs Act, 1962, ss.108, 3(1) - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, ss.3(1), 110(1) - Parole - Preventive detention 'with a view to preventing him from dealing in smuggled goods' - Supreme Court released detenu on parole - Contention that period of parole could not be added to the maximum period of detention of the detenu - Entertainment of application for release on parole - Permissibility - Held, impermissible - Petition dismissed.
