Miles India Limited v Assistant Collector of Customs

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 7; 1985 ECR 289; 1987 (30) E.L.T. 641

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, s. 27(1) - Period of limitation - Claim for refund - Whether customs authorities, justified in disallowing claim for refund as they were bound by period of limitation provided therefore, u/s. 27(1) of Customs Act? - Held, Yes - No infirmity in CEGAT order - Appeal dismissed.
