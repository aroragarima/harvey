Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 166 - Accidental claim - Computation of Multiplier - Deceased, aged 33 years, working as a mechanic operator, died due to injuries sustained in the accident - Appellants (mother and brother of deceased) filed a claim petition before the Motor Accident Claims Tribunal (Tribunal), claiming Rs.1,75,000 as compensation - Tribunal held that the accident had occurred in view of the rash and negligent driving of the second respond
