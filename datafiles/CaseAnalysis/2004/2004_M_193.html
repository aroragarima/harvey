M/S. Pearlite Liners Pvt. Ltd. v Manorama Sirsi

Supreme Court of India

06 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arun Kumar, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 41; (2004) 3 SCC 172; (2004) SCC (L&S) 453; AIR 2004 SC 1373; 2004 (1) AWC 764; 2004 (2) CLR 965; 2004 (1) ESC 116; 2004 (100) FLR 797; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 1; JT 2004 (1) SC 58; 2004 (1) LLJ 1041; 2004 (1) LLN 516; 2004 (3) MahLJ 563; 2004 (2) MLJ(SC) 141; 2004 (3) MPLJ 367; 2004(1) SCALE 280; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 266; 2004 (1) SLR 728; 2004 (1) SLT 647; 2004 (1) Supreme 56; 2004 (1) UPLBEC 489

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 - Specific performance - Contract of personal service - Prayers for injunction against employer - Would tantamount to enforcing contract of personal service - Suit rightly dismissed at threshold by trial court - Order restored - Appeal allowed.
