Commissioner of Income Tax, Kerala v Dharmodayam Company

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 216; (1977) 4 SCC 75; AIR 1977 SC 2211; 1977 CTR 341; [1977] 109 ITR 527; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 319; 1977 UJ 571

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Chit Fund, Exclusion From Total Income

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.11(1)(a) and 2(15) - Exemption from Income Tax - Charitable trust - Business of conducting kuries - Not undertaken by assessee to advance any object of general public utility - Income there from if exempted - Business itself held under trust for religious or charitable purposes - Held, income is exempt u/s. 11(1)(a) of the Act - Appeals Dismissed.
