Deputy Commissioner Of Sales Tax Etc. Etc. v A.B. Ismail Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, P.N. Bhagwati, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 416; (1986) Supp SCC 218; AIR 1987 SC 1885; 1986 (2) CCC 567; JT 1986 SC 427; 1986(1) SCALE 789; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 522; 1986 (62) STC 394; 1986 TAXLR 2382

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Sales Tax

Keywords: Processing

Summary: Sales Tax - Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963, s. 5A - Whether Goat and sheep and the meat got after slaughtering them are same for purpose of sales tax Held, lifeless mutton is 'other goods' different from 'goat and sheep' within the meaning of s. 5A of the Act and High Court fell in an error when it used the expression 'meat of the goat' while discussing the facts - Appeal allowed
