Gulabbai v Nalin Narsi Vohra And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 689; (1991) 3 SCC 483; AIR 1991 SC 1760; AIR 1991 SCW 1971; 1991 (3) Bom.C.R. 174; 1991 (2) GLH 1; JT 1991 (3) SC 112; 1991 (2) MLJ(SC) 28; 1991 (2) RCR(Rent) 453; 1991 (2) RentLR 151; 1991(2) SCALE 60; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 941; 1991 (2) UJ 372

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 227 - Suit for eviction on ground of bona fide requirement of landlord - During pendency of proceedings landlord coming into possession of alternative - Whether subsequent events can be considered by Court - Held, in appropriate cases events subsequent to filing of eviction suit can be taken notice of and can be duly considered by Court if same is relevant in determining question of bona fide requirement - Appeal dismissed.
