Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Pathanamthitta and Others v A. Gopalan

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 297; (1997) 11 SCC 239; AIR 1999 SC 1514; 1999 (82) FLR 784; JT 1998 (9) SC 332; 1999 LabIC 234; 1999 (1) LLJ 1313; 2000 (1) LLN 92

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Departmental proceedings and criminal proceedings on same charge against officer - Effect of acquittal by criminal court - Held, does not conclude departmental proceedings in respect of same charge - Appeal allowed.
