P. C. Cheriyan v Barfi Devi

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 306; (1980) 2 SCC 461; AIR 1980 SC 86; 1979 ALJ 1266; 1979 (5) ALR 622; 1979 (4) E.L.T. 593; 1980 (1) RCJ 268; 1980 (1) RentLR 1; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 961; 1979 UJ 899

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Whether words and Phrases 'Manufacturing purposes' u/s. 106 include business of retreading of tyres - Held, does not include - Lease for carrying on the business of retreading of tyres is not a lease for 'manufacturing purposes' - Appeal dismissed .
