Supreme Court of India

05 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 656; (1992) Supp1 SCC 304; (1993) SCC (L&S) 675; AIR 1991 SC 1406; AIR 1991 SCW 1246; 1993 (24) ATC 559; 1991 (62) FLR 938; JT 1991 (2) SC 123; 1991 LabIC 1227; 1991 (2) LLJ 50; 1991 (1) LLN 1055; 1991(1) SCALE 601; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 198; 1991 (3) SLJ 1; 1991 (2) SLR 406

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Year Of Allotment, Haryana Service of Engineers, Class I, PWD (Roads and Buildings Branch) rules 1960, Uttar Pradesh Forest Service Rules, 1952

Summary: Service - Haryana Service of Engineers, Class I, P W D (Roads And Buildings) Rules, 1960, rr. 2(1) and 2(7) - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Service matter - (A) Promotion to Class I - Inter se seniority among promotees and direct recruits of Class II - Promotee occupying 50% quota of direct recruits - Acquires no right to post - Has to yield to direct recruit though promoted later - Seniority of promotee from Class II service - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Proviso - Main function is to carve exception to main enactment - Held, is to determine from date on which cadre post is available to him - It cannot normally be so interpreted as to set at naught real object of main enactment - Order accordingly.
