Raghunath Thakur v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 229; (1989) 1 SCC 229; AIR 1989 SC 620; JT 1988 (4) SC 728; 1988(2) SCALE 1326; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 867; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 867; 1989 (1) UJ 6

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Contract & Commercial - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19(1)(g)(6), 226 and 299 - Govt. contractor - Black listing - Applicability of rule of natural justice - In absence of express rule - Held, affected person has right of being heard and representation - Order accordingly.
