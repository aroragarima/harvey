Boddula Krishnaiah and Another v State Election Commissioner, A.P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 659; (1996) 3 SCC 416; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 128; AIR 1996 SC 1595; 1996 (2) ALT 21; 1996 (2) CLT 106; JT 1996 (4) SC 156; 1996(3) SCALE 301; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 687; 1996 (3) Supreme 648

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Election of Gram Panchayat - High Court directed not to declare result and to conduct fresh poll for 20 persons - Legality of HC direction in writ petition challenged - Held, illegal - Appeal allowed.
