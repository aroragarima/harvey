Â Punjab Distilling Industries Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Punjab

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, R.S. Bachawat, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 380; AIR 1965 SC 1862; [1965] 35 Comp Cas 541; 1965 (2) CompLJ 55; 1965 (2) ITJ 110; [1965] 57 ITR 1; [1965] 3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Govt. of India Act, 1935, Sch. 7, list 1, item 54 - Constitution, Sch. 7, list 1, entry 82 - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 2(6 A)(d) and 16(2) - Income Tax - Company - Distribution of dividends - Issues are - (A) Validity of s.2(6 A)(d) of 1922 Act - (B) Which is crucial for assesment : year of credit /payment to shareholder or date of declaration in scope of s. 16(2) of 1922 Act - Held, provision falls within entry 54 of List I of Sch. 7 to Govt. of India Act, 1935 - Year of credit or payment to shareholder is crucial - Appeal dismissed.
