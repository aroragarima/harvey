Vidya Vati v Devi Das

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 347; (1977) 1 SCC 293; AIR 1977 SC 397; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 182; 1977 UJ 7

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 115, O. 43, r. 1(w), O. 47, r. 4 , O. 7, r . 7 - (A) Scope of s. 115 - Order of Sub-judge allowing review application - Appeal against such order lying before District Judge and not to High Court - Maintainability of Revision against said order before HC - (B) Suit for possession decreed ex-parte - Directing plaintiff to pay certain amount to defendant or deposit the same in Court - In view of agreement, obligation to make payment found to be concurrent with obligation to hand over possession - On review application, Courts below directing deletion of aforesaid direction - Held, maintainable - Order allowing review application must be set aside - Appeal allowed.
