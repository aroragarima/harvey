Union of India and Another v Major J. S. Khanna

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 576; (1972) 3 SCC 873; (1973) SCC (Cr) 94; 1972 CRLJ 849; 1972 UJ 119

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code,ss. 197 and 173 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 120B and 420 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947,ss. 6 and 5 - Criminal conspiracy to defraud Govt. of India - Special judge framed charges against respondents but High Court quashed proceedings - Whether quashing of proceedings by HC just and proper? - Held, HC was right in quashing proceedings as there was no prima facie case against respondents - Appeal dismissed.
