Megna Mills Co. Ltd. v Ashoka Marketing Co.

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 137; (1970) 3 SCC 168; AIR 1971 SC 166; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 751

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Forward Contracts (Regulation ) Act, 1952

Summary: Forward Contracts (Regulation) Act, 1952, ss. 11 and 15 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - East India Jute and Hessain Exchange Ltd., Bye-laws. 1(b), 15 and 17 - Non compliance of Bye-law 1(b), which is mandatory - Effect upon contract - Held, non compliance thereof invalidates contract - Appeal Dismissed.
