Devi Dass v Mohan Lal

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 507; (1982) 1 SCC 495; AIR 1982 SC 1213

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Constitution, art. 136 - Tenancy - Eviction Order passed in petition by purchaser of building - Without giving definite finding - Plea of tenant that sale was sham transaction - But lower court failed to record finding in this regard - Held, eviction order is invalid - Tenant entitled to challenge transaction as sham - Case remitted - Appeal Allowed.
