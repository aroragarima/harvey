Â Anakapalle Coop. Agrl. & Industrial Societyltd. Etc. Etc v Union Of India & Others

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.K. Mukherjea, Y.V. Chandrachud, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 334; (1973) 3 SCC 435; AIR 1973 SC 734; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 882

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Payment of Bonus Amendment Ordinance 1972, Levy Sugar Supply Control Order 1972

Summary: Service - Payment of Bonus Amendment Ordinance 1972 - Levy Sugar Supply Control Order 1972 - Gratuity - Payment of additional bonus - Promulgation of Ordinance -- Sugar producers challenging validity and legality of 1972 Order made u/s.3 of 1955 Act fixing price of levy sugar in different zones in country and praying for various reliefs - Whether items in respect of payment of additional bonus as provided by Ordinance 1972 and gratuity was justifiable - Held, prices as fixed by impugned order could not be struck down because of promulgation of Ordinance by which amount of minimum bonus had been raised from 4% to 8.33% of salary or wages earned by employees - Bonus Ordinance had been promulgated after prices were fixed by impugned order -- Order could not be struck down on ground that prices fixed by it did not take into account changes in rate of minimum bonus made by Ordinance - Govt. ought to make modifications in impugned order in respect of prices of levy sugar so as to adjust them in accordance with provisions of Ordinance - Petition dismissed.
