Oswal Agro Mills Limited Etc. Etc. v Collector Of Central Excise And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1097; (1993) Supp3 SCC 716; AIR 1993 SC 2288; AIR 1993 SCW 1782; 1993 (47) ECR 14; 1993 (66) E.L.T. 37; JT 1993 (3) SC 260; 1993(2) SCALE 660; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 378

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Tariff Item, Soap, Finance Act, 1964

Summary: Civil - Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - S.8 - Finance Act, 1964 - Classification of toilet soap in tariff item 15 of the Schedule to the Central Excise and Salt Act - Whether the said article would fall within the scope of entry household in Item 15 of Schedule - Held, Yes - Toilet soap used for a number of purposes depending upon preference and needs of individual user - No industrial use of the toilet soap - Classification should be having regard to the headings and the relevant provisions or statutory rules or interpretation - To charge excise duty the article must be specified in positive terms under a particular tariff entry - Appeal allowed.
