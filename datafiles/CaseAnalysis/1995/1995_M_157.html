State of Punjab and Others v Mohabir Singh Etc

Supreme Court of India

21 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1946; (1996) 1 SCC 609; AIR 1996 SC 2994; 1996 (1) CLT 188; JT 1995 (9) SC 301; 1995(7) SCALE 278; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 520; 1996 (1) Supreme 235

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Registration Act, 1908 - Stamp Act, 1899, s. 47A(1) - Undervaluation of instrument - Guidelines prescribed by state for valuation of property on basis of which Registrar satisfies himself as to whether property was valued as per prevailing market rate - Whether guidelines in question prescribing only minimum prices are inconsistent with s. 47A (1) - Held, are inconsistent - State Govt. directed to revise guidelines - Appeal partly allowed.
