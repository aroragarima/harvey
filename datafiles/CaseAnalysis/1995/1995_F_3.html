Regional Executive, Kerala Fishermen's Welfare Fund Board v Fancy Food and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1055; (1995) 4 SCC 341; AIR 1995 SC 1620; JT 1995 (4) SC 394; 1995 (2) KLT 77; 1996 (1) LLJ 757; 1996 (1) LLN 32; 1995(3) SCALE 273; 1995 (2) SCJ 216; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 785

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Service

Keywords: Marine Products Export Development Authority Act, 1972, Kerala Fishermen's Welfare Fund Act, 1985, Kerala Marine Fishing Regulation Act, 1980

Summary: Kerala Fishermen's Welfare Fund Act, 1985, s. 2(d) - Objective of Act - 'Processing for export' - 'Dealer' - Held, promoting welfare of fishermen - Every process either freezing or applying chemical is within definition - Persons exporting fish after processing it after purchase from agents are dealer.
