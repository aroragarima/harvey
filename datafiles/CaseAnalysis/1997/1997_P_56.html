Collector of Customs, Bombay v Perfect Machine Tools Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1583; (1998) 9 SCC 418; 1999 (80) ECR 306; 1997 (96) E.L.T. 214; JT 1998 (8) SC 406; 1997 (23) RLT 563

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Tariff Act, 1975 - Customs Act, s. 25(1) - Notification No. 49/E No Bud (Cus)/78 D/ - 1-3-1978 - Whether concessional rate of customs duty on basis of said Notification can be held to be applicable in respect of Electronic Pitch Tester Attachment imported by respondent? - When an accessory to a machine is imported with machine, unless accessory has been expressly included in exemption notification it would not be entitled to concessional rate of customs duty under notification - Held, it must be held that since notification providing for concessional rate of customs duty has not expressly included accessories for purpose of grant of concessional duty, benefit of concessional duty would not be available in respect of such accessory - Appeal allowed.
