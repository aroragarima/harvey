Automotive Manufacturers Private Limited and Others v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, I.D. Dua, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 294; (1972) 1 SCC 125; AIR 1972 SC 229; 1972 (1) MLJ(SC) 58; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 593; 1972 UJ 300

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles Taxation Act 1963

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1963, ss. 3 and 9 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 2(18) - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 133 - (A) Chassis -(1) Exigible to Tax - (2) Exemption under item 4 of Notification u/s 9 - Availability - To chassis for disposal at journey's end - (B) New plea - Admissibility - Consideration of a contention not urged in writ petition before High Court - Held, chassis is exigible to tax as a motor vehicle notwithstanding that no body is attached to it - Exemption under item 4 of Notification u/s. 9 is not available to chassis driven along roads of Andhra Pradesh for disposal at journey's end - Supreme Court refused to consider contention - Appeal dismissed.
