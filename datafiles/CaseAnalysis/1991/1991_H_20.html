Regional Director, Employee’S State Insurance Corporation v High Land Coffee Works Of P.F.X. Saldanha And Sons Andanr

Supreme Court of India

26 July 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, Veeraswami Ramaswami, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 714; (1991) 3 SCC 617; AIR 1992 SC 129; 1991 (2) CLR 479; 1991 (63) FLR 423; JT 1991 (3) SC 325; 1992 LabIC 58; 1992 (1) LLJ 287; 1991 (2) LLN 462; 1991(2) SCALE 221; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 307; 1991 (5) SLR 88; 1991 (2) UJ 596

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Seasonal, Employees State Insurance (Amendment) Act, 1966

Summary: Insurance - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948, ss. 2(12) and 1(4) - 'Seasonal factory' - Amendment widens definition of seasonal factory - Coffee factory declared to be seasonal factory before amendment - After amendment - Whether excluded from operation of Act - Held, continue to be seasonal factory after amendment and therefore excluded from operation of Act - Appeal dismissed.
