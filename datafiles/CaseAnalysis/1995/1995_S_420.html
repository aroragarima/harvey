Arvind Industries and Others v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 316; (1995) 6 SCC 53; AIR 1995 SC 2477; JT 1995 (7) SC 220; 1995(4) SCALE 843; 1995 (99) STC 333; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 16

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Gujarat Sales Tax Act, 1970, s. 49 - Applicability of promissory estoppel on Govt. withdrawing concession given to new industry, challenged - In absence of any promise in notification giving concession, as to its alteration - Held, claim not tenable - Estoppel is not attracted - Appeal dismissed.
