Hansraj Bagrecha v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 163; (1971) 1 SCC 59; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 412; 1971 (27) STC 4

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Declared Goods, Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959, Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958, Bihar Sales Tax Rules, 1959, Bihar Finance Act, 1966, Andhra Pradesh Sugar Cant (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act

Summary: Sales Tax - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 301, 302, 303 and 304(b) - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 3A, 5A, 42 and 46 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 14 and 15 - Bihar Sales Tax Rules, 1959, rr. 30(1), 31B and 8C - (A) Whether ss. 3A and 5 A as incorporated by 4th Finance Act, 1966 is ultra vires Constitution, being violative of arts. 301 and 304(b)? - Argument that imposition of sales or purchase-tax must be regarded in all cases as infringing guarantee of freedom u/art. 301 cannot be accepted as correct - Power to impose purchase tax u/s. 3A on notified goods is not shown to restrict or impede free flow of trade directly and immediately, it need not seek to derive, for its validity, support from art. 304(b) - Held, impugned provisions are intra vires Constitution - (B) Whether ss. 3A and 5A and r. 8C are void on account of being contrary to s. 15 of Central Act? - Purchase tax on jute is leviable at first point of purchase u/s. 3A of Bihar Act, and rate of tax also is not shown to exceed maximum prescribed by s. 15 of Central Act - Further held, provisions of ss. 3A and 5A of Bihar Act are not therefore inconsistent with provisions of s. 15 of Central Act - (C) Whether r. 31B framed by State Govt. and notification issued on 26-12-1967 were unauthorized and liable to be struck down? - State Legislature has no power to legislate for levy of tax on transactions which are carried on in course of inter-state trade or commerce or in course of export - Rule authorizes restrictions on inter-state transactions and is on that account unauthorized - For same reasons notification issued must be regarded as also unauthorized - Further held, r. 31B and notification issued by State Govt. on 26-12-1967 must be declared ultra vires - Appeal allowed.
