Tata Oil Mills Co. Limited v Collector Of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 193; (1989) 4 SCC 541; (1990) SCC (Tax) 22; AIR 1990 SC 27; 1989 CRLR 691; 1989 CrLR(SC) 691; 1989 (24) ECC 354; 1989 (24) ECR 645; 1989 (43) E.L.T. 183; JT 1989 (3) SC 382; 1989(2) SCALE 313; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 839; 1991 (82) STC 225

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Soap

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, item 15 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Notification Nos. 46 of 1972, 153 of 1973 and 25 of 1975 - Levy of Excise Duty - Manufacture of soap - Concession - Notification granting it, where percentage of rice bran oil used in manufacture exceeds 15% of total oil - Manufacturer using rice bran fatty acid for manufacture of soap - Whether entitled to exemption? - Held, he would also be entitled to exemption under Notification - Appeal allowed.
