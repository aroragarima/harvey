> v Central Warehousing Corporation

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 653; (1986) 2 SCC 343; AIR 1986 SC 1164; 1986 (1) CCC 990; 1986 CRLR 220; 1986 CrLR(SC) 220; 1986(1) SCALE 1265; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 603; 1986 (2) UJ 56

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land Acquisition Act - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Acquisition - Reduction of compensation - Acquisition on behalf of company or local authority - Company cannot challenge amount of compensation by writ petition in view of bar contained in s. 50(2) - High Court entertained writ challenging awards made by collector under Land Acquisition Act - Held, HC wrong in entertaining writ petitions challenging awards and claiming that amount awarded was excessive.
