Chandra Bhan Gosain v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 401; AIR 1967 SC 767; 1964 (30) CLT 145; 1963 (2) ITJ 467; [1963] 50 ITR 195; 1963 KLT 1063; 1964 (1) SCJ 575; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 885; 1963 (14) STC 766

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 132, 133 and 226 - Determination of Court fee for appeal before Supreme Court against petition u/art. 226 of Constitution challenging various assessment orders - Held, appellant liable only to pay one set of court fee and other charges as in a single appeal.
