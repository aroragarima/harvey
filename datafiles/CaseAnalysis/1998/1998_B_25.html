Union of India and Another v Balbir Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, G.T. Nanavati, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 965; (1998) 5 SCC 216; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1310; AIR 1998 SC 2043; 1998 (77) ECR 11; 1998 (79) FLR 694; JT 1998 (3) SC 695; 1998 LabIC 1898; 1999 (1) LLJ 735; 1998 (3) LLN 599; 1998(3) SCALE 422; 1998 (3) SLJ 202; 1998 (4) SLR 351; 1998 (5) SLT 195; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 80; 1998 (4) Supreme 425

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311(2), Proviso(c) - Dismissal was ordered without enquiry - Ground of activities subversive to national security - Satisfaction of President or Governor - Held, circumstances on which it is based can be examined by Court.
