Union of India Through Inspector, Cbi v Purnandu Biswas

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 611; (2005) 12 SCC 576; JT 2005 (12) SC 505; 2005(8) SCALE 246; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 181

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Illegal Gratification

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 211 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Prevention Of Corruption Act, 1988, ss. 13(1)(d), 13(2), 20 - Appeal against acquittal - Held, demand of illegal gratification by Respondent has not been proved - s. 20 of Act is not attracted as Respondent had been charged for commission of an offence u/s. 13(1)(d) r/w s.13(2) - Appeal dismissed.
