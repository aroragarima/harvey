Punjab University, Chandigarh v Vijay Singh Lamba Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 144; (1976) 3 SCC 344; AIR 1976 SC 1441; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 67; 1976 UJ 536

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Punjab University Act, 1947 - Punjab University Calendar, 1973, Vol II, Chap. II, regn. 31 and 32(1) - University - Standing committee - Nominating members, 3 - Quorum, any 2 - Notice of meetings served on all - Proceedings - Disqualifying students - (A) Decision challenged on jurisdiction as only 2 members were present - (B) Precedents - Judicial consistency - Held, cannot be said to be without jurisdiction - Change supported but not frequent - Appeal allowed.
