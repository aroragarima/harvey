Messrs Mahaluxmi Rice Mills and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 828; (1998) 6 SCC 590; AIR 1999 SC 147; 1998 (3) AWC 2282; JT 1998 (5) SC 603; 1998(4) SCALE 594; 1998 (7) SLT 91; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 8; 1998 (6) Supreme 477; 1998 (3) UPLBEC 1984

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Socio-Economic; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Krishi Utpadan Mandi Adhiniyam, 1964, s.17 - Levy of market fee - Transaction of sale for that purpose - What is selling of rice to Government under the levy order - Market committee has the powers to levy market fee - Sale transaction falling within purview of s.17 (iii) (b) (3) - Held, it is a transaction of sale and market committee is entitled to collect market fee from seller - It is for seller to pass the burden on purchaser if he so chooses.
