Sunil Gupta And Ors. v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 1052; (1990) 3 SCC 119; (1990) SCC (Cr) 440; 1990 (2) Crimes 504; 1990 CRLR 441; 1990 CrLR(SC) 441; JT 1990 (2) SC 372; 1990 SCALE 22; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 871; 1990 (2) UJ 535

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Obscene, Handcuff, Madhya Pradesh Police Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Constitution - Madhya Pradesh Police Regulations - Whether detention of petitioners illegal as made without order or remand and whether petitioners entitled for compensation for hand-cuffing and inhumane treatment? - Held, petition dismissed but as far as issue of handcuffing is concerned, it is an inhumane treatment and petitioners may initiate appropriate actions against erring officers - Petitions disposed of.
