T. Fenn Walter and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 July 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, B.N. Kirpal, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1733; (2002) 6 SCC 184; AIR 2002 SC 2679; 2002 (4) CompLJ 170; 2002 (2) CPJ(Sc) 1; 2002 (3) CPR 97; 2002 (94) FLR 745; 2002 (3) G.L.R. 2678; 2002 (3) JhrCR(SC) 78; JT 2002 (5) SC 149; 2002 LabIC 2643; 2002 (3) MLJ(SC) 189; 2002(5) SCALE 185; 2002 (3) SCJ 309; 2002 (8) SLR 181; 2002 (4) SLT 393; [2002] Supp1 S.C.R. 134; 2002 (4) Supreme 577; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1858

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution

Keywords: High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, Inter-State Water Disputes Act, 1956, National Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 124(4), 217(1), 221, 226 and 227 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985 - Inter-State Water Disputes Act, 1956 - High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, s.2(1)(c)(i) - Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 16A - Group of advocates alleging that a sitting judge of that HC ceased to be a judge after being appointed as a member or Chairman of a tribunal and commission - Appeal - Held, if a sitting judge is appointed, considerable time is lost and judge would not be in a position to attend to his regular judicial work - In view of mounting arrears of cases in superior courts, it would be difficult to lend services of a Judge for such commission work - Moreover, report of Commission of Inquiry is often stated to have only recommendatory value and opinions expressed therein are not binding on Govt. - Appointment of a sitting Judge as a Commission of Inquiry has to be made only on rare occasions if it becomes necessary for paramount national interest of country - Having regard to various constitutional issues raised by counsel on either side inclined to formulate some broad guidelines as to manner in which appointment of a sitting judge of a HC to a commission should be dealt with - Appeal disposed of.
