Ram Chandra Mawa Lal and Others v State Of Uttar Pradesh And Others Etc.

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, M.P. Thakkar, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 201; (1984) Supp SCC 28; AIR 1987 SC 1837; 1984 ALJ 235; 1984 (10) ALR 539; 1984(1) SCALE 48; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 348

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Keywords: Constitutional Jurisdiction, Sale Price, Batch, Defence Of India Act, 1971, Defence of India Rules 1971, Fertilizer Control Order 1957, Uttar Pradesh Fertiliser Prices (SUttar Pradeshplementary) Order 1974

Summary: Socio Economic - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Defence of India Rules, 1962, r. 114(3)(h) - Power of fixing price of essential commodity - Fertiliser - State Govt. issued notification fixing price of fertiliser - Price already fixed by Central Govt. under Fertiliser Control - Held, State Govt. has power to fix the price of fertilizer under 1971 Rules and 1955 Act, as they are Central Legislations enacted by the Parliament - The Parliament having competence to legislate in regard to the subject namely, fixation of all prices of all articles has enacted both the legislations - Appeal allowed.
