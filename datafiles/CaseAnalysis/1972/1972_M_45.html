Commissioner of Income Tax, Poona v Manna Ramji and Company

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 264; (1973) 3 SCC 43; AIR 1973 SC 515; 1973 (2) CTR 201; 1973 (1) ITJ 313; [1972] 86 ITR 29; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 1068; 1973 TAXLR 362

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Capital Receipt, Estate, Revenue Receipt

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 28 and 256 - (A) Revenue receipt or Capital receipt - Determination of - Compensation in respect of requisition of part of business premises not resulting in complete closure of business - (B) Use of precedents - Finding of facts of the tribunal - Held, is a revenue receipt - Held, are binding on High Court and Supreme Court - Appeal allowed.
