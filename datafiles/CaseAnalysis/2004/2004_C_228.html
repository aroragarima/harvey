Ghasitey Lal Sahu and another v Competent Authority, Under The Urban (Ceiling And Regulation Act, 1976), Uttar Pradesh and another

Supreme Court of India

30 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 2019; (2004) 13 SCC 452

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Repeal Act,1999, s.4 - Abatement of proceedings - Appellant sought for abatement of proceedings - Hence, instant Appeal.

Held, counsel for appellants urged that having regard to s.4 of the Act, proceedings abate. Appellant submitted that possession of the land in question has not been taken. Appellant further submitted that, all along, there had been interim orders in favour of the appellants and SC had granted interim order. Counsel for respondent states that in view of the letter, appeal may be disposed of. Thus, having regard to s.4 of the Act, proceedings under the Act abate and impugned order will not have any effect and shall not remain in operation. Appeal disposed of.
