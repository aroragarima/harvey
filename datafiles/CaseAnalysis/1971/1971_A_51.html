Commissioner of Income Tax (Central), Calcutta v Associated Industrial Development Company Private

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 364; (1972) 4 SCC 447; AIR 1972 SC 445; [1971] 82 ITR 586; 1972 TAXLR 240

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 66 - Nature of jurisdiction of High Court - Reference - High Court considering a case depending on facts which was not set up by before the Income-tax Authorities and the tribunal - Held, HC exceeds its authority in returning an answer in favour of the assessee by considering a case depending on facts which was not set up by him before the Income tax Authorities and the tribunal and in support of which no material was placed by him before them - Appeal allowed.
