The Divisional Manager, Apsrtc & Ors v P. Lakshmoji Rao & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 26; (2004) SCC (L&S) 416; (2004) 2 SCC 433; AIR 2004 SC 1503; 2004 (3) ALD(SC) 1; 2004 (2) CLR 1016; 2004 (3) ESC 397; 2004 (100) FLR 951; JT 2004 (5) SC 217; 2004 (1) LLJ 1045; 2004 (1) LLN 1154; 2004(1) SCALE 654; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 929; 2004 (2) SLR 61; 2004 (1) Supreme 1029

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 12(3), 25B - Regular appointment and seniority - Effective date of regular appointment - Non-application of mind by HC to crucial aspects of case - Held, respondent-employees have failed to establish their legal right to get status of regular employees right from date of their initial appointment on daily wage basis - Further held, respective dates of regularisation assigned to respondents cannot be legally faulted - Directions issued.
