Assistant Director of Mines and Geology v Deccan Cements Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 93; (2008) 3 SCC 451; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1442; 2008 (222) E.L.T. 321; 2008(2) SCALE 6; [2008] 2 S.C.R. 32; 2008 (9) S.T.R. 449

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Mines & Minerals

Keywords: Matter referred to Larger Bench, Cess And Other Taxes On Minerals, Cess and Other Taxes on Minerals (Validation) Act, 1992

Summary: Cess and Other Taxes on Minerals (Validation) Act, 1992 - High Court held that though levy up to 4.4.1991 was permissible, no collection of cess could be made reliying on District Mining Officer and Ors. v. Tata Iron and Steel Co. and Anr. (2001 (7) SCC 358) - Appeal against - Matter referred to a larger bench to test the correctness of the conclusions that the levy was permissible by the Validation Act, but amounts which have not already been collected, cannot be collected - Order accordingly.
