Mushtaq Ahmad v Mohd. Habibur Rehman Faizi and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3183; (1996) 7 SCC 440; (1996) SCC (Cr) 443; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 895; AIR 1996 SC 2982; 1996 ALJ 600; 1996 (1) CCR 154; 1996 (1) Crimes 80; 1996 CRLJ 1877; JT 1996 (1) SC 656; 1996 (4) KarLJ 391; 1996(1) SCALE 606; 1996 (1) Supreme 689

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 482 - Quashing of Criminal complaint - Documents prima facie suggesting case of cheating, breach of trust and forgery - High Court entering to decide truthfulness of version either of complaint or petition of accused u/s. 482 - Held, is not permissible - Order quashing complaint set aside.
