Kishori Singh and Others v State of Bihar and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3149; (2004) 13 SCC 11; AIR 2000 SC 3725; 2000 (4) Crimes 158; 2001 CRLJ 123; JT 2000 (7) SC 564

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Non-bailable Warrant

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 173 and 319 - Offence only triable by Court of Session - Stage prior to one under s. 319 - Issue of process by Magistrate to persons named in FIR but not charge-sheeted under s. 173 - Power of - Held, Magistrate cannot issue process against them.
