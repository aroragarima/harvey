Parripati Chandresekharrao and Sons v Alapati Jalaiah

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1068; (1995) 3 SCC 709; AIR 1995 SC 1781; JT 1995 (4) SC 187; 1995 (2) RCJ 196; 1995 (1) RCR(Rent) 591; 1995 (2) RentLR 183; 1995(3) SCALE 197; 1995 (2) SCJ 317; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 817

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 26 - Property - Landlord's rights under common law and protection to tenant under special legislation - Distinction - Prevalence of right or protection - Held, rights suspended and protective shield available till special legislation operates - Protection does not create any vested right in tenant - Petition dismissed.
