United Bank Of India v Official Liquidator

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1435; (1994) 1 SCC 575; AIR 1993 SCW 3586; 1994 (1) CalLT 50; [1994] 79 Comp Cas 262; 1993 (3) CompLJ 414; JT 1993 (6) SC 116; 1993(4) SCALE 19

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Onerous

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 448 and 535 - Whether Trust was entitled to claim price of said land which had been sold to Triputi and his submission that he should be given part payment in respect thereof out of amount which had been deposited by Triputi? - Held, rights of Trust, as of all other creditors, had to be determined either by court or by any other authority under directions of court and that it was not inclined for amount to go into question - Appeal disposed of.
