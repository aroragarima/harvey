Frozen Foods Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Pune

Supreme Court of India

08 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 58; (1998) 2 SCC 460; 1998 (98) E.L.T. 295

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Brand product - Question of entry - Question involved in this appeal is as to under what entry does product, sold under brand name of one 'S' which is a dietary supplement of superior proteins, fall - According to appellant, this product would come under Heading 04.04, whereas according to Department, it would fall under Heading 21.07 - Tribunal had concluded that product in question would come under Heading 21.07 - Hence instant appeal.

Held, in Courtâs opinion in this particular case, Court sees no reason to interfere with order of Tribunal. Appeal is accordingly dismissed. Appeal dismissed.
