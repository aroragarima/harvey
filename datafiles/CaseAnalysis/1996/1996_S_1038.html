State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Raj Karan Singh

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2716; (1998) 8 SCC 529; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1709

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Interim direction of Supreme Court permitted ad hoc appointment of respondent to continue till a regularly selected candidate became available - Division Bench of High Court directed that since respondent was retained in service beyond one year, he should be treated as on 'regular service' - Held, Division Bench order goes beyond relief claimed by respondent in writ petition itself - Besides, merely because a person continues under interim orders of court such continuance on post cannot and, in this case, does not confer on him any right for continuance, it does not enhance his case for regularisation - Only an interim arrangement pending decision by court and cannot disturb position in law or equities, as on date of petition - Appeal allowed.
