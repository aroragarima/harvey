Vinod Kumar Singh v Banaras Hindu University And Others

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28471; (1988) 1 SCC 80; AIR 1988 SC 371; 1988 (1) CCC 90; JT 1987 (4) SC 304; 1987(2) SCALE 1046; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 941; 1988 (1) UJ 57

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 20, r. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Judgment pronounced in open court but not signed - Permissibility for its alteration or modification - Held, permissible only in exceptional cases - Appeal allowed.
