Pushparani S. Sundaram and Others v Pauline Manomani James (Deceased) and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3697; (2002) 9 SCC 582; 2000 (40) ALR 564; JT 2000 (8) SC 95

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, s. 16 (c) - Appellant filed a suit for specific performance of a contract for sale of an immovable property in accordance with terms of that contract with respondent - An obligation was cast under agreement on appellant to obtain permission from authorities - Whether appellants were always ready and willing to perform their part under contract? - Held, s. 16(c) of specific Relief Act requires that not only there be a plea of readiness, willingness but it has to be proved so, in present case, not only plaintiff has not come in witness box, but not even sent any communication or notice to defendant about his willingness to perform his part of contract - Appeal dismissed.
