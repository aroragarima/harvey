Automobile Products of India Limited v Rukmaji Bala and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhi Ranjan Das, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 65; AIR 1955 SC 258; 1955 (1) LLJ 346; 1955 (1) MLJ(SC) 148; 1955 SCJ 253; [1955] 1 S.C.R. 1241

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Labour Appellate Tribunal, Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1947

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Service - Industrial Disputes (Appellate Tribunal) Act, 1950, s. 22 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33 - Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1947 - Retrenchment of Workmen - Held, Labour Appellate Tribunal has no jurisdiction to impose conditions as a prerequisite for granting permission to the company to retrench its workmen - Appeal allowed
