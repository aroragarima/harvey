Ashok Kumar Mishra and Others v Collector, Raipur and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 366; (1980) 1 SCC 180; AIR 1980 SC 112; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 491; 1980 UJ 133

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Madhya Pradesh Municipal Corporation (Preparation, Revision and Publication of Electoral Rolls and Selection of Councillors) Rules, 1963, rr. (1) and 4(3) - Appeal against dismissal of writ petition - Laches - Power of High Court - Petition challenging validity of election to Municipal Corporation - Absence of satisfactory explanation for delay in filing petition - Petition was held liable to be dismissed - Appeal dismissed.
