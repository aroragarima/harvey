Â Tara Prasad Singh Etc. Etc. v Union Of India & Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria, N.L. Untwalia, P.S. Kailasam, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 159; (1978) 3 SCC 365; (1980) 4 SCC 179; AIR 1980 SC 1682; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1042

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Keywords: Colliery, Life Insurance Corporation (Modification of Settlements) Act, 1976, Existing Leases, Extraction, Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Amendment Act, 1976, Coal Mines Conservation And Development Act, 1974

Summary: Mines and Minerals - Constitution, art. 14, 19, 31, 31A , 32, 246, sch. 7, list 1, entry 54 - Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Amendment Act, 1976, s. 3(3)(b) - Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Act, 1973, ss. 2(b), 3(3)(a), 30(2) - Writ Petitions u/art. 32 challenging the validity of the 1976 Act on the ground that it is violative of the provisions of arts. 14, 19(1)(f), 19(1) (g) and 31 of the Constitution - Ancillary issues - Applicability ofs.3(3)(a) considering definition of 'Coal Mines' to compostite mines - Interpretation-Marginal Notes-Object and effect of - Held, constitutional - Does not violate provisions of arts. 14, 19 and 31 of Constitution - Definition does not apply to certain composite mines but provisions apply to leases of composite mines - Cannot take away the effect of the provisions - Petition Nos. 150, 151, 180, 205-210, 226, 270-271, 346, 355, 403, 396-398, 599, 541, 543, 626, 635-639, 661, 687-692, 758/77 and 154, 571- 574, 603, 605, 610 and 611/77 dismissed ; Petition Nos. 111, 178, 220, 221, 257, 352, 600 and 1130-1134 partly allowed.
