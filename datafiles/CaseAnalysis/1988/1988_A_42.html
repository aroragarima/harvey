Collector Of Central Excise,Guntur v Andhra Sugar Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 257; (1989) Supp1 SCC 144; (1989) SCC (Tax) 162; AIR 1989 SC 625; 1989 CRLR 122; 1989 CrLR(SC) 122; 1989 (19) ECC 46; 1988 (19) ECR 575; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 564; JT 1988 (4) SC 410; 1988(2) SCALE 1323; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 543; 1989 (1) ShimLC 349; 1989 (73) STC 216; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 543; 1989 (1) UJ 120

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 11 B(2) - Central Excise Tariff, Item 68 - Acetic Anhydride - Benefit of exemption demanded under drug intermediate - Acetic Anhydride is manufactured and sold to drug manufacturers - Interpretation of Statutes was also to be done - Held, meaning ascribed by authority issuing notification is good guide that Acetic Anhydride is drug intermediate and is eligible for benefit of exemption.
