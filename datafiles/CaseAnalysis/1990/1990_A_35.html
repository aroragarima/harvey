All Manipur Regular Posts Vacancies Substitute v State of Manipur

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 499; (1991) Supp2 SCC 643; AIR 1991 SC 2088; 1991 (17) ATC 929; 1991 LabIC 2118

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Dispute regarding regularisation of service - Substituted ad- hoc teachers of education department working for several years - State Govt. refused to regularise them and started taking steps for direct recruitment - Directions issued by Supreme Court to that State Govt. should consider case of regularisation of these employees before starting direct recruitment - Direct recruitments can be done only if there is any additional vacancy or if any vacancy exists after process of regularisation - Appeal allowed.
