A. V. Mohan Rao and Another v M. Kishan Rao and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 July 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1597; (2002) 6 SCC 174; (2002) SCC (Cr) 1281; AIR 2002 SC 2653; 2002 (3) CCR 33; 2002 (3) CLC 1169; [2002] 111 Comp Cas 390; 2002 (4) CompLJ 200; 2002 (3) Crimes 1; 2002 (5) CRJ 77; JT 2002 (5) SC 180; 2002 (3) RCR(Criminal) 561; 2002(5) SCALE 218; 2002 (3) SCJ 381; 2002 (4) SLT 429; [2002] Supp1 S.C.R. 175; 2002 (5) Supreme 8; 2002 (2) UC 423B

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Body Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 60, 63, 68, 68 A and 621 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 482, 4(2)(n) and 188 - Accused persons by making false, deceptive and misleading statements and by suppressing relevant facts induced various persons to pay them money for purchase of shares - Siphoned off those funds into bogus companies exclusively owned by them - Those companies in turn applied and got shares at instance of accused persons - Whether under facts and circumstances emerged from complaint petition and materials filed with it a case for quashing complaint filed u/s. 482 of CrPC, 1973 is made out or not? - Held, it is relevant to note here that from ss. 4 and 188 of CrPC, 1973 it is clear that even if offence is committed by a citizen of India outside country same is subject to jurisdiction of courts in India - High Court was right in declining to quash complaint petition - Appeal dismissed.
